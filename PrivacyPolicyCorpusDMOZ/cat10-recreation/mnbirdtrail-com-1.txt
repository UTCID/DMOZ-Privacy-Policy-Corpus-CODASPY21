Privacy Policy
The Pine to Prairie International Birding Trail is committed to protecting the privacy of its visitors and provide a safe, secure user experience. We will use our best efforts to ensure the information you submit to us remains private.
Where visitors choose to provide mnbirdtrail.com with a name, e-mail address or other information, mnbirdtrail.com keeps all such response lists confidential. Personally-identifiable information will not be sold, rented or exchanged with any other organization or entity unless required to do so by law.

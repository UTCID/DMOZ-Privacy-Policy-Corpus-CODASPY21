Privacy Policy
Almost Heaven Golden Retriever Rescue & Sanctuary,
Inc. (Almost Heaven or AHGRRS) respects your privacy and is the
sole owner of the information collected on this site. The
information we collect (e.g., volunteer application, adoption
application, donations and order forms) is used by Almost Heaven
Golden Retriever Rescue & Sanctuary, Inc. to process the application(s),
answer user inquiries and/or process orders. We do not rent, sell
or otherwise make available this information to third parties,
unless directed to by legal or regulatory authorities. We may,
however, use your e-mail address to notify you of special events
and news that we feel may interest you.

Links

Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other

e-Newsletter
If you wish to subscribe to our
e-Newsletter we ask you to provide your name and email address.
If you no longer wish to receive our e-Bulletin or if you wish
to have your information removed from our records please unsubscribe
from the mailing by contacting ahgrr@frontiernet.net.
Notification of Changes
If we decide to change our privacy
policy, we will post those changes on our homepage so our users
are always aware of what information we collect, how we use
it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

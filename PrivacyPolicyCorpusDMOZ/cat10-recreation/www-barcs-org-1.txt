Privacy Policy
Baltimore Animal Rescue and Care Shelter, Inc. (BARCS) provides excellent service to all visitors of this website and donors who give through the website by respecting your concerns about privacy. We understand that you may have questions about how this website collects and uses the information that you provide to us.
When we need to collect personally identifiable information from you in order for you to make a donation, we will ask you to voluntarily supply us with the information we need to process the transaction such as name, address, telephone number, email address, credit card number and expiration date. We will use your email address to send a confirmation of your payment. We will also use your email address to notify you about events and promotions for BARCS.
If you have any questions, please contact us at 410-396-4695 or info@barcs.org.

Find a RV Park Privacy Policy
Find
a RV Park is committed to protecting your privacy. Any personal
information that you provide to Find a RV Park should be done on a
completely voluntary basis. We only use the information that you
voluntarily provide us to process your reservation. To fulfill this
processing, it may be necessary to provide your information to
prescreened third party businesses affiliated with the RV industry.
These include campgrounds and other outdoor recreation entities.
In
addition, Find a RV Park may provide aggregate statistics about our web
site visitors, traffic patterns and related site information to RV
industry members. Your detailed personal information will not be
released for such purposes.
Any
information you provide directly, may be collected and stored by Find a
RV Park. If you later change your mind and decide that you no longer
want your personal information made available to Find a RV Park, please
email us . Also, please understand that once Find a RV Park has
provided your information to third party business affiliates, it can no
longer exercise control over subsequent users. In the event that you
later decide that you do not want to receive any additional information
from a particular business entity, it will be your responsibility to
contact that entity and request your removal from its mailing list.
Please keep in mind that whenever you provide personal information
online, there is a risk that it may be collected and used by people you
don't know. Although Find a RV Park strives to protect the personal
information of its Web site users, we cannot guarantee the security of
any information you disclose online and you do so at your own risk.
Users should be aware that when you are on this Find a RV Park Web site
you will have options to enter other Web sites, pages or links that are
outside of our service and beyond our control. Protection of your
privacy at these linked sites will be governed by the privacy policies
at these sites. Find a RV Park is not responsible for the content,
requirements, services, performance, policies or any other aspect of
these Web sites. Find a RV Park may release personal information when
we believe, in good faith, that such release is reasonably necessary to
(a) comply with the law, (b) enforce or apply the terms of any of our
user agreements, or (c) protect the rights, property or safety of Find
a RV Park, our users, or others. By using the Find a RV Park Web site,
you provide your consent to this Find a RV Park Privacy Policy. If you
do not agree to this policy, please do not use this site.

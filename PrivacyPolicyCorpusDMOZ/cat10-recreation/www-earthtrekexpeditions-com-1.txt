We Care About Your Privacy
We can be reached via e-mail at raft.et@live.com or by telephone at 800-229-8735
For each visitor to our Web site, our Web server automatically recognizes the domain name which refers consumers to our site, but not their e-mail addresses.
We collect only the referring domain name, and not the e-mail addresses of visitors to our Web site. The information collected is used for internal review, and to improve the content and/or layout of our web site for each individual. It is then discarded. This information is not shared with other organizations.
If you supply us with your postal address on-line, you will receive the information for which you provided us your address. You may also receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending an e-mail to the above address. Please provide us with your exact name and address and we will be sure your name is removed from our list.
Persons who supply us with their telephone number on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding a reservation request you have placed on-line. If you do not wish to receive such telephone calls, please let us know by sending an e-mail to the above address. Please provide us with your correct phone number and we will be sure your name is removed from our list.
E-mail addresses are collected for our Newsletter from a form on our site. E-mail addresses are not shared with other organizations. If you do not wish to receive e-mail newsletters, please let us know by sending an e-mail to the above address. Please provide us with your correct e-mail address and we will be sure your name is removed from our list.

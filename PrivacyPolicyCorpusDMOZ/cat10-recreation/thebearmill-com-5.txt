The Bear Mill Shipping Map and Policy
The Bear Mill will ship expedited shipping upon request.  The charge for this shipping will be calculated separately.
The Color-coded map is for the purpose of estimate only. Actual transmit times may vary. When calculating shipping, please note that number of days in transit DOES NOT INCLUDE weekends, holidays, or the day your order leaves our warehouse.

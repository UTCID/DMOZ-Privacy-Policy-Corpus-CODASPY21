Policy Statement
This is the
web site of Ferrets Unlimited Rescue Services.
Our postal
address is
P. O. Box 34025
Cleveland, OH 44134
We can be
reached via e-mail at rescue@ferretsunlimited.org
or you can reach us by telephone at (216) 502-2977
For each
visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes no
information regarding the domain or e-mail address.
We collect
and adoption application information that you voluntarily submit.
With respect
to cookies: We do not set any cookies.
If you do not
want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending
us e-mail at the above address.
If you supply
us with your postal address on-line you will only receive the information
for which you provided us your address.
Persons who
supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone
contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line.
With respect
to Ad Servers: To try and bring you offers that are of interest to you, we
have relationships with other companies that we allow to place ads on our
Web pages. As a result of your visit to our site, ad server companies may
collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.
If you feel
that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may
contact us at the above addresses or phone number.

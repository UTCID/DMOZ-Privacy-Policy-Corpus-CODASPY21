PRIVACYThis is the privacy policy for Camp Arrowhead. Protecting personal information and using it in a manner that is consistent with expectations that are associated with our camp philosophy is top priority at Camp Arrowhead.Our homepage is located at camparrowhead.com. You can view our dates, rates, important information and refund policies. Please contact us with any questions or concerns you may have.SecurityOur online data management is handled by our professional partners at Circuitree, LLC. Their privacy policy can be found at their website www.circuitree.comNeither Camp Arrowhead, our webmasters, nor Circuitree, LLC, will re-use or redistribute any information that is gathered during your online experience at the Camp Arrowhead website. Camp Arrowhead will use security controls where available to ensure that family and camper information in our computers is protected.As a member of the online Camp Arrowhead community, you will always have access to your information. You will be able to review your information and make necessary changes to ensure that all is complete and accurate.Camp Arrowhead will use any collected data and information for office use and camp purposes only. Never will information or data be sold or redistributed to any third party. Your privacy is our priority.CookiesCookies are a technology which can be used to give special information on a website. Our website may contain areas in which cookies are needed. You can set your computer to notify you when a cookie should be enabled and choose to accept it if necessary.

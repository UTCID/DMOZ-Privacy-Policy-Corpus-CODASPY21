Love Wildlife? Love to Shop?Check out these ways to help!
Shop NWRI's Online Store
Buy yourself or someone you love a gift that benefits wildlife!
Choose from Socks, Jewelry, Books, Cards, Stickers, Kitchen Items, and so much more! We are adding lots of new merchandise the week of November 16, 2020, so be sure to check back frequently!
Our Amazon Wishlists
We keep a running list of the items most in need at the Wildlife Center on Amazon. To view our lists, and purchase a gift for wildlife, click on this link and use the box on the left to navigate between our three wish lists: General, Baby Shower, and Field Rescue Gear
Amazon Smile
Designate Nebraska Wildlife Rehab, Inc. as your charity of choice and then make your Amazon purchases through Amazon Smile whenever you shop. Amazon will then donate 0.5% of the price of your eligible AmazonSmile purchases to NWR.
Together a Greater Good: TAGG
TAGG is a way for you to support NWR without spending a dime! Just download the TAGG app, select Nebraska Wildlife Rehab as your cause, and then scan your receipts from participating local businesses. The businesses will donate a portion of their proceeds to help save our wildlife!

GDPR – Privacy Notice and Cookies
TravelHealth.co.uk is a UK based health website for travellers. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our website visitors.  The website is a tool to provide information to the general public and we do not collect or sell the data of those who visit the site for marketing purposes.
What data we collect
When someone visits TravelHealth.co.uk we use two third-party services, Google Analytics and Jetpack Plugins, to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behaviour patterns via cookies. We do this to find out things such as the number of visitors to the various parts of the site. This information is only processed in a way which does not identify anyone. We do not make, and do not allow Google or Jetpack to make, any attempt to find out the identities of those visiting our website.
TravelHealth.co.uk does not hold a mailing list and does not send out newsletters. We do however sometimes have e-mails sent to us by individuals – these e-mails are regularly deleted after monitoring.

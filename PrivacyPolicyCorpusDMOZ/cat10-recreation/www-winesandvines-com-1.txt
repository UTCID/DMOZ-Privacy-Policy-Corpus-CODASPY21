Cookies
Wines & Vines use of cookies is intended for the convenience of visitors to the www.winesandvines.com website. Wines & Vines subscribers' access to premium editorial content on-line, including the ability to post comments, is controlled via login. Cookies enable users' login parameters to be saved, making it unnecessary to log in again on successive visits to the website. Similarly, cookies ease navigation of the online directory by remembering a user's most recent search results.

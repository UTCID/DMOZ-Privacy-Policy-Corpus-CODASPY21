University of Rhode Island Sailing respects your privacy and we are committed to protecting it. University of Rhode Island Sailing provides this Privacy Statement to inform you of our Privacy Policy and practices and about the way your information is collected online and how that information is used. We make this notice readily available on our home page and at the bottom of every web page. What we collect:
If you inquire through our contact form or site analysis form, any information your submit will be used to answer your inquiry ONLY.
We do track IP’s and user sessions for security purposes only.
How we use this information:
All information you submit to us, will be used internally at University of Rhode Island Sailing to help answer your inquiry or meet your needs.
In the case of client information, we use the contact information of existing clients to inform them of changes and offers at University of Rhode Island Sailing through direct e-mail.
What we do not do:
We will NEVER give your contact information or any related information you submit to University of Rhode Island Sailing to a third party.

MX-3.com - The Mazda MX-3 Page Privacy Policy
MX-3.com - The Mazda MX-3 Page works with a third party
(Engage) for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and
other marketing messages. To do this, Engage collects anonymous data typically through the use of cookies. To learn
more about Engage, including your ability to opt out of the Engage system, go
here.
MX-3.com is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. Below is information outlining our
continued support for the responsible management of visitor information.
Visitor Information
During the process of browsing MX-3.com we do not collect any personally identifiable information
about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address.
However, MX-3.com does obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information such as the Internet Service
Provider you use to connect to the Internet, your browser type, or the type of computer operating system you use
(Macintosh or Windows, for example). The information collected by MX-3.com is used for informational and statistical
purposes only.
However, in some cases, you may voluntarily provide personal information in response to a form or survey.
For
example, The Universal Mazda MX-3 Owners Registry, Site Surveys etc. If this does occur, MX-3.com only uses this
information for the purpose described in the form itself.
In addition, MX-3.com may combine this information with
other users' information and review it in an aggregate form to understand the type of individuals who visit a MX-3.com,
drive, and/or are interested in the Mazda MX-3.
Participation in Industry-side Privacy Initiatives
If you have any additional questions about MX-3.com's dedication to visitor privacy, please contact us at
webmaster @ mx-3.com.

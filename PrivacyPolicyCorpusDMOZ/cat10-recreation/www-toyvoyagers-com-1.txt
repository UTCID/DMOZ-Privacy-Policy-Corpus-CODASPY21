We value your privacy and take every step to ensure that your information

What personally identifiable information does ToyVoyagers.com collect
from me?
ToyVoyagers.com does not collect private or personal information from this web
site, except where you willingly provide it.
If you contact us we may keep a record of that correspondence.
Wherever we collect personal information we make an effort to include a link to
this Privacy Policy on that page.
What are cookies and does ToyVoyagers.com use them?
A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server
and stored on your computer's hard drive. ToyVoyagers.com uses cookies solely
for the purposes of enabling a more efficient navigation system while you are
using our site. We do not use this cookie to collect information about the
user.
With whom does ToyVoyagers.com share my information?
ToyVoyagers.com will not disclose any of your personally identifiable
information except when we have your express permission.
What is the policy on allowing me to update, correct, or delete my
personally identifiable information?
All of your personally identifiable information is available from the Control
Panel screen when you login to ToyVoyagers.com.
You can update this information at any time.
How does protect my personal information?
As with all information, will never rent or sell your personal information.
Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet can be
guaranteed to be 100% secure, unless it is processed through a Secure Credit

result, while we strive to protect your personal information, ToyVoyagers.com cannot
ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us via email
or Contact Us form. Once we receive your transmission, we make our best effort
to ensure its security on our systems.

Album Rules
There are a few stipulations regarding the use of the album.
You must have at least 25 posts to have a gallery on Slot Car Illustrated.
Political, religious, pornographic, lewd, violent, hate images, etc. are
prohibited and your account will be suspended without warning.
Any material deemed inappropriate, may be removed without notice.
Copyrighted images/photos should not be added to any member’s gallery,
unless a written release is granted by the owner of the photo.
On occasion SCI may like to use some images housed in a public folders for
the promotion of Slot Car Illustrated.com and the general hobby of slot cars.
An administrator will ask for permission to do so prior to publishing any
images.
All images hosted in the gallery will carry the "Hosted by Slot Car
Illustrated" watermark.

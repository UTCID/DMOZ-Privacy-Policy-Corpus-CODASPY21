Home / Privacy
We are committed to protecting your individual and business privacy. Information about you is used to provide you with the best possible service. We will not e-mail you in the future unless you have given us your consent.
If you are registering as a consumer we will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with The UK Data Protection Act 1998) and according to The UK Which? Web Trader Code of Practice.
We will give you the opportunity to refuse any marketing from us in the future.
We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit consent. All information is collected by AquaPress Ltd and not made available to any third parties.
The information used is collected through the ordering/registration process and from e-mail correspondence.
The type of information we will collect about you includes details that are necessary to process an order or request for information.
Should you wish to opt out of receiving any marketing material please e-mail info@aquapress.co.uk with the subject heading ‘unsubscribe’ and include the address which you wish to have removed.
To the best of our ability the information we hold will be accurate and up to date.
Any personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance with our internal security policy.
Questions/comments about privacy?
If you have any questions/comments about privacy, you should email us at: info@aquapress.co.uk

Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to Homeland Health Specialists, and our policy is simple.  We will collect no personally identifiable information about you when you visit the website unless you choose to provide that information.  This privacy policy does not describe information collection practices on other sites, including those linked to or from the website.
Info we do collect and use
The server on which the website is located collects and saves only the default information customarily logged by web server software.  Such information may include the date and time of your visit, the IP address, and the pages and images requested.
Like most companies, Homeland Health Specialists also collects and logs information automatically and through the use of electronic web beaconing tools that may be transparent to you, such as your IP address, the name and location of your internet service provider, the type and version of your browser, the length of time that you stay on our site, search queries, your click-stream data, the location that referred you to Homeland Health Specialists, and the average number of pages viewed.  Homeland Health Specialists does not create, place, or analyze data cookies.
Changes and Updates to this Policy
We reserve the right to change the information, services, features and policies at any time.  We will post changes to this and any other policies on this website in writing.

ORDERING AND PAYMENT
Higher Plane wines can be ordered online, or by phone.
Payment is accepted by credit card (Visa or Mastercard) or by Pay Pal.
Orders by phone - phone Higher Plane on +61 8 9755 9000 during business hours, western standard time 10am – 5pm Monday to Friday.
Please note: There is a minimum order quantity of 6 bottles.
SHIPPING
Freight is subsidised and set at $10.00 per case for Perth Metro, $15.00 per case for Country WA and $25.00 per case for shipments to the eastern states.
For international freight rates, prices are available on application. However we encourage you to contact our distributors in the various countries we export to.
Wines will be shipped from the winery within 3 working days from clearance of payment.Australia Post is the carrier used within Australia. Please provide instructions for delivery if you are not home as delivery is between normal business hours, in the event that no alternative instructions for delivery is advised the wine will be carded and returned to the nearest Australia Post Office for collection.
Deliveries cannot be made on weekends or public holidays.
If your wine does not arrive within 10 working days of your order being received please email steve@higherplanewines.com.au or call +61 8 9755 9000.
AGE RESTRICTION:
You must be 18 years to order wine from this web site.
It is an offence to obtain liquor on behalf of a person under the age of 18 years.
By purchasing any of our wines, you warrant that you and the recipient of this order are at least 18 years of age.
REFUNDS & RETURNS:
On the rare occasions that incorrect goods are delivered Higher Plane will fully refund or replace the bottles concerned, including return shipping costs, providing they are returned unopened within one month of delivery.
If a wine is found to be faulty we will be happy to offer a replacement or refund. Notification must be given to us within 5 working days of delivery.
All reasonable steps will be taken to ensure your wine is received in good condition. Should your wine not arrive in satisfactory condition please contact us within 5 working days. Whilst your wine will be replaced or fully refunded, Higher Plane requires damaged goods to be returned within 21 working days so we can take action with the carrier.
In the event that we have run out of an item on your order, we will endeavour to contact you to arrange a replacement. Where an Item ordered is not available we may accept the order for the remaining Items ordered which are available.
However, at peak times of the year, we do reserve the right to replace out of stock items with another of equal value.
PRICING:
Prices are subject to alteration. We shall endeavour to update the website prices whenever a change takes place. We shall contact you prior to accepting the order if there has been a change in price.
All wines are offered subject to availability and remain the property of Higher Plane until payment is received in full.
10% GST payable on shipments within Australia is included in the prices.

WEBSITE COOKIES
HTTP cookies, sometimes known as web cookies or just cookies, are parcels of text sent by a server to a web browser and then sent back unchanged by the browser each time it accesses that server. HTTP cookies are used for authenticating, tracking, and maintaining specific information about users, such as site preferences, how far a page is scrolled and the contents of their electronic shopping carts.
Cookies are also subject to a number of misconceptions, mostly based on the erroneous notion that they are computer programs. In fact, cookies are simple pieces of data unable to perform any operation by themselves. In particular, they are neither spyware nor viruses, despite the detection of cookies from certain sites by many anti-spyware products.
Most modern browsers allow users to decide whether to accept cookies, but rejection makes some websites unusable. For example, shopping baskets implemented using cookies do not work if cookies are rejected.
The Websites do not use cookies to store any personal information.

This is not attached in anyway to visitors personal identification. SharingAt this time, PezCollectors.com does not share any information with advertisers, merchants, or any other third party. Our privacy statement applies solely to PezCollectors.com. NewsletterYou can either elect to subscribe or unsubscribe to our newsletter at any time. All information stored on our database is not shared or sold to any third party what so ever. Subscribers wishing to unsubscribe to our newsletter can select the unsubscribe option on our home page at any time. Sending to a FriendWhen a visitor chooses to send product descriptions to a friend, the names and e-mail addresses are not logged.

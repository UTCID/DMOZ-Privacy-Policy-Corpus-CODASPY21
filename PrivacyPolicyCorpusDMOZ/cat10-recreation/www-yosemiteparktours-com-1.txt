Find Your Season, Pick Your Adventures
Yosemite National Park is the ultimate outdoor playgroundyear round. Warm months are typically filled with sunny days that welcome legendary hikes and breathtaking views. Wintertime blankets Yosemite in snow, creating the perfect landscape for skiing, skating and snowshoeing. Every season has its not-to-be missed highlightswhich is why so many visitors come back time and time again.

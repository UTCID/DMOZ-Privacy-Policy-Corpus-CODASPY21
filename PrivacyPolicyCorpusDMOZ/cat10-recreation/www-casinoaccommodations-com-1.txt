General Information
This privacy policy (“Policy”) outlines the ways in which Casino Accommodations, LLC., which posts this Policy (referred to as “Casino Accommodations”, “we”, “our” or “us”) collect and use information from individuals who visit any of our Sites, social media pages or mobile applications (each a “Site” or collectively “Sites”), respond to our interactive advertisements (“Ads”), purchase goods and/or services from or engage in gaming, junket or entertainment activities offered, promoted and/or sponsored by Casino Accommodations.
What Personal Information We Collect

Specifically, we may collect your Personal Information when you submit information to us through our Site, by email, text messages, through online forms and through other activities in which you engage on Casino Accommodation’s Site. Personal Information that we collect may include your name, address, date of birth, social security number, driver’s license number, government-issued identification card number, passport number or naturalization number, telephone number, and email address.
If you engage in gaming activity during an event offered, promoted and/or sponsored by Casino Accommodations, we may also collect certain information about your gaming activity, including your casino player club card number. (“Card Number”).
Cookies
Our Site and Ads each use cookie technology. A cookie is a small data file that is saved on your hard disk by your computer’s browser when you visit or interact with our Site or Ads. Cookies are then recognized by your Internet browser to identify you and personalize your experience and gather web statistical data, such as which pages you visit, what material you download, your Internet provider’s domain name and country, and the addresses of websites you visited immediately before, and after, visiting a particular website. The cookies we utilize do not store any Personal Information and are used to generate statistics regarding usage of our Site and to help serve relevant, targeted offers and Ads to you based on your browsing history.
Most browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove cookies and to reject cookies. If you choose to remove cookies or reject cookies, this could affect certain features or services of our Site.

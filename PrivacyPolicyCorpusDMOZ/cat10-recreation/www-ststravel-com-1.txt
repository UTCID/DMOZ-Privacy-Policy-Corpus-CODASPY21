STUDENT TRAVEL SERVICES PRIVACY POLICY
(last updated: September 2013)
Student Travel Services is committed to protecting your privacy. The information you provide on this website will be
treated confidentially. Student Travel Services does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.
Student Travel Services may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular
offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your personal information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number)
will not transferred to the third party. If you wish to subscribe or unsubscribe from this mailing list or need more information
about the mailing list, please email webmaster@ststravel.com.
Student Travel Services reserves the right to access and/or disclose your personal information if required to do so by
law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply
with legal process served on Student Travel Services or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of
Student Travel Services; (c) confirm your reservation with airlines, hotels or other travel service providers.
Cookies
Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that we transfer to your computer's hard drive through your web browser.
A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs
or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in
the domain that issued the cookie to you. The primary purpose of cookies is to track our advertising efforts and
provide booking credit to our online affiliates. This information is treated confidentially and will not be shared
with anyone outside of Student Travel Services. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web
browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you
prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully view the entire Student Travel Services web site.
Security
This Web site takes every precaution to protect our users’ information. When users submit sensitive information via
the Web site, their information is protected both online and off-line.
Links
This Web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that Student Travel Services is not responsible for
the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to
read the privacy statements of each and every Web site that collects personally identifiable information. This
privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.
Policy Changes
Student Travel Services reserves the right to modify its Privacy Policy at any time. In the event that we make a
material change to our policy we will post a notice of the change on this page.
Contact Information
If you still have questions about our privacy policies please contact us at webmaster@ststravel.com.

St Mary’s Loch Sailing Club only collects and stores information reagarding members and visitors that is pertinent to the efficient and safe running of the club and events held at the club.
Information is never shared with any other third parties, with the exception of entrants to certain race events where information maybe shared with sailing organistions associated with that race event.

Privacy Policy
At The Bear Mill, integrity is very important to us. We value the trust that our customers place in us and it is for this reason we will never sell, rent, or trade your information with third party companies. Nor does The Bear Mill partner with or have any special relationships with any ad server companies.
Visitor information maintained by us, including proprietary information is located and can be updated by you, after login, at the My Account page.
When placing your order, your information is used to process and deliver your order as requested. Payment information provided allows us to bill your credit card company. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, which is the gold standard in Internet encryption. This highly sophisticated system scrambles your financial data as it travels from your computer to our website, making it useless to anyone but The Bear Mill. If we have any questions regarding your order, we will contact you by phone or e-mail. Additionally, to apprise you of new products, services and upcoming events, you may occasionally receive an email, postal mailing, or telephone call. If you do not wish to be included in these notices, please contact us at the information provided below.
Upon accessing our site, our web server automatically collects cookies which store information such as your domain name, the date and time you access our site, and the internet address of the website that linked you to our site. This information is used for record keeping by storing visitors’ preferences, and recording current and past session information. The collected information is used to improve website content, customize the content and/or layout, and to notify visitors of updates. This is all in an effort to provide better customer service. Again, this information is not shared on any level with other organizations.
Children’s Privacy Statement: The Bear Mill does not sell products for purchase by children. We sell children’s products for purchase by adults. If you are under 18, you may use thebearmill.com only with the involvement of a parent or guardian.
If our information practices change at some point in the future, we will post any and all policy changes to our web site. If you are concerned about how your information is used, check our web site periodically for policy updates. Please know that you always have the ability to opt-out of any policy changes by reaching us at the contact information provided below.
If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, or if you have any questions regarding our policies, we request that you may contact us at the information provided below.

Privacy Policy
The Humane Society Wildlife Land Trust (HSWLT) is committed to your right to privacy and to keeping your personal information private. You can visit our web site without telling us who you are or revealing any identifying information about yourself. However, because our web site includes areas in which visitors can provide personally identifying information, we have developed this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and practices regarding information we collect about you.
This Privacy Policy governs the URL hswlt.org and certain electronic communications maintained and distributed by HSWLT.  This website is owned and operated by HSWLT for the benefit of its members and other visitors interested in the protection of wildlife and habitat. Our headquarters is located at 1255 23rd Street, NW, Suite 450, Washington, DC 20037.

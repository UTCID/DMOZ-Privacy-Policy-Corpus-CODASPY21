Privacy Policy
At SF Shakes we value the privacy of all visitors to our website. We will not collect any personally identifying information except that which is volunteered by users.
Our website may gather usage information collected by tracking software to gauge the effectiveness of promotional notices as well as offer informational updates about Festival events. Personal information about individuals and their visits to the website will not be recorded.Third parties, including Facebook, may use cookies, beacons or other storage technologies to receive information that measures the impact of target ads. You may opt-out of this by visiting http://www.aboutads.info/choices
You may elect to provide the San Francisco Shakespeare Festival personal information when contacting us to register for one of our educational programs, attend one of our events or to make a donation.
This information is provided voluntarily and may include your name, email and other contact informationBy using the SF Shakes website you agree to the above policy.

Privacy Policy
GotJokes.net has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: GotJokes.net. 								We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, to administer our Web site, to investigate fraudulant contest 								entries, and to get general demographics info on our users. 								This site contains links to other sites. GotJokes.net is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. 								However, no information is transmitted from GotJokes.net to sponsor sites except in the following situation:
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
We run contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (primarily, their email address). The contact information of customers is used to contact the visitor when necessary. Users will not be sent unsolicited email and their information will not be distributed or sold in any other fashion.

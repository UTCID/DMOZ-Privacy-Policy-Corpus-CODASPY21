Privacy Policy
Regarding Domestic shipping: Currently there are several states in the United States that do not allow wine to be shipped to their residents.  Due to on going legal cases this list changes.  For the most up-to-date information please email sales@suvinowineryaz.com or call us at 480.994.8466.
Currently we do not ship Internationally.
What is your return policy? Any return must be made within 14 days of receipt of the item.  All returns require prior authorization. On any damaged or broken item, a photo of the item/items is required in order to receive a replacement product or refund, please contact us with 72 hours of receipt of any damaged items.  If the item cannot be repaired or replaced in a timely manner, we will refund you the amount of the item plus shipping fees.If you wish to return an undamaged item, you must call or e-mail.  You will be responsible for shipping costs for returning any items.  We will not refund shipping fees.  Once the item is received in its original condition, we will issue a refund.  We do not refund the original shipping fees for returns of undamaged items.

Privacy
We do not collect personally-identifying
information unless you explicitly provide it to us. Examples include:
Sending us an email (either by clicking a link or using an online form);
Subscribing to our newsletter;
Voting / recommending a property;
Sending an inquiry to a property through our system;
Commenting on an article.
In such cases, your IP address will be recorded in addition to any other information
you provide to us (such as, but not limited to, email address, name, and phone number).
You can request updates or removal of your information by
contacting us.
PetFriendly.ca will not sell or disclose personal, identifying information unless
required to do so by law.
Advertising
We use third-party advertising companies to
serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information
(not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number unless
you provide it to them)
about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide
advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on the PetFriendly.ca
site. Google uses a DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads to you, based on your visit to
this site and other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of the use of the DART cookie
by clicking here.
You can also find out more about how Google manages data in its ads products
here.
PetFriendly.ca is also an Amazon Associate and earns from qualifying purchases.

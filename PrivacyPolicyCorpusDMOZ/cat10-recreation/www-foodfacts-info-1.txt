Security
This site and its server have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
Email and Email Addresses
During your use of Food Facts, you may have occasion to email us. If you do, your email address will not be sold, traded, published, or given to any other entity.
Any information you reveal to us could be given to another entity if we believe or suspect you are violating the terms of use of this site, violating the law, or otherwise misusing this site or its server.

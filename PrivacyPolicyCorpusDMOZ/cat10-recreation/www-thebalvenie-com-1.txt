Members Cask Terms and Conditions
In order to be eligible to receive the free member’s dram when you visit the Balvenie Distillery, you must pre-book a Balvenie Distillery tour (Tour) by following the instructions on the Balvenie Warehouse 24 website (Website) and be a registered member of The Balvenie relationship marketing programme, Warehouse 24 (Warehouse 24). Residents of the USA are excluded from this offer.
You will be required to confirm your membership of Warehouse 24 when you pre-book a Tour. Full instructions are on the booking form for the Tour on the Website.
The Tour costs £35 per person, and the member’s dram is offered at no additional charge.
Warehouse 24 members attending a Tour will be offered a dram (25-35ml) of single malt Scotch whisky from a 1974 Bourbon cask (Liquid), the volume of which will be at the discretion of the tour guide.
The Liquid will be offered exclusively to Warehouse 24 members when they go on a Tour. However, it may occasionally be offered and distributed at events outside of the Balvenie Distillery to attendees, who may not be registered members of Warehouse 24, at the discretion of the host.
The offer is limited to 1 dram per Warehouse 24 member per visit.
Recipients must be aged 18 years old or over.

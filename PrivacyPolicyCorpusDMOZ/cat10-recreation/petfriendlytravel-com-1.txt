Your Privacy is Important to Us
At Pet Friendly Travel, we believe that your private information is just that, private. Therefore we do not share or sell your information with any other parties. For those listing with us, we send all sensitive data via encrypted SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) to ensure that sensitive data can not be intercepted in-transit. In additon, Pet Friendly Travel does not track our visitor’s browsing activity or IP addresses.

ADA Accessibility Policy
Toad Hollow Vineyards is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines. If you have questions or need assistance with any of our web pages, please call us at 707-431-1441 or send an e-mail to web@thv.gfdev and we will respond as soon as possible.
Privacy Policy
At Toad Hollow Vineyards we recognize and respect your privacy. The information we collect is used to notify you about updates, new releases and events. We cherish your loyalty and friendship and know that your privacy is important. We do not share your personal information with any other organizations. We never have and we never will.
When you sign up for membership in our wine club or fill out a form to be on our mailing list, we will send you an e-mail confirming your wish to be added to the list. If, at any time, you wish to be removed from our mailing list, please send an e-mail to the address below with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line. We will see that you are removed from the list.
If you supply us with your address online you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on our wines or events. If you supply us with your telephone number online you may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders placed online or information on new vintages, products or our events.

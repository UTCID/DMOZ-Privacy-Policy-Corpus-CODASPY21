Privacy Policy
When making your purchase, on Eolahillswinery.com you are protected by Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. This technology allows for advanced encryption tools to protect information transmitted between your computer and our server. This encryption technology converts your personal information, including your name, address and credit card information, into code that is securely dispatched over the internet. We will not sell your information nor provide it to any entity other than those required for processing your orders.

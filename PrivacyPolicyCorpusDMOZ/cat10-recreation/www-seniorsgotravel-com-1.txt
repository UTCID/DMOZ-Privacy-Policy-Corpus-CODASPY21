Privacy Policy
The Alberta Prime Times Privacy Code applies to the management of Personal Information in any form whether oral, electronic or written.
Alberta Prime Times reserves the right to amend this Privacy Code from time to time.
The application of this Privacy Code is subject to the requirements or provisions of any applicable legislation, regulations or agreements (such as collective agreements), or the order of any court or other lawful authority.
Purposes for Collection of Personal Information:
Alberta Prime Times collects minimal Personal Information for the following purposes: a) to allow the viewer to navigate through classifieds and other sections. b) to identify a viewer for the purpose of allowing access to certain areas of the Alberta Prime Times site.
The Personal Information collected is the absolute minimum required for the stated purpose and it is never shared with any other individual, company or organization.
Normal precautions regarding e-mail privacy are recommended.

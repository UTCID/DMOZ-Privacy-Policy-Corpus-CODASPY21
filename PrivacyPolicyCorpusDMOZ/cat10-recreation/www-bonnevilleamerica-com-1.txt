Privacy Statement
The owners of BonnevilleAmerica.com have created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this web site.
We will never sell or share the information you give us with anyone else.
Our web site uses cookies to enhance our members visit to our site. Cookies are small, harmless bits of information that are stored on a users computer so that when they are here, we can give them access to all the member features. We use what is called a “session cookie”, which means that when the user quits their browser application the cookie file is removed from their computer. They will receive a new cookie when they sign in to the members area.
BonnevilleAmerica.com is not affiliated or associated with sponsors of sites which hyperlink to or from this site. For more information on this topic see our disclaimer page.
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Our secure sockets layers software (SSL) is the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure data submission. It encrypts all of your personal information, including name, and address, so that it cannot be read as the information travels over the Internet.
Members who collect information about other members from the BonnevilleAmerica.com for the purposes of sending unsolicited emails (“spam”) will be disciplined in accordance with the policies listed in the Acceptable Use Policy (AUP), Violation #7.

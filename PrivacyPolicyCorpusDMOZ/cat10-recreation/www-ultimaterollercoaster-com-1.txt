Privacy Policy
OUR PRIVACY STATEMENT
It is our desire at Ultimate Rollercoaster® to protect the privacy of our guests. This document describes our policy regarding information collected or received during your visit to our web site. Please note that the amount and type of information gathered will vary depending upon your usage.
Collection of Personal Information
You can visit the Ultimate Rollercoaster® web site without revealing any personal, identifiable information. The only information collected during a normal visit is your IP address, computer operating system type and version, screen resolution, color depth, Internet browser type and version, browser size, referring URL (if any) and a record of the content you viewed, along with the date and time of those requests. During normal browsing we do not collect or store information such as your name, mailing address or email address.
This information gathered during this usage may be used to generate reports that measure site usage activity. This information is used to improve the site experience and monitor the activity on our servers. We may also share this information with advertisers and affiliates for information purposes.
There are sections on the site that will require you to submit personal, identifiable information to obtain access or use these features. The collection, handling and use of this information will be discussed below.
IP Addresses
During your visit to Ultimate Rollercoaster we will record your activity by collecting and storing your IP address along with the requests for content from our servers for the purposes of statistics reporting, system administration, and security.
An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you're on the Internet. Servers use the assigned IP address to identify your computer.
When you request content from our web servers your IP address is automatically logged. For general use, you may remain anonymous to us since our web logs do not contain personal, identifiable information connected and/or linked to your IP address.
There are exceptions to linking personal identifiable information to your IP address. When using features such as our discussion forums, user profiles, polls, news discussion or any features that require you to sign in or when contacting us by email, then your IP address may be logged along with any personal information that you voluntarily or are required to submit. This information may include, but is not limited to your name, home address and email address.
Use of Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information that a web server transfers to your hard drive for record keeping purposes. Cookies in most cases are used to enhance your online experience by storing your username, preferences, sessions and enable the use of certain features while using a web site.
We may also use cookies to help us track site activity, including page views, return visits and feature usage. Many of the improvements to the site are based on data that can be collected through the use of cookies.
Web browsers are generally set to accept cookies by default. If you'd prefer not to accept cookies, then you can change the preferences on your browser to not accept cookies or to alert you prior to accepting one.
Ultimate Rollercoaster uses cookies to store preferences, monitor site activity and store session information to enhance your online experience on this web site and to enable features such as our message boards, chat rooms, member registration, polls and member features. Some sections and site features will not work or function properly without the use of cookies.
Ultimate Rollercoaster does not use cookies to retrieve personal information from your computer that was not originally sent to you from our web servers. The information we store in the cookies we set is not shared with third parties.
Some of our cookies sent and stored on your local computer may contain personal identifiable information such as your name, username or ID and other information that possibly could be traced to you.
While browsing Ultimate Rollercoaster advertising networks may be setting third party cookies and reading them from your computer or using web beacons to collect information, in the course of ads being served on our website. Ultimate Rollercoaster, LLC uses third parties to serve advertising on this web site and has no control over the use of these cookies; therefore they are not subject to our Privacy Policy. The use of cookies by our advertisers is common practice and for more information on how these cookies are used, please read the privacy statements for our advertisers listed below.
Advertising
Ultimate Rollercoaster uses third parties and ad networks for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing-messages on the site. The third parties and ad networks we work with provide their own privacy policies that may or may not apply to this web site.
Some of the advertising networks used by Ultimate Rollercoaster may place a third party cookie on your computer. This cookie may be used to tailor ad content both on this site as well as other sites within that network that you may visit in the future.
You may opt-out of receiving these cookies from some of our advertising networks. For more information and to opt-out of behavioral advertising, see Network Advertising Initiative.
Ultimate Rollercoaster, LLC works with the following ad networks. To learn how these third party ad networks handle your personal information, please see the Privacy Policy for each of the following:

Tribal Fusion - To find out more about how Tribal Fusion handles your personal information please consult the Tribal Fusion Privacy Statement.

Collection of Personal Identifiable Information
There are instances or situations on this web site that may require you to submit personal information to our web servers. This information such as your name, mailing address, or email address is received, collected and stored on our web servers. Ultimate Rollercoaster is concerned about your privacy and therefore we take measures to ensure that the information we collect is secure from outside sources.
In some cases the personal information you submit, may be accessible to other visitors of this web site. If it is your desire to remain completely anonymous, then you should not participate or use features of the site that require you to submit personal information.
Please be aware that whenever you give out personal information online (For example: via chat rooms, discussion forums, user profiles) information can be collected and used by people and/or third parties you don't know. While it is our goal to protect our users' privacy, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you disclose for public display and therefore you do so at your own risk.
Information Sharing
Ultimate Rollercoaster follows strict policies to protect your personal information. In general, it is our policy not to share personal information with outside sources and third parties, unless our intention to do so is stated up front.
Ultimate Rollercoaster's policy is to not sell, trade, rent or otherwise reveal your personal information to third parties, unless consent is given or the intended use is stated at the time of collection, except for the following situations:
To protect the rights, property, and safety of Ultimate Rollercoaster, Ultimate Rollercoaster, LLC, our owner(s), users, or others;
To enforce or apply our Terms of Service agreement;
To protect the security and integrity of our web site;
To take precautions against liability;
To respond to judicial process; or
To the extent permitted under other provisions of law, to provide information to law enforcement or for an investigation on matter related to public safety.
In all of these situations, we reserve the right to release any and all of your personal information if we believe that such release is reasonably necessary.
All users should be aware that personal, identifiable information may be gathered by third parties without our knowledge in our chat rooms, discussion forums, user profiles and anywhere else where this information is displayed for public consumption. The information in these areas of the site is on public display, therefore we cannot protect or prevent the collection of it.
Ultimate Rollercoaster may use information gathered from this web site to generate demographic data, which may be shared with third parties, including our advertising partners. The data shared does not contain personal, identifiable information unless consent was given at the time of collection for such a use.
Should exception to our information sharing policy exist, full disclosure will be made at the time of collection.
Email
In some situations we may collect and/or store your email address. Ultimate Rollercoaster is concerned about unsolicited email, otherwise known as Spam. Therefore, it is our policy not to sell or share any email address with third parties, unless consent is given or the intended use is clearly stated at the time of collection. However we do reserve the right to use your email address for internal use.
On occasion you may receive email from Ultimate Rollercoaster for informative or promotional purposes. If at any time you wish to be removed from our e-mail lists, please Contact Us to send your remove request. Please include the email address to be removed and if applicable a copy of the message you received. We promise to do our best to honor all remove requests.

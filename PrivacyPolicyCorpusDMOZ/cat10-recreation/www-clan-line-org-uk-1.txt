The Merchant Navy Locomotive Preservation Society Ltd (MNLPS Ltd) considers the privacy of our members and visitors to be of extreme importance.
We collect your personal information from you when you complete a membership application or renewal.
We use this information for the purpose of managing your membership.
We may, from time to time, send you newsletters and other information.
We also collect your personal information when you use our online store.
This information is solely for the purpose of dealing with your orders, and will not be used for any other purpose.
When you complete our Gift Aid or Contact pages, we will collect your information solely for the purpose stated.
If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy,
please feel free to contact us by email at MNLPS Secretary .
Children's Information
We believe it is important to provide added protection for children online. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to observe,
participate in and/or monitor and guide their online activity.
If a parent or guardian believes that we have in our database any inappropriate personally-identifiable information relating to a child,
please contact us immediately (using the contact above) and we will use our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, clan-line.org.uk makes use of log files.
These files merely log visitors to the site - usually a standard procedure for hosting companies and a part of hosting services's analytics.
The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP),
date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.
This information is used to analyse trends, administer the site, track the user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information.
IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Cookies
clan-line.org.uk uses cookies to store information about the visitor's preferences,
to record user-specific information on which pages the site visitor accesses or visits,
and to personalise or customise our web page content based upon the visitor's browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options.
More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

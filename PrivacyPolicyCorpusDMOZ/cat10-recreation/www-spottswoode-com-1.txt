Privacy Statement
Effective: February 15, 2020
Thank you for your interest in Spottswoode Winery Inc. d/b/a Spottswoode Estate Vineyard & Winery (“Spottswoode,” “us,” or “we”). We value your privacy and have developed this Privacy Statement (or “Statement”) to help explain our privacy practices as they relate to the information we collect from and about you while you interact with us, including by visiting our website www.spottswoode.com, social media pages, or other digital properties (collectively, our “Website”) and in connection with our promotional and other events.
Please read our Statement carefully and should you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us at estate@spottswoode.com or at the address listed below.
Please note that we may update this Statement from time to time by posting a revised version on www.spottswoode.com. The latest version of our Statement will always include its effective date at the beginning. We encourage you to stay informed of our privacy practices by frequently visiting this area of our Website after your first visit.
Access Restricted to People of Legal Drinking Age
Our website and access to all advertising and marketing information are intended only for persons of legal drinking age based on the jurisdiction where such content is being accessed or viewed. You may be required to verify your date of birth prior to entering or interacting with our website. Age verification prompts may also be located within the website.
We do not knowingly collect information from those under the legal drinking age and request that such individuals do not provide any information to us or access our website. If you are under the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction and have entered our site by providing a false date of birth, you must immediately leave the site. If you believe someone under the legal drinking age has provided us with information by obtaining access in violation of the applicable sites’ access restrictions, please contact us immediately and this information will be removed.
Information We Collect
We collect information that you provide to us directly, including:
Contact information (such as your first and last name, company name, email address, physical address, and telephone number),Date of birth (for age verification purposes),Product purchases, andPayment card information (such as your credit or debit card information).
We collect this information from you when you:
Register for our mailing list,Attend our promotional and other events, andPurchase products on our Website.
Cookies and other Website Tracking Technologies
Our Website also uses cookies, web beacons, pixels, and other tracking technologies to collect information such as your Internet Service Provider and IP address, the date and time that you access our Website, and the pages you view while visiting our Website. Some cookies exist only while you are visiting our Website, while others may persist over time across multiple sessions. We use these technologies to remember your preferences, maximize the performance of our Website, products and services, provide you with offers that may be of interest to you, measure the effectiveness of our email campaigns and to personalize online content.
To provide you with a more relevant and interesting experience, Spottswoode may work with third party companies to display ads or customize the content of our Website. These companies may use cookies (a small text file), pixels, tags, or similar technologies that are placed in browsers to gather information about your visits to our Website, as well as your visits elsewhere on the Internet. These companies use this information to provide you with more relevant advertising known as interest-based advertising. Some of these companies may be participants in the Digital Advertising Alliance (“DAA”). The DAA offers a choice mechanism with respect to certain types of data collection and use. We adhere to the DAA’s Self-Regulatory Principles for Online Behavioral Advertising. Visit www.aboutads.info to exercise choice with respect to participants of the DAA or click on the AboutAds icon on a Spottswoode advertisement and follow the instructions on how to opt out. If you change computers, devices, or browsers, use multiple computers, devices, or browsers, or delete your cookies, you may need to repeat this process for each computer, device, or browser. Opting out of interest-based advertising will not opt you out of all advertising, but rather only interest-based advertising from Spottswoode or its agents or representatives.
Some browsers have incorporated Do Not Track (“DNT”) preferences. Most of these features, when turned on, send signals to the website you are visiting that you do not wish to have information about your online searching and browsing activities collected and used. As there is not yet a common agreement about how to interpret DNT signals, we do not honor DNT signals from website browsers at this time. However, you may refuse or delete cookies. If you refuse or delete cookies, some of our website functionality may be impaired. Please refer to your browser’s Help instructions to learn more about how to manage cookies and the use of other tracking technologies.
How We Use Your Information
We use your information for a number of purposes, including:
To keep you informed about our wine and any promotional or other events we host,To personalize our marketing to you,To complete your orders of our products, process your payments, ship our products to you, and respond to customer service inquiries, andor our own internal business purposes.
We may also use and share your information in an anonymous and aggregated format for any purpose.
With Whom We Share Your Information
We may share your information with any of our affiliates with which we share common ownership for the purposes described in this Statement. We may also share your information with service providers working on our behalf, such as companies that provide advertising, marketing analytics, printing, shipping, and payment processing services, or otherwise assist us in making our products and services available to you. We may also share your information with companies that coordinate promotional events on our behalf to showcase our winery and products.
We may also share your information with third-parties, law enforcement or other governmental agencies as required by law to respond to a subpoena or other similar legal process, when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, to enforce our Terms of Use and our other legal rights, to protect your safety or the safety of others, or to investigate and respond to any government or law enforcement request.
In the event that some or all of our assets are acquired by another company in a business transaction with us (such as in the case of a merger, acquisition or sale, including situations of insolvency, bankruptcy or receivership), we may also share your information with the acquiring company.
We do not otherwise sell or rent your information to any third-parties for their own use, including to market their own products or services.
Security
We take reasonable steps to secure your information and protect it from unauthorized access, use, alteration, disclosure and destruction.
Your Choices
If you would like to change the information we have about you, please contact us at estate@spottswoode.com or by phone at the number listed below; we do not accept such requests by mail. If you would like to unsubscribe from our mailing list, please click on the “unsubscribe” button at the bottom of each email, or contact us at estate@spottswoode.com.
Links to Third-Party Websites
Our Website may include links to other third-party websites (including social media) that we do not operate or maintain and likewise, may follow privacy practices that differ from ours. This Statement applies only to Spottswoode’s processing activities and does not apply to any processing activities associated with any other websites. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy statement of any website you visit.

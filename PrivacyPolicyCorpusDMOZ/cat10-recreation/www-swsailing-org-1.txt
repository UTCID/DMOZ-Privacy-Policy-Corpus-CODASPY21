Cookies
To enrich and perfect your online experience, Shaping Travel Industry uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Shaping Travel Industry uses cookies to help Shaping Travel Industry identify and track visitors, their usage of http://www.swsailing.org, and their website access preferences. Shaping Travel Industry visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Shaping Travel Industry’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Shaping Travel Industry’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Shaping Travel Industry’s use of cookies.

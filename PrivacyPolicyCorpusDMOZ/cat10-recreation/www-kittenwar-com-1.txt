Kittenwar's Privacy Policy: so simple a kitten could understand it.
We ask for your email address when you add upload an image. Here's what we use it for:
To email you to ask if we can include the image in any future Kittenwar products.
This is so we can a) confirm your agreement, and b) make sure we have your details correctly. You will be credited if we use your image in the Kittenwar book!
To let you know about cool new stuff from Kittenwar.
But not very often.
Here's what we don't do:
Publish your email address on our site.
Share this information with anyone else, ever.
And that's it.

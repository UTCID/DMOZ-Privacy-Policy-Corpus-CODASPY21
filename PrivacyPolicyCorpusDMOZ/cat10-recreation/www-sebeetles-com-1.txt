Your computer already stores these cookies as they help you to browse other websites as well as ours and we do make you aware as soon as you arrive on the site that we use them. If you don’t want them you can choose to disable or cut/delete them at any time.
We suggest consulting the Help section of your browser or taking a look at the About Cookies website which offers guidance.
So feel snug and secure in all our pages. You haven’t been covered like this since your mother tucked you in at night.

PRIVACY POLICY:
At AlaskaBySea.com, your privacy is very important to us. Your personal information, including but not limited to, credit card information, address, phone numbers, and e-mail address, will never be shared with or sold to any outside organization or person. The only exception to this is sharing of information with cruise lines and other suppliers of travel products, including travel insurance companies, and then only to the extent required by those suppliers to facilitate our clients' reservations. Our promise for the strictest confidentiality of our clients' personal information is one that we take very seriously. If you have any concerns or questions about our Privacy Policy, we invite you to contact us.

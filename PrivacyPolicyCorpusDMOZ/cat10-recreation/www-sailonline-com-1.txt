Sailonline.com is solely dedicated to serving the needs of its visitors. We are totally committed to extensive protection of your privacy and security when you access Sailonline's web site.
You have the right to know everything about the information we collect about you as you use our web site, how you benefit from this information, and how you can prevent the use of any of this information.
Sailonline.com has put in place this privacy policy in order to show our commitment to protecting your privacy and security. For each visitor to our web pages, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address.
What Information is CollectedWe collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, and information shared by the visitors, such as polls and survey information and/or site registrations. We never, ever, "harvest" email addresses from other web sites.
How We Use the InformationIt is used to improve the content of our web pages, to facilitate the visitors' interaction with the site, and to contact consumers for marketing purposes, such as our newsletters.
If you supply us with your e-mail address, you may receive periodic e-mails from us. If you do not want to receive e-mails from us in the future, please let us know by sending us an email. Or simply unsubscribe when you receive one of our newsletters,
Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us with information regarding charter bookings and/or orders they have placed on-line.

Privacy Policy
At Tavern Trove your privacy is important to us. We value the honesty and friendliness of the collecting community. Therefore all transactions and communications are held in strict confidence. All personal and financial information entered into this site will be encrypted and sent across secure lines to our secure server. All emails will be confidential. Tavern Trove has the highest level of security for on line transactions available on the Internet.
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Tavern Trove.   Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads based on their visit to Tavern Trove and other sites on the Internet.
If you have any questions about this policy please contact us or check our frequently asked questions page. Please view our purchasing policy for information regarding returns and refunds.

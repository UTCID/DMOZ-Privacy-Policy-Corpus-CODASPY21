Privacy Policy
We at Muktuk Adventures value your trust and make it a high priority to ensure the security and confidentially of your personal information you provide to us.
What information do we collect?
We collect your name, e-mail address and phone number when you filling out our contact form, or sign up for our newsletter. We collect credit card information when you place a reservation, or buy a product. If you are booking one of our multi day activities we will collect personal information such as gender, experience level, height, weight, medical history, insurance information etc.
How do we use this information?
To process payments that you authorized for a tour you booked with us.
Your information helps us to understand your personal needs and to respond to your personal needs or customizes your adventures with us.
We never share information provided to us in any way unrelated to the tour, service or product you request with us.
How do we protect your information?
Muktuk Adventures has taken extensive measures to ensure confidentially of your information and prevent unauthorized access. We also have organizational processes in pace to limit access to your personal information to a selected group of individuals.
Additional information
Muktuk Adventures is using Fareharbor as a booking platform. Please also refer to their privacy policies at https://fareharbor.com/legal/privacy/

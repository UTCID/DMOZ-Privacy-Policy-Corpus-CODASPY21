Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology
that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy
applies to the Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers Web site and governs data collection and usage.
By using the Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers website, you consent to the data practices described
in this statement.
Collection of your Personal Information
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail
address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers also
collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as
your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.
There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically
collected by Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers. This information can include: your IP address,
browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This
information is used by Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers for the operation of the service, to
maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use
of the Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers Web site.
Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information
or personally sensitive data through Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers public message boards,
this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers
does not read any of your private online communications.
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites
you choose to link to from Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers so that you can understand how those
Web sites collect, use and share your information. Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers is not responsible
for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers
and Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers family of Web sites.
Use of your Personal Information
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers collects and uses your personal information to operate the Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers
Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers also uses
your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services
available from Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers and its affiliates. Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers may also
contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services
or of potential new services that may be offered.
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business
partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases,
your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone
number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers
may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send
you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All
such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to
provide these services to Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers, and they are required to maintain
the confidentiality of your information.
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such
as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers, in order to determine what Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers services are
the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising
within Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested
in a particular subject area.
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice,
only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is
necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process
served on Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or
property of Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect
the personal safety of users of Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers, or the public.
Use of Cookies
The Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online
experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page
server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.
Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the
domain that issued the cookie to you.
One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save
you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned
to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers pages, or
register with Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers site or services, a cookie helps Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers
to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process
of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses,
and so on. When you return to the same Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers Web site, the information
you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers
features that you customized.
You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically
accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies
if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience
the interactive features of the Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers services or Web sites you visit.
Security of your Personal Information
Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers secures your personal information from unauthorized access,
use or disclosure. Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers secures the personally identifiable information
you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from
unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit
card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use
of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

company and customer feedback. Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers encourages you to periodically
review this Statement to be informed of how Atlanta Scuba and Swim Centers is protecting your
information.

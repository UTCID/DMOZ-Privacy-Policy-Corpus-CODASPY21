Privacy Policy
At Butler Winery, our customer’s privacy is very important to us. We value all of our customers and strive to provide a quality product with outstanding customer service. In the course of selling wine we do collect some sensitive information from our customers such as contact information and credit card information. This may be collected in person, over the phone, or through our website.Any information provided to us by our customers will never be shared, rented, traded or sold. We only use your information to sell you wine.We do not collect any information from you when you are simply visiting or browsing our website.There are certain areas of our website that do ask for you to provide us with information.Email Newsletter (Constant Contact)To sign up for our monthly newsletter, we ask you to provide us with your name and email address. We will only send our email newsletter to subscribers. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time. Instructions to unsubscribe are included in a link in the bottom of the newsletter.Contact UsTo send us an email we ask for your name and email information.We only use this information to correspond with you regarding any questions or comments you may have. We will never share or sell your email address to anyone. We hate spam too!Links to Other WebsitesOn our website, we provide links to other businesses and services. These companies are independent from Butler Winery and have their own privacy and security policies. We encourage you to read those policies as well.If you have any questions or comments about our privacy policy, send us an email through our contact page, or call 812-332-6660.

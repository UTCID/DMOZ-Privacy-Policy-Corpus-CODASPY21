About FantasyCars
FantasyCarsTM started as one of the Internet's very first car websites, launching in 1998 and becoming one of the 10 most visited car websites by 2003.
Today, FantasyCars aims to become the premier automotive digital media brand, the de-facto source for reviews, photos, videos and technical data for the world's most desirable cars, that we like to call FantasyCarsTM.
We are currently building an extensive network of contributors worldwide. If your passion is cars, get in touch.

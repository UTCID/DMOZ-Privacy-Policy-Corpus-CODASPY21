Cookies
A number of cookies have been seen to have been set when using this
site but it is believed they only appear when using the site 'login'
capability and this is not available to end users. The cookies are set
by WordPress, the content management system used for most of this site, and are considered to be functional first party cookies so
it is believed that consent to use them would not have been required
anyway. Any analytical cookies that you might encounter will have been
created when 'implied consent' under the 'UK Cookie Law' was valid but
are now historical.
Cookie NameCookie Value
The Scouting Challenge theme can be configured to allow visitors
to choose their preferred sidebar position. If the user chooses to
change the position a first party cookie will be set to record this
setting. No cookie will be set unless the user actively chooses not to
use the default setting, and if the user returns to the default setting
the cookie will be destroyed. This feature is not currently in use on
this site.
This site is hosted by Krystal Hosting in the UK. They will keep access logs that
record your activity on the site and these logs are available in a
processed form to the website owner. This data can be considered as
personal data because it may be possible to link an IP address in use at
a particular time to a particular person.
Contact form details
Various contact forms are used to facilitate communication to various leaders or other personnel within the group.This information is used solely to generate these email messages and is not retained by any website systems. All people receiving these emails are aware of their responsibilities under GDPR. If the person receiving the email feels that an enquiry can be better handled by somebody else within the Group they may forward it to them.

Privacy Overview
This website uses cookies to improve your experience while you navigate through the website. Out of these cookies, the cookies that are categorized as necessary are stored on your browser as they are essential for the working of basic functionalities of the website. We also use third-party cookies that help us analyze and understand how you use this website. These cookies will be stored in your browser only with your consent. You also have the option to opt-out of these cookies. But opting out of some of these cookies may have an effect on your browsing experience.

Policy Manual
The LCCA operates under a constitutional form of governance. The constitution provides for the governance of the club based on elected officers and directors by the membership. The job descriptions of each of the elected positions within the club as well as two of its standing committees are documented here. Persons seeking to run for any of these positions or serve on one of the committees should avail themselves of this information prior to consenting to run or serve.

Privacy
This is the privacy policy for Camp Mishawaka. Protecting personal information and using it in a manner that is consistent with expectations that are associated with our camp philosophy is top priority at Camp Mishawaka.
Our homepage is located at www.campmishawaka.com. You can view our dates, rates, FAQs. Please contact us with any questions or concerns you may have.
Security
Our online data management is handled by our professional partners at CampMinder, LLC. Their privacy policy can be found at their website www.campminder.com
Neither Camp Mishawaka, our webmasters at Ronningen Design, nor CampMinder, LLC will re-use or redistribute any information that is gathered during your online experience at the Camp Mishawaka website. Camp Mishawaka will use security controls where available to ensure that family and camper information in our computers is protected.
As a member of the online Camp Mishawaka community, you will always have access to your information. You will be able to review your information and make necessary changes to ensure that all is complete and accurate.
Camp Mishawaka will use any collected data and information for office use and camp purposes only. Never will information or data be sold or redistributed to any third party. Your privacy is our priority. After all, you are family at Camp Mishawaka!
Cookies
Cookies are a technology which can be used to give special information on a website. Our website may contain areas in which cookies are needed. You can set your computer to notify you when a cookie should be enabled and choose to accept it if necessary.

Information we collect and how we use it:
We offer several services that do not require you to register for an account or provide any personal information to us. In order to provide all our services, we may collect the following types of information:
Information you provide:
When you sign up for an Account or other service that requires registration, we ask you for personal information (such as your name, email address and an account password). other services or third parties in order to provide you with a better experience and to improve the quality of our services. For certain services, we may give you the opportunity to opt out of combining such information.
InternetLastPage.com cookies:
When you visit InternetLastPage.com, we send one or more cookies (a small file containing a string of characters) to your computer that uniquely identifies your web browser. We use cookies to improve the quality of our service by storing user preferences and tracking user trends, such as how people search. Most web browsers are initially set up to accept cookies, but you can reset your browser to
refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
Web log information:
When you use InternetLastPage.com services, our servers automatically record information that your web browser sends whenever you visit a web site. These server logs may include information such as your web request, Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser language, browser plug-ins, the date and time of your request and one or more cookies that may uniquely identify your browser.
User communications:
When you send email or other communication to InternetLastPage.com, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services.

We offer some of our services in connection with other web sites. Personal information that you provide to those web sites may be sent to InternetLastPage.com

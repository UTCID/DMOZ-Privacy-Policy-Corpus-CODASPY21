Privacy Policy
At jadefashion.com we realize that customers are concerned about how the information they provide on-line will be used. We are committed to protecting your privacy and will use the information we collect about you to process your order and provide you with a more personalized shopping experience.
What Information do we collect and How Do We Use It?
When you would like to place an order, we ask for you to provide information that will complete and fill our on-line ordering form. The information collected is your name, e-mail address, mailing address, contact numbers and credit card information. All information regarding our on-line customers is stored on highly secured servers. We do not sell or share this information with anyone else.
Security
Jadefashion.com is set up with SSL. All information you enter in our site is transmitted safe and secured into our system.

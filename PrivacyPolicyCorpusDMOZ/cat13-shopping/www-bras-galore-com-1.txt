Bras Galore Returns Policy
We offer a refund or exchange service for items returned within 28 days of receipt of order.
So if it doesn't fit simply return it to us with the Returns Form below and we will exchange it for something else or give you a refund.
If you would like to exchange an item please email us at mail@bras-galore.com to reserve your exchange items.
If you would like to cancel any order before it is dispatched please email us  mail@bras-galore.com

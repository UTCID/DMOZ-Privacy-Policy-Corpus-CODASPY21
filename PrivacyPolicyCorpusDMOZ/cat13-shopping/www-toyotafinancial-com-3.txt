Data Privacy Request
If you are a resident of California, you have certain rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"). California residents who wish to exercise their rights may submit a request by completing the form below. Please note that in order to protect customers' personal information, you will be asked to provide two or three pieces of information to match our records and reasonably verify your identity.
Please note that submitting the request form below will not extend to Toyota dealers, private distributors, or Toyota Motor Sales (TMS). If you wish to exercise your CCPA rights with any of those entities, you will need to notify each individually. For further information regarding your rights under the CCPA, please refer to our Online Privacy Policy. If you are an authorized agent submitting a request on behalf of a California resident who is a current or former Toyota Motor Credit Corporation finance or lease customer, please call 1-866-422-7210 to speak with a customer service representative who will assist you with your submission.
If you are an authorized agent submitting a request on behalf of a California resident who is a current or former Toyota Motor Insurance Services vehicle protection product customer, please call 1-866-422-7220 to speak with a customer service representative who will assist you with your submission.

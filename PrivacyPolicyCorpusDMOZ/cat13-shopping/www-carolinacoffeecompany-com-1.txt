Privacy Policy
Information which may potentially be gathered from visitors
As with many websites, log files are stored on the web server which may save details such as the visitor's IP address, browser type, referring page and time of visit. In addition to this, cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the website.
Usage of this Information
The information gathered is used to enhance the visitor's experience when using the website, allowing personalized content and more relevant advertisements to be displayed.
E-mail addresses and other personal information potentially gathered during use of this website will never be sold, rented or leased to 3rd parties.
We may occasionally distribute e-mails and newsletters to inform you of services, offers, and news regarding Carolina Coffee Company and its affiliates. Signing up for our newsletter means you have given your express consent to receive and view these e-mails. You may unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions included in the newsletter.
Visitor Options
You may be able to block cookies via your browser settings but this may prevent you from access to certain features of the website. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time.
Google Ads
Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to sites they visit on the Internet.
Website visitors may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
Cookies
Cookies are small digital signature files, stored by your web browser, which allow your preferences to be documented when visiting the website. They may also be used to monitor your return visits to the website. No personal information will ever be knowingly gathered or used.

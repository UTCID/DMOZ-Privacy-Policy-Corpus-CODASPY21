Privacy Notice
Soap Opera World is committed to respecting your online privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information you share with us.
Soap Opera World has established this Online Privacy Policy so that you can understand the care with which we intend to treat your Personal Information.
Personal Information means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address and an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home. In general, you can visit Soap Opera World's web pages without telling us who you are or revealing any Personal Information about yourself.
Personal Information is only collected when customers Create An Account or attempt to make a purchase.
None of this information is shared with a third party for any reason.
All information exchanged between you and Soap Opera World is secured by 128-bit SSL Encryption Encoding to protect sensitive data during transfer.
We do not sell, rent, or otherwise allow anyone to have access to any information you provide us. All information gathered will be confidential and used strictly for the purpose of conducting business with you.
Soap Opera World does not request, gather or collect financial information of any kind.
The only required information that is collected from you at the time of purchase is your name, shipping address and your email address for notification purposes.
All online payments are handled by our payment processor, PayPal.
When you place an order and choose PayPal as your payment method, you are securely redirected to their website where you conduct your transaction and once finished...you are securely redirected back to our website and Checkout is then complete.
PayPal never reveals or shares any of your financial information with Soap Opera World.
All transactions conducted via PayPal are secure and private.
If you have any questions, please email us at sales@soapoperaworld.com

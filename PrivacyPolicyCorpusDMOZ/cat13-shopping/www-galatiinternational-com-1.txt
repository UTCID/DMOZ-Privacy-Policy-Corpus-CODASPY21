Privacy Policy
PRIVACY:
Galati International is committed to protecting your privacy. Any information collected through this site is intended to be used for this transaction. We do have to collect and retain some information from you in order to accurately fulfill an order, send out a catalog or respond to a question. Your personal information is secured and encrypted via SSL (Secure Socket Layer) Technology. Information needed to process your order is:
Your Name
Billing address
Shipping address
Email address
Phone number
Credit card information
We do use third parties to provide credit card processing (charging your card) and shipping fulfillment (Fedex, USPS, UPS). Some of your information must be passed along to these companies in order to finalize your order. We also use analytical software to track our website usage. This helps make our customer experience better on our website, and to help us keep track of advertising dollars. Information from these analytical services may include collecting IP addresses, time and day, time spent on a page, browsers used, etc.
We do not sell your information. If you do not want to receive our email newsletter or catalog, you may unsubscribe at any time.
EMAIL SPECIALS:
Our email subscribers are periodically sent a Galati E-Special Newsletter. These emails include special discounts on products, new products, closeout savings, shipping specials and more.
Subscribers may opt-out of these E-Specials at any time. We never sell or rent your email information.
To ensure that you receive our E-Specials, please add support@galatiinternational.com to your address book. Many internet providers use a spam filter which does not allow emails to go through unless the email address is in your address book.
OPT OUT:
If you wish to no longer receive Galati monthly E-Specials, please contact us with your name and email address and let us know you wish to unsubscribe.
Or you can unsubscribe by clicking on the Unsubscribe link at the bottom of any E-Special email we send you.

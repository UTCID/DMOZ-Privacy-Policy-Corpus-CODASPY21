Netuniform Customer Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy and take great care to safeguard information in our possession. The This & That family of companies (Netuniform, NJT Uniforms, and This & That Uniforms) takes seriously the protection and proper use of your personal information.
Netuniform does not share customer information (including e-mail addresses) outside the This & That family of companies unless it is necessary to provide you with products or services available from Netuniform. We may also disclose information when you tell us to do so, to identify or contact you, to protect your rights or the rights of Netuniform, or as required or permitted by law.
Even within the This & That family of companies, we restrict access to your information to those individuals who need it to provide you with products or services available from Netuniform. Any third-party companies we use to provide you with products or services are required to keep your information secure and confidential.
If you have any other questions or concerns regarding how Netuniform manages, accesses or uses your personal information, please email us at customerservice@netuniform.com or call 919-360-4224.

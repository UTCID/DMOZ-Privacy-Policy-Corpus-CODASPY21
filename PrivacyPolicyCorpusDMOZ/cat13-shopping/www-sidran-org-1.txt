Privacy Policy
Information we collect from you automatically
We may directly collect analytics data, or use third-party analytics tools, to help us measure traffic and usage trends for Sidran Institute. These tools collect information sent by your browser or mobile device, including pages you visit or other information that assists us in improving our service to you. We collect and use this analytics information in aggregate form such that it cannot reasonably be manipulated to identify any particular individual user.
Cookies information
When you visit our website, we may send one or more cookies—a small text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters—to your computer that uniquely identifies your browser and lets Sidran Institute help you view the website faster and enhance your navigation throughout the site. A cookie may also convey information to use about how you use the site (e.g. the pages you view, the links you click and other actions you take on the site), and allows us or our partners to track your usage of the site over time. As a browser user, you can voluntarily clear your cache or cookies at any time.
A connection via an API (application program interface) is made to Facebook for the purpose of facilitating sharing to Facebook in a consistent visual fashion.

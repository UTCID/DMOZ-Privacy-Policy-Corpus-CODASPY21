Privacy notice
Best Soccer Buys Sporting Goods, Inc is firmly committed to protecting our customers privacy. We only collect information which is required to conduct the business on the internet. We do not share your information with any third party. The payment information we receive from you such as credit card is deleted periodically from our database as we feel that there is no need for saving this information for long duration of time. All the data is stored in a secure location and there is no possibility of anyone else using your private information for fraudulent use.
All information you provide is protected by industry-standard SSL technology and stored on secure servers. We may occasionally use the data you provide to inform you of any special offers.
We uses cookies to help serve you better by recording certain information (like what items are in your shopping cart or whether you came from a referring site that entitles you to a specific promotion). A cookie is a small text file that assigns a unique identifier to your computer. It is created by our server and stored on your hard drive.
We feel it is important to inform you about why we collect the information and how we use it. We will be happy answer any questions in regards to our security or privacy policy.

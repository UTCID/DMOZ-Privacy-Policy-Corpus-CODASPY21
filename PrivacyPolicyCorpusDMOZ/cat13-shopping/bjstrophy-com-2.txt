Privacy Policy
Bjstrophy.com, Inc. (hereafter referred to as Bjstrophy.com) has created this privacy policy to demonstrate our commitment to the privacy of the users of our websites. However, Bjstrophy.com needs certain personal information if you wish to purchase our products, register for an affiliate account, receive Bjstrophy.com newsletter, or use certain Bjstrophy.com services.Where required, this information may include your personal contact information and/or your company contact information. Bjstrophy.com will use this information to reply to your inquiries, to provide you with requested products and services, to set up your member’s account, and to contact you regarding new products and services.By accessing the services of Bjstrophy.com and voluntarily providing us with the requested personal information, you consent to the collection and use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.Collection and Use of Non-Personal InformationBjstrophy.com automatically receives and records non-personal information on our server logs from your browser including your IP address, cookie information and the page you requested. Bjstrophy.com may use this information to customize the advertising and content you see and to fulfill your requests for certain products and services. However, Bjstrophy.com does not connect this non-personal data to any personal information collected from you.Bjstrophy.com also allows third party companies that are presenting advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Again, these cookies are not connected to any personal information. Third party cookie usage is subject to their own privacy policies, and Bjstrophy.com assumes no responsibility or liability for this usage.Information Sharing and DisclosureBjstrophy.com may disclose your personal information to third parties who work on behalf of Bjstrophy.com to provide products and services requested by you.

On this Site, cookies are used in conjunction with session variables to track a User's shopping cart and expire as soon as an order is completed or if a shopping cart sits idle for a lengthy period without being checked-out.
Inyo Pool Products Inc. does not use Cookies to retrieve Information about Users from a User's computer unless the User knowingly and willingly provided such Information.

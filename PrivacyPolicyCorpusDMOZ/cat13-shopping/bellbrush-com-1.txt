Privacy Policy
We collect the details provided by you on registration together with
information we learn about you from visits to our web site. We also collect
information about the transactions you undertake including details of payment
cards used. All card information is transmitted to our server by a secure
connection and all information is encrypted. Full credit card information is not
stored on our server to further increase security. All credit card information
is removed from our server after processing.
We may collect additional information in connection with your participation
in any promotions or competitions offered by us and information you provide when
giving us feedback or completing profile forms. We also monitor customer traffic
patterns and site use which enables us to improve the service we provide.
We will never pass your personal data to anyone else, except for any
successors in title to our business and suppliers that process data on our
behalf.

Privacy Statement
Compassion Books, Inc. is committed to being respectful of any personal information we are given. Your address, phone number, email address, and credit card information are all kept strictly confidential. We do not keep credit card information on file once we have completed your order. Your address will be kept by us for future catalog mailings, but we do not share our mailing list with anyone, no exceptions.
Please be assured that we are a secure website and your transaction information is encrypted and protected during transmission using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), provided by GeoTrust, a major provider of secure and authenticated electronic transactions.
Your visit to the Compassion Books website constitutes an understanding that Compassion books, Inc. will not be held responsible for security conflicts incurred as a result of credit card transactions. However, we have the utmost confidence that your use of our website is safe and secure, and we will do our best to resolve any possible concerns.
Thank you for using Compassion Books.

We are a secure web site
We accept payments through the SecurePay payment system which is an independant third party which is a company of Australia Post who offer total security with credit card processing. All Credit Card numbers are encrypted through a SSL (secure socket layer) for security purposes.
Cosmic Maternity does not store any credit card numbers.
Email addresses and contact details
We do not retain any email addresses or contact details for any purpose other than for contacting you about your order, or a special promotional offer. You can be assured your details will never be sold or passed on to any third party.

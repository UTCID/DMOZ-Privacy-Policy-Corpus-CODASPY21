Privacy Policy
Brown-Spath & Associates is committed to respecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information about you unless you provide that information voluntarily. Any personal information you choose to provide us will only be used by Brown-Spath & Associates to conduct official business. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade or lease personal information collected on this site. If you send us an e-mail, we will collect your e-mail address and the contents of your message. We will use your e-mail address and the information included in your message to respond to you, to address the issues you identify, and to improve this web site. If you sign-up to receive Hot Topics email alerts we will periodically notify you about new online resources and updates about our products. Each Hot Topics alert email includes an unsubscribe option.
If you complete a transaction such as an information request form or purchase a product, we will collect the information, including personal information, that you volunteered in completing the transaction. We will use this information only for the purposes for which the transaction was intended.
For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact Robert@brownspath.com.

This web site is operated by S. King Company, Inc..
Our postal address is 6914 53rd Street, #44, Kenosha, WI 53144
We can be reached via e-mail at our contact page (opens in new window) Or you can reach us by telephone at 888-392-8833
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, but not the e-mail address.
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail and aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit.
The information we collect is used by us to contact consumers for marketing purposes and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by sending email to us using our contact form linked to above and tell us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our company.
We have not made e-mail addresses available to other organizations and do not anticipate changing this policy. If we were to change our policy and you do not want us to share your e-mail address with other companies or organizations, please let us know by sending email to us using our contact form linked to above and tell us that you do not want us to share your e-mail address with other companies.
If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by sending email to us using our contact form linked to above. At this time we do not share our customers addresses with outside organizations. You can, however, have your name put on our do-not-share list by sending email to us using our contact form linked to above in case our policy changes in the future.
Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed on-line. We do not share phone numbers with any outside organizations.

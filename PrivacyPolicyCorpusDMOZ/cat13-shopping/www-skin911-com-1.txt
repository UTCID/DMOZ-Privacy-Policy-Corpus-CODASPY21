Privacy Policy
Information Collection and Use
We are the sole owners of the information collected on this website. We only have access to the information you voluntarily give us via the order form. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside or third parties.  To buy from us we request your name, billing and shipping address, credit card number with expiration date and email to notify you with your shipping information. This information is only used to process your order. If we have questions processing your order we use this information to contact you.
Security
We protect your information. When you submit your information via the website it is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. You can verify this by looking for the closed lock icon at the bottom of your web browser, or looking for "https" at the beginning of the URL address of the web page or when you cut and paste the address into a document. We take your security personally!

What This Privacy Policy Covers:
This policy covers how artpaw.com treats personal information that we collect and receive from our visitors. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.
General:
Artpaw.com does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or nonaffiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your direct permission.

Privacy
This Privacy Statement describes and discloses the information gathering practices for the American Country web site.
If you are a visitor to our Web site, you should be aware that visitor Internet provider addresses are monitored to help us in administering the Web site. We use this information to diagnose service problems and to track visitor patterns. This information is used for our internal review to help us improve the overall quality of our Web site and is not shared with any other organization for commercial purposes.
The forms on our site permit customers to request certain information and purchase some of our products. If you are a customer of American Country, your Internet provider address is used to help us identify and track you and your shopping cart. We collect customer contact information, such as e-mail and street addresses, to help us process information requests and purchase orders. If you make a purchase, we may collect account or credit card numbers. We use this information for billing purposes. We respect the privacy of our customers and, therefore, we will not sell or otherwise intentionally release any of our customers' personal information to any third party.
This Web site contains links to other Web sites and American Country is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
This Web site gives visitors the option of modifying information previously provided by e-mailing American Country at the following e-mail address: info@americancountyhomestore.com.
If you do not want to receive mail or e-mail from us in the future, please advise our marketing department. If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site or your use of the Web site, you may contact us as follows:
Our postal address is: 327 Main Street, Ames, Iowa 50010
Our e-mail address is: info@americancountyhomestore.com
Our telephone number is: (515) 232-7813
Our fax number is: (515) 232-9241
We appreciate your questions and comments. Thank you for visiting our Web site.

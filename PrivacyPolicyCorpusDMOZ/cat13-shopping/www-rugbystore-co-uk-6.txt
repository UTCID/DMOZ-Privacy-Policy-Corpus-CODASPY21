Updated 23 January 2020.Tax ChargesWe charge VAT at 20% on all orders where the charge is applicable, as specified by HM Revenue & Customs.
Whether we charge VAT or not is based on a combination of the billing address country and the delivery address country, and is calculated as follows:Where the billing address is within the UK or the European Union and the shipping address is within the UK or the European Union: VAT is charged at the 20% rate on both the goods and the shipping charges.Where the billing address is within the UK or European Union and the shipping address is outside the UK or European Union: the order is exempt from VAT on both the goods and the shipping charges.Where the billing address is outside the European Union and the shipping address is within the UK or European Union: VAT is charged at the 20% rate on both the goods and the shipping charges.Where both the billing address and the delivery address are outside the European Union: the order is exempt from VAT on both the goods and the shipping charges.Customers in the Channel Islands are exempt from VAT on both goods and shipping charges if both the billing address and the delivery address are within the Channel Islands.All other orders are VAT free and we will deduct the VAT for you before we send out your order. Some overseas BFPO addresses are entitled to VAT free shopping. If you qualify for this, you must supply (by fax) a signed letter from your company C.O. before placing your order online. Regrettably we cannot refund VAT on any BFPO order after it has been completed and dispatched.Any customs or import duties charged by the destination country must be paid for by the customer. We have no control over these charges, and rugbystore.co.uk cannot be held liable. Customs policies vary depending on the Country.

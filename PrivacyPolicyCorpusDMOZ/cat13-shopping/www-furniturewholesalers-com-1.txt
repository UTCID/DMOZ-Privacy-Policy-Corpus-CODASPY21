Privacy Policy
On our order form we gather delivery information (i.e. addresses and phone numbers) so that we can process your order and insure that you receive the items for which you paid. We only share this information with the manufacturers and freight companies so that they can call you when appropriate to insure you receive your products.
We do not share, sell, or in any way, disseminate this information with any third party.
We only use your email address to correspond with you in matters related to your purchase or inquiries.
We do not share these email addresses with anyone.
If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us so that we may answer any questions you may have.

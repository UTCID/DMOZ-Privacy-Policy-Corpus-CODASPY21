Returns Policy
Returns are only accepted for items in "as-shipped" condition (brand new) within 30 calendar days of the ship date for full refund (less shipping and gift wrap)
There are no returns on frames, complete bikes, or any food/energy products
Returns received after 30 days of the ship date are subject to a 15% restocking fee
Returns are NOT accepted after 60 days
We recommend using a traceable carrier, such as FedEx, UPS, or Priority Mail w/ Delivery Confirmation, as we cannot be held liable for lost returns.
If you believe we shipped you an incorrect item, we will provide a return label for you, with these steps
Send an email to support@bikesomewhere.com.
Include your name,  order number, and which product is incorrect
Include a picture of the product, specifically the hang tag, bar code, part number, so we can verify what was shipped
Once verified, we will email you a return label from either UPS, FedEx or USPS to return the product
We will also ship out the correct product
If you believe you have a defective product
Depending on the brand, there are different procedures.  We will help you the best we can, providing instructions as we go.
Send an email to support@bikesomewhere.com
Include your name,  order number, and phone number (we might need to call to discuss the product)
Include a picture of the product, specifically the defective part
Include a description of what is defective
Depending on the manufacturer and age of the product, we will notify you how to proceed.
Refunds
We issue refunds within 72 hours of an order being returned
Refunds are issued back to the original payment method.  There are NO exceptions
If you have a credit card that was lost or stolen, refunds will still go back to that same credit card, and the card issuer will transfer the money to your new account
If you need to return an item, please Contact Us with your order number and details about the product you would like to return. We will respond quickly with instructions on how to return items from your order.

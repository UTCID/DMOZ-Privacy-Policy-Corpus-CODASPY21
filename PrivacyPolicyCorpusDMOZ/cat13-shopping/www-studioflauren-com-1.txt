Privacy Policy
1 Hour Tees Inc.  values its customers and respects their privacy. We collect customer information in an effort to improve your shopping experience and to communicate with you about our products, services, contests, and promotions. When you make an online purchase from our website , we use your information to process your order. We will also send you emails to confirm your order, and we may need to contact you via phone, postal mail or email if we have other questions regarding your order. In addition, you will be automatically signed up to receive email updates about our products, services, and promotions.
If at any time you would like us to remove you from our email or postal mailing lists, feel free to contact  1 Hour Tees Inc. customer service by calling 315-505-6261.
Security Commitment: At 1 Hour Tees Inc., we feel you should never have to worry about security when making a purchase online. AmoreTees.com uses SSL (Secure Socket Layer) to encrypt your order and protect personal data sent to us. SSL insures that your private information cannot be seen as it travels over the internet.

OUR PRIVACY POLICY
Your Information is
Secure
We are committed to protecting
your privacy.
We use the information we collect about you to process
your order and to provide a more personalized shopping
experience. By using our Web site, you consent to
the collection and use of this information.
We do
not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.
We don't appreciate it when others do it to us,
so we don't do it to you. If we decide to change
our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on
this page so that you are always aware of what information
we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances
we disclose it. Please read below for more details
about our Privacy Policy.
What Information
do we Collect?
In order to place an order, we need to know your name, email address,
mailing address, credit card number, and expiration date. At no time
will this information be disclosed to the public. Orders are transmitted
over secure servers for your protection.
How we use your
Information
Any information that is collected, allows us to process and fulfill
your order and to notify you of your order status. All information
is encrypted through secure technology and at no time will any
information be disclosed.
How we Protect
your Information
When you place orders or access your account information, we offer
the use of a secure server. The secure server software encrypts all
information you input before it is sent to us. Furthermore, all of
the customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized
access.
What about
"Cookies"?
"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored by your
browser on your computer's hard drive. Our cookies do not contain
any personally identifying information, but they do enable us to
provide features for storing your product purchase in your shopping
cart during your visit to our Web site.
Your
Consent
By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this
information by Magnetic-water.com. If we decide to change our
Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you
are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and
under what circumstances we disclose it.
In
Summary
We are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information
we collect on the site to enhance your overall experience. We do not
sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We abide
by our policy standards and we keep your personal information 100%
private. Thank you for shopping with us.

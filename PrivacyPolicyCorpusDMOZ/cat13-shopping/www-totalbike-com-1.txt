Total Bike respects your privacy. We will not sell, rent, or loan any identifiable information regarding its customers to any third party. Any information you give us is held with the utmost care and security, and will not be used in ways to which you have not consented.
Use of Information
Total Bike will not collect any personal information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals. Examples of such information are: name, postal address, e-mail address, phone number, birthdays, and product commentary. Total Bike will not share non-personal aggregate, or summary, information regarding its customers with other third parties
Cookies
Total Bike uses cookies very sparingly throughout the site. A cookie is a way we assign you a unique identification during your visit. The members area of the online forums is the only area where cookies are given. If you have cookies disabled you will still be able to use these areas, only you login information will not be stored.

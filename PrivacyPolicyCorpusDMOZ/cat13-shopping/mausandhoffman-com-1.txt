Our Commitment To You
Your preferences are extremely important to us. To best serve you, we want you to be aware of our practices and policies and your choices regarding them.
The Information We Collect
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted. The types of personal information collected are:
Name
Address
Phone Number
E-mail Address
Credit/Debit Card Information
The Way We Use Information
We use the information you provide when placing an order to complete that order.
We use e-mail addresses to answer the emails we receive, help successfully complete orders and to share our internet offers. E-mail addresses are not used for any other purpose and are never shared with or sold to outside parties. You may elect at any time not to receive e-mails from our company.
We use the information you provide when placing an order to We do occasionally make our mailing list available to companies carefully screened for content and presentation. If you do not wish to receive these mailings, please copy your mailing address and forward it to us with your instructions. They will be followed faithfully.complete that order.
Our Commitment To Data Security
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
How To Contact Us
Should you have other questions or concerns about these policies, please call us at 1-800-628-6287 or send us an email at info@mausandhoffman.com.

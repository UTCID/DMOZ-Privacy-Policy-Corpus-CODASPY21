Consumer Privacy at Duncraft
Duncraft, Inc. does not exchange, rent or sell consumer email addresses to any third party.
However, Duncraft, Inc. may exchange, rent or sell certain personal information with third parties under circumstances which might be deemed a "sale"
under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and/or the Nevada Privacy Law.
You may request that we exclude your personal information from such arrangements by entering your information below.
You may need to provide additional identifying information before we can process your request. You may also make this request by calling
(888) 879-5095 or by emailing info@duncraft.com.

Privacy Policy Your privacy is extremely important to us. We will not sell, trade or publish any information you have given us.
The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices used by The Extraordinary Baby Shoppe.
Information Automatically Logged
As with many web sites, The Extraordinary Baby Shoppe keeps a basic log of web traffic to the site. This information does NOT contain any personal information like the name or email address of the visitor, and is only used for general purposes such as finding from which other sites visitors are coming from and which pages they visit at The Extraordinary Baby Shoppe.
External Links
This site contains links to other sites. The Extraordinary Baby Shoppe is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Contacting The Extraordinary Baby Shoppe
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can e-mail us.

Privacy Policy
Tempo will not share any information about you with any outside third parties, other than our authorized agents in conjunction with providing you communications services.
We safeguard all customer records and communications in compliance with state and federal laws and regulations.
Tempo does not provide any customer records or communications to any party without following legal process, including the proper receipt of warrants, subpoenas, and court orders. This has been the company’s position since its inception and will continue to be our position into the future.

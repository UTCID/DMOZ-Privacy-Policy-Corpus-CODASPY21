Privacy Statement
Brad's Corvettes is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell or disclose
any information that identifies you to a third party. We may use the information we
collect to periodically notify you about new services or special offers we think you
will find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, you may send an
e-mail to brad@bradscorvettes.com with 'unsubscribe' as the subject line.
Brad's Corvettes does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others.
Brad's Corvettes does not store or keep credit card numbers on file.

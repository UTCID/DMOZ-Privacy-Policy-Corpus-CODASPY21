Privacy Policy
We will never rent, sell or share your personal data to anyone. We use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order to complete that order. Our secure shopping cart is Level 1 PCI-DSS compliant and uses 256-bit SSL certificates to keep order details secure.  Our credit card processor receives the data necessary to process the financial aspect of your order. We may contact you by email regarding the status of your order. And we may contact you by phone, mail or email if we have questions regarding your order. We do not store your credit card number on our web server or any online location.

Privacy Policy
This is a privacy policy for Michelsons UK.
About Us
Michelsons is fully committed to respecting and protecting your privacy and security. We aim to ensure that any information we obtain and use will always be held, used and transferred in compliance with any and all applicable data protection rules.
We will not pass on your details to anyone else without asking you first.
You may opt out of the various uses of your personal information at any time, even if you have previously given your consent for us to use them.
Cookies
michelsons.com uses cookies as part of the mechanism to hold the contents of the shopping basket during each visit that you make to the site, and as such we require both permanent and session cookies to be enabled. There is no requirement to have third party cookies enabled for our site to work.
Security checks
As part of our policy to protect against the fraudulent use of credit cards, we may carry out random security checks on orders. These can take various forms and may involve contacting you by telephone before your order is processed. Please note that we do not store your credit card details online.

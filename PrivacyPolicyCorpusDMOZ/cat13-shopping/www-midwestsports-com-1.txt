Web Site Security
Our customers' security is very important to us.
We have incorporated SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption technology to our online ordering process through VeriSign RSA/Secure Server. This ensures that any credit card or address information entered on our site is encrypted and transferred to our mainframe safely.
PLEASE NOTE:
This site only becomes secure once you click on Check Out, located on any product page, and you begin to enter your personal and credit card information. All information (related to your order) entered after that becomes encrypted for your safety.
Customer Privacy
Midwest Sports Supply will not rent or sell your name, address, phone number, email address, credit card information, or any other personal information to any third party, PERIOD. However, we must cooperate fully should a situation arise where we are required by law or legal process to provide information about a customer.
By visiting our site, third parties may place cookies on your browser for targeted advertising purposes.
This data may be used by third parties to target advertising on other sites based on your online activity.
We also engage a third-party partner for the purpose of identifying users and delivering to them marketing on our behalf (i.e. email or direct mail). Our partners may collect information directly from your device, such as your IP address, device ID, and information about your browser or operating system; may combine personal and non-personal information about you with information from other sources; and may place or recognize a unique cookie on your browser. This activity enables them to identify you to facilitate the delivery of marketing on our behalf. To opt out of recognition services, please go to https://idx.listrakbi.com/opt-out.html. To opt out of third-party cookies, please go to https://www.aboutads.info/choices.

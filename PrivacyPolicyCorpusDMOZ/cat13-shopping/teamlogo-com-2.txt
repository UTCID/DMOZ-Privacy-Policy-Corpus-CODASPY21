Your Privacy
Privacy StatementOur pledge of privacy to you is very simple. Teamlogo.com will NEVER sell, rent or give away any information collected about our customers from this web site. All information collected on this web site is used only by Teamlogo.com to make your shopping experience a pleasant one as well as to notify you of special offers or events. We do not share your contact information with anyone  unlike many of the chain stores who sell or rent their customer's contact information.
Mailing List: Teamlogo.com from time to time sends email announcements to our customers with special offers or introducing new products. These announcements will go out only to those of our customers who choose to sign up for our newsletters. At any time, a customer may remove themselves from our mailing list.

Please note we do not sell, give or rent our mailing lists. Thank you for allowing us to serve you.

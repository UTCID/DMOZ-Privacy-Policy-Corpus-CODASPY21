What are Digital Downloads?
Click here to shop for Digital Downloads >
Digital Downloads are downloadable sheet music files and can be viewed directly on your computer or mobile device.  Just purchase, download and play! Once you download your digital sheet music, you can view it at home, school, work or on any device to which it is downloaded. And the best part is, you don’t have to be connected to the Internet to view your downloaded sheet music! Your downloaded sheet music will have a watermark at the bottom of each page that will include your name (or email), purchase date and number of copies you purchased. PLEASE NOTE: You are only licensed to print as many copies as you have purchased. You may replace a destroyed or lost copy. However, you may not distribute the file to others or print more copies than purchased for use (i.e., you may not print individual copies for friends or students).
Searching for Digital Downloads
Click ‘Download’ from the blue navigation bar or use the search bar at the top of all pages.
• Enter the name of the title of interest in the search field.
• To narrow down your search, enter the title and the composer or arranger’s name. You can also use the left navigation bar to filter by category.
• You can limit your current search results to just Digital Download titles by selecting the Available Instantly Only button located in the grey box at the top of search results.
Not all titles are available as Digital Downloads; however we’re adding more titles everyday!
How To Purchase Digital Downloads
Once you’ve found the Digital Download title(s) of interest, you can proceed to purchase them by following these steps:
• Add the title to your shopping cart
• Log into or create a Sheet Music Plus account (Digital Downloads require a Sheet Music Plus account)
• Enter your payment method: Credit Card or PayPal
• Submit your order
You’ll receive an order confirmation email containing a link to view and print your title from your Sheet Music Plus Digital Library.

Privacy Policy
CarryaBigSticker is not out to get you. We just want your money. If you visit our site or make a purchase from us, we will do our darnedest to make sure your information remains confidential except to the extent absolutely necessary to process your payment and make our site functional for you. If you share your e-mail address or phone number, we will not share it and we may not even use it. However, we will usually send you a receipt for your order via e-mail. If you have asked us to keep you informed about certain matters such as new products or special discount offers, we will try to send you an e-mail about such matters from time to time. You may always opt out of such e-mails if you find that they are not useful to you.

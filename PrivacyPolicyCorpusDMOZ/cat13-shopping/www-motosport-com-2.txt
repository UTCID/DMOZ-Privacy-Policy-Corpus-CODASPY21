Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in your device memory that help us see which areas and features of our Services are popular and count visits. Web beacons are electronic images that may be used in our Services or emails and help deliver cookies, count visits and understand usage and campaign effectiveness. We use Google Analytics to analyze users browsing history, search history, and user generated-activity (e.g., reviews, shared photos, and comments) as well as interactions with our sites, advertisements, and apps.

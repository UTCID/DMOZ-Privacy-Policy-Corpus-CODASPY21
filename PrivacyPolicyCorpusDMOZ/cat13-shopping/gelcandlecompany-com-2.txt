About Us
Welcome to our Store!
Our candles are made with clean mineral oil which gives you the longest burn, a realistic scent and the cleanest burning experience you can find today! In small bathces we hand pour and very carefully assemble Forever Candles which are unique refillable designs. Available in scented and unscented gel candles in 90 hour Classic Jars, 30 hour Votives, 8 hour Tea Lights, Gel Melts(tm) for warmers, premium fragrance oils, incense, teas, and more.

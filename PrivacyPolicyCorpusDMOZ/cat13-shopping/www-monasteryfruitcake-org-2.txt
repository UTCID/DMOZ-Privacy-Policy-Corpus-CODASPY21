Your Privacy Is Guaranteed
When you purchase from Monastery Fruitcake, your personal information remains 100% private and secure. We don’t sell or share your name or address with any one. By providing your name and address, you will become a member of our mailing list. If you prefer not to be on this list, please email us.
When you make a purchase on this website, your billing address and order information will be saved on our server.
Your credit card information is NOT saved.
Customer Accounts
When you create an account with us, we provide access to certain parts of the site using your customer email address and password. This information is maintained in our database along with any transaction information you supply us when you place an order.
This would include the dates on which you made a purchase from us, the amounts and types of purchases, and any contact information you provide us.
Secure Transmission
When you browser our site, all of the transmissions are encrypted using an SSL certificate 128-bit encryption utilizing a modern cipher suite and the latest version of transport layer security.
We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site. Your credit card information is not saved on our servers.

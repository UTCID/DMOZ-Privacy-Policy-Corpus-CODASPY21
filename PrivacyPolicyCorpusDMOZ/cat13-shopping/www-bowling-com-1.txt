Call Us
You can contact our Customer Service Toll Free at 1-800-441-2695 or call 1-601-500-7015. Our office hours are Mon-Fri, 8:30am - 5:30pm (CT). If you call on the weekend or a holiday we'll respond to you the following business day.
Email Us
You can contact our Customer Service by email at HelpDept@bowling.com. Our office hours are Mon-Fri, 8:30am - 5:30pm (CT). Expect a response within 24 hours (often sooner) Mon-Fri. If you email on the weekend or a holiday we'll respond to you the following business day.
Live Chat With Us
For quick help with your order or to ask a product question you can chat live with us, Mon-Fri, 8:30am - 5:30pm (CT).
Send Us a Text
For quick help with your order or to ask a product question send us a text at 601-500-7015, Mon-Fri, 8:30am - 5:30pm (CT). If you message us on the weekend or a holiday we'll respond to you the following business day.
Message Us on Facebook

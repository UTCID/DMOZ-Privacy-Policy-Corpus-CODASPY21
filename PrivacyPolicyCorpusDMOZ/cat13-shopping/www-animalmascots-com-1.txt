Privacy Policy
International Fun-Shop has created this privacy statement to show our firm commitment to maintaining your privacy.
The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for our website network; this includes the following websites: Fun-Shop.com, AnimalMascots.com, MardiGrasPartySupply.com, StageMakeupOnline.com. International Fun-Shop owns and manages these websites.
Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart and to store current session information.
Regardless of whether or not you create an account with us, we maintain your address information and order history on our servers.
This site contains links to other sites outside of the International Fun-Shop network of sites. International Fun-Shop is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Our site uses an order form for customers to request products and services. We collect visitor's contact information (like their email address) and financial information (credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers. The customer's contact information is used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Financial information that is collected is used to bill the user for products and services. Financial information is removed from the server at the time of processing.
Anonymous data is collected by our servers regarding the browser, operating system, and other hardware and software information being used to visit our websites.
We share some portions of customer data with partners who advertise and keep metrics for us. This is used especially for email subscriptions, when users opt in. Otherwise, we do not release any customer information to third parties
If you have any questions about how we collect or use your information, please contact us.

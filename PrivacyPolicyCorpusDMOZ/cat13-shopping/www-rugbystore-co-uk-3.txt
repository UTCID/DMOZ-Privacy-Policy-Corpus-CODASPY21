Wondering what the difference is between a Test and a Pro shirt? Read our Rugby Shirt Type Guide for some information.Measurement Guide: MenHEAD - when measuring for a new headguard:
Start your tape at the centre of your forehead, run the tape around your head, under the bulge at the back of your head, keeping the tape firm but not tight.CHEST - when measuring for rugby shirts, tee shirts, shoulder pads etc:
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure level under the arms and across your back.WAIST - when measuring for rugby shorts, track pants etc:
Measure around the narrowest part (typically the small of your back and where your body bends side to side), keeping the tape measure level. Keep the tape firm but not tight.FOOTWEAR - when measuring for new rugby boots:
Measure from the tip of your toes to the heel.General Size GuidesMen's General Size GuideGarment SizeChest - inches (centimetres)Waist - inches (centimetres)S - Small36" - 38"32"M - Medium38" - 40"34"L - Large42" - 44"36"XL - Extra Large44" - 46"38"2XL - Double Extra Large46" - 48"40"3XL - Triple Extra Large48" - 50"42"Ladies General SizingGarment SizeChest - inches (centimetres)Waist - inches (centimetres)8 - Eight32"24"10 - Ten34"26"12 - Twelve36"28"14 - Fourteen38"30"16 - Sixteen40"32"18 - Eighteen42"34"Kids General SizingGarment SizeChest - inches (centimetres)Waist - inches (centimetres)SB - Small Boys24" - 26"24"MB - Medium Boys26" - 28"26"LB - Large Boys30" - 32"28"Y - Youths34"30"Footwear SizingUKUSEuroJapanese9k9.5k2715cm10k10.5k2816cm11k11.5k2917cm12k12.5k3018cm13k13.5k3119cm1k2k3320cm2k3k3421cm3k4k3522cm4k5k3723cm5k6k3824cm5.5k6.5k38.524.5cm673925cm7840.526cm894227cm8.59.542.527.5cm9104328cm9.510.54428.5cm101144.529cm10.511.54529.5cm111245.530cm12134731cm131448.532cm141549.533cm15165134cm

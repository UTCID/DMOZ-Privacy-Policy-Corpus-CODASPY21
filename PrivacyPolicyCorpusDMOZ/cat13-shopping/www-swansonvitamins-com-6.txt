US Shipping Rates and Delivery Options
SwansonVitamins.com offers several delivery options and rates for domestic shipping within the 48 contiguous United States.
FREE STANDARD SHIPPING on orders over $50!*
*Additional shipping fees apply to all orders shipped to Alaska, Hawaii and US Territories, as well as all international and expedited orders. Overweight orders may also incur additional shipping charges. Certain promotions may offer alternate shipping rates. Please refer to your promotional materials for details.
Standard Shipping - $4.99
Please allow 24–48 hours for your order to be processed.
Please allow 2-5 business days from ship date for delivery
Shipped according to the best rate/time as determined by our system
We use various carriers for delivery of your package
Note: We are unable to ship to General Delivery addresses
Alaska, Hawaii & U.S. Territories - $4.99 (Standard) + $3.99
Applies to orders shipping to Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, American Samoa, Micronesia, Guam, Marshall Islands, Mariana Islands, Palau and the Virgin Islands
Please allow 24–48 hours for your order to be processed.
Please allow up to 10 business days from the ship date for delivery
Note: Remote locations in Alaska may take longer than 10 business days, due to varying delivery schedules in the area
Freight Forwarding Shipments
Swanson Health Products is not liable or responsible for any losses, damages or delays due to the actions or inactions of third party freight forwarding shipping services.
Additional Delivery Options
Fast Delivery - $4.99 (Standard) + $2.99 (Upgrade)
Guaranteed delivery in 1-4 business days from ship date (or you get your $2.99 back!)
Expedited processing—your order gets moved to the head of the line for packaging & shipping. Please allow 24–48 hours for your order to be processed.
Priority handling—if one of your items is close to being sold out, your order will get filled first
Not available for orders shipping outside of the contiguous 48 states
2Day Delivery - $9.99
Delivery in two business days by 8 p.m.
Orders received after 12:00 p.m. Central Time will ship the following business day
2Day Delivery shipments process Monday through Friday
Not available for PO boxes or outside the contiguous 48 states
Orders that include more than 6 units of one item are not eligible
Priority Overnight - $14.99
Delivery the next business day by 8:00 p.m. Monday-Friday.
Orders received after 12:00 p.m. Central Time will ship the following business day
Overnight shipments process Monday-Friday
Not available for PO boxes or outside the contiguous 48 states
Orders that include more than 6 units of one item are not eligible
Delivery Insurance
Available for $1.00 at checkout
Standard shipping & handling plus the $1.00 delivery insurance will be refunded to you if you do not receive your order within 2-5 business days of ship date
Fast Delivery handling service ($7.98) plus the $1.00 delivery insurance will be refunded to you if you do not receive your order within 4 business days of the ship date
Valid for orders shipping within the 48 contiguous United States only
Limit one per order
Additional Shipping Charges
Orders may incur additional shipping charges (minus applicable shipping promotions) based on weight and/or volume.

Frequently Asked Questions
Some pages on your website have a Shipping Restrictions tab. What does this mean?
We are unable to ship certain items or ingredients outside of the U.S. Additionally, some countries prohibit the sale of products containing the indicated ingredients. Consequently, we are unable to fill an order for them if you live in one of the affected countries. Please see the Shipping Restrictions page for more information.Why are some of your products restricted in California?
In order to comply with California's Proposition 65 regulations, we must refrain from shipping items that do not meet those regulations to that state. We apologize for any inconvenience.When will my order ship?
Orders ship Monday through Friday, not on weekends or major holidays.

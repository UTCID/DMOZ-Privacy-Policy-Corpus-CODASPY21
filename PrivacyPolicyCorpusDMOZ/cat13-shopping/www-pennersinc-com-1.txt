Privacy policy
Penner’s Inc. is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell, trade, or disclose any information that identifies you to a third party without your prior approval. We may use the information we collect to periodically notify you about new services or special offers we think you’ll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, you may send an email to sales@pennersinc.com with the title “unsubscribe” as the subject line or simply call the store at 210-226-2487.
We request information from you on our order form. To purchase from us online or over the phone, you must provide contact information (such as name and shipping address) and financial information (such as credit card number and expiration date). This information is used for billing purposes and to fill online and phone orders. If we have trouble processing an order, we’ll use this information to contact you. We will never sell your information to a 3rd party.
Our Commitment To Data Security To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online and over the phone.

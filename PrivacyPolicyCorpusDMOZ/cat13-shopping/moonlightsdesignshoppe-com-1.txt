Privacy Policy:
MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com (“MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com”) operates MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com.com and may operate other websites. It is MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s visitors use its website. From time to time, MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.
MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com.com blogs/sites. MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.
Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s websites choose to interact with MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com in ways that require MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at MoonlightsDesignShoppe.comMonterey.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com. MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.
Aggregated Statistics
MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.
Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information
MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s behalf or to provide services available at MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.
Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com uses cookies to help MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com identify and track visitors, their usage of MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com website, and their website access preferences. MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.
Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by MoonlightsDesignShoppe.com and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

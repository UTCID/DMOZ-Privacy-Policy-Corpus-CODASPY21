Privacy Policy
We at Seaway China place great value on your privacy and have created a privacy policy to demonstrate this firm commitment. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for our website, www.seawaychina.com:
We do not share any personal information gathered online with outside companies.
Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information. We use this information solely within the company to improve our site and services.
Our site uses “cookies” technology as a means of keeping track of your shopping cart. A “cookie” is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive.
This site contains links to other sites. Seaway China is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitor’s contact information (like their name and email address) and financial information (like their credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders and information about our company to our customers. The customer’s contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below. Financial information that is collected is only used to bill the user for products and services.

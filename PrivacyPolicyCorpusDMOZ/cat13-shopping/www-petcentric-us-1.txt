PRIVACY POLICY
Personal information & Email addresses
PetCentric is committed to protecting your privacy. All personal information
and Email addresses gathered from contact forms and order forms will NEVER be
rented out or sold for marketing or other purposes. However, by submitting your
order you agree that we may share with third party payment processors and
shipping carriers any
information necessary to complete the transaction. We require customers paying
by check to register, other customers may register to reserve limited quantity
items and view their order details and shipment information online.
Our site is targeted toward adults and children over the age of 12. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children 12 and under.
This site may contain links to other sites. PetCentric is not responsible for
the privacy practices or the content of those sites.
Cookies && JavaScript
When you visit our site, we send one or more cookies - a small
text file containing a string of alphanumeric characters - to your computer that
uniquely identifies your browser and is used to enhance your navigation through
the site and to store your login and cart information. This site also uses JavaScript
to insure we meet your browsers compatibility needs, make your browsing
experience more pleasant and easier, and to aid in form processing.
CONDITIONS OF USEPlease see our Conditions of Use page, for this websites Terms of Use of this site.
SECURITY
We understand your concerns about Internet Security.
We make every effort to protect your personal information, by employing a
Stronghold server which supports industry-standard 128-bit SSL encryption. SSL
encryption is active in all areas where customer information is
requested.
PAYMENT POLICY
We accept payment by check, or by credit card (through PayPal)
and eCheck (PayPal). Item will be shipped after payment has been
received and verified. There is a $30.00 charge for all non-sufficient funds
returned checks, if this occurs we will bill you for this fee through PayPal.
Payment Information Storage and Processing:
Your billing information collected and stored through PayPal at
PayPal.  This means your billing information is collected stored at one of
securest and largest online payment processors there is.   Although
PayPal does not require you join PayPal to make a payment through their service,
we recommend you do.  Paying with PayPal minimizes risks involved with
online payments, you can make purchases from many different online companies and
individuals without them ever seeing your credit card information.  And,
most online stores now accept PayPal as a payment option.  PayPal also
offers a Buyer Protection Plan for customers of verified
providers like us.

Privacy Policy
Our relationship with you
Bachcroft Labels has the utmost respect for your privacy and your private information. Any information you share with us is understood to be private information and is treated as such.
Whether that information is an email address, a mailing list, a photo or anything else transmitted to Bachcroft for the purpose of data storage or product ordering, we do not, under any circumstances, share that private information with third parties.
Use of your information
Personal information is any information that identifies you, your location, interests, habits, or relationship to others. Bachcroft Labels stores and collects personal information from you when you open an account in the Mailing List Printing Center and when you purchase a product or a service from us. That information is used for the sole purpose of making it convenient for you to place an order and possible for us to process your order and ship it to you.

Privacy Policy
Protecting your privacy is important to us. We hope the following statement will help you understand how Sugar & Spice Extraordinary Sweet Treats, Inc. collects, uses and safeguards the information collected on our website.
Information Collection
The information we collect is confidential and used only by Sugar & Spice Extraordinary Sweet Treats, Inc. in the operation of our business. It is neither shared nor sold.
Registration and Online Purchase Forms
Our registration and online purchase forms ask visitors to provide contact information such as their name, postal address and e-mail address. We collect and use this data for marketing purposes. This information is also used to contact visitors if necessary and to send visitors information about our company. Visitors may opt-out of receiving mailings by Contacting Us. Our online purchase form asks purchasers to provide their telephone number. This information is used to contact purchasers, if necessary, to facilitate completion of orders.
Updating Information
If you wish to update or correct your contact information, please do so via e-mail or in writing. Our contact information is listed at Contact Us.
Contacting the Website
If you have any questions about the privacy policy, practices of this website or your dealings with this website, please contact us. Our contact information is listed at Contact Us.

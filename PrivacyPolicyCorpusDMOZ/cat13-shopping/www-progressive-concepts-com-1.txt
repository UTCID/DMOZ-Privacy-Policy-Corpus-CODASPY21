Privacy Policy Your privacy is of great importance to us.   Because we gather certain types of information about users of Progressive Concepts, we feel that you should fully understand the terms and conditions regarding the capture and use of information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it.   Registration   Progressive Concepts uses registration forms. Registration forms require users to give us contact information, like names and e-mail addresses, as well as identifiers, like IDs and passwords. The user's contact information is used to facilitate order processing for purchases in the future, as well as to contact users about specials and promotions. Individual user information is never disclosed to third parties.   Order forms   Progressive Concepts uses an order form for customers to purchase products. If you place an order at Progressive Concepts, we will collect information from you, including your contact information and credit card information for credit card orders. This information will be used by Progressive Concepts to bill you for products. Contact information from the order form will also be used to ship orders, to contact you for customer service related issues, and to send you information about our company   Cookies   Progressive Concepts uses a feature of your Internet Web browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. Progressive Concepts creates and then uses these cookie files to track your movement "clicks" as you move around the Progressive Concepts web site.   Progressive Concepts only uses cookies to keep track of items you put into your shopping cart and to help determine whether you came to Progressive Concepts from a particular Internet link or banner advertisement.   Cookies can also be used to help save and retrieve usernames used on the Progressive Concepts website. This is done for convenience and means that you do not have to re-enter this information on every visit to Progressive Concepts. Cookies cannot be used by Progressive Concepts to retrieve any personal information about you unless you have previously purchased from or registered with us. Progressive Concepts does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer unless such information was knowingly and willingly provided by you.   Security   Progressive Concepts uses the latest secure transaction processing methods available for web commerce applications. Progressive Concepts has obtained its Secure Server ID from Thawte. Thawte Certification, a VeriSign company, is a global provider of digital certificate products, services and solutions that create security, privacy and authentication in electronic commerce.   Progressive Concepts uses the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) for all encrypted transactions. SSL ensures that your personal information and credit card data is secure when shopping at Progressive Concepts.

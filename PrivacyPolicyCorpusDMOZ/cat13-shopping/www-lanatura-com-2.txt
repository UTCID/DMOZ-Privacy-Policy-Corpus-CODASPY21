Information that is gathered from visitors
In common with other websites, log files are stored on the web server saving details such as the visitor's IP address, browser type, referring page and time of visit.
Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the website, and are not used for any other purpose.
Where registration is required for LaNatura Online, the visitor's email,username and other information submitted to us will be stored on our server.
How the Information is used
The information is used to enhance the vistor's experience when using the website to display personalised content and special offers. Any information stored on the site will not be shared or distributed to any third parties for any reason. E-mail addresses will not be sold, rented or leased to 3rd parties at any time.
For E-mail List subcribers, and LaNatura Online Members, E-mail may be sent to inform you of news or product offers. If you have subscribed to one of our services, you may unsubscribe by following the instructions which are included in e-mail that you receive.

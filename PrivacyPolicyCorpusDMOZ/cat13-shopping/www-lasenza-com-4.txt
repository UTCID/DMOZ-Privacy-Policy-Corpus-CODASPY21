RETURN POLICY
If you are not completely satisfied with merchandise, you may return it in accordance with the Terms & Conditions below and we will refund eligible purchases.
RETURN TERMS AND CONDITIONS
Holiday Return Policy:
Purchases made Sun, Nov 22 thru Thu, Dec 24 will be eligible for return/exchange until Sun, Jan 31 (instead of our standard 30 day limit).
Returns to our online distribution center must be postmarked no later than 30 days after the original purchase date.
Merchandise purchased at a store must be returned to a store in the country in which it was purchased. If store merchandise is sent to our warehouse, it will be sent back at the sender’s expense.
Online exchanges are not available at this time.
Online refunds will be made to the credit card, Interac, or PayPal account used in the original transaction.
Merchandise purchased online from all categories and price points may be returned or exchanged. We are, however, unable to accept merchandise where the sewn-in garment tag has been cut or removed or the price tag has been removed.
Returned merchandise must be in its original state, deemed to be unworn with tags attached, or be defective to be accepted for a refund.
Club La Senza cards cannot be returned.

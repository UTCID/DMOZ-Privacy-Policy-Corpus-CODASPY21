Privacy Policy
What information do we collect?
When you place an order with us, your name, delivery address and contact details will be held on a customer record file.
None of your card information or billing details are kept on record.
What do we use the information for?
To process transactions.
To improve customer service, your information helps us to more effectively respond to your needs.
The email address you provide for order processing will also be used to send you information and updates regarding your order.
We may also occasionally send you company news, updates, product / service information & details of special offers.
Note: If at anytime you wish to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, you are able to so by clicking an 'unsubscribe' link on the email.
Do we disclose any information to outside parties?
Absolutely not, we do not sell, trade or otherwise transfer any of your personal information to third parties.

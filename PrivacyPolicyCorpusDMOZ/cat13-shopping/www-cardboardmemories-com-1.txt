Privacy Policy
Cardboardmemories.com is committed to protecting the privacy of all our customers and visitors to our Web Site. We use the information we collect about you only to process orders.
How do you protect my credit card information?
Cardboardmemories.com uses a Secure Socket Layer (SSL), to protect your credit card information. This means that when you press order by credit card on our shopping cart, you will be brought to an https page, which means it is secure. On the bottom of your browser you will also see a lock. When you see these two things you know that you can order with confidence because the information you are typing in, is being protected.
What information do we collect? How do we use it?
When you order from Cardboardmemories.com, we need to collect your basic order information: name, e-mail address, billing address, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and ship your order.We do not sell, trade, or give out your information to anybody. It is only used for the sale of the item.

Privacy notice
All personal information held by the Human Givens Foundation (HGF) will be treated in accordance with the Data Protection legislation, which means that the HGF will:
use the contact details of individuals only to get in touch about matters relating to the HGF, unless they have given their explicit consent to be contacted for other purposes.
never sell personal information or share it with third parties.
keep personal information secure.
The Trustees of the Human Givens Foundation, April 2018

DavidsBridal.com features a variety of items available to purchase in our stores and/or online. "In Store" means the item is available at our 300+ stores nationwide. Please note that products, colors and styles vary by location however most items can be special ordered at any store. If the product is available for online purchase, color and size must be selected (if applicable). When a size is not listed, or the size box is light grey and not clickable, that size is not available in the selected color. Once you have selected your color and size, click the Add to Cart button to place the item in your Shopping Bag. If you have made your color and size selections and are still unable to add the item, it is currently out of stock and unavailable for purchase. Inventory is updated daily so please check back soon.
As you add items, your Shopping Bag will reflect the product details including the item name, quantity, color, size and price. We will automatically save items in your shopping bag for 2 hours, after which they are removed to become available for other customers to purchase. See Favorites if you are not ready to purchase, but want to save styles for easy access in the future.
During checkout, you will have the opportunity to verify all information and make changes, if necessary, before placing your order. See Placing an Order for more details.

Finding an Item You can quickly find what you are looking for by entering a description of the item, or the style number, in the "Search style # or keyword" box in the top left of every page. Your results will be presented by category for your shopping convenience.
Online Product Availability
Davidsbridal.com features a variety of items available to purchase in our stores and/or online. Each item is clearly marked so you can identify its purchase options while browsing our website.
"In Store" means the style is available at our stores; pleases note that selection varies by store but most styles are able to be special ordered at any store. Please contact the location near you for color and size availability. "Online Exclusive" or "Online" indicates that the style is available to purchase exclusively at davidsbridal.com. Select online styles may be available at stores, however quantities are limited. For your shopping convenience, select styles are available "In Store & Online" Please note that Sale and Outlet items have limited quantities and are only available while supplies last.
Products are removed from the website when quantities are limited and/or they are no longer available for purchase. Items added to your Shopping Bag will remain for a short period of time (approximately 2 hours) and if you do not check out, they are removed so the inventory is made available for other shoppers.
Your Favorites Saving items to Your Favorites allows you to easily access them in the future and to share them with family and friend. When you see a style you like, click on "Save to My Favorites" and the items will be saved to your My Favorites page, where they will remain until you remove them or they are discontinued. Please note that Favorites will not save the personalized details and we cannot guarantee product availability. Because your Favorites are accessed with your unique email address, you can print your favorite styles from any computer and take them to a David's Bridal store near you. Your stylist will be able to access your Favorites lists at your store appointment.
You can create multiple Favorites lists to keep all your items organized, such as wedding dresses, little white dresses, bridesmaid looks, accessories, wedding décor, etc.

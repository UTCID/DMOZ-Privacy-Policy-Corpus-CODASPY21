Privacy & Security
Privacy & Security
Privacy
At Earring Holder Gallery, we understand how important your privacy is to you, and you can trust us to keep your information confidential.
We do need some personal information about you to track and ship your order. But we can promise you that we will not share any of your information with any other organizations. We do not sell or give our list of email addresses to other companies.
We will release account and other personal information when required to do so by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.
Children’s Privacy
The privacy of your children is very important us. www.earringholders.com does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 and we do not advertise to that age demographic. earringholders.com does not send any email information to children under the age of 13 without their parents’ request.
If we find out that we received information from a child under the age of 13 without parental consent, we will immediately delete their information from our database.
Security
All of our financial transactions take place on secure servers at Shopify or at PayPal. You can collect items in your shopping cart and view our entire site on our servers, but as soon as you enter credit card information, it will be processed on secure servers.
In any case, credit card transactions you make through the Earring Holder Gallery website are risk-free. In fact, under the Fair Credit Billing Act, your bank cannot hold you liable for more than $50.00 of fraudulent charges made to your credit card.
Other Ordering Options
If you are still uncomfortable about using your credit card to order through the Internet, you may mail or telephone your order to us. Please refer to our Contact Us page to reach us.

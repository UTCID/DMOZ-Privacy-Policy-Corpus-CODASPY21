Privacy Statement for Arizona Indian Jewelry
Arizona Indian Jewelry has created this Privacy Statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: Arizona Indian Jewelry.com
We currently maintain a mailing list for weekly newsletters. Permanent removal instructions are available in each newsletter.
Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart. Our secure site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart. Our site’s ordering process records your E-Mail address and is used for sending Tracking Numbers and Order Status, and to get in touch with the customer when necessary.
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
We never give out, share or sell your personal information!

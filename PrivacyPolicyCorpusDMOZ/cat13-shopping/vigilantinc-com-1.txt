How We Use Your Information
Like most companies on the web, we automatically track general personal information for the purpose of establishing or enhancing our relationship with our users. We also monitor customer traffic patterns and site usage to help us improve the content, design, and layout of the site. When you visit Vigilantinc.com, our web server gathers this information by tracking your domain name (e.g. aol.com) or IP address, not your e-mail address.
Communication
We use your collected information to provide you with customer support, process transactions, respond to requests, send newsletters with updates and special offers, advertisements, seeking your feedback, and connect you to our website (vigilantinc.com).
When you place an order or fill out a form, we need your name and e-mail address, which we use to fulfill your order and deliver information to your regarding your order. To process, fulfill, and service your request, we also need a mailing address, credit card number, and credit card expiration date. Your credit card information is kept strictly private and confidential.
Optimization
We utilize user information, behavioral metrics, and other non-personally identifiable information to operate, provide, improve, and maintain our website. We also use it to develop new products and services, to prevent abuse and fraud, to personalize advertisements, and for administrative and internal business purposes.
Advertising
We collect use information, including personal information, behavioral metrics, geo-location data, demographic data and marketing preferences to personalize and display advertisements.
Protecting Your Privacy
Whenever you place an order, you automatically use Vigilant's secure e-commerce server and secure server software (SSL). This means that all the information you input is encrypted before it is sent to us, and once submitted, all customer data is protected against unauthorized access.
If you supply us with your telephone or fax number, you may receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders you have placed online or your online user account. We will contact you by phone on an as needed basis. Your phone number will not be used for solicitations.
To learn more about staying safe and secure online visit Google's Safety Center
Use of "Cookies"
Like most e-commerce and membership sites, we use cookies to collect information, which are small bits of information stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Our cookies do not contain any personal information, but they do remember your settings and the last time you visited.
What is a cookie?
Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit a website. Cookies are then sent back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognizes that cookie. Cookies are useful because they allow a website to recognize a user's device. You can find more information about cookies at: allaboutcookies.org.
Cookies serve many purposes, like remembering your preferences, letting you navigate between pages efficiently, and generally improve your overall web experience. They can also help ensure that advertisements you see online are more relevant to your interests.
Performance Cookies
Vigilant collects cookies to capture information about page visits (e.g. "performance cookies"). This information is anonymous and Vigilant uses it only internally to deliver the most pertinent content to our visitors. Information from the cookie is used to gauge page popularity, analyze traffic patterns on our website and guide website development improvements.
Targeting and Functionality Cookies
In our email broadcasts, Vigilant tracks usage statistics (e.g. "targeting and functionality cookies"). We track e-mail "opens" – meaning we track who opens our e-mail messages and when you open it. We also track "clicks" via encoded URLs. This indicates that we track if you click on the links contained in our e-mail messages. This information is used internally only to help us deliver relevant messaging, and is not shared with any third parties.
Also, as part of our marketing efforts, Vigilant uses Google AdWords Re-marketing services which is a Behavioral Targeting process provided by Google. It connects the activity of visitors on vigilantinc.com with the Adwords advertising network and the Doubleclick Cookie to enable us to serve you more pertinent advertisements. Many of these companies collect and use information under their own privacy policies.

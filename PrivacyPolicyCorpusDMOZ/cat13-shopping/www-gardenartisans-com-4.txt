Privacy Policy
The information we collect during checkout is for the sole purpose of billing and shipping your requested order. We do not share or sell your information.
We do not send emails to our customer base unless they have signed up to our newsletter mailing list. So if you would like to receive notice of sales or new products, you will need to sign up to receive these mailings.  You may also get notice through our Facebook page.
All items on this website are to be considered the property of Garden Artisans (this includes photos) and may not be used without prior authorization.
We respect your privacy. Any and all information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, disclosed to third parties or reused without your permission. Any information you give to us will be held with care and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.

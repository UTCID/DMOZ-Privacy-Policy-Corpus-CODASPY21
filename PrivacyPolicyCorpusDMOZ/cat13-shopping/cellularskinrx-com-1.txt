Cellular Skin Rx is committed to protecting your privacy. Any information you provide to CSRx will be handled in strict confidence. No information is released or sold to third parties for marketing or selling purposes. CSRx does collect information from our website for analytical purposes as we continually strive to improve your shopping experience.
CSRx will never sell, distribute, barter or transfer any personally-identifiable information obtained from a user to a third party.
Website Visitors
CSRx tracks traffic patterns throughout the site through non-invasive click-through tracking. CSRx develops summary reports for in-house use only.
Business Relationships
Our website may link to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those sites. We have no control over data collection on other sites. We do not benefit from it nor have access to it.
Cookies
Some parts of the CSRx site uses “cookies.” A cookie is an element of data that a website sends to your browser, which may then be stored on your computer. The cookies that we use do not link to any of the user’s personally-identifiable information.
If you do not want cookies sent to your computer, you can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. Then you decide whether or not to accept it. If you reject cookies from our site, there are some areas of the site that you will not be able to use.
Opt-In, Opt-Out Features
CSRx puts control over e-mail communications in the users’ hands: We provide information through email communications to those who sign to receive this information. Subscribers can easily opt-out at any time. Each CSRx email communication has your e-mail address stamped on it with simple instructions on how to unsubscribe.
Contact Information
If you have any questions or comments about our privacy practices, please contact CSRx either by email (Info@cellularskinrx.com) or by telephone, 1-512-633-5223 (M-F, 9AM-5PM CST) with any suggestions or changes to our privacy policy.

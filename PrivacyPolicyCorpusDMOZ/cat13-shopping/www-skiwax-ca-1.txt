SHIPPING POLICY
Thank you for visiting and shopping at skiwax.ca! Following are the terms and conditions that constitute my Shipping Policy.
Domestic Shipping Policy
Shipment processing time
All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Items in stock are shipped in 1-2 days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.
Be aware some items for sale on skiwax.ca are not always carried in stock and are therefore classified as “special-order”. To see if a product is special-order, check below the “add to cart” button on the product page for its inventory status. Special-order items typically ship in 2-4 weeks. After checkout, you will be notified with an estimated time of delivery for your special-order items.
Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates
Five-dollar shipping on orders above $100 and Free Shipping on orders above $250 to all North American addresses. Shipping costs for orders under $100 or to addresses outside of North America are calculated and displayed during checkout. Five-dollar and free shipping to Canadian addresses are shipped by Canada Post Expedited Parcel with an estimated delivery time of 1-7 business days. Five-dollar and free shipping to the U.S. is with Small Packet USA Air or Expedited Parcel USA Air. Both have an estimated delivery time of 5-8 business days. All shipments are carried by Canada Post.

Shipment confirmation & Order tracking
You will receive a Shipment Confirmation email once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.
Damages
Skiwax.ca is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipping carrier to file a claim.
Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.
International Shipping Policy
Skiwax.ca ships to all physical addresses within Canada, the U.S., and World Wide.
Customs, Duties and Taxes
Skiwax.ca is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).
RETURNS & REFUNDS

PRIVACY POLICY
I. General
Kayser-Roth Corporation respects the privacy of every individual who visits our websites or responds to our interactive advertisements. This Privacy Statement outlines the type of information Kayser-Roth Corporation will collect and discloses how we will use that information. Also this Privacy Statement shall provide directives on either setting up discretionary limits or, altogether, preventing your personal information from being collected or shared, when you visit Kayser-Roth Corporation websites or respond to our advertisements. This website is operated in the United States. Your Personal Information will be stored in the United States and will be subject to laws applicable in that country.
II. Personally-Identifiable Information
Kayser-Roth Corporation will not collect any personally identifiable information about you (that is, your name, address, telephone number, or email address) unless you provide it to us voluntarily. If you have voluntarily submitted any personal information in the My Account section of our website, we will retain it as long as the account is active. By creating an account on our website, your account will earn reward points that are redeemable in $5 increments based on your purchase history; you can learn more about our rewards program here. You may access or edit your Account Information at any time by logging in to your Nononsense.com account. However, if you would like to have your information removed, please contact us at consumercare@nononsense.com.
Nononsense.com may use your information, unless otherwise stated, as follows: a) to conduct customer needs analysis; b) to monitor quality control of product and services; c) to notify you of special offers or events; and, d) to contact you if necessary. Credit Card information is not stored online. Your information is treated as personal and confidential. Nononsense.com will not sell or lend this information to any third party. We do, however, share data with third parties when necessary to fulfill a transaction, complete a service, for administrative purposes, or when required by law. Additional information you voluntarily disclose, by either submitting product reviews on Nononsense.com or posting content on our social media pages, will become publicly available. Kayser-Roth Corporation is not responsible for any personal information you voluntarily disclose in these public forums.
This Privacy Policy was lasted updated June 25, 2019 and may be updated from time to time. Please check back periodically for further updates and changes. The most updated version will be the one that is available on this website.
III. Children
Kayser-Roth Corporation shall neither intentionally nor knowingly collect any personally identifiable information (that is, name, address, telephone number, or e-mail address) from individuals under thirteen years of age. If a child has provided us with personally-identifiable information, a parent or guardian of that child should contact us at the e-mail address or phone number listed at the bottom of this Statement, in order to have this information deleted from our records. We will use reasonable efforts to promptly remove the child's information from our existing files.
IV. Non-Personally-Identifiable Information Collected Automatically
In some cases, we may collect information about you that is not personally identifiable. Examples include: the type of Internet Browser you are using; the type of computer operating system you are using; ; IP address, geolocation, session activity and, the domain name of the website from which you linked to our site or advertisement.
V. Information We May Place Automatically On Your Computer's Hard Drive
No nonsense uses Remarketing with Google Analytics to advertise online. Third-party vendors, including Google, show our ads on sites across the Internet. We use first-party cookies (such as the Google Analytics cookie) and third-party cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) together to inform, optimize, and serve ads based on someone's past visits to our websites. We will not facilitate the merging of personally-identifiable information with non-personally identifiable information previously collected.
VI. Links to other sites
Our website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.
VII. Privacy
We use pixels, or transparent GIF files, to help manage online advertising. These GIF files are provided by our ad management partner, DoubleClick. These files enable DoubleClick to recognize a unique cookie on your Web browser, which in turn enables us to learn which advertisements bring users to our website. The cookie was placed by us, or by another advertiser who works with DoubleClick. With both cookies and Spotlight technology, the information that we collect and share is anonymous and not personally identifiable. It does not contain your name, address, telephone number or email address. For more information about DoubleClick, visit their website at doubleclickbygoogle.com.
VIII. What are Your Rights?
You have the right to request details about the personal data we store for you, and request for this information to be removed or updated. Please contact us for more information.
IX. Who to Contact
Email questions or concerns regarding submission of personally identifiable information to the, consumercare@nononsense.com or call us toll-free at 1-800-575-3497 Monday - Friday, 9 AM-5 PM, Eastern Standard Time (Closed weekends and holidays).

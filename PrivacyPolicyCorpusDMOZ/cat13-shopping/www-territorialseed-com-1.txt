Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
The Territorial Seed Company website does not record any personal information during your visit to our site. We only collect information that is provided by the customer for the online purchase of goods and services. We collect this information only when you place an order or request a catalog online.
We also allow the customer to create an online profile using email and password, where customers can track orders and maintain billing and shipping information. The Territorial Seed Company website does not record your credit card number, and this information must be entered every visit. You can modify your personal information at any time by logging into your account.
Territorial Seed Company does log your IP address, which is used for customer support, system administration and problem solving on the website.
Territorial Seed Company does not rent its mailing list. However, we do on occasion exchange names with other responsible gardening companies. If you do not want to receive other gardening catalogs, please let us know by typing Do not exchange in the notes field when you place an order.
Thanks for signing up for our weekly newsletter!

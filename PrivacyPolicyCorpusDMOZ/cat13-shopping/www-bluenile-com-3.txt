About Paypal
Blue Nile has partnered with PayPal to offer this method of payment for orders to the United States and Canada.
What is PayPal?
PayPal allows you to:
Pay from multiple funding sources, including PayPal balance, bank accounts and credit cards
Pay merchants without having to share your financial information with them
Shop with confidence knowing that PayPal protects you from unauthorized transactions sent from your account
For more information on PayPal, please visit www.paypal.com
What orders cannot be completed with PayPal?
PayPal is not available at this time for the following:
Orders totaling more than $15,000
Orders that use a Gift Certificate as an additional form of payment
Using PayPal
If your order is eligible for payment via PayPal, the PayPal button will display below our "proceed to checkout" button in the shopping basket. After selecting PayPal you will be directed to log in to your pre-existing PayPal account or be directed to create a new account. After logging in you will select your preferred billing, shipping and payment options; upon completion you will be prompted to provide consent for Blue Nile to charge your PayPal account. After consenting you will be directed back to Blue Nile to complete your order. Once complete you will receive a confirmation email from Blue Nile. If you have any questions please feel free to contact us at 1-800-242-2728.

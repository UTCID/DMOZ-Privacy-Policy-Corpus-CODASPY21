Privacy policy
What information do we collect? How will it be used?
When you order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing & shipping address(es), telephone #, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and to notify you of your order status
You are ordering over a secure web site and your personal information will not be shared or sold to any other agency or individual
How do we protect customer information?
When you place orders or access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server. All customer information entered is protected against unauthorized access.
Will your information be disclosed to outside parties?
Absolutely NOT! Revolution Tea does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable agencies, but these statistics do not contain any information, which may be used to personally identify our customers. We may release account information, in good faith, that such release is reasonably necessary to (i) comply with law, (ii) enforce or apply the terms of any of our user agreements, or (iii) protect the rights, property or safety of our company, our users, or others.
How do we allow customer(s) to update or change their account information?
You may update or change your account profile at any time by entering our Customer Service section of the site and selecting Log In. Follow the simple step-by-step instructions to orchestrate any changes.

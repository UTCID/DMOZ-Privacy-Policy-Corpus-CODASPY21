1. INTRODUCTION
Cookies are small data files that may be stored on your computer (or other internet enabled devices, such as a smartphone or tablet) when you visit our website.
These cookies help our website remember information about your visit, like your country, language and other settings. They can also help make your next visit easier.
At Jaeger Retail Ltd we use cookies and other similar technologies such as tags for several purposes as described more fully below, including notably: To remember you and select all of the services and settings you may have selected during a prior visit;
To maintain settings through the shopping basket, to allow you to checkout
To allow us to profile you in an anonymised way for post site visits and marketing messages

3. OPTING OUT AND DISABLING COOKIES
Most browsers have cookies enabled by default. However if you don't want to enable cookies, you'll still be able to browse the site.
You can enable, disable and delete cookies through your browser. If you choose to disable cookies, our website may not operate correctly and you may not be able to access secure areas of our website or make purchases. If you choose to delete your cookies, you may have to update your preferences with us again and the experience you receive upon returning to our site for the first time may be slightly degraded until our cookies update with your preferences again.

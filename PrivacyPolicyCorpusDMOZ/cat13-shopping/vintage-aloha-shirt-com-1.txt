Privacy Notice
Privacy
Vintage-Aloha-Shirts.com will maintain the confidentiality of your personal information and it will be used only to support your customer relationship with us. We intend to only send e-mail to customers or to individuals whom we believe have chosen to receive such e-mails and for order confirmation only. At any time you have the right to opt out of receiving future communications. And our website offers robust encryption technology to protect the transmission of customer information over the Internet.
Security
Multiple safeguards, and data security and encryption technologies are in place to protect customer credit card and personal information. It is the policy of Vintage-Aloha-Shirts.com to cooperate fully with state and federal authorities in matters of suspected fraud. Any offenders will be prosecuted to the fullest extent of the law.

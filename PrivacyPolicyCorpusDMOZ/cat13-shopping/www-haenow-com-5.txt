Privacy Policy
We take your privacy and security seriously. HAE Now does not rent, sell, or share your personal information with other companies or individuals.
Online Security
To ensure your safety, we use encryption software conforming to the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol to safeguard all your company’s sensitive information entered on our website. We use a secure server (https) and an advanced encryption scheme to protect sensitive information being transmitted online. You will see the secured transaction symbol (a secured padlock) at the bottom of your web browser on pages that ask for sensitive info. This means that the link between your web browser and our server is secure, and all information is being transmitted using a high-grade encryption (AES-256 256bit). Credit card information is not stored on the HAE Now website.

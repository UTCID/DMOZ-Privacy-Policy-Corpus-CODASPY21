Delivery:
Q. When will my order be delivered?
A. Next day delivery is available on all orders placed before 5:30pm Monday to Thursday where items are in stock.
Order placed before 5:30pm on Friday will be delivered on Monday (excluding bank holidays).
Orders placed after 5:30pm on a Friday, or on Saturday and Sunday, will be delivered on Tuesday.
Some areas, such as the Highlands and Northern Ireland can not be serviced on a next day delivery, delivery. Delivery to these areas will take 2-3 days.
Q. What time will my order be delivered?
A. Our courier service delivers between the hours of 8.30am and 6pm, Monday to Friday. We cannot state a specific time that your order will be delivered.
Q. What happens if my order isn't delivered on time?
A. If an order you've been expecting has not arrived yet, then please log into the My Account section and select Order Tracking. The most up-to-date information we have will be displayed here.
If you still have questions concerning your order, please drop us an email at sales@monkeyoffice.co.uk , call us on 0845 258 1205 or request a call back via our Contact Us page.
Q. What are the delivery options and charges?
A. All orders over £30.00 + vat qualify for FREE delivery.
If your order is under £30.00 + vat, then delivery will cost £3.95 + vat.
All in-stock items ordered before 5:30pm will be delivered the next working day.
(see 'When will my order be delivered?' for details).
Q. Do you deliver goods internationally/abroad?
A. Unfortunately we do not deliver outside of the United Kingdom.
We do, however, deliver to all mainland and off-shore UK locations (except Jersey & Guernsey) and Northern Ireland.
Ordering:
Q. What happens if I forget my order confirmation number?
A. If you've forgotten your order confirmation number, don't panic. We will have sent you an order confirmation email with your order number included.
If you do not have an order confirmation email for any reason, you can log onto My Account and select the Order History option. Here you'll find a record of the orders you've placed with us and their order confirmation numbers.
Q. How do I check the status of my order?
A. You can Track and Trace your order status live online. To view your order status, log onto the My Account section and select Order Tracking.
Q. My order states that it's been shipped, but it hasn't been delivered. What do I do now?
A. We aim to deliver all orders the next working day. If you haven't received your order by 6pm on the date of delivery, then please Contact Us and we will endeavour to track down your goods.
Payment:
Q. What are the payment options?
A. We accept MasterCard, Visa Debit, Visa Credit, Delta, and American Express.
Q. Is my payment secure?
A. Here at MonkeyOffice.co.uk we recognise the importance of using a secure website. We are pleased to provide our customers with the reassurance of knowing that our entire site is covered by the highly recognised VeriSign SSL certificate and McAfee Secure.
Q. What happens to my order if my Internet is suddenly/accidentally disconnected?
A. If your internet connection is lost during the ordering process, don't worry. Your items will be still be available in your shopping basket when you are able to return to our website. This assumes that you were logged into MonkeyOffice.co.uk at the time or that your browser accepts cookies.
Q. Can I place an order over the phone?
A. Yes, we accept telephone orders.
Returns:
Q. How do I return goods?
A. We understand that from time-to-time mistakes are made and as such we pride ourselves on a no fuss, no hassle returns procedure. Should you change your mind about purchasing an item and you wish to return it, you must notify our Customer Service within 7 working days of receipt of your goods. The item must be returned in the same condition as you received it. Products that are damaged or used will not be credited.
To organise a return simply call a member of our Customer Service Team on 0845 258 1205 or complete the Call Me Back form.
Q. How can I exchange a product?
A. We are currently unable to provide an exchange service. However, if you have changed your mind and would like to purchase another product, please Contact Us within 7 working days to organise a return and then purchase the preferred product via MonkeyOffice.co.uk as you normally would.
Q. I have received a faulty or damaged item, what do I do next?
A. Should an item be damaged or faulty, please accept our apologies and report it to our Customer Service Team within 3 working days of receiving the delivery.
With the exception of Rexel and GBC Business Machines, the warranty for the first 6 months lies Monkey Office. The remainder of the warranty period lies with the manufacturer. For further assistance please contact us by calling 0845 258 1205 or emailing us at sales@monkeyoffice.co.uk
Q. I have received an incorrect item or an unordered item, what do I do next?
A. If you've received an item that you've not ordered or the item is incorrect, we apologise.
If this occurs, please let us know within 3 working days of receipt of your goods, any picking errors can't be rectified after this time.
My Account:
Q. Why can't I log in?
A. If you're having difficulties logging in to MonkeyOffice.co.uk here are a few options to help.
• Close your Internet browser, re-start and press Ctrl + F5 on your keyboard, this will clear your browser for a fresh start.
• If you've forgotten your password, click here to get a new password emailed to you.
• Still having problems then contact our customer service department on 0845 258 1205.
Q. What happens if I've forgotten my password or username?
A. If you've forgotten your password, please go to the Forgotten Login Details? page and enter your username and register email address, we will then issue you a new password shortly.
However if you've forgotten your username, please Contact Us and we'll be happy to confirm you login details.
Other:
Q. Who can I contact if I am having technical difficulties with your website?
A. If you're experiencing problems with our website, you can contact our helpdesk on 0845 258 1205, Monday to Friday, between 8:30am and 5:30pm.
Q. Who has access to my personal information?
A. We understand that privacy is important to our customers. We abide by the UK and EU rules on Data Protection.
Your information is only used for internal purposes. We collect basic login data (e.g. email address, password, company name) when you register with us. This information is used to identify our customers and to send order confirmations. We collect delivery and billing information (i.e. billing/delivery addresses, phone number) at the Checkout page in order to process your order and to contact you should there be a problem with your order.
We understand that you don't want your personal information being passed to any third parties. We will not sell, trade or rent your information in any way to a third party. We only provide your information to a third party if it is necessary for processing or delivering your order, or for executing marketing programmes on our behalf. They are also required to keep your information protected.
By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of the personal information you provide. If we decide to change our privacy policy in any way, we will notify you via this page, so that you're aware of the information we collect, how we use it and the circumstances under which we would disclose it.
If you've any further queries regarding privacy, please contact our customer service team via the Contact Us link on our website.
Q. I can't find the answer to my specific question, what can I do now?
A. If you still have questions, then please contact one of our friendly Monkey Office advisors on 0845 258 1205, email us at sales@monkeyoffice.co.uk or why not let us pay for the call by requesting a Call Back?

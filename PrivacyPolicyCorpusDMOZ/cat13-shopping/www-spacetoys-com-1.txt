Space Toys Return/Cancellation Policies
You must contact SpaceToys.com to get an RMA (returned merchandise authorization) number before returning any item. Any item(s) received without an RMA will be refused.Defective returns are eligible for replacement or merchandise credit only.Defective returns are eligible for return only within 30 days of sale to the retail consumer. After 30 days, the consumer must return the product to the manufacturer's authorized warranty station for warranty repair. Under any circumstance, defective returns will not be accepted after 180 days from SpaceToys.com invoice date or within 30 days of retail sale, whichever is shorter.A copy of the Space Toys E-mail invoice must be included with all defective returns. All items must be returned complete and with all original parts/documentation. Incomplete returns will incur a 20% manufacturer/refurbishing fee.Items showing installer or consumer abuse will not be accepted.We do not accept returns or exchanges on preordered items.Products must be returned unopened and in saleable conditionReturns will be credited at the price paid on the E-mail invoice or current selling price, whichever is lower, less any restocking fee.We do not accept returns on custom made items. In these cases all sales are final.Space Toys is not responsible for late items or any charges created by delay in delivery.Non-defective products may be returned at Space Toys's discretion and will be credited at the current Space Toys selling price less a 20% restocking fee and shipping. No returns will be accepted after 30 days.Non-defective returns will only be accepted for items featured for sale in current Space Toys publications.Space Toys will only accept products purchased from Space Toys.Sales of closeout items are final. Only defective products may be returned for credit as per normal Space Toys policies.All returns must be shipped freight prepaid.Items designed for consumer use but used commercially are not eligible for return.Allow 10 business days for processing returns.Credit will not be issued until Space Toys has received and examined the returned merchandise. Damage/LossAll claims for damage/pilferage must be filed with delivering carrier within 15 days of receipt. Space Toys cannot file this for you.Save all boxes & packing material!All claims for shortages/mis-shipments/ mis-billings must be made within 10 days of receipt.If you choose for international items to ship via USPS.com we will not assume any responsibility for the item on the delivery time frame or condition of the delivered item.If the cancellation is requested after an item has been shipped. You may be charged a 10% credit card/paypal processing fee. When cancelling a space suit order you may also be charged a 10% credit card/paypal fee depending on where the suit is in the production process. We will be let you know the updated ship times for the suits, but please be aware we cannot guarentee delivery dates for items shipped USPS.com.

BlindsOnSale.com Privacy Policy
The privacy of your data — and it is your data, not ours! — is a big deal to us. When you purchase from BlindsOnSale.com, we ask for your name, contact, and payment information, so that we can send you your order. We'll never sell your personal info to third parties.
Encryption
All data is encrypted via SSL/TLS when transmitted between our servers and your browser.
Credit card details
We do not store your credit card details or handle them directly. Instead, we use a secure service called Braintree (a PayPal company), to process credit card payments. So even in the unlikely event that our servers are compromised, your credit card would not be.

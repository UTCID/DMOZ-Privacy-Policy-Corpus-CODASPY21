Privacy Policy
Thank you for visiting our web site.
Please read this privacy policy before using the site or submitting any personal information. By using the site, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy. These practices may be changed, but any changes will be posted and changes will only apply to activities and information on a going forward, not retroactive basis.
Please note that the privacy practices set forth in this privacy policy are for this web site only. If you link to other web sites using any of the hyperlinks on our site, please review the privacy policies posted at those sites.Information
Minimus is the sole owner of the information collected on this website. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement. We collect personally identifiable information, like names, postal addresses, email addresses, etc., when voluntarily submitted by our visitors.
Minimus does not target registration to, or knowingly collect information directly from, anyone under 13 years of age. Additionally, Minimus will never distribute any personally identifiable information concerning any minor, regardless of the source from which it was obtained.

Account RegistrationOur website allows you the option of registering for an account with us.
This is an optional feature, you are not required to register for an account in order to make purchases from our site.
This information is used to contact you regarding your order and any additional information you may have requested from us.
Additionally, your account access will allow you to check your order information online as well as use other account-related services.OrdersWe request information from you on our order form. Here you must provide contact information (like name, shipping address, email) and financial information (like credit card number, expiration date). This information is used for billing purposes and to fill your orders. If we have trouble processing an order, this contact information is used to get in touch with you.Cookies & IP Addresses
A cookie is a piece of data stored on your hard drive containing information about you. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our website. Once you close your browser, the cookie terminates. If you reject the cookie, you may still use our website. The only drawback to rejecting the cookie is that you may be limited in some areas of our website, such as the ability to retain items in your shopping cart after navigating away from our site. Our website advertises with various other search engines and companies that utilize tracking information on our website to provide us with statistics on the usage of the advertising.
This tracking information is not linked to personally identifiable information.
We may also use IP addresses for analyzing trends, administering the website, tracking user’s movement, and gathering broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.PartnersWe use an outside shipping company to ship orders and a credit card processing company to bill you for goods and services. These companies do not retain, share, store, or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes.Newsletters & Special OffersWhen you place an order with us, you are provided the opportunity to subscribe to our newsletter and to receive special offers as they become available, sent to the email address you have provided.
You may also subscribe to our newsletter and to receive special offers without placing an order by providing us with your email address in the area marked “Join our mailing lists” on our home page.Email to a FriendIf you use our referral program to inform a friend about our website, we ask you for the friend’s name and email address. Minimus will automatically send the friend a one-time email containing the information that you requested to be sent and a link to our website.
Minimus stores this information for the sole purpose of sending this one-time per request email.SecurityYour personally identifiable information is kept secure both online and offline. When our registration/order form asks you to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number and/or social security number) that information is encrypted and is encrypted using SSL security.
To verify our security or for more information regarding how SSL encrypts and secures your personally identifiable information, click on the Thawte certificate seal at the bottom of our home page.
Offline, visitor and customer information is accessible only by authorized employees, agents and contractors (who have agreed to keep information secure and confidential).Fraud Prevention & InvestigationsWe may share information with governmental agencies or other companies assisting us in fraud prevention or investigation. Policy
GDPR is a regulation in European Union (EU) law on data protection and privacy for individuals within the EU and the European Economic Area (EEA). This policy was created to support our compliance with the law and applies to all EU/EEA Client’s customers.

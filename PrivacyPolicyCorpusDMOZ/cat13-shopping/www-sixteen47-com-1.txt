is used and shared online. We respect and value the privacy of everyone who visits this website, sixteen47.com

that is consistent with Our obligations and your rights under the law.

of Our Privacy Policy is deemed to occur upon your first use of Our Site. You are required to accept this Privacy
Policy when signing up for an Account. The new regulations state that if you do not accept our Privacy Policy,
you will not be able to use Our Site.

We do not pay for return postage.
We try and be as fair as we can and welcome feedback on our policy.
Our postage is a flat rate, whatever the size of your parcel.

EU £25.00

Rest of World £45.00
Our postage cost covers the cost and ensures that staff who handle your order can be properly paid.

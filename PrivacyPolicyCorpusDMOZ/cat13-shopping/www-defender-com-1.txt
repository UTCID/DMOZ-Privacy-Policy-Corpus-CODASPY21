When you visit our websites, our web servers automatically recognize only the IP address (where possible), but not your e-mail address.

The information we collect is used for internal review to improve the content and functionality of our websites, is then discarded, and is not shared with any other organizations.

Cookies
We use cookies to store visitor preferences, record session information, such as items that consumers add to their shopping cart, and to record user-specific information on what pages users access or visit.
Cookies provide a convenience feature to save you time, or tell the web servers that you have returned to a specific page.
For example, if you personalize pages on our websites, or register for the Subscription Service, a cookie helps recall your specific information on subsequent visits.
When you return to the same website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the customized features.
Cookies set by our website are called "first-party cookies".
Cookies set by parties other than our website are called "third-party cookies".
Third-party cookies enable third-party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website, like analytics.
Defender does not participate with any advertising networks, and does not use clear gifs, web beacons, web bugs or flash cookies.
You can access and browse our website without enabling cookies, but you will not be able to keep items in your shopping cart.
The functionality of the website will be limited without cookies enabled.

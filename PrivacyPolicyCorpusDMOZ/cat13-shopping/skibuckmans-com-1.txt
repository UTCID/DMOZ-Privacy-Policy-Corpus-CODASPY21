Cookies
Buckmans.com uses a feature of your Internet Web browser called a "cookie" to assign a unique identification to your computer. Cookies are files that your Web browser places on your computer's hard drive. Buckmans.com creates and uses these cookies in order
to maintain your browsing session and preferences, as well as to allow the website to remember which shopping cart in our system belongs to you.
We only uses cookies to keep track of items you put into your shopping cart, to tell us whether you have visited Buckmans.com previously, and to help us determine whether you came to Buckmans.com from a particular Internet link or banner advertisement.
Cookies cannot be used by Buckmans.com to retrieve any personal information about you unless you have previously purchased from or registered with Buckmans.com. Buckmans.com does not and cannot use cookies to retrieve personal information about you from your computer unless such information was knowingly and willingly provided by you.

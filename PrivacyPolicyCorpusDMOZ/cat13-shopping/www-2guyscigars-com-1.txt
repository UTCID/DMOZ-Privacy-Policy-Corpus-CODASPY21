Privacy statement
2GuysCigars.com
has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
The following discloses the information gathering and
dissemination practices for this Web
site:
2GuysCigars.com
Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our
server and to administer our Web site. We also use the IP
address to run demographic statistic reports so we may improve
our Web Site.
Cookies
Our Site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart and to auto fill forms with information of frequent customers.
Registration Forms
Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact
information (like their name, email, and postal
address). Contact information from the registration forms is
used to ship purchases, information about our company. Users may
opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out
section below. This information is private and will not be sold,
shared or otherwise distributed in any manner.
Order Forms
Our site uses an order form for customers to request
information, products, and services. We collect contact
information (like their email address), and financial
information (like their account or credit card
numbers). Contact information from the order forms is used to
ship purchases, information about our company. Users may
opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out
section below. Financial information that is collected is used
to bill the user for products and services.
External Links
This site contains links to other sites.
Two Guys Smoke
Shop
is not responsible for the privacy practices or the
content of such Web sites.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss,
misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All
order are placed using an SSL certificate issues by Thawte.
Opt-In Choice/Opt-Out
All of our email campaigns are sent to email addresses that
have opted to receive them. We use a double opt-in system
which insures that you verify that you want to receive email
offers from us. There is no possibility that someone can
subscribe you without your permission.
We also provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving
promotional/marketing information from us. Every one of
our emails contains links that will automatically remove you
from our mailing list. Please check for these links on the
bottom of any promotional email we send out. If you prefer,
you can call the following telephone
number: 603 898-5322 and request that you be removed from the
list
Personal Privacy
All information gathered in our forms is private and will not be
sold, shared or distributed in any manner.

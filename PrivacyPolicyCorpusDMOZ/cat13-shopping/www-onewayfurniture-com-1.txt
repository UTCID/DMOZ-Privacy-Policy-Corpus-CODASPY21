Room Divider Screens: Folding Privacy Panels In Wrought Iron, Wood & FabricThe perfect way to section off a small part of a room or office is to place a beautiful folding screen room divider. Without being intrusive or unattractive, you can portion off any area with our gorgeous 3 or 4 panel room dividers. We have a variety of modern styles to choose from like our Oriental screens, Shoji hand-painted dividers and wrought iron and metal partitions. For those that appreciate fabrics you'll find really neat dividing curtains that slide back and forth. When you need that extra space just fold it up and store it away. For years studio apartments and movie directors shooting films have utilized privacy panels and screens to conserve space.

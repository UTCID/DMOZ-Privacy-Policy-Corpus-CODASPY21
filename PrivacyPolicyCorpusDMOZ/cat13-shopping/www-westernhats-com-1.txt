Privacy Policy
West Texas Western Store has been serving customers since 1973. We are a locally owned and operated business in Amarillo and Canyon in the Texas Panhandle. Our goal is to serve our customers with the best customer service possible. Your privacy is very important to us!
Privacy Notice
West Texas Western Store is providing this privacy notice explaining our online information practices.
Data about your activity while here on our site is automatically provided to West Texas Western Store by our Web server which only recognizes your domain name. We only have access to your domain name, what pages consumers access or visit, how many visits we get. This information is only used for internal review in non personally-identifiable form.
Email addresses and other personal information is known to West Texas Western Store only when you voluntarily submit it on our secure order form or if you post a message to us via email.

Privacy Policy
Pangea Reptile LLC Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy.
Any and all information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, disclosed to third parties or reused without your permission. Any information you give to us will be held with care and will not be used in any way other than processing your order.
We use the highest level of security software available, including SSL ordering and checkout pages, data encryption, and we NEVER store your credit card information.
We are GDPR compliant. Should you ask us to delete your account and any personally identifiable information we will do so immediately and without question. Please contact us if you would like to have your information removed permanently.
Thanks for Choosing Pangea!!!
If you have any questions about this Privacy Statement or concerns about the way we process your Personal Data, please contact us.

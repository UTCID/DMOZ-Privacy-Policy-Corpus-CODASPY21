Privacy Policy
Yukon Fitness respects and is committed to protecting your privacy. if you are purchasing online from Yukon Fitness, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card information (including number and expiration date). This information allows us to process and fulfill your order and notify you of your order status. Credit Card information will never be shared or sold to third parties for any purpose whatsoever. Yukon Fitness' "Check Out" uses a shopping cart feature that safeguards your information by way of a SSL (Secured Socket Layer) encrypted servers. SSL encodes your information, when you transfer it to the server, rendering it unreadable to anyone trying to intercept your information. Thank you for shopping at Yukon-fitness.com We appreciate your business and are always committed to satisfying our customers.

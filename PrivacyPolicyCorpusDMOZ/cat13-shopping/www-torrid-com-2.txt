We hope you love what you've ordered, but we know that sometimes things don’t work out:
Initiate your return within 90 days of the purchase date on your receipt or packing slip.
If you want to replace an item in a different size or color, we recommend processing a purchase quickly as items do sell out.
Damaged or missing items? Contact Customer Service for assistance.
Note that a $6.00 return shipping fee will be deducted from your total return amount.
Please allow 1-2 weeks for processing returns.
Note that Klarna, PayPal, Apple Pay or Google Pay purchases can only be returned by mail and not in stores.
Temporary Return Policy
We are starting to open our stores back up. Due to the shipping and receiving delays at our distribution center, we strongly advise that you return items to a local store once we reopen in your area. We have extended our return policy and will gladly accept your returns for up to 30 days after we open our stores. You can check the status of your local Torrid with our Store Locator.
If you need to ship your return by mail, please note: we’ve adopted new practices to protect our employees. Our online return policy is temporarily adjusted to 90 days from the purchase date for all existing and new orders.
What's Not Returnable?
Items not purchased at Torrid.com or a Torrid Store (e.g., a third-party reseller, an auction site, etc.).
Items without a Torrid Store receipt or a Torrid packing slip.
Items with removed tags or items missing their original packaging (e.g., shoe boxes).
Clearance items or items marked as “Final Sale”.
For hygienic reasons, these items are “Final Sale”:
Washed or worn items
Accessories: earrings and cosmetics
Intimates: panties and lingerie sets
Clothing: tights
Other similar intimate items

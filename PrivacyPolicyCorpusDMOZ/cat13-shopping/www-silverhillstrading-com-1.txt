Here at Silver Hills Trading Company we respect your privacy.  In the hustle and bustle of this busy world privacy is an important commodity.  Any and all information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, disclosed to third parties or reused without your permission.  Any information you give us will be held with care and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.

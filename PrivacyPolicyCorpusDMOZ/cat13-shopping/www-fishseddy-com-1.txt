Thanks for shopping at FishsEddy.com! We take your privacy seriously and have written our Privacy Policy to detail the information we collect and how we use it. If you have any questions, please email info@fishseddy.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
Collected Information
FishsEddy.com collects the information you provide when registering on our site, placing an order, subscribing to a newsletter, or filling out a form. The information you may be asked to provide includes your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, credit card information, or other details to help you with your experience.
The information we collect may be used for the purposes of:
Processing your orders and completing transactions
Updating you on your order status via email
Administering our site
Responding better to customer service requests
Notifying you of products or special offers that may be of interest (only if you opt into our mailing list)
We never store account passwords or credit card information anywhere on our site or servers.
Our Email List
We only want to send you emails if you want them - if you sign up for our email list, you will receive emails about our newest products and special offers. The emails that we collect are for keeping you up to date about what’s happening at Fishs Eddy, and we do not share or sell the email addresses with anyone else. If you would like to unsubscribe from our email list, please click here.
Secure Shopping
To keep your information protected, all sensitive/credit information you supply is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. FishsEddy.com features an SSL certificate that uses industry standard 256-bit encryption technology. (This is the same level of encryption used by large banks to keep your information secure.)
All transactions are processed through a gateway provider and are not stored or processed on our servers.
Cookies
Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your web browser (if you allow it) that enables site’s or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.
FishsEddy.com uses cookies to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart. They are also used to help us understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity, which enables us to provide you with improved services. We also use cookies to help us compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.
You can choose to have your computer warn you each time a cookie is being sent, or you can choose to turn off all cookies through your browser settings. Each browser is a little different, so look at your browsers Help Menu for more information.

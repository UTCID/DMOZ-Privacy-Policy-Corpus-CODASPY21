Privacy information
We guarantee that all information received by us on our customers is strictly confidential. The only customer information we store is the name, address, e-mail, telephone number, fax number and/or web site details and used for general information purposes.
Customer information will never be passed on to any third parties. Data is securely maintained on our database which is password protected and access controlled.

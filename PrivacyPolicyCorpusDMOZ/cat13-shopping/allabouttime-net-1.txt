Privacy/Security Policy
At All About Time we understand your concerns about security and privacy. Statistically, it is much safer to use your credit card on the Web than it is to use it in a restaurant or store. We process your credit card information on a secure server (equipped with a 256-bit SSL certificate). This means that all information given is sent encrypted during transit to prevent any unauthorized party from intercepting your card number.
All About Time does track some information while visitors navigate the site. None of the information tracked can be used to identify you personally, nor is it ever sold or distributed to any 3rd parties. The following information is logged and analyzed for the purpose of enhancing the site for visitors using cookies:
The type of browser and operating system used to access our site.
The date and time you access our site.
The pages you visit in the allabouttime.net domain.
If you linked to our web site from another web site, the address of that web site.
Search terms used in search engines if applicable.
In addition, cookies are used for store administration, such as items that have been added to the cart.
If you have any questions or concerns regarding the information you provide All About Time please contact us toll free at 440-234-4027.

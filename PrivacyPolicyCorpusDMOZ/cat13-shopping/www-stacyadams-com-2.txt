STACY ADAMS PRIVACY POLICY
THIS PRIVACY POLICY DESCRIBES:
The categories of information Stacy Adams collects when you provide us information or use the Site.
The sources from which we collect personal information.
The purposes for which we collect personal information.
The categories of persons with whom we may share personal information.
How we place digital advertising.
I.	CATEGORIES OF PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT.
When you use the Site and when you place an order, we collect or receive personal information.
We describe those categories of personal information in more detail below.
1.1	Contact and Other Information Which Can Be Used to Identify You..
Stacy Adams collects information that you provide to us or one of our service providers when you use the Site.
We may collect your name, email address, IP address, mailing address, billing address, shipping address, telephone number, birthday month, account user ID or account password.
We may also collect the name and address of the recipient of a gift card you purchase. If you choose to create a Stacy Adams account or order products from the Site, you must provide your name and email address.
You may choose to submit your birthday month when you create a Stacy Adams account.
1.2	Commercial Information.
We collect information when you communicate with us about products, when you consider or place an order for our products or gift cards, when you place an item in your shopping cart, when you engage in Live Chats with us, when you call our call center and when you make on-line or in store purchases. We also maintain a record of your purchases.
1.3	Financial Information.
When you order Stacy Adams products from the Site, you will need to provide certain Financial Information to one of our third-party payment processers, Authorize.net, PayPal, Afterpay or Amazon Pay.
"Financial Information" includes your credit or debit card numbers, your PayPal or Amazon Pay account information, expiration dates and security codes.
Stacy Adams does not directly access, handle, or store your Financial Information.
However, Stacy Adams will receive certain personal information, which you provide to our Payment Processor, Authorize.net, PayPal, Afterpay, or Amazon Pay as necessary to finalize your order such as your name, address, mailing address, billing address, phone number and email address.
We will maintain a record of the purchases that you make when ordering from our Site.
1.4 Information Related to Your Use of our Site.
Stacy Adams may collect information regarding your interactions with and use of the Site. That information includes your electronic device identifier, hardware information, operating system version, browser type and version, browser language, media access control (MAC), screen resolution, cookie information, system type, search history on our website, access times, and referring website URLs. We also collect information when you use the store locator on our Site.
2.	THE SOURCES FROM WHICH WE COLLECT PERSONAL INFORMATION
We may collect personal information from the following sources:
2.1 Communications with You.
We collect personal information from you when you communicate with us by e-mail, regular mail or phone.
2.2	Use of Our Site.
We collect personal information you provide when you use the Site. For instance, at the end of the Site, we invite you to provide us with information so that we may contact you regarding your visit to the Site.
We collect personal information when you sign up for previews, offers, promotions and other electronic communications regarding our products.
We also collect personal information when you provide ratings and feedback regarding products and the Site.
2.3	Electronic Communications.
If you sign up to receive electronic communications, you consent to our sending such electronic communications to you using the email address you provide.
If you no longer want to receive electronic communications, you should use the “unsubscribe” button found on the emails you received or contact Stacy Adams Customer Service, and request to be unsubscribed.
Within ten (10) days of receipt of your unsubscribe request, Stacy Adams will remove you from our distribution list unless you request to be added again in the future.
You may also elect to receive text messages from us. When you sign up to receive text messages, we will send you information about promotional offers and more. To the extent, you voluntarily opt to have SMS notifications sent directly to your mobile phone, we receive and store the information you provide, including your telephone number or when you read a text message. You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by texting “STOP” to our text messages. For more information about text messages, see our Terms and Conditions.
2.4	Your Stacy Adams Account.
We collect personal information when you create and use your Stacy Adams account.
2.5	When You Make a Purchase.
When you place an order on the Site, our service providers who provide payment processing services, PayPal, Amazon Pay or Afterpay, as applicable, will receive your Financial Information Stacy Adams does not receive your Financial Information when you make a purchase.
We do collect personal information required to finalize your order, such as your name, shipping address, telephone number, email address, and billing address.
We do not retain credit or debit card information you provide when you make a purchase in our stores or over the phone.
2.6	Gift Cards.
When you purchase a gift card through the Site, we collect any messages and other information that you may provide, such as the name and address of the gift card recipient.
Gift card purchases are subject to the terms of our gift card program.
2.7	On-Line Sweepstakes, Contests or Other Promotions.
When you choose to participate in our online sweepstakes, contests, or other promotions, you will need to submit certain personal information to enter, to receive any prize awarded and as otherwise specified in any official rules or other entry guidelines.
We may combine the personal information we receive when you enter our sweepstakes, contests, or other promotions with the information in your Stacy Adams account. We may also ask whether you wish to receive information about Stacy Adams promotions and products and you may opt-in to receive this information by checking the appropriate opt-in box.
If you choose to opt-in to receive communications, we will collect personal information and will use that personal information to send you information via email or regular mail about Stacy Adams promotions and products.
2.8	Live Chat.
We collect certain personal information when you use the Live Chat feature on the Site.
We collect such information from the transcript of that communication and other information that you may submit or input using that feature.
If you use a chat feature while signed into your Stacy Adams account, we may link the submitted information to your account.
2.9	Store Locator.
Stacy Adams uses the Google Maps API(s) as part of its "store locator" feature.
Pursuant to the Google Maps APIs Terms of Service, use of this location feature is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
For information on how Google categorizes location information, please visit "Types of location data used by Google."
Both the Google Maps APIs Terms of Service and Google’s Privacy Policy are incorporated into this Privacy Policy by reference.
2.10	Social Media Platforms.
We collect and store personal information when you post on our social media accounts, and we use personal information to serve marketing messages via those social media channels.
2.11	Use of Cookies.
We collect certain information using a variety of tools including, but not limited to, cookies, pixel tags/web beacons, search boxes, web forms and fields, clear GIFs and other technologies/methods.
If you turn off certain tools, you may not have access to features that make your experience on our Site more efficient and some of our features may not function properly.
(a)	Cookies. The Site may automatically use cookies to track your use of the Site.
We use cookies to facilitate and enhance your user experience on the Site and for other purposes described below.
"Cookies" are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer, tablet or cell phone device’s hard drive by your browser.
The Site may use both Cookies that we implement and Cookies implemented by our suppliers and other third parties.
We use Cookies to track how you access and use the Site, to learn when and how users visit the Site, to learn how frequently visitors view certain Site pages, to learn which search terms are used to find the Site, and to learn which websites direct you to the Site.
We also use Cookies to help display certain information on the Site and to improve your enjoyment of the Site, for example, by remembering your contact and other information when you access or use the Site.
Most browsers automatically accept Cookies.
You can disable this function, but disabling Cookies may impact your use and enjoyment of the Site.
(b)	Use of Cookies by Others. Our service providers may use Cookies and other tracking technologies, such as pixel tags, to track Site visitors across the Internet to understand how you get to the Site.
We do not have access or control over these Cookies and this Privacy Policy does not cover the use of Cookies placed by others.
(c)	"DO NOT TRACK" Signals. Some browsers have a “do not track” feature that allows you to tell a website that you do not want to have your online activities tracked.
Our Site does not support "do not track" requests.

We collect personal information for the following purposes:

We use your personal information to respond to your requests or questions.

(b)	Right to Request Deletion. You have a right to request that we delete personal information we collected from you by submitting a verifiable request following the instructions in 15.2 below. We will comply with such request, and direct our service providers to do the same, subject to certain exceptions permitted by applicable law.
(c)
Right to Opt-Out of the Sale of Personal information. Under California law, consumers have the right to opt-out of the Sale of their personal information. However, we do not sell your personal information.
(d)	Right to Non-Discrimination. You have the right to non-discrimination for the exercise of your consumer Privacy Rights under the CCPA.

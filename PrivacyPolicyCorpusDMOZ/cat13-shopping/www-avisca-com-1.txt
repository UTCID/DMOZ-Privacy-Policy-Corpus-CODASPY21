PRIVACY POLICYProtecting your privacy is important to Avisca.com. We promise to keep your personal information confidential. The following statement describes how we gather and use personal information about you.Why we collect personal informationAvisca.com collects personal information you enter on our Web site or give us in any other way. We use the information that you provide (your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, credit card information) to process orders. In addition, we use your personal information to keep you up to date on new items, announcements, special offers, and other information we think you'd like to hear about. You may opt-out of receiving future communications at any time by emailing us at contact@avisca.com.Avisca.com will not rent, sell, nor share your name, address, email address, credit card information or personal information to any third party without your permission.How we collect personal informationWe collect information about you in a few ways. When you purchase from us, give us your contact information when you correspond with us, request to join our mailing list, or register for any contests or promotions, we store the information you provide in our secure database.Like many web sites, we use "cookie" technology to make web shopping easier for you. Avisca.com does not share your personal information with those web sites and is not responsible for their privacy practices.

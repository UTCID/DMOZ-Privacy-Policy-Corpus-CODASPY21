Accessibility Policy
Boot Barn® is firmly committed to compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act. As part of this commitment, we are continually improving our website – www.bootbarn.com – to ensure that it is as accessible as possible. If you have any questions about the accessibility of the website, or if you have suggestions about improving the usability or accessibility of the website, please forward them to custserv@bootbarn.com.
If you are having trouble accessing www.bootbarn.com, or if you have any questions about our site or accessibility policy in general, please call Customer Support at 1-888-440-2668 or e-mail us at custserv@bootbarn.com

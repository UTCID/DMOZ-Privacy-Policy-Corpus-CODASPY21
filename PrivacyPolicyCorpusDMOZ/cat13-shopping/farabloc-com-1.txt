Our Privacy Policy
We value your business and respect your privacy. We do not rent or sell information about you to any outside individuals or organizations. We collect only minimal information that is essential to provide outstanding service. All information you give us is kept secure and is not released to others. The privacy standards of Farabloc conform to the British Columbia Personal Information Protection Act (BC PIPA) .
Secure purchasing
Our secure server encrypts all payment information you send us, including your name, address, credit card number and its expiration date. The encryption process (secure socket layer or SSL) converts all this information into code that is securely transmitted over the Internet.
Using “Cookies” for secure purchasing
When you visit the Farabloc website, we place a text file called a “cookie” in the browser directory of your computer’s hard drive. A cookie is a small piece of information that a website can store on your web browser and later retrieve. Cookies enable our website to recognize the information you have consented to give us, such as your credit card information. The cookie cannot be read by any website other than ours. We do NOT use cookies to examine your surfing behavior before or after leaving the Farabloc website. If at any time you believe that Farabloc Development Corporation has not followed the above policy, or if you would like your information to be removed from our system, please contact us at: info@farabloc.com and we will make every reasonable efforts to identify and correct any problem.

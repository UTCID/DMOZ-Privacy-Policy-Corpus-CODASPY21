Your right to privacy is a primary concern to us. Kids Travel Zone does not sell, rent, or loan any of your personally identifiable information to any third party without your consent. When an order is placed or when you register with Kids Travel Zone, we collect basic information including: name, address, email address, phone number and other personal preferences. We use this information to confirm your order and to respond to any questions you may have.
When you purchase products from kidstravelzone.com, we include you in our list to receive email updates about special offers, new products and services. There is a checkbox when registering to not be included to receive email updates. We will only send you email communications that you expect. If for any reason you do not wish to receive email updates, simply contact us via email at office@kidstravelzone.com and type "unsubscribe" in the subject line. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time.
Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit. All data is stored in a highly secure database with state-of-the-art backup and firewall technologies. It is not accessible to any unauthorized parties.
If you have any questions about Kids Travel Zone’s privacy policy or have comments on how we can improve the Kids Travel Zone shopping experience, please contact us at office@kidstravelzone.com. We appreciate hearing from you.

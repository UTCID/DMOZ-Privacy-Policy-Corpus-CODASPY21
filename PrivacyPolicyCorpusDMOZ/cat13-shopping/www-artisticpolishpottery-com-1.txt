Site Security
We
care about your protection and privacy. You can rest assured that your
financial and personally identifiable information are handled with the
utmost care. ArtisticPolishPottery.com protects your order information
through the use of secure socket layer (SSL) technology. SSL encrypts
(scrambles) your order information so that it cannot be read by anyone
other than us. The lock in the lower right corner of the billing
information page indicates that the billing information submitted on
the order form is secure. Financial information is used to obtain
payment for products, services shipping and handling. Your financial
information is not shared with any unaffiliated company.
Artistic
Polish Pottery.com makes no representations of the security of any site
accessed/linked from our website. We have no responsibility or
liability for your visitation to, and the data collection and use
practices of those websites. We encourage you to acquaint yourself with
the privacy protection policies of each company before disclosing any
information.

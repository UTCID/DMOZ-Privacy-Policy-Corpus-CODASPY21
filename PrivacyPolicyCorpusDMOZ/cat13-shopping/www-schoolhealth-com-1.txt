Privacy & Security
At School Health Corporation, your privacy and security is a primary concern.
We have created this privacy and security policy to demonstrate our commitment to protecting the information you provide on our site. Note: The privacy & security practices set forth in this policy are for this web site only. If you link to other web sites from this site, please review the privacy policies posted at those sites.
How will we use the information you provide on the site?
The information you provide on this site will be kept confidential and used only so that we can support you as a customer or to provide a more personalized shopping experience. Information you provide to us will not be given or sold to any 3rd party for use in marketing or solicitation without your consent.
We may, however, share information with governmental agencies or other companies assisting us in fraud prevention or investigation. We may do so when:
Permitted or required by law
Trying to protect against or prevent actual or potential fraud or unauthorized transactions
Investigating fraud which has already taken place. This information is not provided to these companies for marketing purposes.
Is my information secure?
Our site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. All orders are transmitted over secure internet connections using SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption technology. All of your personal information, including your credit card information and sign-in password are stored in encrypted format at all times. Web pages where SSL encryption is activated are typically identified by a lock or key symbol displayed in your browser. Our website, and all your user information, is further protected by a multi-layer firewall based security system.
What other information does your site collect?
In addition to the information you input into our site, our web server also automatically collects other information such as the domain name of the site providing you with internet access, the internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the internet, your browser type and version, operating system and platform, the average time spent on the site, pages viewed, information searched for, access times and other relevant statistics. Our site uses cookies, which are unique user IDs automatically saved to our computer that identifies your computer every time you visit our site. The information collected is used to provide our visitors with a more personalized shopping experience, to deliver content specific to your interests, to communicate special offers and featured items, and for other purposes. You have the option of setting your browser to reject cookies. However, if our cookie is rejected, we will not be able to keep items in your shopping cart and you will not be able to complete your order online.

You can send an email to unsubscribe@schoolhealth.com with unsubscribe in the subject line. Please be sure to email us from the address you want removed.

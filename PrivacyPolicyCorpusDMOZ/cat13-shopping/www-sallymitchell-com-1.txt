(Don't forget to add the Ltd please)Sally Mitchell Fine Arts LtdThornlea,Askham,Newark,Notts,NG22 0RN,England.2. PrivacyWe Do Not sell, loan, rent or share data with any third parties.Any personal information collected by Sally Mitchell Fine Arts is held in the strictest confidence. It is used solely for the purpose of providing you, the customer, with the highest possible level of service, in helping us help you find what you are looking for.We will Not bombard you with hundreds of Emails (SPAM) and or catalogues.We will send you the occasional Email or letter if we genuinely believe that we have a picture, an offer or some news that may interest you.  You may at any time request to be removed from our database by sending and Email to: info@sallymitchell.com please quote your full name and email address or simply click onto the remove button at the bottom of the email-shot you recieve.By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Sally Mitchell Fine Arts. If we make any changes to this privacy policy, they will be posted on this page.Our Registered offices are: Sally Mitchell Fine Arts Ltd Eastcroft Lane Thornlea, Askham, Newark, Notts, NG22 0RN UK Registered in England and Wales: Number 564053

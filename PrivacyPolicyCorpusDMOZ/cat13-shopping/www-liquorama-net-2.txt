Coupon Code Terms
Liquorama Promotional Terms - Coupon Code cannot be used with any other coupons, promos or free shipping.
- Coupon codes good for one-time use only and valid for online orders only. - Coupons valid on select wines only, not valid for spirits orders.- Coupon Code cannot be used to purchase rare vintage wines and rare cellar wine (SKUs that begin with "cellar"). - Promo codes do not apply to items with prices ending in 5 or 7 (i.e. $9.95 or $9.97). Please note if the discount is applied when placing your online order we will manually remove the discount and resend an updated invoice via e-mail. - Coupon Code cannot be used for previous orders or orders in progress. Free Shippings Terms When we are running free shipping promotions the following terms apply:  - Free shipping promos cannot be used in the following states: HI, AL, AK, MS, or UT. - Free shipping promos cannot be used on beer and Bordeaux futures or presales. - Free shipping does not apply to items with prices ending in 5 or 7 (i.e. $9.95 or $9.97). - Free shipping via Fedex Ground only. $5 handling fee is included with all orders.

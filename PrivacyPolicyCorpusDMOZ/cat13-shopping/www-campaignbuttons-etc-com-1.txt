Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy:
Any customer placing an order with campaignbutton-etc.com can be assured any personal information provided will not be used for any purpose other than completing a sales transaction and shipping the item(s) to the purchaser.   We will not ever trade, sell, give away, bargain, barter or otherwise exchange this information with any other persons, parties, companies or entities.
campaignbutton-etc.com does not keep your credit card information on file.
campaignbutton-etc.com makes every attempt to treat your personal data the way we would expect to have ours treated.
campaignbutton-etc.com does collect the e-mail addresses of our customers.  Periodically campaignbutton-etc.com will use these e-mail addresses to notify our customers of new merchandise that has been posted and any upcoming sales. This e-mail list will never be sold, given away, bargained, bartered or otherwise exchanged with any other persons, parties, companies or entities.  Customers have the option of being removed from the list by entering “unsubscribe” in any e-mail that they may receive and returning it.
We use no cookies except the temporary ones utilized by our shopping cart which disappear after the purchase.

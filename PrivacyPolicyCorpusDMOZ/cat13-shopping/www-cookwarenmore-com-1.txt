Privacy
Cookware & More is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell or disclose any information that identifies you to a third party. We only use your personal information internally to support and service you, our customer. We may use the information we collect to periodically notify you about new services or special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, you may send an e-mail to customerservice@cookwarenmore.com with 'unsubscribe' as the subject line.Cookware & More does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others.

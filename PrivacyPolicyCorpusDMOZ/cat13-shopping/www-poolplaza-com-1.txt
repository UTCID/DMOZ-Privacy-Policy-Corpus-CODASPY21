Privacy Policy
Our Privacy and Security Pledge to You...
We are committed to respecting your privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of personally identifiable information you share with us.
In general, you can visit POOLplaza.com without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. That information is only required when you make a purchase.
To protect your privacy, we have adopted the following measures and policies to secure the information you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure:
All of your personal information stays private.
All order information is encrypted and transmitted to us over secure socket connections.
We do not retain your credit card information or store it on any of our computers.
We record your personal information only if you provide it to us when placing an order or expressing an interest in one of our products.
We use financial information only to bill you for products and services that you have purchased.
Once we receive the order, the information is stored on private servers not accessible from the internet.
We do not share this information with anyone.
We use cookies only to assist your shopping.
A "cookie" is a bit of info stored on your computer (not ours) which says "Hi,this is customer #1234".
When you are ready to place an order, the cookie will fill in the form with the address info you provide to us when you registered. This makes ordering much easier.
In short, we use your personal information only to serve you better. We want you to be comfortable contacting us or placing an order with us. We want you to be confident that placing an order
will not compromise your privacy in any way.

SHIPPING
Shipping method and delivery times
Free UPS Ground shipping on all orders in the U.S.
Transit time will vary based on your location and takes between 2 and 5 business days.
UPS 3 Business Day Shipping (not available in AK/HI). Order must be placed by 3pm EST to ship same day.
UPS 2 Business Day Shipping. Order must be placed by 3pm EST to ship same day.
UPS SurePost will be utilized for continental U.S. deliveries to PO boxes.
We only ship to the continental U.S., Alaska and Hawaii. We do not ship internationally. Please also note that we do not ship on public holidays.
Delivery days for UPS shipments are Monday-Friday.
Shipping costs
Eligible orders can be upgraded for expedited delivery:
UPS Ground Shipping - free
UPS 3 Business Day Select - $15 for all orders (not available in Alaska or Hawaii)
UPS 2 Business Day Select - $25 for all orders ($30 to Alaska and Hawaii)
Shipping costs are non-refundable.
Know where your items are at all times!
You will receive an email confirmation as soon as your order ships.
You can check the status of your order by
entering your order number and email address on the order status tracking page,
using the UPS tracking number in your shipping confirmation email,
or by viewing order status in your account - if your order was placed using an existing account.

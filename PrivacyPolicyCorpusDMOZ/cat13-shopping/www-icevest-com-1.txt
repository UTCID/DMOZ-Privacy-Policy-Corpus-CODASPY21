We use tracking information to determine which areas of our sites users like and don’t like based on traffic to those areas. We do not track what individual users read, but rather how well each page performs  overall. This helps us continue to build a better service for you.COOKIES We may place a text file called a "cookie" in the browser files of  your computer.

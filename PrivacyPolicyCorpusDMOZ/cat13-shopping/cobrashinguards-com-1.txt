Billing Questions
Do I have to pay sales tax on orders from this site?
Sales tax is charged only on orders shipped to Utah.
What are my payment choices?
You can purchase your Cobra Shinguards via the web using PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, Discover Card or American Express.
Customer Service
What is the phone number for customer service?
Please feel free to call customer service at (844) 49-COBRA (M-F 8am-5pm MST).
Shipping
Do you ship to my country?
Currently we ship Cobra Shinguards worldwide. Call customer service at (844) 49-COBRA to arrange special shipments.
How do I know when my order has been shipped?
All items are shipped via USPS First Class. You will receive an email directly from USPS when you order has been shipped along with a tracking number. Everything is shipped from Bountiful, Utah. Depending on your location in the United States it can take anywhere from 4-7 business days to be delivered for most orders. Orders for the east coast generally take longer to arrive than orders for the west coast.
Once my order has shipped, how long will it take before I receive it?
Your order will be processed within 1-2 business days. Everything is shipped from Bountiful, Utah. Depending on your location, it can take anywhere from 4-7 business days to be delivered. Orders for the east coast generally take longer to arrive than orders for the west coast.
What is the shipping cost on Cobra Shinguards?
Shipping is $3.50 in the contiguous United States and $18 for all International orders on all Cobra Shinguard products on this website. We do offer Free Shipping on any order over $50.
Product Returns
If I return the shinguards, will you reimburse me for the shipping costs?
For the first 30-days, return shipping will be included. After the first 30-days, the customer is responsible for all return shipping costs unless there is a defect in the product.
What if I don’t like the shinguards, can I return it?
CobraShinguards.com offers a 30-Day return policy on all products. If you decide Cobra Shinguards are not for you, please contact our customer service department at (844) 49-COBRA to receive a return merchandise authorization number. Your product will need to be returned in new condition, packaged securely in a box. Once the shinguards are received and inspected, a full refund of the purchase price will be issued within 10 working days.
Can I cancel my order?
You can cancel your order anytime before it ships and we will fully refund your payment.

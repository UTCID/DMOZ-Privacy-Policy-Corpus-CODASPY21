We take your privacy seriously. Please review what we have committed to in order to protect your personal information.
Overview
The privacy and security of your personal information that we receive and collect from you are important to us. As such, we have set forth certain privacy and security standards that are intended to safeguard your personal information.
Information Collection, Use, Sharing and Access
1. Information Collection
Personal information is information about you that we collect when you register with buyovulationtests.com, or when you voluntarily provide such information.
We may collect information relating to:
Contact information such as your name, email address and mobile phone number. Browser information such as your IP address, cookies and recorded information such your browser type, language, transactions, contributed content and the date and time of your activities.
2. Information Use
We may combine personal information collected from you with information from third parties or other companies for providing better user experience and customized services for you.
We may use information for purposes relating to:
Customizing the content and advertising that you see
Conducting analyses and research
Providing/improving products and services that you’ve requested or that you are most active with
Providing anonymous information to internal and external parties
Complying with legal processes, subpoenas, court orders, prevent fraud or imminent harm, and ensuring the security of our network and services.
Buyovulationtests.commay also use information to communicate with you information about buyovulationtests.com, promotions and products and services. You may opt-out of receiving non-essential communications; however, certain essential emails, which are necessary for the administration and maintenance of your account, may still be communicated with you.
3. Information Sharing
We do not rent, sell or share your personal information with other people or companies except when we receive your permission.
In the case buyovulationtests.com is acquired by or merged with another company, buyovulationtests.com will transfer your information to the acquired or merged company. However, TheSkywoods-Singapore.com will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
4. Information Access
You have the right to view or edit your personal information yourself when you are signed into your account, or have the right to request that buyovulationtests.com edit the information on your behalf.
For personal information that may not be accessible via the websites, you may still access that information by writing to us by email to enquiry@buyovulationtests.comand we will contact you within 30 days regarding your request.
You also have the right to limit certain personal information from being displayed on your user profile for the public to view. You may request that buyovulationtests.com limit the information for you.
We limit access to your personal information to persons under the employment of, contracted by, or managed under buyovulationtests.com who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with your information in order to do their jobs.
Links
Buyovulationtests.commay provide, or third parties may provide, links to other websites. Because buyovulationtests.com has no control over these sites, buyovulationtests.com cannot acknowledge or endorse the availability, existence or practice of any privacy policies on these external domains. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policy of these external websites before providing your personal information. At no time will we share your personal information with these non-affiliated domains.
Use of Cookies
Buyovulationtests.com may use “cookies” to enable you to sign in to our services. Your preference and information may be stored on the cookies in order to simplify your experience by saving you time from repeatedly entering the same information.
Cookies may also allow your e-mail address or username be pre-populated, so that you will only need to type your password the next time you sign in. If you are using a public computer or do not otherwise want this information to be stored, you can select the appropriate radio button on the sign-in page, and this cookie will not be used. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.
Changes to Privacy Policy
We may occasionally update our privacy policy and respective statement to reflect changes in our products and services. When we post changes to this Statement, we will update the effective date at the top of this statement.
If changes to our privacy policy are material, we will notify you either by prominently posting a notice of such changes prior to implementing the change or by directly sending you a notification.
We encourage you to periodically review our privacy policy and this statement to be informed of how buyovulationtests.com is protecting your personal information.
Questions, Suggestions and Contact
If you have any questions or suggestions relating to our privacy policy and information practice, please contact us at enquiry@buyovulationtests.com

This is the Web site of Der Freiwillige Military Antiques. Our postal address is P.O. Box 1693, Titusville FL 32781. We can be reached via email at headquarters@derfreiwillige.com.
We collect only the information that visitors to this Web site provide.
We do not set any cookies.
The information visitors provide is used only to process orders, to answer requests for information, and to update this Web site.
If visitors provide credit card information via our secure order form, that information is used only to contact a virtual terminal to process credit card transactions.
We do not provide any customer or visitor information to any other companies, organizations, or servers, other than secure information used to process credit card orders.
We may send emails to visitors alerting them to Web site updates or for advertising new items or services offered on this Web site.
If visitors supply us with their postal address on-line, we may send them periodic mailings with information on new products or services or Web site updates.
If you do not want to receive such emails or mailings from us in the future, please let us know by sending us email at the above address, or writing to us at the above address.
Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers on-line will only receive telephone contact from us with information regarding orders they have placed.
From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy policy.
If our information changes at some time in the future we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses.
If you have concerns, you should check back at our Web site periodically.
Upon request we provide site visitors with access to all information that we maintain about them.

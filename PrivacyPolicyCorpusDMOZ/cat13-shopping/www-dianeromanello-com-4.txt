PRIVACY POLICY
At Bentley Global Arts Group, we recognize and respect the importance of maintaining the privacy of our customers and visitors and have established this privacy policy as a demonstration of our firm commitment to privacy. Below we describe why we gather customer and visitor information, what information we collect, how we collect it, and what we use the information for.
Summation
Information and data collected by Bentley Global Arts Group is used exclusively for order fulfillment, in-house research, and our own site development and marketing efforts. No data or personal information will be sold or traded to any third party for any reason. Ever.
Payment Information
All payment data (credit card number, expiration, etc) is encrypted and stored temporarily to enable the transaction to be processed.
Limited information is stored long term, allowing us to edit or credit the account being used in the transaction.
The only data stored long term are the the last four digits of the credit card used as well as the transaction ID.
Personal Information
When you order from our web site, we collect the following information: your name, address, email, and telephone number.
This information is used exclusively for processing your order and is routinely purged from servers and archived on secure servers within our corporate offices.
How we use "cookies"
A "cookie" is a small file stored on your computer by a web site which gives you a numeric user ID and stores certain information about your activity on the site. We use cookies to store information on your product selections and search queries as you shop on our site, but we do not store any personal information or financial information about you using cookies.We also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to help identify you and your shopping cart and to gather broad demographic information. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but most allow you to instruct your browser to prevent the use of cookies. However, if you disable this feature, certain aspects of this site, such as the shopping basket and site search, will not function properly.
Data Sharing
We do not sell, share, trade or distribute any personal information relating to you and your order history to third parties for ANY reason.Furthermore, you may opt to remove your name and contact information from our own internal promotional lists by following the details included with the promotional material, e-mail, etc.
Your Security
Bentley Global Arts Group employs security measures to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Our web site encrypts your credit card number prior to transmission over the Internet using secure socket layer (SSL) encryption technology.

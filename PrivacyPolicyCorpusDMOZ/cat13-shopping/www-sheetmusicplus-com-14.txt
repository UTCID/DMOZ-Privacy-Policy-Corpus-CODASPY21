About Purchase Orders
Ordering music with a Purchase Order is easy! Simply send us the Purchase Order and we will place the order for you.
Please include an email address on your Purchase Order so we can give you timely updates.
Send us your Purchase order via:
1. Fax: 510-373-6520
2. Email: invoiceorder@sheetmusicplus.com
3. Mail:   Sheet Music Plus
Attn: Purchase Order
PO Box 13920
Milwaukee, WI 53213
USA
Or, if you create a School or Church Account, you will be able to place an order yourself and be billed via invoice.
If you have any questions please call our customer service department at 1-800-743-3868.

Privacy Policy
At BeginnerBabyCare.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and/or collected by us.  It also outlines how we use that information.
Log Files
Like many other Web sites, we make use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement around the site, and gather demographic information.
IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
Cookies
BeginnerBabyCare.com also uses cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

BeginnerBabyCare.com has no access to or control over these cookies. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these Amazon for more detailed information about them. BeginnerBabyCare.com’s privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, Amazon.

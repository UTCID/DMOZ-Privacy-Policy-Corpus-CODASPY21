Privacy Policy
Privacy PolicyThe information that you provide when placing an order is never sold or shared with other companies or parties.  You will not be placed on any mailing list unless you specifically sign up for them although we may occassionally notify past customers of new items or sales.  Any information provided to Ornamental Things during an order will be used strictly for completing that order.  Orders are processed through a secure server at Authorize.net so your credit card information is not kept on file at the offices of Ornamental Things.  This website may also contain links to other websites.  Please note that when you click on one of these links, you are moving to another website.  We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policy may differ from ours.

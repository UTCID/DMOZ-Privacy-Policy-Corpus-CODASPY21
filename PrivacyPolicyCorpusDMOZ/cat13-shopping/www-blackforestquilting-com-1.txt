Privacy Policy
Our Commitment To Your Privacy
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy the following notice explains the information we collect, how it is used, how it is safeguarded, and how to contact us if you have any concerns.
What Information Is Collected:
As part of the order process, the following information is collected from our customers:
Name, Shipping/Billing Address, Email address, Phone number.
How That Information Is Used:
We may need to contact you for information about finishing your quilt, and we may add your e-mail address or postal address to our mailing list. If you would not like to receive our newsletter or correspondence just let us know. YOUR PRIVACY IS IMPORTANT TO US!!
Our Commitment To The Security of Your Data
We do not share any information about our customers or people on our mailing list with anyone, ever.

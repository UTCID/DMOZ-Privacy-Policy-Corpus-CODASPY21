Satisfaction Guaranteed
TMGDeals.com believes in providing its customers with the highest quality of products and services and backing them with our Guarantee of Satisfaction.If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with our product, it will be replaced, exchanged or the full purchase price refunded following its return.TMGDeals.com will stand behind the exceptional products, service we offer. We believe our commitment to quality products and exceptional service is essential to building long term relationships with our customers.
Return Policy
Merchandise may be returned within thirty (30) days from the date of purchase and within (15) fifteen days after we provide you the return instructions. Please contact us prior to returning any product(s). We will provide you return instructions. Please follow the return instructions to return any product purchased from our company.
Unwanted returns will be issued a full refund less shipping charges and a 15% restock fee. The 15% restock fee will be waived for defective or damaged products only. Refunds will be issued after the return has been received and inspected by our Returns Manager. Refunds will be credited back to the account that was used to make the original payment.
Exchange Policy
Only Defective / Damaged Merchandise may be exchanged within thirty (30) days from the date of purchase and within (15) fifteen days after we provide you the exchange instructions. Please contact us prior to exchanging any product(s). We will provide you exchange instructions. Please follow the exchange instructions to return any product purchased from our company. Once we received the merchandise, our technician confirm the defect / damage and send you a replacement at no additional cost. Incase we are not able to find any defect / damage with the product we will issue you a refund.
Product Warranty
TMGDeals.com does not accept returns after 30 days, however defective items may be returned to the warranty provider for repair or exchange. All merchandise come with a manufacturer warranty. The warranty period on each product varies from 90 Days to upto 3 years. All defective merchandise that are within the warranty period will be repaired or replaced with the same or better. The warranty information is included in the package.
Privacy Policy
TMGDeals.com respects your right to privacy and will not share or sell your personal and billing information to any 3rd party not involved in the fulfillment your order. Our company does not sell, rent, loan or transfer any information regarding its customers. TMGDeals.com keeps this information on a secure server to protect it from outside parties. Your information is used only for the limited purposes of processing your orders, administering our site, notifying you of products or special offers (only if you select to be included on our mailing list).

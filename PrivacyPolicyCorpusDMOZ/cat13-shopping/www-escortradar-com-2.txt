CANCELING ORDERS PLACED ON ESCORTRADAR.COM
To submit a cancellation request for Escortradar.com please email ordercancel@cedarelectronics.com. Depending on the status of order you might be able to cancel your transaction.  Our fulfillment system continues to process the order while you attempt to cancel.  The time-frame to cancel the order might expire while submitting your request.
If your cancellation request was successful, you will receive a confirmation email.  Your card will not be charged, but a fund authorization will hold until released by your bank.
If you did not make the cancellation window, you can refuse the order upon delivery or visit our Order Return section for complete details.  When we receive the order return, we will process the return and email confirmation regarding your refund.

Privacy
We do not share the information with other organizations for any purpose. All information collected by Elite Cigars will only be used to notify customers about updates to our web site and periodic specials.
If at any time you no longer wish to receive such e-mail or postal mailings from us, please let us know. We will be sure your name is removed from our lists. Please provide us with your exact name and address or email address to maillist@elitecigars.com.
Security
Every credit card transaction processed is 100% safe.
We use secure server software (SSL), which is the industry standard, and among the best software available today for secure transactions. Your personal information will be encrypted when transferred over the Internet so it can not be read by anyone other than us.

Privacy Policy
Morning Song Gardens is committed to keeping any information that we receive via email or written correspondence private. It will not be given or sold to anyone period. We avoid SPAM or unwanted advertising via U.S. mail or telephone. We emply  Authorize.net, a trusted company  to authorize  all our transactions, and WorldPay to distribute the funds. They both are extremely secure companies, making the use of your credit card very safe. We do not  save your credit card number on our web site or store it at our location for your protection. We are PCI Compliant and train all our employees in security measures. Additionally we keep our SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), the standard security technology for establishing an encrypted link between a web server and a browser, up to date at all time. If you have any questions involving our security policy please feel free to contact us at:
Email: Sales@MorningSongGardens.com

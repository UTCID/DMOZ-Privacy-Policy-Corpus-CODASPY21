Privacy
Your privacy is very important to us.  We collect information from you when you register on Joe Collector, place an order or subscribe to our newsletter.  You may also visit anonymously.  The information you provide will be used to process your transaction and deliver your order.  It will not be shared for any other reason than these.  We use a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.

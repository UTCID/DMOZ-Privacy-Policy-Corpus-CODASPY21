Privacy Policy
General Statement
Medalye Designs is committed to protecting the privacy of any information communicated to us via our online forms, email, phone, mail, or any other communication medium. Under no circumstances will any of that information knowingly be made available to anyone outside of our organization, except to process your order. Even within our company, only those people that require your personal information in order to complete your transaction will have access to that information.
In an effort to respond to our customers’ interests, we may occasionally add questions to our online forms which do not directly relate to fulfilling your order. All such questions are optional.
Any personal information that we do collect is for internal purposes only. We do not sell nor share your personal information with any external organization. Your name will not find its way onto any mass circulation databases and there will not be any new forms of “junk mail” in your mailbox because of your involvement with Medalye Designs.
If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy please contact us.

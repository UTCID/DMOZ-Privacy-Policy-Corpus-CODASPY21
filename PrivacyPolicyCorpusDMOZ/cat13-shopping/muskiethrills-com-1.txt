Privacy Notice
You can shop at Muskie Thrills with confidence.  We respect your privacy and will not, under any circumstances, reveal or sell your customer information to anyone.  All information provided to us is held in the strictest of confidence.
All Muskie Thrills business and correspondence is conducted via this webpage and email.  As Muskie Thrills and Wiley Lures are both home based businesses, we do not publish our phone numbers in order to protect our privacy and prevent unwanted solicitation.
Since we respect your privacy as well, the ONLY time we would contact you via phone is if we have a question on your order and cannot reach you via email due to an incorrect email address or full mailbox.  Please be sure to double check your phone number and especially your email and shipping  address for accuracy when placing your order.

Privacy Policy
The ItalianCharms privacy policy is designed to ensure that any personal
information you provide us with will remain private. We only collect
personal information in order to process billing, fulfill orders, and
provide you with a personal and customized shopping experience. We will
never voluntarily sell your personal information to any third party for
any reason.
When placing an order, you provide your name, mailing address,
telephone number, email address, credit card number and expiration date.
We collect information to verify your identity for payment
authorization, to ship your order to you, to contact you about your
order status, to personalize your shopping experience, to send you
helpful information about ItalianCharms, and generate anonymous
statistics to help our site improve.
Your personal information will be shared with our employees, your
credit card company, and the party through whom your product is shipped
(e.g. UPS or other delivery company). This shared personal information
may include you name, your street address, and your telephone number.
Your personal information will not be sold to mailing list firms.
If you wish to update your personal information that we have
gathered from you or wish to remove any personal data you have
previously provided to us ('opt out'), please send an email to us at icharms.general@gmail.com or contact us at (209) 787-0346.

Our Commitment to Protecting Your Privacy
We are committed to providing you with an Internet experience that respects and protects your personal privacy choices and concerns. Our Privacy Policy and practices are detailed below. Please read them thoroughly.
We make two pledges to you:
We do not collect, use or disclose personally identifiable information about you on this Web site unless we require contact information to respond to a request from you for services;
We will do our best to respond to your privacy questions and concerns.
User Questions or Suggestions
Occasionally you may have questions, comments or suggestions about various aspects of our Web site, we welcome such communications via email to our Web Master.
Any information you provide will be used solely for the purpose of responding to you or improving our Web site and Services.
Do Any Third Parties Receive or Collect Your Information Through Mates?
No, Mates will not sell or disclose your personally identifiable information as customer lists to anyone.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information attained when placing an order. Our site uses encryption technology to protect your information.
Contacting Mates about Privacy
Questions regarding our privacy policy should be directed to the Mates site coordinator, at web@matesuniforms.ca.

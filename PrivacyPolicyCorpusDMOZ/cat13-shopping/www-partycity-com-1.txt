Last updated: August 18, 2020This Privacy Policy explains how Party City and its affiliates (together, “Party City”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collect, use, and share information about our customers (“customer”, “you”, or “your”), and your choices in connection with this. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our privacy practices. If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, please see the Contact Us section at the end of the Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not cover any information collected by third-party sites or content or applications that may link to, or be accessible from or on, our website.We collect personal information, which is information that identifies, relates to, describes, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with you (“Personal Information”). We collect Personal Information from customers through a variety of touch points during the course of our business. For example, we may collect Personal Information about you at our retail locations (“in-store”), online when you visit our website www.partycity.com or its related subdomains (“Website”), or through our mobile application (“Mobile Application”).Collection and use of personal informationInformation You Provide To UsYou will be required to provide us with Personal Information if you choose to engage with certain functions on our Mobile Application or Website or with us in-store. This includes when you:Create a Party City account. To create a Party City account, you will provide us with personal identifiers, such as your name, date of birth, and email address. Once your account is created, we will maintain your Party City username and password.Make a purchase. If you make a purchase, you will need to provide your personal identifiers, such as your name, physical address, and email address, as well as customer records information, including payment card number. When you make a purchase, we also collect and maintain commercial information about you, such as products purchased, obtained, or considered, and purchasing and consuming tendencies.Participate in social media. You may choose to share your own personal identifiers, such as your name and any other personal information you choose to include in your post, through our ratings and review pages, and social media pages. If you tag a publicly available social media post with a reference to Party City, with your permission, we may also collect an image of you that you choose to include in your post.Interact with us. When you communicate with us or send us any feedback, questions, requests for support, comments, suggestions, ideas, or interact with us in any way, you will need to provide personal identifiers, including your name and email address.Subscribe to Party City updates and promotions. You have the option of signing up for Party City email updates or our FUN text message updates for products and offers that may be of interest to you. To sign-up, you will need to provide us with personal identifiers such as your name, email address and/or telephone number. When you sig-up, we may use your personal information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information that may be of interest to you. You may opt out of receiving any, or all, of these communications from us by following the unsubscribe link or instructions provided in any email we send or by texting “STOP” to 94467.Move around in our store. If you visit one of our Party City locations, video of you will be captured by our CCTV footage.Apply for a job with Party City. Our use of personal information of applicants is addressed in our Applicant Privacy Notice. Enroll in Party City Perks. If you enroll in Party City Perks in a store, we will collect your personal identifiers such as your name, email address, and birthday. Any information collected to enroll in Party City Perks will be used to convey to enrolled customers benefits that Party City may occasionally offer such as gifts, coupons, discounts, prizes, and other benefits. We also use the information you provided to us, together with information collected from your purchases (including items and quantities purchased and locations and date or purchase) to better understand your preferences (for example what products you purchase or when) and based on this understanding, provide you with additional offers.Contact our customer care team. If you contact our customer care team via telephone, we may collect any personal information that you choose to provide to us over the phone.Information Collected Automatically from YouWe also collect internet and other electronic network information and location information automatically as you navigate our Website or Mobile Application. This information is collected by cookies and other similar technologies. Cookies are text files that your web browser places on your device when you visit a website. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies stay on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them, while session cookies expire when you close your web browser.The cookies and other technologies deployed through our Website and/or Mobile Application collect internet and other electronic network information and geolocation data as follows:Usage Information.

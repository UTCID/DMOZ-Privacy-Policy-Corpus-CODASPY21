SECTION 1 - WHAT DO WE DO WITH YOUR INFORMATION?
Jumpy Monkey Coffee Roasting Co. will not intentionally disclose any personally identifying information about you (including information obtained by the Site from cookies and information regarding your IP address) to third parties without your consent except where Jumpy Monkey Coffee Roasting Co., in good faith, believes such disclosure is necessary to comply with the law or to enforce these Terms and Conditions of the Site.
Jumpy Monkey Coffee Roasting Co. reserves the right to collect general demographic and other market information that does not personally identify you to any person without additional consent.  We utilize e-mails to notify you for sales purposes, to let you know of special promotions, events and policy changes.
New customers are automatically opted-in to receive Jumpy Monkey Coffee's email, promotional marketing e-mails, promotional partner e-mails, newsletters, fairness e-mails or announcement (i.e. site enhancement) e-mails.

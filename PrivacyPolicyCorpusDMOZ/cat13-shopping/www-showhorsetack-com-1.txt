Privacy Notice
We respect the privacy rights of visitors visiting our site.
Show Horse Tack does not release individual information obtained from Web site visitors to any third parties for any purpose. Show Horse Tack may share non-personal aggregate, or summary information regarding its customers with partners or other third parties, but does not share any information at the customer level.
We do not use cookies. (Cookies are small files downloaded to your computer by some Web sites in order to monitor your movements within the sites.)
We do not actively solicit or request any information from visitors except in the following instances:
1. Customers wishing to purchase our products directly from us either over the Internet or via telephone need to provide their name, address, e-mail address and credit card billing information.

3. If visitors wish to subscribe to our e-mail mailing list to receive hard copies of our catalog, they need to provide an e-mail address.

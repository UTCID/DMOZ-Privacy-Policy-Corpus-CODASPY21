Privacy Notice
Account Registration
When creating a user account, required information includes but is not limited to name, shipping and billing addresses, telephone number, and email address. This information is used strictly for the processing, shipping, and communication with the customer in respect to orders.
Collection and Use of Information
Lilyblooms Aquatic Gardens does not sell, rent or share collected customer information with any outside parties other than those required for the processing and delivery of ordered items. FedEx and USPS are the only used shipping methods, and for obvious reasons shipping information must be shared. We do not store any financial information. All financial information is processed by  To view PayPal's privacy policy click here.
Cookies and Sessions
Any use of cookies or sessions on our site is for the functionality of placing an order. IP addresses may temporarily be stored. If you are a registered user and logged in to your account, your shopping cart will be stored indefinitely unless you empty it.

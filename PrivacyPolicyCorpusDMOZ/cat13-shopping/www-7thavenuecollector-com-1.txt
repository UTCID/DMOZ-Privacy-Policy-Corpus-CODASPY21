Rest Assured We at 7th Avenue Collector want to assure you that the information you have provided will not be shared with other companies or any third-party. It will be used to customize our products and services so that our communications with you are personal and truly relevant. We do not make our customer list available to other companies and do not share individual information except as necessary to ship and complete your order.
We do not share our e-mail lists with any third parties, so you won't receive any unwanted e-mails from unknown or unexpected sources. We do keep a list of e-mail addresses for the purpose of sending out our free electronic newsletter, which let's you know about upcoming promotions and new releases on the site. You may unsubscribe from the newsletter at anytime by following the simple instructions at the bottom of the e-mail newsletter.

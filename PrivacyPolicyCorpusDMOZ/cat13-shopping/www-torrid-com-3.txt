FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How do I find a store near me?
Click here to access our store finder. Just enter your city, state or zip code.
How do I return an item I purchased?
It's easy! Click here for instructions and to view our returns/exchange policy.
Torrid must receive all returns and exchanges, including the replacement of missing or damaged items, within 60 days of the purchase date listed on your receipt.
I've forgotten my password, how do I get a new one?
You can click the "I forgot my password" link on the My Account Log In page then enter your E-mail.
What payment types do you accept?
Torrid.com accepts Torrid Credit Card, MasterCard, Visa, Discover, American Express and ATM/debit cards with the Visa or MasterCard logo. You may also pay with a Torrid gift card, PayPal, or store credit. For orders being shipped to the contiguous 48 United States, Hawaii, Alaska and Washington D.C., Klarna is also an available payment type.

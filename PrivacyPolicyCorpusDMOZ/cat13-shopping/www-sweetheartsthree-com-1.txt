Sweethearts Three Privacy Policy
Sweethearts Three will never sell or rent any information you provide.
All information is considered confidential. On occasion, we will
send informational e-mails to you with updates or special offers.
If at any time you wish to stop receiving these e-mails, we will
give you the option to remove your name from our mailing list. You
can also cancel Sweethearts Three mailings at any time by sending
us an e-mail.

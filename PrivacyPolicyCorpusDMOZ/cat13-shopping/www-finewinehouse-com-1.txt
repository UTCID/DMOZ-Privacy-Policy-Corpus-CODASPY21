Coupon Code Terms
FineWineHouse Promotional Terms - Coupon Code cannot be used with any other coupons, promos or free shipping.- Coupons valid on select wines only, not valid for spirits orders.- Coupon Code cannot be used to purchase rare vintage wines, rare cellar wine (SKUs that begin with "cellar") or Opus One wine. - Coupon Code cannot be used on for previous or orders in progress. - Promo codes do not apply to items with prices ending in 5 or 7 (i.e. $9.95 or $9.97). Please note if the discount is applied when placing your online order we will manually remove the discount and resend an updated invoice via e-mail.- Free shipping cannot be used in Alabama, Arkansas, or Mississippi, as we do not ship to those states.
Free Shippings TermsWhen we are running free shipping promotions the following terms apply:- Free shipping promos cannot be used in Alabama, Alaska, or Mississippi, as we do not ship to those states.- Free shipping promos cannot be used on beer and Bordeaux futures or presales.- Free shipping does not apply to items with prices ending in 5 or 7 (i.e. $9.95 or $9.97).- Free shipping via UPS Ground only. $5 handling fee is included with all orders.

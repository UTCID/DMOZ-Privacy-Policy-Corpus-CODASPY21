Privacy Statement In Harmony Herbs & Spices Privacy Statement
It is our intent to keep all information regarding our customers private. This includes all personal information we receive via the Internet, through the mail and on the telephone. Our Internet server is secure, so you can place your order online with confidence. We do not sell our customer database to other companies. We do require your phone number so that we may contact you if we have any questions regarding your order. This often expedites the time it takes to ship your products.
We believe that your personal life is private and we intend to keep it so! In Harmony

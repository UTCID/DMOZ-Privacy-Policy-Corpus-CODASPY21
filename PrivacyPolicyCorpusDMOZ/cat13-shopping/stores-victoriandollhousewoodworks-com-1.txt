Privacy
Victorian Dollhouse Wood Works is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell or disclose any information that identifies you to a third party without your prior approval. We may use the information we collect to periodically notify you about new services or special offers we think you'll find valuable. If you would rather not receive this information, you may send an e-mail to bgdesign@optonline.net with "unsubscribe" as the subject line. Victorian Dollhouse Wood Works does not sell, trade or rent your personal information to others.

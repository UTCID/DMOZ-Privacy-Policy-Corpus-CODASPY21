About Us
Welcome to our Store!
Mobile GPS Online is an Ottawa-based company, founded in 2002 by Dr. -Ing. Detang Zhong. With more than 30 years experience in academic research institutions, high-tech companies and self-motivated business and marketing development plus close partnerships with several popular GPS hardware vendors and system developers, we have the expertise to meet your GPS application needs. Mobile GPS Online became a subsidiary of Geoidsoft Inc. in 2016 after serving our happy clients for 14 years. As always, our commitment is to offer you high quality and effective solutions for your GPS needs and applications with the ultimate experience in convenience, value and satisfaction. Our mission is high quality product, competitive price and outstanding support.

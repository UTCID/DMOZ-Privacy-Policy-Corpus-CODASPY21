> Privacy StatementPrivacy StatementYour privacy on the Internet is of the utmost importance to us. While shopping at The Serpentarium we want your experience to be both satisfying and safe.Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we want you to fully understand our policy and the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it. Information The Serpentarium Gathers:RegistrationUpon registering, customers must fill out their Full Name, Email Address and Shipping Address. This information is required for shipping purposes and the convenience of auto-filled form fields. We do not sell, lease or otherwise share any customer information whatsoever.eNewsletterWe offer an eNewsletter to keep customers up to date on the latest happenings at the Museum; from new exhibits and events to sales and important news. Users can subscribe to the eNewsletter at any time via the main home page, or via Personal Information page under My Account as well as during registration. Users may remove themselves from this e-mailing list by simply following the link provided at the bottom of every eNewsletter, which points to the subscription management page."Send to a friend" linkOur users can choose to forward a product link to someone by clicking the "Send to a friend" link found on every product page. The user must provide their friend's name and email address. This information is used only for the convenience of letting the recipient know about a product on our site. The information is not used for any other purpose.

Privacy Policy
Studio 52 music store is deeply committed to protecting the privacy of website users.
Studio 52 online music store does not collect any personally identifiable information
about you such as name, address, phone number, or e-mail address,
during the process of delivering an information to you via our website.
These information are required and asked only when you submit any kind of Order Form
or Contact Form. The personal information that you submitted in these Forms, are
used only for completing and shipping your orders, follow-up with you on product
availability and shipping notification. All these personal information are
encrypted and protecting against disclosure to third parties by us and are used
with secrecy.
Studio 52 will under no circumstances use your personal
information that you have submitted, for any purposes other than the ones you
have provided them for.
Studio 52 online music store may or may
not obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information with out
submitting any kind of Form, such as the Internet Service Provider you use to
connect to the Internet, your browser type, the type of computer operating
system you use (Macintosh or Windows, for example), or what screen resolution do
you use. These non personal information collected by Studio 52 are used for
the purpose of improving your browsing experience on our website and our
services to you.
Studio 52 music store, as well as
most sites on the Internet, uses "cookies" to enhance your experience
on the web. These cookies, cannot be used to personally identify you in any way.
(Security
& Privacy: An HTTP Cookie cannot be used to get data from your hard
drive, get your email address or steal sensitive information about your person.)
For any question about our privacy policy, please contact us via e-mail at info@studio52.gr

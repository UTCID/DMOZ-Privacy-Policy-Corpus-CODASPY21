Our Privacy Policy
Old School Leather Co. DOES NOT SHARE ANYTHING ABOUT ANYONE WITH ANYBODY!  Any information we collect from you is used solely to complete your order.  The paperwork from completed orders is used as kindling in our backyard fire pit.
At the most, we receive your email address, shipping address and the last four digits of your credit card number.  Otherwise, we receive your Order details and any additional notes or comments you add.
This site and the shopping cart are powered by Shopify.  You can view the Shopify Privacy Policy here:  https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy
We also accept PayPal payments.  View the PayPal privacy policy here:  https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full

Privacy Policy
We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998).
We collect information about you for a variety of reasons:
To process your order,
To contact you in case we have a problem with your order,
To send you our regular newsletter.
You always have the option to easily unsubscribe from our mailing list.
We do not sell your details to advertising companies...ever!
You can check the information that we hold about you by emailing us (info@rarenewspapers.com) or by calling us (570-326-1045). If you find any inaccuracies we will delete or correct it promptly.

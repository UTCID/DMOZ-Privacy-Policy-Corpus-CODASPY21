AUSTRALIAN LIFE SPICE PRIVACY POLICY
Australian Life Spice has created this statement in order to disclose our information gathering and dissemination of practices for this website, www.ausslifespice.com.
Any questions regarding this statement should be directed to the Australian Lifespice site coordinator by emailing support@ausslifespice.com or writing to us at the following address:
4903 AVENUE M
BROOKLYN, NY
USA 11234
Australian Life Spice is the sole owner of the information it gathers on its site.
We at Australian Life Spice will not share any of your information with any outside party without your explicit consent, unless we are required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is in necessary order to conform to the edicts of the law, cooperate with law enforcement agencies, or comply with a legal process served on our website.
Information that is Collected and How it is Used
We use e-mail links and Web-based forms located on the contact page and other areas of the site to allow you to contact us directly with any questions or comments you may have.
We read every message that is sent, and try to reply promptly to every one. This information is used to respond directly to your questions or comments.
We may also file your comments to improve the site and our services, or review and discard the information.
Your personal information is never shared with third parties without your explicit permission.
Protecting your privacy and your information is a top priority at Australian Life Spice.
We have taken careful measures to prevent the loss, misuse and alteration of your information.
All Australian Life Spice employees are aware of our privacy and security policies.
Your information is only accessible to those employees who need it in order to perform their jobs.

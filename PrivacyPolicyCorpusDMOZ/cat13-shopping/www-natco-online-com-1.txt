Privacy Policy
Natco Foods does not disclose customer information to third parties except for goods delivery purposes. Cookies are used on this shopping site to keep track of the contents of your shopping cart once you have selected an item, to store delivery addresses if the address book is used and to store your details if you select the 'Remember Me' Option and create a Customer Account. Data collected by this site is used to:a. Take and fulfill customer ordersb. Administer and enhance the site and service
We will only contact you in relation to your order, and not for any other purposes, unless you have opted to receive our newsletter. We will only send out a newsletter occasionally, so that it is useful for you.

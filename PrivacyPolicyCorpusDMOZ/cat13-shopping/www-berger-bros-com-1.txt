Privacy
Our privacy policyLike most eCommerce sites we collect cookies. It is unavoidable given that we must rely on Third-Party vendors to keep our site running.  Please rest assured that all information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential, period. It will not be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise be disclosed, including membership forms or email lists.Changing your personal informationYou can change any of your account information by calling Berger Bros at 800-542-8811 or 516-496-1000, by mail at 226 W. Jericho Tpke, Syosset, N.Y. 11791, USA, or via e-mail at customerservice@berger-bros.com. To protect your privacy and security, we will take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections. Secure On-line Order Entry All on-line ordering is secured by our SSL Secured Socket Layer 128 bit encryption standard. This provides for the highest level of security when you place your order without the fear that your credit card information will be compromised.
Trustwave (tm) Monitoring
We also employ Trustwave to monitor our site 24/7. Trustwave mandates that we adhere to PCI standards (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard). This added oversight guarantees that we are following PCI industry standards.When you place an order, you will be asked to complete an order form. The order form requires your name, billing and shipping address, credit card number and expiration date. You should also include other information such as a primary and secondary telephone number (such as cellphone, work phone, home phone, etc.) which will allow us to contact you regarding your order if necessary.Still have questions? Contact customerservice@berger-bros.com.

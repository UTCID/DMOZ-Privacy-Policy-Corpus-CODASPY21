How we use cookies
A cookie is a small file which asks to be placed on your computer’s hard drive. If you agree, the file is added and the cookie helps to analyse web traffic or informs you when you visit a particular website. Cookies also allow web applications to respond to you as an individual, tailoring its operation to suit your needs, likes and dislikes, by gathering and remembering information about your preferences.
Usa.hudsonreed.com uses traffic log cookies to identify which website pages are being used. This helps us to analyse data about web traffic and improve our website. This information is used solely for statistical analysis purposes. The data is then removed from the system.
Cookies enable us to monitor which pages our customers find useful and which they don’t, helping us to provide you with a better website and shopping experience. They do not give us access to your computer or any other information about you, other than that which you choose to share with us. You can choose whether to accept or decline the use of cookies on your browser. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but this setting can be altered if you so wish. Do be aware that declining cookies prevent you taking full advantage of the Hudson Reed website.

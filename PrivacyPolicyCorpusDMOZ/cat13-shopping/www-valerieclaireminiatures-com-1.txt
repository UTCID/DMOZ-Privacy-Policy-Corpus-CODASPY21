Valerie Claire Miniatures Ltd is the sole owner of the information collected on this site, we do not share your information with any third parties.  We only have access to and collect information from you that you voluntarily give us when placing an order.
General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
The information we hold is to make an online purchase or create an account.  We treat your data with the utmost care and take appropriate steps to protect it.  Access to all transactional areas of our website use ‘https’ technology, i.e., payments are taken via Paypal or BAC’s.  Access to your personal data is password protected, and sensitive data such as your payment card information is secured and tokenised to ensure it is protected.

John Deere SMS
What are John Deere Mobile Messages?
John Deere Mobile Messages are SMS messages that contain promotions and information for John Deere and our products and services.
How do I sign up for John Deere Mobile Messages?
Sign up for John Deere Mobile Messages by texting DEERE to the short code 33373. By signing up, you will only receive 4 messages per month. John Deere Mobile Messages are not mobile spam. The service is a recurring message program.
Is it free?
Although all John Deere Mobile Messages are complimentary, Message & Data Rates May Apply. Depending on your text plan, you may be charged by your carrier.
What if I don't want to receive any more promotional messages?
To stop receiving all John Deere Mobile Messages, simply text STOP to the short code 33373. After doing so, you will receive confirmation of your opt-out, and thereafter you will no longer receive any John Deere Mobile Messages.
What if I want to opt back in?
You can start receiving John Deere Mobile Messages again by following any of the steps outlined in the above section: "How do I sign up for John Deere Mobile Messages".
What if I want more info?
To request more info simply text HELP to the short code 33373.
Commands
Stop: At any time you can text STOP or STOPALL to the short code 33373. This will prevent you from receiving any future John Deere Mobile Messages. Help: At any time you can text HELP to the short code 33373. You will then be presented with the following message: John Deere: 4msgs/mon. Mobile terms: JohnDeere.com/sms, email JDFeDirect@johndeere.com or call 8003545863. Msg&Data Rates May Apply. Reply STOP to cancel.

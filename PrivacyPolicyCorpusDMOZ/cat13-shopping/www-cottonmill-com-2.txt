Privacy Policy
Site Privacy:
Your confidence in us is very important to us! We use the information you give us only to fulfill the order you have placed with us. We will never add your email address to our own email list unless you ask us to do so. We "live" on the Internet too, and know how annoying it is to get unwanted advertising.
Under no circumstances will we give out any information you give us to a third party. That includes your email address, your name, address or credit card information. No individual information is provided to any third-party without your consent.
Email List Policy:
We only send our Email Newsletter to those who ask for it on this site. If at any time you would like to stop receiving the newsletter, you can unsubscribe by going to the newsletter link and following the instructions there or by going to the link in the newsletter - follow the unsubscribe link or contact us and we'll unsubscribe you.
To report any abuse or violations of this Email List Privacy Policy, please send us an email (see below for email address) and attach an unedited copy of the email message that you feel was sent to you in error or without your permission. Include all header information so that we can trace origin of the message and any notes or other information that you feel are pertinent.

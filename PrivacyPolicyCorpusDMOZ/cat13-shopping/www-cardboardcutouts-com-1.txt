Privacy Policy
Our commitment to protecting your privacy
Cardboard Cutouts is committed to protecting your privacy and developing
technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This
Statement of Privacy applies to the Cardboard Cutouts Web
site and governs data collection and usage. By using the Cardboard Cutouts
website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.
Collection of your Personal Information
Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Cardboard Cutouts
This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and
referring Web site addresses. This information is used by Cardboard Cutouts for
the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to
provide general statistics regarding use of the Cardboard Cutouts Web site.
Cardboard Cutouts encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites
you choose to link to from Cardboard Cutouts so that you can understand how
those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Cardboard Cutouts is
not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites
outside of Cardboard Cutouts, and the Cardboard Cutouts family of Web sites.
Use of your Personal Information
Cardboard Cutouts collects and uses your personal information to operate the Cardboard Cutouts
Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Cardboard Cutouts does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.
Cardboard Cutouts does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or
political affiliations.
Cardboard Cutouts keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within Cardboard Cutouts,
in order to determine what Cardboard Cutouts services are the most popular.
This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within
Cardboard Cutouts to customers whose behavior indicates that they are
interested in a particular subject area.
Cardboard Cutouts Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so
by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a)
conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on
Cardboard Cutouts or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of
Cardboard Cutouts; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the
personal safety of users of Cardboard Cutouts, or the public.
Use of Cookies
The Cardboard Cutouts Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your
online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by
a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to
your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a
web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.
One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The
purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a
specific page. For example, if you personalize Cardboard Cutouts pages, or
register with Cardboard Cutouts site or services, a cookie helps
Cardboard Cutouts to recall your specific information on subsequent visits.
This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as
billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same
Cardboard Cutouts Web site, the information you previously provided can be
retrieved, so you can easily use the Cardboard Cutouts features that you
customized.
You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies,
but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you
may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Cardboard Cutouts services or Web sites you visit.
Security of your Personal Information
Cardboard Cutouts secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure.
Cardboard Cutouts secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a
controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal
information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted, it is protected through the use of encryption,
such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
Changes to this Statement
Cardboard Cutouts will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback.
Cardboard Cutouts encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Cardboard Cutouts is
protecting your information.
Contact Information
Cardboard Cutouts welcomes your comments regarding this Statement of Privacy. If you
believe that Cardboard Cutouts has not adhered to this Statement, please contact us.

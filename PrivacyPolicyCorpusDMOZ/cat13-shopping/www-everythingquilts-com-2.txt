Privacy Policy
At Everything Quilts Inc., you have entrusted us with the following personal data and we value and protect your privacy.
Everything Quilts (Everything Quilts Inc and Everything Quilts.com) is committed to protecting your privacy and GDPR standards. This privacy statement describes how we collects data and how this date may be used. By accessing this site, you are consenting to the collection and use practices described in this policy statement and our Terms of Service.
Our website pages and shopping cart are secure as displayed with "https" and we have displayed our "NS Secure Site Seal" in the left or right frame. You may view this information at any time when you visit our site. Every page on our website is secure so you can shop with peace of mind.
Collection and use of your personal information:
We may need to collect information that identifies you or allows us to contact you in regards to doing business - processing your order on our website, authorize a return, answer questions, send an email, ship your order, download ePatterns, provide tracking information or shipment status updates. We use your personal data to manage your relationship with us, to allow you to purchase from us, deal with invoicing and ensure that we can communicate with you efficiently. We may need to collect personal information for doing business with you and order fullfilment. This information may include your name, email, phone, address and credit card information. All credit card information is handled through secure channels and automatically deleted in accordance to PCI standards. No credit card information is saved in our registered log-in section of our website for consumers to access.
As you navigate our secure website, information about your visit may be captured to manage your relationship with us and help us to serve you better. The types of information that may be captured includes your IP Address, your web browser and version information, time and date of your visit to our website, pages you viewed on our website, search engines you may have used to locate our website or product, items that you entered into your shopping cart and the last page you visited when you left our website. Cookies are used to remember items in your shopping cart until you are ready to order, pages visited and other shopping related features.This information is also used to improve our website, analyze trends and popular products as well as technical support.
We may use your information to send out periodic emails for promotional, educational, and informing you of new products and services that are being offered. We may use your information (first name. last name initial only and state) for display of testimonial comments for promotion on our website.
We collect your information when you register your account with us. You may update your personal information and contact preferences you provided by logging into your account and selecting the Profile link. Please note that you must have created an account with our website to access this information. You may also contact us if you would like us to delete your registration. Please note that registering your information allows you to access previous order, download your ePatterns, save a wishlist and more. If you have us delete this information, you will no longer be able to access this information, even if you create a new account.
Everything Quilts may use information collected to operate the website and provide service or carry out transactions you have requested.
Data recorded by 3rd party services: For business, analytical and administrative purposes, Everything Quilts utilizes the following 3rd party services:
Scheduled updates and newsletter are handled by Quiltropolis and may have your email, name and/or IP addresses used to open emails and geographical data as provided by browsers. You may update or unsubscribe this information at any time by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our newsletters, or by clicking on the "Contact Us" and sending us an email. We email customers who are not subscribed to our eNewsletter an invitation to join when they place an order with us.
Anonymous data for analytics is collected by Google Analytics and such data does not contain any personal identifiable records of users

This supplement to our global privacy policy applies to the processing of personal information in The Philippines or of Philippine residents and shall be read in conjunction with our global Privacy Policy.
Your Controls and Choices
You may also have the right to lodge a complaint, when appropriate, before the proper regulatory authorities.
Data Transfers, Storage and Processing Globally
Except when you request us to delete your personal information that we collected, we will keep your personal information for as long as necessary for the fulfilment of the purposes for which they were obtained as mentioned in the global Privacy Policy or for the establishment, exercise or defence of relevant legal claims.
We have designated an individual who is responsible for compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 when transferring personal information from the Philippines. We will give you the identity and contact details of such designated individual upon your request.
Point of contact
We welcome your questions, comments and concerns about the above. Please send us any feedback or any other issue at TWDC.DL-SG-DPO@disney.com.

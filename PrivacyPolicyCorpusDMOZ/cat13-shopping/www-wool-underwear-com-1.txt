Privacy Notice
Privacy Policy: wool-underwear.com, collects only the information needed to ship goods and process credit card transactions using SSL(secure socket layer). During the purchase process, we will share your information with PayPal or the Commonwealth Bank of Australia, strictly to process your credit card transaction.
We use Paypal for secure PCI compliant credit card processing.
Comodo SSL Certificate has the
highest possible encryption levels for online transactions.Each certificate is signed with NIST recommended 2048 bit signatures and provides up to 256 bit encryption of customer data.
Cookies: wool-underwear.com shopping cart will use cookies to identify you during your session and to store items in your shopping cart between visits. Cookies are never used to store sensitive customer information such as names, addresses, credit card numbers or passwords.
Automatic Information:We automatically track certain information. This information may include your IP address.
Advertisers: We work with third party advertising companies. These third parties may serve other cookies. These third parties use non-personally identifying information.
Third Party Sites: You may visit other web sites, through links on our site, who may collect, use and share information in accordance with their own privacy policies. The information practices of those websites linking from or to our site, are not covered by this Policy.

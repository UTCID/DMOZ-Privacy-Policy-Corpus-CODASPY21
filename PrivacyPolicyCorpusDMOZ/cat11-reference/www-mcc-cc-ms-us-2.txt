Annual Public Notice
Meridian Community College is an accredited comprehensive Mississippi public community
college that provides university transfer courses, career and technical education
programs, adult basic education and high school equivalency (ABE/HSE) services and
continuing education and workforce training programs which are responsive to the needs
of the East Mississippi community. MCC is committed to providing excellent post-secondary
educational opportunities. MCC does not discriminate on the basis of race, color,
national origin, sex, disability, religion or age in admission or access to, or treatment
or employment in its programs and activities.
MCC ascribes to an “open door” admission policy and offers courses leading to the
Associate in Arts Degree and/or transfer to senior colleges and universities; Associate
of Applied Science Degree and occupational certificate programs, and customized workforce
training, leading to entry-level and/or enhanced employment opportunities. Current
programs offered include:

All career and technical program applicants are required to meet the college’s general
admission criteria as well as the individual program’s admission criteria. Some programs,
such as those in nursing and health education, are competitive and may have required
minimum standards, however, all career and technical education programs are offered
without regard to race, color, national origin, sex, disability, religion or age.
Detailed program requirements maybe found in the MCC Catalog, by calling the advising
center at 601-483-8241 or the MCC website at https://meridiancc.edu/programs/career_and_technical_programs/index.html .

Erasmus+ Charter and Policy
All institutions participating in the Erasmus+ programme are required to adhere to the Erasmus+ Charter for Higher Education (ECHE). Should you wish to participate in the Erasmus+ programme, you may wish to familiarise yourself with the details within the ECHE.

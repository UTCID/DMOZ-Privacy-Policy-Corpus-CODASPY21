Privacy & Security Policy
Privacy Policy
Martin Luther College (MLC) generally collects non-personally identifiable information about you in a number of ways, including tracking of your activities through your IP address, computer settings, or most recently visited URLs. MLC does not collect personally  identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it to us by completing and submitting registration forms or surveys. In general, this information will only be used for the purpose for which it was provided.
MLC may also use this information to deliver information about MLC or information related to the Christian faith. To opt-out of receiving information from us, use the Contact MLC link located on the bottom right of every MLC Web page; mail a letter to MLC, 1995 Luther Court, New Ulm, MN 56073; or call (507) 354-8221 x295.
MLC does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 without the consent of a parent or guardian. In the event that MLC is made aware of such situation, MLC will delete such information and/or attempt to contact a parent or guardian.
Security Policy
MLC websites utilize industry standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software to render information unreadable while it is transferred over the internet.

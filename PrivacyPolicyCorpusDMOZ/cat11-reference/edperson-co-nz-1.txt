EP Child Protection PolicyKaupapa Whakaruru Tamariki
The EP Child Protection Policy outlines our approach to supporting our education sector clients in ensuring child welfare and safety. It describes our management of the Vulnerable Children Act 2015, and details the process and procedure for maintaining the standards and ethics that our clients have come to expect of us. Please contact us if you require any more information.

ISO 9001 Certificate
Cambridge Assessment International Education has held ISO 9001 certification since January 2008. ISO 9001 certification confirms the effectiveness of our quality management system. The management system and activities delivered from our Cambridge offices are regularly independently assessed to make sure we continue to meet the requirements of the ISO 9001 standard and continue to improve our quality management system.
The following activities are certified as meeting the internationally recognised ISO 9001:2015 standard for quality management systems:
Design, development and delivery of products and systems for the provision of international education programmes and qualifications for 5 to 19 year olds
Provision of associated assessment, education services, teacher support and training.

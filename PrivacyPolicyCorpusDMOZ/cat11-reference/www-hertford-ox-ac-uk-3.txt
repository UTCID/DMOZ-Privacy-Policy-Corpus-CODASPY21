Privacy
Hertford College is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data.
Our Privacy Notice notice explains what personal data Hertford may hold about you, how we use it, how we share it, how long we keep it, and your legal rights in relation to it.
Any queries concerning Hertford’s use of your personal data may be referred to our Data Protection Officer, at dpo@hertford.ox.ac.uk

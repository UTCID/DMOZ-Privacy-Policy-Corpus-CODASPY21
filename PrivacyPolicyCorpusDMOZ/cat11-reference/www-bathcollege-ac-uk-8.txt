Funding and Fees
Help and Advice
This section explains the financial help you might get. We keep this information as up to date as we can but please come into our Student Advice Centre for further help and advice about funding if required. Alternatively you can call us on (01761) 438 526 or email studentfunding@bathcollege.ac.uk.

Bursary and Learner Support Fund
The Bursary and Learner Support Fund (LSF) can help students in a variety of different ways.
Bursary and Learner Support Fund
Free Meals
Student 16-18 may be eligible to receive a free meal each day they are at college if their parents receive benefits.
Free Meals
Funding for Carers
If you provide regular unpaid care for someone else such as a family member, friend or neighbour, you may be able to get support.
Funding for Carers

19+ Advanced Learning Loans
A loan for learners aged 19 and over studying in Further Education at Level 3 or above.
19+ Advanced Learning Loans
City and Guilds Bursary
City & Guilds offer a bursary for students wishing to enrol on a course leading to a C&G qualification.

Funding for HE Students
Details of the funding available for Higher Education students.
Funding for HE Students
Welsh Assembly Learning Grant
This grant is for students 19+ who live in Wales and are studying at least 275 guided learning hours.
Welsh Assembly Learning Grant
Residential Support Scheme
If your course is not available close to home and you need to move in order to attend College, you may be able to get funding towards your rent.
Residential Support Scheme
Professional and Career Development Loan
This loan is available to help pay for course expenses.

External Charities and Grants

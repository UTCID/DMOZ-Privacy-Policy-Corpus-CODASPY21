Policies & Guidelines
As an accredited, nonprofit institution of higher learning, Franklin University is committed to ensuring the quality of your college education and the integrity of your onsite and online experiences. As such, we developed a variety of policies, regulations, codes and standards to support a safe and supportive learning environment for students, faculty, staff, visitors and guests.

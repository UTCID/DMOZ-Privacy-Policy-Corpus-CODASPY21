City and Islington College is part of Capital City College Group. Capital City College Group is the trading name of The WKCIC Group, an independent corporation with exempt charity status formed under the Further and Higher Education Act 1992.
The group was formed on 1 August 2016 through the merger of City and Islington and Westminster Kingsway Colleges. Capital City College Training was launched on 23 February 2017, encompassing the group’s apprenticeship and employer engagement provisions. The College of Haringey, Enfield and North East London joined the Group on 1 November 2017.

Cookies
Our website (royalarmouries.org) places small files (‘cookies’) onto your computer to collect information about how you browse the site.
We use cookies to:
measure how you use the website so it can be updated and improved based on your needs
remember the notifications you’ve seen so that we don’t show them to you again
Essential cookies
These cookies allow the site to function properly. They can be blocked using our cookie control tool but this may cause some parts of the site not to function correctly.

Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics software (Universal Analytics) to collect information about how you use royalarmouries.org. This information helps us make improvements and meet the needs of its users.
Google Analytics stores information about:
the pages you visit on royalarmouries.org
how long you spend on each royalarmouries.org page
how you got to the site
what you click on while you’re visiting the site
We do not collect or store any personal information that can be used to identify who you are.
We do not allow Google to use or share our analytics. For details, read the
Google Analytics privacy document.

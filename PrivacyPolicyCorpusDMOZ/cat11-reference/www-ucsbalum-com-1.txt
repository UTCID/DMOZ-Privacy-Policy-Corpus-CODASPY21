UC Santa Barbara Alumni Affinity Partners
We may share your name and address with our affinity partners (companies that we partner with to offer products or services to our alumni).  Our affinity partners may send you offers to purchase various products or services that we have agreed they can offer in partnership with us.
You have the right to control whether we share your name and address with our affinity partners.
Unless you say NO, we may share your name and address with our affinity partners.
You may decide at any time that you do not want us to share your information with our affinity partners. Your choice will remain unless you state otherwise. However, if we do not hear from you, we may share your name and address with our affinity partners.
Declining to share your information with our affinity partners does not prohibit us form sharing your information when we are required to do so by law. This includes sending you information about the alumni association, the university, or other products or services.
Decline from sharing your information with affinity partners
If you decide that you do not want us to share your information with our partners, please fill out and submit the following form and we will no longer share your information.

Every month we learn new words and gain skills to enter kindergarten!
OUR MOST SINCERE THANKS TO THESE FOUNDATIONS AND BUSINESSES WHO SUPPORT MEMPHIS ORAL SCHOOL FOR THE DEAF!
Your generous support funds the unique and specialized speech and language training so that our young students are prepared to go to their local kindergarten with their hearing classmates ready to learn and succeed. You make a life-long impact by giving these children limitless possibilities. Thank you!

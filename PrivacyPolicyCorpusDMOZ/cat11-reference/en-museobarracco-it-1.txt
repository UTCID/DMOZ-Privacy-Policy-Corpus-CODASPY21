Legislative decree n.196 of 30.06.2003 has the aim of guaranteeing that personal data is used in a manner which respects rights, basic freedoms, including the dignity of the physical person, with particular reference to discretion and to personal identity.
In accordance with and in effect of legislative decree 196/2003 notice is given that:
- The personal data of the users of this site is collected and used in accordance with the procedures envisaged under the prevailing norms regarding the protection of personal data. Personal data can only be used for purposes previously declared to the users and on conditions that the users, in circumstances where authorization is required, have expressly authorized it.
- Specific security measures have been observed to prevent the loss of data, its unlawful or incorrect use, and unauthorized access.
- As regards the usage of the data collected, interested subjects can exercise the rights envisaged according to article 7-8-9-10 of the above mentioned legislative decree 196/2003. In particular they can obtain from the owner or from those responsible for the usage of the data confirmation of the existence or otherwise of their own personal data and have it made available to them in an intelligible form; they can be informed of the origins of the data, including the reason and purpose on which its use is based; they can obtain the cancellation of data used in violation of the law, including the adjournment, rectification or integration of data; they can also, for legitimate reasons, object to the usage.- The owner of the data, who is responsible for its use is the Zètema Progetto Cultura srl, whose offices are in Via Attilio Benigni, 59, 00156 Roma, e-mail info@zetema.it

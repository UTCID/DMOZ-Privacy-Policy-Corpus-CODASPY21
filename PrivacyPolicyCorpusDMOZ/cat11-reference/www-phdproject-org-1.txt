Privacy Policy
The PhD Project is dedicated to protecting the confidentiality and privacy of information entrusted to it. As part of this fundamental obligation, The PhD Project is committed to the appropriate protection and use of personal information (sometimes referred to as “personally identifiable information” or “PII”) that has been collected through its Web sites.
Generally, our intent is to collect only the personal information that is provided voluntarily by visitors to our Web sites so that we can offer information and/or services to those individuals or offer information about employment opportunities.

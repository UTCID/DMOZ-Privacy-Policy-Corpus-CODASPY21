Services the College Can Provide for Schools and Students
Attendance at Parents evenings/Careers Fairs/Options/Post 16 choices evening
Assembly talks for year 11 students
College Tours (an overview of the college)
We cannot offer individual taster sessions
College information update sessions for Connexions Advisers and other young people’s agencies
Lunchtime drop-in sessions for students in school (to assist with applications forms and course choices)
Participation in schools mock interview programmes
College information update sessions for Connexions Advisers and other young people’s agencies
If you require further information about any of the above services, please contact us.

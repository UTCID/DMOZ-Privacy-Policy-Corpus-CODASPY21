Thank you for visiting Iowa Central Community College's website. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy notice applies to only the Iowa Central Community College website.
During normal website usage, Iowa Central Community College does not collect or store personally identifiable information such as an individual's name, mailing address, email address, or telephone number. If you choose to share personal information by sending us a message or filling out an electronic form, we will use that information to respond to your request.
The Iowa Central Community College website is designed to provide information and to efficiently provide online services. Following industry standards and best practices, our servers log information about your visit in order to continuously improve its usability. This information does not contain personal data. Pursuant to federal laws and regulations, this data is never matched to personally identifiable information.
If the information you are entering is confidential in nature, look for the icon in the shape of a lock appearing on or near the address bar of your browser, and appropriately use the lock to indicate that the transfer of your information is secure.
If you have any questions or comments about our Privacy Policy, you may send us an email to webmaster@iowacentral.edu

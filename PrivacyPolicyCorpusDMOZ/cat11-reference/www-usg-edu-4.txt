The office of Academic Affairs and Policy supports the efforts of USG institutions to be successful in the classroom and beyond.
With key responsibility for academic and student affairs policy and guidance, as well as opportunity to advance innovation and excellence in key areas, the office supports collaborative activities on campuses that advance the System’s core mission. Academic Affairs and Policy promotes excellence in the classroom, drives innovation in delivery and pedagogy, and supports quality instruction and curriculum across the state.

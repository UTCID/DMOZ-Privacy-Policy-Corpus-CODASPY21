Old York Historical Society does not share personal information with other institutions or businesses. OYHS is committed to protecting your privacy when you visit our website, or use the website to purchase tickets to our event, obtain an OYHS membership, or make a donation. OYHS does not collect personal information about individuals, such as names, mailing addresses, and email addresses, unless the individual knowingly provides it.

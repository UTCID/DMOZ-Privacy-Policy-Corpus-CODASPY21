Overview of Website Policies
Accessibility Policy
UT Southwestern Medical Center is committed to making its website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. This policy addresses those features included in our site designed to improved accessibility. Read the Accessibility Policy.
Compact with Texans
UT Southwestern Medical Center is committed to becoming the premier public academic health university in the nation and providing customer service and assurance to the public on their investment and confidence. This compact details our goal, objectives, and core values in achieving this commitment. Read the Compact with Texans.
Notice About Certain Information, Laws, and Practices
UT Southwestern Medical Center patients are, with few exceptions, entitled to be informed about the information we collect about them. This information outlines our patients’ rights in receiving, reviewing, and correcting this information, as well as the guidelines regarding retention of records. Read the Notice.
Privacy Policy
UT Southwestern Medical Center is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our visitors. This policy educates visitors about privacy issues regarding the use of this site. Read the Privacy Policy.
Website Linking Policy
This outlines UT Southwestern’s policies for linking to and from all UT Southwestern web properties, our link acceptance criteria, the review and removal processes, and conditions for link maintenance. Read the Website Linking Policy.

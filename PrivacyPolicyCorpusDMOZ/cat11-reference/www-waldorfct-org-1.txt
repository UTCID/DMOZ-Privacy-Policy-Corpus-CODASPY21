Thank you for visiting Housatonic Valley Waldorf School’s website. The purpose of this site is to share information about the School with current and prospective parents, staff, alumni and alumni families, and those interested in Waldorf education. This website is only intended for adults and should not be used by children under the age of 13. All content is protected and may not be modified or reproduced without permission from the School. While we make every attempt to keep the information on the website up-to-date we disclaim all liability for errors or omissions.
Privacy Policy
Housatonic Valley Waldorf School is committed to maintaining the privacy of our website visitors and we handle your personal information in a confidential manner. No personally identifiable information (name, address, e-mail address, etc…) is collected unless you knowingly submit it to us. Personally identifiable information voluntarily submitted via a form on our website will never be shared with a third party for commercial purposes and will only be used for school communication. All online payments are encrypted.
As a general visitor to the HVWS website, we track the pages you view for ourperformance records using Google Analytics. For each visitor to our website, our web server recognizes the visitor’s domain name, IP address, and the date and time of the session. If you would like to opt-out of this type of tracking,Google has created a Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available here.
Our website uses cookies (small text files sent to and stored by web browsers) to remember the preferences of our website visitors. You may modify the settings of your web browser(s) to accept, reject, or delete cookies. Please note that rejecting or deleting cookies may negatively affect your web browsing experience.
If you would like to opt out of school communication at any time, please e-mail us.

Privacy Policy
The Heldrich Center has created this privacy policy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy and to describe the information-gathering practices for our website. We do not collect personally identifiable information about our website visitors. We use third-party vendors such as Google Analytics to track data on visits, activity, and trends on our website for the sole purpose of improving our website presentation and utilization. We do not sell information collected on the website or share it with others, unless it is required by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary in order to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process involving the website. The Heldrich Center website contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of linked websites.

Accessibility Standards
TERC is committed to providing an environment that is accessible to individuals with disabilities. If you cannot access content or use features on our website due to a disability, please let us know.
We
endeavor to adhere to The Worldwide Web Consortium’s Web Content Accessibility
Guidelines version 2.0, Level AA Conformance (WCAG 2.0 Level AA) to the highest
degree possible.
Exceptions
Downloadable PDFs and third-party
content may not be assumed to meet accessibility standards.

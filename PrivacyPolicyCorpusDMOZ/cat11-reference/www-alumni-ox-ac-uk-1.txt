PRIVACY STATEMENT
This website runs on Oxford Mosaic, the University of Oxford’s web platform. The Oxford Mosaic Privacy Statement explains what types of personal information will generally be gathered when you visit sites created using the platform.
For information on the data held about alumni, donors and supporters, see the University of Oxford– Alumni, Donors and Supporters Privacy Notice.
This website collects user demographic information via Google Analytics, this information is not for public consumption and is used for benchmarking by the Alumni Office. You can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page or control the use of device identifiers by using your device’s settings.
Other My Oxford Online websites - using the alumniweb.ox.ac.uk domain - use a different platform, and information on the information gathered through those sites can be found in the specific information reached through those pages, and on this cookies information page.

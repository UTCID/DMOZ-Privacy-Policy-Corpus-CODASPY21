Principles of Good Practice
Looking for PGPs?
The NAIS Principles of Good Practice for member schools define high standards and ethical behavior in key areas of independent school operations. The PGPs reflect the overall dedication to quality education that has always characterized independent schools.
Find a PGP
Sharing stories about inspired learning in independent schools
Focusing on Student Wellness, Mindfulness
Leaders at The Bolles School (FL) have been reflecting on ways the school community can help students manage stress, make healthy choices, and better emphasize wellness in their daily lives. This focus has resulted in many new programs and initiatives, including an increased number of teachers facilitating mindfulness techniques in the classroom at almost every grade level.

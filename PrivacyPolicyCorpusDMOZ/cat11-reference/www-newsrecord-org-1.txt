Our server automatically recognizes information from each visitor’s domain and browser so that our web pages display and function properly. The servers discard that information afterwards.We use cookies to store visitor preferences and record session information.The News Record collects information from users who post comments, upload content, or communicate with staff through web forms or email. The News Record reserves the right to aggregate information volunteered by the user. This information may include survey responses, site registrations, name, address, phone or fax number, and payment information.The News Record will not provide identifying information to anyone, not even the person for whom the information was collected. Users may request to have information corrected by sending an email to the address below.The News Record does not share any identifying information with external entities, except when legally required to do so. Such circumstances include, but are not limited to, requests by government authorities that are conducting investigations, efforts to verify or enforce our website use policies, and efforts to protect against misuses or unauthorized uses of our website.When we receive and transfer payment and other sensitive information, our server redirects visitors to a secure server and notifies users of this redirection. This server has appropriate security measures in place to protect sensitive information.The News Record has, or may enter into, relationships with specific third parties, allowing them to post advertisements on our website. Those companies may collect information such as your domain name, your IP address and clickstream data.In the future, we may retain visitor information for new, unanticipated uses chosen to enhance our web offerings.

Legal and Privacy Policy
Under the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA), higher education institutions must take steps
to ensure the privacy, security, and confidentiality of customer records. Montana
State University Billings has registered or otherwise protected the names, logos,
symbols, taglines, indicia, insignia, trade names, service marks, and trademarks (collectively
"Trademarks") of the University and its programs. The University has delegated the
responsibility for maintaining, managing and licensing University trademarks to the
University Communications and Marketing Department.

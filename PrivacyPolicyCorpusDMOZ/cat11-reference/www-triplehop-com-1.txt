Oracle and TripleHop
Oracle Buys TripleHop
Oracle has completed the acquisition of certain assets of TripleHop Technologies, Inc. ("TripleHop"), a provider of context-sensitive enterprise search products and technology, to extend Oracle's development of comprehensive enterprise search. We would like to welcome TripleHop customers to Oracle.
Search is a key technology, not only for higher productivity, but also for making better decisions. By joining the talents of both organizations, our customers will benefit from the superior functionality offered through the combination of our technologies. Our combined scale and expertise will enable us to accelerate and deliver innovative search solutions.
As we work to integrate TripleHop and Oracle technologies, we will provide a roadmap for the resulting product set, as well as any proposed migration path for current and future TripleHop MatchPoint customers. We look forward to our continued relationship with TripleHop's MatchPoint users. We will keep you apprised as our work progresses.

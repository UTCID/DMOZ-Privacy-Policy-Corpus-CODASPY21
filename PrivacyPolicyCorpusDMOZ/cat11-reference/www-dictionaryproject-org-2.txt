Our Commitment to Our Sponsors and Donors
We will not sell,
share or trade our sponsors’ or donors’ names or personal
information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on
behalf of other organizations.
This policy
applies to all information received by The Dictionary Project, both
online and offline, on any Platform (“Platform”, includes
The Dictionary Project’s websites and mobile applications), as well
as any electronic, written, or oral communications.
To the extent any
donations are processed through a third-party service provider, our
donors’ information will only be used for purposes necessary to
process the donation.

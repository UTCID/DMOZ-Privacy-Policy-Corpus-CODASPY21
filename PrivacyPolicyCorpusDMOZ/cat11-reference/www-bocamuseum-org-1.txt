We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, shared or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to the Boca Raton Museum of Art will be held with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us know.
Resale or Disclosure of Information to Third Parties:
The Boca Raton Museum of Art does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.
Browser Information Collected on the Website:
The Boca Raton Museum of Art analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value of the materials available on website. Our website logs are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Privacy of our Email List:
The Boca Raton Museum of Art maintains several email lists to keep Boca Raton Museum of Art members informed about breaking events. Individuals must affirmatively ask to join our mailing lists by signing up on our website or contacting Boca Raton Museum of Art staff directly at  boca@bocamuseum.org. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized Boca Raton Museum of Art, including other list subscribers.

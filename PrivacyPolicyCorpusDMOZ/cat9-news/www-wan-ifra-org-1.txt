Privacy Policy
Thank you for visiting our website and for your interest in our association, as well as our content, services and products. It is important to us to protect your personal data throughout the entire process and we want you to feel secure when engaging with us online. In our privacy policy, we explain what information WAN-IFRA collects when you visit our website and how this information is used.Contact
If you have any queries concerning the processing of your personal data, kindly contact the WAN-IFRA Data Protection Officer who will be pleased to assist you with all requests for information, proposals or complaints.

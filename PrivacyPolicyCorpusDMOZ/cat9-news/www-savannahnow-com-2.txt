UPDATE: Port Wentworth police locate missing 16-year-old
Savannah Morning News
Friday
Sep 25, 2020 at 1:56 PM
UPDATE: Cooper has been located, according to Port Wentworth police.The Port Wentworth Police Department is looking for a missing 16-year-old named Zikee Cooper.Cooper was last seen on Thursday at 1:30 p.m., wearing a white polo shirt, black jeans and white Tommy Hilfiger shoes. He is possibly traveling in a Mazda 6.He is type-one diabetic and has not taken his medication in three days. He is possibly on the east side of Savannah.Call 911 if you have seen Cooper or have any additional information.

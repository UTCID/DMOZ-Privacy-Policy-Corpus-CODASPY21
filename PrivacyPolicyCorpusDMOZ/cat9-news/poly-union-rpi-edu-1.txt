Privacy policy
The following discloses The Polytechnic‘s information-gathering and dissemination practices.Information gatheringWe use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our web servers and to administer our website by identifying which parts of our site are most heavily visited. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means that user sessions will be tracked, but the visitors will remain anonymous.Use of informationThe Polytechnic‘s staff uses the information gathered above to tailor site content to visitor needs and to generate aggregate statistical reports.

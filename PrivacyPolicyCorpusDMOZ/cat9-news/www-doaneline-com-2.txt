Some features of the Doane Line may require detailed contact information, including but not limited to, name, address, city, state, zip code, phone and email. This information is used to allow us to respond to inquiries for additional information. We will only disclose this information when we are legally required to do so. Such circumstances include, but are not limited to, the request of governmental authorities conducting an investigation, to verify or enforce compliance with the policies governing our Web site and applicable laws or to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our Web site. This policy does not, however, constitute waiver by the Doane Line or its staff of any applicable statutory or constitutional privilege protecting the confidentiality of newsgathering sources or information.Features on our website may give you the chance to communicate with other site visitors through blogs, forums and comments. When you use these features, other visitors may collect your data. This is usually in the form of your username or "screen name." Ad server companies may collect information such as your domain type, your IP address and clickstream information.We do not rent, sell, trade or otherwise release the information given to us by site visitors.It is not our policy to send spam or unsolicited emails to you. While some features will be unavailable without disclosure of contact information, visitors have the option to opt-out.Our postal address is:1014 Boswell Ave.,Doane University,Crete,NE. 68333.We can be reached via:e-mail at doaneline@doane.edu,or by telephone at (402) 826-8657.Our web server automatically recognizes information regarding each visitor's domain and browser type to best display the page requested. The server then discards that information.Doaneline collects unique identifying information on users who:• post comments to article pages• post comments to news logs or community blogs• communicate with staff members of the Doane Line via e-mailDoaneline reserves the right to aggregate information regarding what pages consumers access or visit and information volunteered by the consumer, such as:• survey information• site registrations• name• address• telephone number• fax number The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web page. If you supply us with your address, telephone number or email, you will only receive the information for which you provided us that contact information. If you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please let us know by sending us email at the above address or by using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email you wish to discontinue.For security and legal reasons, Doaneline will not provide any information we have collected to anyone, even the person the information has been collected about. Upon request, we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information. Community members can request that this information corrected by sending an email at the above address. We use cookies to store visitors' preferences and record session information. From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change in the future, we will contact you before we use your data for these new purposes to notify you of the policy change and to provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses.If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the above addresses or phone number. CHILDREN'S GUIDELINES We do not knowingly collect information from children since we assume all visitors to Doaneline are of legal age. Content on Doaneline may not be appropriate for children and may contain mature material. Parental guidance is encouraged.

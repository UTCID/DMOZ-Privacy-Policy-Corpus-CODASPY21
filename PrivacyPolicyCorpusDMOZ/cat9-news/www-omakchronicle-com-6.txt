Details for FOOD SERVICE 3Updated
Oct 5, 2020
FOOD SERVICE 3Okanogan School District has an opening for the above position. Position is 5 days per week, 2 hours per day. Successful applicant needs HS Diploma or GED, WA State Driver’s License and current Food Handlers Permit. Please submit a letter of interest and application to the Okanogan School District, P.O. Box 592, Okanogan, WA 98840. For more information, call Brandy Wilson at 509-422-3629. www.oksd.wednet.edu. EOE

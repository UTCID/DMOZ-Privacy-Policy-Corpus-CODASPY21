This applies to the Cannon Beach Gazette's website and social media outlets:  1.  Be Civil. No bullying, name calling, insulting or verbally attacking others. 2.  Keep it Clean. We’d love to hear eyewitness accounts, the history behind an article. 8.  WARNING: Abuse of these rules will result in the thread being disabled, comments denied, and/or user blocked. 9.  Stay On Topic.

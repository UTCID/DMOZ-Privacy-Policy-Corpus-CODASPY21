Privacy Policy
News-Banner Publications, Inc. values your privacy. Your registration and subscription information is subject to our Privacy Policy. Any and all personal information you provide us for purposes of registration or subscription will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, rented, or loaned to anyone outside News-Banner Publications except as may be required by law or valid court order.
Forum-wide registration data may be aggregated for use and distribution by News-Banner Publications but no personally identifiable information will be released.
Use of the website involves use of “cookies.”. This includes retention of your username and password on the computer you are working on, which helps track your last-read message when you revisit the forum. If your browser doesn’t support cookies, or if you have cookies turned off, these preferences will not be set.
We advise you to remember to log out after each session to prevent unauthorized access under your account.

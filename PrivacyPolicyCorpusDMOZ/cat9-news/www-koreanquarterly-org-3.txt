Korean Quarterly is an independent, non-profit (501c3) news organization which serves the Korean American community.  It accepts original writing, photography, artwork and other material for publication.
All submissions are voluntary contributions, and Korean Quarterly may decline to publish submitted material at the discretion of the editor.
Korean Quarterly reserves the right to edit submitted news, features, and opinion for conventional editing qualities such as style, clarity, brevity, grammar, spelling, punctuation, word choice, and order of information.
Korean Quarterly also accepts material for reprint, with permission.
All of Korean Quarterly’s content is copyrighted by Korean Quarterly.
All submitted material are assumed for publication in both the print and online version of Korean Quarterly.
To inquire about submitting to us, or to send unsolicited material for consideration, please use the “Leak to Us” link at the bottom of the website.

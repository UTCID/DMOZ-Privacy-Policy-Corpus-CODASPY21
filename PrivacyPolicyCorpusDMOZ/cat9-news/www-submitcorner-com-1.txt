Tell a Friend About This Page
Submit Corner (A wholy owned website of Wired 2000 Corporation),
its partners and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and concerns our Internet
users may have. We have outlined below information and our guidelines that we observe for responsible
management of our client and customer information.
The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices
for this Web site: Submit Corner
Information Automatically Logged
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site.
Additionally, we use your IP address to determine network traffic based per country of origin
from aggreggated data. We do not archive individual IP addresses, only volume and percentage
breakdown per country.
Information Collected From Our Tools
Our Tools automatically log your email, date of activity and website
address to help diagnose problems with our tools or network activity.
Additionally, we use this information to compose aggreggated statistics of usage. Your personal
information will not be released or divulged to any other parties.
Advertisers
We use an outside ad company to display advertisements on our site. These ads may contain
cookies and the associated information is collected by our ad company, and
we do not have access to this information.
External Links
This site contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for
the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration
of the information under our control (email, date and website addresses).
Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site,
or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact us by the form on our site.

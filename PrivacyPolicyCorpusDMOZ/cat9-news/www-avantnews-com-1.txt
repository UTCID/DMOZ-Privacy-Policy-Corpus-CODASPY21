Privacy
Internet user privacy and confidentiality are of the utmost importance to avantnews.com.
All visitors to avantnews.com retain complete anonymity. As content or
ads are delivered, we do not acquire or collect any personal information about
you. Your name, address, phone number, and email address all remain your private
properties. avantnews.com does, however, collect information which is
specific to how you use the Internet. We note non-personally identifying details
such as, but not limited to, the server you are logged onto, the geographic
location of the ISP you use, the type of browser you use, and your IP address.
This information is obtained solely for the purpose of evaluating site traffic
in general terms. We do not share any of the information we may collect with
any other parties.

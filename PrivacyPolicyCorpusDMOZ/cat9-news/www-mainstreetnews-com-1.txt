Internet websites exist to allow for and promote interaction. In that interaction, users may provide information either anonymously or purposely about themselves, either statistically or personally. mainstreetnews.com is such a site.Visits to mainstreetnews.com are monitored for the accumulation of statistical information, including the number of visits to the site, the pages people visit, from which domains the visitors come and the browsers people use to visit the site. Such information is important to allow MainStreet to evaluate the effectiveness of the services it providies through mainstreetnews.com, to monitor its own performance and to make it easier for visitors to use the site.MainStreet Newspapers will strive to maintain the confidentiality of all user communications which contain personal user information and which are transmitted directly to mainstreetnews.com. Posting by a user on any bulletin board or in any chat roomis of course not protected as confidential information or confidential communication. MainStreet Newspapers shall have the right, but not the obligation, to monitor the content of its bulletin boards and chat rooms to determine compliance with mainstreetnews.com policies and terms of use and any other operating rules established for mainstreetnews.com. MainStreet Newspapers shall have the right, in its sole discretion, and without prior notice, to edit, refuse to post or remove any materials submitted to or posted to its bulletin boards or chat rooms.Various web sites collect user information and create lists and/or databases utilizing the same. These lists or databases are sometimes sold to third parties under contractual agreements that place limits on how the purchasing parties can reuse the information. In the future, MainStreet Newspapers, through mainstreetnews.com, may participate in this activity. If it chooses to do so, MainStreet Newspapers will notify the users of mainstreetnews.com of such participation and of the users' option to exclude their information from any such lists or databases that may be made available to third parties.For further information about mainstreetnews.com, see its Copyright Notice and Terms of Use.

Delivery
Orders UNDER $300 will be shipped directly to the shipping address you provided. You will receive a tracking number by e-mail. If you are not home during the day
to receive your order, a Delivery Notice Card will be left. The card will contain information about the local post office your order has been redirected to.
It will be held for 15 business days to allow time for you to pick it up. If you do not pick up your order within those 15 business days, it will
be shipped back to our warehouse.
Orders OVER $300 will be shipped directly to your local post office and you will receive a Delivery Notice Card. Photo identification and a signature
will be required to pick-up your order.

online, at any time. We also reserve the right to change contents, features, and offerings of the site from time to time at our discretion without prior notice.
Unsuccessful Delivery Attempts
If your order is returned to our warehouse after an attempt to deliver has been made, we will contact you to confirm the shipping address information you entered is correct.
Once confirmed, we will re-ship your order. If you fail to receive your order after the second delivery attempt and it is returned to us again, we will cancel your order. Your
credit card will be refunded minus the shipping cost for the second delivery attempt and a $10.00 restocking fee.
Track Your Order
You will receive a shipment notification email from Fedex or Canada Post once your order has been shipped out. The email will contain a tracking number which can be used
to track your delivery status online.
Unavailable Items
If an item you purchase is unexpectedly out of stock, the rest of your order will be filled and shipped out and the missing item(s) will be cancelled and your card refunded. If the
item(s) cancelled results in your order total to be less than $49, we will still ship it for free and you will not be charged for shipping.
Order Cancellations
Orders may be cancelled at any time prior to being shipped without penalty. If an order is cancelled after it has been shipped but before it has been delivered to you, it will be subject
to a $10.00 restocking fee plus any shipping fees incurred. Your card will not be refunded until after the order has been returned to our warehouse. Once you have accepted delivery of your
order, it can no longer be cancelled. If you need to cancel your order, please contact us here with your order details.

INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED AS YOU NAVIGATE OUR WEBSITES
When you visit our websites, you will be assigned a permanent "cookie" (a small, unique identifier text file) to be stored on your computer's hard drive. The purpose of the cookie is to identify you when you visit our Site so we can enhance and customize your online experience. You can choose to use this website without cookies if you wish. To use the website without cookies, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or notify you when a cookie is set. If you do not use cookies to browse our website, some of the functionality of the website may not work.
Usage data is collected about your visits to our website, including the number of visits and your location (IP address), length of time spent on the site, pages clicked on and where you originally came from to reach our website. Additionally, we collect other usage data including your browser type and language, access times, searches done on our websites and other browsing habit information. We collect this usage and browsing habit information in order to help personalize your experience on our websites as well as to better modify our web services to meet the needs of our customers.
When you access our Website from a mobile device like a tablet or phone, we may receive information about your location and your device, including a unique identifier for your device and your mobile carrier. We may use this information to provide you with location-based services, such as language choices, search results, and other personalized content. Most devices allow you to turn off location services.
We use analytics tools and other third party technologies, such as Google Analytics and Next-Generation Affiliate Tracking Software, to collect information in the form of various usage and user metrics when you use our Website. These analytic tools provide data that allows this website to study broad demographics of who is visiting our websites and when, but also allows us to advertise our products through affiliates.
Some content or applications are provided by third parties within our sites. These third parties are currently chat and cam companies. These third parties may use cookies alone or in conjunction with other tracking technologies to collect information about you when you use our Website. The information they collect may be associated with your personal information or they may collect information, including personal information, about your online activities over time and across different websites and other online services. They may use this information to provide you with interest-based advertising or other targeted content.

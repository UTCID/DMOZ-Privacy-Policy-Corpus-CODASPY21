MPL Studios Privacy Policy
By visiting mplstudios.com or sweet-lilya.com, you are accepting the practices described in our Privacy Policy.
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy please contact us by email at
webmaster [@] mplstudios.com
What kind of information do you collect?
When you join the membership portion of our web site you are asked to provide your name, address, e-mail, and credit card number.
This information is collected and processed using SSL encryption, provided by a third party payment processor.
When users request pages from our web server, our server automatically collects some information about the users, including
their IP addresses, location, browser type, login dates and time, and download history. We also collect information regarding
your use of our site through ‘cookies’.
We use cookies to improve the quality of your browsing experience, for storing your site
preferences,
and tracking user trends, such as how people surf the site. These cookies do not contain any private information and
cannot be traced back to you as a user in any way. If you send us personal correspondence, such as emails or letters, we may collect
such information into a file specific to you. We will maintain a record or notes of
communications with you regarding your account.
How do you use my information?
We use your information solely to process your credit card payment, to facilitate your viewing of our web site, and to communicate
with you. We also use this information to compile broad demographic and website usage information about our users in general that
does not contain anything that could be used to identify you personally. If requested by you, site owner
uses your e-mail address to
send periodic e-mail newsletters and promotional e-mail to you about services and products offered by us, You will not receive any
e-mail from us unless you have requested it and can indicate at any time that you do not wish to receive e-mail information from us.
We do not provide your e-mail to third parties without your express consent.
How secure is my information?
Site owner takes reasonable precautions to maintain the security of any information obtained from and about you. We provide you with
a secure payment gateway to submit your billing information when you join the membership portion of our website. We maintain
your personally identifiable information in a secure operating environment, including storing such information behind a firewall.

PRIVACY POLICY
LatinBoyz.Com is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. Please read the following policy to understand how your personal information will be treated as you make use of our service.

Information collected during the sign-up process and usage of membership may be used to dispute credit card chargebacks and/or fraudulent transactions.
This listing is not intended to cover every possibility.
If you have any other questions that are not addressed above, please feel free to contact us at service@latinboyz.com

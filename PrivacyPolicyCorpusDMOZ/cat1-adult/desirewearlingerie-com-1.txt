DesireWear Payments, Privacy and Security Information
Paying for your purchases at DesireWear
At Desirewear (DesireShoes & DesireToys too) we accept the following form of payments
MasterCard
VISA
PayPal
Check
Money Orders
Discover, American Express, and electronic Check payments are currently accepted through our PayPal account (DesireShoes.com).
If you do not already have a PayPal account and want to use this method for payment,
you can sign up for one (FREE)
during our checkout process or by visiting www.PayPal.com.
Please rest assured that by using PayPal your data is secure and you have the same
protections as if you had paid directly with your credit card.
If you are paying by Check or Money Order, mail your payment to
DesireWear Lingerie
Attn: Sales
360 Lynwood Drive
Severna Park, Md 21146
Please include your order number (or print out the invoice and enclose) with any payment sent.
Security
All personal and payment information is transferred between your personal computer and
DesireWear across an encrypted conection using what is called Secure Sockets Layer (SSL).
When you create or log into your account you will notice that the address in your browsers address bar
changes from http://www.DesireWearLingerie.com/... to https://www.DesireWearLingerie.com/DesireShoes/...
and your browser will display a small padlock which indicates that all data being entered is being encrypted (scrambled).
We also do NOT store your credit card information on our server, so for returning customers
that is why you need to re-enter your credit
card information.
Privacy
All information that you enter is strictly confidential and will not be divulged to any 3rd parties.
We may make use of "cookies" to make your shopping experience better and we do use JavaScript for the
popup window used for large pictures.
If you would like to better protect your privacy and have a more secure internet experience ...we provide a link at the bottom of each page to the FireFox browser.
If you have any questions that we did not address, please send us a message using the "contact us"
link, or give us a call.

Online Chat Rooms and In-site Communications
A submissives journey does make random checks of open chat room
logs and may periodically monitor open chat room conversations and
activities to ensure all activities are in accordance with Asj Guidelines
and in the best interest of the Asj Web Site and membership.  On
occasion, some of the logs of these conversations or discussions may be
posted on the Asj web site (for example, class or discussion logs).
Direct communication between individual site members (Private Messages
(PM's) and on site messaging (Site Messages) are not monitored and are
considered a mutually agreeable private conversation between the members
involved.  No attempt will be made to monitor the content or nature of
these conversations, however all members are reminded that such
conversations or messaging should only be done when all members
participating in the conversation are doing so willfully and voluntarily.
No member may insist that another member join them in that conversation, nor
should any member abuse this resource by sending continued unwarranted or
undesired messages. Members are reminded, it is against Asj policy to post
real life, personally identifying information (such as real names,
addresses, telephone numbers, etc.) in an open chat room forum.  Asj
does not maintain logs or recordings of video and audio chats.

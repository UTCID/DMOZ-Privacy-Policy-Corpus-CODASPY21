Privacy Information
PRIVACY
We take your privacy seriously!  BootyCocktails.com will never sell or share your personally identifiable information (PII) with anyone other than our payment provider and supplier shipping your order.
Tracking is limited to functionality of our website only, and we do not send marketing communications unrelated to active orders unless you specifically ask for them by opting-in.
Furthermore, for an added layer of security, we do not have direct access to your payment information for any reason.
If any questions about how we collect and use your information, or to request we delete your PII completely (which will also delete your account and history), please contact us.

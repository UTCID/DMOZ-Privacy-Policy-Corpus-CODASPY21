Central California Swimming (CCS) provides online membership registration services for individuals, both athlete and non-athlete, who wish to become members of CCS and its parent organization, USA Swimming. CCS also provides online meet entry services for athletes wishing to participate in swimming competitions sanctioned by CCS.
CCS has a "No Refund" policy. All monetary transactions associated with membership registrations are considered final and CCS will not issue refunds for those transactions. All monetary transactions associated with meet entries are considered final and CCS will not issue refunds, partial or full, for those transactions, unless the swim meet is cancelled (full refund) or, in order to comply with time limitation rules, swimmers are required to scratch one or more events (partial refund).

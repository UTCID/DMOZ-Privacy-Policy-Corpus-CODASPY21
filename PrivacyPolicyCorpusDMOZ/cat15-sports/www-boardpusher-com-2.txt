About BoardPusher / Privacy
Other than the information which you supply willingly via the BoardPusher.com
website (like username, email, etc...), the only information that is collected
by BoardPusher is basic information about the internet communication between
your PC and the BoardPusher.com website. This information includes but is not
limited to your IP address, operating system, type of browser, and list
of pages you visit. This information is collected in order to improve the overall
flow and functionality of the BoardPusher.com website.
No information collected via BoardPusher.com will ever be divulged or sold
to a third party.
For more information about the BoardPusher.com Privacy Policy,  please
feel free to
contact us.

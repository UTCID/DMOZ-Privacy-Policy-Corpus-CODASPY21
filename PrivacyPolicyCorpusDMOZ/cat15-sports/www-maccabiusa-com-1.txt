Maccabi USA Privacy Policy
Maccabi USA will not trade, share or sell a donor’s personal information with anyone else, nor send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations.
Please let us know if you do not want your name excluded from emails by opting out. We will respect your wishes.
If you would prefer not to receive information about Maccabi USA’s programs, please send your full name and street address exactly as they appear on your mailing to maccabi@maccabiusa.com.

Open Flame Policy Variance Request
Residential Housing Fire Safety
This form should be used to request exceptions to the open flame policy on Westminster
College campus. This form is not required for pre-approved exceptions to the policy,
which can be found on the Fire and Life Safety webpage.
The Campus Fire Marshal will review your request and contact you with any questions,
approval status, and/or special conditions that may be required for your request to
be approved.

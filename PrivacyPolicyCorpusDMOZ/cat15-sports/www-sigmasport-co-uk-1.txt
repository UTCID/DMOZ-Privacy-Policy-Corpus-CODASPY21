Sigma Sports Legal
Cycling specific claims management brought to you by Sigma Sports Legal. Offering a unique service for where the repair or replacement of your bike is carried out upfront, without having to wait for the insurance company to pay out. Our service is designed to save you time and effort in what can be a stressful and even traumatic event. There is no cost to you involved, just peace of mind knowing you will be back riding in no time at all.
Services we Provide
Whether you are insured or not, Sigma Sports Legal are cycling accident specialists, designed to get you back riding in no time at all. Below are the services we offer:
Arrange the repair or replacement of your bike quickly
Replacement of clothing and accessories
Make provisions to legal representation
Ensure loss of earnings are covered
Address out of pocket expenses
Why Choose Sigma Sports Legal
Sigma Sports arrange the repair or replacement of your bike upfront. There is no need to wait until the insurance company pays out
We take the stress out of an already traumatic experience, allowing you to carry on with your day to day activities
A fair and proper valuation of your bike will be carried out by a local bike shop
Valuation of bike can be carried out at the bike shop of your choice

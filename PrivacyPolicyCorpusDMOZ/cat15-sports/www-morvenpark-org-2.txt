Enjoy these Yoga in the Park videos made possible by a partnership with RealeYoga.com.Click on image to play video.
Beginner Yoga:
If you haven't yet tried it, Anna Reale (RealeYoga.com) has put together a perfect "starter" video where you can take part in yoga for beginners.
Vinyasa flow:
Anna Reale of RealeYoga.com leads this yoga session which is a Vinyasa Flow offering suitable for all skill levels.
Yin yoga:
This video features Anna Reale of RealeYoga.com offering basic yoga moves and gentle stretches. We hope you enjoy it!
Meditation Moment:
Anna Reale of RealeYoga.com will lead you through an all-levels 10-minute meditation that focuses on checking in on the body, breath control, and clearing the mind. This practice can be done while lying on your bed or on a yoga mat.
Yoga for hips and glutes:
Gentle yoga moves to help relax and stretch hips and glutes.
Nadi Shodhana:
Join Anna Reale from RealeYoga.com for Nadi Shodhana, a breathing exercise that uses an alternate nostril technique to improve stress, help you focus, and calm the mind.
#NamasteFriday:
Anna Reale of RealeYoga.com was kind enough to visit us and film this week's session of #YogaInThePark on the Davis Mansion portico. #NamasteFriday!
Spinal flexibility:
This #YogaInThePark video features Anna Reale from RealeYoga.com demonstrating stretches for spinal flexibility.
Forward Fold:
Anna provides an in depth tutorial on the Forward Fold, a beginner yoga stretch that can be done by anyone.
Viloma:
This week's Yoga in the Park video highlights a breathing exercise called Viloma. Enjoy!

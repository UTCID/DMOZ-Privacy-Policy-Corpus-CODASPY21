Voluntarily Submitted Information
White Horse Golf Club collects personal data about you when you choose to provide it.
On certain pages, visitors have the opportunity to provide personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, and e-mail address in order to request details about our company and our product.
The personal information visitors provide is used to respond to our visitors’ inquiries.
Automatically Collected Information
White Horse Golf Club uses Web site tracking software to capture technical information that is stored on our server log files.
This information may include, but is not limited to, user domain, the type of Internet browser being used, which of our Web pages are visited and the amount of time spent on our site.
Feedback
On the contact page of our Web site, we offer a contact form in which you can use to offer us any feedback about our Web site.
White Horse Golf Club’s web site contains links to other web sites. Please be aware that White Horse Golf Club is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites.

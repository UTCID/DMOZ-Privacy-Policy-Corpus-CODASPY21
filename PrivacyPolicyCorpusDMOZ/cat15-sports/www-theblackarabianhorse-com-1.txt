PRIVACY STATEMENT
TheBlackArabianHorse.com is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of any personal information received from you.
The purpose of this statement is to explain to you what personal information we collect and how we may use it.
When you submit information to us for inclusion on the site
it may include personal details such as name, address, contact telephone
number, fax number, email address and web site address. We may also
request personal details.
We use your personal
details to accompany the information you have asked us to place on the site.
We do not sell, rent or exchange your personal information with any third party for commercial reasons.
We do not collect sensitive information about you except when you specifically knowingly provide it.
In order to maintain the accuracy of our web site, you can check, update or
remove information or personal details you have submitted to us by emailing
ads@theblackarabianhorse.com.
If you have any questions about privacy please contact us at
ads@theblackarabianhorse.com.

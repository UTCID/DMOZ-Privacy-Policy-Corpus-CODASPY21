PRIVACY POLICYEffective: January 1, 2020Last Revised: January 21, 2020This Privacy Policy describes how Anaheim Ducks Hockey Club, LLC ("Anaheim Ducks") treats your information when you visit our websites, use our mobile apps, purchase tickets or other products, register for a promotion through us, or otherwise interact with the Anaheim Ducks, including through its programs, including without limitation, Anaheim Ducks S.C.O.R.E., Anaheim Ducks G.O.A.L. Hockey Program, Anaheim Ducks Die Hards and the Wild Wingers Kids Club. It also describes how your information is used and disclosed to other parties.By interacting with and providing your information to us, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. You also agree that the Anaheim Ducks may share your Personal Information (defined below) among its affiliates (including affiliated entities, sponsors, and vendors). If you do not agree, you must refrain from using our services or interacting with us.  HOW WE TREAT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATIONWe collect information from you that identifies, describes, or is reasonably capable of being associated with you or your household ("Personal Information"). Sometimes we also receive your Personal Information from our service providers and marketing partners. Personal Information does not include publicly available information in government records or any data that has been deidentified, aggregated, or otherwise anonymized.We collect and use different kinds of Personal Information for different reasons depending on how you interact with us. The paragraphs in this section describe how we collect, use, and disclose Personal Information based on these types of interactions.The Anaheim Ducks does not and will not sell your Personal Information to third parties without first providing you appropriate notice.When You Use Our Online ServicesWhen you access Anaheim Ducks' websites, visit one of our ticketing platforms, or other otherwise interact with our services online or through the mobile app, the Anaheim Ducks collects the following categories of Personal Information from you:•   Personal identifiers, such as your name, phone number, or email address;•   Data collected through cookies, tags, or similar technologies;•   Device identifiers, IP addresses, and other online identifiers;•   Information about how you interact with and use our website or services; and/or•   Geolocation information, if your device or browser settings are enabled to share your location.We may collect additional Personal Information when you take some action to provide that information to us. For example, if you contact us regarding our clubs or events, we may request that you provide your name and contact information so that we can respond to your inquiry. (See the paragraph below on "When You Contact Us" to understand more about how we treat this information.)Nhl.com/ducks/ is the Anaheim Ducks' official website and the Anaheim Ducks Mobile App is the Anaheim Ducks' official mobile app, and they are both hosted and operated by the NHL. This means that the NHL also collects your Personal Information when you use any NHL.com website or NHL mobile app, including our official website and mobile app, through cookies and other means. We do not control the NHL and we are not responsible for how the NHL treats your Personal Information. Instead, you should visit the NHL's Privacy Policy for more information or to exercise your rights.When You Make A PurchaseIf you buy tickets or any of our goods or products, we collect the following categories of Personal Information:•   Personal identifiers, such as your name, phone number, email address, or billing address;•   Financial information, including your credit or bank card information;•   Professional information, if you make a purchase through your work or in relation to a work event; and/or•   Commercial information about your purchase, type of event, or other transaction details.If you purchase season tickets, annual suite(s), club seats, or other premium ticketing options, we may also collect a copy of your driver's license or other government-issued identification.We use this information to process your transaction and provide you with goods, products, or services. We may also use your contact details and information about your transaction to provide you relevant information about promotions, marketing materials, or other incentives.We share this information with financial institutions who process payments; companies such as Ticketmaster who promote or process ticketing; and other business partners who help us analyze and understand what goods, products, or services are popular and relevant to our fans and guests.When purchasing tickets through Ticketmaster, for example through Ticketmaster.com, Ticketmaster's policies and practices may apply with respect to Personal Information collected by Ticketmaster through your transaction. Ticketmaster is an independent third party and we are not responsible for how they treat such Personal Information.    When You Create An AccountThe Anaheim Ducks Account Manager gives you the ability to quickly and easily manage your tickets. If you choose to register, we collect the following categories of Personal Information:•   Personal identifiers, such as your name, phone number, email address, billing address, or password;•   Financial information, including your credit or bank card information; and/or•   Commercial information about your purchase, type of event, or other transaction details.We use this information to manage your account and enable you to customize features of our services, for example, by linking your tickets to your mobile device.

Perth Glory Football Club
Privacy Policy
1. Perth Glory Football Club
Perth Glory Football Club (ACN 053 910 133) of 203 Underwood Avenue Floreat WA 6014 is a Football Club that competes in the Hyundai A-league. Perth Glory Football Club (referred to in this policy as we, us and Perth Glory Football Club) is committed to managing your information in a secure and responsible manner and in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This policy sets out the way in which Perth Glory Football Club collects, uses and discloses personal information and also explains your rights and how to contact us.
2. Your Personal Information
2.1          In the course of its operations, Perth Glory Football Club may collect, use and disclose the personal information of players, ticketholders, administrators, participants and other individuals for the purpose of internal marketing and communications as further described in the remainder of this Policy. In each case, the precise nature of the collection, use and disclosure of personal information by Perth Glory Football Club depends on the services you request and receive from, and your interaction with Perth Glory Football Club.
2.2          The types of personal information held by Perth Glory Football Club may include your name, address, phone numbers, email address, date of birth, gender, occupation and social media details. Depending on the services you receive from Perth Glory Football Club, Perth Glory Football Club may also collect and hold details of about your participation history, membership history as well as your credit card information and details of the items ordered or purchased from Perth Glory Football Club.
2.3          In certain circumstances, Perth Glory Football Club may collect information about your health or other sensitive information about you (for example, information about your racial or ethnic origin or information about your physical health). Perth Glory Football Club may use health information about you to ensure that football programs in which you participate are run safely and in accordance with any special health needs you may have and for insurance purposes. Perth Glory Football Club may also use health information as required by the FFA Statutes, including medical and anti-doping testing and investigations.
2.4          In addition, Perth Glory Football Club may use de-identified health information and other sensitive information to carry out research to prepare submissions to government or a government body or to plan events and activities. De-identified information is information which has been aggregated or otherwise de-identified so that it cannot be used to identify you or any other individual.
2.5          1.1          When you provide Perth Glory Football Club with personal information, this Policy will apply to the manner in which the information is collected, used, disclosed and stored by Perth Glory Football Club. If you chose not to provide certain personal information, Perth Glory Football Club may not be able to communicate with you. It may also affect Perth Glory Football Club’s ability to provide you with the products and services you require. For example, you may not be able to register as a member of the Perth Glory Football Club or participate in some of the activities on the Perth Glory Football Club network of websites (including www.footballaustralia.com.au/perthglory and the Perth Glory newsletter Glory Fanatical), and any related websites which link directly to this Policy (collectively, the Perth Glory Football Club Sites)) if you do not provide certain personal information.
3. Collecting Your Personal Information
3.1          Personal information about you may be collected by Perth Glory Football Club from you or from a third party. Perth Glory Football Club uses forms, online portals and other electronic or paper correspondence to collect this information.
3.2          For example, Perth Glory Football Club may collect personal information from you:
(a)           via the Perth Glory Football Club Sites;
(b)           when you provide it to Perth Glory Football Club by filling in forms approved by Perth Glory Football Club from time to time;
(c)           when you contact Perth Glory Football Club
(d)           when you engage with Perth Glory Football Club via social media;
(e)           when you subscribe to receive Perth Glory Football Club related newsletters or other information; or
(f)            when you enter a competition that involves or is being conducted by, or on behalf of Perth Glory Football Club.
3.3          Perth Glory Football Club may also collect personal information about you from third parties, including:
(a)           from ticketing agents and event organisers, when you purchase tickets to a football related event;
(b)           from vendors, when you purchase a Perth Glory Football Club membership package or Perth Glory Football Club related merchandise;
(c)           Perth Glory Football Club corporate partners, licensees, sponsors, suppliers and broadcasters (Perth Glory Football Club Partners);
(d)           your representatives;
(e)           Football Federation Australia (FFA), or an FFA Member Federation, association, Perth Glory Football Club and/or a competition administrator registered with FFA from time to time (Football Administrators)
(f)            the organisations identified under section 5 (Disclosing Your Personal Information) below.
4. Using Your Personal Information
4.1           Perth Glory Football Club may use the personal information collected about you in a variety of ways, including:
(a)           to verify your identity;
(b)           to organise, conduct and promote Perth Glory Football Club matches, programs and other events (including for the purpose of distributing newsletters, providing information and making ticket offers);
(c)           to develop programs, activities, events, products, services and merchandise relating to the Perth Glory Football Club;
(d)           to provide you with information about our products and services, including information about tickets, merchandise and special offers, or which we reasonably believe may be of interest you;
(e)           to investigate and take action in the event that applicable laws or regulations are breached, including terms of admission and codes of conduct;
(f)            to carry out market research and surveys;
(g)           to contact you if you win a prize in a competition you have entered and to send the prize to you; and
(h)           any use that is reasonably apparent at the time the information is collected.
4.2          Perth Glory Football Club may also use the personal information collected about you:
(a)           to administer, manage and improve the Perth Glory Football Club Sites and to provide you with access to those Perth Glory Football Club Sites;
(b)           to ensure that content from the Perth Glory Football Club Sites is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer;
(c)           to allow you to participate in interactive features of a Perth Glory Football Club Site, when you choose to do so;
(d)           for direct marketing communications from Perth Glory Football Club in relation to products, services, tickets, merchandise and special offers made available by Perth Glory Football Club or our respective Perth Glory Football Club Partners.  You will be able to opt-out of direct marketing at any time if you so choose, by utilising the unsubscribe feature on electronic marketing communications, or in the case of other direct marketing materials by contacting the Perth Glory Football Club Privacy Officer in accordance with section 10 below;
(e)           where permitted by Perth Glory Football Club, to enable Perth Glory Football Club Partners who have a relationship with Perth Glory Football Club to market and promote their products and services to you. You will be able to opt-out of direct marketing at any time if you so choose, by utilizing the unsubscribe feature on electronic marketing communications, or in the case of other direct marketing materials by contacting the Perth Glory Football Club Privacy Officer in accordance with section 10  below; and
(f)            any use that is reasonably apparent at the time the information is collected.

You should refer to that privacy policy for further details about how the relevant third party may collect, process, use, store and disclose your information and how you can contact the relevant third party if you have any queries, or wish to make a complaint, about their handling of your personal information.
6. Perth Glory Football Club Sites, Cookies and Other Technologies
6.1           If you visit a Perth Glory Football Club Site, Perth Glory Football Club may record information such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, the Perth Glory Football Club Site pages accessed and any information downloaded.  This information is used for statistical, reporting and website administration and maintenance purposes, including identifying usage trends, to track users’ movements around the Perth Glory Football Club Sites and to gather demographic information about Perth Glory Football Club users collectively.
6.2           Like many other websites, the Perth Glory Football Club Sites may use ‘cookies’ from time to time. A cookie is a piece of information that allows us to identify and interact more effectively with your device. The cookie helps Perth Glory Football Club to maintain the continuity of your browsing session and remember your details and preferences when you return.  You can configure your web browser software to reject cookies however some parts of our website may not have full functionality in that case.
6.3           In some cases FFA and its third party service providers may use cookies and other technologies such as web beacons and JavaScript on a Perth Glory Football Club Site in connection with online services like banner advertising, website analytics and surveys to collect information about your use of an FFA Site.  The use of these technologies allows Perth Glory Football Club and its third party service providers to evaluate a user’s use of a Perth Glory Football Club Site, deliver customised advertising content, measure the effectiveness of the advertising and provide other services relating to website activity and internet usage.  The services we may use from time to time include Google Analytics, Nielsen, AdTech, Omniture and Facebook.  You can find more details in the privacy policies for those services, including information on how to opt-out of certain conduct.
6.4            When we send you emails or other electronic messages, Perth Glory Football Club may record where you open the message and click on particular links. This helps us to better understand what information is of interest to you.
7. Protecting Your Personal Information
7.1           Perth Glory Football Club takes the security of personal information seriously.  Perth Glory Football Club has appointed a Privacy Officer to oversee Perth Glory Football Clubs management of personal information in accordance with this Policy and the  Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).
7.2           Perth Glory Football Club takes reasonable steps to protect the security and privacy of your personal information. Perth Glory Football Club has directed its staff that personal information must be dealt with in accordance with this Policy and kept secure from unauthorised access or disclosure.
7.3           If you are considering sending us any other personal information through a Perth Glory Football Club Site or other electronic means, please be aware that the information may be insecure in transit, particularly where no encryption is used (e.g. email, standard HTTP).  Any transmission is therefore at your own risk.  Once Perth Glory Football Club has received your personal information, Perth Glory Football Club will take reasonable steps to protect all personal information within its direct control from unauthorised access.
7.4          Where Perth Glory Football Club has given you (or where you have chosen) a password which enables you to access certain parts of a Perth Glory Football Club Site, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential.  Perth Glory Football Club suggests that you do not share the password with anyone and to change it regularly.
8. Disclosing Your Personal Information Abroad
8.1           Perth Glory Football Club will store personal information on data servers that are controlled by Perth Glory Football Club and are located within the geographical borders of Australia where reasonably possible. However, in some circumstances the personal information that Perth Glory Football Club collects may be disclosed to certain recipients, and stored at certain destinations, located outside Australia from time to time.  For example, your personal information may be transferred outside of Australia if any of Perth Glory Football Club’s servers from time to time are located outside Australia or if one of Perth Glory Football Club’s service providers or suppliers is located in a country outside Australia.  As at the date of this Policy, Perth Glory Football Club utilises service providers and suppliers in Australia only. Perth Glory Football Club may also disclose your personal information outside of Australia in accordance with section 5 (Disclosing Your Personal Information).
8.2          In all cases, by providing your personal information to us or using our services, you consent to the disclosure of your personal information outside Australia as set out in this Policy, and acknowledge that Perth Glory Football Club is not required to ensure that overseas recipients handle your personal information in compliance with Australian privacy law.  However, where practicable in the circumstances, Perth Glory Football Club will take reasonable steps to ensure that overseas recipients use and disclose such personal information in a manner consistent with this Policy. You may have rights to enforce such parties' compliance with applicable data protection laws, but you may not have recourse against those parties under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) in relation to how those parties treat your personal information.

9.1           Perth Glory Football Club takes However, the accuracy of that information depends on the information you provide.  Perth Glory Football Club recommends that you let us know if there are any errors in your personal information and keep us up-to-date with changes to your personal information such as your contact details.

10. Opting Out of Direct Marketing Communications
You can opt out of receiving further messages or communications from Perth Glory Football Club at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in the message sent to you.  If you wish to unsubscribe from communications Perth Glory Football Club send you, you can also send an email to membership@perthglory.com.au email address and we will action your request accordingly
11. Changes To This Policy
We may modify, alter or otherwise update this Policy at any time by publishing the new version of the Policy on the Perth Glory Football Club Sites.  We will indicate any changes by updating the date of this Policy.  If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact the Perth Glory Football Club Privacy Officer using the details below.
12. Contacting the Perth Glory Football Club Privacy Officer and Making Complaints
12.1           You may contact the Perth Glory Football Club Privacy Officer by writing to Mr Peter Filopoulos CEO Perth Glory Football Club and posting your letter to PO Box 526 Wembley WA 6913 or emailing peter.filopoulos@perthglory.com.au
12.2          You may complain about our collection, use or disclosure of your personal information by contacting the Perth Glory Football Club Privacy Officer using the contact details above with your full name and contact details, together with a detailed description of the complaint. We will respond to your complaint within a reasonable period of time and inform you of the next steps we will take in dealing with your complaint. If we are unable to resolve your complaint, we will inform you of the steps available to you in those circumstances.

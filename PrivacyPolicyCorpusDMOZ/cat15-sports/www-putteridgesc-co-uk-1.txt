Putteridge Swimming Club is based in Luton and is one of Bedfordshire’s highest performing competitive swimming clubs. PSC is a fun, friendly and competitive swimming club, attracting swimmers straight from swim school through to those who wish to compete at a more serious level. We encourage swimmers to reach their full potential by swimming at local, county, regional or at national levels.
The clubs professional coaching team work with young academy beginners through to performance squads, masters swimmers, triathletes and disability swimming.

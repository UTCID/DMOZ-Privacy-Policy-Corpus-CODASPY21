Kilsyth Basketball Website – Privacy Statement
The Kilsyth Basketball respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.  Personal information is information or an opinion (including information or an opinion forming part of a database) from which it is possible to determine your identity.  Examples of personal information include your name, postal address, telephone number and email address.  We collect personal information in a number of ways, including directly from you if, for example, you provide information to us through this Website.
Kilsyth Basketball has a privacy policy which sets out how it will deal with your personal information.  For a copy of the privacy policy, please contact Kilsyth Basketball on + 61 3 9728 1033.

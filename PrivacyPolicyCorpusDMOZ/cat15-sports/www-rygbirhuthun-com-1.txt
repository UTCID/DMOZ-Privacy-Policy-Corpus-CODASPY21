Clwb Rygbi Rhuthun Privacy Policy
Clwb Rygbi Rhuthun takes your privacy seriously and will only use the personal information you provide to administer your membership and to provide you with information in relation to your membership.
The information that you provide to the club may be stored in paper format and/or by using dedicated membership software including web-based systems but will not be shared with any person or organisation outside of the club without your consent.
If you have any concerns about our use of the membership data that you have or are providing to the club please contact our membership secretary Sioned Foulkes.
Clwb Rygbi Rhuthun

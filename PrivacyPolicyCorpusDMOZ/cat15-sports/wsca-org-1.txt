How will we use your Email Address?
To send periodic emails regarding WSCA news, your WSCA membership or other products and services that WSCA may offer.
For information about how to opt-out of communications with WSCA or messages sent on behalf of its corporate partners, see the “Opt-Out” section below.
Opt-Out
You may opt-out of receiving WSCA emails sent through our email marketing service by clicking on “Unsubscribe” at the bottom of any email and following the prompts.
Third Party Disclosure
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information unless we provide you with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others’ rights, property, or safety.
However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.
Third party links
Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include or offer third party products or services on our emails. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

Privacy policy (Reviewed in May 2018 for GDPR Regulations)
At Ombersley Golf Club we strive to protect your privacy and rights. We have set forth this statement to explain the information we collect from you, how we use that information, and your legal rights regarding that information.
Who has access to my information?
Any information you provide is controlled by John Dowty Ltd, Ombersley Golf Club, Bishops Wood Road, Ombersley, Droitwich, Worcestershire, WR9 0LE
("Data Controller").
What information do we collect and how do we use it?
When you choose to contact us through a web form, we may ask for your name, address, email address, and/or telephone number. This information will be used to send any information you request, or contact you if we need to respond to your question.
No cookies are used on this web site and no attempt is made to identify individual visitor through their IP address or ant other means. However, the following non-personal information may be collected for internal tracking to create summary statistics and to identify levels of interest in the information displayed on our site; Date and time of site access, IP address, web browser, operating system, screen resolution, search engine, search terms, domain and country.
How do we protect your information?
In accordance with UK Data Protection legislation we follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information you provide to prevent unauthorised access. Our procedures mean we may occasionally request proof of identity before disclosing information to you. We cannot however guarantee the security of any data you disclose to us online.
Do we disclose your information to third parties?
We do not sell, trade or rent your personal information to third parties for any reason whatsoever.
We occasionally employ other companies and individuals to perform functions on our behalf. These may include packaging companies to fulfil orders, couriers to make deliveries and Royal Mail to send post. These companies may have access to your personal information, but may not use it for any other purpose and are required to process the data in accordance with UK data legislation.
Your consent and rights
By using our website you consent to the collection and use of this information by Ombersley Golf Club. Changes to this privacy policy will be posted to this page so that you are kept informed of the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
Please note the information you provide will not be transferred out of the UK.
Under UK Data Protection legislation you have a right to see the personal information that we keep about you, upon receipt of written request (by post or email) to the address below.

Credit card details
PongWorld.com will never ask for Credit Card details and request that you do not enter
it on any of the forms on PongWorld.com
External Sites
PongWorld.com is not responsible for the content of external internet sites. You are
advised to read the privacy policy of external sites before disclosing any personal information.
Cookies
We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our site
and to record session information such as whether you have answered our survey question.
User Accounts
PongWorld.com allows users to register in a Find A Partner database either as a member or a non-member
of PongWorld.
Members may update their information by visiting
https://www.pongworld.com/community/mypw/ and non-members may update their
information by contacting us.
To change information that is not editable as a member,
you may request changes by contacting us.
To Unsubscribe from our Newsletter

To unsubscribe, please follow instructions provided by Your Mailing List Provider.

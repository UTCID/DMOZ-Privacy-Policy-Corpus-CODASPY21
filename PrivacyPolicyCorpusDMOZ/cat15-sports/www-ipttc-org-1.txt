PRIVACY STATEMENT
Thank you for visiting www.ipttc.org. We are
committed to protecting and safeguarding your privacy.
What Information We Collect and How We Use It
Some information we display on our website falls into the Personally Identifiable
Information category which refers to information that lets visitors to the website
know specifically who you are. However, you can visit our website without us being
able to identify who you are or revealing any Personally Identifiable Information.
Sharing Your Information
We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise retransmit any Personally Identifiable
Information we collect.
While unlikely, we may be required to provide Personally Identifiable Information
in response to court order, subpoena, or government investigation and we reserve
the right to do so.
Links To Other Web Sites
Links to third-party website are provided only for your information and convenience
and we are not liable for the privacy of other websites.
Changes To This Statement
Any updates or changes to this Privacy Statement will be posted on this website
and the date of the newest version posted below.
By using our website, you acknowledge acceptance of this Privacy Statement.
Contact Us
If you have any concerns about our use of your information or about this Privacy
Statement, please send an e-mail to webmaster@ipttc.org.
This Privacy Statement was issued on 10 November 2011.

Our mission is to create a positive, safe and supportive environment, enabling the Club and its Members to attain enjoyment and success. We will achieve this by:
Showing respect for one another and the Club;
Displaying sportsmanship at all levels;
Developing the individual;
Teamwork; and
Strong communication.

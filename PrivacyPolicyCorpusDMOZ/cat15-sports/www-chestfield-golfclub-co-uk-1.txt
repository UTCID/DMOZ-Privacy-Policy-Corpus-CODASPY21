Privacy
This Website is provided by Intelligent Golf on behalf of Chestfield Golf Club. Chestfield Golf Club uses this website to provide online information and services, competition management and CONGU ratified handicap maintenance, for Playing members of its Golf Clubs.
Access to this website is via Chestfield Golf Club's Membership ID and a User managed PIN number and is limited to Golf Club Members and selected staff who need access for the purpose of supplying and maintaining information relative to the primary functions of the website, as stated above.
Chestfield Golf Club will transfer from its own membership system only those personal details relevant to Golf Club membership such that a Golf Club Member may utilise this website effectively.
Users of the website are able to specify additional personal details by accessing the 'Preferences' area under the 'My Details' menu option. The details specified here will be used solely for the following purposes:-
1) Communication between members to facilitate the management of matches and competitions
2) Communication between Golf Club Members and Golf Club Committee Members and Officers to promote accessibility and enable the effective and smooth running of the club.
3) Contact information may be passed to Chestfield Golf Club to enable them to maintain up to date contact information.
Cookies are used throughout this website for tracking session data, and this data is discarded after you leave the website automatically.
Persistent cookies may also be used to remember your club selection preference.

The Kennett Brothers are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we manage your personal information.
We comply with the Privacy Act 1993 regarding how we collect, use, disclose, store and give access to personal information.
WHY TO WE COLLECT INFORMATION?
Information is collected purely for the purposes of: responding to and processing your requests and orders, and occasionally providing you with information about new books that we think you might be interested in.
HOW DO WE ENSURE THE SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION?
We do not pass your personal information on to anyone else, or use it for any other purpose. Information is held securely and only accessed by the Kennett Brothers and staff that work for us.
WHAT ARE YOUR RIGHTS?
You are entitled to access and request corrections to your information, and you can also ask us to delete your information if you wish.
Please feel free to contact us at info@kennett.co.nz.

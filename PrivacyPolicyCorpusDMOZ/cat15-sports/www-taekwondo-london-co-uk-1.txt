Who we are
Our website address is: http://taekwondo-london.co.uk.
What personal data we collect and why we collect it
Shop
When you place an order on this website you give us personal details which we may use to identify you as a current student and process your order. We may share this information with other students and instructors in order to process your order. If you don't want us to store us this information on the website, then place your order directly with your instructor in class.
Embedded content from other websites
Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracing your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.
Analytics
We may collect anonymised website analytics and statistics.
Who we share your data with
We don't share your data collected via this website with anyone else. The administrators of this website can see any personal data you choose to give us. Any usage data we collect is used to monitor and improve the effectiveness of our website.
How long we retain your data
We may retain anonymised website analytics and statistics indefinitely. We will retain any ordering/purchasing information you give us in order to fulfil any financial or legal obligations we may have.If you are looking for our members' privacy policy, please go to the Information for Members page.

Unsolicited Submissions Policy
Dallas Cowboys or any of its affiliates or employees do not accept or consider unsolicited ideas, including ideas for new creative, marketing, promotions, products or technologies. Please do not submit any unsolicited ideas, original creative artwork, suggestions or other works ("submissions") in any form to the Dallas Cowboys or any of its affiliates or employees. This policy is not intended to serve as a barrier to receiving and responding to feedback from our valued fans. The sole purpose of this policy is to avoid potential misunderstandings or disputes when Dallas Cowboys or affiliates' products, strategies or other materials might seem similar to ideas submitted to the Dallas Cowboys. If, despite our request that you not send us your ideas, you still submit them, then regardless of what your letter says, the following terms shall apply to your submissions.
Terms of Idea Submission
You agree that: (1) your submissions and their contents will automatically become the property of the Dallas Cowboys and its affiliates, without any compensation to you; (2) Dallas Cowboys may use or redistribute the submissions and their contents for any purpose and in any way; (3) there is no obligation for the Dallas Cowboys and its affiliates to review the submission; and (4) there is no obligation to keep any submissions confidential.
Feedback
The Dallas Cowboys do, however, welcome your feedback, contact us here.
Please do not include any ideas that the Dallas Cowboys' policy will not permit it to accept or consider.
Feedback and Information
Any feedback you provide at this site shall be deemed to be non-confidential. The Dallas Cowboys and its affiliates shall be free to use such information on an unrestricted basis.

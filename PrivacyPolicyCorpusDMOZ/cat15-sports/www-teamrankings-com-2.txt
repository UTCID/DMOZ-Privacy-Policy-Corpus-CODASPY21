Privacy Policy
We are committed to safeguarding the personal information of Team Rankings users. Having been victims of identity theft ourselves, we understand the critical importance of keeping personal data private in the digital age.
We pay top dollar to use a world-class data hosting provider to manage and secure our application and database servers
We never rent or sell email addresses or any other personal information to third parties
We encrypt highly sensitive personal information such as passwords
When we make use of third party services to help run our business, we choose industry leading providers with rock solid security technology and processes
We use advanced security (including SSL) to process all credit card transactions, and we do not store credit card information. As a result, your card number is not at risk of getting stolen if we have an unintended security breach.
If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy approach, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@teamrankings.com.

Privacy Policy
Cookies
We use Cookies to store transient information that we need while you are using our site. We do not use cookies to track your behaviour or what sites you visit.
Personal Data
If you register as a member, we store your Registration Information such as your name, email, phone numbers, membership number, league selection etc. We use this information provide you the services that you are entitile as a member. If you fail to complete the Registration Process by paying your dues by the Due Date, then we will delete your information. IMPORTANT: We do not sell, give or otherwise give access to your personal information to any third party, other than the two exceptions noted below.
If you are elegible for BCIMCA membership, we provide BMIMCA with a count of eligible members.
We transmit information, such as your initials, gender, age to CurlBC.
Financial Transactions
Though we collect and retain payment amounts, dates and methods (Credit Card, Cheque, Cash, etc.), We do not store any other details of the transaction such as your credit card number.

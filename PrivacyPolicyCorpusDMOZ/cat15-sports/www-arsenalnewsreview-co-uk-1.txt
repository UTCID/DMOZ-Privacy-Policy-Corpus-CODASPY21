Cookies:
A cookie is a small file sent by the web server to the web browser. It is stored by the web browser and returned to the web server on each request. The purpose of the cookie is to help track the use of the website. Most browsers enable you to block or delete cookies, but this may impact on the usability of the website.
ANR uses cookies and makes use of third-party affiliates, including Google Analytics, who use cookies to collect information about accesses to the website. Google Analytics is used to analyse the use of the ANR website and to produce reports. Please see Google Analytic’s privacy policy for details of how the information is used.

The webserver logs contain information about each access to the website, including your IP address, browser type, referral source, length of visit and number of page views. The logs are used to help administer the website and to track down problems.

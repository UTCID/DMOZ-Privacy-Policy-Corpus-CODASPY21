Bournemouth Hockey Club aspires to be one of the leading clubs in the South for the playing and development of hockey for all. This will be built on strong, sustainable foundations through the development of young players and a club renowned for its friendliness and pursuit of good sportsmanship.
We will help our players of all ages and abilities to develop and realise their full potential.
The club will provide a high class, quality environment with superb coaching and management structure, which will enable all members of the club to access great quality hockey.

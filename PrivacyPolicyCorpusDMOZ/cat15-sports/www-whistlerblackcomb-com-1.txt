Alpine Forecast Synopsis
Updated December 10, 2020 at 3:00 PM
A weak upper flow with only weak disturbances will allow alpine temperatures to remain near seasonal values and only isolated flurries are expected for the reminder of the week. The next slightly more organized system is currently expected with light snowfall early on Sunday, but confidence in any amounts of this system continues to be low at this point.

Your Privacy
At Spokes Etc., we take your privacy seriously and we carefully protect any information that we collect in our stores and through our website. We only ask for information that helps us to serve you better, and we never share our data with anyone unless you've given us your permission in advance.
If you choose to provide us with your mailing address or email address, we may communicate our current promotions, new products or upcoming events with you. If you do not wish to receive these updates, you may ask us to remove you from our mailing list or unsubscribe from our emails at any time.
We collect information about our site use through Google Analytics and Google Analytics Display Advertiser. Google Analytics collects information on site visitors including but not limited to demographic information and on-site activity. We use this information to improve our on-site user experience, and we do not share this information with any outside parties. Click here to learn more about Google Analytics or to opt out of Google Analytics.
If you have any questions regarding your privacy or our communications policies, please contact us at info@spokesetc.com.

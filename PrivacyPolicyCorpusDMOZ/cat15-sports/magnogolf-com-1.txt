Privacy Policy:
Magnolia Gold Club ("Magnolia Gold Club")
operates magnogolf.com and may operate other websites. It
is Magnolia Gold Club policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect
while operating our websites.
Website Visitors
Like most website operators, Magnolia Gold Club collects non-personally-identifying information of the
sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference,
referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Magnolia Gold Club purpose in
collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand
how Magnolia Gold Club visitors use its website. From time to
time, Magnolia Gold Club may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by
publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.
Magnolia Gold Club also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol
(IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on magnogolf.com
blogs/sites. Magnolia Gold Club only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same
circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that
commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site
where the comment was left.
Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to Magnolia Gold Club websites choose to interact
with Magnolia Gold Club in ways that require Magnolia Gold Club to gather
personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Magnolia Gold Club gathers
depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at magnogolf.com to provide a
username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Magnolia Gold Club are asked to
provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to
process those transactions. In each case, Magnolia Gold Club collects such information only insofar as
is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with
Magnolia Gold Club. Magnolia Gold Club does not disclose personally-identifying information
other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with
the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.
Aggregated Statistics
Magnolia Gold Club may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its
websites. Magnolia Gold Club may display this information publicly or provide it to others.
However, Magnolia Gold Club does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as
described below.
Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information
Magnolia Gold Club discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information
only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information
in order to process it on Magnolia Gold Club behalf or to provide services available
at Magnolia Gold Club websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some
of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by
using Magnolia Gold Club websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to
them. Magnolia Gold Club will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and
personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated
organizations, as described above, Magnolia Gold Club discloses potentially personally-identifying and
personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or
when Magnolia Gold Club believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the
property or rights of Magnolia Gold Club, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered
user of an Magnolia Gold Club website and have supplied your email address, Magnolia Gold Club
may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to
date with what's going on with Magnolia Gold Club and our products. If you send us a request (for
example via email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us
clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. Magnolia Gold Club takes all
measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of
potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.
Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser
provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Magnolia Gold Club uses cookies to
help Magnolia Gold Club identify and track visitors, their usage of Magnolia Gold Club
website, and their website access preferences. Magnolia Gold Club visitors who do not wish to have
cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before
using Magnolia Gold Club websites, with the drawback that certain features
of Magnolia Gold Club websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
Business Transfers
If Magnolia Gold Club, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event
that Magnolia Gold Club goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the
assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that
any acquirer of Magnolia Gold Club may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this
policy.
Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies.
These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to
compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among
other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy
Policy covers the use of cookies by Magnolia Gold Club and does not cover the use of cookies by any
advertisers.

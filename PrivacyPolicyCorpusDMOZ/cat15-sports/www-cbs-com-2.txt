The Late Show Suggests These Charities Helping With COVID-19 Relief Efforts
Looking for ways to help out? You don't need to leave home to make a difference!
Posted on Mar 30, 2020 | 02:35pm
This is an unusual time for all of us, but we're in it together. For those in a position to help, we encourage you to give generously. And for everybody, this is an especially important time to be kind.
Here is a list of some of the many charities and organizations that are on the ground helping folks in areas hit hard by the COVID-19 pandemic. Visit the websites below to learn more about each organization, and to find out if donations are tax-deductible in the U.S.
- World Central Kitchen
- GiveDirectly
- Feeding America
- No Kid Hungry
- International Medical Corps
- Meals on Wheels

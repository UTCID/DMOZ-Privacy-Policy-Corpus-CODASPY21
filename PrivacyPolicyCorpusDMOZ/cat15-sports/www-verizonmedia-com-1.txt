Why this ad?
For consumers
The sites and apps you use work with online advertising companies to provide you with personalised advertising that is as relevant and useful as possible. Personalisation is informed by various factors including:
the content of the site or app you are using
information you provide
your activity on our Products and Services, like your searches in our Search Products
what your friends or contacts recommend to you
apps on your device
See How Advertising Works for more information about how we use your data to provide personalised advertising and for more information on your choices and controls.
Who placed this ad?
This ad was served by Verizon Media or one of Verizon Media's advertising technology partners. Learn more about our advertising technology partners in Our Partners page.
Why was this ad served?
The personalised ads that you see are based on certain factors like your activity, searches, demographic data, apps on your device and location information. Visit How Advertising Works to learn more about what data we use to personalise ads.

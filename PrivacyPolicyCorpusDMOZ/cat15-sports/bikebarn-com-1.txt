Your Privacy
How We Protect Your InformationThank you for reviewing our policies regarding any personal information you may choose to provide at our website. We take your privacy very seriously and carefully protect any and all information entrusted to us. We only collect information that helps us serve you better and that you voluntarily provide. We do not share your information with anyone else unless you've approved of such sharing in advance.
What Information We CollectThe following types of information may be collected at this website (if you volunteer it):your email addressyour first and last nameyour street address, city, state and ZIP Codeyour phone numberpertinent information specific to an event or activity (where relevant)Third-Party Service ProvidersWe sometimes use other companies to perform functions on our behalf. Examples include fulfilling orders and delivering packages. They need access to some personal information in order to perform their duties but may not use this information for other purposes.More About Email & Postal AddressesIf you supply us with your email address, postal addresses, and/or phone number, and do not specify otherwise, you may receive periodic messages from us with information on new products, services or upcoming events.At any time, if you decide you no longer wish to receive such messages, you may let us know by sending an e-mail, calling or can unsubscribe at the bottom of any email we send to you.  If calling or notifying us by email, please be sure to include your complete name and address and all email addresses that may be associated with your record. This helps us accurately locate your information in our file.If you would like to confirm your contact information, this can be confirmed in all of our stores or thru email request.Periodic UpdatesPlease check this page for periodic updates on our policies regarding any personal information you've provided to us via our website. Thank you for visiting us online. We appreciate the opportunity to serve you.Effective as of March 9, 2018

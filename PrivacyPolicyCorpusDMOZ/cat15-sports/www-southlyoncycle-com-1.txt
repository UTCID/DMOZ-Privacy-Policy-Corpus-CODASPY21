Bicycle DisclaimerSouth Lyon Cycle has one of the best selections of in-stock bikes in the area. However, not all models listed are in-stock. We are happy to special order any model of bike or product from our suppliers. Shipping and handling charges, if any, will depend on the product and time of order. Price DisclaimerAll product information and prices are subject to change without notice. South Lyon Cycle makes every reasonable effort to ensure the accuracy and validity of pricing provided. However, when mistakes occur the correct price at date of purchase will prevail. Your PrivacyWe take your privacy very seriously and carefully protect any and all information entrusted to us. We only collect information that helps us serve you better and that you voluntarily provide. We do not share your information with anyone else unless you've approved of such sharing in advance.What Information We CollectThe following types of information may be collected at this website  (if you volunteer it):your e-mail address your first and last name your street address, city, state and ZIP Codepertinent information specific to an event or activity (where relevant) More About E-Mail  & Postal AddressesIf you supply us with your e-mail and/or postal addresses, and do not specify otherwise, you may receive periodic messages from us with information on new products, services or upcoming events.If, later, you decide you no longer wish to receive such messages, you may let us know by sending an e-mail, calling or writing us. To ensure we correctly identify you (many names are similar), please be sure to include your complete name and address and all e-mail addresses that may be associated with your record. This helps us accurately locate your information in our file. Such information will only be used to comply with your request.If you would like to see your contact information we have on file, please stop in or let us know to what address you'd like us to send a copy.Periodic UpdatesPlease check this page for periodic updates on our policies regarding any personal information you've provided to us via our website. Thank you for visiting us online. We appreciate the opportunity to serve you.Updated: June 2017

PRIVACY POLICY
Dundee United Retail is the retail division of The Dundee United Football Company LTD, company registration number SCO13690. Our company address is Tannadice Park, Tannadice Street, Dundee, DD3 7JW, VAT registered – 268 6798 84.
The process of making a purchase from Dundee United retail’s online store the following information may be collected from you:-
NAME, AGE, DATE OF BIRTH AND GENDERBILLING AND SHIPPING ADDRESSTHE LOCATION THAT THE ORDER WAS PLACEDTHE LOCATION THAT THE ORDER WAS SHIPPED TOYOUR BROWSING AND SHOPPING PREFRENCESTHE TYPE OF DEVICE THAT YOU USE TO ACCESS OUR SERVICESDundee United Retail may use the data collected for the following reasons:-
To contact you regarding offers, promotions, products and services we believe that you may be interested inTo maintain the account that you have registered with usTo identify you as the shopper to process paymentsFor any legal reason that we are required to disclose the information such as the investigation of a crime or fraudulent activity.Dundee United Retail and The Dundee United Football Company LTD will not disclose, sell or rent any of the personal data that is collected in the process of setting up an online account. The information collected in your account can be viewed, deleted or amended at any time by the user in the account settings.
If you do not wish to have data held in an account you can choose at the point of checkout to checkout as a guest and data will only be used to complete the sale and shall not be retained.
Marketing
We may send you retail marketing emails as well as marketing emails from Dundee United FC such as club news and ticketing information; you may also receive emails regarding items that have been left in your basket. If you do not wish to receive these emails you must opt out of the marketing preferences.
3RD Party Data
Dundee United Retail and The Dundee United Football Company LTD shall not sell or rent the data collected to a 3rd party. You may receive marketing emails from the Clubs Unite partners regarding exclusive offers, if you do not wish to receive these you must opt out.
Email Contact
Dundee United Retail, The Dundee United Football Company LTD will never contact you via email to confirm account or payment information, if you receive an email from a party asking for this information in our name please contact us here.
You’re Rights
You can exercise the following rights at any time,
You have the right to ask what personal data is being held.The right to amend or correct any out of date or incorrect data held.And the right to opt out of any marketing contact.If you wish to exercise any of these rights you can do so by contacting us here.
We reserve the right to amend the privacy policy at any time. This policy was created on 1st of June 2019.

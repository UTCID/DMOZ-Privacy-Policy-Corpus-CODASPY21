Privacy Policy Par 2 Birdie Mixed Golf League
Information Gathered
Web Server Information Gathered
The web server provides the following reports:
General Summary - Displays the most important overall
statistics.
Request Reports - Number of page requests by month, day
and hour.
Domain Report - Countries that requests have come from.
Organization Report - Organizations that requests have
come from.
Refer Reports - Referrerals that had to be redirected,
failed referrerals that had to be redirected, broken links and list of servers
that referred people to my site (how people are finding the site). The referals
are recorded as requests from servers, not individual visitors.
Search Word Report - The words people typed into the search
engine to find my site.
Browsers - Details of the version and type of browsers
used to view the site.
Operating System Report - What operating systems were
used by the visitors to the site.
Status Code Report - Lists each HTTP status code that
you received.
Files and Directories - The files requested to be viewed,
the type of files requested and which directory they are in. Also, which
files were downloaded.
None of the reports above can identify you personally. These statistics
are used by Par 2 Birdie Mixed Golf League to improve our marketing plan and make improvements
to the site.
Information You Provide
Information you provide to Par 2 Birdie Mixed Golf League and SRT Services Group of
Websites is kept in the strictest of confidence. It will not be released
to anyone unless SRT Services is served with a court order to release this
information.
Spam
We have a zero tolerance policy regarding spam. If you provide your email
address you have to request that we contact you. Members are required to provide
a evenings and weekend means of contact (preferably by email) for the purpose
of last minute changes and occasional announcements.
Electronic Payments - Not Applicable
Database
If you wish to determine is you are in our database please Contact
Us with Database at Par 2 Birdie Mixed Golf League in the subject.
To check what information we have in our database on you please Contact
Us with Check Database at Par 2 Birdie Mixed Golf League in the subject.
To remove yourself from our database please send
us an email with the subject "REMOVE". You will receive one final email
from us confirming that all information about you has been removed and after
that, you will be permanently removed from our records.
Cookies
No cookies are used on this site.
Scripts
Javascript is used to hide email addresses on this site from spammers. It
is also used for some functions of the website. No person information is collected
and no harm is done to your computer.
Security Systems - N/A
Forums and Message Boards - N/A
Linked Sites
Links within the website are provided for your convenience. We have no control
over what information is collected or how it is used therefore by clicking
the links provided you accept the responsibility to verify how the site collects
and uses information.
Unsubscribe from Our Mailing List
If at anytime you wish to be removed from our records send an email with
the subject REMOVE. You will receive one final email confirming that you have
been removed from all records.

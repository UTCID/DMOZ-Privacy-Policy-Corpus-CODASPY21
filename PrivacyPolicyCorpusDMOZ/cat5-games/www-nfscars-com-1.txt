The visitor counter works with IP's, which means that when someone visits NFSCars, his IP will be recorded and stored for 24 hours. These cookies will only be used by NFSCars to keep you logged in. This IP is only visible for administrators and is only intended to track down visitors abusing the NFSCars comments system. The IP will then be used to set a ban.

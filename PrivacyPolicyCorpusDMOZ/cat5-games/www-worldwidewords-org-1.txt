Cookies and privacy
General
Since I am based in the UK, I manage the site to conform to British and EU data protection legislation.
Cookies
This website uses Google Custom Search. Google may set cookies when you use the search function. Google’s privacy policy is here.
A cookie is created and read by AddThis.com, a social sharing service. This ensures that you see the correct updated count if you share a page. You can opt-out of AddThis cookies. The AddThis privacy policy is here.
Subscriptions
Any details you give for subscription purposes will be used solely to maintain the World Wide Words mailing list. I never pass subscription information to anyone else.
The subscriptions list server (which manages subscription requests and the despatch of newsletters) blocks unauthorised attempts to view the list of subscribers; even subscribers cannot gain access.

Privacy Policy
This privacy policy explains how information about you is collected and used when you access our website.
COLLECTION OF INFORMATION
We collect information you provide to us, such as your name, address, phone number, email address, payment information and any other details you choose to provide.
This information is used to contact you with order confirmations and to answer questions about your order.
We also send you advertising by mail which may include free samples.
Your personal information is never sold.
When you visit our website, cookies and other tracking technologies are used to collect information about you and your interaction with our website.
This includes information about your browsing behavior, purchase behavior, and other interaction with our website.
Usually, web browsers are set to accept cookies by default, however, you can change your browser settings to remove or reject cookies.
Please be aware that cookies are required to browse and place an order on our website.
Disabling cookies will prevent you from properly interacting with Chick.com.
USE OF INFORMATION
Information we collect about you is used in several ways.
For example, to:
Process and ship orders
Process payment transactions
Investigate lost orders and send refunds
Send Chick News email advertising to those who have opted in to our advertising.
Send advertising and samples by mail to U.S. customers who have ordered in the last 12 months.
Respond to your questions and provide customer service.
REQUEST TO HAVE YOUR INFORMATION DELETED
If you want to have your online account removed, please call us at 909-987-0771.
Please note that we may retain certain information about you for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

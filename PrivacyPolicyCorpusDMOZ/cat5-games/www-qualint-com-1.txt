Privacy Policy
Personal Information That Qualint May Collect Online
Qualint collects personal information online, including names, postal and e-mail addresses, phone and facsimile numbers, and billing information. Qualint does not knowingly collect or maintain any personal information from children under the age of 13. In addition, no part of our site is designed with the purpose of attracting any person under age 13.
How Qualint Uses Personal Information Collected Online
We will use your personal information only for the purpose for which it is submitted. For example, we will use your personal information to reply to your emails and process your purchases.
How Your Information May Be Shared
Orders for Qualint products may be placed with vendors linked to the Qualint site. These vendors will use your data solely for the purposes of the transaction for which it was submitted. For more information, see the individual privacy policies for these vendors:
SWReg
BMT Micro
PayPal
In no instance will Qualint, or vendors selling Qualint products, sell or rent your personal information.
Qualint may release personal information where release is required by law (for example, a subpoena), or regulation, or is requested by a government agency.

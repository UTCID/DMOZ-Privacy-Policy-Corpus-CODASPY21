General Policy Details
dowedo.net will not rent, sell, or share personal information about you or your business with other people.
You agree that information you enter will be used, by us, only in the process of supplying information, or our products and services in response to your initial contact.
The dowedo.net web site dose not use cookies to track or store information about users. Cookies are used in some areas of the site for storing or saving information from our online games in order that they may be continued at a later date.
If information you enter has the intent to abuse our Web Site or online systems for spamming or any other unlawful activity, we reserve the right to report any information entered to the service provider or associated companies used in connection with any such activity.
Confidentiality and Security
dowedo.net is committed to ensuring the security of your personal data. In order to prevent unauthorised access to or disclosure of your personal data we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
Changes to the Privacy Policy

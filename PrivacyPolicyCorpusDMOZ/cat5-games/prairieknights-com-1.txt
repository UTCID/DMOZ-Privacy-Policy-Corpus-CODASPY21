Privacy Policy
Protecting the privacy of our visitors is important to us. Any contact information you provide in an information request will be used only to respond to you. We will not divulge any personal information to third parties without your authorization unless required by law to do so.
SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES
News and marketing information, such as information about promotions and concerts, is sent to you from this site by subscription only. We do not share your email address with any third parties but may use email addresses to provide custom content for users. You may unsubscribe at any time using the automated “unsubscribe” feature or by contacting Prairie Knights. Contact information can be found at http://prairieknights.com/contact us.
COOKIES AND IP ADDRESSES
We may collect information regarding the IP addresses of visitors to our site. This information will be used internally to anticipate demands on our servers and predict site traffic flow.
We may also use cookies and/or IP addresses in some areas to provide a unique designation for each user. Aggregate cookie and tracking information may be shared with third parties.

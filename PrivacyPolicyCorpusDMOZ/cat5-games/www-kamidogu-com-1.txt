Your access to and use of Kamidogu is conditional on your understanding of our policy. This policy applies to all visitors who access or use Kamidogu.
Personal Information
Keep in mind that any information shared in your comments is viewable by the public, thus you should exercise caution when choosing to disclose any personal information.
Cookies
Kamidogu makes use of a technology called “cookies,” which are files that allow you to remain logged in to your account. Cookies may have other uses around the site, but exist solely as a means of improving your overall experience.
Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. If you disable cookies you may still use Kamidogu, but your ability to use some features or areas of our site may be limited.

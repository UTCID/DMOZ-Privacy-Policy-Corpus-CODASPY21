We also use session cookies, which track whether you’re logged into a section of the website, such as the forums or the backer exclusive site. These cookies are placed when you log in. This saves you the hassle of having to log in each time you visit a new page. The cookies are normally removed or cleared when you log out to ensure that others cannot access restricted features and areas where you see your personal information.

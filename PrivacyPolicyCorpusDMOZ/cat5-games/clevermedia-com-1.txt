CleverMedia, Inc. Privacy Statement
CleverMedia, Inc., and its advertisers and affiliates are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of Internet users. We do not collect information for any marketing purposes at all. No membership or sign-up is required for playing any of the games on our sites. All of our ads are served by Google. Read their Privacy & Terms.
Mobile Game Apps: Several of our apps use Apple's GameCenter system for high scores. Our Island Golf game uses Apple's GameCenter for nicknames, but uses our own server to compile daily high score boards. Your GameCenter name and ID are recorded this purpose only and not used in any other way. No other personal data is collected. You can find Apple's GameCenter terms and conditions and privacy policy here.
GameScene.com High Score Boards: At this site, you can submit your score using your Facebook ID. No other information is recorded and this is only used for the purposes of displaying high scores.
CleverMedia, Inc. does not collect email addresses or any other information about our visitors except where specified above.

Privacy Statement for TourneyTime.com, LLC
TourneyTime, LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses
our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: https://www.tourneytime.com.
Data Collection
TourneyTime may use the information collected to improve the content of our service, customize the content and/or layout of our pages for each individual visitor
and diagnose problems with the web server.
We may collect only the IP address, domain name, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit,
user-specific information on what pages consumers access or visit, information volunteered by the user, such as survey information, contests, and/or site registrations
and the registration information.
TourneyTime's registration form requires users to provide contact information (like name, email address, etc.). This captured data will be used to identify the
user to TourneyTime.com, personalize the TourneyTime.com experience, send the user information about our company, updates for upcoming events, or notifications
when action is required on the user's behalf. Users may opt-in and opt-out of receiving future mailings; see the choice/opt-out section below.
We use cookies to customize web page content specific to each member in order to enhance your TourneTime experience and provide security while using our website.
With respect to Ad Servers: We do not partner with or have special relationships with any ad server companies at this time.
TourneyTime's member data and information will not be sold or provided to another entity, except in the event of a merger or acquisition of TourneyTime.com, LLC.

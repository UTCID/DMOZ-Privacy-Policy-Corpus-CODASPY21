Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy is designed to assist you in understanding how this website collects and uses the personal information you provide.
What Information is Collected?
When you visit Weirdness Central you may provide two types of information: personal information you knowingly choose to disclose that is collected on an individual basis and Web site use information collected on an aggregate basis as you and others browse Weirdness Central.
Email Information.
If you choose to correspond through email, the content of your email messages together with your email address and the response may be retained for future reference.
Web Site Use Information
Each time someone accesses Weirdness Central the server records technical information about that person's operating system, browser type and version, screen resolution, time taken on each page etc. No personal information is collected by this process. This information is analysed in order to improve this website.
How the Information you provide is used?
Personal information is used solely for the purposes of administering this website.
Use of Cookies
Weirdness Central does not use the technology known as cookies.
Sharing Information with Third Parties
E-mail or other information is not shared with third parties.

Privacy Policy & Disclaimer
PRIVACY POLICY
The privacy of our users is important to us, therefore we try our very best to fulfill the General Data Protection Regulation EU Law! Our server is located in Germany, E.U. If we can do better, we will be happy about hints.
PDRoms supports and recommends HTTPS.
Our regular visitors don’t need to input any sensible data to enjoy what we offer.
If the opportunity for the input of data is given, the input of these data takes place voluntarily.
A registration is not needed. The login system is for authors and administrators.
We locally collect data. IP’s are stored for a period of two months for the purpose of blocking malicious or suspicious actions only and protect our data and the the data of our existing users.
We use the existing Spamhaus Block List (“SBL”) to pre-block access to our servers. If we detect a spammer ourself, we just block the IP and do not submit it back to the SBL.
We use the existing Spamhaus Exploits Block List (“XBL”) to pre-block access to our servers. If we detect bad buddies ourself, we just block the IP and do not submit it back to the XBL.
We use cookies: Cookies are small text files that are placed on your machine to help the site provide a better user experience. In general, cookies are used to retain user preferences. However, you may prefer to disable cookies on this site. The most effective way to do this is to disable cookies in your browser. We suggest consulting the Help section of your browser or taking a look at the ‘About Cookies’ website which offers guidance for all modern browsers.
Sharing buttons for social medias are not active unless actively pressed.
Embbed medias will load data from external servers, to make them play. This applies to medias linked to YouTube, VIMEO, Soundcloud, etc. API pre-loading is reduced to an absolute minimum.

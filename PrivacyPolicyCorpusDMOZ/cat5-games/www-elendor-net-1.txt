The following text appears in-game under 'news disclaimer':
The Elendor system does not encrypt incoming or outgoing transmissions so
connections may be eavesdropped. Information stored on this system is
viewable, modifiable, and reproducible by the system admins at their
discretion. The Elendor administration is not responsible for any comments
made by its users which may be seen as obscene or otherwise inappropriate.
All realtime information transmitted through the Elendor system including
pages, commands, and network routing information provided by users' host
computer systems and other third-party networks is subject to monitoring
and/or censure. By logging on to this system you give up privacy rights to
this realtime electronic information. Also we'll get your little dog too.

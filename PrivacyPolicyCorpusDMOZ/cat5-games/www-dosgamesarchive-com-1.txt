Our commitment to privacy
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available from every page of this website.
The information we collect
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on DOS Games Archive. We use return e-mail addresses to answer the messages we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.
We never use or share personally identifiable information provided to us online without providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.
We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website, but we would not disclose anything that could be used to identify those individuals.
Our hit tracking service tracks general information about all visitors to the site, such as type web browser, operating system, screen resolution, etc. No information about specific visitors is ever tracked or stored. This information is used to ensure that the site is compatible with the majority of visitors.
This site uses the services of ad services, which include Google AdSense and/or others for the serving and/or targeting of ads, promotions and other marketing messages. To do this, the advertiser(s) may collect anonymous data through the use of cookies.
Third-party advertising
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, e-mail address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, you can find that on the website of Network Advertising Initiative.
Our commitment to data security
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
Disclosure
DOS Games Archive openly volunteers its practices of collecting information, and reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time.
How to contact us
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please contact us.

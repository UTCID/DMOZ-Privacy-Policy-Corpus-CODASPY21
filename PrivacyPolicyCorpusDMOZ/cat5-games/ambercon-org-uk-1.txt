AmberCon UK will never sell our members data and will only pass it on to third parties as necessary for the running of AmberCon.
This will include (but is not limited to) passing the names of attendees
to the Con venue and passing names and email address of players to
GMs/GMs to players. We will also add your email address to a mailing
list which will be used for Con communications.
If you have any concerns about our use of your data please use the contact form to get in touch.
Please note that we are not responsible for the content of external websites, nor for keeping those updated.

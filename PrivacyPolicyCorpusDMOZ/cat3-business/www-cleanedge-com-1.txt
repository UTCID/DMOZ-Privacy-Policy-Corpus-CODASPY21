Clean Edge is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. We recognize that when you provide us with information about yourself, you trust us to act in a responsible manner. The following discloses our information gathering and usage practices for our website www.cleanedge.com, newsletters, alerts, and other services (Clean Edge). Please read the following closely to understand our practices. Types of Data CollectedWe may collect personally identifiable information from you that may include your name, address, e-mail address, company or organization, position or title, company size, company type, what report you downloaded and other information. We do not, however, knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of thirteen. This information is sent to and stored by MailChimp and used by Clean Edge to send bulk emails.Furthermore, we automatically collect non-personally identifiable IP addresses and web site usage information from you when you visit Clean Edge. This information is sent to Google Analytics and helps Clean Edge track site traffic and optimizes the user experience. If you wish to unsubscribe from Clean Edge emails, you may do so by using this link or by following the unsubscribe instructions included in Clean Edge electronic mailings. You may also contact us at any time to request your data be deleted from our website or MailChimp.Use of Data Collected We use contact data to send you free reports and newsletters, information about our company or our products or services, and promotional material from some of our partners, or to contact you when necessary. We do not sell, rent, or swap your name, address, or other personal information with our sponsors or other parties.

Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy applies when you call Sioux Steel Company and its affiliates (“Sioux”) with account and financial information to make a payment on your account with Sioux. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.
What information do we collect?
We collect financial account information as well as the personal information necessary to complete a payment by phone.
How do we protect your information?
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal and account information.
Do we disclose any information to outside parties?
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information except to those necessary to conduct the transaction. This also does not include trusted third parties who assist us in conducting our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law.

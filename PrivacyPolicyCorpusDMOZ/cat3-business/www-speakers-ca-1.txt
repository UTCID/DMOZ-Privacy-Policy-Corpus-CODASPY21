Privacy Policy
Speakers’ Spotlight is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party. Speakers’ Spotlight uses our website to provide information about speakers, to respond to your requests and to assist you in selecting a speaker for an event. You may be asked to provide personal information such as name, address, e-mail address and telephone number. This information will only be used by Speakers’ Spotlight to contact you in regards to your specific request(s).
Any information you provide or have provided in the past will be used to assist you with your speaker event and keep you up to date on the activities of Speakers’ Spotlight, including new speakers, hot topics, special events, showcases and more, through periodic contacts.  If at any time you wish to be removed from our email or phone list simply contact us at 1-800-333-4453 or info@speakers.ca and we will gladly accommodate your request.

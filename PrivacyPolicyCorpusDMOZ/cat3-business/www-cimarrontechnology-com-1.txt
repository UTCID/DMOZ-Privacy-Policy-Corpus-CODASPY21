Automatically Collected InformationInformation about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by us. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of our business, to maintain the quality of our products and services, and to provide general statistics regarding use of this website.
Use of CookiesThis website may use “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver malware to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie.One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize our pages, or register with this site or its services, a cookie helps this site to recall your specific information for subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, etc. When you return to this website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the features that you customized.You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of this website.
Security of Your Personal InformationWe secure your personal information, in particular your financial information, from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. When personal information, including credit card payment information, is transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol.Our website is equipped with Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. Online orders to us must be placed from a secure browser that supports SSL encryption. Secure browsers will automatically enter the encrypted mode when you make a purchase. If you do not have access to a secure browser, or would like to place an order using another method, please contact us for further information on placing an offline order.

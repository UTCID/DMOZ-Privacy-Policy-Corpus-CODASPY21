Privacy Policy
BudgetPlanners.net respects your right to privacy. We are committed to the privacy of all individuals who visit and use our web site. Our site is intended to be used only as an educational resource and as a way for you to gain information about the services BudgetPlanners.net provides.
If you are just using our site to gain education about financial matters, you do so with complete anonymity. We will never ask for personal information during your visit unless you request the services of a counselors by filling out the on line form. This form does not require disclosure of account numbers, social security number, date of birth, or any other identifying information.
If you request a call back via the web or contact one of our representatives directly, BudgetPlanners.net will not disclose your information to any third party, except as permitted or required by law.
Any sites referred to within the contents of this site are only meant as a supplement to the information or products that BudgetPlanners.net provides. Although we believe these other sites are reputable, we cannot be responsible for their data collection and use practices. Please refer to that site's privacy policy regarding how they will use any information and how they gather that information. We will never share any information about you with any of these sites.
If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy or would like additional information, please contact us and we will gladly discuss our privacy policy with you in further detail.

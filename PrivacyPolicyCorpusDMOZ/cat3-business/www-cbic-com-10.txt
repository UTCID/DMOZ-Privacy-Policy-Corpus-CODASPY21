Contractors Bonding and Insurance Company
Contractors Bonding and Insurance Company (CBIC), an RLI company, specializes in surety bonds and niche property and casualty insurance products.
CBIC is a strong and stable partner you can count on. Our underwriters have proven experience that enables them to respond quickly to the unique business demands of the industries and customers you serve. We’re an A+ rated carrier that has been serving small to medium sized businesses since 1979.
Our Products
Artisan and General Contractors Insurance (Contrac Pac®) — Our Contrac Pac program offers artisan and general contractors a package for contractors license bond, general liability, property and inland marine insurance.
Package Policies for Business Owners — CBIC writes a wide variety of small to medium-sized commercial packages and monoline business that does not qualify for a BOP policy.
Package Policies for Contractors — Our package policies are designed for larger contractors that exceed our Contrac Pac appetite.
Surety and Fidelity Bonds — We offer an array of surety and fidelity bonds to meet the unique needs of our customers including notaries, individuals operating in a fiduciary capacity, public officials, contractors as well as public and private companies operating across diverse industries.

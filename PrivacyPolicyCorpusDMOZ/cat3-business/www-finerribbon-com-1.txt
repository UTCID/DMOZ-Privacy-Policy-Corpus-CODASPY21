How We Use Gathered Information
FinerRibbon.com may request personal information, including your name, shipping address, telephone number, e-mail address, billing address, credit card number, and expiration date. FinerRibbon.com collects the requested information in order to:
Process an order.
Verify your identity for payment authorization.
Contact you regarding your order status.
Ship your order to you.
Maintain an order history for future support.
Enter you in a contest or sweepstakes (only if you wish to participate).
Send you news from FinerRibbon.com (if you sign up to our newsletter).
Provide you with the best online shopping experience.
Once FinerRibbon.com gathers your name and address, this information will then be transferred to your credit card company, if applicable, for confirmation to complete your purchase. Your shipping information may also be supplied to a third-party fulfillment center, but your information will not be used for any other purpose than delivery of your order. Your name and address will not be sold to mailing-list firms or any mass advertising entities.

FinerRibbon.com verifies that these third parties do not use your information for any other purpose other than to manage the promotion.

FinerRibbon.com works with a third-party advertising company to collect information on advertisement performance and Web site usage. To do so, this company may use information about your visits to the FinerRibbon.com Web site and other Web sites. Unless otherwise notified, our third-party advertising company does not use names, addresses, e-mail addresses, or telephone numbers and does not link any online actions or cookies to an identifiable person. The use of your cookies is subject to this privacy policy.

Protection Against Fraudulent Charges
If a third party obtains your personal information from a purchase made with FinerRibbon.com and uses that information to perpetrate credit card fraud, you are legally protected. Under the Fair Credit Billing Act, your bank may hold you liable for up to $ 50.00 USD of fraudulent charges. If your bank holds you liable for any portion of the $ 50.00 USD, FinerRibbon.com will cover up to the entire $ 50.00 USD. FinerRibbon.com will cover this liability if the unauthorized use resulted through no fault of yours from purchases made on FinerRibbon.com's secure server. In the event of unauthorized use of your credit card, you must notify your credit card company in accordance with its reporting rules and procedures.

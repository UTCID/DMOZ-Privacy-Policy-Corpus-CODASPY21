Odessa Pumps
From groundwater pumping, treatment to distribution, Odessa Pumps strives to meet customer's needs by representing quality products at the forefront of the water/wastewater industry. We offer booster station pumping equipment, water plants, lift stations, custom controls and accessories, plunger and piston type pumps, pump repairs, and rental services. We are your turnkey pump solution resource.
We're here to help.

Keep Your Production Flowing
Odessa Pumps serves the complete pump needs of oil and gas operators across the full spectrum of applications. We provide turnkey solutions, packages, parts, repairs and state-of-the-art machining for pump remanufacturing and repair. Odessa Pumps serves customers across Texas, New Mexico, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Louisiana and Colorado. Plus we export products worldwide.

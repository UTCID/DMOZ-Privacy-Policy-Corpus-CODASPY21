Privacy Policy
As you may be aware, the new General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), comes into effect on 25th May 2018.
For us to be compliant we need to let you know about the personal data we hold about you, our legal basis for holding it and how your personal data is used.
Personal data we control or process.
First name
Last name
Job title
Company email address
Company telephone / mobile
Device IP address
We take your privacy seriously and will only use your personal data to ensure continuity of service and supply and / or to provide you with information on relevant products and services unless you object to us doing so.
Lawful basis for processing.
Our lawful basis for processing is ‘legitimate interest’. We have carried out a ‘Legitimate Interests Assessment’ and followed ICO guidelines to arrive at this decision.
Email marketing.
We send occasional product or service emails, but this does not exceed one per month. You can of course unsubscribe from these at any time by sending us an email.
Updated privacy policy.
For details on how we use your personal data and your rights under GDPR legislation please contact us and a hard copy can be posted or emailed.  The Privacy Policy can also be viewed on our website. Full Privacy Policy HERE

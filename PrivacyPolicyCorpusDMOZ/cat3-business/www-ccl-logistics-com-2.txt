CCL Cookie Policy This cookie policy outlines how we use cookies on CCL websites including advanceforwarding.com, advancepacking.com, alpharoute.co.uk and parcelsplease.com. They help us make the experience of using our website as easy and useful as possible.
About cookies in general Delivering services over the internet can involve placing small amounts of information on your computer, mobile phone or device – this is done via small files called cookies.
How we use cookies We use cookies to improve the user experience on our websites. These cookies monitor how visitors use our website so that we can continually improve the service we provide and allow us to tailor information provided to you. Such cookies are used to identify general visitor information.
Cookies used for web analytics We use third party services to gather data on how our customers interact with and use our website – this is done using cookies on our websites. These cookies are used for information such as the number of views each web page has received and the links which are used most frequently. They are also used to maintain the log-in to your account during a visit if you are using it.

Some of our cookies are provided by Google Analytics which is a service from by Google Inc. and uses a cookie to track a visitor’s use of our websites.

For further information on how Google use cookies to store information and how you can opt out, please visit: http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/
Controlling and deleting cookies We will not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information about you. Should you wish to restrict the use of cookies on this or any other website, you can disable cookies in your web browser settings. Restricting cookies on our websites can restrict the functionality available to you.

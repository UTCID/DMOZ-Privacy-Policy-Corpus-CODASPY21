Privacy Policy
At Mode Beads, we value your need for privacy. Our secure server encrypts all information ensuring none of your personal information is ever misused or altered.
Modebeads values and respects the privacy of our customers. Our privacy policy provides you with information on how Modebeads collects, uses, and shares the information we request from our customers.
Information we collect: We may collect the name and all relevant contact information of our customers prior to being approved to our website for shopping, such as billing and shipping addresses, phone numbers, email address and the like. In addition we will always request a Tax ID or EIN number in order to be approved as a wholesale account.
When you make a purchase we will collect your payment information, including information from your credit or debit card, Paypal account or gift card. International orders may require you to provide bank information as well as additional identification.
When using our website, we may collect information about what browser you are using. We may track the pages you visit, the path you take along our site and the amount of time you spend on our site. We use this data for analysis to better improve your shopping experience.
We will not share any of your information with any third parties. The information you share with us is safely stored in our private databases. The secure socket layer (SSL) encrypts all information ensuring none of your personal information is ever misused or altered.
All customer data we collect is protected against unauthorized access. We use a 256- but encryption SSL certificate through Verisign to provide you with the highest level of security when ordering with us.

Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Kaman Corporation's policy is to protect personally identifiable information provided to us at the Kaman Site from unauthorized access. Personally identifiable information means data that can identify you, including your name, address, telephone number or e-mail address.
Information Collected
You can visit the Kaman Site without providing any personally identifiable information. However, we may need to collect such data from you for a specific purpose, such as when you request information or to monitor user traffic on the site or for other purposes. There are also portions of the websites of certain Kaman subsidiaries that permit you to register for the purpose of purchasing products or services and personally identifiable information will be collected for that purpose. The Kaman Site is not intended for use by persons under the age of 13. Kaman does not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from or about children or markets its products and services to children.
Kaman subsidiary websites or other listed sites may have their own privacy policy statement, in which case any additional terms in such policy will apply to your accessing of the subsidiary website.
Cookies
Kaman may use cookies on this site. Cookies are small text files sent to and stored on users’ computers that allow Websites, such as Kaman.com, to recognize repeat users, facilitate users’ access to sites, and allow sites to compile aggregate data that will allow content improvements. Cookies do not damage users’ computers or files. If users do not want cookies to be accessible by Kaman, most browser programs allow users to deny or disable the use of cookies.
Use of Collected Information
Kaman uses the personally identifiable information that you provide only to respond to your inquiry or process your request. Kaman may need to share this information with companies that have been hired to perform services, however, those service providers are required to use the information only for the service they have been hired to provide (e.g., when you pay for a purchase with a credit card). Kaman does not share personally identifiable information with any third parties for marketing use. Kaman will release such information if you direct us to, if we are required by law to do so, or under other limited circumstances as permitted by law.
Security
The Kaman Site uses various systemic and administrative means to protect personally identifiable information from unauthorized or improper access. Within Kaman, access to such information is limited to those having a need to know in order to process your inquiry.
Contacting Kaman
If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us.
If you are an EU employee or customer and would like to request your information, please do so through our Data Subject Request Portal.

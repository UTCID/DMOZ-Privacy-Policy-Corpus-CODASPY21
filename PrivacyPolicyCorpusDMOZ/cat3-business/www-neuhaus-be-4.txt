HOW TO ORDER A GIFT ONLINE
Discover our tutorial to easily order a gift online.

You can also follow the guide below
1
ADD PRODUCTS TO YOUR BASKET
By clicking on the "Add to basket" button, your products will automatically be saved in your cart.
2
PROCEED TO CHECKOUT
After reviewing your shopping cart, click on "Proceed to checkout".
3
ADD YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
Here you can fill in your personal information. Please note that this is not yet the delivery address.
4
ADD DELIVERY ADDRESS
Add the first and last name of the recipient. Uncheck "My  shipping address is the same as the billing address ". Add the delivery information of the person to whom you wish to send the package.
5
ADD A PERSONAL MESSAGE
You can write your personal message to your order (don't forget to mention your own name). Select "I accept the general terms and conditions" and click on the "Buy" button.
PAY AND FINALIZE YOUR ORDER
Choose your method of payment and proceed with the purchase. We are a secure online boutique and guarantee a secure payment thanks to a trusted partnership with our suppliers.

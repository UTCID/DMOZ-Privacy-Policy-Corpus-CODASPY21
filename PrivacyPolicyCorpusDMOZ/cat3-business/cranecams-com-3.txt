Privacy Policy
Your privacy is a primary concern to us at Crane Cams®. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the way your information is used. This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on our website.
This policy is effective commencing on January 1, 2018. Crane Cams® reserves the right to change this policy at any time and such changes shall be effective as soon as they are posted. Users should review this site periodically to ensure that you are aware of such changes.
We respect your privacy. All information collected at this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, exchanged, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you provide to Crane Cams® will be held with the utmost care and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.
Crane Cams® will not disclose any of your personally identifiable information except when we have your permission, contest winners or under special circumstances, such as when required by law. Any statistics that we gather from our surveys do not include any identifiable information about any individual person.
If you do not want to receive e-mail or mailings from us simply reply "No" to any questions requesting approval. If you indicated that you are interested in receiving offers or information from Crane Cams®, we may occasionally e-mail you about products and services that we feel may be of interest to you. Only Crane Cams® will send you these direct correspondences, and only if you indicated that you do not object to these offerings.
If you later wish to be omitted from these mailings let us know by mail or phone, and Crane Cams® will act accordingly. To ensure we honor your request, please provide us with your exact name, address and e-mail address.

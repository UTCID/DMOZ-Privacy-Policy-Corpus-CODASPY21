https://www.zrecords.ltd.uk/privacy-policy/
Z Records takes the privacy of its customers very seriously, and will only collect information in order to fulfill customer orders and (if requested by the customer) to provide information via our e-mail new release lists. We never send unsolicited e-mails to customers, and we do not sell or share mailing lists or customer information to any other companies or individuals. Details of how to contact us if you have any questions about privacy or data protection can be found below in the Contact Details section.
Information collected:
To process an order you must create an account where we require your name, e-mail address, delivery address and billing address. We do not collect or retain any credit card or payment information. If you sign up to be on our mailing list we will send promotional emails about new products, special offers or other information which we think you may find interesting using the email address which you have provided. If you no longer wish to receive any communication for us you can Unsubcribe at any time via the links at the bottom of our email communication or by contacting us directly.
Security:
The shopping cart part of the site is secure and the payment for your order is facilitated through PayPal. We do not retain any of your payment information. We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

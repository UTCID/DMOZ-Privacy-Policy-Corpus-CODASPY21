SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION
The security of your Personally Identifiable Information is very important to us, and Lincare takes reasonable steps to protect the information you provide us from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.We follow generally accepted industry standards to safeguard your information, however, no data or email transmission is ever fully secure or error free.Therefore, please take special care in deciding what information to send us.We have reasonable safeguards in place to protect your information, and we have taken the following security measures:
Transport Layer Security (TLS) - We use TLS encryption when transmitting certain kinds of information, such as Health Information through the App from your mobile device to the Lincare server.An icon resembling a padlock is displayed in most browsers during TLS sessions.We also encrypt the Health Information you provide to us when such Health Information is at rest and stored in our servers.When you send text messages or emails to your friends, family or other individuals in your contact list, those messages or emails are outside of our control and are not encrypted.Alerts and reminders sent to your mobile device are also not encrypted.
Secure Storage - We deploy reasonable and appropriate industry standard physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your personal information.
Vendors and Partners - To protect the security and privacy of your information, we may provide information to partners and vendors who work on our behalf or with us and require them to agree to maintain the confidentiality of your information.
Access to Information - We limit access to your Personally Identifiable Information to those employees who reasonably need access to such information in order to provide you with services and process your information appropriately.

LINCARE COOKIE POLICY
Google Analytics is a Google tool that we use for web analysis and allows us to understand how Users engage with our Website and other Sites.  Google Analytics may use cookies to collect Non-Personally Identifiable Information .Cookies are small bits of information cached or stored on a computer, mobile or other device of a User for record keeping purposes.  Cookies may be used for many purposes, including, but not limited to, remembering you and your preferences and tracking your usage in order to improve the service.  Cookies themselves do not typically contain any Personally Identifiable Information.  We may analyze the information derived from these cookies and other technological tools we employ to make improvements and in keeping with observed User preferences.  They allow us to remember useful information which allows certain functionality within the App or other Site to work, for example, remembering if you’ve logged in to the App.  Most web browsers automatically accept cookies; however, you can usually change your browser settings to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent.  If you choose to disable cookies, some parts of the Sites may not function properly or may be considerably slower, or there may be some features that will not be available to you.  For more information on Google Analytics, please visit www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.
Other Types of Cookies
Strictly Necessary Cookies. These cookies are used to make sure our Site works and that you can use it effectively, such as remembering certain information on a form when you go to another page before the form has been completed.These cookies are used to remember information you’ve entered on our forms when you navigate to different pages in a single web browser session using our Site.These cookies are not used to gather information that could be used to advertise products or services to you, or to remember any preferences you have set beyond the current visit.If you prevent these cookies you may not be able to use certain features on our Site
Performance Cookies.These cookies are used to measure the performance of our Site, improve how our Site works, and measure our marketing activity. Information that is collected using these cookies is aggregated and anonymous and we are not able to identify individual users.We may use these cookies to provide aggregated statistics on how our website is used, see how effective our marketing activity is, and provide feedback to partners that one of our visitors also visited their website. We may also use these cookies to measure any errors that occur, improve the performance of our Site, and test different page designs on our Site.We do not use these cookies to target you with advertising on other websites, or to remember any preferences you have set beyond the current visit.
Functionality Cookies.These cookies allow websites to remember choices you make (for example, your user name, language or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personal features. These cookies can also be used to remember changes you have made to text size, fonts and other parts of web pages that you can modify. They may also be used to provide services on the site such as watching a video. We use these cookies to remember settings you've applied such as layout and text size, and remember if you have already completed an online survey.We do not use these cookies to target you with advertising or track your browsing activity on other websites.

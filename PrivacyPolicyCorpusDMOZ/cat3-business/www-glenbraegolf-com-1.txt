WEBSITE PRIVACY & DATA SUBMISSION POLICY
This policy explains how we, Spectrum Yarns Limited (company number 1142407) (trading as "Glenbrae"), who operate the website www.glenbrae.com ("the Website"), require you to abide by our terms and conditions with regard to the data you submit for us. It also explains how we use the information we collect about you, how you can instruct us if you prefer to limit the use of that information and the procedures that we have in place to safeguard your privacy.
By submitting data to us you agree with the following terms:
YOUR SUBMISSION OF DATA
1.1 You are authorised to submit data on behalf of the named person or business.
1.2 You confirm, warrant and represent to us that the data you submit ("the Information") is true and accurate.
1.3 You confirm, warrant and represent that the Information does not contain any sensitive personal data (as defined in the Data Protection Act 1998), is not obscene, is not defamatory and does not infringe the rights of any other person.
The information we collect and how we use it
2.1 When you submit Information you must provide your name, delivery address and email address and any other information that we may from time to time reasonably request from you.
2.2 We gather the Information to allow us to process your order(s). The relevant Information will subsequently be used by us, our agents and sub-contractors to communicate with you on any matters relating to your order(s) and on our products, and offers on the Website.
2.3 We may aggregate information provided by you to create or refine statistics for the purposes of monitoring website use in order to help us develop the Website and our services and we may provide such aggregate information and/or statistics to third parties. This will not include information that can be used to identify any individual.
2.4 We may provide you with information about our products or about features of the Website or about trade shows or about any other matters which we think may be of interest to you either in a regular newsletter or otherwise. If you would rather not receive this information, please send a blank email message to information@glenbrae.com.
2.5 We may provide you with related information from third parties we think may be of interest to you. If you would rather not receive this information, please send an email to information@glenbrae.com.
2.6 We may provide your information to third parties whose products or services we believe may be of interest to you. If you do not wish us to disclose your information in this way, please send an email to information@glenbrae.com

Privacy Policy
Radius Display Products has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the website http://www.radiusdp.com.
As a convenience to you, and to further enhance your online experience, this site may contain links to other sites. Radius Display Products is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. Check any privacy policy on the target site before giving out personal information.
What We Collect and How We Use It
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information, and to help provide you with a relevant online visit to the site. Cookies are used to further enhance the visitors browsing experience.
Our online registration and information request pages ask visitors for contact information (like their name, phone, address, and email address). We use contact data from these forms to send the user information about our company and our products. The visitor's contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary. This information may be forwarded to one of our resellers or outside sales representatives solely for the purpose of following up on your request for information on our products. We never make this information available to third parties outside our sales network. We will never sell, rent, or otherwise share your information with outside parties beyond our immediate sales network.
If you arrive at this website by clicking on a link in one of our broadcast emails, we may track your entry point in order to provide a more relevant online experience and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing.
Opting Out of Our System
This site gives users the following options for modifying information previously provided, or to opt-out of any or all of our marketing communication channels. When contacting us, please include your contact number (first line on mailing label or last line in broadcast email), or a recent job or invoice number, to aid us in identifying you in our database and verifying your identity. Also tell us if you want us to stop contacting you via mail, fax, e-mail, or all.

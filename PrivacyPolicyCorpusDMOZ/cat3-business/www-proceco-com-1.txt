Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which PROCECO Ltd. collects, uses, maintains, discloses and otherwise manages personal information of users of the www.proceco.com website ("Site") and our customers (collectively, "Users").
This Privacy Policy applies to the Site and other interactions with PROCECO Ltd.
Collection of Personal Information
We may collect personal information from Users including, but not limited to, when Users visit our site, register on the site, fill out a form to request a quote, technical support or information, order products, or contact us about an employment opportunity or in connection with other activities, services, features or resources we make available on our Site.
Users may be asked to provide, as appropriate, a name, company name, job title, business address, email address, telephone or fax number, and payment information.
Users may also provide us with feedback about our Site, products or services, survey responses and employment information (such as a resume, cover letter, or similar employment-related materials).
Website Information
We may collect information about Users whenever they interact with our Site.
Such information may include the browser name, the type of computer and technical information about User's means of connection to our Site, such as the operating system, the Internet service providers utilized and other similar information.
Web Browser Cookies
Our Site may use "cookies".
A cookie is a tiny element of data that our Site sends to a User's browser, which may then be stored on a User's hard drive.
We use cookies to enhance the User experience and gather and analyze information about our Site.
Users may choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert them when cookies are being sent.
If they do so, note that some parts of the Site may not function properly.
How We Use Personal Information
PROCECO Ltd. collects and uses Users personal information for the following purposes:
To fulfill requests for our products and services
We may use a User's name and contact information to process requests for certain services, such as to download a brochure or to request a service call or technical support. We may also use a User's contact and payment information to process product orders and ensure proper delivery. We may also use this information to improve our product and service offerings.
To improve our Site
We continually strive to improve our Site offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from Users.
In addition, we use information, such as browser name, type of computer and other technical information listed above to help us understand our Site activity, and to monitor and improve the Site.
To improve customer service
We use a User's name and contact information to help us to more effectively respond to
requests for a quote or information, sales or support needs.
To process job applications
We may advertise employment opportunities on the Site. We may use a User's employment information for the purposes of processing and responding to a User's application for current and future career opportunities.
To send periodic e-mails
If a User decides to opt-in to our mailing list, they will receive emails occasionally that may include company news, updates, related product or service information, etc. If at any time the User would like to unsubscribe from receiving future e-mails, we include detailed unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email.
How We Protect Your Information
We have adopted reasonable technical, physical and administrative measures designed to protect against loss, misuse or unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction of personal information.
Disclosure & Sharing of Personal Information
We do not sell, trade, rent or otherwise transfer User's personal information without consent, except as required or permitted by law or as set out herein.
We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal information regarding visitors and Users with our business partners, trusted affiliates and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.
Service Providers
We may transfer (or otherwise make available) personal information to our affiliates or other third parties who provide services on our behalf such as to host our website, to send emails on our behalf or conduct customer research.
Sale of Business

Legal

We may also disclose personal information where necessary for the establishment, exercise or defence of legal claims, or as otherwise permitted by law.
Third Party Websites

Except as provided herein, we do not provide personal information to these sites without consent.

Privacy Policy
Share this:
Your privacy is very important to us. Foliot Furniture respects your concerns about maintaining strict privacy. Any information that you divulge to us is kept in confidence. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) on our website or otherwise, unless you choose to give it to us via our online inquiry form, an electronic mail message, a phone call, or by postal mail. The type of information you provide through our online form may include email address, name, mailing address, city, state, zip code, and phone number. Even when you choose to give us information, we keep it confident. We will only release your PII if you have given explicit consent. We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose mailing lists or other PII with third parties. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please fill out the form on the “Contact” menu above.

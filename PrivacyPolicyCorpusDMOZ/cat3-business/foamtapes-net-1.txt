Shipping Information
Please note that all orders ship from: Paulsboro, NJ 08066
All orders are shipped between 8:00 am and 5:00 pm Monday through Friday EST. Foamtapes.net does not ship orders on weekends or legal holidays. On average, orders ship the next business day, however all orders are subject to a 1-3 business day processing time, unless otherwise previously agreed to by Foamtapes.net. If an order will take more than three (3) business days to process, we will contact you prior to shipping.

Coffee Facts
More than two thirds of adult Canadians enjoyed at least one cup of coffee yesterday with the average at 2.7 cups/day.
Coffee is the most popular beverage among adult Canadians over 16 – even more than tap water.
By age, consumers under 35 are more likely to consume gourmet coffee beverages and frozen blended coffee beverages are most popular among those 18-24.

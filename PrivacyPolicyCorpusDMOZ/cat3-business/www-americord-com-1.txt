Your Privacy is Our Priority
At Americord, your privacy is of utmost importance to us.
Americord has created this privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy & security. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website.
We seek to maintain the highest level of service in our business, including the way we handle our customer's personal information.
Our site uses a secure shopping cart basket for customers to place orders safely & securely. Customer's contact information is strictly used for processing orders and sending company newsletters with the customer's consent. This information may also be used to contact the customer about the order, if necessary.
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Our shopping cart uses industry-standard SSL encryption techniques to ensure the protection of your personal and financial information. However, if you feel uncomfortable you are welcome to e-mail, fax, or phone in your order toll free at 1-800-647-6231.

Northlander Industries Privacy Policy Statement
Northlander respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it at all times. We collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address, email address, phone number(s), postal code, and resume when you submit through the careers or contact us page. Until you submit your information, you are anonymous to us and we have no way of identifying you.
PERSONAL INFORMATION
We do not share with, sell to or allow any third party to make any use of your personal information including your e-mail address. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at innovations@northlanderindustries.com.
COOKIES
A cookie is a small data file that web sites often store on your computer’s hard drive when you visit their sites. We use cookies but do not store personally identifiable information in your cookie. We use cookies in order to improve your shopping experience. Cookies enable us to keep track of your order as you shop on our site. We may also use a cookie to monitor and maintain information about your use of our website in a manner that does not identify you. In either case, this information helps us track your order, improve your shopping experience and improve our site design.

Your Privacy
Your privacy is secure at Fetpak. Whether you place an order, request a catalog, email us or anything else, we will never sell, rent or distribute any of your information for any purpose whatsoever. Your privacy is safe with us. We guarantee that.
Your Security
Since Fetpak originally went online back in 1997, there has never been a case of credit card fraud reported by any of our customers. We have taken many steps to insure that all transactions and all of your information is secure. When you enter our checkout area you move into a secure area as shown by "https" in the URL. We use Secure Socket Layers (SSL) which encrypts your order information into an extremely high indecipherable code which is then processed immediately.
Our security company, Alpha SSL has verified our security methods and has approved us as shown from their seal placed on every page of our website. Click their seal to see more details on our approval certificate.
For additional security, Fetpak is also verified by Authorized.Net where transactions are processed without us ever seeing your full credit card number. Therefore we never store your credit card number. See our Authorized.net verified seal at the bottom of every page.

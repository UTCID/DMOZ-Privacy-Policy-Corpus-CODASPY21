Data PrivacyThe use of our website is possible without providing personal information. With regards to how we collect personal data (for example name, address or email addresses), this takes place, as far as possible, always on a voluntary basis. These data will not be disclosed to third parties without your explicit consent.Please note that data transmission over the Internet (for example, when communicating via email) may have security vulnerabilities. A complete protection of the data from access by third parties is not possible.
CookiesWe use cookies to facilitate and improve your visit on our website. The use of cookies constitutes a legitimate interest on our part. The legal basis for this is Article 6- GDPR.Popular browsers offer the preference option of not allowing cookies. Note: There is no guarantee that you will be able to access all the functions of this website without restrictions if you make the appropriate settings.
Collection and Processing of Personal DataPersonal data will only be collected by us and only to the extent that you provide us.  For example, in the context of a boat purchase, inquiry, brochure request, email newsletter registration, or J/Boat owner website, your personal data may be stored electronically, physically on paper, or via contracted data services.  You may see the J/Boats Privacy Policy Roadmap here on this link (a PDF document). Personal information means all information that is used to identify your person and which can be traced back to you – such as your name, email address, physical address and telephone number.This data is kept confidential and used only for the original purpose. Basically, the data is never transmitted to third parties. Unless it is required, or permitted by law, you have given us your consent or an official order has been made to store your personal data solely for the use by J/Boats.If you contact us via email, phone, or mail, the emails and the related data will be stored with us in the mail system, in the CRM (customer relations management system), or physically on paper. The legal basis for this is either Article 6- GDPR.The emails themselves are not encrypted.
Newsletter DataIf you would like to receive the newsletter offered on the website, we need an email address from you, as well as information that allows us to verify that you are the owner of the specified email address and that you agree to receive the newsletter. Further data is not collected. We use this data exclusively for the delivery of the requested information and do not pass it on to third parties.The newsletter is sent by MailChimp, 675 Ponce de Leon Avenue NE, Suite 5000, Atlanta, GA 30308 USA. For information about the privacy practices of the shipping service provider, please visit: https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/.Withdrawal and termination: You can revoke your consent to receive the newsletter at any time and thus cancel the newsletter subscription. After your cancellation, the deletion of your personal data takes place. Your consent to the newsletter shipping expires at the same time. At the end of each newsletter you will find the link to termination.

Your RightsAt any time you have the right to information according to Article 15- GDPR, whether we process personal data from you.

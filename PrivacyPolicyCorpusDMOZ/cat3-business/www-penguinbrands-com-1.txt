Implus Acquires RockTape
October 02, 2018 - Implus, the market leader in athletic, fitness and outdoor accessories, today announced the acquisition of RockTape®, a leading provider of kinesiology tape, medical education and other health and wellness products. RockTape's products will be additive to Implus' existing suite of health and wellness brands which include TriggerPoint™, Harbinger®, Spenco®, Perfect Fitness® and SKLZ®.

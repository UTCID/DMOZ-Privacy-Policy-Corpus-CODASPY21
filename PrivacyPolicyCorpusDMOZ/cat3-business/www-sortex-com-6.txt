Process Technologies overview
Bühler Group | Homepage
Optical sorting is essential in any processing operation. It sorts grain, food and plastic, helping you meet food safety requirements and achieve a purer product quality.
Our sorting equipment uses a range of technologies including cameras, lighting and machine learning software. It removes product defects and foreign materials by color, shape and texture.
We also have an extensive product range based on budget, capacity and the product quality you need to achieve.
+
Highlights
A rice sorter powered by AI and machine learning
The Sortex S UltraVision is one of our easiest optical sorters to operate. It requires minimal monitoring and configuration – just start sorting in three easy steps. It also has a small footprint and a capacity of up to 18 tons per hour.
Hygienically designed to reduce food contamination
The Sortex F optical sorter is designed to reduce microbial contamination in fruit and vegetable sorting. It has sloping surfaces to prevent food from clogging. Its open frame allows you to easily clean surfaces in contact with food.

Better seed germination and yields
Our optical sorters sort a wide range of seed varieties from field crops, herbage seeds to vegetable seeds. They detect unwanted colors, seed defects, diseased seeds and foreign materials – helping you achieve better germination and promote consistent seed growth.

Helping you reach high product quality and yields
Our optical sorters meet the most stringent requirements in food safety and quality. They remove color defects, unwanted seed varieties and foreign materials at minimal yield loss. Helping you meet food safety requirements while maintaining yields.
For consistent yields and quality
Our optical sorters are suitable for all types of pulses, including beans, lentils, grams and peas. They are designed to minimize product damage and efficiently target defects such as spots from as small as 0.5 mm in size. Helping you achieve better yields and quality.

Maximize the recovery of plastics and yields
Our range of sorters for plastics covers PET flakes, HDPE, uPVC window regrinds to WEE/ELV, ABS, PS and pellets. They can remove the same color PVC from PE or color groupings.

Preserve the product quality of spices

High product quality and yields

Improve pasta quality and yields

High standards of tea
Our tea sorters are suitable for different varieties of tea, such as black and green flakes. They are equipped with the latest camera and lighting technologies to remove unwanted tea flakes and foreign materials at minimal loss.
Delivering safer food and higher yields
Our optical sorters sort a wide range of processed fresh and frozen vegetables. They remove large and subtle color defects, EVM such as pea pods, stalks, sticks and stems from green beans. They also remove foreign materials such as insects, stones, wood, cardboard and plastics.

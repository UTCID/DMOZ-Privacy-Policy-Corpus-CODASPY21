Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining how we process the information collected on this website.
Information Collected:
On www.absalonequipment.com you can browse information, order products, and fill out request forms. The types of personally identifiable information that may be collected at these pages include: name, address, email address, telephone number, and credit card information.
How We Use this Information:
AB Salon Equipment will only use the information you provide to fulfill your request. The information will not be disclosed to third parties, and will not be published unless you specifically permit us to do so. AB Salon Equipment will not use the collected information to send you unsolicited mailings.
Cookies:
To enhance your experience with our website, some of our web pages use “cookies”. Cookies are text files we place in your computer’s web browser to store your information. Cookies, by themselves, do not tell us any of your personal information unless you choose to provide this information to us by filling out a form. We only use cookies to improve the content on our web site. For example, some forms may recall the previously entered information when you revisit a web page.
Security Policy:
We use the latest technologies to protect and provide you with secure online commerce.
True 128-bit encrypted SSL. Tested, audited, and proven secure.

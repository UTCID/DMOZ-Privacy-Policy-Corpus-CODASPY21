INFORMATION NOTICE CONCERNING THE PROCESSING OF PERSONAL DATA
CSM TUBE spa (hereinafter “Company”) provides this information notice pursuant and for the effects of art. 13 of (EU) Regulation 2016/679 (hereinafter “GDPR”) on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data in order to inform you of the purposes and methods of processing of the personal data that you may disclose while browsing this website (hereinafter “Site”).
We would like to point out that this information notice refers solely to the Site and not to third-party websites that the User may reach via links herein.
CATEGORIES OF PROCESSED DATABrowsing dataThe IT systems and the software procedures that are used for operating this website acquire, in their normal operation, certain personal data, the transmission of which is inherent in the use of Internet communication protocols. This information is not collected to be associated with identified data subjects but may, by its very nature, through processing and association with data held by third parties, allow the users to be identified. This category of data includes, for example, the IP addresses or domain names of the computers used by users who connect to the site, the URI (Uniform Resource Identifier) addresses of the requested resources, the time of the request, the method used to submit the request to the server, the size of the file obtained in response, the numerical code that indicates the status of the reply given by the server (success, error, etc.) and other parameters related to the user’s operating system and IT environment. These data are used for the sole purpose of retrieving anonymous statistical information on the use of the website and to monitor its correct operation, and are deleted immediately after processing.
Data provided intentionally by the UserOptional, express and intentional sending of e-mail to the addresses indicated in the Site results in the subsequent acquisition of the sender’s address necessary to reply to the requests, as well as of other personal data contained in the message. Specific summary information notices are also contained or displayed in the sections of the Site that are arranged for particular services on request and subject, if required, to the consent of the data subject.
CookiesIn accordance with the Decree of the Italian Data Protection Authority no. 229 of 8 May 2014 “Simplified Arrangements to Provide Information and Obtain Consent Regarding Cookies”, the Company would like to inform users on the use of navigation cookies on this Site. We would like to point out that this information notice refers solely to the Site and not to third-party websites that the User may reach via links herein.
What are cookies?A cookie is a small file sent to the browser in use and stored on your device when you visit a website such as csmtube.com. Cookies assure efficient operation of the Site and improve its performance, provide the owner of the Site with information for statistical or advertising purposes, mainly to personalise your browsing experience by remembering your preferences.

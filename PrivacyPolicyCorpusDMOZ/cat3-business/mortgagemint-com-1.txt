PRIVACY POLICY
The Mortgage Mint®,LLC recognizes and respects the importance of maintaining the privacy of our customers and has established a privacy policy as a result.
Why do we gather information?
We only gather information necessary to better your visit to MortgageMint.com.
Specifically we gather information to:
Better understand your needs
Better serve your requests
Keep accurate records of your preferences and requests
The Mortgage Mint®,LLC. does not believe in selling mailing lists or any other mass marketing techniques. Your private information will remain private at all times. The Mortgage Mint®,LLC. is a Colorado Mortgage Lender, Colorado Mortgage Company.
Security Information:
The Mortgage Mint®,LLC understands that financial information is a sensitive subject with our past, current and future clients. With your safety in mind, we have done everything possible to protect your private information.
This site encrypts your information prior to transmission over the Internet using secure socket layer (SSL) encryption technology.

Social Security Number and Income

When you are NO LONGER our customer, we continue to share your information as described in this notice.

What we do….How does the MORTGAGEMINT, LLC protect my information?
To protect your personal information from unauthorized access and use, we use security measures that comply with federal law.  These measures include computer safeguards and secured files and buildings.

Why can’t I limit all sharing?
Federal law give you the right to limit only:

DEFINITIONS?
Affiliates: Companies related by common ownership or control.  They can be financial and nonfinancial companies.
Non-affiliates: Companies not related by common ownership or control.  They can be financial and nonfinancial companies.
Joint Marketing:  A formal agreement between nonaffiliated financial companies that together market financial products or services to you.

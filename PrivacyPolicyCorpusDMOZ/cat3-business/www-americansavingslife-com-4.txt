Affidavit of Lost Policy
If you are unable to locate your original life insurance policy, you may use an Affidavit of Lost Policy in its place. This affidavit may be used in lieu of an original policy for the following cases:
Making a death claim on a policy
Surrendering a policy for its cash value
PLEASE NOTE: An Affidavit of Lost Policy must be signed by the owner and his/her signature must be witnessed by a valid Notary Public. For your protection, all Affidavits of Lost Policy will be verified with the policy owner's signature we have on file.
Instructions on Completing an Affidavit of Lost Policy
Open the Affidavit of Lost Policy form below in Adobe Acrobat Reader
Fill out the fields in the form and then print it out
Have a Notary Public witness your signature
Mail the form to the Company home office:

Privacy Policy
Here at Future Buildings we value your privacy. We will NEVER release, sell, loan or disclose your personal details in any way.
The information we collect is the bare minimum we need to process your quote and ensure you receive a 100% quality service.
Resale or disclosure of information to third parties
Future Buildings does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected on our site, including contact forms, download requests, or email lists.
Browser information collected on the web site
Future Buildings may analyze web site logs to improve the value of the materials available on the web site but no personally identifiable information is collected from these methods.
Privacy of our email lists
As with all personal information collected, we do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. Our entire email list is “double opt-in” meaning that subscribers must first approve their subscription on our free brochure or quote request forms, then respond to a confirmation email sent to the email account subscribed.
Unsubscribe Policy
We strive to only send e-mails to those who want to receive them. If you would not like to receive future e-mails from us, reply to any email with UNSUBSCRIBE as the subject or click the unsubscribe me button which is highly visible in every email sent.

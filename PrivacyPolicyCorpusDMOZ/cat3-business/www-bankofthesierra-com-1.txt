PRIVACY POLICY
Visitors to Bank of the Sierra’s website remain anonymous. We do not collect unique identifying information about you individually, unless you voluntarily and knowingly provide us that information.  Our website does collect certain information using “cookies”.  Cookies are small files that your browser places on your computer.  We may use session cookies, persistent cookies, and other tracking technologies such as pixels and tags to better understand how you interact with our site, to monitor usage by our users and web traffic routing on our services, and to improve and personalize our site.  Many internet browsers automatically accept cookies.  You may be able to instruct your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting cookies from the sites you visit. Google provides some additional privacy options relating to Google analytics, described at www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/.   We do not respond to “do not track” browser signals at this time.  You may elect to provide us with more specific information, such as when you give us feedback or send us an email.  If you provide us this information, it is used internally and in furtherance of the purpose for which it was provided.  We do not sell information we collect or that is provided to us regarding visitors to Bank of the Sierra’s website to any third parties, and although we do use it for marketing purposes, we would never Spam someone who previously visited our website.
If you believe any of the information is in error, or believe any information collected about you has been used for an improper purpose, please contact Customer Service immediately at 1-888-454-BANK.
Links:
Bank of the Sierra has a number of links to other sites.  These are offered as a courtesy and a convenience.  Bank of the Sierra assumes no responsibility for the content, safety, security or privacy of any such site or any transactions with respect thereto.  If you have questions or concerns about any other site’s policy regarding these matters, you should contact that site or its sponsor.
Amendments and Changes:
Bank of the Sierra reserves the right to amend or change this policy at any time and without notice.  For the most current version of this policy, contact our customer service department at 1-888-454-BANK or visit us online at www.bankofthesierra.com

Requests
You can make requests related to your California privacy rights at the attached links:
Access My Personal Information

Do Not Sell My Personal Information
You may also make those requests by calling 1-888-454-2265.
Please be aware that we do not accept or process requests through other means (e.g., via fax, social media, email addresses, etc.).
When you make a request, we will ask you to provide verifying information, such as the phone number on file with the bank and the date and amount of last deposit. Please also be aware that making any such request does not ensure complete or comprehensive removal or deletion of Personal Information or content you may have posted, and there may be circumstances in which the law does not require or allow us to fulfill your request.

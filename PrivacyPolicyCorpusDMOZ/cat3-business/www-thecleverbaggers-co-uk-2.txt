Privacy Policy
This privacy policy sets out how The Clever Baggers uses and protects any information that you give The Clever Baggers when you use this website.
The Clever Baggers is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.
Why we collect and process personal data
Personal data is collected by The Clever Baggers under the lawful basis of Contract. That is, so that we can provide the goods you order from us, or so that we can answer your enquiry which may lead to an order or other form of contract between The Clever Baggers and yourself.
There are three ways we collect personal data through our website:
Enquiry forms such as 'Get a Quote for Printing', 'Get in Touch', 'Employment Application'. Data collected using these forms is processed by us in order to respond to your enquiry During the process of requesting a quotation or placing an order. We require certain personal data, such as name and contact details, to be able to fulfil your order, including delivering your goods and keeping you informed of progress. Some data will be shared with third parties in order to achieve this. Data collected automatically. Our system will record your IP address when you visit our website or complete any online forms on our website. This is an anti-fraud measure used by the majority of businesses operating online.
What we do with your personal Data
Personal data is held in our order management system databases. Some data is shared with third parties in order for us to complete and deliver your order.
Third parties with whom we share your personal data
Please find listed below the third parties with whom we share personal data for the reasons given above. Each business name links to their privacy policy, which will open in a new tab when clicked.
Couriers:FedEx and DPD Payment processors: SagePay and PayPal Mailing list service provider: MailChimp  please note this is only if you have opted to receive our newsletter. Further information in Marketing section.
Website visits and coordination of cookies
In order to better understand the needs of our customers and visitors to our website, we use web analytics services to gather anonymised information via standard tracking technologies.
These services are provided to us by Google, PayPal and Bing (links are to their privacy policies and will open in a new tab).
We also use cookies to enable functionality of the website such as keeping track of shopping cart contents.
For a full list of cookies required for correct functionality of The Clever Baggers website, please see List of Cookies below.
Marketing
Personal data collected when you sign up to our email newsletter is collected and processed under the lawful basis of Consent. As stated above, our mailing service provider is MailChimp and their privacy policy can be viewed here.
You may withdraw consent for this at any time by unsubscribing from the newsletter.
Right to be forgotten
The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) gives you a right to be forgotten. To request this, please contact us via our Contact us form.
Data Retention
In compliance with the standard for ISO 9001:2015, we retain records for a minimum of 2 years from date of order completion.
In compliance with UK law relating to financial record keeping, we retain information for a minimum of 7 years.

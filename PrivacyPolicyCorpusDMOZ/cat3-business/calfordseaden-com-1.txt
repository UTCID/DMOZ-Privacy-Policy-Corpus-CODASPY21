Privacy Policycalfordseaden is committed to ensuring the protection of your privacy. Should you choose to submit your contact details to us, we will only use them in accordance with this Privacy Policy.Collection and use of informationThe information we collect includes your name and email address. We use this information to provide you with email newsletters notifying you of updates about calfordseaden. In continuing to use this website, you agree to have these cookies installed in your browser.Google Analytics stores information about the pages you visit on our site, the duration of time spent on each page, how you got to the site and what you have clicked on whilst visiting the site.Keeping your information secureWe are committed to ensuring that your information is secure which is why we have exemplary safety procedures in place, both physically and electronically, to prevent unauthorised access to the information we collect.We will retain your information for as long as you would like to receive information from us.

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site’s or service provider’s systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. For instance, we use cookies to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart. They are also used to help us understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity, which enables us to provide you with improved services. We also use cookies to help us compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future. Sound Training College uses Google Analytics (a tool which uses cookies) to collect traffic data to help us analyse how visitors interact with the site. Information such as what page you entered the website, what pages you visited, and what page you exited are all analysed. They are used to collect anonymous data on user behaviour and not used to personally identify a visitor. It is the policy of Sound Training College not to disclose such technical information in respect of individual website visitors to any third party apart from STC’s internet service provider, unless we are required to disclose that information by law. For further information on the privacy on Google Analytics data, including details of how to opt-out, please visit: http://www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html We suggest consulting the Help section of your browser or taking a look at the About Cookies website which offers guidance for all modern browsers

Privacy Policy
Gimle Ltd respects your privacy. We will not sell or give away any client information for any reason whatsoever. We use your information as a means to contact you and you alone. When you choose to provide us with personal information, you can be sure that it will only be used to support your customer relationship with us.
Gimle Ltd uses information it collects about customers specifically for the registration of product ownership, and provision of warranty service and technical support. This information is also helpful to us so that we can understand customer needs and provide better services. From time to time we might contact you for market research, or to provide you with market or product information that we think will be of interest to you. At each contact we will give you the opportunity to choose whether you wish to receive further direct communication from us.
Gimle Ltd will not sell, rent or lease your personal information to any 3rd party.
Gimle Ltd places all appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures in place to safeguard the security of your information.

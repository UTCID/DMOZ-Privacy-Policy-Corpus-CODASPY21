PRIVACY/DATA USE STATEMENT And Updated Cookie Policy
Mechatronics, Inc.
is committed to protecting any personal information that you may provide to
us. In particular, we believe it is important for you to know how we treat information about you
that we may receive from this Web site.
Information Collected
In general, you can visit this Web site without telling us who you are or revealing any
information about yourself. Our web servers collect the domain names, not the e-mail addresses,
of visitors. In addition, there are portions of this Web site where we may need to collect personal
information from you for a specific purpose, such as to provide you with certain information you
request. The information collected from you may include your name, address, telephone, fax number,
or e-mail address.
Use of Collected Information
Domain name information that we collect is not used to personally identify you and instead is
aggregated to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed, etc. We
use this information to measure the use of our site and to improve the content of our site.
When other information is collected from you, such as your name and e-mail address, we generally
let you know at the time of collection how we will use the personal information. Usually, we use the
personal information you provide only to respond to your inquiry, to process your request, or to
alert you to an update of our service of your inquiry. We do not share, sell, or lease personal
information about you to any third-parties for their marketing use.
A technology called cookies may be used to provide you with tailored information. A cookie is
a tiny element of data that a Web site can send to your browser, which may then be stored on your
hard drive so we can recognize you when you return. You may set your browser to notify you when you
receive a cookie.
Links to Other Sites
Mechatronics Group Web site may contain links to other sites such as industry partners and sales
representatives. While we try to link only to sites that share our high standards and respect for
privacy, we are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices employed by other
sites.
Security of Collected Information
We maintain strict physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to protect your personal
information from unauthorized or inappropriate access. We restrict access to information about you
to employees who need to know the information to respond to your inquiry or request.
Changes to this Policy
Please check this privacy policy periodically to inform yourself of any changes.

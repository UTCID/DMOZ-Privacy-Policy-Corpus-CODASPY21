Privacy

We do not collect any personally identifiable information on the web site except in cases where the visitor offers it voluntarily for the purpose of viewing web site content or receiving additional information. We use this information solely to respond to and process these requests.

Cookies
A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes. We use a cookie to store your web site registration information so you don’t have to enter it more than once.
You can remove cookies by following directions provided in your Internet browser’s Help file.
If you reject cookies, you will need to register each time you access the Resource Center.

Privacy Policy
Your Privacy Is Important to Us
Industrial Louvers, Inc. recognizes that privacy is important. Industrial Louvers may request personal information from you via Internet forms, such as your name, e-mail address, company name, company address, telephone number, etc., to which your response is completely voluntary. We will use this information for business purposes only, such as providing you with literature and information you request through trade publications, seminars and direct contact with INDUSTRIAL LOUVERS. We may also use this information to alert you to new products and services.
Industrial Louvers may collect domain information as part of our website use analysis. This data enables us to enhance our web-based offerings by tracking how often visitors view site content and which site content is viewed most often. This information is collected automatically and requires no action on your part.
Industrial Louvers, Inc. does not share any information collected via the Internet with third parties.
Industrial Louvers, Inc. provides links to other websites not maintained by Industrial Louvers, Inc. or its partners. Industrial Louvers, Inc. does not endorse these sites and is not responsible for site content. When you visit these websites, Industrial Louvers, Inc. encourages you to read individual site privacy policies, as their standards may differ.
Industrial Louvers reserves the right to change, modify or update this policy at any time without notice.
If you have any further questions about Industrial Louvers, Inc.’s privacy policies, please direct your e-mail to: ilinfo@IndustrialLouvers.com.

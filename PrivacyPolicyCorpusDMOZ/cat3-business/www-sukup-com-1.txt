They are then sent back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to any other visited website that recognizes that cookie.The most common use is to store a user's session after logging into a website. Some cookies are used by some websites to store items places in a shopping cart. Other cookies allow you to navigate between pages efficiently and generally improving your user experience. Many cookies collect information about how visitors use a website, for instance which pages visitors go to most often.For further information on cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set on your device and how to manage and delete them, visit http://allaboutcookies.orgSukup websites may use cookies to distinguish you from other users.

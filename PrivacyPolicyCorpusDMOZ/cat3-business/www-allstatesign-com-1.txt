Privacy and Security Policy
Privacy
Your privacy is very important to Allstate Sign. Our privacy policy is simple, we only collect the information needed to complete your order and we don’t share it with any 3rd parties.
Security
All transactions conducted on our website are encrypted over a secure SSL connection. We do not store credit card information on our servers. Only basic information like email and address are stored for your convenience.
If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us.

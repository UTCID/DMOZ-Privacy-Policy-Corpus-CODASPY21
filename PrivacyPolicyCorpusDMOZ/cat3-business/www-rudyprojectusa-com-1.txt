Your privacy is very important to us. This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on www.rudyprojectna.com including but not limited to subscriptions to our newsletters as well as all data you enter while placing an order with us. Details on 3rd party data collection while visiting our website is listed at the bottom of this policy.
We use your email address to respond to and answer questions we both may have regarding your order. We also use your e-mail address for future marketing and important product and promotion announcements.
We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website for a better shopping experience in the future.
Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above. We will never rent or share your information with a 3rd party for any reason.

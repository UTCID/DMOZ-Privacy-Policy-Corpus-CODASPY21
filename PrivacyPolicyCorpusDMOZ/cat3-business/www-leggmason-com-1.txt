Personal data is any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (someone who can be identified, directly or indirectly, or by reference to an identifier such as name, identification number (for example, passport, driver’s license, social security number, etc.), location or online identifier (such as GPS data, IP or MAC address, username and password), or other signifier related to that natural person.

Service Information: When you use a password or similar identifier to access the site, we will recognize your identifier and may collect various categories of information about you such as information you give us, data related to your use of the site and its services, and information about your communications with us.
Technical Information: In addition to the online information that you voluntarily provide to us, we may use cookies and retain cookie information separately or with the information you provide to us. A cookie is a small piece of data assigned to the browser on your computer when you visit a website. Your web browser may alert you or permit you to refuse, disable, or delete cookies. If you refuse cookies, you may not be able to use certain features or services on the site.

Information Security
We have in place necessary and appropriate administrative, technological, physical, and procedural safeguards, including, but not limited to, state-of-the art technologies and layered security controls, corporate governance, and cybersecurity tools, as well as business continuity and disaster recovery plans and procedures, designed to protect this Website and your personal data.
Our information security program is based on legal and regulatory obligations and guidance as well as industry best practices and includes, but is not limited to: physical security, hardened and patched infrastructure, intrusion detection systems, authentication, access and data integrity controls, password protection, virus and other malware/ransomware protection, logging and monitoring, multi-factor authentication and encryption. Legg Mason’s internal information security policies include, but are not limited to: change management, vulnerability management, incident response, security training and awareness, and software development lifecycle.  We restrict access using a “least privilege” model, meaning that only those authorized employees on a “need-to-know” are given access to your information, including your personal data, and for Legg Mason business purposes only.
Except as otherwise permitted by applicable law, Third Party Providers and any other service providers or our group company(ies) acting on Legg Mason’s behalf are obligated to (i) keep your information and data, including your personal data, that we provide to them confidential and to follow appropriate standards of security and confidentiality in doing so, and (ii) use the information Legg Mason shares only for the services for which Legg Mason hired them and not to use or share such information for any other purpose.
These safeguards are designed to prevent or reduce the impact of cybersecurity events and are reviewed and adjusted on a regular basis and in connection with security threats and trends; however, there are inherent limitations in such plans and systems and no system should be considered infallible.
Although we strive to protect your information, including your personal data, we do not warrant or guarantee the security of any information or data you provide or transmit to us, and you do so at your own risk. In the event of a breach of the confidentiality or security of your information or personal data, Legg Mason will attempt to notify you as necessary and in compliance with applicable law so that you can take appropriate protective steps. Clients having consented to using electronic means for communications will be notified using the most current email address you have provided to us with respect to your user account.
The transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its security during its transmission to our website and any transmission is at your own risk. Once we have received your information, we will use our strict procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorized access.

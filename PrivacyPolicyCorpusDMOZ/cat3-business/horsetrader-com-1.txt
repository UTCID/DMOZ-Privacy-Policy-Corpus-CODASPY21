Privacy Policy
Horsetrader.com is firmly dedicated to protecting your private information.
General Statement of Principles
As described in detail below, any information we gather through this web site is strictly for internal use and is
not shared with any other entity, public or private, for any reason - period. We will not sell or give away any email
lists or other personal data that we may retain, and we do not purchase such information from other sources.
Statistical Data
Our servers (as most) track IP addresses and referring pages to help with site maintenance and improvements.
This data is viewed only as anonymous statistics to show the busiest times of the day or week, pages with errors
and how well our systems are running. This information is not used for any other purpose.
Personal Information Collected - Order Forms
We store the information from your order form to allow us to track customer service issues or refer to a
previous order to help provide some customer service. You may elect to have your information completely removed
from this system by emailing us at help@horsetrader.com with your request.
Information Correction or Removal
If you wish to correct, update or remove any information about you that may be in our records, please send an e-mail
to help@horsetrader.com with the details of your request. If you wish to
contact us further, please call us at 760-546-1184.

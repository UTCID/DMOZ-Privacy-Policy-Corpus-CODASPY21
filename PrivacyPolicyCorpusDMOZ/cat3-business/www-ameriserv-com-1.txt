Privacy Policy
Our mission at AmeriServ is to meet the financial goals of our customers. As financial service professionals entrusted with sensitive financial information, we respect the privacy of our customers and are committed to handling customer information responsibly. When you entrust us with your financial information, you can be certain it will be used only within our strict guidelines.
AMERISERV FINANCIAL PRIVACY POLICY
We have prepared this information and an easy to read fact sheet to explain what types of information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we may share it. It covers all affiliates of AmeriServ Financial, Inc. including AmeriServ Financial, AmeriServ Trust and Financial Services Company (trust company), West Chester Capital Advisors, and all other subsidiaries of AmeriServ Financial, Inc. (hereafter “AmeriServ”).
We hope that you will read through all the details of AmeriServ’s official position on privacy. At the very least, however, we want you to know that AmeriServ uses information we know about you responsibly to:
Provide you with the services that you have requested
Offer you other bank products and services
Alert you to financial services products made available by our carefully selected marketing partners
At no time do we provide account or personal information to non-AmeriServ companies for the purpose of independent telemarketing or direct mail marketing of any non-financial products or services of those companies. In addition, we do not disclose any nonpublic personal information about trust company clients, except as permitted by law.
AmeriServ respects your privacy.
As technology continues to advance, you can be sure AmeriServ will provide the most up-to-date safeguards possible to keep your personal financial information confidential and secure. Your trust is important to us and we are committed to protecting your privacy, whether you do business with us on the Internet, in person or via telephone or mail. As always, we thank you for choosing AmeriServ.

Privacy Policy of insider-monitor.com
We (insider-monitor.com) do not collect any personally identifiable information about our users (you).
We do obtain some types of non-personally identifiable information
such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, your domain, your browser type,
or the type of computer operating system (Macintosh or Windows for example).
This helps us to determine what our pages will look like on your browser.
The information is collected in general categories and is not specific to individual users.
We note that information collected by our commerce partners will be subject
to their privacy policies, not this Privacy Policy.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Web site. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

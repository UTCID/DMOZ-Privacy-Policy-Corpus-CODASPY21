Privacy Policy
At Tynor we respect your privacy. Whether you request a catalog, place an on-line order, or e-mail us, your personal information is handled with your privacy and security in mind.
Information Use
The information collected by Tynor is used to aggregate statistical information, facilitate system administration, improve the content of the site, to fill orders, and also to respond to user requests for information. Tynor uses the information that is collected from the site to better serve its customers. Tynor may also use the information collected to notify site visitors of changes to the site, to notify site visitors of new products made available via the site, and for any other legitimate and lawful business purposes.
Links
This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware that Tynor is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our sites and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by Tynor Orthotics web site.

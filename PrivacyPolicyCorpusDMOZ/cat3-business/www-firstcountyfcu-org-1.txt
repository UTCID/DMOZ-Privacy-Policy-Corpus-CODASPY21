The following discloses our information and dissemination practices relating to www.firstcountyfcu.org.
Analytics:  We may use your IP address, browser information and visit trails to enhance this website for our members and visitors, administer this website, and help diagnose any problems with our servers.
External sites:  This website, www.firstcountyfcu.org, contains links to other sites.  First County FCU is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites.
Confidential information:  This website's forms (including feedback form and credit/loan applications) require users to provide contact information such as name, address, phone number and email address.  Applications may request unique identifiers (i.e. Social Security Number), financial information (i.e. account numbers, credit card numbers), and demographic information (i.e. zip code, age, income level).  Unique identifiers are used to verify the user's identity and for use as account numbers in our records system.  Financial information that is collected is used to check the user's qualifications for obtaining a loan or credit card from First County FCU.  Demographic and profile data is used to identify current members and new members.  All data collected on this website is kept confidential and never sold, distributed or shared with any outside advertisers.
Security:  This website has security measures in place to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of information under our control.  We use 128-bit key digital certificates through GeoTrust Global to ensure secure transfer of information data to and from our web servers.

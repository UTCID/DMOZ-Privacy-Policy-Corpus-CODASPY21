Privacy Policy
At Laboratory Systems Group, Inc. (LSG), we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process orders and to provide a better shopping experience. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy.
Information we collect and what we do with it.When you place an order, we need to know your name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and ship your order and to notify you of any changes to your order status.If you sign up for our notification of product updates or web specials we do require an e-mail address to send the information that you have requested.If you enter a contest, we might ask for your name, address, or e-mail address so we can accurately track the contest and notify the winner(s).We may also use the information we collect to occasionally notify you about any changes to the website, new LSG services, and web specials or other offers we think you might find of interest.
How does LSG protect customer and sales information?When you place orders, we use a secure server. The secure server software (SSL) encrypts all information sent to us. Any customer data we collect is kept on-site and is not available outside LSG. Any sensitive data collected at LSG is shredded or deleted in a timely manner.What is your policy if I link to a third party web site?When you click on links that take you to third-party web sites, you will be subject to the third parties' privacy policies. While we support the protection of privacy on the Internet, LSG cannot be responsible for the actions of any third parties. We encourage you to read the posted privacy statement whenever interacting with any website.Do you use cookies?Yes we do. Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored by your browser on your computer's hard drive. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your browser to prevent that. Even without a cookie, you can still use most of the features in our store, including placing items in your shopping cart and purchasing them.Will LSG disclose the information it collects to outside parties?LSG doesn't release any of your information to others. LSG may release customer information when we believe that such release is reasonably necessary to comply with the law, enforce or apply the terms of any of our user contracts or protect the rights, property or safety of LSG, any other users of LSG, or others.In conclusionWe are committed to protecting your privacy rights. We only use information collected on the site to make your experience here at labsysgrp.com possible.Your consentBy using our website, you consent to the collection and use of this information by LSG. If we do change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of the information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
LSG welcomes your questions and comments.

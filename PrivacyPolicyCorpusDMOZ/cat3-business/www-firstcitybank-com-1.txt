FDIC Information for First City Bank of Florida, Fort Walton Beach, FL
On Friday, October 16, 2020, First City Bank of Florida, Fort Walton Beach, FL was closed by the Florida Office of Financial Regulation. Subsequently, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) was named Receiver. No advance notice is given to the public when a financial institution is closed.
All deposit accounts have been transferred to United Fidelity Bank, fsb, Evansville, IN. For more information on United Fidelity Bank, fsb, visit us at www.unitedfidelity.com.
The FDIC has assembled useful information regarding your relationship with First City Bank of Florida. Besides a checking account, you may have Certificates of Deposit, a business checking account, a Social Security direct deposit, and other relationships with the institution.

Privacy Policy
We
respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at
this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not
be sold, reused, rented, loaned, or otherwise disclosed.
Any
information you give to us will be held with the utmost care,
and will not be used in ways that you have not consented to.
A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal
information is described below. If you have any questions,
please don't hesitate to let
us know.
Resale
or disclosure of information to third parties:
The MLM Woman web site does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or
lease any personal information collected at our site, including
customer contact information or email lists.
Browser
information collected on the website:
MLM
Woman analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the
value of the materials available on website. Our website logs
are not personally identifiable, and we make no attempt to
link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Use
of cookies:
We
do not use cookies on this site.
Privacy
of our email lists:
We give visitors the opportunity to opt-in to our MLM Woman
Newsletter Update e-mail list. We
do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on
our list to anyone. In addition, we configure our list server
software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list
subscribers to anyone but authorized MLM Woman staff, including
other list subscribers.
Other:
If
you have questions regarding our privacy policy, feel free
to contact us.

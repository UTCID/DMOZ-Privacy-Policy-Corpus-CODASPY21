California Consumer Privacy Act Rights
If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Please refer to our product privacy notices for your rights relating to specific products, or learn about your California Consumer Privacy Act rights.

Your Privacy Preferences
You may contact Nationwide to set your privacy preferences in one of the following ways, depending on your relationship with us.
If you have an insurance or financial product from Nationwide, you may:
Opt out of the sharing of your personal information with affiliates or third parties by calling 1-866-280-1809
Access or correct your personal information by sending a notarized form with your name, address and policy contract or account number to Nationwide, 1000 Yard Street GH-2D-OCA1, Columbus, OH 43212
If you are a consumer residing in California, you may have additional rights:
You may request access to your personal information by using this access request form or calling us at 1-844-541-4300
You may request deletion of your personal information by using this deletion request form or calling us at 1-844-541-4300
Nationwide does not sell your personal information. Therefore, there is no option to opt out of the sale of your personal information. Please refer to our FAQs for Nationwide and California Consumer Privacy Act
If you would like to instruct Nationwide not to market to you or call you, please call 1-844-541-4300.
If you receive emails from Nationwide and you would like to opt out of receiving those emails, please click the unsubscribe link within the email or manage your contact preferences in your online account.
Online Safety & Security
Protecting your information is important to us. That’s why we:
Use multiple types of verification to confirm your identity before giving you access to your online accounts
Ask you to register the devices you use to access your online accounts
Use an https protocol, which encrypts information passed between the server (where your information is stored) and your browser (where your information is accessed)
Learn what steps you can take
Nationwide retains and uses your information as required or permitted by law, even if you do not become a member.
The Nationwide family of companies may share member information with each other or nonaffiliated third parties for marketing purposes, unless prohibited by law. Please contact Nationwide at 1-866-280-1809 at any time to opt-out of such sharing practices.

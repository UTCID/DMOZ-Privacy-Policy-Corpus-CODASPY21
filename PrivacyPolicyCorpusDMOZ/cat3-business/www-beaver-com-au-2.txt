Privacy Policy
Thank you for visting the Beaver Brands website.
Privacy
Protecting your privacy is important to us. As a national wholesaler
we are committed to the protection of the personal data you provide us
with. We comply
with the National Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988
(Cth) when we collect and deal with personal information.
Policy
Beaver Brands will only collect personal information for the
operation of our functions and business activities. This policy relates
to all personal data
within the control of Beaver Brands.
Collection
Beaver Brands will only collect personal data for the operation of
our functions and business activities. The types of information which
will usually be
collected may include the following your name, billing and/or
delivery address, phone number and email address. Other information may
include, the name of
the domain from which you access the internet, the website from
which you linked to our site and the date and time you access our site.
This information will only be obtained through lawful and fair means
and not in an unwarranted intrusive way. The collection of your
personal data will
always be directly related to the specific purpose of which it has
been collected and only information that is specifically required will
be collected.
Use
The main purpose for which Beaver Brands will collect your personal
data will include: accessing your order information, allowing you the
ability to track
orders, the periodical Electronic Direct Marketing (EDM) to inform
you of sales, promotions & latest product releases of which you may
opt out of at
any time, query and complaint handling and general business correspondence.
Your use of our Website
You agree that you will be personally responsible for the use of the
Beaver Brands website and all of your communication and activity
within. Beaver Brands
holds the right to deny you access to this website if you have
violated the Terms & Conditions, on a temporary or permanent basis.
Your use of the Beaver Brands website and its contents including any
graphics, photographs, video, or text, grants no rights to you in
relation to any
copyright, designs, trademarks, and any other intellectual property
and material rights relating to it including our software and all HTML
and other code
contained within it. All such IP, including third party trademarks,
designs and related intellectual property rights mentioned or displayed
on this website
are protected by national intellectual property and other laws and
international treaty provisions. Without limiting the foregoing, copying
and use of the
above listed materials to any other server, location or support for
publication, reproduction or distribution is expressly prohibited. The
Beaver Brands
website is for your personal, non-commercial use only. You may not
use this website for any commercial purpose, including any advertising
or advertising
revenue generation activity on your own website.
Disclosure
The data that is collected by Beaver Brands is within the strictest
confidence. We will not disclose, sell, share or pass that information
on to any third
parties other than those who are contracted to Beaver Brands to keep
your information confidential including frachisees in the Beaver Brands
Group or to
related companies within our corporate group for marketing purposes.
We may disclose your information to a law enforcement organisation
without your
consent.
Email
The email address you provide us with, will only be used for the
purpose in which it was provided. It will not be added to a mailing list
or used for any
other purpose without your consent being given.
Access and Correction
In most cases, you will be able to gain access to personal data
specifically related to you that is held by Beaver Brands. We may
provide you with this
information verbally or in writing as appropriate. Where we do not
agree to provide you with this information, will give you the reasons
why.
We will take reasonable steps to amend or correct your personal data
upon request, to keep it accurate and up to date. You should notify
Beaver Brands of
any changes to your personal data as soon as possible.
Privacy Concerns or Complaints
If you have any queries or concerns about your personal data, or
would like to make a complaint, please contact Beaver Brands for
assistance.

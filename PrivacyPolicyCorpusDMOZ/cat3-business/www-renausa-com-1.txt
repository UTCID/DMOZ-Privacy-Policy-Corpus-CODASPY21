Privacy Policy
At RENA Systems, Inc. we are committed to protecting our customers’ privacy. The following is a description of how information is collected from you and how it is used.
Collection of Information:
When you shop on our site, we collect personal information from you such as your name, e-mail address, billing and shipping addresses, phone numbers, items selected to purchase, credit card or other payment information so we can verify the authenticity of the order.
Use of Information:
RENA Systems, Inc. does not sell, rent, exchange, or distribute your personal information to others. We use the collected information for the sole purpose of processing/verifying your order, contacting the purchaser to inform them of their order status, enhancing the operation of our site, protecting the cardholder from fraudulent purchases, statistical purposes, and to administer our system. We do not disclose your personal account information to anyone. By using our website you consent to the collection and use of this information by RENA Systems, Inc.

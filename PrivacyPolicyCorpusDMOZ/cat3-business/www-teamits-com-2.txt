Privacy Policy
ITS realizes personal privacy is an important issue. By visiting our web site, you are accepting our Terms of Use and the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
Information We Gather
We gather information you actively provide to us through our web site, such as contact information and other information you enter into online forms. For clients ordering products or services, or making payments online, we gather payment account information that is retained by our payment gateway (not ITS) for records purposes and to enable automatic recurring transactions if appropriate. Payment information is obtained via encrypted web connections (SSL).
We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.
Our web server logs information in aggregate (not personalized) about which pages were accessed, to help us improve our site. These logs include the IP address from which you established the connection, information about your web browser, and so forth. We may from time to time use cookies to personalize visitors' experiences or temporarily save settings for our web site during browsing.
Information We Share
ITS does not share information about its clients with third parties for any sort of marketing or information gathering purposes.

Privacy Policy
Getz Equipment Innovators treats all customer information as confidential; we never reveal or release private information about you or your order to third parties. We do not sell or distribute our customer mailing list, and will not use your email address for anything other than issues related specifically to your order, or, with your permission, to send you occasional information about Getz Equipment Innovators products.

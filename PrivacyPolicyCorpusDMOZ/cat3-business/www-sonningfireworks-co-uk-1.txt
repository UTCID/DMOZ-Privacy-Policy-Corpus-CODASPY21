Sonning Fireworks Ltd is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology

By using the Sonning Fireworks Ltd website, you consent to the data practices described

Sonning Fireworks Ltd collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail
address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Sonning Fireworks Ltd also
collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as
your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.
There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically
collected by Sonning Fireworks Ltd. This information can include: your IP address,
browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This
information is used by Sonning Fireworks Ltd for the operation of the service, to
maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use
of the Sonning Fireworks Ltd Web site.
Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information
or personally sensitive data through Sonning Fireworks Ltd public message boards,
this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Sonning Fireworks Ltd
does not read any of your private online communications.
Sonning Fireworks Ltd encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites
you choose to link to from Sonning Fireworks Ltd so that you can understand how those
Web sites collect, use and share your information. Sonning Fireworks Ltd is not responsible
for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the Sonning Fireworks Ltd
and Sonning Fireworks Ltd family of Web sites.
Use of your Personal Information
Sonning Fireworks Ltd collects and uses your personal information to operate the Sonning Fireworks Ltd
Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Sonning Fireworks Ltd also uses
your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services
available from Sonning Fireworks Ltd and its affiliates. Sonning Fireworks Ltd may also

or of potential new services that may be offered.
Sonning Fireworks Ltd does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.
Sonning Fireworks Ltd may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business

you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All
such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to

Sonning Fireworks Ltd does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such

Use of Cookies
The Sonning Fireworks Ltd Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online
experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page
server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.
Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the
domain that issued the cookie to you.
One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save
you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned
to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Sonning Fireworks Ltd pages, or
register with Sonning Fireworks Ltd site or services, a cookie helps Sonning Fireworks Ltd
to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process
of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses,
and so on. When you return to the same Sonning Fireworks Ltd Web site, the information
you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Sonning Fireworks Ltd
features that you customized.
You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically
accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies
if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience
the interactive features of the Sonning Fireworks Ltd services or Web sites you visit.
Security of your Personal Information
Sonning Fireworks Ltd secures your personal information from unauthorized access,
use or disclosure. Sonning Fireworks Ltd secures the personally identifiable information
you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from
unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit
card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use
of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

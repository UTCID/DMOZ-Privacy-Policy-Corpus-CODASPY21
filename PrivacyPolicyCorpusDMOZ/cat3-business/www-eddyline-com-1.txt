Privacy Policy
Updated as of July 13th, 2016.
Eddyline Kayaks, LLC (“We”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this privacy policy. This privacy policy explains the types of information that may be collected from you when you visit our website (www.eddyline.com) and our practices for collecting, using, and sharing that information.
The short version: The personal information (like name, email address, mailing address, and phone number) we collect is provided by you voluntarily and is used to better serve you. We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise use the information you provide to us in a manner inappropriate to the reason it was provided.
Collection, Use, and Sharing of Information
You may generally browse our website anonymously without providing any personal information. However, there are cases in which we may ask you for personal information. Non-personal information (like IP address) may be collected automatically while browsing the website. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone. We will not share your personal information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request.
Voluntary Contact: When you voluntarily contact us with a question or comment, we ask you to provide contact information (like name and email address). This information is used to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us.
Kayak Registration: When you register your kayak, we ask you to provide contact information (like name, phone number, and mailing address). This information is used to contact you if there is ever an issue or concern with your kayak.
Orders: When you purchase a product from us, you must provide contact information (like name and shipping address) and financial information (like credit card number and billing address). This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfill your order. If we have trouble processing an order, we will use this information to contact you.
Automatically Collected Information: See Google Analytics and Google Maps API under Cookies and Tracking.
Your Choices and Access to Information
You may update or correct your online account information by logging into your online store account and updating your information. You may also do any of the following at any time by contacting us using the information provided at the bottom of this policy:
See what information we have about you, if any.
Change/correct any information we have about you.
Have us delete any information we have about you.
Express any concern you have about our use of your information.
Security
We take reasonable measures, including administrative, physical, and electronic safeguards to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
Links
Our website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our site and read the privacy statements of any other site that collects your information.
Cookies and Tracking
Google Analytics: The only cookies in use on our site are for Google Analytics. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit this site, what pages they visit when they do so, and how long they stayed. We use the information we get from Google Analytics only to improve our site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without personally identifying individual visitors. Google’s ability to use and share information collected by Google Analytics about your visits to this site is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy. You can prevent Google Analytics from recognizing you on return visits to our site by disabling cookies on your browser.
Google Maps API: Our Dealer Locator uses the Google Maps API to function. Google’s ability to access, use, and share any information collected by the Google Maps API is restricted by the Google Maps API Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy.
California Privacy Rights
California Civil Code §1798.83 permits our visitors who are California residents to request certain information regarding their disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please contact us using the information at the bottom of this privacy policy.
Children
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected the personal information of a child under 13, we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.
Changes to this Policy
We may update and revise this privacy policy from time to time. Please check this privacy policy regularly for any updates or revisions.

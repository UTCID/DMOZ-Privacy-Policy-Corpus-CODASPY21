A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. wmpsmusic uses cookies to help wmpsmusic identify and track visitors, their usage of wmpsmusic website, and their website access preferences. wmpsmusic visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using wmpsmusic's websites, with the drawback that certain features of wmpsmusic's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

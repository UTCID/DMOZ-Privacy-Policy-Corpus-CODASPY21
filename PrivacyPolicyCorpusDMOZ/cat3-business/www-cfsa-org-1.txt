Privacy Policy
Your privacy is very important to us.  This is why:
we will not ask you for personal information unless we truly need it. For example: to register you for a training event or to sign you up for an fyi newsletter subscription.
we will not share your personal information for any reason, without your permission.
we do not store your personal information on our servers.
Website Visitors
Cookies: A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your computers, set your browsers to refuse cookies before using our website, with the drawback that certain features may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

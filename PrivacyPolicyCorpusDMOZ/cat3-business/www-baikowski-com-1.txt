QHSE Policy (extract)
Baikowski® considers the health and safety of stakeholders, the protection of the environment and the quality of its products as an integral part of its responsibility at the heart of its activities
QHSE management enables:
Support in issues regarding the strategy of Baikowski®.
Improvement of our results as regards as accident prevention, health preservation, environmental protection and product quality, while optimizing costs to ensure the company’s sustainability.
Every colleague and stakeholder, through their daily actions and professionalism, is a key player in QHSE policy of Baikowski®.
Environment
At Baikowski®, we perform an effective treatment of gaseous & liquid effluents. The self-monitoring on our gases & liquid discharges allows us to manage our local footprint while working on close collaboration with local authorities.

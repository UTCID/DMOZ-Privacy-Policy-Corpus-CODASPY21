Privacy Policy
Thogus will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you visit our site, unless you choose to provide such information to us.
Thank you for visiting the Thogus web site. Thogus is committed to protecting your privacy and will collect no personal information about you unless you choose to provide that information to us. Such information is securely stored within the Thogus private network. Some information may also be shared with Thogus business partners and other organizations and individuals with which Thogus has established a strategic alliance or other contractual relationship. The primary purpose behind the sharing of information is to better the services being provided to you by Thogus and by the organizations and individuals for which Thogus has established contractual relationships. Information will also be used to improve Thogus sales, marketing, and business development efforts.
how we handle information about your visit to our site
If you read or download information, we automatically collect and store:
the requested web page
the domain from which you access the Internet (e.g. wowway.com)
the date and time you access our site
status of the request (e.g. success or failure)
the type and version of your web browser (Firefox, Explorer, etc.)
the operating system (Windows, Mac, Unix, etc.) of the machine running the web browser

Thogus is not responsible for the availability of the systems being linked to and these links are provided to you as a service for you to better understand the different business relationships that Thogus has established.

Privacy Policy
ETS is committed to protecting your privacy on this Web site.
Our privacy policy follows the Coalition for Advertising Supported
Information and Entertainment (CASIE) Goals for Privacy
for Marketing on Interactive Media.
ETS will handle the information we learn about you from your visit to
our website in the following manner:
We will not obtain personally-identifying information about you when you
visit our site, unless you choose to provide such information to us.
We will never release, sell or give your information to any other party or
organization, without your explicit permission.
Moreover, the information we receive depends upon what you do when
visiting our site.
If you visit our site to read or download information, such as news
stories or articles:
We collect and store only the following information about you: the name
of the domain from which you access the Internet; the date and time you
access our site; and the Internet address of the Web site from which
you linked directly to our site. We use the information we collect to
measure the number of visitors to the different sections of our site, and
to help us make our site more useful to visitors.
If you identify yourself by sending an e-mail message:
You also may decide to send us personally-identifying information, for
example, in an electronic mail message containing a request. We may
use personally-identifying information (e-mail address) from visitors to
send periodic e-mail updates regarding ETS services and events.

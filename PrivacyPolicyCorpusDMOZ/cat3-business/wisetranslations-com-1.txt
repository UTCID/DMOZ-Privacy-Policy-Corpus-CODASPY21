Translation Policy
WiseTranslations.com is committed to protecting your personal, contact and other information as well as your privacy. Our privacy statement was designed to make it clear why we collect certain information from you, to whom that information is disclosed, and how we protect your privacy.
Information collected
By submitting any of our online forms, you agree to the collection, processing and use of the information by WiseTranslations.com To respond to any of your inquires, we require the following information:
your name
e-mail address
valid telephone number
country of residence
How your information is used
Your information may be used in any one of the following ways:
To provide you with an instant estimate of our services.
To monitor customer traffic and site usage to help us improve the design and general layout of the site.
To analyze your browsing preferences to help us select products or services that you may be interested in.
How your information is protected
Your information is protected in the following ways:
We will not ask for unnecessary information, only information needed to process your order.
We do not store any information about you which is no longer relevant.
We protect your information against unauthorized use. We enforce highly secure procedures in the storage and disclosure of information to prevent unauthorized access.

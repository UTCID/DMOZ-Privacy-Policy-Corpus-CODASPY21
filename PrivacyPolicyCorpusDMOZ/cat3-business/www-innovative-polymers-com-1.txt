Our Commitment To Privacy
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available at the bottom of every page.
The Information We Collect
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Innovative Polymers website (http://www.innovative-polymers.com). On some pages, you may use forms to contact us and request information about our products. The types of personal information collected at these pages are:
Name
Email Address
Credit/Debit Card Information
We do not collect or store credit card or debit card information on our website.
The Way We Use Information
We use the information you provide about yourself to respond to your inquiry. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to secure a quote on your behalf.We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. Such addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.
Our Commitment To Data Security
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
Our Commitment To Children's Privacy
Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

What are cookies?
For almost any modern website to work properly, it needs to collect certain basic information on its users. To do this, the server of a website as www.permasteelisagroup.com will create files known as cookies – which are small text files – on its users’ computer, tablet, mobile phone or other mobile device (referred to collectively as “device”). Cookies allow efficient Site operation and improve its performance; they provide information for statistical purposes to the owner of the Site, mainly to personalize your web browsing experience, remembering your preferences. We use cookies to measure and improve the performance of this site and to analyze the web traffic to this site.
The Site uses different types of first-party cookies (prepared and managed by Permasteelisa Group) and third-party cookies (prepared and managed by third parties in accordance with their own privacy policy and not under the control of Permasteelisa Group). This Site does not use profiling cookies.

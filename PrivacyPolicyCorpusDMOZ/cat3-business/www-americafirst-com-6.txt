Intuit QuickBooks FAQs
What is the customer ID & PIN?
Customer IDs are America First account numbers, while your PINs/passwords are the same as those you use to access online & mobile banking.
Is this an online program or do I download software?
QuickBooks Online is securely available securely by web browsers or our mobile app—no downloading required. For QuickBooks Desktop, download Apple or Windows software to a single computer.
Can I add my accountant, bookkeeper, or tax pro?
Yes, you can invite collaborators to work with you on QuickBooks. Once invitations are accepted, they can work in your account. You can also delete their access anytime.
Can I work from my mobile device on QuickBooks Online?
Yes, you can download the QuickBooks accounting app to either iPhone or Android devices.  This will allow you to do receipt capture, mobile banking and keep track of your finances at any time.

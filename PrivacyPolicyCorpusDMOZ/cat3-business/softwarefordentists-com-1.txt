Information you provide: We collect information affirmatively provided by you, such as survey information and/or site registrations.
Website usage information: We use your IP address to help diagnose problems and to administer our Web site. We do not link your IP address to any personally identifiable information. We use tracking information to determine which areas our site users visit based on traffic to those areas. Dentists Management Corporation (DMC) does not track what individual users read, but rather how often each page is visited. This helps us improve site usability and content for our users.
Dentists Management Corporation does not otherwise retain information about consumers who browse our Web page. Our Web server does not automatically recognize information regarding the domain or e-mail address used to access the Web page.
Collection and Use of Information: The information we collect is used to notify consumers about updates to our Web site and used by us to contact consumers for marketing purposes. In addition, if you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive periodic mailings from us with information on new products and services or upcoming events. If you do not want to receive such communications from us in the future, please let us know by sending us an e-mail at the address set forth below or calling us at the telephone number provided.
From time to time, we may use customer information for new purposes not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change at some time in the future, we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses.
Maintaining the privacy of your information is of paramount importance to us as it helps foster confidence, goodwill and stronger relationships with you, our customers. If, at any time, you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please feel free contact dmcinfo@dmcdental.com.
Correction of Information: Upon request, we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information.
Sharing of Information: Dentists Management Corporation shares information obtained from consumers in only the following situations:
You have consented to the proposed use or disclosure;
With an affiliate, subsidiary or agent of Dentists Management Corporation and only for purposes specified in our privacy policy, as may be modified from time to time; or
Such information is required or permitted to be disclosed by law.
Cookies: Many Web pages use cookies to collect information about an individual’s activities on a particular Web site. However, Dentists Management Corporation does not set any cookies. The information we obtain from you is limited to that information you voluntarily disclose.
Security Practices: Dentists Management Corporation seeks to protect online information in accordance with applicable laws and industry practices. We have security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information within our control; however, we cannot guarantee the confidentiality or security of electronic transmissions because they may be sent via unsecured computers or links, and such information may be intercepted by unauthorized parties during the transmission process.
We will promptly notify you if we learn of any interception or misuse of your information.

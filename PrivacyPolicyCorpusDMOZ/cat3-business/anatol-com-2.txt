To that end, Anatol has established strict privacy guidelines and practices to ensure that your private information remains safe, and we offer this Privacy Policy for your assurance. We WILL NOT SELL, TRADE, RENT, or GIVE your personal information to third parties. Registration on our website is voluntary and for the purpose of providing customers with requested product and/or company information. Because we value your trust, we provide this statement to inform our users of how we gather and utilize personal information.Collection and use of informationWhen you register on the Anatol website, we ask you for very limited contact information, which we store in a database on our server. We use this information to process your requests for additional information, and to communicate with you about the status of your inquiry. If you have asked to receive newsletters and/or special offers and promotional announcements, we use your email address to deliver them to you.Viewing and updating your information with usYou can always change the information you have provided to Anatol by logging in to “Your Account” and clicking on “View/Update Your Account Profile.” You can also use this page to change your account password.If you no longer wish to receive an Anatol Newsletter or other notification, please modify your preferences by logging in to “Your Account” and clicking on “Manage Your Email Subscriptions,” then deselecting the appropriate checkboxes.CookiesAs you browse the Anatol site, we place cookies on your computer’s hard drive. (Cookies are small pieces of data that are sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your hard drive.) Cookies allow us to track sessions and personalize your experience on our site. Such uses allow us to improve our site and better tailor it to meet our users’ needs. Anatol will not share your information with anyone who is not an employee or affiliate of Anatol Equipment Manufacturing Co.If your browser is NOT set to accept cookies, you will still be able to use the Anatol site and services. However, our server will not recognize you, and you may have to retype your contact information to request newsletters or equipment information.DisclosureAnatol will not sell, trade, rent, or give your personal information to third parties. We never provide anyone with customer information collected from our website. Registration on our website is voluntary and for the purpose of providing customers with requested product and/or company information. Internally, we may use this information to customize your experience on the Anatol site, to analyze site usage, and to improve our content and product offerings.From time to time, we may partner with companies whose products we believe will interest our users. In conjunction with these partners, we occasionally send joint promotional announcements to our registered customers. However, we do not provide your name or contact information to our marketing partners. We may use a vendor to carry out these mailings, but the partner company never sees your email address or any other information that could identify you or be used to contact you directly. No exceptions to this policy will be made without your permission.Customers should check back for updates to this policy. However, Anatol will not use your information in a manner different from that stated when you registered without first obtaining your consent.ContactIf you have questions concerning your registration information or this privacy policy, please contact us.

Throughout the course of 2020, SRS Acquiom has been collecting data and observations around the issues that deal makers are grappling with during these challenging times. This program will include a discussion of deal terms and trends that existed prior to the pandemic, a review of what has (and hasn’t) changed over the last six months and examples of the hot topics that deal parties are dealing with both in negotiations and after a deal closes.

Use of this Site is also subject to NTN’s Terms and Conditions of Use.
Use of Personal Information
Certain features of the Site require you to provide personal or business information. This information will provide you access to email alerts and access to new tools we may offer in the future. The information you provide us helps us administer these features. This information can be changed by contacting the Webmaster of the Site by email.

No data you provide to NTN will be released to outside organizations, with the exception that NTN reserves the right to disclose user information when in good faith NTN has reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interference with (intentionally or unintentionally) NTN’s rights or property, other users of the Site, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. NTN may use IP addresses to identify a user when necessary to enforce compliance with NTN’s Terms and Conditions or to protect our service, site, customers, or others.

NTN’s Privacy Policy does not extend to anything that is inherent in the operation of the Internet, and therefore beyond NTN’s control. This Policy is not to be applied in any manner contrary to applicable law or governmental regulation.

NTN cannot guarantee the security of information you transmit to or from the Site. You do so at your own risk.

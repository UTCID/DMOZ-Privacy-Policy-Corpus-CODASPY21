Privacy PolicyThis privacy policy sets out how Simpsons Motorhomes uses and protects any information that

We do not sell your information to third parties, but we do work closely with selected partners who help us to provide you with the information, products and services that you request from us. However, we may from time to time disclose your information to the following categories of companies or organisations to which we pass the responsibility to handle services on our behalf: warranty providers, vehicle manufacturers, DVLA.We take steps to ensure that any third party partners who handle your information comply with data protection legislation and protect your information just as we do. We hold your personal information for this period to establish, bring or defend legal claims. We hold your personal information for this period to give us an opportunity to form a relationship with you.The only exceptions to the periods mentioned above are where:the law requires us to hold your personal information for a longer period, or delete it sooner.

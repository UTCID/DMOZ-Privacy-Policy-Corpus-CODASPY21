Privacy policy
We know that you take the privacy of your information and how it’s used and shared very seriously. We will only use your personal information in accordance with the current data protection law in the UK and this privacy policy. This notice describes Our privacy policy and how We aim to repay the trust you have shown by sharing your personal data with us.This policy applies to Our websites, Our online store, Our use of emails and text messages for communication/marketing purposes, and any other methods We use for collecting information. It covers what We collect and why, what We do with the information, what We won’t do with the information, and what rights you have.This privacy policy only applies to personal information We hold about individuals. It does not apply to information We hold about companies and other organisations.We may update this privacy policy from time to time by publishing the amended version on Our website. Unless we have legal grounds to do otherwise, We will provide you with at least 30 days’ notice of the effective date of the revised privacy policy. We may post the notice on Our website and/or send you the notice by email.Who we areIn this policy, whenever you see the words (‘We’, ‘Us’ or ‘Our’), it refers to Town and Country Petfoods Limited, Registered Address – 3 Castlegate, Grantham, NG31 6SF Registered in England Company No: 1189456. For the purposes of UK Data Protection Law, We are a data controller in respect of the personal information that We collect and process about you as described in this privacy policy.We are a privately owned limited liability company founded in 1974.The range of products and services offered by Us include, but is not limited to, the sale and marketing of cat and dogfood products including via our online store, website and social media channels (Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.)Information We collect
We will only ever collect the information We need – including data that will be useful to help improve Our products and services.We may collect and process the following data about you:1. Personal information and details of your interactions with Us, such as name, postal address, phone number, email address, that you provide by filling in forms on Our website https:www.hilifepet.co.uk, using Our online store and when you contact Us via telephone. This includes information provided when you:• submit a question to Us or provide Us with feedback;• purchase products in Our online store;• enter a competition or promotion;• request a call back;• request information via telephone;• enquire about a specific product or service;• subscribe to receive Our e-communications or digital newsletter; and/or• respond to a campaign.2. If you have a web store account with us, we may additionally collect your, billing/delivery address, records of orders and receipts, details of your shopping preferences. For your security we will also retain an encrypted record of your login password.3. Details of your visits to Our websites and online store including but not limited to, IP addresses (the location of the computer on the internet), pages accessed and files downloaded. This helps Us to determine how many people use Our website, how many people visit on a regular basis, and how popular Our pages are. This is statistical data about Our users’ browsing actions and patterns, and does not identify any individual. It simply allows Us to monitor and improve Our service.You are provided with an opportunity to opt out from receiving further communications from Us on each occasion that you are contacted. Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of Our site. This helps Us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and allows Us to improve Our website. For detailed information on the cookies We use and the purpose for which We use them please see Our Cookie Policy.
How We use the information We collect
We may process your personal data for Our legitimate business interests. “Legitimate Interests” means the interests of Our company in conducting and managing Our business and providing you with the best services and products in the most secure way. These interests include:• To ensure that content from Our website and online store is presented in the most effective manner for you and for your computer;• To provide you with information about Our products, services or offers that you request from Us or that are of a similar nature to products or services that you have previously purchased from Us;• To provide existing customers with newsletters and details of services, offers and competitions through email communications;• To help Us identify you when you contact Us or visit Our website or online store;• To help Us improve the relevance of Our communications to Our customers; • For general administration purposes;• To help Us improve the quality of Our products and services, including invitations to participate in product testing;• To help Us to improve Our social media campaigns and inform Our business decisions;• To help Us detect and prevent fraud and money laundering;• To help Us recover debts;• To carry out analysis and customer profiling ; and/or• When you communicate with Us for customer service or other purposes (e.g. by emails, faxes, phone calls, online forms, tweets, etc.), We may retain such information and our responses to you to protect Our legitimate interests.When We process your personal data for Our legitimate business interests, We always ensure that We consider and balance any potential impact on you and your rights under data protection laws.If you have any concerns about the processing described above, you have the right to object to this processing. For more information on your rights please see “Your rights” section below.Choice / opt-in and opt-outWhenever the processing of your personal data requires your consent then you will be given the opportunity to opt-in or opt-out to having your contact details used as set out above, upon submission.If you no longer wish to receive correspondence from Us, you may opt-out of receiving communications at any time by one of the following methods:• by replying to our email with ‘unsubscribe’ in the subject line; or• by writing to Town and Country Petfoods Limited, 3, Castlegate, Grantham NG31 6SF.Sharing your information
Just like most other organisations, We work with third-party service providers which provide important functions to us that allow us to be easier, faster, and friendlier in the way we deliver Our services. We need to disclose user data to them from time to time, for any of the purposes set out above, so that the services can be performed.We have listed in this Privacy Policy the third-party service providers and business partners to whom we may disclose your data together with the purpose of disclosure:• Couriers – to enable us to deliver any products we may wish to send you as part of your enquiry or contact.• IT companies who support our website, online store and other business systems.• Marketing and Telesales agencies, for example to seek permission to use a photograph you have shared with us on Facebook, or to send you a competition prize.• Banks and card payment service providers – to enable us to process payments (all payment information is protected by SSL certificates that use 256-bit encryption technology).• Google/Facebook to show you products that may be of interest while you are browsing the internet. This is based on your marketing consent or acceptance of cookies on our websites, please see our Cookie Policy for further details.We may also disclose your personal information to third parties, if We are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data for legal or regulatory purposes, in relation to existing or future legal proceedings, for the prevention of fraud/loss or to protect the rights, property, safety of Our Group, Our customers or others.In order to personalise your use of our online services we currently use the following companies, who will process some of your personal data as part of their contracts with us:• Shopify (including Shopify Payments)• Paypal• Woobox• Accentuate• Rewind• Omnisend• Jotforms• Stocky• Google Analytics• Facebook• Flexify• PrivyData security
Information is stored by Us on computers located in the UK and EU. We may also store information in paper files.We have security protocols and policies in place to manage and record your data privacy and preferences correctly and your data is stored securely to protect against its loss, misuse and alteration.We take steps to ensure that any organisations that we share your data with will have security protocols and policies in place to manage and record your data privacy and preferences correctly and that your data is stored correctly.We may transfer your personal data to reputable third-party data processors as explained above – they may be situated inside or outside the European Economic Area (this includes all EU Member countries and Iceland, Liechtenstein and Norway). If we do this, we will ensure that your personal data receives the same level of protection it does within the EEA by using one or more of the following safeguards:• The receiving organisation is located in a country that has been recognised by the UK or European Commission as providing similar levels of protection for personal data.• The receiving organisation participates in PrivacyShield for transfers from the EEA to the U.S.• We and the receiving organisation have entered into written agreements (Standard Contractual Clauses as approved by the EU Commission) that require them to provide the same level of protection for personal data.Unfortunately, the transmission of data across the internet is not completely secure and whilst We do Our best to try to protect the security of your information We cannot ensure or guarantee that loss, misuse or alteration of data will not occur whilst data is being transferred.We will keep your information only for as long as We need it to provide you with the goods, services or information you have required, to administer your relationship with Us, to comply with the law, or to ensure We do not communicate with people that have asked Us not to. When We no longer need information, We will always dispose of it securely, using specialist companies if necessary to do this work for Us.Job applicants, current and former employeesWhen individuals apply to work for Us, We will only use the information they supply to Us to process their application and to monitor recruitment statistics. Where We want to disclose information to a third party, for example where We want to take up a reference or obtain a ‘disclosure’ from the Criminal Records Bureau, We will not do so without informing them beforehand unless the disclosure is required by law.Personal information about unsuccessful candidates will be destroyed or deleted.Once a person has taken up employment with Us, We will compile a file relating to their employment. The information contained in this will be kept secure and will only be used for purposes directly relevant to that person’s employment. Once their employment with Us has ended, We will retain the file in accordance with the requirements of Our retention policy and then delete it.Your rights
Under the data protection legislation, you have the right to request copies of your data, request rectification of your data, request erasure of your data, object to Us processing your data, the right to prevent your data being used for direct marketing, request Us to restrict the processing of your data and where our systems allow, the right to access a copy of the information We hold about you (a subject access request).If you wish to exercise any of these rights please contact Us in writing at Town and Country Petfoods Limited, 3 Castlegate, Grantham NG31 6SF.For more information about your rights under the Data Protection Act contact the Information Commissioner’s Office here.

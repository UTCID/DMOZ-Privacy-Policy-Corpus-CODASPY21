Reopening the Workplace: Recommendations for Guidance
Go Back
As governments worldwide continue to respond to the COVID-19 pandemic and focus on the economic recovery, ITI outlined recommendations for guidance on reopening workplaces across the globe. ITI identifies areas where the tech sector seeks clear guidance to balance safely reopening workplaces with protecting public health. Among the areas ITI includes: assessing readiness, health monitoring, transmission mitigation techniques, employee support. Officials should regularly review and modify the guidance as appropriate given the evolving nature of the pandemic.

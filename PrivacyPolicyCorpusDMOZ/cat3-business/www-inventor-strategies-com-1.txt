Privacy Policy
At inventor-strategies.com we are dedicated to protecting your data and privacy.
We recognize that your privacy is important. This document outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use inventor-strategies.com, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information. This will help you make an informed decision about sharing personal information with us.Inventor-strategies.com strives to maintain the highest standards of decency, fairness and integrity in all our operations. Likewise, we are dedicated to protecting our customers', consumers' and online visitors' privacy on our website.
About Our CookiesA cookie is a string of information that inventor-strategies.com stores on your computer, which your browser provides to inventor-strategies.com each time you return to the site. We use cookies to help us identify and track visitors, their use of our website, and their website access preferences.
Google Analytics CookiesInventor-strategies.com uses Google Analytics to analyze the use of this website. Google Analytics collects demographic and interest data, and generates statistical and other information about website use, by means of cookies. These cookies are kept on your computer. We share this information with Google, which will store and use this information on its servers. Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Inventor-strategies.com uses information from Google Analytics to track the number of visits to the website, browsers and operating systems. No personally identifiable information is obtained from these cookies. We use the information that Google Analytics generates relating to our website to create reports about the use of the site. These reports allow us to update the site and improve your usage of it.
If you do not wish your data to be collected via Google Analytics, you can install its opt-out browser extension or add-on at: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/
Google Advertising CookiesWe use third party non-personalized advertising on our website using GoogleAlthough non-personalized ads don’t use cookies or mobile ad identifiers for ad targeting, they do still use cookies or mobile ad identifiers for frequency, aggregated ad reporting, and to combat fraud and abuse.
Refusing CookiesYou can set your browser to accept or refuse all cookies automatically, or notify you when a cookie is being requested. Taking this action should not cause a problem with the continued use of our site. Consult the Help section of your browser for guidance on how to refuse all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is requested.If you choose to refuse inventor-strategies.com cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the site.

may collect and use your personal information for the following purposes:

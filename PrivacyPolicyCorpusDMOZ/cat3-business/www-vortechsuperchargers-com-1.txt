Privacy
Vortech respects your right to privacy.
The information utilized to process catalog or other requests includes but is not limited to your name, street mailing address, city, state, zip code, phone number, and e-mail address. This information is required to properly process and fulfill requests for information or to process orders.
Vortech Engineering respects your right to privacy. Therefore, we do not sell, trade, or rent this information to outside parties in any form. Vortech Engineering, Inc. may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, traffic patterns, and other related site information to reputable 3rd party vendors. These statistics will not include individual and personal identifying information.
Upon entering this web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information by Vortech Engineering, Inc. If we change our privacy policy, those modifications will be reflected here. You will always be made aware of what type of information we collect, how it is utilized and under what circumstances it may be disclosed.

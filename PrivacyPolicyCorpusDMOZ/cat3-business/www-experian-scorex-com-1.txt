Global Partnership CrossCore
We know that choice is important to you. It’s why we designed Experian CrossCore to be the industry’s first open fraud and identity platform. For the first time, you’re in the driver’s seat to choose the services you want, when you want them.
But, no one vendor can do it all. A critical part of the CrossCore strategy is the ability to access any solution – ours, yours and even 3rd party capabilities. Our partnership programme brings together the best solutions from trusted vendors to help strengthen fraud mitigation efforts without the burden of custom integrations for every new service.
We are rapidly adding partners to give you more choice than ever before. Interested in learning more about the CrossCore partner programme? We look forward to hearing from you.
CrossCore factsheet
Emailage
Emailage delivers an intelligent risk score that is derived from reputation far beyond a black list or simple validation check. Emailage uses sophisticated machine learning and proprietary algorithms that evolve with new fraud trends to provide real time alerts of risky transactions.
Learn more
Ekata
Ekata provides global identity verification solutions via enterprise-grade APIs for automated decisioning, and Pro Insight, a SaaS solution for manual review for cross-border businesses to grow revenue by maximizing their predictability of good transactions.
Learn more
BioCatch
BioCatch is a cybersecurity company that delivers behavioral biometrics analyzing human-device interactions to protect users and data. The company uses a trusted partnership approach that provides our customers access to the best minds in fraud prevention and biometric authentication.
Learn more
Daon
Throughout the world, Daon has developed identity assurance software that allows government and commercial enterprises to establish trust that someone is who they claim to be.
Learn more
NuData Security
NuData Security is a behavioral biometrics company that helps companies identify users based on their online interactions.

Mitek Systems
Mitek Systems provides global real-time identity document verification for account opening and multi-factor, risk-based authentication.

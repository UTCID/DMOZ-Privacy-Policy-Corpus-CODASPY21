In this case, we will notify you and keep you updated.Credit Card SecurityWhen an order is placed at our website or on the telephone, credit card numbers are encrypted and transmitted using 256 bit encryption to SagePay. Sage pay is audited annually under the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DSS) and is a fully approved payment services provider (PSP). We never see your credit card number and they are not held in clear text on any web site or database. It’s that simple.

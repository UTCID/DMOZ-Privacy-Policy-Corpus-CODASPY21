Privacy Policy
Use And Disclosure Of Information
J. F. Ahern Co.’s online privacy policy applies only to the J. F. Ahern Co. website and does not apply to activities that are "offline" or unrelated to the website.
Personal information collected on the J. F. Ahern Co. website will never be sold, shared or presented to any person or persons outside of J. F. Ahern Co., except in the limited circumstances stated in this paragraph. J. F. Ahern Co. may disclose your information to service providers who assist the Company in operating the website or conducting its business. J. F. Ahern Co. may disclose information about you if and when the Company believes it is necessary to comply with any law, rule, court order, or subpoena, or to enforce its legal rights or the rules of this website, or to protect its business, property and operations, or in connection with a sale, acquisition, merger or other business combination of its business with a third party.
J. F. Ahern Co. collects certain anonymous data regarding the usage of the website. This information does not personally identify users, by itself or in combination with other information, and is gathered to improve the performance of the website. The anonymous data collected can include information such as the type of browser you are using, your IP address and the length of the visit to the website.
You may also be asked to provide personally identifiable information on the J. F. Ahern Co. website, which may include your name, address, telephone number, and e-mail address. This information can be gathered when feedback or e-mails are sent to J. F. Ahern Co. or when you attempt to locate offices in your area, or request additional information or literature.
If you voluntarily submit your information through the “Contact Us” or "Careers" sections of the J. F. Ahern Co. website, J. F. Ahern Co. may use that collected information for the purpose it was provided to contact you by e-mail or other means.
Other Websites
J. F. Ahern Co.’s web site contains links to other sites. J. F. Ahern Co. is not responsible for the content of the websites or the privacy policies of the business or organizations operating those sites. If you provide any information to such third parties, different rules regarding the collection and use of your personal information may apply. We strongly suggest you review such third party's privacy policies before providing any data to them.
Security
J. F. Ahern Co. takes appropriate steps to ensure data privacy and security through various hardware and software methodologies. However, J. F. Ahern Co. cannot guarantee the security of any information that is disclosed online.
Questions Or Comments
If you have any questions or comments regarding this privacy policy, please contact jfainfo@jfahern.com, or use the “Contact Us” portion of this website.
Modifications Of The Privacy Policy
J. F. Ahern Co. reserves the rights to modify its privacy policy at any time and will notify you by posting an amendment to the privacy policy on the website. Such modifications will become effective on the day they are posted. J. F. Ahern Co. encourages you to frequently review the privacy policy for any modifications.

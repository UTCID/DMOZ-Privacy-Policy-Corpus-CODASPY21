Cookie PolicyIntroductionThe Electrosonic website (www.electrosonic.com )  uses cookies to store information on your computer. This Cookie policy sets out how Electrosonic uses the information that is collected using these cookies when you use our website.About CookiesCookies are small pieces of data that are downloaded from a web server to your computer or mobile device whenever you visit a website. Cookies allow a website to recognise your device and store some information about your preferences and actions each time that you visit the website.Use of CookiesAs you navigate the pages of the Electrosonic website, we use cookies to help improve our service to visitors of the website. We do this by gathering information about your visit. This information includes how you arrived at the website, the pages that are visited and how much time is spent on each page.We use this information to track behaviour, compile statistical reports of website activity and identify devices that are returning to the website.We do not use cookies to gather personally identifiable data or any information that would, on its own, allow us to identity you as an individual without your permission.Where we do collect personal information, we will make it clear why we are doing this and how the data will be used.Any cookies that may be used by this website are used solely on a per session basis or to maintain user preferences.Controlling the use of CookiesYou may control the use of cookies through the settings that are available in most browsers that are in common use.To find out how to manage cookies on browsers that are in common use, visit the browser developer’s website.For more general guidance and to find out more about how to control the use of cookies, including how to delete them visit https://www.aboutcookies.org/ Please be aware that the performance of the website may be adversely affected if you restrict the use of cookies.Third Party CookiesCookies for web analyticsWe use third party services to gather data regarding the usage of our site using cookies, log file data and code which is embedded on our website. We use this type of information to help improve the service we provide to all visitors.Google AnalyticsGoogle Analytics is a tool offered by Google that collects information anonymously and that uses its own set of cookies to track website traffic and usage.We use Google Analytics to help us analyse the information that we gather using cookies as described above.To find out more about google analytics and the cookies that are used, please visit the Google help and privacy policy pages:https://developers.google.com/analytics/devguides/collection/analyticsjs/cookie-usagehttps://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en To opt out of being tracked by Google analytics across all websites visit https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout Cookies that we use

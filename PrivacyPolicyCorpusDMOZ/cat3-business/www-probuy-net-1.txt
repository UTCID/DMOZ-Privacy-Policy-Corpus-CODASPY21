Probuy Safety Solutions Privacy Policy
Privacy
All sales information is kept confidential and is used only for our internal administrative purposes at Probuy.net . We DO NOT sell or distribute this information in any form to 3rd parties.
Security
We know that security is very important to our clients. We use SSL (Secured Socket Layer) which encrypts the information that you input. This is the same level of protection used by banks and financial institutions to ensure that you bank account information can be viewed online. Never do we pass credit card numbers by email or unsecured in any way, and never do we store unencrypted data.

Privacy policy
This site www.amateur-invest.com does not collect any personal information that can identify you specifically.
amateur-invest.com does however use 3rd party sites to handle advertising, analytics and payments. And those 3rd party sites might have other policies, so you should read their policies seperately.
They are all trusted, and internationally operating companies like Google and PayPal.
The way they can track your behavior on the internet, is by storing a little piece of information on your computer. This piece of information is called a cookie.
By tracking your behavior on amateur-invest.com - we can get a chance to see which pages you have been to.
By you, we don't mean you specifically, because we have no knowledge of your name, where you live or your telephone number. The only thing that we get to know is a computer identifier known as an IP number.
By knowing which pages are visited the most, we strive to improve the user impression, and improve other pages as well - to make the site better.
If you have questions, please find information about how to contacts us on the menu on the right.

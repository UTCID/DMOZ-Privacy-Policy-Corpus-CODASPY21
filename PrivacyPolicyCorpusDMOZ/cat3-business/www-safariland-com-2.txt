Winners of random social media contests will be chosen at random from "follower" participation. Entrants must follow the action requested in the contest announcement. For example, "like" a photo; "post" and "tag" a photo; "tag" yourself on a provided photo; "comment" on a post. Safariland is not responsible for and will not consider incomplete or incorrect entries, or emails sent or entries mailed but not received by stated contest deadline for any reason, as potential contest winners. Winners of all contests will be contacted at the "follower" social media profile used to enter the contest, and the person receiving and replying to the winner announcement message will be considered the winner unless they specify another person within the reply message as the winner. Safariland will mail the contest prize to the address supplied by the recipient of the winner announcement message. Only one entry per social media profile used to enter will be considered for contest entry, unless multiple entries are specifically allowed in the contest posting. Unless otherwise specified in an individual contest post, no person may enter any contest more than once using multiple social media profiles. Claiming of prizes requires a reply to Safariland from the winning social media profile within 15 days of being notified of winning at the social media profile used to enter. Failure to respond shall mean that the winner forfeits the prize. Safariland is not required to award elsewhere any prizes forfeited by the chosen winner(s).

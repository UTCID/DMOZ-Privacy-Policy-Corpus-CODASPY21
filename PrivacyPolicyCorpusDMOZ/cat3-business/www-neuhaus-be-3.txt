Warm Weather Guarantee
Neuhaus ships chocolates in a perfect condition everytime, guaranteed.
We absolutely ship chocolates during warm weather and to warm climates all year long.
Our shipping systems monitors the temperatures where your package is delivered to ensure your gift arrives in a perfect condition by including a cold pack.
We carefully pack your delicious chocolates in an elegant box, especially designed to control temperature and protect the chocolates all the way to their destination.

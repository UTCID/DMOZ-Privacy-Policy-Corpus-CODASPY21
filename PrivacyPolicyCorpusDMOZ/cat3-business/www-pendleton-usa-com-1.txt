Do Not Sell My Personal Information
Pendleton Woolen Mills, Inc. ("Pendleton") values your privacy and your right to make informed decisions about your personal data.
If you are a resident of California and would like to exercise your right to opt-out of sales of personal information pursuant to the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 (CCPA), please follow the instructions below. Any terms defined in the CCPA have the same meaning when used on this page.
If you are a resident of California and would like to learn more about your rights under California law, the information Pendleton collects, and how that information is used, please visit Pendleton’s Privacy Notice for California Residents. If you are not a resident of California, please see our Privacy Policy for more information.
California residents may submit a request to opt-out by doing one of the following:
Calling 800-649-1512
Emailing pendletoncatalog@penwool.com
Writing Pendleton Woolen Mills Customer Service, PO Box 3030, Portland, OR 97208
All requests must include your (1) name, (2) e-mail address, and (3) either mailing address or phone number. If you are submitting a request on behalf of another person, you must also provide proof that you are authorized to submit the request.

Privacy Statement
We take your privacy seriously and will not sell or share your data. We will use your email and other information to send newsletters and communications regarding our photography. From time to time we may send you emails about our photography such as events we participate in, awards we have won, and new images we’ve added to the gallery. You can click on the unsubscribe links in these emails at any time and update your preferences. If you have questions or comments, you can contact us at service@sumitdua.com.

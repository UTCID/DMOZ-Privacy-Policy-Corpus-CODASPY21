Vellano, Chino Hills, California
These policies relate to information that we at the Greg Norman Company gather about you and the use that we make of it in the course of our business.
The Greg Norman Company collects personally identifying information only during contest, sweepstakes, commerce transactions and site membership registration. In addition, information is collected in certain portions of the site in which guests specifically and knowingly provide such information along with content submissions, contact forms, community postings (i.e., chat or bulletin boards), suggestions or voting. the Greg Norman Company may use such information for editorial and feedback purposes (to the extent that is explained when guests provide the information).
By submitting an email address through a contact form, you consent to receive correspondence as it pertains to your inquiry. In addition, your email address will be added to a database for future marketing efforts. You may opt-out at any time by emailing us or by written request to:
The Greg Norman Company
2041 Vista Parkway, Level 2
West Palm Beach, FL 33411
The Greg Norman Company does not share information with any third parties whatsoever.
Policies for Individuals 16 and Under:
No information should be submitted to or posted on the Greg Norman Company by children 16 years of age or under without their parent's or guardian's consent.
Unless otherwise disclosed during collection, the Greg Norman Company does not provide any personally identifying information, regardless of its source, to any third party for any purpose whatsoever.
Although children are allowed to participate in contests, children ages 16 and under must get their parent's or guardian's permission before entering any sweepstakes or contests.
No information collected from children 16 years of age or under is used by the Greg Norman Company for any marketing or promotional purposes whatsoever, either inside or outside the Greg Norman Company.
Guests' e-mail addresses are never posted on the Greg Norman Company.
The Greg Norman Company encourages parents and guardians to spend time online with their children and to participate in the interactive activities offered on the sites.

Information submitted at the time of any form of registration by a guest who is over 16 years of age may be used for marketing and promotional purposes by the Greg Norman Company. the Greg Norman Company does not share this information with other companies. Use of Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information that a Web site transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes while at the site. Cookies make Web-surfing easier by saving your passwords, purchases and preferences. The use of cookies is an industry standard, and you'll find cookies at most major Web sites.
The Greg Norman Company does not share information with any third parties whatsoever.
The Greg Norman Company does not use cookies to retrieve information from your computer that was not originally sent in a cookie.
Cookies help make the Greg Norman Company better by showing how and when guests use the site. Many content improvements and updates are based on such data as total number of visitors and pages viewed. This statistical information is most easily tracked with cookies.
Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you'd prefer, you can set yours to refuse cookies. However, it is possible that some areas of the site will not function properly if you do so.

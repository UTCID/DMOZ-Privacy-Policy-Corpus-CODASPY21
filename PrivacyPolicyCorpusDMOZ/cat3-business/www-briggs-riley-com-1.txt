Privacy Policy
Your right to privacy is a primary concern to us. Briggs & Riley Travelware does not sell, rent, or loan any of your personally identifiable information to any third party without your consent. When an order is placed or when you register for services (like warranty registration), we collect basic information including: name, address, email address, phone number and other personal preferences. We use this information to confirm your order and to respond to any questions you may have.
When you make a purchase with Briggs & Riley, we will only send you email communications that you expect. If you opt in to our email list, you will receive email updates about special offers, new products and services. If for any reason you do not wish to receive this information, simply click the unsubscribe link in the email, contact us via email at customercare@briggs-riley.com and type "unsubscribe" in the subject line or call us at 1-877-745-3978. You may opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time.
Our site uses cookies to keep track of your shopping cart so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit. All data is stored in a highly secure database with state-of-the-art backup and firewall technologies. It is not accessible to any unauthorized parties.
We continually review and update our privacy policies as required. If you have questions about Briggs & Riley Travelware's privacy policy or have comments on how we can improve the Briggs & Riley shopping experience, please use our contact form. We appreciate hearing from you.

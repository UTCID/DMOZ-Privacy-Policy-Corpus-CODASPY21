OPTICUS PRIVACY POLICY JANUARY 2019
PRIVACY POLICY: Opticus based at 1-1 Titan Enterprise, 1 Aurora Avenue, Clydebank G81 1BF are committed to preserving your privacy. You are receiving this communication because we are in the process of doing business with you or you have submitted a request for information from us. Any data such as your name, email address and telephone number will be stored securely and not passed to third parties without specifically asking you for permission. If you have requested information, we will answer your enquiry and this may include details of products, services, prices, offers, technical recommendations and tips. Under GDPR rules this is classed a Legitimate Interest to supply information to a potential or existing customer.
UNSUBSCRIBING: You can request us to stop communicating with you at any time by emailing us.
DISCLOSURE OF YOUR DATA: The Data you provide to us is confidential and will not be disclosed to third parties unless you request this or in the event there is a legal requirement to do so.
SECURITY: We employ security measures to protect your Data from access by unauthorised persons and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction or damage. Please help us keep our records updated by informing us of any changes to your e-mail address and other contact details.
THIRD PARTY WEBSITES: Because we want your experience of the Opticus website to be as informative and useful as possible, we provide a number of links to websites operated by third parties (“Third Party Websites”). Please be aware that we do not control such Third Party Websites and that such Third Party Websites may send their own cookies to users, or otherwise collect data or solicit personal information. We assume no responsibility for the information gathering practices of Third Party Websites that you are able to access through our Site, and we encourage you to review each Third Party Website’s privacy policy before disclosing any personally identifiable information.
CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY: We may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any substantial changes we will notify you by posting a prominent notice on our Site.
YOUR RIGHTS: You have the right to ask for a copy of the Data held by us in our records. You also have the right to require us to correct any inaccuracies in your Data.
COOKIES: If you have an account and you log in to this site, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser. When you log in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for two days, and screen options cookies last for a year. For users that register on our website, we store the personal information they provide in their user profile. All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time.

PRIVACY POLICY AT PROFOTOS.COM
Profotos.com Privacy Statement
INTRODUCTION:
Profotos.com, has created this privacy statement in order to
demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy issues. The following
discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices
for profotos.com.
At profotos.com, we are very committed to your privacy, so here
is our PRIVACY PROMISE TO YOU: Profotos.com will never sell, exchange,
or release any of your individual personal information to a third
party without your express permission.
INFORMATION WE MAY COLLECT:
At the time of signing up for membership at profotos.com, we
ask users for contact information (like email addresses, mailing
addresses, etc.) and demographic information (like photography
field, or photography level). We use contact data to send Customers
information about our company and the demographic information
is used in coordination with our advertisers and sponsors.
We understand that you may not want us to send you email, or
use your demographic information for our purposes. Accordingly,
at the time of signing up for membership, we give you the option
of declining to receive information from profotos.com and the
option to withold your personal information from us. If you do not want
to receive email from us, just let us know, and we will not send
it. It is that simple.
Like other web sites, your IP number is visible to us when you
visit our web site. What is an IP address, you may ask? It is
a number automatically assigned to your computer whenever you're
connected the Internet. Your IP number is visible to the majority
of the websites that you visit. At profotos.com, we only use IP
numbers to help diagnose problems with our servers, to administer
our Web site and to help with advertising purposes.
From time to time, we may share with third parties aggregate
information-- such as, how many of our users are professional
photographers, or how many profotos.com Customers are from the
United States.
COOKIES
Like many sites, profotos.com uses cookies. Cookies are small
data files that are stored on your computer. They contain identification
information that enables profotos.com to see how our users are
interacting with our site and how frequently they are returning.
Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information.
We use cookies for the benefit of our users. Cookies let us know
if we have repeat visitors (a strong indication of happy consumers)
and if we are delivering a quality product to our consumers. We
also use cookies to identify which sites our repeat visitors are
coming from, so that we can place more of our online advertising
on these sites. Essentially, it is an anonymous way to survey
how we are doing and to look for other consumers who we think
will like our site.
If you do not want to accept cookies from profotos.com or other
sites, your browser software should allow you to not accept cookies.
LINKS TO OTHER WEB SITES
Profotos.com contains many links to other sites, especially within
the "Reference Desk" and "Photograper List"
areas of the site. Profotos.com is not responsible for the privacy
practices or the content of the third party Web sites, with which
it provides links to. Any damages incurred by a profotos.com Customer,
while visiting one of these third party sites, will not be covered
by any warranty, either express or implied, on behalf of profotos.com.
Profotos.com does not share any of the individual personal information
you provide profotos.com with the sites to which profotos.com
links, although profotos.com may share aggregate data with such
sites (such as how many people use our site). This includes other
third party sites on the Internet for which profotos.com provides
services.
Please check with those sites to determine their privacy policy.
OPT-OUT
If you do not wish to receive future communications from us,
you can send an email to support@profotos.com.

How we protect your privacy

Why did you receive an email from us?
If you received an email from us, either (a) your email address is listed as the sending or contact address for a form or otherwise submitted communication (including, but not limited to, inquiries, requests for quotes, technical support, etc.) as submitted by you or your company, or (b) you have purchased, are in the process of an order or have an existing relationship with us. Sharing and Usage
We will never distribute, sell, or rent individual personal information without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to employees managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for information or other form of initial contact.
9
How can you stop receiving email from us?
Each email sent contains an easy, automated way for you to stop receiving emails from us, excluding your requested information. If you wish to do this, simply click the link in the footer of every email to send us an unsubscribe notice.
If you have received unwanted or unsolicited emails sent via this system or purporting to be sent via this system, please forward a copy of that email with your comments to sales@southernavionics.com for review.

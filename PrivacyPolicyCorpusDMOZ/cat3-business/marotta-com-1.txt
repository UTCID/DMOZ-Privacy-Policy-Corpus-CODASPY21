Purpose of our Privacy Policy
Thank you for visiting marotta.com (the “Site”). This privacy policy and agreement (our “Privacy Policy”) governs and describes how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from and/or about you when you use the Site. We operate our Site pursuant to the statutes and regulations that apply to this Site (“Applicable Law”).
PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY POLICY CAREFULLY. YOUR CONSENT TO THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY CREATES LEGAL OBLIGATIONS FOR YOU AND FOR US. IF YOU DO NOT CONSENT TO THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY, PLEASE DISCONTINUE YOUR USE OF OUR SITE.
Your Consent
By accessing and using our Site as a site visitor, you are acknowledging that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to be legally bound by it. If you submit an inquiry to the Site, you will be asked to confirm that you have reviewed this Privacy Policy and that you agree to its terms. Confirmation constitutes your agreement to be bound by this Privacy Policy.
The Site is owned and operated by Marotta Controls, Inc., a New Jersey corporation (“Marotta Controls,” “we,” “us,” and/or “our”), the entity that is responsible for the processing of the Personal Data collected on our Site, as described below. The Site has been created to provide information about our company and the “Services” to all users of the Site (the “Users”). This Privacy Policy sets forth our policy in regards to any information that is collected from Site visitors, including any personally identifiable data (“Personal Data”) submitted.
Information Collected
When you interact with the Site or the Services, we may collect Personal Data and other information from you, as described below.
Personal Data You Provide Through the Site: We collect Personal Data when you voluntarily provide such information, including when you use the “Submit an Inquiry” service. Personal Data may include contact information such as your name, email address, phone number and extension, and the purpose of your request. By voluntarily providing us with Personal Data, you are consenting to our use of it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you provide Personal Data, you acknowledge and agree that such personal Data may be transferred from your current location to the offices and servers of Marotta Controls.
Information We Collect When you Use the Site: When you interact with Marotta Controls through the Site or Services, we receive and store certain information about you. Such information may be collected passively using various technologies or other means described below.
Similar to most websites, we gather some information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. Marotta Controls may store such information itself or such information may be included in databases owned and maintained by Marotta Services affiliates, agents or service providers. The Site may use such information and pool it with other information to track, for example, the total number of visitors to our Site, the number of visitors to each page of our Site, the domain names of our visitors’ ISPs, and how our users use and interact with the Services.
Cookie Policy
In operating the Site, we may also use a technology called “cookies.” A cookie is a piece of information that we transmit to your browser when you access the Site. Our cookies help provide additionally functionality to the Site and help us analyze usage of the Site more accurately. In all cases where we use cookies, we will not collect Personal Data except with your permission. Most web browsers include a “help” section in the toolbar that describes how to receive notification when receiving a new cookie and how to turn cookies off. We recommend that you leave cookies turned on because they allow you to take advantage of some of the Site’s features.
The use of cookies by any of our affiliates or service providers is not covered by this Privacy Policy. We do not have access or control over these cookies.
Do Not Track & Google Analytics
Our Site does not respond to Do Not Track signals.
This Site uses Google Analytics. Google Analytics helps us to track viewing trends and analyze how visitors use the Site. This information helps us to improve the Site and overall user experience. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information in an anonymous form. None of the information collected is personally identifiable in nature without your express permission.
Use of Personal Data
Information collected by the Site will be used only to measure site activity, develop ideas for improving the Site, and respond to inquiries. We will never sell or rent information collected by the Site to any other company. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request. We will endeavor to keep personal information accurate and up to date and to provide the opportunity to challenge the accuracy of the information. Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.
Retention and Storage of Personal Data
We will not store information longer than required for the above-mentioned purposes.  Unless otherwise required by law, information collected during the submission of an inquiry will be deleted 24 months after the resolution that inquiry. Information collected during regular browsing of the site will be retained for a 36 month period.
The computer/servers in which we store identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

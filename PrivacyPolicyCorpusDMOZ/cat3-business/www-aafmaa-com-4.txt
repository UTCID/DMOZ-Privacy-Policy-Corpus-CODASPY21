Social Security Number Policy
HomeAboutSocial Security Number Policy
AAFMAA may collect Social Security numbers in order to provide our products and services to our customers and as required by law. The Social Security numbers we collect and maintain are subject to physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. We take steps to limit access to Social Security numbers and help prevent unauthorized access and disclosure of Social Security numbers. Our customers can obtain more information on our privacy practices by reviewing our Consumer Privacy Notice (via the Member Center) and our Online Privacy Policy.

2019 Privacy Notice
Every AAFMAA Wealth Management & Trust LLC relationship is private in nature and subject to the provisions of the Right to Financial Privacy Act of 1978. As financial services professionals entrusted with sensitive financial information, we respect the privacy of our clients and are committed to treating customer information responsibly. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to guard your information. With all electronic information, our systems use a 256-bit Secure Socket Layer to scramble data to prevent access to unauthorized users. AAFMAA Wealth Management & Trust LLC never shares any information about you to outside third parties. For purposes of member- ownership status with our parent organization, AAFMAA, we share only demographic information to establish and maintain member-ownership status.
Should you have any questions regarding this notice, please contact your Relationship Manager. Thank you for the trust you have placed in AAFMAA Wealth Management & Trust LLC.

Privacy Policy
Foremost Fastener Company respects your privacy and our site visitors' information. We do not share, sell, or rent any of the information collected to any third parties and do not intend to do so in the future.

If you request or submit information to us by sending an Email via sales@foremostfastener.com or by filling out a "contact" form, we may save your Email address as well as any other information you provide.

Our Data Protection Statement
Trident Trust Group means each of the companies listed here.
All members of the Trident Trust Group are committed to holding and using Personal Data responsibly and securely and process Personal Data in accordance with applicable data protection law.
Personal Data is information relating to a natural person which identifies that person, directly or indirectly.
Personal Data will be held in confidence unless we have a legitimate reason, or are required, to provide it to someone else.
In addition, the spirit of our Privacy Notice applies as appropriate and reasonable throughout the Trident Trust Group.
USATrident Fund Services Inc (“TFSI”) complies with the requirements of the EU - U.S. Privacy Shield Framework and the Swiss - U.S. Privacy Shield Framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce (the “Principles”). TFSI has certified that it adheres to the Principles with respect to personal information  (as described in our Privacy Notice) that is transferred from the European Union and its Member States, the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and/or Switzerland (“EU”) to the United States. If there is any conflict between the terms of the TFSI Privacy Notice and the Principles, the Principles shall govern. TFSI is subject to investigatory and enforcement powers of the U.S. Federal Trade Commission.

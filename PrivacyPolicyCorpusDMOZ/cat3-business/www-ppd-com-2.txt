2020 Clinical Trial Insights Report
The year 2020 has seen a multitude of adaptions within the life science industry, including an accelerated pace of adoption for novel approaches and innovations impacting clinical research.
Focusing more acutely on patients and their unique sets of hurdles and needs has given rise and increased value to the concept of patient centricity
With patient feedback taking precedence in research design, clinical teams are witnessing the benefits that digital and decentralized trials create for patients and their families
In an effort to navigate the evolving clinical landscape, sponsors are continually seeking regulatory insights to ensure compliance across their programs

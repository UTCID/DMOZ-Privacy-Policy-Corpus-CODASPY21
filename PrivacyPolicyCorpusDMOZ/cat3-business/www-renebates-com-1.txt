In Texas, the Public Information Act, formerly known as the Open Records Act, is located at chapter 552 of the Government Code.
The Act provides a mechanism for citizens to inspect or copy government records.
It also provides for instances in which governmental bodies wish to, or are required by law to, withhold government records from the public.
For more information on the Public Information Act in Texas, please go to www.texasattorneygeneral.gov.

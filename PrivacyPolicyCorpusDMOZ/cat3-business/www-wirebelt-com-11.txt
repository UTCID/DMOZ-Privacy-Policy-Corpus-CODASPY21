Typical conveyor layouts
There are a number of popular conveyor layouts that are appropriate. Below are the most frequently used conveyor layouts which are divided into two groups: General Best Practices and Application-Specific Practices.
In general, less complicated conveyor layouts equal longer conveyor belt life. This would make, in most cases, the Simple Conveyor Layout the preferred method for maximum belt life. All these layouts have been utilized in numerous conveyor designs. CLICK HERE to Contact Support for more information.

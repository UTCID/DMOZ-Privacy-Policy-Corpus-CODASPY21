PRIVACY POLICY
McCormick Distilling Company takes the protection and proper use of your personal information seriously. We respect your privacy, and take great care to safeguard information in our possession. Your preferences for use of your information are our highest priority.
McCormick Distilling Company does not share customer information (including e-mail addresses) unless it is necessary to provide you with Holladay Distillery products or services, or we are legally required to do so.
We restrict internal access to your information to those individuals who need it to provide you with services. Any third-party companies we must use to provide you with services are required to keep your information secure and confidential.
McCormick Distilling Company is happy to provide further details of our privacy policies. For more information, please contact us.

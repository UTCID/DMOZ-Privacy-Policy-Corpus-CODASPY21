Privacy Policy & Terms of UseEffective November 23, 2017Our Commitment to Privacy
Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.
The Information We Collect
This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the RF Technologies, Inc. website. On some pages, you can make requests and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are name, company, address, email address and phone number.
The Way We Use Information
We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive. If you opt-in, such addresses may be used to communicate new products, services or discounts. These email addresses are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with outside parties.
You can register with our website if you would like access to our technical specifications sheets, as well as updates on our new products and services. Information you submit on our site will not be used for this purpose unless you fill out the registration form.
Finally, we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the ones described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.
Contact Information and Opt-Out
Users may elect to opt-out from receiving communications by (1) checking appropriate opt-in or opt-out boxes if provided on the collection form, or (2) following the instructions described in the message sent from RF Technologies.
Registration Information
Certain portions of the registration information may be used for determining qualifications for the use of particular applications. RF Technologies may use registration information to communicate with users regarding available transactions, changes in functionality of the website or applications, billing, help desk and other activities relating to the operation of the site.
Our Commitment to Data Security
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
How To Contact Us
Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please complete a contact form.

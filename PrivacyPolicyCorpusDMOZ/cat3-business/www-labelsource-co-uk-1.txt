Below the personal information retained, measures taken to safeguard such information, and the need to keep such records is covered. Type of information collected Some personal information may be gathered when you register on this site. These details will include Username, Password, Security Password, your name, telephone number, fax number, e-mail address, company name, company address, type of business undertaken, job function, number of employees in your company, your primary product interest, and where you heard about our site. Site activity information also may be retained such as products searched for, products purchased and information downloaded. If you accessed the site following an internet search, we may record the search you undertook and how it relates to your use of the site. Copies of information submitted by you, when you e-mail any feedback, ask questions, seek quotations or report a problem may be retained. Label Source may, on occasions, ask users to complete surveys in order to understand your needs and to be able to provide new products and services to meet your requirements. Use of your information Label Source uses the information for purposes of order fulfilment, administration, marketing activities, credit checking, customer services, and to provide pertinent information to you. On occasions, we may collect Internet Protocol addresses to help diagnose problems with our servers and for systems administration. In such cases, we do not link these addresses to any individual's identity. Protection of your information It is our intention to provide the highest level of service and security to our customers. Any sensitive information such as credit card numbers and account details are encrypted, using the latest technology, and such information stored on our in-house systems will also be in an encrypted format. Your Label Source account information details are password protected, so that you alone have access to your personal information. We recommend that you do not indulge your password to anyone, and Label Source will not ask for your password by means of either an unsolicited telephone call or an unsolicited e-mail. Use of cookies If you search our site or download information, your computer browser may alert you to the fact that a cookie is being sent to you. This enables our website to access information about your preferences, either until you exit your current browser window, or until you disable or delete the cookie. Cookies can assist you in the navigation of our site, and contain no more information than you volunteer. Disclosure of information The personal information you provide to this site is collected by Label Source. We may have links to other sites which also may collate personal information. The information practices of any linked web sites are not covered by this Privacy Statement. Label Source will never sell, rent or give away information that personally identifies customers to third parties, except where elements of the business have been out-sourced, and the provision of the information is needed to fulfil customer requirements. Under such circumstances, the security and confidentiality of personal information would form part of the legal requirement between us and our supplier. From time to time, we may release aggregated, anonymous market information to Government departments, or to business partners, or may use these in press releases, advertising or published reports. Only with your prior consent would we identify you, or any other customer, for use in marketing initiatives. There may be occasions when there is a legal requirement to disclose information. Changes or deletion of personal information You may edit your personal information by accessing the site or by e-mailing sales@labelsource.co.uk or by writing to Database Administration, Label Source, P O Box 130 , Cardiff CF14 7TX . Label Source may, from time to time, send you information regarding our products or services by e-mail, telephone or direct mail. If you choose not to receive such information, contact us by any of the above means to be removed from our mailing list.

What is GiftPay?
GiftPay is the ability to send a gift check with a personalized message on paper especially designed for birthdays, new babies, weddings, graduations, anniversaries, or just because. There is an additional fee for this service as displayed before you schedule a GiftPay. The fee is collected when the payment processes. To send a standard check without these features at no additional cost, add your individual recipient as a payee and schedule a payment.You can also send a donation to your favorite charity or organization in honor of or in memory of someone special. You can send a personalized message about your donation using up to 4 e-mail addresses and receive a copy for tax purposes (optional). There is an additional fee for this service as displayed before you schedule this type of GiftPay. The fee is collected when the payment processes. To send a standard donation without these features at no additional cost, add your charity as a payee and schedule a payment.

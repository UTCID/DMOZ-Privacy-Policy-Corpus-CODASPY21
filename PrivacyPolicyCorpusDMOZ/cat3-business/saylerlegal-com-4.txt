Affiliations
Sayler Legal Service, Inc. is proud to be affiliated with the following organizations and to be amongst the industry leaders:
The National Association of Professional Process Servers (NAPPS) was founded in 1982 by a group of concerned business owners. NAPPS is a worldwide organization founded on the Principles of Professionalism and High Ethical Standards.
California Association of Legal Service Professionals (CALSPro) is an association comprised of over 350 company and individual members who provide professional legal support services to the legal community. Stephanie Sayler currently serves as Conference Committee Chair for this association and is a Certified Process Server (CCPS). The CALSPro designation was established to acknowledge those process servers who have extended their professional education and successfully passed the CALSPro process server certification examination. The CCPS examination is administered by the CALSPro Continuing Education Committee and consists of a series of questions relating to the law.
ServeNow.com is a directory of local pre-screened process servers. By using a process server listed on ServeNow.com, you are going straight to the person or company who is going to be serving your papers. You will save time and money, and you will have a shorter chain of communication regarding your serves should you have questions regarding the status of your service.
Thervo is the best way for customers to connect with pros that are suited for their exact needs. Customers answer questions about their needs, and they match their answers with pros instantly. Then, customers can read reviews, message, or call pros and be able to make an educated decision when their ready to hire.

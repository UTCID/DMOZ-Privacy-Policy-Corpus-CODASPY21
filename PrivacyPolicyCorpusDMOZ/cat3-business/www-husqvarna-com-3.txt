California Privacy rights
CCPA
California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018
As a resident of California, you have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018. You also can exercise “Your Right to Know” or “Your Right to Deletion”. Submit the form below to exercise your rights or call us at (833) 674-9153.
If you select “Do Not Sell My Personal Information”, we will confirm receipt of your request within 15 days. If you select “Your Right to Know” or “Your Right to Deletion”, we will confirm receipt of your request within 10 days. We will let you know if we need additional information or time to respond to your request. For more information about your rights, please read our Privacy Notice for California Residents located here.

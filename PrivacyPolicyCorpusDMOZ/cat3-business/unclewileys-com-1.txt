Returns Policy
You can cancel this order within two weeks after we have received it.
You can return new, unopened items from a cancelled order within 2 weeks after they have been delivered to you. Items should be returned in their original packaging.
Privacy Policy
At Uncle Wiley’s Specialty Foods, Inc, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process orders and to provide a more personalized shopping experience. Please read on for more details about your privacy.
Uncle Wiley’s Specialty Foods, Inc does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.
When you order, we need to know your name, email address, mailing address, telephone number, credit card number, and expiration date. This allows us to process and fulfill your order and notify you of your order status.  However we DO NOT store your credit card information thus you will be required to enter it for each order.
When you place orders or access your account information, we offer a secure server. The secure server software encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Futhermore, all of the customer data we collect is protected by law.
By using our Web site, you consent to the collection and use of this information. If we decided to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

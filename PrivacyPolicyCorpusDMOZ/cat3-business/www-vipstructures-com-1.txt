Privacy POLICY
Effective Date: June 8, 2015
Last Modified: June 3, 2015
VIP Structures (“VIP Structures,” “Company,” “our,” “we,” or “us”) respects your privacy.
This Policy applies to and describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit, use, and interact with our website currently located at https://www.vipstructures.com/ (the “Website”).
Please read this Policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we collect it and will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is to not access, use, or interact with our Website. By accessing, using, and/or interacting with our Website, you agree to this Privacy Policy. This Policy may change from time to time. Your continued access and/or use of our Website after we make changes is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the Policy periodically for updates.
Information We Collect About You and How We Collect It
We collect several types of information from and about users of our Website, including information:
We collect directly from you when you provide it to us:
by which you may be personally identified such as your name, company name, address, email address, postal address, telephone number, cell phone number, date of birth or age, credit card or debit card number, or any other identifier by which you may be contacted online or offline (“personal information”).
We collect automatically as you navigate through and use the Website:
that is about you but individually does not identify you, such as statistical data related to your use of the Website;
about your use of and interaction with the Website; and/or
about your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our Website and usage details, and location of such equipment.
Information You Provide to Us
We may ask you to or you may otherwise provide some personal information if you do any of the following on or through the Website:
contact us from the Website by, for example, filling in forms on our Website;
request information;
share a message or comment with us (via email, text, or other electronic messages) or through others who use the Website (on or through the Website), or otherwise providing information on or through the Website;

carry out any other transaction on or through the Website; or
request further services, or alert us of any issues or problems with our Website, services, products, materials, events etc.
You may choose whether to enter personal information when you visit our Website. You are not required to do so. If you choose not to provide the information that we request and/or otherwise may be required to do so under certain circumstances listed above, we may be unable to respond to your request, provide a requested service, or proceed with a requested transaction etc. We do, however, collect, store, and/or use personal information when it is voluntarily submitted to us, as noted above.
Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies
As you navigate through and interact with our Website, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, and/or browsing actions and patterns, including:
details of your visits to, use of, and interaction with our Website, including traffic data, location data, and other meta data including information regarding resources that you access and use on the Website; and
information about your computer or mobile device and internet connection, including your IP address, operating system and browser type, location based data including geographic location of your computer or mobile device, and unique computer or mobile device identifiers.
The information we collect automatically is statistical data and may include personal information, or we may maintain the statistical data or associate it with personal information we collect in other ways or receive from third parties. It helps us to improve our Website and to deliver a better and more personalized service, including by enabling us to:
estimate our audience size and usage patterns;
store information about your preferences and particular uses of our Website, allowing us to customize and/or improve the performance, content and functionality of our Website according to your individual interests; and
recognize you when you return to our Website.
The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:
Cookies (or browser cookies). A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access or properly use certain parts of our Website. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you direct your browser to our Website; or
Google Analytics. This is a specific type of metric tracking. See Google’s privacy policy http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/ for further details regarding the type of information collected using this tool.
VIP Structures does not use tracking or measurement tools that would allow your individual browsing activity to be tracked across third-party websites or that would give information about you as an individual to third-parties based on your browsing activity.

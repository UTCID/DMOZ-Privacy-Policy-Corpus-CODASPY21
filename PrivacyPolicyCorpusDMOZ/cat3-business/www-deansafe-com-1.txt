Our Commitment To Your Privacy
Your privacy is important to us.
To better protect your privacy, the following notice explains the information we collect, how it is used, how it is safeguarded, and how to contact us if you have any concerns. Third-Party Web Beacons: We use third-party web beacons ("cookies") from Yahoo! and Google to learn about where visitors go and what they do while visiting our website. Yahoo! and Google may use anonymous information about your visits to this and other websites in order to improve their products and services and provide advertisements of interest to you based on your prior visits. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by Yahoo!, click here. To opt-out of Google's use of cookies, visit the Google advertising opt-out page
click here.
What Information Is Collected:
As part of the order process, the following information is collected from shoppers:
Name
Shipping/Billing Address
Email address
Phone number
Credit/Debit Card Information
How That Information Is Used:
We use this information to fulfill your order. In some cases, we will contact you to clarify an order or if additional shipping information is needed. We do not sell or share this information.
Return Policy:
We will accept any item for return that is in the original package and is in condition to be resold. The customer is responsible for original shipping charges to get the product to the customer. If you call us for an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) we will send you a UPS label for return to our warehouse in Southern California. If the item is defective we will send out a new item with a return label inside the box to return the defective item to our warehouse. If you have questions please call us at (818) 899-2222 for clarification.
Contact Us:
If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy of your data, please contact us at (800) 827-7534 or email us at info@deansafe.com.

Whether you are just surfing our site for inspiration and eye candy or buying the world’s most beautiful blooms, we appreciate the time you spend at passionroses.com. We want you to feel good about the time you spend with us and the information you share.
We know how important it is to feel safe and confident when you are on the intewebs!  Consider this the “fine print”, that part that no one ever reads and that we all agree to every time we do anything. It’s super important but not super fun.
The info provided by people like you help us personalize and elevate your experience here at PassionRoses.com  And our intent is always to provide a great experience, even in the fine print legal mumbo jumbo.
We know you care how information about you is used and shared. We appreciate your loyalty and trust. If you are still reading, buckle up, because this is the boring part and it’s not as pretty as looking at our roses. But we get it. So here we go:
We receive and store info you enter here. We do not share that information.  We receive and store “cookies” just like all the other sites you visit. You can disable cookies at any time but will not experience the full functionality of the website.  If cookies are disabled, we cannot recognize you as a registered user or have access to your account info. Cookies on our website last for one year. Cookies in our lunch room won’t make it through the day.
If you have an account and you log in to this site, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser. Yes, on occasion we throw away cookies.
All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (except they cannot change their username). Website administrators can also see and edit that information.

If at any time you wish to unsubscribe from our emails, please do so at the link provided in the email. We will be sorry to see you go!

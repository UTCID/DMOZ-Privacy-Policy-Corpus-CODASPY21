Privacy Policy
Home / About Us / Privacy Policy
Atlanta Attachment Company is very concerned about the protection of online privacy for all of our Web site visitors. Although we track the total number of visitors to each of our sites in an aggregate form to allow us to update and improve our sites, personally identifiable information is not extracted in this process. Any cookie technology (where our servers deposit special codes on a visitor’s computer) will only be used to track non-personal information such as type of browser, operating system, and domain names to improve our visitors’ online experience.
Atlanta Attachment Company only collects personally identifiable data, such as names, addresses, email addresses and the like, when voluntarily submitted by a visitor in an e-mail request for sales, service, or in response to one of our online surveys. Atlanta Attachment Company does not release any information (i.e. names, addresses or phone numbers) gathered on our Web sites to any third parties outside of our strategic product and service partners; all information gathered online will be used by Atlanta Attachment Company to improve our product and service offerings. Information submitted such as news, industry articles, success stories, press releases and industry/personal recognition may be posted on the Web site.
In some instances, when a visitor has indicated that he/she would like to receive communications from us, we will contact visitors. Our modes of contact may include e-mail, newsletters, site visits, printed newsletters and brochures and other electronic and non electronic mail.
Atlanta Attachment Company reserves the right to change this policy at any time by notifying visitors of the existence of a new privacy policy. This will be done by placing the amendments or changes to the policy on this page with the date that the changes are being implemented.

Dapra Corporation Website Privacy Policy
Dapra Corporation respects your privacy and the purpose for which you provide us information.
Information Collection, Use, and Sharing
Dapra collects information from you when you complete webforms located on our website. Dapra uses this information to respond to requests for quotations, improve our website and improve customer service. The mail/email address you provide enables us to send you the information you requested. Unless you ask us not to, your email address and contact information will be added to our database and may be used for future contact via mail or email campaigns. You may opt out of these communications at any time.
Dapra does not sell, trade or otherwise provide your information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep your information confidential. Communications made through our websites is protected by industry-standard encryption technology. Dapra’s computer systems are protected by a firewall to prevent unauthorized access to your data.
Your Access To and Control Over Information
You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting us by emailing unsubscribe@dapra.com or calling Customer Service at (800) 243-3344:
See what data we have about you, if any.
Change/correct any data we have about you.
Have us delete any data we have about you.
Express any concern you have about our use of your data.
Cookies Policy & Third-Party Services
Our site utilizes "cookies." A cookie is a piece of data stored on a site visitor's hard drive to help us improve your access to our site and identify repeat visitors to our site.
Dapra uses a combination of session cookies and persistent cookies in order to track how you use and experience our website, build anonymous statistical data about how our website is performing, and to power the personalization of website content to you. However, as you navigate through a website, certain information can be passively collected (that is, gathered without you actively providing the information) using various technologies and means. Our sites may use cookies dropped by third parties for a variety of purposes, including to operate and personalize the website. Cookies may also be used to track how you use the site to target ads to you on other websites. You can choose whether or not to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser.
This site uses Google Analytics, which is the most widespread and trusted analytics solutions available for helping us to understand how users interact with our site and the ways that we can improve your experience. These cookies track data such as how long users spend on the site, pages most commonly visited, and geographic region of our site visitors. Click here to learn how Google uses data when you use our site.

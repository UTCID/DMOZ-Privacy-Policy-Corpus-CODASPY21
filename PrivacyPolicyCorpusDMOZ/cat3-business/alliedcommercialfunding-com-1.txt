PRIVACY POLICY
Our Privacy Policy has been designed to protect the information you give us while providing you with our exceptional service. This is how we handle information we learn about you from your visit to our website when you fill out a form, by your fax, mail or electronic mail to us. The information we receive depends on what you personally send/give us. Your information is not saved on our website from any form you may fill out located on our website.
Your privacy is important to us.
WHAT WE COLLECT
We Collect No Cookies.  In general, we collect personally-identifying information from you when you fill out our online loan application or when you send us personal information by fax, mail or give us verbally by phone. This information is used to help secure financing for you at your request. The type of information we collect is listed on any of our forms that you fill out.
WHAT WE WILL NOT DO
We will not share, sell, rent or furnish your information to outside parties without your consent except as required by law.
HOW WE DISPOSE OF YOUR INFORMATION
Any information send to us in connection with a loan or trust deed investment is held in a secure area for a period of at least 3 years and may be held up to 5 years before being disposed of.
CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
We do not knowingly solicit data from persons under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly market to persons under the age of 13. We recognize that protecting children’s identities and privacy online is important and that the responsibility to do so rests with both the online industry and with parents.

We also want to present information on Celgard.com that is tailored to the needs of our visitors. For this reason, we may ask you to register or to provide personal information when you visit certain areas of Celgard.com, download document files, or subscribe to a newsletter.We will use the personal information you share with us to ensure that the content, services, and advertising that we provide on Celgard.com (and related sites) are tailored to your current and future needs. If you provide Celgard with your mailing address or telephone number Celgard and its partners may use this information to alert you to product upgrades, special offers, updated information, and new products or services. If you provide your e-mail address, Celgard may contact you via e-mail. If you do not want Celgard or our partners to contact you (currently Celgard does not sell or rent personal information or e-mail addresses to third parties not affiliated with Celgard, LLC), you may opt out when you provide personal information or contact Celgard directly. For instructions, please see the Contact Us information below.If you choose not to register or provide personal information, you can still visit most of Celgard's website. However, you will not have access to areas that require personal identification.How Celgard Will Protect Your Personal Information
Celgard will protect the personal information that you share with us. If we request demographic information, we will not share information about you as an individual with any other entity. If we do share demographic information with third parties, we will give them aggregate information only.Celgard's Affiliates may send you information, by postal mail, about their products or services.

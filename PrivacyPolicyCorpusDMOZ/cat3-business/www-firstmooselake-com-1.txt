Children’s Online Privacy Policy
Protecting Children Online
We do not knowingly collect or use personal information from children under 13 on our website.  Should a child whom we know to be under 13 send information to us, we will only use that information to respond directly to that child or provide parental notice.
Our website contains links to other internet sites that may be of interest to your children.   We are not responsible for the privacy, security, and use practices of non-affiliated third parties to which our website may link. Although our intent is to provide links to other quality sites, we cannot guarantee the safety and privacy of information provided to these linked sites. Parents should review the privacy policies of these sites closely before allowing children to provide any personal identifiable information.

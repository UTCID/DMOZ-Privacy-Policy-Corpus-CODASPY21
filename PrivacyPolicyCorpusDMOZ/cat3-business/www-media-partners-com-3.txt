Media Partners Limited Warranty
Media Partners offers a free one-year Limited Warranty on all Media Partners’ productions.
For other Producers’ productions, any Producer’s warranty on their product will apply when purchased on Media Partners. To receive a free copy of the producer’s warranty, either before you buy or as a replacement, please email us at support@mediapartners.com or call us at 800-408-5657.  The Producer’s name is always listed at the bottom of each Product web page.
For Media Partners’ productions, this Limited Warranty covers DVD’s and USB’s for 12 months from the original date of shipment to the purchaser. However, Media Partners does not warrant against normal wear and tear, product misuse, or accidental damage. You must obtain prior authorization for your warranty claim before shipping any product to Media Partners, provide (upon request) a copy of your purchase receipt and satisfy all other terms and restrictions set forth in this Limited Warranty.
For other Producers’ productions, please contact us and we will apply any remaining Warranty offered by the Producer. You must obtain prior authorization for your warranty claim before shipping any product to Media Partners, provide (upon request) a copy of your purchase receipt and satisfy all other terms and restrictions set forth by the Producer.

Privacy Policy
Coast Label Company respects your privacy. That’s why we feel it’s important that you know the following:
We make use of retargeting pixels to market to visitors of Coastlabel.com.
These pixels are hosted by 3rd party vendors (e.g. Google, Bing). They drop cookies (yum!) in order to serve ads to people who have previously visited our website.
As part of serving ads based on those cookies, we also use 3rd party vendors to host and deliver the creative messaging.
If this creeps you out, you can go to the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) and opt out from one, many, or all the cookies on your internet browser at once.

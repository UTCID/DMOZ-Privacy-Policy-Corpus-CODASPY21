Privacy Policy
When you choose to make an online booking, we collect your details to process your booking and to improve our service to you. These details will never be shared with any other third parties.
We may use ‘cookies’ to track your activities on our site.  Your browser options allow you to stop this process, however it may restrict the website from operating to its full potential.
If you have any concerns over your privacy,  please contact us at secretary@iverkshow.ie.  We will treat your enquiry with the highest priority.
You will be asked to enter your card security number in order to complete your booking. We do not store your credit card details or make them available to you when you make another order through your account, for your protection.

Cookies
To enrich and perfect your online experience, TEAM Group uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. TEAM Group uses cookies to help TEAM Group identify and track visitors, their usage of https://sandbox1.team-group.com, and their website access preferences. By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to TEAM Group’s use of cookies.

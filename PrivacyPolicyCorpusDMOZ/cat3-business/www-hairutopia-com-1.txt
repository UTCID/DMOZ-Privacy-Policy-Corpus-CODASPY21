Privacy Notice
Hairutopia is committed to providing its customers with complete privacy. You can shop for our wholesale hair and fashion accessories with confidence.
By visiting Hairutopia , you are accepting the practices described in this privacy statement. Our site uses a shopping cart ordering system that collects personal information (name, address, telephone number, etc.) and financial information (account or credit card number) from the customer. This contact information is used to send orders and information about our company to you and to get in touch with our customers when necessary.
Financial information is used solely for the purpose of billing customers for products ordered. We do not keep credit card information on file for future use. We thank our repeat customers for their patience in resubmitting their credit card details with subsequent orders.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect against the unauthorized access and misuse of customer information. Our secure ordering system uses SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technology to keep your credit card transactions and other information safe. We do not employ the use of cookies for storing the contents of your shopping cart between visits. We guarantee the safety of shopping online with Hair Utopia. However, if you are not comfortable providing personal and financial information over the Internet, please call us at 360-866-8840 to complete your order.
Effective Date & Amendments
This policy is effective beginning October 26, 2003. Amendments to this policy will be posted at this URL and will be effective when posted.

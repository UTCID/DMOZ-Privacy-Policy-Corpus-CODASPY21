Our Privacy Policy
We are committed to protecting your privacy in every way. Mincor will only use your information for the following purposes:
To be able to get back to you with a quote,
To process your order, and
To provide you with the best posible service.
We will e-mail you only with an order confirmation message, updates on your order, any possible questions about your translation project, and finally the delivery of your finished job.
The type of information we will collect about you includes:
Your name and/or company name
Phone number
Email address
We store this information securely in our system, and we will neither share nor sell it to anyone. We will never collect sensitive information about you without your knowledge and consent.
The information contained in your texts for translation will be securely stored in our system. We do not share any of that information with anyone. Mincor’s management and translators have signed non-disclosure agreements with regard to the information in our system. We are happy to sign a non-disclosure agreement with you or your company to ensure your peace of mind with regard to the safety of your information.
As with most websites, certain technical information about your visit may be logged (e.g., IP addresses), but this is not linked to any specific data we store about you and will only be used to monitor web traffic. Mincor, Inc. does not use ‘web bugs’ or other programs to retrieve more detailed information about our visitors.
If you have any questions/comments about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.

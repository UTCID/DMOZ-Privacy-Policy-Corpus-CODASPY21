Cookies
Like most websites, Tpage uses cookies to store a member's profile. A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's hard drive containing information about the User. Usage of a cookie is taken place in no way linked to any personal private information while logging in our site. Once the member closes their browser, the cookie simply terminates. For instance, by setting a cookie on our site, the user would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. If the user rejects the cookie, they may still use our site, but access to some functions can be denied. Tpage does not use cookies to track a member's Internet viewing habits or for marketing purposes.

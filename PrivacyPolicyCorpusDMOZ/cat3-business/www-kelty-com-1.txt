Transaction data will be provided to our payment services providers only to the extent necessary for processing your payments, refunds, and managing complaints and queries relating to such payments and refunds.  In the event of bankruptcy or a sale or transfer of all or a portion of our business or assets, an acquisition, merger or divestiture, we reserve the right to transfer your Personal Data.

With the exception of the terms in this Privacy Policy, we do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your Personal Data with any third parties outside of Exxel Outdoors or with our affiliated companies for monetary or other valuable consideration.

If you reside in a country in the European Union (EU/EEA) and we need to transfer your Personal Data to a country which has been deemed to have an inadequate level of protection for Personal Data, we will implement available safeguards in accordance with the EU requirements.

TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES INCLUDING COOKIES AND WEB BEACONS
Exxel Outdoors’ sites use Cookies. Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit a website that allow a website to recognize a user’s device. Cookies improve the use of our sites through various website functions such as allowing users to “log in” and navigate more efficiently and remembering your preferences such as language. Cookies can also help with making the content and advertisements you see online more relevant to your interests.  Our newsletters and emails may include web beacons so that we know if the messages and links therein have been clicked.
Our business partners may sometimes set web beacons and Cookies on our site. Third-party social media buttons may log certain information such as your IP address, referring website addresses, browser type, and language. If you are logged in to those social media sites, they may also link such collected information with your profile information on that site.
To learn more about cookies or how to opt-out of cookies from your browser, visit www.allaboutcookies.org for further information. If you disable cookies, some of the features on our sites may not function as a result.

Privacy Policy
Security Information
This site uses an encrypted security system called SSL ("Secure Socket Layer").
This is a system which takes your name, address, order details, shipping information
and credit/debit card details and encrypts them so that only we can view them.
It creates a direct connection between your computer and ours, which can not
be intercepted by any third parties.
Privacy Information
We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only use the information
that we collect about you lawfully (in accordance with the Data Protection
Act 1998).
We collect information about you for two reasons: to process your order and
to provide you with the best possible service.
We will not contact you in the future unless we need to contact you in response
to an order or an enquiry you have made from us.
The type of information we will collect about you includes your name, address,
phone number, email address & Credit/Debit card details. We only use Credit/Debit
card details to process your order, are not permitted to retain these details.
We will never collect sensitive information about you without your explicit
consent.
The information we hold will be accurate and up to date. You can check the
information that we hold about you by emailing us. If you find any inaccuracies
we will delete or correct it promptly.
The personal information which we hold will be held securely in accordance
with our internal security policy and the law.
If we intend to transfer your information outside the EEA (European Economic
Area) we will always obtain your consent first.
We may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our
site. This can include using a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser.
You can usually modify your browser to prevent this happening. The information
collected in this way can be used to identify you unless you modify your browser
settings.
Please contact us if you have any questions or comments about privacy.

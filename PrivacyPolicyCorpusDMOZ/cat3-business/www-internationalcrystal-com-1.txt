Privacy
Simply put, we do not share information with anyone unless you specifically authorize this. If you call or fax credit card information, the numbers are sent to our processor over a phone line from a terminal in our office. When you use the pay by credit card function you will not leave our web site. We think limiting the movement of a transaction increases privacy and security.

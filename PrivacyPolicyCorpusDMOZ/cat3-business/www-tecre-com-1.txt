Tecre Privacy PolicyTecre Co., Inc. recognizes and appreciates the importance of responsible use of information. In general, you can visit www.tecre.com without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself.However, there are times when we may need information from you, such as your name, address, phone number, and e-mail address. Some examples of when we may need information are if you choose to purchase a product directly from our website, request a catalog, or send us questions or comments. We will not collect any personally-identifiable information about you unless you provide it to us voluntarily by filling out and submitting an online form. We will not disclose any information you provide to a third party.This site contains links to other, non Tecre websites. Tecre Co., Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.If you have any questions or comments about our privacy practices or would like to correct or update information provided to Tecre via our website, please contact us.

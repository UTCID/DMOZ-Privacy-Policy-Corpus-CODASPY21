BNP Media Acquires Beverage World And BevOps Fleet Summit
At the end of September 2016, Beverage Industry’s parent company BNP Media acquired Beverage World as well as its successful and well-attended BevOps Fleet Summit. Through this acquisition, the BNP Media Events team now will manage and produce the BevOps Fleet Summit, while Beverage World’s digital and print properties ceased operations, with October being the final print publication.
Under BNP’s ownership, we have combined a shared dedication to editorial excellence and integrity as we help to empower beverage market professionals through our resources and increase engagement across all media platforms.
Beverage Industry is the best read and most widely distributed magazine covering the entire $240 billion North American beverage marketplace. With a BPA Worldwide audited audience of 35,745, Beverage Industry reaches top management, sales and marketing, production/operations/quality control and distribution/warehouse readers. Beverage Industry reports on a wide range of marketing and manufacturing subjects with emphasis on new products, research and development, packaging, production, distribution, and marketing innovations.
Read the full press release.
The BevOps Fleet Summit will now be operated by through the BNP Events Division with the goal of expanding and growing the event
by utilizing BNP’s massive food & beverage databases, abundant infrastructure and deep industry reach.
We are currently working on plans for the 2017 event which we intend to move from the spring timeframe to the fall of 2017.

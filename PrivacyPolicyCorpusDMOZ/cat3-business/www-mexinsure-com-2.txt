Privacy Policy
Here at Mexico Insurance Services we do our best to proactively protect your data. There is no completely secure system but we employ the latest technologies and techniques to ensure your data security. We use SSL on the web forms to ensure the transmission tunnel is encrypted and we use Paypal (formerly Verisign)'s 128-bit encrypted payment gateway. This means we don't even store your credit card information locally, that they are housed and encrypted at a world-class facility far out of reach of hackers.
We created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to the privacy concerns of users of our website and to disclose our information gathering and dissemination practices. Except for the purposes set forth herein, Mexico Insurance Services will never sell or transfer your personal information to any unaffiliated person for any reason. "Affiliated person" may include, but is not limited to, third parties that have been retained to aid with customer service, help with hardware/software maintenance and upgrades, and/or our foreign insurance/roadside assistance providers.

Cookies
"Cookies" are pieces of information that are placed on an individual's computer hard drive to enable the individual to more easily communicate and interact with Web sites. Mexico Insurance Services may use cookies to customize your experience on the Mexico Insurance Services Web site. We may use cookies to deliver content specific to your interests, save your password (if you have registered with us) so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site, and for other purposes. You may, however, disallow receiving cookies at any time through your web browser. Mexico Insurance Services will never use cookies to retrieve information from a computer that is unrelated to the Mexico Insurance Services web site or your interaction with the Mexico Insurance Services Web site.

Clickstream
As you browse the Internet, a trail of electronic information is left at each Web site you visit. This information, which is sometimes called "clickstream" data, can be collected and stored by a Web site's server. Clickstream data can tell us the type of computer and browsing software you use, the address of the Web site from which you linked to the Mexico Insurance Services Web site, and in some instances, your e-mail address. We may use clickstream data to determine how much time visitors spend on each page of our site and how they navigate throughout the Mexico Insurance Services site. We will only use this information to improve the Mexico Insurance Services Web site. Any collection or use of clickstream data will be anonymous and aggregate, and will not contain any personal information.

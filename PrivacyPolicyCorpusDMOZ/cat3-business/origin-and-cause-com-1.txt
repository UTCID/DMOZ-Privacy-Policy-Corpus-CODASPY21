How do you get my consent?
When you provide us with personal information to complete a transaction, we assume you consent to our collecting it and using it for that specific reason only.
If we ask you for personal information for a secondary reason, like marketing, we will either ask you directly for your consent or provide you with an opportunity to say no. Saying no is called “opting out”. By opting out, you can tell us not to collect the information and/or not to share it with other companies.
How do I opt out?
When you to receive our marketing communications, at the bottom of the email, there is link to unsubscribe if you do not want that information to be used for marketing purposes or to be shared with other companies.

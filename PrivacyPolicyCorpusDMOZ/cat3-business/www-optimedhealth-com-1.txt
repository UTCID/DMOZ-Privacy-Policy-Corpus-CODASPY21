OptiMed Privacy Policy
At OptiMed the privacy of our visitors is very important to us.
Our policy is to collect personal information (name, address, phone, email address, etc.) only when it is necessary for the proper execution of a service we offer. This information is obtained on a voluntary basis when a visitor submits a form on our site. The information collected is solely for the purposes of providing you with the best service possible.
It is not our policy to share or sell any personal information we may collect with a third party unless it is necessary for the proper execution of a service that a visitor has requested or the visitor has given us permission to do so.
NEWSLETTERS
OptiMed's newsletters are optional benefits for clients and newsletter subscribers to update you with the latest OptiMed news, program updates. At any time, you may request that OptiMed not send further promotional materials by contacting us via email at sales@optimedhealthplans.com.
TRACKING
This website may at any time employ Google Ads remarketing service to advertise on third-party websites (including Google) to previous visitors to our site. This is accomplished by placing a small section of code on your device. This text snippet does not in anyway identify you nor does it provide access to your device. Google Ads remarketing enables us to tailor ads to the interests you indicated by visiting this site.
This site also uses tracking code supplied by social media sites that compile lists on their platforms of those who clicked a link from them to this site. This neither reveals your identity nor your email address; it only enables us to advertise to you on the specific social media platform.
COOKIES
The OptiMed website may use “cookies” to help personalize your visit to this site. A cookie is a text file placed on your hard disk by a web page server. A cookie from this website cannot run programs nor deliver viruses to your device. Cookies are assigned exclusively to you, and can be read only by the web server that issued it.
The purpose of a cookie is to save you time by offering to auto fill form fields on subsequent visits with information your provided previously.

Cookies
A "cookie" is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows Environment Control to recognize you each time you visit this site (customization, etc). Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and Environment Control does not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by third-party content providers such as newsfeeds.

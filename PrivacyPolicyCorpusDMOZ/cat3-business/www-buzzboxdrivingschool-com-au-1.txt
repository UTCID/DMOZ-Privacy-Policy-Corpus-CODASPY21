Payment Conditions

Cancellation and No Show Policy
Failure to give a minimum of 1 hour notice will incur a $30 cancellation fee or 30 minutes will be deducted against your pre-purchased package. For Keys2Drive lessons a minimum 24 hours notice must be given for cancellation.

Lesson Refund Policy
If you purchase a package deal and cannot complete the required number of driving lessons in the package before passing your final drive, the cost of the driving lessons will be reverted to an individual lessons price.
A $15 administration fee will be charged for Cheque refunds. No administration fee will be charged for refunds back to credit card or Electronic Fund Transfer (EFT).
Driving Test Refund Policy
Your test fee will not be refunded if your test is cancelled for reasons such as:
incorrect date or time of VICROADS drive test appointment
not meeting logbook requirements at the time of the test
failing to successfully complete your drive test

Lost or stolen Gift Cards will not be replaced or refunded.
Gift Cards are not redeemable for cash and cannot be exchanged or refunded.
Gift Cards expire 3 years from the date of issue.

Learners Pearmit/License Requirements
To undertake any lesson you warrant that your Learners Permit/License is not cancelled or suspended.
In the case of an Overseas License, you warrant:
If a Permanet Residency has been issued that it’s not longer than 6 months since issued; or if Permanet Residency was granted when you were outside Australia, not more than 6 months has elaspsed since you arrived.
That you have not undertaken a driving test with VicRoads.
In the case that you don’t have a Valid License, you warrant that you will obtain a Learners Permit from VicRoads before undertaking a driving lesson.
Medical Requirements
You must notify Buzz Box Driving School if
you are taking any medication or if you suffer from, or have ever suffered from an eyesight or hearing defect, neurological conditions, heart conditions, dementia, epilepsy, diabetes, psychiatric or sleep disorder or any medical condition or other disability. If you have a medical condition you will require a clearance from VicRoads and you warrant that you have been given medical clearance from VicRoads before taking driving lessons.
For further information please visit your local VicRoads office or website https://www.vicroads.vic.gov.au/licences/health-and-driving/medical-conditions-and-driving to get medical clearance.
Traffic Offences
All trafic offences commited while you are driving during a lesson will need to be paid by you and demerit points will be allocated to your Learners Permit/License.
Drugs and Alcohol
You warrant that you are alcohol and drug free whilst taking a driving lesson. No refund will be given if you are found to be intoxicated with alcohol or drugs and your lesson will be immediately terminated.
Fitness to Drive
If at any time you are assessed as unfit or unsafe to drive during a lesson, your instructor may terminate the lesson immediately and may assume control of the vehicle. This includes if you show symptoms of Coronavirus (COVID 19) such as fever, cough, shortness of breath, sore throat, runny nose or loss of sense of smell.

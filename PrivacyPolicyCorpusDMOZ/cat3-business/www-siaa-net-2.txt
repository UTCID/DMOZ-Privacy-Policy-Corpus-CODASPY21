https://www.siaa.net/news/agents-can-help-small-businesses-stay-covered-covid-19-world/
Whether employers have their staff working from home or cautiously moving back and forth to the office, COVID-19 has created new and expanded risks for many small businesses. To identify and mitigate these risks – and know what insurance coverage may be needed – businesses need to help of their insurance professionals.
In this environment, an independent agent can offer valuable insight into understanding these risks, assist in implementing risk management practices and provide the appropriate coverage.

How do I change my cookie settings?
When you access the Website for the first time, a short notice on the use of Cookies will appear as a pop-up. Such pop-up will also include a link to this Cookie Policy.
By clicking the ‘Accept’ button on the pop-up you will provide your consent to the installation of all the Cookies.
Alternatively, you can set your cookie preferences by clicking on the ‘Manage your preferences’ button and then adjust the available sliders to ‘On’ or ‘Off’. Preferences can be changed at any time directly from this page. Click here

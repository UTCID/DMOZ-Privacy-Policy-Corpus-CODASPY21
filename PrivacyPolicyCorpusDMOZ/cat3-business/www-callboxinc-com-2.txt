What are cookies?A cookie is a small text file containing an identifier which a web server sends to your web browser and is stored in your browser. The identifier consists of a string of letters and numbers. Each time your browser requests a page from the web server, the identifier is then sent back to that server.Cookies allow the Callbox website to recognize your browsing device on your return visits in order to remember your preferences, improve your online experience, and provide other uses described in this Cookie Policy.2. How do we use cookies?Callbox uses cookies to measure site traffic and usage activity to help us improve and enhance the functionality of our website, to detect possible fraudulent activity, and to better understand the sources of traffic and transactions on our website. Cookies also allow our servers to remember your account information for future visits and to provide personalized and streamlined information across related pages on our website.Cookies typically do not include information which can personally identify a website user. But to the extent any personal information is collected through the cookies on our website, the Callbox website Privacy Policy applies and complements this Cookie Policy.3.

WHAT ARE COOKIES?
Cookies are files stored on your computer when you visit certain web pages. Lifestylesports.com uses cookies to keep track of what you have in your basket, and to remember you when you return to our site.
To place an order on lifestylesports.com, you need to have cookies enabled. If you don't wish to enable cookies, you'll still be able to browse the site and use it for research products. Most web browsers have cookies enabled, but have a look at the Managing Cookies section for help to turn them on should you need to.
Cookies can't harm your computer.
We don't store personally identifiable information such as credit card details in cookies we create. We use information gathered from cookies to make lifestylesports.com better for you, for example, they help us to identify problems, fix errors and to improve our page designs.
We also use the information from cookies to identify related products and products you may like based on what you have looked at whilst using lifestylesports.com We also work with carefully chosen and monitored suppliers who may set cookies during your visit to lifestylesports.com.
These cookies are used for re-marketing - this means they are used to show you advertisements for different products and services based on what you appear to be interested in. You can easily opt out of these cookies by going to the Network Advertising Initiative website. This link will open in a new window - we're not responsible for the content of external websites.
In addition to re-marketing, a lookalike audience may also be built to identify customers who may have similar interests to you. This audience is identified through using a custom audience which includes either a customer’s email address or phone number. The custom audience is shared in an encrypted format to maintain privacy. Life Style Sports remains responsible for managing this data and the audience selection.
The uploaded encrypted data is compared against a partners encrypted data to identify a lookalike audience.
Customers identified through a lookalike audience can then be targeted with relevant online advertising. Please note, this lookalike audience is only used by Life Style Sports and the data processor aligns with GDPR.
Customers can request to be removed from this targeting by contacting dataprotection@lifestylesports.com
We're sharing this information with you to comply with recent European legislation, but more importantly to ensure that we’re honest, clear and upfront about your privacy when using our website.
THE LIFE STYLE SPORTS COOKIES POLICY
To make full use of the online shopping and personalised features on lifestylesports.com, your computer, tablet or mobile phone will need to accept cookies. This is because the shopping basket needs to use cookies to function and because we can only provide you with certain personalised features of this website by using them.
Our cookies don't store sensitive information such as your name, address or payment details. They do hold a “key” that allows us to associate information with your computer when you are shopping with us or when you come back to visit us.
We split the cookies that we use into 4 different types:
1. Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for you to use the shopping features of Lifestylesports.com, they enable you to add items to your shopping basket, to checkout and to use the My Account section of the site.
These cookies are not used to advertise products or services to you.
2. Performance Cookies
These cookies collect information about how you use lifestylesports.com, for instance the pages you visit most often or any error messages you may see on the pages you visit. These cookies don’t collect your personal information. The information we collect is used to improve how our website works.
These cookies are not used to advertise products or services to you.
3. Functional Cookies
These cookies allow the lifestylesports.com website to remember items such as your user name, the region you are shopping in and more personal features like product recommendations. The information these cookies collect is anonymous and cannot be used to track your activity on other websites.
These cookies are not used to advertise products or services to you.
4. Targeting Cookies
These cookies are used to advertise products and services to you.
These cookies are used to make our advertising more relevant to you and your interests. We also use them to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.
These cookies may be placed on your computer, tablet or mobile phone by us or by a third party with our consent.
When these cookies are placed by us, they may be used only by us. When these cookies are placed by a third party with our consent, they may be used by that third party and other parties to make adverts more relevant to you and your interests. Examples of this include various social networks such as Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest. These social network sites may place cookies on your device and use these to target advertising to you on their own or other websites.

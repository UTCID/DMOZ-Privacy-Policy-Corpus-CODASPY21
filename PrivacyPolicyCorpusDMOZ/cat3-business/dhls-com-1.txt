Privacy Policy

Nonpublic Personal Information We Collect and to Whom We Disclose It.
The information we collect from you is nonpublic personal information provided to us by you or obtained by us with your authorization. Permitted disclosures include giving your information to our employees in the normal course of the services we provide for you, or on a limited basis and with your authorization, to unrelated third parties who need to know that information to assist us in providing services to you. We always stress the confidential nature of the information shared.

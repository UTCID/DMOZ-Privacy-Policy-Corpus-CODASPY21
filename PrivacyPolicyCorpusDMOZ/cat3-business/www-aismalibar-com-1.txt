WHAT COOKIES ARE
A cookie is a file that is downloaded to the user’s computer / smartphone / tablet when accessing controlled web pages to retrieve and retrieve information about the navigation that is done from said computer. We can differentiate between session or permanent Cookies and own or third party Cookies. “Session cookies” are a type of cookies that can be used to collect and specify data while the user accesses the Website. “Persistent cookies” are those that have data stored in the terminal and can be accessed and processed for a period defined by the person responsible for the cookie, and which can range from a few minutes to several years. “Own cookies” are those that are sent to the user’s terminal equipment from the computer or domain managed by the editor and from which the service requested by the user is provided. “Third-party cookies” are those that are sent to the user’s computer from a computer or domain that is not managed by the editor, but sends another entity that processes the data that is transferred through cookies.

You have the right to accept or not certain Cookies or their entirety. However, if you choose to reject or eliminate them, this decision could affect the availability and functionality of the services and tools of the Website. To adjust your preferences or to disable cookies use the Cookies Panel.

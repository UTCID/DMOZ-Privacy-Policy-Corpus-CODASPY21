Dave’s Septic Service
Dave’s Septic Service
United Site Services (“USS”), is excited to announce the acquisition of Dave's Septic Service, Inc. (“Dave's Septic”) with operations in Manchester, NH and servicing the Greater Boston metro area. We would like to extend a warm welcome to all Dave's Septic customers. We look forwarding to providing Easy, Safe & Clean services for all of your needs from septic pumping to portable restroom rentals.
With USS, you now have access to an expanded line of temporary site services including temporary fencing, roll off dumpsters and the nations largest fleet of portable restroom trailers and shower trailers for any application. In addition to these new services you will continue to have portable toilets, hand wash sinks, waste holding tanks and septic system services available for your needs, as you have come to expect as a Dave's Septic customer.

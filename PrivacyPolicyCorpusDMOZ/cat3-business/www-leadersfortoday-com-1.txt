The First String and Leaders for Today Join B.E. Smith
As The First String and Leaders for Today have joined forces with B.E. Smith, an AMN Healthcare Leadership Solutions Company, we would like to take this time to welcome new visitors to our site.
B.E. Smith will continue to provide the same excellent support you have come to know and expect from The First String and Leaders for Today, along with greater access to recruitment specialists who can help clients and candidates find the right match for their specific organizational or career needs.
Benefits of Working with B.E. Smith
This integration will enable us to better serve our clients with advanced technology, dedicated resources, and larger candidate pools.
Some of these increased capabilities also benefit our candidates, including:
The largest selection of job postings we have ever been able to provide
A world-class support model solely dedicated to interim leader success
Dedicated travel agents for all interim leader travel needs with 24-hour support
Thank you for visiting our site! We look forward to working with you.

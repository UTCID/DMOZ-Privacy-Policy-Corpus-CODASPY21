PRIVACY POLICY
Air One Equipment, Inc. recognize that you value the privacy of your personal information while visiting and ordering from our website. In order to retain your trust and confidence, we will make every effort to utilize your information to enhance your overall interaction with our companies.
We collect information on browser type, pages visited and information volunteered by the consumer, such as email addresses of those who place orders. The information that we collect will be used to improve our website and your shopping experience.
We may also use the information to provide with special product offerings and other marketing information. We will not share your information with other organizations for commercial purposes.
If you do not want to receive email from us in the future, please inform us by sending an email to airone@aoe.net. If you provide us with your postal address when placing an order, you may receive mailings from us of our current promotions. If you do not want to receive such mailings, please email your request or write to us as Air One Equipment, Inc., 360 Production Drive, South Elgin, IL 60177.
Customers who provide their telephone number when placing an order will only receive telephone contact from us regarding their online order.

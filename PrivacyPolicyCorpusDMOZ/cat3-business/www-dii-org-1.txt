Privacy Policy
The Defense Industry Initiative (“DII”) is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust, and recognizes your right to keep personal information private. We reserve the right to change our privacy policy at any time. We will collect no personal information about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information to us. Any personal information provided to DII, including or similar to name, address, telephone number and e-mail address, will not be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside of our organization, except as necessary to further the purpose for which you provide the information, with 3rd party providers that we have retained to perform services on our behalf (for example to host our website and send email communications), and to comply with legal requirements.When you visit our site to view any pages or use our online reference materials, you are free to browse our site without entering any personal information and therefore remain anonymous during your visit.While we do not collect identifying information about visitors to our site, we do automatically collect information about your visit for the strict purpose of tracking activity on our site, such as:
Internet domain and IP address from which you access our website;
type of browser and operating system used to access our site;
date and time you access our site;
pages you visit; and
If you linked to our site from another website, the address of that website.

Refund policy
Returns
Within 30 days of purchase online RaceFace will accept returns of all returned with original packaging in unused condition. To initiate the return please fill out the return authorization form below and await contact by email from our Customer Service Team. Please note shipping charges will not be refunded.Please pack and seal your return securely using a tracked and insured carrier service and send to:Fox Factory USA38 Isidor Court, #120Sparks, NV 89441
Please write your RA number provided by our service team on the outside of the box for quicker refunds.

Privacy Policy
We respect the privacy of visitors to this site. No cookies are set as a result of your visit.
We use a standard statistics package that collects bulk information on the number of visitors, their IP addresses, search terms used to find this site, and the addresses of sites referring traffic to us. This information is used to evaluate the site’s search engine performance. We do not analyze or share the addresses of individual visitors.
If you contact us using our contact form, we will use the email address and other information you provide solely to enable us to return your message. We do not share this information with any other entities or sites.
If you sign up for our newsletter, we will send you periodic emails, and that’s it.
When and if you make an online purchase, we collect your contact information so that we can contact you if there is a problem or question regarding your order. We do not share this information with any other entities or sites.
All credit card processing for our online store takes place on PayPal’s secured server. We never see any of your credit card information or PayPal account information, other than whatever address information you give us.

Accounting Specialist
St. Joseph the Worker
Phoenix, Arizona, United States
1 day ago
Icon-Share
Apply Now
Description
Job Summary SJW seeks an organized, detail-oriented Accounting Specialist to provide support to the Director of Finance by assisting with financial documents, analyze information, maintain records, assist with routine accounting and bookkeeping duties. This position requires the individual to be able to take direction and instruction, work independently as well as part of a small team to complete tasks, and have excellent time management skills. Responsibilities
Assist Director of Finance with creating and editing financial documents Maintain accurate and complete records Assist Director of Finance with daily, monthly and annual activities Organize bookkeeping processes for the organization Evaluate budgets and track expenses Reconcile financial books
This job description describes the principal functions of this job and its scope of responsibility, but should not be considered an all-inclusive listing of work requirements. Individuals may perform other duties as assigned.
Requirements
Qualifications
Bilingual Spanish speaking individual a plus Experience in nonprofit accounting and/or bookkeeping Degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field a plu Must possess excellent written and verbal communication skills and strong organization skills Ability to complete work according to deadlines Ability to work on a small team Strong desire to empower homeless, low-income, and disadvantaged individuals Excellent computer skills with knowledge of QuickBooks, Adobe, and MicroSoft Office programs Excellent math skills Excellent attention to detail

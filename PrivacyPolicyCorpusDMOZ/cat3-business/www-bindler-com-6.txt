Cocoa and Chocolate
Bühler Group | Homepage
The challenge when working with chocolate mass lines in giving products a shape that adds esthetic appeal to exquisite flavor.
Our depositors and complete moulding solutions work with chocolate masses and fillings, with or without additional inclusions, as well as aerated masses – all on the same machine. Our Application Center enables you to test new ideas and allows us to develop the technology that can bring them to life, time after time.

Leading the market in chain moulding
Product quality, system availability, variable throughput and proven reliability are key for cost-effective one-shot production. Our ChocoMaster and ChocoMaster Compact chain moulding lines are customized to deliver your bars, tablets and pralines to the highest quality, every time.
Solutions for even the most demanding fillings
Your challenge when making filled products is meeting increasingly stringent product quality requirements with quick changeovers and maximum reliability. Our high-performance moulding lines are proven with even the most difficult high-viscous liquid fillings, producing exquisite quality end products.
Unique shapes and distinctive characters
We are the global market leader in rotating shell production. Our RotoStar solution with innovative rotation technology gives shape to even the most distinctive characters. Your whole range of filled and unfilled hollow figures can be created on just a single line – with a highly scalable output.

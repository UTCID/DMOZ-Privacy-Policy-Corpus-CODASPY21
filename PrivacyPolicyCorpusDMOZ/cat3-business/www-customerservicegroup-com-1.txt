Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
We are committed to protecting your privacy.
We collect personal information in order to respond to your requests and/or register you as a User of the website.
We may send you information about our products and services that we feel will be of interest to you. If you do not wish to receive any unsolicited mail from us you may notify us at any time by emailing info@CustomerServiceGroup.com.
Security
Alexander Communications Group does not make email addresses, phone numbers, or fax numbers available to third parties.
We follow strict security procedures in the storage and disclosure of information that you have given to us and take all reasonable care to prevent any unauthorised disclosure of your data.
Access to certain areas of our site is restricted to those in possession of a user name and unique password. Users are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of their user name and password.
Cookies
You may choose to allow Alexander Communications Group’s system to set a cookie on your computer for your convenience. A cookie is a small data file that is stored by your browser on your computer’s hard disk. The purpose of this cookie is to automate the login process.
Changes and contacts
If we make any changes to our privacy policy, we will notify you by posting any changes on our website so that you will always know what information we collect and how we use it.
Please contact us if you require any further information.

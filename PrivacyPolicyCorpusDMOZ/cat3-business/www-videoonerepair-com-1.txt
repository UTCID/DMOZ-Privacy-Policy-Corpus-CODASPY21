Privacy Policy
Thank you for visiting Video One Repair.
This privacy policy tells you how we use personal information collected at this site. Your personal information will only be used by Video One Repair to directly communicate with you.
We never share your personal information such as name, address, phone number, email address, etc. with any third party and we never will.
Please feel comfortable emailing us anytime. Rest assured your information will only be used for us to respond and communicate
We use cookies on our website to provide targeted advertising services. Meaning that after visiting our website you may receive a targeted ad by us or ads that are more relevant to you.
You can opt out of receiving targeted ads served by us or on our behalf by clicking on the blue icon that typically appears in the corner of the ads we serve. You can set your browser to reject all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is set. Go to your browser’s help menu to learn how to change your cookie preferences.
If you delete your cookies or upgrade your browser after having opted out, you will need to opt out again.Also, if you use multiple browsers or devices you will need to execute this opt out on each browser or device.

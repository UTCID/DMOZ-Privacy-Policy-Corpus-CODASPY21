Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to Patrick Industries. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Patrick Industries collects, and the ways in which Patrick Industries uses that personal information.
Personal Information Collection
Patrick Industries may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:
information about your use of this website (including pages visited, time on each page, etc.)
information that you provide using for the purpose of registering with the website (including name, email, phone number)
information about transactions carried out over this website (including billing address, shipping address, phone number, etc.)
information that you provide for the purpose of subscribing to the website services (including email address, phone number, address, etc.)
any other information that you send to Patrick Industries.
Using Personal Information
Patrick Industries may use your personal information to:
administer this website
personalize the website for you
enable your access to and use of the website services
publish information about you on the website
send to you products that you purchase
supply to you services that you purchase
send you statements and invoices
collect payments from you
send you marketing communications.
Where Patrick Industries discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.
In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Patrick Industries may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.
Securing Your Data
Patrick Industries will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.
Patrick Industries will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers.
Information relating to electronic transactions entered into via this website will be protected by encryption technology.
Cross-Border Data Transfers
Information that Patrick Industries collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which Patrick Industries operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.
In addition, personal information that you submit for publication on the website will be published on the internet and may be available around the world.
You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.
Updating This Statement
Patrick Industries may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.
You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.
Other Websites
This website contains links to other websites.
Patrick Industries is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third party.

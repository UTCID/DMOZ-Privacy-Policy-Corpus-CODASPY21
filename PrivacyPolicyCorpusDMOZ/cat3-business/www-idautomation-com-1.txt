Privacy and Security Statement for IDAutomation
IDAutomation.com, Inc. has created this privacy statement in order
to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses information
about the privacy and security for this website:
Privacy
We do not sell, share, trade, or in any other way, make your name, email
address, or other information available to any other party. Your
order information will be treated as strictly confidential.
Cookies
This site only uses cookies with search engine analytics, if necessary for
website navigation, affiliate tracking (so we know what affiliate to pay for
orders) and in the shopping cart to help us identify how you located our web
site and track order progress. IDAutomation does not share this information with
any third parties.
Order Information
IDAutomation does not store entire customer credit card numbers. Instead, all orders are handled by a credit card processor. All order information is treated as strictly confidential.
Email
IDAutomation will only contact you by email under the following
conditions:
If you place a request for customer service, support or a product price
quote.
Anything relating to the processing or follow up of an order or
unprocessed orders.
After every completed order, receipts are emailed.
If a critical update or security update is made for a product ordered.
If you ordered a subscription and it is about to expire or has expired.
If you subscribe to the
IDAutomation
Newsletter.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. All information on our online order forms is encrypted before transmission to prevent possible interception by a third party. The encryption used with our online order forms is designed to provide government level security for your credit card transactions.
This site contains links to other sites. IDAutomation.com, Inc.
is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such
sites.
General Data Protection Regulation
In compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the following information is gathered by our website:
The
website collects information that is aggregated for analytics purposes (e.g.,
Google Analytics). The aggregated information provides analytics tracking for
the IDAutomation Store.
We collect IP addresses in our store and on the forum
for use in Spam and Abuse management.
Cookies are used to track sales for
resellers and affiliates.
If a user is a registered member of the forum, we will
have collected the email address of the user.
If a user made a purchase through
the store, we will have collected the email address, IP address and any
information that the customer has entered except for any credit card
information. We do not retain any credit card information; it is passed through
to Authorize.net, the credit card processor and blind to our system once the
payment attempt is completed.
Contacting Us
If you have privacy-related questions that are not addressed here, please
contact us.

Privacy Policy
Champion Rescue Tools respects your privacy, and as such all customer data collected is kept secure, confidential, and is protected against unauthorized access. We do not sell, trade, rent, lease, or loan personal information about you to any third party. The information you provide is used to provide sales quotes, process your order, make contact if we have questions about your order, provide ongoing service, and to assist us in making your overall experience with us the best it can be. At times, we will use your contact information to alert you to specific product information or marketing opportunities. Should you wish to not receive such communications from us, please feel free to contact us and we will immediately remove your name from such communications.

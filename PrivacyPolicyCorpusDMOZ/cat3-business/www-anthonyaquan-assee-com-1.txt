Privacy Commitment
When you purchase online from Anthony Aquan-Assee be assured that we respect your privacy and that our shopping cart is safe to use. We will never give or sell your personal information to anyone or any organization. The cart is secured by a SSL (Secure Socket Layer) Certificate.
All payment will be made through PayPal (an e-Bay company).
PayPal automatically encrypts your confidential information from your computer to theirs and is heavily guarded both electronically and physically.
When purchasing through Paypal, the Merchant never receives your credit card information, Paypal handles the transaction for us.

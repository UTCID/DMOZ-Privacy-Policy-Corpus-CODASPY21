Group will not release your name, address, telephone number, emergency information or health data related to a patient's medical history to any third party without our clients consent unless, 1) you expressly direct Roffé Enterprises, Inc. t/a H.H.C. This non-personal information is used to improve the Web site and to better address the needs and interests of our Web site visitors. We will not pay you for what you have written and will be able to use it in whatever ways we feel are appropriate. No personally identifiable information will be linked to the questions or answers. This general data serves to guide us as we improve our site. We may also share aggregate data with partners or other trusted third parties. Your individual identify will not be revealed.
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. Roffé Enterprises, Inc. t/a H.H.C. Group uses special software to protect your privacy. All data is encrypted and transmitted over this internet site is through a secured channel established by an SSL certificate. The in-house storage of your personal information in the Roffé Enterprises, Inc. t/a H.H.C. Group' database is highly secure. Only employees authorized to access your data are able to do so based on a series of permission levels protected by passwords and software algorithms. All employees sign a confidentiality of data agreement at time of employment in which they agree to uphold the confidentiality of any such information. The information is not web accessible and access to the information is restricted. Users may optionally choose to have a cookie written to their hard drive for the sole purpose of remembering their user name for them. No other information will be stored in this optional user name cookie. Our users are given the opportunity to 'opt-out' of having their information used for purposes not directly related to our site at the point where we ask for information.

WAYNE will not use the Personal Information, except as set forth herein, without your permission; provided, however, WAYNE shall own all such Personal Information and maintain copies of such as part of its records.

WAYNE also may make its mailing list available to companies whose products and services might interest you. If you do not wish to receive any such information or to allow WAYNE the right to use your Personal Information as described in this paragraph, please complete the Opt – Out Notice contained in the Privacy Notice below. If you are a customer of WAYNE, further details about the information we collect and how it may be disclosed is contained in our Privacy Notice below.

Cookies
“Cookies” are pieces of data sent from a Web server to a Web browser that enable a Web server to identify users that visit a site hosted by that server. WAYNE may use Cookies in order to identify you to our Site so that WAYNE can provide more customized information and services to you. In general, if you choose, you can identify or disable the use of Cookies by reviewing your browser’s preferences and options; however, the Site will not function correctly if you disable the Cookies.

Veritas Marketing, LLC recognizes that privacy is important. Veritas Marketing may request personal information from you via Internet forms, such as your name, e-mail address, company name, company address, telephone number, etc., to which your response is completely voluntary. Veritas Marketing will use this information for business purposes only, such as providing you with literature and information you request through trade publications, trade shows and direct contact with Veritas Marketing. Veritas Marketing may also use this information to alert you to new products and services.
Veritas Marketing may collects domain information as part of our site use analysis. This data enables us to enhance our web-based offerings by tracking visitor IP addresses, how often visitors view site content and which site content is viewed most often. This information is collected automatically and requires no action on your part.
Veritas Marketing does not share any information collected via the Internet with third parties.
Veritas Marketing provides links to other sites that are not maintained by Veritas Marketing or its partners. Veritas Marketing does not endorse these sites and is not responsible for site content. When you visit these sites, Veritas Marketing encourages you to read individual site privacy policies, as their standards may differ.
Veritas Marketing reserves the right to change, modify or update this policy at any time without notice.
If you have any further questions about Veritas Marketing’s privacy policies, please direct your e-mail to: info@veritasmarketing.com.

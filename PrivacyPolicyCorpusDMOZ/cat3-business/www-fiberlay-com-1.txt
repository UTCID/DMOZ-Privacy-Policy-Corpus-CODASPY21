Privacy & Policy
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).
#1
How do I complete payment?
In order to checkout and complete payment you must be registered and logged in to the website. Only then will the "Check Out" button appear to complete the order.
#2
How do I login?
Customers login by clicking the "Login" button located under the main navigation bar, in the top right of the page.
#3
What information do I use to login?
If you have never logged into your account before, you can use your customer number and your zip code. If you do not have an account with us, you will need to register on the website.
#4
How does a customer see their profile page?
Once logged in they can click on either their username located at the Welcome message, or navigate to "My Account / My Profile", to open their customer account profile.
#5
How does a customer update their password?
In the customer profile you can enter a new password and update your email address. The customer profile is where you can view the status of your account.
#6
How do I view my discount pricing?
You will only be able to view your discount pricing after you have logged in.
#7
I do not see my invoices?
Not everyone is able to view their open invoices yet. We are working on resolving this issue.
#8
Is PayPal your only method of payment?
PayPal is our only online method of payment. However, if you phone in or email your order, we can make arrangements for payment via credit card or check.
#9
Do I have to have a PayPal account to make payment?
No, you are not required to have a PayPal account to complete payment. PayPal also allows you to pay with credit card with out creating an account with them.
#10
The fiberlay_fiberlay.com website is not appearing correctly, I can't access the menus?
The fiberlay_fiberlay.com website requires browser versions Internet Explorer 9+, Chrome 22+, or Firefox 16+. Javascript should also be enabled.

Returns will be accepted within 30 days of purchase. Special orders, closeouts or cut goods are not returnable. Full rolls may be returned. Fiberlay reserves the right to impose a 10% restocking charge (minimum $5.00) on returned orders. Returns paid by credit card will be credited to the customers credit card (less restocking charge). Contact customer service at 800-942-0660, or create a support ticket.
Quantity and Bulk Purchases

This notice discloses the privacy practices for Inserso.com and the information collected by this website.

What information do we gather?
We may collect and process the following information:
Our website collects information about your computer, including your IP address to report on statistics.

Our website collects details of your visits to our website, the pages you view and resources you access or download. We collect and use this analytics information in aggregate form such that it cannot reasonably be manipulated to identify any individual user.

The privacy and confidentiality of your information is a top priority for PDS. This privacy policy will advise you about how we protect your information. Please read this policy carefully. By using the Website, you consent to the collection, use and disclosure of your information in the manner provided in this policy.

However, we will release specific information about you if necessary in connection with services being provided to you or to comply with any valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute or court order.

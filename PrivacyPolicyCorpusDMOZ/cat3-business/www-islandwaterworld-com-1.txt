Privacy Policy
Our commitment to Privacy
Island Water World respects the privacy concerns and needs of our customers. We have developed this Privacy Policy to inform you about our privacy practices for the entire Island Water World website. We assure you that we do not collect information from you unless you provide it to us. Be assured that we do not provide or sell information about our customers or site visitors to anyone except our payment gateway provider for the credit card processing. Please note that our sites contain links to other sites. Island Water World is not responsible for the privacy policies or content regarding these other sites. Because our customers are important to us, we do not participate in bulk email you have not consented to receiving.
Collection of your information
We know that your personal information is very important and we appreciate your trust in Island Water World. We do not collect any information from our visitors to our sites unless that visitor explicitly and intentionally provides it. If you are browsing our sites, we do not gather any information about you. If you choose to buy and ship something from our online shop, we will request information from you such as your name, address, email, telephone number and credit card information. When you visit our site we may collect statistical non-personal information about your visit. Information such as: type of browser, domain name, times of visit and products and services in which you are interested.
Use of your information
We use your information when placing an order only to complete the order. We do not share this information with other parties except the extent necessary to complete the order. We use your email address to answer the email we receive. Your email address will not be used and shared by other parties.
Opt-out
If you do not wish to receive special offers and promotional information email from us, please let us know by choosing not to be on our mailing list during registration. Please note that if you choose not to receive promotional emails, we will continue to send you important service related communications.
Protection of children’s privacy
Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important to Island Water World. We do not collect or maintain information at our website from or about children. We do not market to children and our website is not structured to attract children. We do sell children’s products for purchase by adults.
Use of cookies
Cookies are pieces of information that our website sends to your browser and which then is stored on you computer. You may disable or delete cookies at any time. Cookies let us personalize your shopping experience based or your preferences. We do not control the use of cookies by third parties.
Security
We consider the protection of all personally identifiable information we receive from our website visitors as critical to our corporate mission. We have security measures in place to protect against the loss and misuse of any information we receive from you.
Access and correct your information
You can access and correct your personal information online. To protect you privacy, we will take steps to verify your identity before making corrections.

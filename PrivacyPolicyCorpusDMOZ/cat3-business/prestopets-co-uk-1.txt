Who we are
Our website address is: https://www.prestopets.co.uk
As from 25th May 2018 in accordance with The General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR), we confirm that we do not share your information with any third parties. We use the postal address and/or email address that you provided to notify you of our weekly stock lists, and offers. You can unsubscribe anytime from any of our communications by contacting us or simply clicking on the unsubscribe link that is visible on the bottom of our weekly email broadcast.
Any details that are taken for credit card payments of our invoices are held securely and then destroyed within four weeks.
BRIEF BACKGROUND OF PRESTOPETS
Prestopets has over 40 years experience of importing and distributing ornamental fish.
Our trained staff are always on hand with expert advice and provide a tailored service to every customer.
We also offer competitive and efficient wholesale, factor and consolidation rates for coldwater fish and tropical fish.
Prestopets uses extensive industry knowledge to buy a wide variety of stock from around the world. We use the best sources of supply available and keep a large range of fish in our stocking facility.
Many of our fish are tested at independent laboratories in the UK and overseas. Regular checks are also carried out by CEFAS at point of import and at the Prestopets facility.

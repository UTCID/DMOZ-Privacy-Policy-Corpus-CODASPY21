Privacy Policy
We take your privacy very seriously.
In order for you to better understand our data collection methods, we have compiled the following list:
1. Website Forms
When you fill out a form on our website we may require you to provide your
e-mail address or other information.
This information is used only to
contact you.
We do not make this information available to any third parties.
2. Newsletter
If you elect to join our newsletter we will send you a notification once a month
about new services, products and special offers.
For your convenience,
each announcement contains instructions on how to be removed from all future mailings.
You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time and we do not make email addresses
available to any third parties.
3. Cookies
Our site uses "cookies" which are small pieces of information that your browser
stores on your computer's hard drive. The cookies are also used to track website analytics and metrics for marketing purposes.
Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can
usually set your browser to refuse cookies.
If you refuse or disable our
cookie, you will still be able to use our web site.

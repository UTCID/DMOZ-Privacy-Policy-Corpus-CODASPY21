Privacy Policy
We are committed to protecting your privacy at Merchant Account Services.
We will not collect any personal information from you that you do not volunteer, and we are
the sole owner of all information collected on this site. We do not sell, share, or rent
this information to others in any way.
Cookies
Merchant Account Services does not use cookies anywhere on its site as a regular practice.
However, we do use cookies when a user has rated an article so as to prvent an article from being rated
multiple times by one user.
Security
We use reasonable precautions to keep the personal information you disclose when you
contact us. We store this information on only one computer that is not connected to the
Internet full-time.
Obtaining Your Consent
By using any of our web forms, forums and blog, you give
Merchant Account Services permission to use your name and email address unless
explicitly declared by you.

Charlwood Design is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. There is no need to reveal any personal information online whilst looking at our site. Any personal information you choose to give to us will only be used to clarify communication with Charlwood Design and will not be shared with third parties.
We will only record your email address if you send us a message. Your e-mail address will only be used by Charlwood Design for the purpose for which you have provided it, and will not be added to a mailing list or used for any other purpose without your consent. We will only distribute information via email to you if you have asked to receive it. If you are receiving such information and do not wish to receive this information any longer, we will remove your details from our mailing list upon request. We will not disclose your e-mail address without your consent.
Charlwood Design is committed to ensuring the security of your information. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure and ensure the appropriate use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect.

Boston Asset Management Privacy Policy
Effective Date: October 9, 2014
At Boston Asset Management, we are committed to protecting and preserving your privacy. Boston Asset Management is the sole owner of any information collected via its website, mailings, off-site events, or marketing activities. Any information collected by Boston Asset Management will not be rented, sold, or traded to any outside sources. This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used and disclosed by Boston Asset Management when you access or use our website or otherwise interact with us.
Collection and Use of Information
Boston Asset Management does want to communicate information that is relevant to you. We do this through our website. The information we collect while you are on our website allows us to communicate information to you that is personalized to your interests and needs.
As you use the Boston Asset Management website we may collect information about you including the following:
Forms: Our forms require you to give us information such as your name, email address, subject, and products and services interested in. We use customer information from this form to send you information about our services. The customer’s contact information is also used to contact the visitor when necessary or requested.
Log Information: We collect information about your use of our website including the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address and the page you visited before navigating to our website.
Device Information: We collect information about the computer or mobile device you use to access our website, including hardware model, operating system and version, mobile network information and unique device identifiers.
Location Information: We may collect information about your location when you access our website.
Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: OLMFG.com uses “cookies” which are small text files that are stored on your computer to keep track of information related to your use. We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about you and your interactions with our website including browsing behavior and engagement with our website. This information is used to provide you with an experience on our website that is relevant to your interests and needs. Although many website browsers are set to accept cookies, you can set your website browser to reject cookies, but this may prevent you from taking full advantage of our website features or receiving personalized information and services.
Information that you give us may be combined with other personally identifiable information (such as updated address and demographic information) from third-party sources to make our marketing efforts more efficient. Examples of other information we receive from third-party sources include updated delivery and address information, which we use to correct our records and deliver your next mailing more easily.
We use the information we collect for a variety of purposes, including to:
Assist you with your wealth and asset management needs
Personalize your experience with our website to be relevant to your interests and needs
Provide, maintain and improve the website
Monitor and analyze the website usage and trends
While on our website, you may choose to fill out a form requesting more information.
Sharing of Information
Boston Asset Management is the sole owner of any information collected via its website, mailings or marketing activities. Any information collected by Boston Asset Management will not be rented, sold, or traded to any sources outside of Boston Asset Management. Boston Asset Management may share information with you from selected companies that we believe offer products or services of interest to you and are complimentary to your relationship with Boston Asset Management. We will not share your information with these companies; all such communications will originate from Boston Asset Management.
Your Choices
Cookies: You can set your website browser to reject cookies, but this may prevent you from taking full advantage of our website features or receiving personalized services.
Social Sharing Features
The website may offer social sharing features or other integrated tools, which let you share actions you take on the website with other media, and vice versa. The use of such features enables the sharing of information with your friends or the public, depending on the settings you establish with the third party that provides the social sharing feature. For more information about the purpose and scope of data collection and processing in connection with social sharing features, please visit the privacy policies of the third parties that provide these features.
California Privacy Rights
Under California law, California residents who have an established business relationship with Boston Asset Management may opt out of Boston Asset Management disclosing personal information about them to third parties for their marketing purposes. If you do not want Boston Asset Management to disclose information to other companies for their marketing purposes, please contact Boston Asset Management at (877) 484-1070 or via postal mail at Boston Asset Management, 4912 Creekside Drive, Clearwater, FL 33760.

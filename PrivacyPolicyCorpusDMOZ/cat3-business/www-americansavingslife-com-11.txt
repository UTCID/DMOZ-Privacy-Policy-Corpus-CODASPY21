Ever get tired of always having to mail in your payment?
Ever worry about not getting your payment in on time?
Sign up for AutoPay and let us worry about it.

When you are done, staple a voided check to the application and mail it to us or drop it off at:
American Savings Life Insurance Company
935 East Main Street, Suite 100
Mesa, AZ 85203
OR BY FAX:
Alternatively, you can fax us the completed AutoPay Form & a copy of your voided check.
Send the fax to: (480) 835-5355 Attention: AutoPay

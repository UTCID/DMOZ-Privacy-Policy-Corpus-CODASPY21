Do we keep your data?
We keep your data:
-  for as long as we need to in order to use it as described above in section 4;and/or,-  for as long as we have your permission to keep it, such as using it to send you our newsletter or other marketing materials;and/or,-  if you are an active client.

Personally identifiable data that you submit through our website will not be stored or transferred outside of the European Economic Area (“the EEA”) (The EEA consists of all EU member states, plus Norway, Iceland and Liechtenstein).
Do We Share Your Data?
No, Link Microtek Ltd will never share your data with any third-party.

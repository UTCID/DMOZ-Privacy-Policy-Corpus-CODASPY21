ETPS only requires your telephone number as an alternate method of contact. By browsing the ETPS website you are agreeing to our use of cookies.
Cookies
Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored on your computer.
This website uses first-party cookies to complete the ordering process for quotations. ETPS uses cookies to provide you the user with a tailored experience on our website.
You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. If you've set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use this site.
Cookies are also used by this website for Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to help the website operators analyse how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of ETPS's website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website and compiling reports on website activity. Google may transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Google states that it will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google.
ETPS will NEVER use your email for spam, or sell it to third party companies. A cookie can't read data from your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites.
If you have any queries regarding ETPS's Privacy Policy, or if you have any other queries, please contact us.
Server Logs
This website automatically collects and stores certain information in server logs pertaining to our services or content. This may include:-
Details of searches and content requested from our server
Internet protocol (IP) address
Cookies that may uniquely identify browsers (if allowed and accepted)
Device event information such as hardware settings, agent headers, browser settings, language, operating system, the date and time of requests and the referral URL
These logs are routinely cleared and no third-party tools analyse this data in any way.

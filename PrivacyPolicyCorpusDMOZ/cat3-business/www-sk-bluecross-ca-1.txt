Privacy
Saskatchewan Blue Cross is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and protecting their personal information.
Our privacy policy outlines how we handle and store records in all formats. It also outlines how employees access records maintained and held by Saskatchewan Blue Cross.
If you have any questions about our privacy policy or its application, please contact us.

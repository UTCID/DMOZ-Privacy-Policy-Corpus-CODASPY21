Astro Wood Stake recognizes the importance of and respects your privacy.  The information collected is in no way shared or sold to 3rd parties and is handled safely and securely. Only the employees managing your information and the contracted service provider hosting Astro's website has access to your information.  Your information is used exclusively for the sole purpose of shipping, billing and updating you on our special offers at your own request.
This web site contains links to other sites. Astro Wood Stake, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites.  We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any sites you may enter that requires personal information.
Your account page is protected by a password of your own choice. For your protection, it is important that you keep your password in a safe place and we advise you not to allow your computer to remember the password for you. Forgotten passwords can easily be replaced.

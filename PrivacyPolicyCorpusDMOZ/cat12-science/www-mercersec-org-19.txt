If you are a sponsor or potential sponsor then you have come to the right place.
The Mercer Science & Engineering Club hosts this site and all of the competitions outlined on this site including the annual Mercer Science and Engineering Fair in conjunction with Rider University.
The club is recognized by the IRS as a 501(c)3 charitable non-profit organization, and a copy of the IRS determination letter is attached. The Tax ID number of the Mercer Science & Engineering Club is: 20-1346005.

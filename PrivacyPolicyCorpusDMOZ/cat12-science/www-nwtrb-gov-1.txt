NWTRB Privacy PolicyThe
Nuclear Waste Technical Review Board will collect no personal information
about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that
information. Only the
visitor's Internet domain and Internet Protocol address, the type of
browser and operating system used to access the site, the file visited and
the time spent in each file, and the time and date of the visit are automatically collected.If you have comments about the NWTRB privacy policy contact NWTRB.

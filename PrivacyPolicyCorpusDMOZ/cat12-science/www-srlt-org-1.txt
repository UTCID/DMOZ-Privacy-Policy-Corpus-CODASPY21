Cookies
To enrich and perfect your online experience, Scenic Rivers Land Trust uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Scenic Rivers Land Trust uses cookies to help Scenic Rivers Land Trust identify and track visitors, their usage of https://srlt.org/, and their website access preferences. Scenic Rivers Land Trust visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Scenic Rivers Land Trust’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Scenic Rivers Land Trust’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Scenic Rivers Land Trust’s use of cookies.

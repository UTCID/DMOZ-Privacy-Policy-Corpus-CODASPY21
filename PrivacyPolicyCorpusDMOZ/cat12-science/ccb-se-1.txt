Coalition Clean Baltic > CCB Travel Policy
The CCB Travel Policy is meant to guide the travelers and the staff administering the travel in making appropriate judgments about the use of the CCB Secretariat’s travel funds.
The text was adopted at the Board Meeting on 16-17 May 2019 in Baltezers, Latvia.

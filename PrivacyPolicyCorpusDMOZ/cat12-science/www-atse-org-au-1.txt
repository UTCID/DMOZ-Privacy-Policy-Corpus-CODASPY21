Research & policy
Australian Academy of Technology & Engineering
We apply science, technology and engineering expertise to solve the big issues facing our nation in a fast-changing world.
The Academy contributes robust and practical thinking to Australia’s big debates.
Big issues
Getting Australia technology ready
The rate of change never slackens – from driverless cars to artificial intelligence, from biomedical advances to new power sources. We’re working to ensure Australia stays at the cutting edge.
Find out more
The climate change challenge
Technology has a big part to play in cutting greenhouse gas emissions and mitigating the impact of global warming on our cities and regions.
Find out more
The changing world of work
Robots, algorithms, Big Data – the tide of automation is heading this way. We’re developing strategies to meet the challenge.
Find out more
No one left behind
Rapid change empowers many in our society but risks leaving some behind. We need strategies to protect our social fabric.
Find out more

Collection of your Personal
Information
For each visitor to our Web page, our Web server automatically
collects certain information. This information may include: your IP
address, browser type, operating system, domain name, access times
and referring Web site addresses. This information is used for the
operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to
provide general statistics regarding use of QwickLoad.com.
QwickLoad.com also collects information about which pages
our customers visit within QwickLoad.com. This site
visitation data is identified only by a unique ID number, and it is
not  linked with personal information.
Use of your Personal Information
The general information we
collect is used to improve the content of our Web page and to
customize the content and/or layout of our page for each individual
visitor.
Control of your Personal Information
QwickLoad.com may
send out periodic e-mails informing you of special events, updates,
new releases or other notifications. You will not be able to choose
to unsubscribe to these mailings, as they are considered an
essential part of the service provided to QwickLoad
customers.
Enforcement of this Privacy Statement
If you feel that this site
is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us
using the contact information provided below.
Changes to this Statement
QwickLoad.com will
occasionally update this privacy statement.  When we do, we
will also revise the "last updated" date at the top of the privacy
statement.
Contact Information

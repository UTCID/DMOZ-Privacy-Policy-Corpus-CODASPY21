The annual Mercer Science and Engineering Fair is affiliated with two competitions that are sponsored by the Society for Science and the Public. These are:
Regeneraon Internationl Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF)
Open to grades 9 through 12
Our Senior division grand prize winner gets to compete at ISEF, all expenses are paid by the club and its sponsors
ISEF competition is each May after MSEF that occurs in March
Broadcom Masters Competition
Open to grades 6 through 8
Top winners of our Junior division move on to compete
All finalist projects are reviewed online
The top 50 finalists get to compete in the final competition

No personal data is collected on this website, except for information you supply voluntarily via the “Contact Us” form or similar pages. This information is not passed on to advertisers, nor used for any purpose other than to supply you with the information you request about our organization.
This site uses cookies only to give us an understanding of how it is navigated by visitors—for instance, which sections are visited most often, or how many of the site’s hits are repeat visitors—so we can continually make improvements to the site’s design, layout, and messaging. Most web browsers provide the option to disable cookies. If you wish to do this, refer to your browser’s help menu.
If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, contact webmaster@aaa.org

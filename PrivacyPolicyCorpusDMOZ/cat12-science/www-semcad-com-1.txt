Overview
SEMCAD X Matterhorn
SEMCAD X Matterhorn full-wave 3D electromagnetic (EM) simulation software offers a novel suite of seamlessly integrated solutions tailored to address a variety of engineering challenges. It is suitable for simulations of fields of all frequencies – from direct current (DC) to extremely low frequency (ELF) to microwaves to light – in support of a wide range of applications, such as safety assessment, EM interference and compatibility (EMI/EMC), antenna design and optimization, 5G, wireless power transfer (WPT), dosimetry, optics, high-performance computing (HPC), and design of microwave and mm-wave waveguide devices.
SEMCAD X Matterhorn or Sim4Life?
In many respects, SEMCAD X Matterhorn is identical to Sim4Life, the leading computational life sciences platform. The two software products share the same code base and differ by the computational modules they include by default. They are fully compatible one with the other: i.e., SEMCAD X simulation projects can be opened and run within Sim4Life. Please contact us for inquiries related to the Sim4Life upgrade.

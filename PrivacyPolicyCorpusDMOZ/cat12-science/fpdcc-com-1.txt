Information Collected by the Forest PreservesIn order to improve the content and usability of Forest
Preserves websites, the Forest Preserves automatically collects basic website
browsing information from Forest Preserves website users.The
Forest Preserves uses this information to identify site performance needs, to
ensure compatibility with the technology used by the Forest Preserves website
visitors, and to generally add and improve services offered on the Forest Preserves
websites.  The
Forest Preserves does not collect data for commercial purposes and does not
sell, exchange or otherwise distribute the data collected by the Forest
Preserves websites for commercial purposes.2. Information Submitted to the Forest Preserves WebsitesDuring a session on the Forest Preserves websites, a user may
affirmatively submit information to the Forest Preserves by, among other means,
electronic mail (“email”) or by filling in and submitting an online
form or by allowing map.fpdcc.com to track your location. Such affirmatively

Forest Preserves does not rent or sell personally identifiable information
(i.e., information such as name, address, phone number, email, etc., that

exchange or trade such information with third parties without a user’s explicit
permission. 3. CookiesCookies are text files, or entries in larger files, utilized to
distinguish between visitors to a website, and to track information during
multiple visits to a website.  Cookie storage can be either temporary
(“Session”) or permanent (“Persistent”).  A Session cookie is deleted when
you exit your web browser and a Persistent cookie remains on your hard drive
until you manually delete it or the cookie expires.  The Forest Preserves
may use both types of cookies to improve the content and usability of Forest Preserves
websites.The
use of cookies is a standard practice among Internet websites. Most Internet
web browsers may be customized to reject cookies, to only accept or reject
cookies by user intervention, or to delete cookies. Rejecting and/or removing cookies,
however, may lead to loss of functionality on those Forest Preserves website
pages requiring cookies to function fully.4. Third Party ServicesIn order to improve the content and usability of Forest
Preserves websites, the Forest Preserves enlists the services of third
parties.  In such cases, the third party’s use of Forest Preserves website
user data is restricted by and subject to the third party’s own Terms of
Service and Privacy Policy. 5. ChildrenNone of the Forest Preserves websites specifically solicit information from minors or seeks to determine whether the visitor is a minor.

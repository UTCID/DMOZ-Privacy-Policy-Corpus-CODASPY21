Working for us
The Australian Academy of Technology and Engineering abides by the Code of Conduct to which all Academy personnel must adhere.
The Code of Conduct outlines the obligations and standards of behaviour expected from all employees to comply and contribute to the achievement of a professional and productive work culture characterised by the absence of unlawful and unacceptable workplace behaviours.
Corporate conduct and behaviour
Maintain confidentiality of Academy and related information
Remain openly accountable for its actions within the framework of the Academy’s responsibility to Academy Fellows, government and other stakeholders
Disclose and avoid any conflict of interest
Comply with all relevant laws of Australia
Provide a workplace in which employment decisions are based on merit and value equity, diversity and inclusion
Provide a workplace that treats all employees fairly and is free from discrimination and harassment
Remain responsive to the Fellows in providing frank, honest, comprehensive, accurate and timely advice
Provide leadership of the highest quality
Establish workplace relations that value communication, consultation, co-operation and input from employees on matters that affect their workplace
Provide a fair, safe and rewarding workplace
Focus on achieving results and managing performance
Personal conduct
Act ethically, fairly, honestly and with integrity
Respect co-workers, Fellows, stakeholders, suppliers and other service providers
Act with due care and diligence
Treat everyone with courtesy and avoid all forms of bullying, harassment and discrimination
Comply with all applicable Australian laws and company policies
Comply with any lawful and reasonable direction given by the Academy’s management
Maintain appropriate confidentiality about the Academy’s dealings
Not make improper use of inside information or power or authority to gain or seek to gain a benefit or advantage for the employee of for any other person
At all times behave in a way that upholds the Academy’s values, integrity and good reputation.
Fellows of the Academy
This Code of Conduct applies to Fellows of the Academy while representing the Academy in an official capacity and/or engaging directly with Academy employees.
Other Academy policies and procedures that apply to Fellows officially representing the Academy and engaging directly with staff are the Academy’s Workplace Equal Opportunity, Harassment and Bullying Policy and Privacy Policy.
For more information, email governance@atse.org.au.

Privacy Policy
We recognize that privacy of your personal information is
important. Here is information on what types of information we
receive and collect when you visit us. We never sell your
information to third parties.
Log Files
As with most other websites, we collect and use the data
contained in log files. The information in the log files include
your IP (internet protocol) address, your ISP (internet service
provider, such as AOL or Shaw Cable), the browser you used to
visit our site (such as Internet Explorer or Firefox), the time
you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our
site.
In addition to internal data-gathering software, we may also
use third-party traffic statistics services such as Google
Analytics.
Cookies
We may use cookies to improve functionality of certain features
on our site. We do not use cookies to gather personal
information or browsing habits.
You can chose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies
or third-party cookies in your browser settings. However, this
can affect how you are able to interact with our site as well as
other websites.
Contact and Quote Forms
All information collected by us that you submit through a form
on this site is used only to fulfill your request. All our
visitor information is held in strict confidence. We do not send
unsolicited messages with product offers or links to websites.
We cannot be responsible for the policies or content of other
sites, even if we link to them.

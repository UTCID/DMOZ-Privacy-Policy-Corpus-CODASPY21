Some of our services allow you to make certain information public or accessible to certain individuals. If you make use of these services Airsweb will share your information as you request.What are Cookies and Why do We Use ThemCookies are a standard Internet technology, and many major web sites use them to provide useful features for their members. Cookies allow us to store and retrieve login and other information on a user’s system. They provide us with data that we can use to improve our service to you. Cookies themselves do not personally identify users, although they do identify a user’s computer. The default setting for most browsers is to accept cookies; if you prefer you can set yours to refuse them.

Heinz College Policy Impact
From Washington, D.C. to Silicon Valley, Heinz College experts create and inform policies that will shape the future of our nation, society, and world.
Whether it's driving groundbreaking research,speaking on expert panels, testifying before Congress and other government bodies, elevating the use of technology in the public policy sphere, testing new ideas and programs, or sharingperspectives in the media,Heinz College's cross-disciplinary thought leaders are out in front on the most pressing issues of our time.From health care and trade to criminal justice and national security, thereis no shortage of consequential policy debates in the United States and abroad. Heinz Collegeconfronts such complexproblems withnovel approachesand innovativemethodologies guided by evidence.Heinz College doesn't just participate intheseconversations. Welead them, shift them, and shape them.

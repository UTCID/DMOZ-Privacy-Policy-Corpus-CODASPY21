Web Site Privacy Policy
The Erie Zoo is committed to protecting the privacy of its online guests, customers, members, and patrons. This privacy policy applies to transactions made, and data gathered, on the Erie Zoo web sites. By using the Zoo web sites or communicating with the Zoo by email, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
Privacy Policy
The Erie Zoo web site offers broad public access to and use of a wide range of information comprised of data, images, and text files to visitors of all ages. We are dedicated to safeguarding all information collected online.
To help ensure a rewarding online experience for all visitors, the Zoo provides this summary of our information practices. This privacy notice governs the collection and use of information on our web sites.
As the zoo continues to offer our visitors new and different types of content and services, we may modify our practices from time to time. However, we will treat all personal information collected in accordance with the privacy notice in effect at the time the information is collected.
Information We Collect
When you order from the Erie Zoo, we collect your name, billing address, shipping address, email address, phone number, and credit card number, including expiration date and v-code. We also gather information on the person who will receive any gift you send (e.g., that person's name and address) and we will retain a record of your purchases.
How We Use the Information
The Erie Zoo uses visitor's personal information for internal purposes only. We may also use personal information provided by shoppers to send them, by email or direct mail, notification of special offers or sale events. We may also use your contact information to notify you about upcoming events and or exhibitions. If you do not want to receive information, you will have the opportunity to opt out.
We never sell, rent, or otherwise share our visitor's personal information with third parties. We will disclose information we maintain as required by law, for example, in response to a court order or a subpoena. We also may disclose such information in response to a specific law enforcement agency's request.

Security Policy:
Because your credit card security is a high priority, we have taken numerous steps to ensure your payment information is processed confidentially, accurately, and securely. Our site uses encryption technology, such as Secure Sockets Layer (SSL), to protect your credit card information during data transport. SSL encrypts ordering information such as your name, physical address, and credit card number.
In order to process a return from your credit card please call the Zoo at 814-864-4091.
To provide you with an increased level of security online, when you register for an online account you also select a password to access your personal information. The Erie Zoo will not ask you for your password in an unsolicited communication.

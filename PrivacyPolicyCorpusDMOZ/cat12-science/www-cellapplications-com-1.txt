Privacy
Some webpages found in cellapplications.com require that information, called "cookies," be placed on a visitor's computer. Cookies do not collect personally identifiable information, but allow us to determine which pages in our site are visited, and at what frequency. Cell Applications, Inc. collects personally identifiable information, such as names, addresses, e-mail addresses and the like, only when voluntarily provided by a site visitor.
If customers provide further feedback to Cell Applications, Inc. the information shall be deemed non-confidential and non-proprietary. Cell Applications, Inc. is free to reproduce and disclose information, ideas, protocols, or techniques contained in feedback for any purpose, without limitation.

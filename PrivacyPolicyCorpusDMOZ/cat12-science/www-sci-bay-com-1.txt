Shipping
Shipping cost is determined at the point of shipping by using UPS ground rate tables (combination of weight and dimensions). Actual shipping cost plus $$ packing is added to the invoice.
Request expedited shipping in the order comment box if desired.
In case of truck shipments, please call for carrier discounts and transit time.

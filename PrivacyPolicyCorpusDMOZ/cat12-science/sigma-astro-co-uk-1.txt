Privacy Notice
Data Protection – Use of the contact information you provide to SIGMA Moray’s Astronomy Club (SIGMA)
The data provided to SIGMA by you will be held in accordance with EU General Data Protection Regulation.
Individuals can request removal of their data at any time by contacting the Membership Secretary ( membership@sigma-astro.co.uk ).
Your contact details will be shared with the appointed committee members of SIGMA as required by them to perform their duties.
No-one outside of SIGMA will have access to any information we hold about you.
Data will be stored for two purposes only:
• Communication with members about their membership and associated matters, and
• To enable distribution of information about SIGMA activities, and any other related correspondence.

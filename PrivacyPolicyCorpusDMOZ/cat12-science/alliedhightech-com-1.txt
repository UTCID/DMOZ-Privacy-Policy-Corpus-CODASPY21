Privacy Policy
At Allied High Tech Products, Inc., we know that you care about the security of your information. We value your trust, and make every effort to safeguard the information you provide to us. That's why we observe the following guidelines with regard to your privacy:
What we do with the information you provide to us: We receive and securely store any information you enter on our web site or provide to us in written or verbal form. We use the information that you provide for purposes including: responding to your requests, filling your online orders, answering your questions, and facilitating communication with you.
Who we share your information with: Other than information required by those with whom we interact in the normal execution of your business wishes, we do not divulge, sell or distribute customer information in any way.
How we secure your information: We work to protect the security of your information during transmission by using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts the information you input. Of note: it is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password(s) and your computer. Be sure to sign off after using a shared computer.

Privacy is very important to us!
We would like to take a moment and share with you some details about how we treat your personal information.
Certain information that you enter into this website may be viewed by others, such as if they visit your personal profile page or by viewing the member map if you so choose to participate in those areas of the website.
Like any other forum or discussion style / community driven website, the words you enter into parts of the website can be viewed as part of the ongoing discussions that can be viewed by just about everyone that visits the site.

Getting Started
If you are a teacher or administrator then you have come to the right place. If you are a mentor or interested parent then this is also a good starting point. If you are a student or are looking for student information then check out For Students page.
The links below will get you started depending upon what you need to do and what you may or may not know about our events.
Our Fairs and Events - information about our competitions
How to do a project - best place for students to get started
Fair registration help - how to register for the fair, useful if you are registering your students
You may also want to:
Register for a competition - all active events are listed here
Sign up for an account - you need an account on this site to register for a competition, you can also use a teacher account to track applications for students from your school
Contact us - when in doubt, we can be reached via phone and email

Cookies
A "cookie" is a small data text file that is placed in your browser to allow a site to recognize you each time you visit the site for customisation purposes etc. Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information. Mass Spectrometry Instruments Limited uses cookies where essential for correct operation of the site and to track visits to the site. The information collected is used to improve and monitor the use of the website and is not used in any way  to identify individuals. Cookies may also be used by other sites that link to or from this site.

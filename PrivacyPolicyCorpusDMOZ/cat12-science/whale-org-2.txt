Ocean Alliance Privacy PolicyOcean Alliance has created this Privacy Policy in order to demonstrate our commitment to the privacy and protection of your information. As part of the normal operation of services, we collect and, in some instances, disclose information about you. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. 1. Information Collected During Your Use of the Whale.org / Ocean Alliance website. – Certain information, such as ‘cookies’ and other related server reporting is for use in our records.2. Our Use of Your Information – We use customer contact information from the registration form to send you information about Ocean Alliance and promotional material from some of our partners. Your contact information is also used to contact you when necessary. We may use your IP address to help protect our partners and us from fraud.3.

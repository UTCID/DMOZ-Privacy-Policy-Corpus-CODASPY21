Thank you for visiting the Inventors Network of The Capital Area’s website and reviewing our privacy policy. Our privacy policy is simple: we collect no personal information about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information to us. Any personal information you do choose to share with us is kept confidential and is not shared with any third parties.

Information you post to our Google™ Group is subject to the rules and regulations set by Google™. Also note that posting to our Google™ Group makes public any information you share in your post.

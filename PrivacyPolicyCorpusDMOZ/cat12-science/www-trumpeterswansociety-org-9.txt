Pick a ProjectWould you like to support a particular project helping Trumpeter Swans?
Below is a brief summary of how your one-time or monthly "Pick a Project" donation will be used.
Area of Greatest Need
Your donation will be used where it is needed to help swans the most.
Adopt A Swan- health and habitat
Your "adoption" is symbolic. Your donation will be used to support general swan health and habitat issues.
Genetic Testing of Zoo swans
This project supports genetic testing of zoo Trumpeter Swan breeding pairs for best placement in Flyway projects having genetic restrictions.
Lead Poisoning- Get the Lead Out
Your donation will be used for education and outreach to raise awareness about the serious, usually fatal, impacts of lead poisoning on Trumpeter Swans and other wildlife.
North American Swan Fund
Your donation to the North American Swan fund will help fund vital research and special swan habitat projects.
Oregon Restoration Project
Your donation will restore Trumpeter Swans back to Oregon through the Oregon Restoration Project. The Project is a partnership between TTSS, Oregon Department of Fish & Wildlife, and the United States Fish & Wildlife Service.
Membership and Gift Membership
Your membership donation supports all our programs and activities across North America.

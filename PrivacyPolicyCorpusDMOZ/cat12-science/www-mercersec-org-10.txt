So you are planning on doing a science or engineering fair project. Excellent.
These are the basic steps for doing a project for our fair. Some are only required in the Junior and Senior division (*). Not all steps need to be done in the order listed.
Confirm eligibility for this event
Decide on a topic, research it and develop a hypothesis/purpose
Start online application (this can be done later)
Create a research plan
Deal with ISEF forms - only for events like the Mercer Science and Engineering Fair
Complete the project
Write your abstract
Create and upload your presentation materials
Finalize online application
For some events: Print forms from online application to bring to fair with your project

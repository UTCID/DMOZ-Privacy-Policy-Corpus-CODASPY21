Policy and research hub
Policy and research hubWe commission and contribute to a range of policy and research activities covering emerging technologies, new sources of fuel and issues affecting energy policy. Through this work and collaboration with key industry partners, we offer policy makers access to essential information and expertise, helping them shape their responses to policy issues impacting the gas industry and its consumers.

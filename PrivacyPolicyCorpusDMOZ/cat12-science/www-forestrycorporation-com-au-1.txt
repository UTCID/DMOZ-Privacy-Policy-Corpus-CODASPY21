Ensuring PrivacyForestry Corporation of NSW complies with the Australian Privacy Principles as outlined in the Privacy Act 1988 (Act), the Privacy Amendment (Enhancing Privacy Protection) Act 2012.Forestry Corporation understands and respects your personal information is important to you and we are committed to maintaining your privacy. Our Privacy Policy provides information on how we collect and manage personal information.Forestry Corporation will only collect personal information which is reasonably necessary for its dealings with relevant individuals and for the purpose specified or which is reasonably apparent at the time the information is collected.Forestry Corporation will take reasonable steps to protect any personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.If you would like more information on how we manage your personal information, you can contact our Privacy Officer at:Forestry Corporation of NSW, Privacy OfficerPO Box 100Beecroft  2119T:  02 9872 0111F:  02 9873 1048E:  privacy@fcnsw.com.auFor details related to personal information collected through use of our website please refer to the NSW Trade and Investment Privacy Statement and consider the privacy statements displayed when you access any online systems through our website.

Privacy Policy
This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for W.I.S., which is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement.
W.I.S. reserves the right to retain all messages received from individual clients. The information supplied will not be misused or distributed in any way without the permission of the client. Messages are protected and accessed only in legally justified cases and only when required from any Governmental Authority. Any personal detail and/or information collected by W.I.S. are strictly confidential.
This Web site takes all necessary safety measures to protect the users’ information, and we are using every precaution in place to look after the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our management. W.I.S. takes the security of your personal information very seriously. W.I.S. online services protect your personal information during transit using encryption such as Transport Layer Security (TLS).
Lastly, we are also working with our partner organizations around the globe that may at some point handle personal information gathered by us in the course of our relationship; however, each third party partner has agreed to meet our standards of privacy, confidentiality and security.

The Florida Autism Treatment Centers (FATC) and Behavior Analysis, Inc. are dedicated to maintaining the privacy of our clients. We promise to keep any identifying information fully confidential. This includes data submitted to us via our online platforms, including Facebook contact forms. We also vow to never sell, share with third parties, or use the data you provide for any unauthorized purpose.
Please be aware that consent has been provided by parent(s) or guardian(s) of any children who have their photographs uploaded to our online platforms (including Facebook, Instagram, and our website).
We also ensure that we comply with up-to-date HIPAA regulations. For more information regarding HIPAA compliance, please feel free to contact us.

Policy submissions and reports
Over the years we have utilised the collective expertise of our members to contribute to many government and public consultations in official statistics, education and science. From measuring inflation, digital governance and data ethics to A level mathematics and assessing teaching quality in universities, the policy work we do covers many different areas.
A number of policy reports, on topics ranging from the gender pay gap to ethical data science, are listed on our Reports and guides page.
Key submissions we have made in recent years include:
Letter to Scottish First Minister supporting Pre-Release Access Bill (15 September 2020)
Response to Justice Select Committee Coroner’s Inquiry (September 2020)
RSS Education Policy Advisory Group statement on grade adjustment in UK exams (August 2020)
Letter to the Office for Statistics Regulation to make the case for a review of the models and processes adopted by the UK qualifications regulators (August 2020)
RSS Response to the Education Select Committee call for evidence: The Impact of Covid-19 on education and children's services inquiry (July 2020)
Contribution to UKSA strategy (July 2020)
Response to UKSA-HMT RPI consultation (March 2020)
Response to DCMS consultation on National Data Strategy (July 2019)
RSS response to Treasury Select Committee: Regional Imbalances in the UK Economy inquiry (July 2019)
Response to The Centre for Data Ethics and Innovation call for evidence on online targeting and bias in algorithmic decision making (June 2019)
RSS response to Sir Adrian Smith’s call for evidence on ‘Future Frameworks for International Collaboration on Research and Innovation’ (May 2019)
Response to the independent review of the Teaching Excellence and Student Outcomes Framework (TEF) (Feb 2019)
Response to the Public Administration and Constitutional Affairs Committee (PACAC) 'Governance of Statistics' inquiry (Feb 2019)
RSS evidence to the Economic Affairs Committee on ‘The Use of the Retail Prices Index’ (June 2018)
Response to the Department for Education's Teaching Excellence and Student Outcomes Framework (TEF) Subject-level Consultation (May 2018)
Maths, English and digital requirements: response to UK Government consultation on the implementation of T-level programmes (Feb 2018)
Statement on the inclusion of students in the UK’s international migration statistics (Jan 2018)
Response to the ESRC call for evidence on knowledge and skills (Oct 2017)
Response to the UK Statistics Authority consultation on a refreshed Code of Practice for Statistics (Oct 2017)
Evidence to the Scottish Parliament inquiry on Economic Data (Sept 2017)
Response to ONS consultation on measuring and reporting UK progress against the Sustainable Development Goals (Sept 2017)
Response to BEIS consultation on Industrial Strategy Green Paper (April 2017)
Evidence to the Science and Technology Select Committee's inquiry on algorithms in decision-making (April 2017)
Evidence to the Culture, Media and Sport Select Committee's 'Fake news' inquiry (April 2017)
Response to the Higher Education Funding Council's consultation on the Research Excellence Framework, by the Council for Mathematical Sciences (March 2017)
Response to the British Academy's consultation on Skills in the Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences (March 2017)
Please see our Price indices and the measure of inflation page to see a summary of our work in this area.
The above list is not exhaustive; please contact policy@rss.org.uk if you have any specific queries about any of the issues we have been working on.

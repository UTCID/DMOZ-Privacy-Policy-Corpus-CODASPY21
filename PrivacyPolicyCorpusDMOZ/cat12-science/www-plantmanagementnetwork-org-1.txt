YOUR PRIVACY
The Plant Management Network values your privacy.
The following applies to those who subscribe to the Plant Management Network (PMN) at www.plantmanagementnetwork.org or sign up to receive emails via the PMN Update newsletter.
PMN, via its owner, The American Phytopathological Society, collects only personal information that you provide. Such information is requested as necessary to
enable you to participate in the activities described below.
Subscribe to The Plant Management Network
Your personal subscriber information will not be shared with any third party. The Plant Management Network will use your personal information only regarding your
subscription and to send you Plant Management Network UPDATE, PMN's free e-mail newsletter, from which you may unsubscribe at any time.
Make inquiries and correspond with The Plant Management Network
Inquiries and other correspondence will be forwarded or shared only as needed.
Recipients may include the editorial board, editorial office, business office, and others as warranted, but correspondents will not be added to mailing lists or
the like unless specifically requested. The Plant Management Network forwards correspondence responsibly but is ultimately unable to restrict
subsequent use.

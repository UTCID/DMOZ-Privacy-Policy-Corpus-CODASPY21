Privacy PolicyPrivacy PolicyYour privacy is important to usWe respect your privacy and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy provides information on how we collect and process your personal data through your use of the Magritek website, including any data you may provide us when you request to be contacted, buy something from us, request a quote, sign up for a newsletter or interact in other ways with our website and company. From time to time we may update our Privacy Policy by updating this webpage.What information do we collect?When you use our website, fill out a form, send us an email, or otherwise interact with our company we collect, retain and update the personal information you provided to us. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, the company you work for and their mailing address, product/service interests and preferences or other details provided to us at the time. How do we use the information we collect?We use the information you provide in the following ways:To enable us to meet our contractual obligations if you buy something from us, such as sending information regarding your order or related services.To allow us to better respond to your customer service requests.To send you the content, related product information, services and offerings which you are most likely to be interested in.To help improve our website in order to enhance your experience.To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site feature.We will never sell or pass your personal information to third parties not related to our business, products or services.You will only be contacted by Magritek to inform you about products, services or other matters related to your relationship with Magritek.The basis for processing your information is either with respect to a contract we have with you, for our legitimate business interests (taking into account any potential impact on you both positive and negative), or for us to comply with the law.We use a number of cloud-based software services to administer and run our business. Some of your personal information may be stored in these systems. We only use services that respect your privacy and that do not allow your information to be shared with other users of those services. We will only send your personal information to third parties where it necessary for us to perform the service with respect to your business interests, such as passing a quote request to one of our distributors who is located in the country you are located in. If you have any specific questions about our use of your personal information not answered here, please contact us at CookiesOur website uses cookies, which are small files that a site transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your web browser (if you allow it). Cookies enable our website to recognise your browser, capture and remember certain information and respond to you as an individual. You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies.We use cookies in conjunction with Google Analytics to aggregate data about website interactions to help us improve our service. We have configured Google Analytics to anonymize IP addresses so that no personal information is captured or shared with Google.We also use cookies to record information about your site visit so we can improve your experience by delivering more relevant and personalised information. When you first visit our website you have the choice to accept or refuse these cookies.We use Google AdWords as a third party vendor for marketing purposes, this allows our advertisements to be displayed on other websites, including ones you visit. As well as using your browser to refuse cookies, you can also opt-out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting Google’s Ad Settings.MarketingYou will receive marketing communications from us if you have purchased goods or services from us, provided us with your details when you entered a competition, filled out a website form, or registered for a promotion and you have not opted out of receiving these communications.All marketing emails we send include an opt-out link in the footer.

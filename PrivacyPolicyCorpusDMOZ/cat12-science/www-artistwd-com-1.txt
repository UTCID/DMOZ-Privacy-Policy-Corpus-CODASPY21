Our Privacy
Guarantee
We respect your privacy! Any and all information collected at
this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, reused, rented,
loaned, or otherwise disclosed. Any information you give to JoyZine will be held
with the utmost care, and will not be used in ways that you have not consented
to. A more detailed explanation about how we safeguard your personal information
is described below. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to let us
know—just click on the Contact Us button on the menu bar.
Resale or disclosure of information to third parties:
JoyZine does not sell, rent, loan, trade or lease any personal
information collected at our site, including membership forms or email lists.
Browser information collected on the website:
JoyZine analyzes our website logs to constantly improve the value
of the materials available on our website. Our website logs are not personally
identifiable, and we make no attempt to link them with the individuals who
actually browse the site.
Privacy of our email and newsletter lists:
JoyZine maintains an email list to keep our contributors and members
informed about updates to the website and related information. Individuals must
affirmatively ask to join our mailing lists through some form of notification,
including checking a specified box when submitting a form, sending an e-mail requesting
membership, or signing up through the egroups.com membership box.
We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our
list to anyone. In addition, we configure our list server software to refuse to
divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized JoyZine & Artist
Web Design staff, including other list subscribers.

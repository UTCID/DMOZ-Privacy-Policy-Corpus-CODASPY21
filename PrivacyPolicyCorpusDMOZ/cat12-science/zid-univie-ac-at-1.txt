ZID Helpdesk
The ZID Helpdesk gives general information on the services of the ZID and helps members of the University of Vienna with their university-specific IT problems. It supports users in managing their u:account and password. Furthermore, it takes on reports about maintenance work and service interruptions and shares their information.
Note
Due to data protection laws the ZID Helpdesk can’t give information about personal data (example: password) by telephone.
Availability (on weekdays)
In writing
All day via Servicedesk or via helpdesk.zid@univie.ac.at
By telephoneMonday–Thursday 8:00–16:00, Friday 9:00–12:00Summer and semester break, Easter and Whitsun holidays: Monday–Thursday 10:00–14:00Christmas holidays: on weekdays (except 24.12. and 31.12.) 9:00–12:00Phone number: +43-1-4277-444
In person
Currently, the ZID Helpdesk is not personally accessible due to the limited university operation.

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Quanterion Solutions Incorporated collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (each, a “User”) of the Quanterion Solutions Incorporated website (“Site”). Our Site may use cookies to enhance User experience. User’s web browser places cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information about them.

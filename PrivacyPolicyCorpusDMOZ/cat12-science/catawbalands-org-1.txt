Privacy Policy
CLC does not share its membership mailing lists or e-mail addresses with commercial businesses or other nonprofit organizations. CLC staff considers a joint mailing with another organization or entity beneficial, the development director should require that the list be coordinated only through a mail house, that it be a one-time use and that the mail house signs a confidentiality agreement. The Board of Directors must approve any other non-routine uses of the membership list.
Website Analytics
To improve your experience on our site, we may implement the use of ‘cookies’, a small text file placed on your computer as a tool to remember your preferences. Our website uses Google Analytics which transmits website traffic data to Google servers. We use this data to help us understand website traffic and user preferences to enhance your experience on our website.

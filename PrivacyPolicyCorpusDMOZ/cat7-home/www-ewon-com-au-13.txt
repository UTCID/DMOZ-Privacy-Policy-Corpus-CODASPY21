Whistleblower Policy
EWON does not tolerate unethical or corrupt conduct and is committed to providing an environment where staff and stakeholders feel safe to speak up. Our policies aim to encourage the highest standard of professionalism including the recognition and reporting of any inappropriate behaviour. All reports about unacceptable behaviour will be considered seriously.

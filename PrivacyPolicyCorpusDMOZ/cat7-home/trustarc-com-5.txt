Nevada Privacy Law Compliance
The new Nevada privacy law, Nevada SB 220 went into effect on Oct 1, 2019. Are you compliant?
Nevada SB 220 grants consumers who live in Nevada the right to opt-out of the sale of their personal information. With some exceptions, Nevada SB 220 applies to website operators that collect information about consumers in the state of Nevada, no matter where the business is located. In addition, Nevada SB 220 requires that companies respond to consumer requests on a specific timeline or face penalties.

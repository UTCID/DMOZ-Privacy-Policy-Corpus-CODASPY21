Statement for Refundsweepers.com
Refundsweepers.com
has
created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment
to privacy. The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination
practices for this Web site: Refundsweepers.com
Information
Automatically Logged
We use your
IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer
our Web site.  Almost all websites use IP address logging to track
the number of visits to certain pages.  We do the same.  In no
way is this information used to track visitors habits.
Cookies
Our
Site uses cookies to save your user id and password so you don't have to
re-enter them each time you visit our message boards. The message boards
will also save your preferences using cookies.

We use outside
ad companies to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies.

have access to this information.  Disclosure:  Material connections
with our sponsoring Advertisers exists including compensation.

necessary.

contests on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like

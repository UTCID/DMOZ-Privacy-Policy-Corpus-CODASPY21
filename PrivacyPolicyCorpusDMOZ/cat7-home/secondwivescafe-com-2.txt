Privacy at SecondWivesCafe.com
Registration
We request that potential members provide detailed personal
information when they initiate an account with us. We ask you for
personally identifiable information such as your name, telephone
number, email address, mailing address, zip code, country, gender,
member name and password. This information is necessary so we at
SecondWivesCafe.com can assure the quality of our forums.
Registration is free.
Your Privacy
We won't share any personal information without your express
permission with anyone outside SecondWivesCafe.com administration.
This information is kept, password-protected, on our secure website. We
won't publish your last name, postal address, phone number, or IP
address.
Third Parties
We do not provide any information to third-party advertisers or
partners.
IP Addresses
Your IP address is a number that is assigned by your ISP (Internet
Service Provider) to your computer to identify its location. We use
your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to
administer our website. We also sometimes use IP addresses to
communicate with members and subscribers and possibly even ban
those members who are not complying with our Terms of Service.
Cookies
Your computer's internet browser stores
"cookies", or small packets of information from websites.
SecondWivesCafe.com can then read the cookies whenever you visit
our site. We use cookies to save your password so you
won't have to re-enter it each time you change pages. We may also use them to deliver content tailored to your interests, to track the pages you've visited and to make sure you don't see the same ads repeatedly. These cookies will allow us
to use the information we collect to make your SecondWivesCafe.com
experience as relevant to your needs and interests as possible.
Your browser can be modified to provide you with cookie choices.
You can have the choice to accept all cookies sent by a site, to be
notified whenever a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies. The
choice to reject all cookies will cause you to have to log in each
time you change pages at SecondWivesCafe.com.
Security
SecondWivesCafe.com has numerous security measures in place to
protect you from the loss, misuse and alteration of the information
under our control. Your member information and other sensitive
services like our free email service, require you to enter your
password. Please, to be safe, don't share your password with
anyone.
SecondWivesCafe.com uses industry-standard tools, such as
firewalls, to ensure that we keep the information you've entrusted
to us secure and private.

you!

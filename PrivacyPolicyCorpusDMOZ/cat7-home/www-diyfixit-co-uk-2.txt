Cookies
To enrich and perfect your online experience, DIY Fix It uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. DIY Fix It uses cookies to help DIY Fix It identify and track visitors, their usage of https://www.diyfixit.co.uk, and their website access preferences. DIY Fix It visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using DIY Fix It’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of DIY Fix It’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to DIY Fix It’s use of cookies.

1. How does BookFinder4U.com use
my personal information?
Our primary goal in collecting personal information is to provide
you, the user, with a customized experience on our site. We do not
sell, give away, or otherwise distribute your email address or other
member information to any third party.
2. Our Privacy Commitment
BookFinder4U.com is totally committed to protecting your
privacy. We make every effort to assure your confidentiality and
to keep your trust. The information you provide us in your account
is used to provide you with a more personalized book searching and
price comparison experience. BookFinder4U.com utilizes every available
technology to ensure that your confidential information remains
secure.

better shopping experience.

Privacy Policy
What information we collect and why
It is our policy that personal information like your name, address and
telephone number is private and confidential.
This information is secured
on our web servers and is only accessible by Abby's Guide staff.
From time
to time we may allow a sponsor to send you an email message.
You will have
the option to opt out of mailings if/when they begin.
As with most other web sites our server collects the following information:
Number of visitors to our sites
What pages were viewed and for how long
What keywords users used to find us
The browser used
Server of visitor
IP address of visitor
We may employ cookies on Abby's Guide. Cookies enhance the user experience
by saving preferences and login information.
Cookies also allow Abby's
Guide to find and remove spammers and people who harass others using our
forums or review sections.
We use the above information to enhance our web sites to better serve users.
At times, Abby's Guide works with third-party advertisers that employ a
different privacy policy than Abby's Guide.
Please see our advertisers web
sites for details.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
We invite your opinions or ideas about our privacy policy.

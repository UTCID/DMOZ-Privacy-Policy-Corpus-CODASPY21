Reports
The FTC produces a number of reports that examine antitrust and consumer protection trends. The agency uses this research to inform policy and to evaluate the agency’s performance. FTC policy reports include:staff and commission reports about specific topics of interesteconomic analyses and reportscompetition reportsfinancial reportsannual reports and other reports about the agency’s activitiesAs part of its engagement with the international community, the FTC also submits reports to the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD) and other international forums.In addition, the FTC publishes these periodic reports:Magnuson-Moss Warranty Public Audit FilingsRotational Health Warnings for Cigarette LabelingWebb-Pomerene Act Filings

Housing Counseling
HomeServicesHousing Counseling
DebtHelper.com counselors can assist you with advice on buying a home, default and foreclosure avoidance, credit issues or reverse mortgages also known as home equity conversion mortgages. As a HUD Approved Multi-state Housing Counseling Agency, DebtHelper provides housing counseling for over 9,000 families per year. Services are available over the phone, over the internet or In person. Face to face counseling is available in Debthelper’s North Carolina, Massachusetts and Florida Offices. DebtHelper is approved to provide Reverse Mortgage Counseling in 49 states. Debthelper’s HUD Approval Numbers are: 83607  Nationwide excluding MN; M81080 North Carolina; and 81063 Massachusetts. For alternative methods or translation services please call us direct. We look forward to serving you.

Consumer Fees for Housing Counseling
Foreclosure prevention counseling services are free of charge. Debthelper provides many housing counseling and education services, including pre-purchase, reverse mortgage, and non-delinquency post-purchase counseling services:
DebtHelper has a fee waiver and fee reduction policy for those that qualify
Fees are commensurate with the level of services provided and are provided at registration.
There is no cancellation fee for services. Once services are rendered,  fees are non-refundable.

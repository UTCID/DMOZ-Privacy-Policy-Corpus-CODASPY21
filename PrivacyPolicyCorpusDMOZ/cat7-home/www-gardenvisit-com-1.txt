Cookies
Gardenvisit.com makes use of cookies to allow stakeholders and admins to login to administer the site.
Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on GardenVisit.com.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to GardenVisit.com and other sites on the Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy. If you wish to know more you can read how Google uses your data when you visit a partner site like Gardenvisit.com.

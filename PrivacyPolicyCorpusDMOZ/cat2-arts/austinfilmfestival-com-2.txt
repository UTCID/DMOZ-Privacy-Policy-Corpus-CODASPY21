Store Policy
Austin Film Festival has the right to refuse a sale or admission for any AFF product or event at its sole discretion.
All merchandise, membership, individual film tickets, special events, AFF Presents, Virtual Badges, Virtual Film Passes, or Conversation in Film purchases are non-refundable for any reason.
By making a purchase you understand that your email address will be added to AFF’s email list. If you are not interested in receiving emails you can opt out.
REFUND POLICY:
Refund requests for the upcoming live Austin Film Festival must be in writing and sent by U.S. first class mail or email to refunds@austinfilmfestival.com, postmarked by July 15, 2021, and all refunds will incur a $75 processing fee. After July 15, 2021, all payments are non-refundable for any reason, including but not limited to illness, acts of God, or travel-related problems.  All add on purchases are nonrefundable.
In the event that Austin Film Festival has to cancel our physical event, Badge and Film Pass Holders (only applicable to live event purchases) will have the following options:
A full refund of your 2021 Badge or Film Pass
Apply the purchase price of your registration towards our virtual experience* (Depending on original purchase price, you may owe additional funds for your Virtual transfer)
Transfer your registration to 2022’s Austin Film Festival Live event (10/22/22 – 11/2/22)
Support our organization as we continue to support the writing community! Austin Film Festival is a year-round 501(c)(3)non-profit organization. In the event that we are not able to host a physical event, we hope you consider contributing the price of your Badge or Pass to our organization.
Badges are non-transferable and are the property of Austin Film Festival, Inc.
Refunds will be issued either via the original method of payment or other method approved by Austin Film Festival, if such original method is not available. Unused registrations cannot be credited for future year’s attendance.
Badges and Film Pass privileges may be revoked at any time for any reason without liability to Austin Film Festival. You hereby release and hold harmless Austin Film Festival, Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliated companies, successors, assigns, and licensees, and their respective officers, directors, employees, and agents from any claims resulting in attendance of an Austin Film Festival, Inc. event.
Special events may require purchase of separate ticket for entry. If you have any questions about the Festival, your membership, badge, or film pass purchase, please contact info@austinfilmfestival.com or call 1-800-FEST (3378).
SUMMER FILM CAMP REFUND POLICY
Austin Film Festival has adjusted our refund policy regarding both our in person and virtual Film Programs for the Summer of 2021.
If you purchased a Summer Film Camp for 2021, a refund of 80% of class tuition is offered only with written notice, which may be received by by email to refunds@austinfilmfestival.com and yfpdirector@austinfilmfestival.com from the parent or legal guardian of an enrolled student received no later than Friday, April 30th, 2021.  After this date, all purchases are final and will not be refunded
Students who cannot attend camp due to pandemic exposure will have the option to switch to a different in-person camp session or virtual camp session at no cost, camp capacity/space permitted. The difference in class price from an in-person camp to a virtual camp is non-refundable and will be considered a donation to the Young Filmmakers Program.
Due to the minimal time between purchase and camp, all  purchases of Austin Film Festival’s Virtual camps or workshops are nonrefundable.
Please note that in the event that Austin Film Festival must cancel a camp or workshop session, a full refund will be honored to all those who have purchased. This policy applies to both virtual and in person camps.
Special events may require purchase of separate ticket for entry. If you have any questions about the Festival, your membership or badge purchase, please contact info@austinfilmfestival.com or call 1-800-FEST (3378).
IF YOU REGISTERED FOR SUMMER FILM CAMP:
Our Young Filmmakers Program Director will contact you further within the next few weeks for your child’s registration information.
IF YOU ORDERED MERCHANDISE:
If you ordered merchandise online, your items will be mailed to the address you provided. The purchaser assumes all risk and damage incidental to the mailing of these items, whether occurring prior to, during, or after the mailing process. Service/Handling charges are non-refundable.
IF YOU PURCHASED A MEMBERSHIP:
If you ordered a membership, you will be added to our Membership database and begin receiving regular emails and letters about upcoming events.
IF YOU PURCHASED ATTENDANCE TO CONVERSATIONS IN FILM OR ANOTHER SPECIAL EVENT SCREENING:
If you ordered a Conversation in Film or special screening ticket, your name will be added to the will-call list and you will receive an email notification with further information.
IF YOU PURCHASED A BADGE OR FILM PASS:
If you ordered a film festival Badge or Film Pass, a copy of this order confirmation WILL BE NECESSARY to pick up your Badge or Film Pass at registration.
Austin Film Festival’s registration for a Badge or Film Pass is located at The Driskill Hotel at 604 Brazos Street. You will receive your registration packet here which will include your Badge or Film Pass and other information to help you navigate the Conference and Film Festival.
Registration hours are as follows:
Wednesday, October 20, 12:00pm – 11:00pm
Thursday, October 21, 8:00am – 11:00pm
Friday, October 22, 8:00am – 11:00pm
Saturday, October 23, 8:00am – 9:00pm
Sunday, October 24, 10:00am – 5:00pm
Registration moves to our tech center (location TBD) after the Conference wraps, the hours are:
Monday, October 25, 12:00pm – 7:00pm
Tuesday, October 26, 12:00pm – 7:00pm
Wednesday, October 27, 12:00pm – 7:00pm
Thursday, October 28, 12:00pm – 7:00pm
*These hours are subject to change at any time.
FILM ADMISSION POLICY: Badge holders and Film Pass holders MUST be in line no later than 20 minutes prior to the scheduled start time. Priority admission is given to all Badge holders. Film Pass holders will be seated after all Badge holders have entered the theater. General Admission tickets will go on sale 20 minutes prior to each screening. Once General Admission tickets have gone on sale, Badge holders and Film Pass holders will no longer have priority admittance. Badge holders and Film Pass holders arriving AFTER the 20 minutes mark must go to the back of the General Admission line. ALL theater seating will be on a first come, first served basis. Neither Badge holders nor Film Pass holders are guaranteed a seat, only admission up to capacity. NO ONE will be admitted into the theater 15 minutes after start time. Austin Film Festival Staff will adhere to this policy even in the event of a delayed screening. Thank you for your cooperation. *SPECIAL EVENTS MAY REQUIRE PURCHASE OF SEPARATE TICKET FOR ENTRY. If you have any questions about the Festival, your membership or your badge purchase, please contact info@austinfilmfestival.com or call 1-800-310-FEST (3378).
BADGE PHOTOS: ALL Badge holders are required to have their pictures printed on their credentials (Film Pass holders are not required to send in a photo.) Please email a passport style photo to pictures@austinfilmfestival.com . If we do not receive your photo, your Badge WILL NOT be available for pickup at registration, and you can expect up to a 1-2 hour wait at registration while we take and print your picture on-site.
PURCHASING BADGES/PASSES FOR MULTIPLE PEOPLE
If you are purchasing Badges or Film Passes for multiple people, please send the name, email address, and phone number to registration@austinfilmfestival.com once you receive your confirmation email.

PRICE PROTECTION POLICY
From time to time, we offer limited flash sales. As with any commercial transaction, there is always a chance that a purchased item will go on sale at some point in the future. Flash sales cannot be combined with any other offer. While our flash sales rarely offer a lower price than previously available, in the instances where they do, we offer price protection for those who purchased a product within 72 hours of the start of a flash sale: one-time price adjustment for items marked down within seventy-two (72) hours of the date on your purchase. Items purchased with coupons or promotional discounts are not eligible for price adjustments and cannot be combined with any other offer (including entrant discounts). Limit one coupon or promotional discount per transaction. To obtain a price adjustment on a qualified purchase, you must call 1.800.310.FEST, or send us an email message at info@austinfilmfestival.com within a seventy-two (72) hour window of the flash sale start date. Include your order number in your message.

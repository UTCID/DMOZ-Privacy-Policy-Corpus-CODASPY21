Privacy Statement
When using the MartinBalmer.com web site  your privacy is both protected and respected to the maximum possible extent. MartinBalmer.com has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination practices for MartinBalmer.com and any web site that forms part of MartinBalmer.com denoted by the MartinBalmer.com header.
Information Automatically Logged
Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information, such as which pages are downloaded most, number of actual people using this web site,   popular search terms and popularity of our pages around the world.
When Contact Details Are Requested
Should you need to provide contact details to MartinBalmer.com please be assured that these will be solely used for the purpose for which you provided them. MartinBalmer.com DOES NOT sell or share such information.
External Links
This site contains links to other sites. MartinBalmer.com is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.
Advertising and Cookies
Google Analytics and Google Analytics Advertising Features are implemented on this web site.
Google advertising on this web site can be controlled from the Google Ad Preferences control page.  You can reach this by selecting ‘Ad Choices’ on an advertisement or ‘Ads by Google’ below an advertisement.
Contacting the Web Site

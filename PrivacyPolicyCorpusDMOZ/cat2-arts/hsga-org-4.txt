Non-Discrimination Policy
Posted on
March 10, 2020
by
Mike Wittmer
The Hawaiian Steel Guitar Association (HSGA) does not and shall not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, creed, gender, sexual orientation and gender expression or identity, age, national origin, ancestry, disability, marital or familial status, or military status, in any of its activities or operations. These activities include, but are not limited to, hiring and firing of staff, selection of volunteers, members, Board members and officers, and vendors, awarding of scholarships, festival and event guests and provision of services. HSGA is committed to providing an inclusive and welcoming environment for all.
Adopted by the HSGA Board of Directors on March 9, 2020

Find a Health Insurance Helper
Having someone in your corner who knows how things work and who will take time to help you understand can make all the difference in your care. Use this tool to connect to a free health insurance helper or contact us for advice on locating one. Don't give up and let us know how we can help.
A Sweet Guide to Health Insurance for Minnesota Artists
Health insurance may be complicated to keep and get, but there are lots of resources out there to help. Here's a PDF guide for Minnesota artists (with useful information for artists nationally!) on health insurance basics, and which resources are the best for answering your questions and getting you covered.

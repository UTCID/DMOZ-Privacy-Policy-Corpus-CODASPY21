Privacy Policy
Our general privacy policy is to not share any member or mailing list data, with the following exception:
Partners who are co-producing events with us and/or providing sponsorship for events will be provided the names of those who attend the co-produced/sponsored event. All partners are encouraged to have their own sign-up sheets at events to voluntarily collect any other data.

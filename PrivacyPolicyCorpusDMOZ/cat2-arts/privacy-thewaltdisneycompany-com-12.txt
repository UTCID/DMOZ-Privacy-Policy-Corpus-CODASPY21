This supplement to our global privacy policy applies to the processing of personal information in Malaysia or of Malaysia residents and shall be read in conjunction with our global Privacy Policy.
DATA PRIVACY OFFICER
If you have a comment or question about this privacy policy, please contact our data privacy officer at TWDC.DL-SG-DPO@disney.com. You can also make an access or correction request or a complaint in relation to personal information by sending an email to the data privacy officer at TWDC.DL-SG-DPO@disney.com or call on +65 6692-7399.

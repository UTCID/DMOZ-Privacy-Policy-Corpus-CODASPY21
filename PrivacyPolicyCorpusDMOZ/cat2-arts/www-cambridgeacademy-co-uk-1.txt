What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files which are downloaded to your computer, mobile, or tablet device when you visit a website.
Your web browser sends these cookies back to the website on each subsequent visit so that they can remember things like personalised details or social media preferences.
Without cookies, some fundamental site functions could not function correctly.
Third party Cookies
When you share a page by using a social media sharing button on this website, the third party social media website will use a cookie to enable this function.
Analytic Cookies
To ensure the best user experience we use Google Analytics which uses a cookies to track how you browse the website. This allows us to monitor statistics such as page popularity and the geographical location of visitors. Your personal data will not be captured by analytic Cookies.
Managing Cookies
Most modern browsers will allow you to:
•   See what cookies you’ve got and delete them on an individual basis
•   Block third party cookies
•   Block cookies from particular sites
•   Block all cookies from being set
•   Delete all cookies when you close your browser
By deleting cookies, your preferences for this website will be lost.

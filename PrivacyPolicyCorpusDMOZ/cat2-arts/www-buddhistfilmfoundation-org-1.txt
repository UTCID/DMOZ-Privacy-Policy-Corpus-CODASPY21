Privacy Policy
Buddhist Film Foundation (BFF) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to our website and governs data collection and usage at this site and service.
Through its website, BFF collects personal information provided, such as your email address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Information collected by BFF is used solely for the purpose of billing and servicing any online purchases or donations, or informing you of news and events at BFF (pursuant to your instructions when you subscribe). BFF does not collect any information about your computer hardware and software. BFF does not sell, rent or lease its subscriber or donor lists to third parties.

The following statements describe the privacy practices for comedyevents.ca:
Any personal information collected from our users will be kept in strict confidence and not sold or shared with any other parties.IP addresses are logged for aggregate statistics and diagnosis.Security settings protect the misuse of sensitive information.comedyevents.ca does not use cookies to track any user information.
Personal User Information
davidcjones.ca does not collect any information about its users or visitors with the exception of those who choose to sign up for the comedy events mailing list or submit information and quote requests. The information collected in those cases is basic and kept strictly confidential.
Users are free to visit davidcjones.ca, and navigate all its pages, without any user registration.
Log Files
davidcjones.ca uses IP addresses to diagnose server problems, analyze trends, administer the site, and gather demographic information for aggregate use.
IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.
davidcjones.ca has standard security measures in place to protect the loss and misuse of sensitive information.
Cookies
davidcjones.ca does not use cookies, but since we may use ad companies to display ads, these ads might contain cookies.
Links
davidcjones.ca contains links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. This privacy document applies only to comedyevents.ca.
Contacting the Web Site
For any questions about this privacy statement, please contact: info@davidcjones.ca

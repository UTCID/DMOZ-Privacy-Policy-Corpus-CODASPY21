Privacy PolicyUpdated February 8, 2017Our Commitment To PrivacyYour privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we make this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. Should you have questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please email tours@magicspace.netThe Information We CollectThis notice applies to all information collected or submitted on MagicSpace Entertainment, Inc. (“MagicSpace”) company websites. It does not apply to information collected on third-party websites linked to from MagicSpace websites. On some pages, you can order products, make requests, and register to receive materials. The types of personal information collected at these pages are:NameBilling & Mailing AddressEmail addressCompanyPhone numberCredit/Debit Card InformationThe Way We Use InformationWe use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order, making a request, or registering to receive materials. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. We may use the information provided to contact you by phone, mail, or email for marketing and fundraising purposes. You can ask to be removed from marketing and fundraising lists by contacting the MagicSpace Ticket Office at tours@magicspace.netWe use the information you provide about someone else when placing an order only to complete an order. We do not share this information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to complete that order. You can register with our website if you would like to receive marketing materials related to MagicSpace.We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website. We use third-party vendors, including Google and AdRoll, to show advertisements for our organization on the internet. These vendors use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visit to our website. You may opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting the Google advertising opt-out page, and AdRoll by visiting http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/.Our Commitment To Data SecurityTo prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.Our Commitment To Children's PrivacyProtecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.How You Can Access Or Correct Your InformationYou can access your personally identifiable information that we collect online and maintain by contacting MagicSpace at tours@magicspace.netTo protect your privacy and security, we will also take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access or making corrections.

Rain - A Tribute to the Beatles Tour Info
The Spring 2020 Tour of RAIN – A Tribute to the Beatles, originally scheduled for Spring 2021, has been postponed to Fall of 2021 and Spring of 2022 due to the COVID-19 Pandemic. Ticket holders may hold onto their tickets to guarantee their same great seats for the new engagement when dates are announced or request a refund or account credit from their original point of purchase. Ticket holders will be contacted with details via email. We appreciate your patience as we navigate this global pandemic as it greatly effects the world of live entertainment. We look forward to entertaining you once again when it is safe to do so for our cast, crew and local partners.Thank you for your continued support of RAIN – A Tribute to the Beatles.Peace & Love-The cast and crew of RAIN

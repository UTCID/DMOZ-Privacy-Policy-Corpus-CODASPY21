The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Ads appearing on our website may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. To enrich and perfect your online experience, Big Heavy Stuff uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Big Heavy Stuff uses cookies to help Big Heavy Stuff identify and track visitors, their usage of http://bigheavystuff.net/, and their website access preferences. Big Heavy Stuff visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Big Heavy Stuff’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Big Heavy Stuff’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Big Heavy Stuff’s use of cookies.

We may also provide this information to an agent acting on Storyteller Genot Picor’s behalf in connection with the activities described above. Storyteller Genot Picor will occasionally hire other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, such as packaging, sending, and delivering purchases and other mailings; answering customer questions about products or services; processing event registration; or performing statistical analysis of our services. Storyteller Genot Picor will only provide those companies the personal information they need to deliver the service, and they are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose. Except as described above, Storyteller Genot Picor will not sell, rent, or in any way intentionally disclose personal information that you provide to us on this Website to any third party (other than an agent acting on our behalf as described above), unless: (a) Storyteller Genot Picor is required to do so by law, regulation, warrant, subpoena or court order, (b) Storyteller Genot Picor is required in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of Storyteller Genot Picor employees, users of Storyteller Genot Picor products or services, or members of the public, or to enforce Terms of Use; or (c) such disclosure is part of a sale of all (or substantially all) of the assets of one or more divisions or affiliates of Storyteller Genot Picor. Use of CookiesStoryteller Genot Picor uses cookies to help us provide better customer service and to enhance the user experience and deliver personalized content. Many improvements and updates to this Website are based on the data collected through the cookies, such as total number of visitors and pages viewed. A “cookie” is a small text file stored locally in the computer used to access your account, and contains information relating to your past use of this Website. Storyteller Genot Picor may disclose the non-personally identifiable information collected, in aggregate form only, to third parties including advertisers or potential advertisers. By posting the information on this Website you are providing Storyteller Genot Picor and its affiliates the right to use the information in connection with the operation of Storyteller Genot Picor’s business.

Privacy Policy

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation ((EU) 2016/679) (“GDPR”), we, Author Jean Brashear, are the “data controller”. If you have any questions about this policy or about how we use your Personal Data, please contact us via our contact details at the end of this policy.
This policy is effective as of May 7, 2018. We review our privacy practices on an ongoing basis, and as such we may change this privacy policy from time to time. Please check this page frequently to ensure you are familiar with its current content.

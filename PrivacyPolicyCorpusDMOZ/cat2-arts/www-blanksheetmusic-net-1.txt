Privacy Policy
Cookies
Cookies are small text files that can be used by web sites to
make a user's experience more efficient.
We use cookies to remember user preferences, like paper
format, margins, last printed format, to personalize content
and to analyze our traffic. We also share information about
your use of our site with our social media, advertising and
analytics partners, who may combine it with other information
you’ve provided to them or they’ve collected from your use of
their services.
Please note that without cookies and javascript enabled it
could not be possible to print blank sheet music, which is
what this website is for.
Therefore we assume you are happy with it and that using or
browsing this website yuo give explicit consent to us to
store cookies on your device.
If you are not comfortable with this behaviour, you have the
option to leave this website and clear cookies in your
browser settings. Search for "delete browser cookies" along
with your browser name on your preferred search engine and
follow instructions.

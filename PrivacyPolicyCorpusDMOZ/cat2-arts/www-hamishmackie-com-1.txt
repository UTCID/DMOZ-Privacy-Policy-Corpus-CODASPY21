HAMISH MACKIE SCULPTURE
Hamish Mackie has works in public and private collections around the world. His wildlife sculptures are cast in bronze, silver or stainless steel as limited editions; each one signed, dated and numbered.
You are welcome to come and view Hamish’s sculptures at his studio in Oxfordshire. Please telephone first for an appointment.
Sculptures can be shipped worldwide directly from the studio.

Privacy Policy
The Penn State Blue Band is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. We want to provide a safe, secure user experience. We will make our best effort to ensure that the information you submit remains private, and is used solely for purposes outlined in our website. Information stored on our database can only be viewed by Blue Band personnel. We do not provide information to any outside vendors.

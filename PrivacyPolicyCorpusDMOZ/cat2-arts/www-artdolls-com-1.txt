Shipping & Delivery
Johnston Original Artdolls ships the same day you place your order. It is our policy to ship within six hours of receiving your order. If you place your order Monday through Friday, your package will be shipped to you that same day. Normally Domestic packages will arrive at their destination within 3 working days. International orders are delivered within 7-10 days.
Johnston Original Artdolls ships all of its packages via United States Postal Service. We use flat rate Priority Air Mail for all of our shipments. USPS flat rate priority air mail is the fastest and cheapest method available. There are several reasons that we prefer USPS over other mail carrier. They track all domestic packages, insure them up to $100, and ship within six hours of receiving the packages. When shipping Internationally USPS track packages to the Customs office in the country to which we are shipping.

This allows the website, through its server to provide the users with a tailored experience within this website. Users are advised that if they wish to deny the use and saving of cookies from this website on to their computers hard drive they should take necessary steps within their web browsers security settings to block all cookies from this website and its external serving vendors.

RETURNS, REPLACEMENTS & CANCELLATIONS
Thanks for shopping at Johnston Original Artdolls. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help. Please call or email about the problem. You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. Your item must be in the original packaging. Please, provide your receipt or proof of purchase.
Refunds… Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your credit card company or to you through PayPal.
Shipping… You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of original shipping will not be refunded.
Seminars and Workshops… You may cancel a seminar or workshop 30 days or longer before the event for a full refund. If you cancel less than 30 days before the event we will allow you to reschedule a subsequent event of the same kind as long as it is done within one year. If you wish to substitute someone else in your place that is acceptable.
ORDERING
Orders are processed within six hours of our receiving your order on Monday through Friday. If you place your order on the weekend or holidays, your order will be shipped on the following business day.
You will be sent an automatic notice of your shipment and tracking information via the United States Postal Service as long as you have provided a current eMail address with your order.
PAYMENT, PRICING & PROMOTIONS
We accept payment via all major credit card companies. We use authorized.net as our payment gateway for all credit cards. Our checkout process is encrypted with a 128-bit SSL certificate. The certificate ensures all sensitive data, such as credit card information and billing and shipping addresses are processed securely without any compromising factors.
You have several options for payment when you make your purchase. You may use conventional credit cards, or pay via PayPal. If you have the need to use checks or bank transfers you may contact us directly at jack@artdolls.com or call us toll free at 800-290-9998.
In the event of a special promotion or sale, we will do our best to give you the lowest price possible. Occasionally we offer discounts or coupons to our customers, we expect you to take the lowest price available, but ask you not to use a coupon on a product that is already discounted beyond the value of your coupon.

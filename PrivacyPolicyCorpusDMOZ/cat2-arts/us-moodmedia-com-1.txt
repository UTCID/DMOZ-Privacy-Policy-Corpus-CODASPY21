This information is not personally identifiable.Personally Identifiable InformationAs described in this policy, “Personally Identifiable Information” is any information that concerns you individually and would permit someone to contact you, such as your name, address, telephone/fax number, email address, or any information you submit to the Sites that identifies you individually. Mood may also use aggregated anonymous information to provide information about the Sites to potential business partners, advertisers and other unaffiliated entities.

Privacy Policy For glasspainting.co.uk
[Please note we are working towards full GDPR complience. In the meantime you should note we use Cookies for the following reasons:
A Statcounter cookie which gives us stats about how many visitors use the site, which pages they view  etc.
A Cookie for Google ads.  We have opted to only show “non-personalised ads to EU visitors which we believe to be less intrusive.
A cookie so you don’t see the Email List pop-up on every page you visit.
The shop uses a cookie to keep track of what you have added to your basket etc.
We hope to shortly have a system which will enable you to in/out of individual cookies. In the meantime please feel free to leave the site if you find them intrusive.]
*************************************************
Due to the nature of our sites we sometimes ask you to supply us with information: e.g. an email address if you sign up to our email list or a username etc if there is site membership or forum.
This information NOT shared with anyone else, even with other sites we run.
You may automatically unsubscribe from our email list by revisiting the subscription page, putting your email address in again and selecting the unsubscribe box. Any problems please just contact us at admin@glasspainting.co.uk not forgetting to tell us which email address you wish to unsubscribe.
Our Google Adverts use the DART cookie to serve ads to our users based on your previous visits to our site and other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html .
All information we gather is kept completely confidential, we do not share it with anyone. You may request any / all information we hold relevant to yourself to be deleted at any time. All requests will be honored within 3 working days. Please make sure the request comes via the email address we hold for you. If you have any question, comments or complaints please contact us at the email address above.
Yours Bob

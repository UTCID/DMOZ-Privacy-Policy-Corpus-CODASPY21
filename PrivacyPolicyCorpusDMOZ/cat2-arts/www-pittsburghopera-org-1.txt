Pittsburgh Opera Donor Privacy Policy
Pittsburgh Opera places a high priority on protecting privacy.
We do not exchange, sell, or share donor’s personal information for any reason.
We do not send mailings on behalf of other organizations.
Information supplied to us by patrons is used for specific, limited purposes.  Patrons (donors or other customers) may always "opt out," at any time. Any patron who requests to be taken off our mailing list will be excluded from any future Pittsburgh Opera mailings or any list trade.

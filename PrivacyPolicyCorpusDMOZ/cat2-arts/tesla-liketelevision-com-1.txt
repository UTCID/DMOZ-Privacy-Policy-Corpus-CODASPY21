Information Collected
We use cookies to authenticate Members, to deliver content specific to your interests and to save your password information so you don't
have to re-enter it every time you visit LikeTelevision's web site.
LikeTelevision uses Google AdSense to help us pay the bills, which helps us help you to watch stuff for free. As part of this insertion of ads, the LikeTelevision
sites use Google, as a third party vendor, to use cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users
based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the
Google ad and content network privacy policy.
Our site's registration form requires users to give us contact information (name and email) and demographic information
(city, state, zip code, country, age and gender).
We use customer contact information from the registration form to send our users information about new content and information about the company, rarely.
We have no interest in bugging you or being a pest. So, if we do send out something to our users, this might happen a few times a year.
We get annoyed by others who pester us, and we do our best to follow the Golden Rule, to do unto others, as we would like to be treated.

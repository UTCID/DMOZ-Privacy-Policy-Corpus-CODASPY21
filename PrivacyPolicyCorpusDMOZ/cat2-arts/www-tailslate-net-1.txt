Privacy Statement for Tail Slate: The Source for Film Knowledge
Tail Slate (www.tailslate.com) has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website:
Visitor Information:
We do not collect any information on visitors who browse our site. You can access our homepage and browse our site without disclosing your personal data.
Email Addresses:
The only email addresses that are collected by Tail Slate are those volunteered by our visitors for special announcements, newsletters, contests and our comment boards. These e-mail addresses are not shared with outside sources, and are used solely for our internal services.
External Links:
This site contains links to other sites. Tail Slate is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.
Comment Boards:
This website utilizes comment boards on every review, article and photo collections. We wish to remind visitors that comments posted on these boards are publically available and you should exercise caution when deciding to disclose your personal information.
Contacting the Web Site:
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with Tail Slate, you can contact us at tailslate@tailslate.net.

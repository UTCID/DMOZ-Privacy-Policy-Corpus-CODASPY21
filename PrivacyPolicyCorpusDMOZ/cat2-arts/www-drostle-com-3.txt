Privacy Policy
We take your privacy seriously and only keep the basic contact details you supply to us.
If at any time you would like your details removed or verified simply contact us by email or post at the address below.
What information do we keep?
If you send us your contact information, i.e.. name, job title, company, address, email address and phone number. We will keep this on our contacts database. We will not share this information with anyone else and will not keep any other information apart from the above mentioned fields. We will use this information from time to time to keep you updated with our news.
If you have a contract with Drostle Public Arts Ltd for the provision of an artwork, designs, workshops or reports we will also keep any information needed to execute the contract such as artwork details, dates and value of works, relevant insurance details and other connected contact and contract details.
If you are a supplier to Drostle Public Arts Ltd we may also keep your Bank Payment details for invoice payment purposes only and order records.
Data Security
You information is kept securely in our database and is not shared with any third party. We do not use cookies.
Links to other websites
Our website contains links to other websites connected with our projects or articles. If you have use these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website. Therefore, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such sites and such sites are not governed by this privacy statement. You should exercise caution and look at the privacy statement applicable to the website in question.
Your information
You may request details of the personal information which we hold about you under the Data Protection Act 1998. If you would like a copy of the information held on you please write to the data controller at the address below.
If you believe that any information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please write or email us as soon as possible, at the below address. We will promptly correct any information found to be incorrect.
If you would like your information removed from our records after you have consented previously you can write or email requesting the information is deleted to the address below or use the unsubscribe button on any of our emails to you.
If you would like to know more feel free to contact us.

Privacy Policy:
thecarburetors ("thecarburetors")
operates thecarburetors.net and may operate other websites. It
is thecarburetors's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect
while operating our websites.
Website Visitors
Like most website operators, thecarburetors collects non-personally-identifying information of the
sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference,
referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. thecarburetors's purpose in
collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand
how thecarburetors's visitors use its website. From time to
time, thecarburetors may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by
publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.
thecarburetors also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol
(IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on thecarburetors.net
blogs/sites. thecarburetors only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same
circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that
commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site
where the comment was left.
Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to thecarburetors's websites choose to interact
with thecarburetors in ways that require thecarburetors to gather
personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that thecarburetors gathers
depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at thecarburetors.net to provide a
username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with thecarburetors are asked to
provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to
process those transactions. In each case, thecarburetors collects such information only insofar as
is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with
thecarburetors. thecarburetors does not disclose personally-identifying information
other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with
the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.
Aggregated Statistics
thecarburetors may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its
websites. thecarburetors may display this information publicly or provide it to others.
However, thecarburetors does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as
described below.
Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information
thecarburetors discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information

Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser
provides to the website each time the visitor returns. thecarburetors uses cookies to
help thecarburetors identify and track visitors, their usage of thecarburetors
website, and their website access preferences. thecarburetors visitors who do not wish to have
cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before
using thecarburetors's websites, with the drawback that certain features
of thecarburetors's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies.
These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to
compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among
other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy
Policy covers the use of cookies by thecarburetors and does not cover the use of cookies by any
advertisers.

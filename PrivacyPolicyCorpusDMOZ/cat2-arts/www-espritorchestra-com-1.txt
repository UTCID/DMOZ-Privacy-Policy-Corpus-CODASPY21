Privacy Policy – Esprit Orchestra
Privacy Policy
Esprit Orchestra is committed to protecting the privacy of its patrons. This site has security measures in place. Esprit Orchestra makes every effort to ensure the security of its network and systems. We do not store contact information or credit card details on our web servers.
Contact Information
Esprit Orchestra collects mailing and email address information primarily to inform its patrons about upcoming events of the organization. We do not, under any circumstances, rent, sell, or trade our mailing lists or contact information with commercial businesses, non-profit organizations, or third party entities.
If you would like to stop receiving information and updates from Esprit Orchestra, please contact us at 416-815-7887 or info@espritorchestra.com. When you contact us, please let us know if you do not wish to receive mail, phone calls, or e-mails from Esprit.

Privacy Policy
If you purchase music through this website, we never possess nor store your financial information. It is passed through immediately via Authorize.net to Cayan for processing. The only information we store is email interaction via the address above. We do not share your information with anyone else, ever, for any reason.
When visitors leave comments on the site we collect the data shown in the comments form, and also the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection.
An anonymized string created from your email address (also called a hash) may be provided to the Gravatar service to see if you are using it. The Gravatar service privacy policy is available here: https://automattic.com/privacy/. After approval of your comment, your profile picture is visible to the public in the context of your comment.
Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service.
We use Google Analytics to track statistics about who visits the site.
Your Personal Data
Your name, shipping address (where applicable), email address and phone number are collected and kept as a reference in case stevenbryant.com needs to contact you regarding your order. Your credit card information is used ONLY to complete your transaction, and will NOT be stored anywhere after the transaction is complete.
Your information is NOT shared with any other individual, company, website, or service. If you join my mailing list, your email address will never be shared or sold to anyone else.
We may contact you regarding any questions or concerns about your order (such as shipping options, special requests, follow-up confirmation, etc.) using your provided email address.

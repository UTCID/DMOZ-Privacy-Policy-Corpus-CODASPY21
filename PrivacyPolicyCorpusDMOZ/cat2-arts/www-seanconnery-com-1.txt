privacy policy
Information that you provide on the seanconnery.com website is used to inform you of new information about Sir Sean Connery the actor, his patronage, upcoming events, special offers, and other information that may be of interest to you. Your information is considered private, and it is kept secure to protect it from outside parties. We periodically generate reports from website traffic logs. Such reports are used to improve the seanconnery.com website and bring new services and special offers that may be of interest to site visitors.
We will not rent or sell your name, address, email address, or any of your other personal information to any third party without your explicit permission.
Please note that we must cooperate fully should a situation arise where we are required by law or legal process to provide information we may have on-file.

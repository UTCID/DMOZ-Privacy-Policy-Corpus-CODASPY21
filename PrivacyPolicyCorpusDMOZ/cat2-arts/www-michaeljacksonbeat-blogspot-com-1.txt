Your Privacy and Use of Cookies At MJB
Privacy Policy
I in no way collect any personal information about you when you visit my blog. Unless you so choose to provide it. However, this blog collects and stores certain information automatically. Here is how I handle information about your visit to my web blog.
What This Blog Stores and Retrieves Automatically
If you do nothing during your visit but browse through my blog, reading pages, or downloading information, this blog by default will gather and store certain information about your visit automatically. This info does not identify you personally. I automatically collect and store only the following information about your visit:
* The Internet domain (for example, “website.com” if you use a private Internet access account, or “highschool.edu” if you connect from a university’s domain) and IP address (an IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are surfing the Web) from which you access our website.
* The type of browser and operating system used to access my site.
* The date and time you access my site.
* The pages you visit.
* If you linked to my blog from another place, I gather the address of that website.
I use the information to count the number and type of visitors to the different pages on my blog, and to help me make my site more useful to visitors like you.
If you send me an e-mail or a comment, you may choose to provide personal information, such as your name or your e-mail address. I use the information to improve my service to you or to respond to your request.
Except for authorized law enforcement investigations, I will not share your e-mail with any outside organizations.
Cookies
To save your comment’s information every time you visit Michael Jackson Beat, I place a little text file called a “cookie” on your hard drive. It sits in your web browser directory.
A cookie lets me store your profile information on your computer and let’s my blog retrieve it when you visit my site. No other web site can use or view the cookie set by Michael Jackson Beat.
Cookies help me create a speedy web experience by remembering your address and email information, so you don’t have to re-type it when making comments.
I do not use cookies to track your movements throughout my blog.
I do not use cookies to intrusively track your movements throughout my blog. I only use them to view traffic stats for Google Analytics as well as deliver relevant third-party ads via Google Adsense. By visiting this site, you consent to my use of these cookies. But if you prefer, you can opt out of cookies anytime by managing your browser settings.
Third Party Advertising
Third party advertisers use information(not including your name, address, email address or telephone number) about your visits to this and other Web sites in order to provide advertisements on this blog and other sites you visit on goods and services that may be of interest to you.
If you would like more information about this practice, please click here.

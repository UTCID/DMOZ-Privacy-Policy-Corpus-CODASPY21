Privacy PolicyInformationWilliam A. Karges Fine Art takes your online privacy seriously. You may visit the site anonymously as many times as you like without providing any personal information. We do not collect personal information from you unless you purchase something through the site, or request information from us. In that case, we will never sell, rent, or provide your information to a third party. We may, on an occasional basis, send you notice of new works, catalogs and books via email. If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please use the "Contact Us" button to let us know your concerns.

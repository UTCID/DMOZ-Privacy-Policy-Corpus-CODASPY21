The Concert Band comprising of around 50 brass, woodwind and percussion instruments has a wide ranging repertoire . . .
The Marching Band comprises all musicians with the addition of flag bearers, marching percussion and sousaphones. . . .
The Stage Band regularly music for Balls, Dinner Dances, Birthday Parties and Weddings. For these occasions the band provides an excellent selection of strict tempo dance music . . .
The band boasts a very talented Bavarian group. For these performances the band wears German style alpine hats with some members dressing in complete alpine uniform.
The Camtown Dixielanders Playing Traditional jazz arrangements, the eight piece "Camtown Dixielanders" provide toe-tapping music for fetes, shopping centres . . .
The Daytones are the latest group to be formed under the Campbelltown Camden District Band banner. This group provides an opportunity for those musicians who wish to rehearse and perform in a daytime group.
The Youth Ensemble or "Youthies" caters for younger band members of high school age of 4th grade standard.

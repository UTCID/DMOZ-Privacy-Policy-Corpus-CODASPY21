Privacy Policy
Our site permits visitors to submit contact information, should they so choose. Submitted information is used to contact visitors to answer questions and send program information. Information gathered from users during surveys is only gathered in order to learn more about general user trends and interests. Submitted email addresses are used for no other purpose and will not be disclosed to third parties. Any personal information you provide to us during registration, including your name, address, telephone number and email address, will not be released, sold, or rented to any entities.
This site contains links to other sites. The School of Professional Studies is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Our site uses a cookie to collect anonymous Web traffic data.

Privacy, Terms & ConditionsTickets are available by calling 603-542-4433, online at www.claremontoperahouse.org or by stopping in at the ticket office in City Hall Complex at 58 Opera House Square.
Ticket office is open Monday through Friday 1 pm to 4 pm.  Please do not ask City Employees for assistance.  They do not sell tickets.
If COH Box Office is closed you can call Louanne in the Claremont Opera House Business office at 603-542-0064 or check in with her at the  Claremont Opera House Business Office which is located at the top of the stairs by City Collections and Welfare Office.

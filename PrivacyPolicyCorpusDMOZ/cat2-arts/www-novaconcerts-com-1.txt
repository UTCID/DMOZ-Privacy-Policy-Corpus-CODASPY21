On Tour:
Kyle Eastwood
Bassist Kyle Eastwood is an outstanding performer and bandleader whose sound straddles the line between straight-ahead acoustic post-bop and contemporary jazz. In Co-Operation with V.O MUSIC / France
Available / On Tour:
March, April, May, June, July, August 2021
Kenny Garrett
Grammy award winning saxophonist Kenny Garrett is a true virtuoso on the saxophone and is considered to be the 'most important alto saxophonist of his generation' (Washington City Paper). In conjunction with Nathan Horne.
Available / On Tour:
July, October, November 2020, May 2021
Steve Harley Acoustic Band
Steve Harley celebrates his 47th year in music in 2020 and continues to play concerts around the world. He says that playing 'live' is more than a job, it’s almost his life’s blood.
Available / On Tour:

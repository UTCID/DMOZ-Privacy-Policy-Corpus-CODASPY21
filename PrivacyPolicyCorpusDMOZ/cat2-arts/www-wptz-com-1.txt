An Upper Valley Hospital is helping get milk to infants who need it. Dartmouth-Hitchcock opened a donor milk depot and dispensary at its women's health resource center in Lebanon. It is partnering with Mothers' Milk Bank Northeast to provide locations for women with extra milk to donate it to babies who need it.Mothers who are donating must register and get screened beforehand. Families of infants with prescriptions for donor milk can pick it up. Babies can get donor milk at 11 New Hampshire hospitals, but once they leave the hospital, families must travel to a dispensary or arrange for shipping from a milk bank.

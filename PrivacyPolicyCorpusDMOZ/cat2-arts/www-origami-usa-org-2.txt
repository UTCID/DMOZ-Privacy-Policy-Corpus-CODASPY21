Privacy Policy
OrigamiUSA does not sell or trade user's information with any third party. We don't use any third-party advertising or tracking services. We don't accept third-party ads. If you buy something from us, we'll send your information to our payment processors, but we do not retain your credit card information at all.
Users that have created an account on our website can edit their own information at any time on their user account page. There you can also set more fine-grained privacy settings, including opting out of the (relatively few) emails that we send to our members and friends.

Donor Privacy Policy
Our Commitment to Our Donors
We will not sell, share or trade our donors’ names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.
This policy applies to all information received by Honolulu Theatre for Youth, both online and offline, on any Platform (“Platform”, includes the Honolulu Theatre for Youth website and mobile applications), as well as any electronic, written, or oral communications.
To the extent any donations are processed through a third-party service provider, our donors’ information will only be used for the purposes necessary to process the donation.

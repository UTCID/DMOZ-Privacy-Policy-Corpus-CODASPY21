Update: Authorities have located Jaden Caro and he is no longer considered missing endangered.Multiple police departments are working together to locate a missing 19-year-old who was last seen in Iowa. South Sioux City Police Department in Nebraska announced they are searching for Jaden Caro who was last seen at 5 a.m. Thursday. Authorities said he was last seen in Newton and Marshalltown. Police believe he could have been traveling to Chicago. Caro is about 5 feet, 10 inches tall and weighs about 245 pounds. He was driving a 2006 gray Hyundai Sonata. Anyone with information about Caro's whereabouts should call the South Sioux City Police Department at 402-494-7565.

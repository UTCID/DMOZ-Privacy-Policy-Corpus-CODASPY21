The Local Development Plan (LDP) sets out the future shape of Pembrokeshire Coast National Park.
The LDP identifies what can be built and where. It tackles the need and locations for housing, employment, community facilities, retail, open space and other land uses.
The links below provide information on the Local Development Plan and the relevant Guidance Notes.

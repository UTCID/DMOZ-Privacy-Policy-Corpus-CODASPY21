PRIVACY POLICY
This is the website of Music Theater Works
Our office address is 516 4th Street, Wilmette, IL 60091-2829.
We can be reached via email at info@MusicTheaterWorks.org, or by telephone at (847) 920-5360.
For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer’s domain names, but not the email address (where possible).
Music Theater Works will not trade or sell your email address or telephone number to any other business or organization.
When you order tickets, or ask to join our mailing list, and give us your email address, you may receive periodic emails from us about new productions, discount offers or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such emails, please let us know by emailing info@MusicTheaterWorks.org, and including your name, customer ID (if applicable) and email address.
When you order tickets, or ask to join our mailing list, and give us your postal address, you may receive periodic postal mailings from us about new productions, discount offers or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please let us know by emailing info@MusicTheaterWorks.org, and including your name and postal address.
You may also receive postal mailings from other reputable arts organizations that may interest you. You can ask to have your name put on our “Do-not-share” list by emailing info@MusicTheaterWorks.org. Please give us your name and postal address and we will remove you from the list we share with other arts organizations (we share only postal address—no phone numbers and no emails).
When you order tickets, or ask to join our mailing list, and give us your telephone number, you may receive calls from us about new productions, discount offers or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such calls, please let us know by emailing info@MusicTheaterWorks.org, and including your telephone number.
This website may contain links to other websites. Please be aware that Music Theater Works is not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when you leave our site, to read the privacy statements of each website that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement policy applies solely to information collected by this website.
From time to time we may use customer information for new unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change in the future, we will post the policy changes on our website to notify you of these changes and provide you with the ability to opt out of these new uses. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our website periodically.
We collect information volunteered by the consumer such as survey information and/or site registrations.
Upon request we can provide site visitors with access to a description of the information that we maintain about them. Consumers can access this information by writing to us at the above address.
Upon request we offer visitors the ability to have inaccuracies corrected in contact information. Consumers can have this information corrected by emailing info@MusicTheaterWorks.org.
When you order tickets and/or donate online, you are redirected to a secure server. We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you at our site.
If you feel this site is not following its stated information policy, you may call us at (847) 920-5360, or email info@MusicTheaterWorks.org.

Your privacy is important to us at The Cleveland Jewish News (“CJN.org”). Accordingly, we have developed this Privacy Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate, disclose, and make use of your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use carefully. By visiting this website you are agreeing to be bound by the Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy.The Types of Information We CollectWe gather information from you in three ways:1) Information Gathered by Voluntary SubmissionYou are not required to provide information about yourself when visiting CJN.org. However, to make use of certain features on our website, such as requesting subscriptions or e-newsletters, entering contests, sending letters to the editor, accessing interactive forums and submitting information for lifecycle events, visitors need to provide personal information as part of the process. We may ask, for example, for:Contact information, including, name, address, email address, phone number, facsimile number, and mobile number;User name, password, and password reminder questions and answers;Age and date of birth;Other demographic information, such as gender, job information, and postal code;Public information, including profile information, you provide CJN.org for the publication, such as your user name, comments, likes, interests, status, pictures and the address of your website;Communications preferences, such as which newsletters you would like to receive;Search queries;If you are ordering a subscription, we will ask for a credit card number and date of expiration.In limited circumstances, payment and identification verification information, such as a credit card number, social security number or comparable national identifiers, and driver’s license number where needed for payment, to complete a particular transaction, or to access a particular service (e.g. contests);Information posted in community discussion and other interactive online features;Correspondence you sent to us; andInformation collected about you offline (including via facsimile, postal mail, or in person and CJN.org sponsored events or contest entrance forms).The information you supply will help us satisfy your requests and allow us to offer more personalized features and make our site more useful to you. Supplying information is entirely voluntary. Please note that if you do not supply the information we request, you will not be able to access that service.2) Information Automatically Gathered About All VisitorsCJN.org may collect other certain types of information about you automatically, including,Your browser type and operating system;Your Internet Protocol (IP) address;Websites you visited before and after visiting CJN.org;Web pages and advertisements you view and links you click on within CJN.org;Unique identifiers, including mobile device information numbers, that can identify the physical location of such devices in accordance with applicable law;Information collected through cookies, web beacons and other similar technologies;Information about your Interactions with audio and video content, such as the type of content listened to (including music applications like iTunes, Spotify and Last.fm) and content viewed, and about your interactions with email messages, such as the links clicked on and whether the messages were opened or forwarded;Standard server log information;3) Information We Obtain From Other SourcesWhen permitted by applicable law, we may collect information about you from other sources, including through interactive applications (e.g. mobile devices, third party services, and embedded audio and video players), co-branded partners and websites (for example our Facebook page), and from commercially available sources (such as data aggregators and public databases). The following are examples of information we may collect from other sources:Name, postal address, and telephone number;Demographic data, such as age, gender, and income level;If you access third party services (e.g. Facebook, Google+ ,Twitter, or Yahoo) through a CJN.org service, your username and password for those services and other information available about you on those services, as described in our Social Networking Policy below;Your interests and purchase behavior;Publicly-observed data, such as activity on blogs, videos, and other online postings; andOther navigation data, such as websites visited and advertisements viewed or clicked on.The Privacy Policy does cover the practices of third parties, including our advertisers, partners, and other content providers and those that may disclose information to CJN.org. Please also note that all of the information we collect about you may be combined, including to help us tailor our communications to you and to develop rich online content and services across CJN.org.The Way We Use Information We CollectWe generally use information we collect about you to:Fulfill your requests for products, services, and information, including to send you electronic newsletters, authorize a purchase, and complete a transaction that you have requested;Enable you to participate in features such as surveys, polls, sweepstakes and message boards;Provide you with special offers and promotional materials on behalf of CJN.org, companies within our corporate family, and third parties, in accordance with applicable law;Analyze the use of CJN.org by visitors to our website and use of our services to understand and improve our offerings and, in accordance with below to produce anonymous or aggregated data and statistics that might help third parties develop their own products and service offerings;Customize the content you see when you visit CJN.org;Develop and provide advertising tailored to your interests;Prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities and otherwise in accordance with the CJN.org Terms of Use; andFor any other purposes disclosed to you at the time we collect your information or pursuant to your consent.We present the information we automatically gather about all visitors to our advertisers, in order to help them understand our audience and confirm the value of advertising on our website. We provide the information to the advertisers in the form of aggregated statistics on traffic to various pages within our website.We may also share the personal data you provide to us by voluntary submission with parties that market products or services that we believe may be of interest to you.

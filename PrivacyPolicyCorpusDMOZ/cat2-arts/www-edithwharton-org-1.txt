We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of any personal information that you give us. We will keep your information strictly confidential and will not sell, distribute, or share your information with any other organization. If you choose to donate to The Mount using our on-line donation form or online store, be assured that the security of your personal information and credit card information is handled throughout the transaction via Verisign, the nationally recognized provider of on-line security.
Your payment and personal information is always safe. Our Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software is the industry standard and among the best software available today for secure commerce transactions. It encrypts all of your personal information, including credit card number, name, and address, so that it cannot be read over the internet.

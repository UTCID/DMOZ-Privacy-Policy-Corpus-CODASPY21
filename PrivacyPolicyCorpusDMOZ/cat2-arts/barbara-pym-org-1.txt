Who we are
Our website address is: http://barbara-pym.org
The Society is based at St Hilda’s College, Oxford. The North American Chapter is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt educational organisation based in Cambridge, MA.
What data we collect
We only collect information (name, email address, phone, mailing address) from people who choose to purchase memberships or merchandise. We do not sell or share our membership list or any other information with any outside organization. We email current and former members with information about the Society and other Pym-related events; you may opt out at any time.
All financial transactions are handled through PayPal or St Hilda’s College, we do not collect any credit card or banking data on our web site.

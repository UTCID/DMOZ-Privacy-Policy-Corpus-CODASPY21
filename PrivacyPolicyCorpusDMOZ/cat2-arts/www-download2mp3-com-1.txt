Privacy Policy
The move of this site to Donationware status
means that we no longer collect any personal
information from our visitors on this site.
Donation Privacy
The Rotary Club of Croydon Whitgift have
partnerships with PayPal and BT that ensure
that we receive the minimal personal details we
need to deal with refunds for example. These
details can include names and e-mail accounts
so that we can communicate in the event of any
failures or problems. In the case of My Donate
BT process the personal information required
by HMRC for UK Gift Aid refunds.
ISP Site User Data
Our ISP collects usage data in order to provide
performance stats but no personal information
is held.
Google Site Use Data
This web site uses Google Analytics, a web
analytics service provided by Google, Inc.
(Google). Google Analytics uses cookies, which
are text files placed on your computer, to help
the web site analyze how users use the site. The
information generated by the cookie about your
use of the web site (including your IP address)
will be transmitted to and stored by Google on
servers in the United States .

to web site activity and internet usage. Google
may also transfer this information to third
parties where required to do so by law, or where
such third parties process the information on
Google's behalf.
Google will not associate your IP address with
any other data held by Google. You may refuse
the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate
settings on your browser, however please note
that if you do this you may not be able to use
the full functionality of this web site. By using

data about you by Google in the manner and for
the purposes set out above.

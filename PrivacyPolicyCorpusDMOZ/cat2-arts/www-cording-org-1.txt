Privacy Policy
Your PrivacyI believe your privacy to be important. To better protect your privacy I provide this notice explaining my online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, I make it available on my homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.
Product DeliveryMy digital products are delivered via a link sent by email. On rare occasions an email can be delayed or missed, or end up in a junk folder. If you don’t receive an email containing the download link within a few hours after placing an order, please contact me and I will send you a fresh download link.
Refund / Exchange and Limitation of LiabilitySince I offer non-tangible, irrevocable, digital goods, I do not issue refunds based on satisfaction. All sales are final and I do not offer exchanges on my music. You are responsible for understanding my refund policy before making a purchase.

(i) any incidental, consequential, or indirect damages (including, but not limited to damages for loss of profits, business interruption, expenses, other damages or costs, attorney’s fees, or any other negative impacts) arising out of the use of or inability to use my music, or download of my music, or any delay in receiving my music, or
(ii) any claim attributable to errors, omissions, or other inaccuracies in my music and/or musical materials downloaded or delivered from this site.
All the music found here is protected by international copyright. You may not modify, copy, reproduce, republish, upload, post, transmit, or distribute, in any manner, the music from this site. You may download and print music you have purchased from this site for your own use.
Collection of Personal InformationWhen visiting Cording.org, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement and gather broad demographic information for internal use. Most importantly, any recorded IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.
Any personally identifiable information you may choose to provide, in order to obtain newsletters, services or products, is never shared with any third parties except as may be required by law to comply with issues of national security or law enforcement. I do not sell your personal information to anyone for any purpose. Period.
I make every reasonable effort to safeguard your personal information, but please recognize that no procedure or method employed to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure is ever 100% secure.
Also be aware that you are responsible for the personal information you share in publicly viewable areas, such as comment posts, etc., and that information may become public immediately. Please take care when using these features.
Links to third party WebsitesI have included links on this site for your use and reference. I am not responsible for the privacy policies on these websites. You should be aware that the privacy policies of these sites may differ from my own.
Changes to this Privacy StatementThe contents of these statements may be altered at any time, at my discretion.
If you have any questions regarding the privacy policy of Cording.org you may contact me by using the email link appearing at the bottom of every page.

MenuAbout UsDEFENDING ARTISTIC FREEDOMHow We AdvocateHow we advocateFreemuse defends the right to artistic freedom worldwide. We advocate and take action to free artists, change repressive laws and fight censorship.
We use our thorough research and documentation of violations of artistic freedom to influence governments and decision-makers to change laws and practices that limit artistic expression.
We take action on behalf of individual artists and audiences whose rights to artistic freedom have been violated.

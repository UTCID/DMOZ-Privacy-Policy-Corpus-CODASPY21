Privacy PolicyLast update: 2019-02-04INTRODUCTIONThe protection of your privacy and personal information is important to Fremont Symphony. In this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy"), you will find information regarding how we collect, use and otherwise process your personal information.This Privacy Policy applies to the website fremontsymphony.org as well as all related Fremont Symphony websites, mobile applications, social media accounts and services that link to this Privacy Policy (collectively, the “Website”) unless we have posted a specific privacy policy to a particular website or service. In that case, that specific privacy policy will apply.Moreover, when you use the Website, you also have responsibilities which are outlined in the Terms and Conditions section of the Website. We recommend that you consult these Terms and Conditions prior to using the Website in order to inform yourself of your obligations.This Privacy Policy covers all personal information we collect, use and otherwise process when you interact with the Website or with us. All personal information will be processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy and with applicable laws, which can vary depending on your location of residence. If you do not agree with any provisions or changes to this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Website.Personal information is collected, held and more generally processed by Fremont Symphony, it’s owners and applicable employees, management staff and marketing affiliates ("we", "our" or "us").If applicable, for legal residents of the European Union, note that the processing of your personal information is founded on at least one of the following legal bases:You have consented to the processing activity;The processing activity is necessary for compliance with one or more legal obligations;The processing activity is necessary for the performance of a service you have requested;The processing activity is necessary for one or more of the following legitimate interests pursued by us, our affiliated entities and/or a third party:To better understand our customers’ interests and preferences by analyzing, profiling and monitoring their behavior on the Website in order to globally improve the Website, our products and services;To offer you an optimal customer experience, namely by tailoring the Website, our products and services (including advertisements and e-mails) to your interests and preferences;To ensure the proper functionality and security of the Website.These processing activities benefit both us and you without undermining or overriding any of your rights, interests or fundamental liberties.Note that when the processing of your personal information is based on consent, you can withdraw such consent at any time by contacting us.We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy to adapt it to any technological or legal change, to adjust it to reflect changes made to the Website or for any other purpose. All updates to our Privacy Policy will be posted on this page or otherwise mentioned to you if required by applicable laws.HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATIONWe collect personal information under the circumstances listed below. In certain circumstances or for certain purposes, providing your personal information is voluntary (for example, when you sign up to FAE Club when made available). However, in other circumstances, providing your personal information is mandatory and we may not be able to provide you with the services or products requested if you choose not to provide such information. For example, if you purchase a ticket online, some personal information is needed to be able to send you the ticket.We collect your personal information in the following circumstances:When you sign up for the Fremont Symphony newsletter;When you purchase a Fremont Symphony, tickets, products, merchandise or any item made available through Fremont Symphony;When you interact with the digital content on the Website;When you participate in a contest and/or a survey;When you send us an inquiry or otherwise communicate with us;When you apply for a job, volunteer opportunity or any offer made through the Website;When you provide us with your personal information while onsite at an event, such as when you log in to the Wi-Fi network or participate in onsite technology such as surveys or games;When you post comments on the Website;When you choose to give us permission to access your information from third party websites (for example, by linking to social media networks like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.).Some of your personal information may be provided by our affiliated companies and/or commercial partners, including companies that sell our products, tickets for shows and/or special offers. All efforts will be taken to ensure that information will only be shared if it is to benefit both you and us.WHAT INFORMATION IS COLLECTEDUnder the circumstances listed in the "How We Collect Information" section of this Privacy Policy, the following information may be collected:Personal InformationTo facilitate reading, the expression "personal information" is used throughout this Privacy Policy. For interpretation purposes, this expression shall have the meaning set forth in the legislation that is applicable to you. For instance, for legal residents of the European Union, “personal information” shall be read throughout this Privacy Policy as “personal data” as defined under the General Data Protection Regulation. In any case, we treat any information that makes it possible to identify you as an individual as personal information. For example, we consider your given name, surname, date of birth, postal address, telephone number, e-mail address, mobile number, as well as any information that is considered personal information under applicable laws to be personal information.We collect and process the following categories of personal information:Contact information, such as your given name, surname, postal address, telephone number, e-mail address;Economic information, such as financial information that may be provided in connection with our services (including to purchase tickets to shows, to purchase gift cards, to purchases special offers or services provided through the Website);Login information (if applicable), such as the ID and password you use to connect to your account on our Website;Interests and preferences information;Any other information that you provide directly and voluntarily, including any message you send to us by e-mail, mobile phone, social media access and/or by direct contact.Information Collected Automatically (‘usage information’)We, as well as third-party advertisers, service providers and content providers, may use a variety of cookies and similar tracking technologies (“Cookies”) that automatically collect certain Website usage information whenever you visit or interact with us ("usage information”). Usage information may include without limitation your information about your browser, operating system, all areas within our sites and e-mails that you visit, including content that you browse, and the time of day, among other information. Usage information may also include your IP address or other unique identifier ("device identifier") for the particular device you use to access the Website. A device identifier is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer, mobile phone, or other device used to access the Website and our servers identify your device by its device identifier. Subject to applicable laws, some mobile service providers may also provide us or our third-party service providers with information regarding the physical location of the device used to access our Website.Some usage information may be considered as personal information under certain laws, such as the IP address under the laws applicable in the European Union. To the extent that we combine usage information with your personal information, we will treat the combined information as personal information under this Privacy Policy.Cookies can be used by us and/or by third parties for analytics purposes and to send you interest-based advertisements. We use cookies and similar technologies to run this website and help us understand how you use it.Information about someone elseIf you submit information on behalf of another individual (for example a friend, a family member, a member of a group you are affiliated with organizing), you must ensure that you have their consent to share their personal information with us and you confirm that you have this consent.Children’s InformationWe take children’s privacy seriously.

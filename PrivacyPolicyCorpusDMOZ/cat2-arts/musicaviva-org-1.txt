Privacy Policy
Musica Viva NY is committed to protecting the privacy of our patrons, friends and guests. The information that you choose to provide Musica Viva NY including name, email address, mailing address, and phone number will not be sold or given to any other organization.
If you no longer wish to receive concert announcements from Musica Viva NY via email, please notify us at info@musicaviva.org and put “unsubscribe” in the subject line.
You may also ask us to remove you from our postal mailing list via email, phone or in writing at any time.

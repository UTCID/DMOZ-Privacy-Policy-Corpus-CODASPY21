Privacy & Confidentiality Policy
This policy outlines practices for this website, which is maintained by the Society of Design Administration Canada (SDA Canada.) ConfidentialityThe SDA Canada respects the confidentiality concerns of our website visitors. It is the policy of the SDA Canada that no personal information such as full names, addresses, telephone numbers shall be collected automatically from visitors to this site. Such information is known only to SDA Canada when it is voluntarily submitted or posted to us. Information that is voluntarily provided may be forwarded to the appropriate representative of the Society for the purpose of a response or service. This information is not saved or disclosed outside the Society.Contact & Financial InformationOur site may use forms to place orders and/or request information. We may collect visitor's contact information (like their email address) and financial information (like their account or credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders and information about our organization to purchasers. The purchaser's contact information is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary. Financial information that is collected by SDA Canada is used only to bill the user for products and services.Financial Information provided to PayPal for the purchase of certain products and services is retained by PayPal and not divulged to SDA Canada. Use of PayPal services is subject to their Terms and Conditions.CookiesWhen you visit our site, we may place a "cookie" on the hard drive of your computer to track your visit. A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your hard drive through your web browser that can only be read by the web site that placed the cookie on your hard drive. For example, we may place a cookie on a visitor's computer when he/she visits the Site. The next time someone visits the Site using the same computer, our server will recognize the cookie and the visitor. The cookie allows our site to identify you so we can tailor the presentation of certain pages, or deter you from viewing a specific page again. Cookies are also used during the processing of purchases and payments through PayPal. Certain web browsers may allow you to block the use of cookies, but this may affect the navigation of our site. If you are concerned about the use of cookies, we recommend that you delete your Web Browser's cache after visiting our site.Involuntary Information CollectionCertain non-personal information about our visitors is collected by the standard operation of the Society's Internet servers, such as Internet browser types being used, operating systems, and the domain names of Internet providers. Uses of this information include internal review of the aggregate number of visits to our site, but only in a non-personally identifiable form. We may also use this information to make our site better and more responsive to the needs of our visitors. We do not disclose this information outside the Society.External LinksThis site contains links to other sites. SDA Canada is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.SDA Canada may modify, alter or otherwise update this policy at any time, so visitors are encouraged to review it from time to time.If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: webmaster@sdacanada.com

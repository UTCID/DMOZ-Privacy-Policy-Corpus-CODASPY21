1) You ask to be notified by us through any one of our membership or
e-commerce applications
2) peaceloveproductions.com has important web site updates, web sweepstakes or

and we terminate your account.

decision, simply contact us and we will promptly remove your email address
from our mailing list.

email to you nor will we supply any third party with your email address unless
you specifically request that we do so. Peaceloveproductions.com will only
share personal information with third parties when:
1) We need to share this information to provide a service or product you have
requested.
2) A court order or legal process requires us to relinquish this data.

policies.

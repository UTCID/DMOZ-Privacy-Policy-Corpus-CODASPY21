Shipping Information
Shipping and handling charges are determined by the merchandise dollar amount of your order before any deductions.
All orders are subject to verification.
Delivery dates at checkout are estimated times provided by the carrier.  Floyd Rose is not responsible for any shipments that do not arrive by the shipping carrier's estimated delivery date.
International Shipping Restrictions
Unfortunately, due to certain embargo restrictions, we currently do not ship all products to all countries.
Countries That Are Restricted From All Shipments:
Cuba (BIS).
Iran (OFAC).
North Korea (BIS).
Sudan (OFAC).
Syria (BIS).
Customs and Import Duties
Your order may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are levied once your package reaches your country.
Floyd Rose does not collect duties and taxes and cannot predict what your particular charges may be. If you do incur these additional charges, they must be rendered in order for your package to clear customs.
For more information regarding your country's custom policies, please contact your local customs office.
Free Shipping
Free shipping is available to continental US orders only with a total product value of $100 or more.  The Free Shipping option is only for the most economical shipping method and does not include any expedited shipping options.
Refund Policy
We want to ensure our customers are satisfied with our products. If you aren't satisfied for any reason with a product purchased from us, we will gladly issue a refund of the cost of the item received by floydrose.com within two weeks of the purchase date.  Please note that unless the return is due to an error or malfunction on our part, we will deduct the cost of shipping and impose a 10% restocking fee from the refund.  Items returned must be in original condition with original packing slip enclosed, with reason for return indicated. Products must be purchased from floydrose.com to be eligible for refund.Floydrose.com is not responsible for returned items lost or damaged in transit.We recommend purchasing insurance for the item being sent for return from the shipping carrier of your choice. To contact us concerning a return, please call us at 732.919.6200 or send an email to returns@floydrose.com. We will issue you a return merchandise authorization number and provide return shipping details. Only once an item has been received into our warehouse, inspected, and processed, we will then either send a replacement or issue a refund. We'll notify you via e-mail of your refund once we've received and processed the returned item. You can expect a refund in the form of original payment within 7-14 business days of our receiving your return.

What is The Smurfs - The Lost Episode?
The Lost Smurf Episode is a parody. It is also a Flash animation which features very rude video and audio. The featured song, which includes the lyrics "Papa Smurf can I lick your (expletive)", is definitely for mature audiences.
Who wrote The Smurfs The Lost Episode?
"The Lost Episode" was created by Double G Productions.

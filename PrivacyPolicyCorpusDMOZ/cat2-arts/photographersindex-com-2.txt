Privacy Policy
Photographers Index is committed to protecting online user
privacy. In order to ensure your privacy, information Photographers Index collects through our web site at www.photographersindex.com ("Site")
is used only in the manner and for the purposes described in this
Photographers Index Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy.")
User Consent to Privacy Policy
Any
person accessing, browsing, or otherwise using the Site, either
manually or via an automated device or program, shall be considered a
"User." All Users are bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy. Users
consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of personally
identifiable User information ("Information") pursuant to the terms of
this Privacy Policy.
Information Collected from Users
Photographers Index collects the email address of Users that email a
question or comment to Photographers Index or submit a customer
review.
Photographers Index collects the billing and
shipping addresses, including relevant e-mail, phone and credit card
numbers and expiration dates of Users who purchase a product from
Photographers Index through the Site. This includes Users who
register to become Photographers Index members or enter any Photographers Index contests or other promotional features available on the
Site.
Through the use of "cookies" (For more
information regarding Cookies, see below), Photographers Index may
collect and store anonymous information relating to Users' browsing
patterns, including, for instance, the User's browser version, site
referral information, IP address, operating system, and other technical
Site use information.
Cookies
The Site uses a
feature of Internet Web browsers called a Cookie, which is a file that
a User's Web browser places on a User's computer's hard drive, to
assign an identification code to the computer and to collect anonymous
browsing information. On this Site, cookies are used in conjunction
with session variables to track a User's shopping cart and expire as
soon as an order is completed or if a shopping cart sits idle for a
lengthy period without being checked-out.
Photographers Index does not use Cookies to retrieve Information about Users from a
User's computer unless the User knowingly and willingly provided such
Information.
You may set your Internet
Web browser to notify you when you receive a cookie or to prevent
cookies from being sent. If you prevent a cookie from being sent, you
may limit the functionality Photographers Index can provide you when
you visit the Site, especially when purchasing an item on the Site.
Use of Information
Photographers Index uses the Information collected from its Users to:
Respond to Users' questions or comments;
Administer a User's contest entry or entry in other promotional feature;
Fulfill a User's purchase request and notify Users of their order status; and
Provide
Users with important functionality changes to the Site, new Photographers Index services and special offers we think you will find
valuable.
Disclosure of Information to Third
Parties
Unless
we have your consent or except as required or permitted by law, Photographers Index will not sell, share, trade or give away any Information
that it collects or receives regarding its Users.
Photographers Index may disclose information about our Users to others if we
have a good faith belief that we are required to do so by law or legal
process, to respond to claims, or to protect the rights, property or
safety of Photographers Index or others. Photographers Index may
disclose Information to third party in the event that a third party
acquires Photographers Index or its assets. (See, Sale or
Acquisition section below.)
Linked Internet Web Sites
The
Site may provide hyperlinks, which are highlighted words or pictures
within a hypertext document that, when clicked, take you to another

different than this Site. Photographers Index is not responsible for
the collection, use, or disclosure of information collected through
these web sites, and Photographers Index expressly disclaims any and
all liability related to such collection, use, or disclosure.
Children

Photographers Index Site is not directed towards children under 18
years of age and Photographers Index does not knowingly collect any
information from children under 18 years of age through the Site.
Security
Photographers Index has security measures to protect against the loss,
misuse, and alteration of the Information under our control, including

When Users place orders or access their account information, the Site

protect the Information we collect from you from unauthorized access.
Unfortunately, no system is perfect; therefore, Photographers Index

agree that we may collect and use your Information, as discussed above,

Brothers’ Ink Productions promises never to publish, sell or give away any personal information such as a name and email address to anyone, including 3rd parties without your exclusive written permission.
We may gather your name and address on this site and on our Facebook Fan page, but will keep the information you give secure and private. We will contact you only for the news and updates or specific purpose you signed up for in a contact us form or Fan page form.
We may use your email address to send you Service-related notices (including any notices required by law, in lieu of communication by postal mail). We may also use your contact information to send you marketing messages, if they correspond to the film, book or project that you have specified an interest in. If you don’t want to receive these messages, you can opt out by following the instructions in the message. If you correspond with us by email, we may retain the content of your email messages, your email address and our responses.

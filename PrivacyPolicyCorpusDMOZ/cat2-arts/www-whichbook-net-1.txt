Privacy Policy
We are committed to making sure your privacy is protected.
Information we collect
If you contact us we will collect your email address.
How we use information
We use this information for:
internal record-keeping
contacting you for feedback
emailing promotional materials which may be of interest to you
Security
We have physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
Cookies
A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer’s hard drive. We use session cookies which are destroyed when your browser closes and occasional cookies to help track your preferences. We use Google Analytics to anonymously track site usage.
Links to other websites
Our website contains links to other sites. Once you leave our website, we cannot be responsible for privacy and protection of information you provide to websites outside our control.
Controlling personal information
We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we are required by law. You may request details of personal information which we hold about you at any time.

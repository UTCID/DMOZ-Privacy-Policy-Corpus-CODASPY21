What is My United States of Whateva?
My United States of Whateva is political satire Flash animation based on Liam Lynch's song, "United States of Whatever". Liam Lynch is known for MTV's Sifl and Olly TV show. Although at first glance the animation appears to be anti-George W. Bush, the parody actually has supporters on the right and left of the political spectrum. Look closely - there is a loveable Smurf dancing along throughout the song!
Who wrote the United States of Whatever animation?
John & Richie created the My US of Whateva animation.

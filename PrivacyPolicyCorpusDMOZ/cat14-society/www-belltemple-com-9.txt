Web Site Disclaimer
Information submitted through this website is forwarded to a Bell Temple LLP lawyer who will determine if you are eligible to for the pro bono legal advice service.
Eligibility is restricted to health professionals who have purchased liability insurance through an association that has an arrangement with Bell Temple LLP for the pro bono legal advice service.
The service pertains to questions regarding prevention of malpractice claims and College Complaints.
Questions regarding other areas of the law are not covered by the pro bono service but we may provide you with a referral to another lawyer or other resources.
The pro bono program provides for a total of 30 minutes of service per year, including the time spent reading and responding to emails, research and telephone consultations.
Time incurred in excess of 30 minutes per year will be billed at an agreed hourly rate.
By submitting the form, the user consents to and authorizes:
Release of your personal information to a Bell Temple LLP lawyer for the purpose of responding to your question, or for referral to another resource in the event your question falls outside the parameters of the pro bono program,
Receipt of electronic messages or telephone contact from a Bell Temple LLP lawyer in response to your enquiry.
The use of this web site does not create a lawyer-client relationship. Bell Temple LLP does not guarantee the security of any communications made through this web site. Do NOT include any private health information or information that would serve to identify a client or other individual.

CSHP Website Privacy Policy
Similar to most websites, a limited amount of information is collected when you visit our site (e.g., IP address, referring and requested web pages, browser, date and time).  The sole purpose of collecting this information is to improve the use, functionality, content, and performance of our website.
The only personally-identifiable information we collect is if you join our mailing list to receive publication alerts and other research notices.  This information is never sold or provided to third parties.
Please consult the individual privacy policies of any other sites reached through our website as we are not responsible for their content or data collection practices.

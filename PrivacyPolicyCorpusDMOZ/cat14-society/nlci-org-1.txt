NLCI Privacy Policy
NLCI believes in the importance of privacy and in the protection of young children. We strive to assure compliance with our privacy policy and we would never sell or rent out your information.
The NLCI website is the center of our communication with our constituents and supporters. Through interaction online we stay connected and gather information to assure the effectiveness of the website.
NLCI collects information from you when you visit our website, join our organizations or request materials. We do not sell your personal information. We use information collected to keep you informed and to support the activities of NLCI and affiliated organizations. We may disclose information when necessary to protect the legal integrity of the organization or as required by law.
We participate in interest-based advertising and you may see advertising on our website. We will make every effort to work with advertisers who are committed to the well-being of children. You can opt out or unsubscribe if this is a problem and if appropriate we would want to hear from you.
NLCI is committed to protecting young children online and their privacy. In order to protect children, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 without the permission of their parent or guardian. Please contact us if your child’s information was erroneously collected so we may delete it.
You can contact us through our website on any feedback/question, your website experience, our initiatives and general questions or concerns.

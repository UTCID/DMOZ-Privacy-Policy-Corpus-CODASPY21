Privacy Policy
YWCA Toronto respects your privacy and adheres to all legislative requirements with respect to protecting privacy. Personal information collected by YWCA Toronto from donors, members and other supporters is used to process and receipt donations, to help us better understand our supporters, to keep them informed about YWCA Toronto program developments and activities and to ask for their support of our mission to improve the lives of women and girls.
We also acknowledge the support of donors in publications such as our Annual Report. We do not sell, trade or rent our donor lists.
We protect your personal information through staff and volunteer training about privacy issues, the signing of confidentiality agreements by those who access personal information in order to perform their duties, the use of locked files, a secure computer system and the shredding of discarded personal information.

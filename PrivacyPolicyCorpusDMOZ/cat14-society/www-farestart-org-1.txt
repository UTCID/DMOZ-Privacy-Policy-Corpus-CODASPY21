Privacy Policy
Your privacy is important to us. When you make a donation to FareStart, whether online, by mail or phone, or in person, your personal information will not be shared with anyone other than FareStart and the online payment processor, if required, to securely process the transaction.
Through our website we collect information from users only in connection with financial transactions such as online donations, sales of tickets to special events, and other similar purposes that are clearly and explicitly identified. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others under any circumstances. FareStart does not utilize any means of information collection or third parties to collect information and create profiles of visitors to our website. Like most standard web servers, we use log files that include internet protocol addresses, type, internet service provider, referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp and clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in aggregate. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, or if you would like to review or modify information previously provided, please contact us: info@farestart.org or 206.443.1233.

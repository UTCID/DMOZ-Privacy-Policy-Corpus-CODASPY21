Prep for Degree Study : FREE 6 Week Evening Course!
At Carmel College we provide access to eduroam (education roaming). This is a wireless network available at many different colleges and universities in the UK.
If you visit another academic institution that has also signed up to eduroam, you can use their Wi-Fi network with your Carmel College email address and password.
Click here for more information.

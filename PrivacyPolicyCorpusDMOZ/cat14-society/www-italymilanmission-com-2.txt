Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy:italymilanmission Inc. ("italymilanmission") operates italymilanmission.com and may operate other websites. It is italymilanmission's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.
Website Visitors
Like most website operators, italymilanmission collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. italymilanmission's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how italymilanmission's visitors use its website. From time to time, italymilanmission may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.
italymilanmission also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on italymilanmission.com blogs/sites. italymilanmission only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site where the comment was left.
Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to italymilanmission's websites choose to interact with italymilanmission in ways that require italymilanmission to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that italymilanmission gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up
at italymilanmission.com to provide a username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with italymilanmission are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process those transactions. In each case, italymilanmission collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with italymilanmission. italymilanmission does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.
Aggregated Statistics
italymilanmission may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. italymilanmission may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, italymilanmission does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.
Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information
italymilanmission discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on italymilanmission's behalf or to provide services available at italymilanmission's websites, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using italymilanmission's websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. italymilanmission will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, italymilanmission discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when italymilanmission believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of italymilanmission, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user of an italymilanmission website and have supplied your email address, italymilanmission may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with what's going on with italymilanmission and our products.

Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. italymilanmission uses cookies to help italymilanmission identify and track visitors, their usage of italymilanmission website, and their website access preferences. italymilanmission visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using italymilanmission's websites, with the drawback that certain features of italymilanmission's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
Business Transfers

You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of italymilanmission may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.
Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by italymilanmission and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

If you have a italymilanmission.com account, you might also receive an alert informing you of these changes. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.

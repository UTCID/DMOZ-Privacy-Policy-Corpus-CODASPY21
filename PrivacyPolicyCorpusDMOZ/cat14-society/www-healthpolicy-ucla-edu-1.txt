Privacy Policy
Information Gathering
As with most sites on the Internet, when you visit this Web site, we collect information regarding your Internet location (IP address), which pages you visit on our site, from which web page you came to our site, which software you use to visit our site, and your software's configuration. None of the information is associated with you as an individual.
Use of Information
Our Web site does not collect personally-identifiable information unless you submit it yourself to join a mailing list or to otherwise make direct contact with our Center. We never provide or sell any personally-identifiable information to outside agencies or third parties.
We collect information to improve the functionality and content of the this Web site and to monitor its performance. Sometimes data are aggregated and analyzed to help answer specific questions about the usage and performance of the Web site or individual web pages.
Contact about the Website
If you have any questions about the practices of this site or your dealings with this site, you may contact the web administrator at gdriscoll@ucla.edu.
Disclaimer
This Web site provides links to other Web sites within the UCLA network and to some outside of the 'UCLA.edu' domain. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.

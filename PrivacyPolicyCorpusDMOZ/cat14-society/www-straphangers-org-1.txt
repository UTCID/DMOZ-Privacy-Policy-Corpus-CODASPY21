Contacting Us
This privacy statement discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for www.straphangers.org. This website was created and is managed by the Straphangers Campaign, a project of the New York Public Interest Research Group Fund, NYPIRG.
Your identity and contact information will be treated confidentially and will not be shared.
The names and addresses of those who fill out forms on straphangers.org, are stored in a database. This database may be used for future activities such as action alerts and campaign updates.
The Straphangers Campaign will not sell or give away any personal information you provide (such as your name or contact information), nor will it make available your information to any outside organization without your express consent.
Outside Links
This site contains links to other sites. www.straphangers.org is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Web Site Statistics
Statistics kept on this site aggregate usage information for campaign evaluation purposes. These statistics do not reveal personal information.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.
This policy can be updated at any time, without providing notice to subscribers.
Contacting us
If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us.

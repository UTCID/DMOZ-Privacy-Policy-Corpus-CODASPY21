Our privacy policy is simple. We do not collect any information about you when you visit this website and we don’t store any cookies on your computer.
If you choose to send us a message through our Contact page then we will obviously collect the information that you provide on that page. We will retain this information only for as long as it takes us to deal with your enquiry and then we will delete it. We will not pass on your details to anyone other than the person (or people) in our church who are best able to deal with your enquiry.

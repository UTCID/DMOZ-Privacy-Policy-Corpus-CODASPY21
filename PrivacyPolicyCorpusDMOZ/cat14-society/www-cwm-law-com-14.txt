Guilford D. Ware
Mr. Ware is Of Counsel with CWM, practiced primarily in the areas of Maritime Law, Civil Litigation and International Commerce. He has broad experience in all matters of admiralty and maritime law, including Jones Act, collisions, salvage, pollution, cargo and vessel arrest/attachment. Mr. Ware is a former Managing Partner of the Firm and a Retired Lt. Colonel in the United States Air Force.

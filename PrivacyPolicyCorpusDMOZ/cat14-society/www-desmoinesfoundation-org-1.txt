Privacy Policy
Privacy Statement
Your right to privacy is very important to us. We recognize that providing the Community Foundation of Greater Des Moines your personal information is an act of trust.
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.  If you provide us with your name and address, you may receive periodic mailings from us with news and information about the Community Foundation and its programs and events.
We do not provide any personally identifiable information we gather or develop about our visitors to any third parties for any purpose. If you are a Community Foundation fund holder and you use our online tool to manage your fund(s), you provide personal information to authenticate your identity. That information is only used on this site and is not distributed or sold to any other party or site.
If you have any questions about this privacy statement contact us at Community Foundation of Greater Des Moines, 1915 Grand Avenue, Des Moines, IA 50309.

Privacy Policy
Good Shepherd Services is committed to protecting the privacy of its supporters.
Data collected from site visitors via both the donations page and contact forms, as well as via the Human Services Workshops registration form, is not shared with third parties.  In accordance with PCI best practices, no payment data is stored on our website. All credit card data transmissions to and from the website, and to and from Authorize.net, our credit card processing company, are secured via Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption.
Non-payment information is only used for website reporting and tracking purposes and not shared with third parties.
The information we collect is used to improve the content of our web page and used to notify consumers about updates to our web site, and not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes.For each visitor to our web page, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer’s domain name, but not the e-mail address (where possible).
We collect only the domain name of visitors to our web page, the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via-e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations.
If you do not want to receive e-mail from us in the future, please let us know by contacting us via the website to us at the above address and telling us that you do not want to receive e-mail from our Organization.If you supply us with your postal address on-line you may receive mailings from us with information on our services or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive such mailings, please also let us know by contacting us via the website .

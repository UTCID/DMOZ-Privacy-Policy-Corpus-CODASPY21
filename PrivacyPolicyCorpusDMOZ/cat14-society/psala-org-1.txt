Privacy Policy
Personal Information We Collect Online
Personal Information means personally identifiable information such as information you provide via forms, surveys, applications or other online fields including name, postal or email addresses, telephone, fax or mobile numbers, or account numbers.
How We Use Personal Information
We may use Personal Information: to respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests; to inform you about important information regarding the Association, products or services for which you  may be interested in, or in which you are already enrolled, to deliver marketing communications that we believe may be of interest to you, to allow you to participate in surveys.
We may combine this online information with information collected from offline sources or information we already have; for business purposes.
How Personal Information is Shared
Personal information is not shared with any entity outside of the membership organization to which you belong.
Website Cookies
We use cookies and similar technologies to run this website and help us understand how you use it. Cookies are used for analytics purposes only and for us to see which pages get the highest ranking so information can be added to most viewed pages.
Please feel free to contact us at webmaster@psala.org for additional questions.

A Kid's Heart Privacy Policy
Sharing
is Nice, But Don't Share Information Without Your Parents Knowing About It!
A Kid's Heart does not collect, access, nor maintain any personal
information
A Kid's Heart does not install cookies on your computer in attempt to
acquire any personal information.
We do not acquire or collect any personal information from sponsors or from
the external services.
Web site statistics is viewed from the information
provided by Google Analytics. No information is further collected, archived or
shared by A Kid's Heart.
We do not provide any information to third parties.
We use non-personalized advertisements from Google Adsense. The non-personalized ads don’t use cookies or mobile ad identifiers for ad targeting. However, they do still use cookies or mobile ad identifiers for frequency capping, aggregated ad reporting, and to combat fraud and abuse. For more information about Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/product-privacy
Please also see: http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp
Information on how to block cookies can be found in your browser's help files.
To block cookies on Google Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en
To block cookies on IE: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows-vista/Block-or-allow-cookies
To block cookies on Firefox: http://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/block-websites-storing-site-preferences
To block cookies on Safari: http://support.apple.com/kb/ph5042
We also sell ad space directly to individual buyers and companies. These ads are not enabled to install cookies.

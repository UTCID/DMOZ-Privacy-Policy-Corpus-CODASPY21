Privacy Policy
Privacy and personal information security is very important to us and Providence Animal Center fully respects the privacy of our adopters, donors, clients and volunteers.  Providence Animal Center collects personal information from our supporters to properly acknowledge gifts, enable transaction processing and send future correspondence – unless otherwise requested. Providence Animal Center does not sell, trade or share any supporter information to outside agencies, nor does it send information on behalf of other organizations.
Anyone who participates in our services, events or online marketing will be placed on our mailing list unless otherwise requested.
Reach our Adoption Center by calling or texting our NEW NUMBER: (610) 566-4575. For Vet Services, call or text (610) 566-1370.

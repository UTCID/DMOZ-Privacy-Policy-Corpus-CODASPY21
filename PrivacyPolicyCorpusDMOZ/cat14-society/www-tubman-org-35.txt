Melissa's Connections GroupNote: Due to COVID-19, this group is temporarily on pause. Please call Tubman Chrysalis Center at 612.870.2426 for more information or options.
Melissa’s Connections Group is a free, drop-in support group for women experiencing chemical and mental health issues or concerns. No registration or fee is required. This group meets at Tubman Chrysalis Center on Thursdays from 1:30 to 3:00 p.m.
This group is for women who:
Are waiting to begin individual or group counseling.
Need extra support during a difficult period.
Are seeking counseling that does not require insurance.
Are looking for access to additional community resources.

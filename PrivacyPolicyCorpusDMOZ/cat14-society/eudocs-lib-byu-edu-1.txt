EuroDocs:Privacy policy
Contributors to the EuroDocs website are welcome.
However, a sign-in and password are necessary in order for you to add and edit material; thus your contributions will be evaluated for retention and you will not be anonymous.
In all other ways, your privacy is sancrosanct.

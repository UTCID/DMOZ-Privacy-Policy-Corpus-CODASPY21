Membership Automatic Renewal Policy
Membership Automatic Renewal Policy
Automatic Renewal Authorization Your membership selection will be processed immediately and the credit/debit card you provide will be charged the price shown. Depending on billing option chosen, your membership will continue to renew monthly or annually unless we are otherwise notified. You will receive several electronic reminder notices, at the primary email address you provide, prior to the expiration of your membership specifying the amount you are to be charged for the next billing cycle. You authorize the account you provide to be charged the membership rate on the notice unless you choose to cancel. Cancel anytime at 800-333-4499. Please print/screenshot this page for your records.
Terms & Conditions
By selecting automatic renewal, you understand you are enrolling in A.R.E.’s Automatic Membership Renewal program. Depending on billing option chosen, your selected payment method will be charged every month / year until you tell us to cancel. Your selected payment method will be charged the annual auto-renew rate within 7 days of your renewal date. Additionally, you are agreeing to receive digital automatic renewal notices at the primary email address you have provided to A.R.E.
Enrollment in A.R.E.’s Automatic Membership Renewal can be canceled at any time by calling 800-333-4499.
Enjoy your A.R.E. Membership! If we can assist you in any way, please call Customer Service at 800-333-4499. Please print/screenshot this page for your records.

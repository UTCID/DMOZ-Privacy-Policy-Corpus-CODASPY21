In operating the Site, we may include registration, and other online forms that ask users to provide their names, e-mail addresses, and other contact information. Otherwise, we do not collect personal information or individually identifying information from any user, other than user names and passwords for private client websites. We do not direct any of our content to children under 13 years of age, and if we learn that a Site user is under age 13, we will promptly delete any personal information we may have collected from that user.

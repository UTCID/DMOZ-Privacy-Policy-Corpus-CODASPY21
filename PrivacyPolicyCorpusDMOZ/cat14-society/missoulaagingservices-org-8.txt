Independent LivingMany older adults prefer to age in place in their own homes and communities. Missoula Aging Services (MAS) can connect you to housing options and other services that support independent living.
Housing options in Missoula CountyMAS maintains a list of available housing options, including:
Emergency housing
Low cost and subsidized housing
Retirement communities
Organizations that can help you find housing, or help you stay in your current home
Some independent living residences and communities provide meals, housekeeping services, and social activities.
Independent Housing List
Resources to support living independentlyMAS offers a number of programs in support of independent living. These include:
Nutrition programs such as home-delivered Meals on Wheels,
In-Home Support Services for help around the house
Help with finances such as paying bills and utilities.
Volunteer Senior Companions can assist with daily living tasks and provide companionship to help reduce social isolation and feelings of loneliness.
Personal Emergency and Alert SystemsMAS can also help individuals who may need to communicate medical needs to first responders in an emergency. These range from medical alert bracelets to devices that can immediately connect to 911.

Safe Journeys Transitional HousingSafe Journeys Youth Transitional Housing is currently full and not accepting applications.
If you are in need of immediate services, please call Tubman's 24-hour resource and crisis line at 612.825.0000.
Young people of all ages, backgrounds, and experiences are at risk for being trafficked. Domestic violence and homelessness are leading risk factors for sexual exploitation.
Safe Journeys Transitional Housing provides a limited number of rooms for youth and young adult victims of violence and exploitation, including sex trafficking and homelessness. We offer customized individual and group support as well as resources to help with safety, basic needs, school, parenting, legal, financial, spiritual, social, emotional, recreational, leadership, career and other self-identified goals.
Available for youth and young adults of all genders ages 16-20 (and up to age 24 with variance approval), Safe Journeys is located at our Maplewood location, Harriet Tubman Center East, 1725 Monastery Way, Maplewood MN 55109.
To learn more about Safe Journeys, you can call 651.789.7130.
If you are in need of shelter or in crisis, call Tubman's 24-hour resource and crisis line at 612.825.0000.

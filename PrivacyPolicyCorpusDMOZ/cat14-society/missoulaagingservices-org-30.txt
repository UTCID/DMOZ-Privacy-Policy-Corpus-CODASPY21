Call Missoula Aging ServicesLooking for more information or trying to figure out where to start? Just call us at (406) 728-7682 to talk to a highly skilled member of the friendly Call Center team.
Call Center staff are trained to provide immediate help with a wide range of issues. If we aren’t able to help right away, we’ll work with you to schedule a Resource Center appointment or refer you to the appropriate resource for your need.
When is the Call Center open?The Missoula Aging Services Call Center is open from 8am to 5pm Monday through Friday, except holidays.
Call us at (406) 728-7682 and a friendly Call Center staff member will help you.
Call Center staff can also assist in scheduling appointments with the Missoula Aging Services Resource Center for additional help on an issue. Click here for more information about types of appointments available through the Resource Center.

Aish HaTorah-VFJ offers various plans for using and paying for the Aishaudio Download Service, including but not limited to those described below. All such plans for using and paying for the Aishaudio Download Service shall be referred to herein, collectively, as "Aish Plan(s)".
A. Individual Plan. This is the default Aishaudio Download Plan. If you are on the Individual Plan, you pay for the Aish Content you select and your credit card is charged each time you make a Purchase.
B. Monthly Program Plans. A Monthly Program Plan is for an automatically renewable one (1) month term unless you have purchased a Device in connection with your Monthly Program Plan, in which case the minimum term of your Monthly Program Plan will the term specified at the time your purchased your Device. At the end of your minimum term, your Monthly Program Plan will automatically renew for one (1) month terms thereafter. Each month, on the day of the month that you signed up for the Monthly Program Plan ("Renewal Date"), Aish HaTorah-VFJ will charge your credit card the relevant monthly fee for your chosen Monthly Program Plan ("Monthly Fee").
You may use the Monthly Fee to Purchase any Aish Content to which you are entitled under your chosen Monthly Program Plan but it may not be applied to purchases of Devices, other hardware or accessories. If you do not Purchase all the Aish Content to which you are entitled under your chosen Monthly Program Plan within one month, you may carry forward entitlement to such Aish Content to the subsequent month(s) up to a total of 18 units. No entitlement to such Aish Content may be carried forward past your date of termination, if you choose to terminate your membership. If you make Aish Content Purchases in excess of the Aish Content to which you are entitled under your chosen Monthly Program Plan, your credit card will be billed the appropriate additional amounts as described in Paragraph A hereof for the Individual Plan. "First month free offers" applies one per user and one time only, regardless of the number of accounts you've opened under various email addresses, or if you've previously cancelled and are restarting a monthly plan, and your card will be charged $10 for each additional monthly account you create after the first one.
You may upgrade your Monthly Program Plan category on the Aishaudio Download Site by yourself. You may, subject to any minimum term commitment that may apply, downgrade your Monthly Program Plan category by contacting Customer Service via telephone at (800) VOICES3. Such Aishaudio Download account changes will be effective as of the date of your request. You agree to pay any additional fees that may be applicable to your new Monthly Program Plan.
C. Cancellation of Your Aish Content Plan. You may cancel your Monthly Program Plan by contacting the Aish HaTorah-VFJ Customer Service Department via telephone at (800) VOICES3. Aish HaTorah-VFJ will be entitled to rely on a notice of cancellation that appears legitimate and cancel service on that basis. Aish HaTorah-VFJ 's responsibility for improper cancellation is limited to reinstating your Monthly Program Plan or Subscription or to offering a refund, at Aish HaTorah-VFJ 's sole discretion. The consequences of and charges associated with cancellation are as follows:
Monthly Program Plans. If you cancel your Monthly Program Plan, your Aish HaTorah-VFJ will default to the Individual Plan.
Without a Device. If your Monthly Program Plan does not include the purchase of a Device, you may cancel your Monthly Program Plan at any time within the first month of its commencement without any charge or penalty and your credit card will be credited with the Monthly Fee billed for your chosen Monthly Program Plan. If you do not cancel your Monthly Program Plan, it will be automatically renewed each month for additional one (1) month terms and your credit card will be charged on the monthly Renewal Date with the relevant Monthly Program Plan Monthly Fee. You may cancel your Monthly Program Plan at any time after the initial month term and be billed for the month of cancellation but for no subsequent months.
With a Device. If your Monthly Program Plan includes the purchase of a Device, you may return your Device and cancel your Monthly Program Plan within the first month and you will receive a full refund for the Device less shipping, handling and restocking fees and charges on the Aish HaTorah-VFJ Plan for any Aish Content you may have Purchased. You must return the Device within seven (7) business days of the cancellation date or your credit card will be billed the relevant cost of the Device plus applicable tax. You may cancel your Monthly Program Plan after the first month, however, if you do so, a charge for the number of months remaining on your minimum term commitment will immediately be charged to your credit card and you will no longer be entitled to any Aish Content. For example, if you choose to cancel your Monthly Program Plan in the third month of twelve (12) month minimum commitment Plan, you will immediately be billed for the nine (9) months remaining of your commitment and you will not be entitled to any Aish Content after the date of cancellation.

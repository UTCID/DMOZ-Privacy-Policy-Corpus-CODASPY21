Learn more about our Volunteer Attorney opportunities at TubmanFor over 35 years, Tubman’s team of pro bono attorneys have been serving our community to support individuals and families as they work towards safety, hope, and healing. Whether you are a practicing family law attorney or work in another specialty, we invite you to join our cause!
Family Law Volunteers
Tubman provides case screening, consultation with our staff attorneys, community resources for clients, and malpractice insurance for your pro bono work. Read more about our Family Law Programs here.
Tubman Safety Project
Tubman provides training and resources for all volunteers, case screening, consultation with our staff attorneys, community resources for clients, and malpractice insurance for your pro bono work. Learn more about joining the Safety Project team here.

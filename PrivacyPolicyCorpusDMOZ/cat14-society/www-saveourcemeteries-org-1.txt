How To Help - DONATEYour gifts, both large and small, will help Save Our Cemeteries achieve our mission of preserving, promoting, and protecting the historic cemeteries of New Orleans.
Did you know?
$5 will purchase a bag of sand
$10 will purchase a box of gloves
$25 will purchase a box of graffiti cleaning solution
$50 will purchase 2 boxes of contractor grade trash bags or 10 scrub brushes
$75 will purchase a bag of lime
Save Our Cemeteries thanks you in advance for your generous support to preserve, promote, and protect New Orleans' "cities of dead"!

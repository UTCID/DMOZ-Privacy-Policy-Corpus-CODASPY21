Privacy and Cookies
The following document relates solely to information gathered via this website - www.queenscollege.org.uk.
What we collect, and how we use it
Personal Information
Our website features a small number of forms in which you are asked for personal information such as your name and contact details. In these circumstances, you will be anticipating a direct response from Queen’s College. The information you provide will be retained purely for internal use and communicating directly with you. We fully respect the privacy of parents, prospective parents, guardians and Old Queenians, and will never pass this information on to a third party.
Third party websites
Although we provide links to many third party websites, Queen’s College bears no responsibility for the content of these websites as they do not fall under our control. Please refer to the privacy statements of those websites for details on how they use your information.
Contact Us
If you wish to let us know directly about your experience of our website, or flag up any problems you may have encountered while using it, you can email the webmaster by CLICKING HERE.
Privacy
Please see our privacy notice which can be found by CLICKING HERE. This explains your rights under the Data Protection Act and how you can get in contact with us if you have any issues.

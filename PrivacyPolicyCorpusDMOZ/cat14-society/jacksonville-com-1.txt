Police want help with Jacksonville hit-and-run investigation
Dana Treen
Thursday
Sep 16, 2010 at 6:04 PM
Police are asking the public for help in identifying a pickup truck involved in a hit-and-run accident in Jacksonville that left a man in life-threatening condition.The accident occurred about 6 a.m. Sept. 10 in the 14400 block of Wonderwood Expressway when a truck described as a tan 1993 Ford F350 pickup with dual rear wheels struck bicyclist Brady McTigue, 46, of Duval County as he was riding eastbound on the bridge, according to the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office.The right side passenger mirror struck McTigue, knocking him from his bicycle.Anyone who was in the area of the Wonderwood Expressway Bridge, just east of Girvin Road and may have witnessed the accident can call the Traffic Homicide Unit at (904) 630-2178 or the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office at (904) 630-0500 or e-mail JSOCrimeTips@jaxsheriff.org.

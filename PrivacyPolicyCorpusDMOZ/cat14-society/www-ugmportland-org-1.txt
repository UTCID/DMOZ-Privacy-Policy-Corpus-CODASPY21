Privacy PolicyUnion Gospel Mission of Portland values the privacy of our friends, partners and supporters. We will never sell, trade nor exchange your confidential information. The only mailing list to which your name will be added is our own. If at any time you wish to have your name removed from our mailing list, please contact donor relations at 503-274-4483 or carlp@ugmportland.org and we will be happy to honor your request.

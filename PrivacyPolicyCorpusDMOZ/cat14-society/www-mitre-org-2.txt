Space Policy Podcast
Print
MITRE has joined forces with the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA), the Space Foundation, and the U.S. Chamber of Commerce to produce a podcast series on space entitled Space Policy Pod. The podcast examines events and ideas affecting the space sector.
Each episode features discussion on relevant space topics with prominent policymakers from the field.

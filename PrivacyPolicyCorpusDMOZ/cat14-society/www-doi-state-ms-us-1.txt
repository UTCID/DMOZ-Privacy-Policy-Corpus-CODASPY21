Regulations
MID Regulations are promulgated pursuant to statutory authority and have the force of law. They are filed with the Mississippi Secretary of State's Office
and are the topic of a public hearing before they go into effect.
The regulations are available for viewing in the Adobe Acrobat format.
To search for a particular regulation, you may use the search feature in your web browser by pressing "Ctrl" + "F" on your keyboard (Command + "F" on a Mac).

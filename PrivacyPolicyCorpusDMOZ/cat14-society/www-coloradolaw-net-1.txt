(303) 222-2222 Bachus & Schanker, LLC Privacy PolicyBachus & Schanker, LLC. (“Bachus & Schanker, LLC“) is a lawfirm that operates several websites including coloradolaw.net, masstortlaw.net.com, louisianajustice.com and several other topically-based websites. It is Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.Website VisitorsLike most website operators, Bachus & Schanker, LLC collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s visitors use its website.Gathering of Personally-Identifying InformationCertain visitors to Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s websites choose to interact with Bachus & Schanker, LLC in ways that require Bachus & Schanker, LLC to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Bachus & Schanker, LLC gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who fill out one of our free case assessment forms to provide their name, email address and contact phone number, as well as details about their potential case. Bachus & Schanker, LLC collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with Bachus & Schanker, LLC. Bachus & Schanker, LLC does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from Bachus & Schanker, LLC contacting them about their potential legal case.Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying InformationBachus & Schanker, LLC discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s behalf or to provide services available at Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s lawfirm, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using (filling out) Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s case assessment forms, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Bachus & Schanker, LLC will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Bachus & Schanker, LLC discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Bachus & Schanker, LLC believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Bachus & Schanker, LLC, third parties or the public at large.If you have provided Bachus & Schanker, LLC your email address for the purpose of case assessment and/or signing up for our newsletter, Bachus & Schanker, LLC may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new legal information, firm news, (in the case of newsletter sign ups) or email you via a third-party to fill out a post-case client feedback survey (only for confirmed clients of Bachus & Schanker, LLC). Both our newsletter and our post-case surveys include opt-out links to stop us from further contacting you.CookiesA cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Bachus & Schanker, LLC uses cookies to help Bachus & Schanker, LLC identify and track visitors, their usage of Bachus & Schanker, LLC website, and their website access preferences. Cookies are also used in conjunction with third-party applications, such as our chat with us now feature. Bachus & Schanker, LLC visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s websites, with the drawback that certain features of Bachus & Schanker, LLC‘s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.Business TransfersIf Bachus & Schanker, LLC, or substantially all of its assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Bachus & Schanker, LLC goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Bachus & Schanker, LLC may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Privacy Policy
The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank shares your concerns about privacy on the Internet. In order to better protect your privacy, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank has provided this notice explaining its online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used on the Site. Furthermore, this Privacy Policy, being part of the Terms, works in conjunction with the Terms and Conditions of Use, but does not modify or supercede it.
We will not sell, trade or share your personal information with anyone else, and we will not send mailings on behalf of other organizations. We will only share personal information if you give us specific permission to do so. If you wish to be removed from our mailing list, please contact us at 330.535.6900 or info@acrfb.org.
The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank's Collection and Use of Your Information
In the course of using the Site, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank may ask you for your name, e-mail address, postal address, telephone number, fax number as well as other relevant information. The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank also may collect certain non-personally identifiable information when you visit many of our web pages such as the type of browser you are using, the type of operating system you are using, the domain name of your Internet service provider, aggregate data about the number of visits to the Site and/or aggregate data about the pages visited.
How The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank Uses the Information
The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank uses the non-personally identifiable information ("Aggregate Data") to improve the design and content of the Site and to enable the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank to personalize your Internet experience. The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank may use the Aggregate Data to analyze Site usage as well as create services and/or products to fit your needs. Also, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank shares this Aggregate Data with the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank's related entities, advertisers, partners and/or other third party vendors. The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank shall own such Aggregate Data and maintain copies of such as part of its records.
Except as otherwise indicated, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank uses the personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you provide about yourself: (a) to respond to any of your inquiries or questions about our services; (b) for internal marketing purposes and as part of the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank's donor database; (c) to provide you additional and updated information, materials and other advertisements regarding the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank services; (d) to offer you other materials and/or information that the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank believes may be of interest to you; (e) to cooperate with government officials or parties in litigation under process of law, or as otherwise required by law; (f) to protect the security or integrity of the Site; (g) to protect against a threat of safety or destruction of property; and/or (h) to protect against legal liability. In all cases, however, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank agents, employees and contractors who have access to Personal Information are required to protect this information in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Policy.
Furthermore, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank encourages all its business Partners to adopt and adhere to standards of protection consistent with this Privacy Policy. The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank will not use the Personal Information, except as set forth herein, without your permission; provided, however, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank shall own all such Personal Information and maintain copies of such as part of its records. If you do not wish to receive any such information or to allow the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank the right to use your personally identifiable information as described, please contact the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank in the manner identified in the "Opt-Out" section below. The Aggregate Data and Personal Information may be transferred, distributed or sold as part of the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank or its assets.
Opt Out
You may choose not to receive such information and/or to allow the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank to use your information in the manner described above by simply notifying the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank directly at www.akroncantonfoodbank.org or at 330-535-6900. Upon receipt and processing of an "opt out" request, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank will, within its commercially reasonable amount of time, remove your information from any future marketing communications.
Security; No Liability
The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank has implemented security measures to help protect against the risk of loss, misuse and alteration of any information under the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank's control. The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank protects your online order information by using Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL") technology. SSL encrypts your order information to avoid the decoding of that information by anyone other than the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank. Nevertheless, such security measures may not prevent all loss, misuse or alteration of information on the Site, and the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank is not responsible for any damages or liabilities relating to any such security failures. You understand that data and communications, including e-mail and other electronic communications, may be accessed by unauthorized third parties when communicated over the Internet. Furthermore, the Site contains links to other web sites. The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank is not responsible for nor can the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank ensure the privacy practices or content of such other sites.
IP Addresses and Cookies
Like other web sites, the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank's servers, to administer the Site and for security purposes.
"Cookies" are pieces of data sent from a Web server to a Web browser that enable a Web server to identify users that visit a site hosted by that server. The Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank may use Cookies in order to identify you to our site so that the Akron-Canton Regional Foodbank can provide more customized information and services to you. In addition, our site may contain advertisements that may contain Cookies. In general, if you choose, you can disable the use of Cookies by reviewing your browser's preferences and options.

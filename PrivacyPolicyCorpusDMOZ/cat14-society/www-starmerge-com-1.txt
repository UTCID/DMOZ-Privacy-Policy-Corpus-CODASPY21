Cookies
Cookies
are a technology which can be used to provide you with tailored
information from a Web site. A cookie is an element of data that a Web
site can send to your browser, which may then store it on your system.
You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie,
giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it.
We do
not make use of HTTP cookies.

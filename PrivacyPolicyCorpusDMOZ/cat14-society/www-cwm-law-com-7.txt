Kambria T. Lannetti
Kambria is a member of the firm's Litigation team and focuses her practice in the areas of Admiralty and Maritime, Business Disputes, Government and Public Sector, and Railroad and Transportation.
She holds a J.D. from the Regent University School of Law, and a B.A. from Christopher Newport University. Prior to joining the firm, Kambria practiced family law in the Hampton Roads area.

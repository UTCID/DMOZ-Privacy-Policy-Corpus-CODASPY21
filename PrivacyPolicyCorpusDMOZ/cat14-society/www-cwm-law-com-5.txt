C. Wiley Grandy
Wiley focuses his practice on admiralty and maritime litigation, complex business litigation, business transactions, and employment law.
He regularly assists businesses in navigating all manner of legal issues that arise during the business's lifetime from entity formation, asset purchases and sales, contract negotiation, and litigation.
Wiley also advises clients in the healthcare industry, including on Medicare over payment disputes, HIPAA/HITECH compliance, corporate restructuring, redevelopment and housing authorities, special purpose authorities, and local, state, and federal governments.
Prior to joining the firm, Wiley served as a law clerk for the 4th Judicial Circuit of Virginia. He received his B.A. from the University of Virginia and his J.D. (cum laude) from the University of Richmond’s T.C. Williams School of Law.

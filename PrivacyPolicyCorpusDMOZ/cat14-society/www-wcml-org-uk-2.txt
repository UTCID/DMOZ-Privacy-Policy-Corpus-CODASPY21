Cookie policy
What are cookies?
This website, along with most other major websites, uses cookies. A 'cookie' is a small piece of data sent from a website and stored in a cookie file on your computer's hard disk while you are browsing a website.
Two types of cookies are used on this website:
Session Cookies. These are temporary cookies, which only exist until you close the browser you are using to access the website.
Persistent Cookies. These are cookies, which remain in your cookie file after you have visited our website. These cookies help us identify you when you return to our site.
What do we use cookies for?
None of the cookies used on our website collect personally identifiable information about you.
We employ cookies for the following purposes:
Maintaining user preferences. Our website has a link to allow you to view the site with a larger font size, for example - a cookie is set to allow that preference to remain between page visits and on your future return to the website.
Monitoring our website traffic. We use Google Analytics, a popular web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics uses cookies to help us to analyse how users use the site. To find out more about how these cookies are used, visit the Google Privacy site
To allow certain functions and to help you navigate the website efficiently

How to control and delete cookies
We will not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information about you.
You can use the cookie bar at the top of this site to allow or block any non-essential cookies. However, if you wish to restrict or block all the cookies which are set by us, or indeed any other website, you can do this through your browser settings. The Help function within your browser should tell you how.
Alternatively, you may wish to visit www.aboutcookies.org which contains comprehensive information on how to do this on a wide variety of browsers. You will also find details on how to delete cookies from your computer (including those from this visit) as well as more general information about cookies. For information on how to do this on the browser of your mobile device you will need to refer to your handset manual.

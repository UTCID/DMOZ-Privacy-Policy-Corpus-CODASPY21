AmazonSmile
Did you know that you can support JFS when you shop on Amazon? Amazon will donate 0.5% of the price of your eligible AmazonSmile purchases to JFS! AmazonSmile is the same Amazon you know – same products, same prices, same service! Go to smile.amazon.com and select Jewish Family Service to receive the donation and go shopping! Every eligible purchase you make at smile.amazon.com will result in a donation to JFS.

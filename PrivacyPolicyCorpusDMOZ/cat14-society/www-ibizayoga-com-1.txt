Privacy Policy
Ibiza Yoga is committed to protecting your privacy. The storage and use of any information that we collect about you will be in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998.
The type of information we might collect includes: your name, address, phone number and email address.
Our aim is to ensure that any information we hold is accurate and up to date. However, if you find any inaccuracies, we commit to delete or correct it promptly.
We will never collect sensitive personal information without your explicit consent.
We will give you the chance to refuse any marketing email from us in the future.
We may use technology to track the patterns of behaviour of visitors to our site to see which sections are the most popular so that we can improve the service we offer. This could include using a "cookie" which would be stored on your browser. You can usually modify your browser to prevent this happening. The information collected in this way could be used to identify you unless you modify your browser settings.
If you have any questions/comments about privacy, or would like to check the information that we hold about you, please email us at privacy@ibizayoga.com.

Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Siddha Yoga Foundation
Australia is committed to the privacy of visitors to the "Siddha
Yoga Meditation Australia Website".
It is the policy of Siddha Yoga Foundation
Australia not to share your name or information with any unaffiliated
individual or organization. If for some reason this policy would
change in the future, Siddha Yoga Foundation Australia will not
share any identifiable information about you without your permission.
Registration Forms
There is no registration necessary to visit or participate in
this Website. However, certain portions of this site may condition
your access on the provision of personally identifiable information.
Also, portions of the site allow you to join our mailing list and
receive e-mail or regular mail information from us. These same sections
provide for the ability to opt-out of receiving future mailings
at any time. When making purchases from the bookstore, users will
be required to provide contact information such as their name, email,
and postal address and financial information such as credit card
numbers. Financial information that is collected is used solely
to bill the user for products and services.
External Links
This site may contain links to other web sites. Siddha Yoga Foundation
Australia Limited is not responsible for the privacy practices
or the content of such Web sites.
Policies for children (Individuals Under 13 years of Age)
This website does not target children (under
age 13) for collection of information online. We do not solicit
or knowingly collect user identifiable information on children under
the age of 13. No information should be submitted to or posted at
this website or to the Siddha Yoga Meditation on-line bookstore www.siddhayogabookstore.org.au by guests under
13 years of age. We do not knowingly accept online requests for
information from any of our departments from guests under 13 years
of age.
Additional Privacy Protection for Children
We encourage children to ask parental permission
before sending any information about themselves (name, address,
e-mail address, etc.) to anyone over the Internet. We encourage
Parents/Guardians to get involved with your children's online usage
and to be aware of the activities in which they are participating.
Choice/Opt-Out
Our site provides users the opportunity to opt-out of receiving
information from us .
1. Click here to change status or remove
yourself form the mailing list.
2. You can send mail to the following postal address:
Web Department, Siddha Yoga Foundation Australia, PO Box 2827
Fitzroy Vic 3065 Australia.
Data Quality/Access
This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying
information previously provided:
1. You can send email to webmaster@siddhayoga.org.au
2. You can send mail to the following postal address:
Web Department, Siddha Yoga Foundation Limited, PO Box 2827, Fitzroy Vic 3065, Australia.
Notification of Changes
Any change to our privacy policy will be
posted on our site so you are always aware of what information we
collect, and how we use it.
Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices
of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, you can contact: webmaster@siddhayoga.org.au

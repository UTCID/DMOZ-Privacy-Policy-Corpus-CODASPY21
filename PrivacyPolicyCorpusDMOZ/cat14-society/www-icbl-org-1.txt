Privacy Policy
The ICBL respects personal information that may be disclosed to it through its website. We are committed to protecting the privacy of users in accordance with high levels of privacy standards and confidentiality.
The ICBL does not share personal information with other organizations or companies.
We will never share, sell or rent individual personal information with anyone without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law.
Information submitted to us is only available to employees managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for information.
To unsubscribe from the ICBL-CMC newsletter, follow the instructions under Newsletter Subscription on the Newsletter page.

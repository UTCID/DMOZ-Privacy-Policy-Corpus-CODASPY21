Mason is an attorney with a passion for trial advocacy.An Austin native, and graduate from the University of Texas School of Law, Mason competed extensively in moot court and national mock trial competitions. A skilled trial advocate, Mason was inducted into The National Order of Barristers, and awarded the Niemann Cup – recognizing him as the best oral advocate of his graduating class. Moving forward from his success in law school, Mason joined Byrd Davis Alden & Henrichson to put his advocacy skills to use helping real people with real problems. Mason’s practice is 100% litigation, handling cases from personal injury to medical malpractice and business disputes.

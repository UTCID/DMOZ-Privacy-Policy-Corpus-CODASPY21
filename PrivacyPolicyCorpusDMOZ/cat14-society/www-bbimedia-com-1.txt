Privacy Notice
BBI Media and it's magazines SageWoman, PanGaia, newWitch and the Blessed Bee
does not rent, sell, or share your name, address, phone or email with any other
party.
You will receive renewal notices from us when your subscription is in
danger of expiring. Occasionally you may receive mailings or e-mailings from BBI
Media or our magazines promoting special offers or new products.
All magazine subscriptions, back issues, renewal notices and other items are mailed in opaque paper envelopes or boxes to protect your privacy. The outside of the envelopes are printed only with our company name BBI Media and return address.
If we start publishing an e-newsletter, we will only send it to you if you subscribed to
that list. We don't like junk mail either, so we will try to refrain from falling into that category in your eyes.
You are a very important person in our family of customers and we would like to give you good service. If you have a problem with our service, let us know and we will try to fix the problem.

Privacy Policy
Private Communities Registry (PrivateCommunities.com) has created this statement in order to
communicate our solid commitment to maintaining the privacy of all individuals who have entrusted our firm with their
personal contact information.
Email addresses, names, physical mailing addresses and other information provided to
PrivateCommunities.com are NEVER sold, rented, or provided to other companies.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our
Web site.  Your IP address is used to gather broad demographic information.
Our site uses cookies to keep track of a visitor's information when requesting information
for a given community.  Cookies set by the PrivateCommunities.com web server can only
be read by the PrivateCommunities.com web server.
This site contains links to other sites.  PrivateCommunities.com
is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Our site uses a request form for the convenience of visitors seeking specific information
about their selected community(s).  These requests are forwarded only to the community(s) specified.
Security
PrivateCommunities.com has high level security measures in place to protect the access to,
and alteration of, the information under our control.
Remove Information Option/Opt-Out
Our site provides visitors the opportunity to receive email updates of communities as they are added to this site. Updates are emailed once every two weeks.
In each email update, PrivateCommunities.com provides a link to unsubscribe from future emails, and anyone who chooses to unsubscribe is promptly removed from our email list.
Individuals may also remove their information from our private database in order not to
receive future communications by our firm by:
You can send email to info@PrivateCommunities.com
You can visit the following URL: www.PrivateCommunities.com/EmailList/Remove/
Correct / Update
This site gives users the following options for changing and modifying information previously provided.

This Donor Privacy Policy applies to all information received both online and offline, on any platform (website, social media, and associated linked sites), as well as any electronic, written, or verbal communications.
Our Commitment to Our Donors
Project Zawadi Inc. respects and protects the privacy of our donors. We will never sell, share, trade, or rent our donors’ names or personal information with any other entity, nor contact or send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations. The information you provide is used only to complete your donation, solicit your feedback, or share information related to Project Zawadi Inc.
The Information We Collect
Project Zawadi Inc. collects the following types of information:
personal information such as your name, mailing address, e-mail address, and phone
donation information such as payment method, credit card numbers, amount of donation, and intended use of donated funds
job, internship, or volunteer applications
periodic surveys soliciting feedback on our programs and communications
voluntary submissions requesting direct information or signing up to receive information from us via email subscriptions to newsletters or blogs
How We Use Your Information
To process, acknowledge, and communicate with you regarding your donations and any questions regarding the intent or use of your donation
To respond to your inquiries
To send you notices or our blog or e-Newsletter (if you have signed up to receive them)
For any other purposes disclosed at the time the information is collected or to which you consent

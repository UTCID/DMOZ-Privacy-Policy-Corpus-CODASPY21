PRIVACY POLICY
Persons requesting to be notified by the Authority when the Authority posts a consultation paper should be assured that the Authority takes the protection and proper use of your personal information seriously, and takes great care to safeguard information submitted to us.
We do not share personal information with any other person (including e-mail addresses); unless required to do so by law.
We do not use "cookies" or other mechanisms for tracking specific visitor habits or identifying returning visitors.
For information regarding our on-line privacy policy, please e-mail enquiries@bma.bm.

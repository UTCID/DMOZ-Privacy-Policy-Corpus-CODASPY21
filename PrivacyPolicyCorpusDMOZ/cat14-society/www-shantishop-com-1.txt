Our Privacy Policy:
The Shanti Shop only collects information about its customers that is offered willingly. We don't use cookies or other information tracking devices to monitor our customers. We use Google Analytics to help us improve the navigation and organization of our site, but not to collect private information about our customers.
Emails and orders you send us will be kept on file securely, but never shared with third parties.
We use Google Checkout to make your orders easy, fast, and secure.

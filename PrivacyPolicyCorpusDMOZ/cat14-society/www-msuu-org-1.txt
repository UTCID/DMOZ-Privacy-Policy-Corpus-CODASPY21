Privacy Policy
This web site is owned and operated by the Michael Servetus Unitarian Society (MSUS). We recognize that visitors to our site may be concerned about the information they provide to us, and how we treat that information. This Privacy Policy addresses those concerns. This policy may be changed or updated from time to time. If you have any questions please email us.
The first principle of the member societies of the Unitarian Universalist Association (UUA) is that we will covenant to affirm and promote the inherent worth and dignity of every person. We believe that this principle impels MSUS to protect the privacy of visitors to our website.
Personal Information
We only collect personal information from our visitors on a voluntary basis. Personal information may include name, address, phone number, email address. We do not require this information to obtain access to any part of the public portion of our site.
The personal information we collect is only used by us to process a request that has been placed by a visitor. Typically this is in the form of voluntary submittals of similar information for survey purposes or for event registrations.
We do not give, sell or rent this personal information to other organizations.
We Care About Doing It Right
In addition to maintaining privacy and security controls over information you share with us, MSUS works to accurately process your information. However, if inaccuracies occur, we will make every effort to correct them in a timely manner. To report any web site inaccuracies or for questions about MSUS's Internet Privacy Policy please email us.

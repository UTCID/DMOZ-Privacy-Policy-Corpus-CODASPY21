Privacy Policy for the Internet
This company is dedicated to protecting the privacy of the personally identifiable information
our visitors choose to share with us, wherever they live or travel.
We follow the principles of
disclosure and choice, which allows us to provide desired information and services to our customers
while protecting their personally identifiable information.
Personally identifiable information is
information that can be associated with a specific individual or entity, including, for example, a
customer’s name, address, 	or telephone number, e-mail address, personal preferences and
information about on-line activities that are directly linked to him/her.
Some of our web pages will send a "cookie," a small summary file of who you are, to your computer to
give you the courtesy of instant recognition the next time you visit, without bothering you with another
request to register. If you do not wish to be recognized refer to your Internet Browser to erase cookies
from your computer hard drive, block all cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored.

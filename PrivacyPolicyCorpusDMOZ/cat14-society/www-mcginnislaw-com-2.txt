Overview
McGinnis Lochridge is a member of SCG Legal (formerly the State Capital Group). Membership in this global network is by invitation only, currently comprising more than 145 preeminent, independent law firms worldwide.
SCG Legal provides our clients with a trusted resource for identifying local counsel worldwide. As a member firm, we can connect our clients to local or regional experts anywhere in the world, using the network to identify an attorney or attorneys of another member firm with the experience to help solve our clients’ legal and business challenges.
Visit SCG Legal's Site
Note: Member firms of SCG Legal practice independently and are not in a relationship for the joint practice of law.

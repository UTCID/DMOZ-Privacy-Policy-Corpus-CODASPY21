Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy, we provide this notice explaining our information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.
How We Use Your Information
We use the information you provide about yourself to complete orders and transactions, respond to inquiries about our products and services, acknowledge donations, and subscribe you to our mailing lists on your request. We also use it to inform you about new products or programs or for future updates and appeals for contributions. You will have the ability to unsubscribe from such lists. Your contact information will not be shared with outside parties.
Our Commitment to Data Security
To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
How to Contact Us
Should you have questions, concerns, or requests related to Engaging Schools’ privacy policy, please contact us at 800-370-2515, 617-492-1764, or info@engagingschools.org. You can also reach us by mail at 23 Garden Street, Cambridge, MA 02138.

Invitation to tender – Research project on effective literacy, numeracy and digital skills models and policies
The aim of the research project is to review and analyse existing data including NALA’s Guidelines for Good Adult Literacy Work (2012), NALA’s policy and practice research reports and evaluations and other relevant national and international data with a view to;
firstly; identify effective adult literacy, numeracy and digital skills models and policies, and
secondly, provide evidence of the benefit to adult learners of improved literacy, numeracy and digital skills.
Closing date for receipt of tenders: Friday 11 September 2020 at 5pm.
Return tenders to: tbyrne@nala.ie

At a GlanceGet Help
Food
Good food is an important part of good health. Missoula Aging Services is here to provide support through Meals on Wheels, nutritional supplements, and other opportunities. Find out more here.
Housing
Missoula Aging Services seeks to promote affordable housing that meets the needs of older adults. Click here for more information about both assisted living options in Missoula and support for continuing to live independently at home.
Medicare & Finances
Missoula Aging Services can help you understand your Medicare and Medicaid options, find ways to save money, or even provide money management services. Click here for more details.
Caregiver Support
Are you a caregiver for a family member or friend? Click here for information about ways Missoula Aging Services can help, including caregiver support groups, respite and homemaking services, and resources for caring for an older adult with dementia.
Daily Living
Many older adults are able to live independently at home. Find out more here about services that can help make daily living easier, such as transportation or help with chores and errands.
Elder Rights
Missoula Aging Services defends the rights of older adults through legal guidance, oversight of assisted living facilities, support for victims of Medicare fraud, and partnership with Adult Protective Services. Find out more here.
Dementia Resources
Dementia affects many older adults and their caregivers, and Missoula Aging Services is here to help. Click here for information and support.
Talk to an Expert
The Missoula Aging Services Resource Center team is ready to help over the phone, by email, or through one-on-one meetings. Click here to get started.

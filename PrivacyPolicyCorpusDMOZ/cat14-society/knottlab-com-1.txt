Privacy PolicyKnott Laboratory, LLC recognizes the need to protect all information deemed personal and private, and requires as a matter of policy that any and all information submitted online, such as name, company, email, phone, etc. be used solely for the express purpose of responding to your inquiry. All personal information is held in confidence, and is only shared with your express consent. It is not our policy to send unsolicited emails, and all users can unsubscribe from our email mailing list at any time.
Additionally, it is Knott Laboratory policy to prohibit the sale and/or other distribution of personal information to third party companies, and Knott Laboratory, LLC will enforce all privacy agreements to protect our customer information.
Knott Laboratory, LLC is committed to protecting your privacy, and all persons with any privacy concerns or questions are asked to contact Knott Laboratory, LLC for further clarification.

We understand that privacy is a major concern for Web users and work to enforce ethical policies that govern the gathering and usage of sensitive information on our website.We do not sell customer information to any third party, nor do we employ cookies or other means of active-user tracking. Our Web-server software passively gathers anonymous, general information such as Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, and time spent on different pages.

The Georgia Public Policy Foundation’s Web server does not automatically recognize a visitor’s e-mail address. We do collect information on which pages visitors access or view, and store information volunteered by the consumer, such as site registrations. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page. We use cookies to record past activity on our site in order to provide better service when visitors return to our site.
If you sign up to receive email but later decide you do not want to receive e-mail, please send an email requesting to be removed from the email list or follow the instructions to unsubcribe yourself. We do not share our email lists with other organizations.
Thank you for your interest in the Georgia Public Policy Foundation.

HARRIET'S CLOSET IS CURRENTLY CLOSED.
Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, and to protect the health and wellbeing of Tubman's clients, residents, staff, and volunteers, our community clothing closet is closed until further notice. We apologize for the inconvenience. Please call our 24-hour resource line at 612.825.0000 with any questions or for other service updates during this time.
Harriet's ClosetHarriet’s Closet is a clothing boutique that offers new and gently used clothing for youth and adults of all genders at no cost. Volunteers work directly with individuals to select up to three complete outfits every 30 days.
At Harriet Tubman Center West, Harriet’s Closet is open:
Mondays from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m., Wednesdays from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. - Individuals of all genders
Mondays from 12 p.m. to 2 p.m. - Male-identified individuals
Tuesdays and Thursdays from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. - Female-identified individuals
At Harriet Tubman Center East, Harriet’s Closet is open Tuesdays and Thursdays from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. to individuals of all genders.
Each day that Harriet’s Closet is open, clients must arrive 30 minutes before close. For more information, call 612.825.3333

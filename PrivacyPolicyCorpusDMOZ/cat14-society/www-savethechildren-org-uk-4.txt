Writing or updating your will is a significant personal decision, and we understand that your loved ones come first. But we also know that our life-saving work wouldn't be possible without gifts in wills and the generous support of hundreds of people like you.
If you would like to know more about how a gift in your will can change the future, as well as how to write or update your will, download our free will writing guide now.

A gift in your will can help empower children around the world and here in the UK, helping them reach their full potential and build a better future together. We depend on gifts in wills in order to deliver our life-saving work, and children like Alice, Adam and Siraj will never forget your generosity.
Thank you for considering this life-changing decision and leaving a gift that lasts a lifetime.
*Names changed to protect identities
Save the Children are a member of Remember A Charity, a collaboration of over 200 charities that raise awareness of the importance of gifts in wills.

(St Paul, Galatians chapter 3 verse 28)Young and old, student and teacher, local and foreigner, man and woman, black and white, short and tall, poor and rich, happy and sad...All Are Welcome!WHO ARE WE?Heslington Church (St Paul's) is a local ecumenical partnership (LEP) between the Church of England and the Methodist Church together with the Roman Catholic Chaplaincy to the University of York. All services of worship are held jointly between Anglicans and Methodists. Our congregation is an eclectic mix of ages, backgrounds and nationalities. We seek to be an open, caring and inclusive church and everyone is welcome to worship with us.

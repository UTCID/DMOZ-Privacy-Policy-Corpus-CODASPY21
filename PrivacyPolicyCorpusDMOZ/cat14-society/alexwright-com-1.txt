Privacy
By Mark Bain
September 6, 2018
Who I am
Hi, I’m Alex Wright.  My website address is: https://alexwright.com.
What personal data I collect and why I collect it
I do not collect any personal data about visitors to this website. However, please keep in mind the following:
Analytics
While I do not track visits to my website, my hosting provider does provide some anonymous visitor stats, such as total page hits, geolocation based on IP addresses, and so on.
Embedded content from other websites
Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.
These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.
Contact forms
If you contact me via the contact form on this website, the information you provide will be forwarded to my email account, just like if you sent me an email directly. No additional copies of this information are stored on the server or elsewhere. I’ll keep your information as long as I feel it’s useful to do so, or until you ask me to delete it.
Cookies
This site doesn’t use cookies.
Contact info
If you have any concerns about privacy, please contact me.

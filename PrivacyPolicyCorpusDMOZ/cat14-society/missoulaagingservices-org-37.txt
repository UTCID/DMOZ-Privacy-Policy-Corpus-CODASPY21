Elder Justice Program
The Elder Justice program provides no cost guidance and assistance navigating the legal system for older adults, and those who care for them. Click here for more details.

The Long-term Care Ombudsman program advocates for the legal rights of residents of assisted living and long-term care communities. Find out more here.

Click here for immediate steps you should take if you believe an older adult is being harmed or neglected.
Senior Medicare Patrol
Montana Senior Medicare Patrol is a statewide program to help Medicare recipients prevent fraud, correct billing errors, and take charge of their medical bills. Learn more here.

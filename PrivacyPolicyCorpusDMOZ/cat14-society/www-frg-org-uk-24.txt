Who is the group for?
The group is for carers who are approved Kinship Carers, those who have a Special Guardianship Order on a child or young person.Most are grandparents though not all.  Most of the parents of the children will have issues such as drugs, alcohol, domestic violence etc.
Where does the group meet?
Basildon
Who runs the group?
Essex County Council
How often does the group meet?
Last Friday of the month, excepting the school holidays

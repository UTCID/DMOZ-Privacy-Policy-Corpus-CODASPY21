Privacy Policy
All information collected on this site will be kept strictly confidential and will not be sold, leased or rented to parties outside NPI. We will not disclose your information to third parties not involved in any transaction process, or use it for other purposes, such as unsolicited mailings.
A number of other organisations’ websites have links on our site. If you link to their sites, they may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. Such sites are not within our control and are not covered by this privacy policy.
If you post offensive, inappropriate or objectionable content anywhere on or to www.npi.org.uk or otherwise engage in any disruptive behaviour on www.npi.org.uk, NPI can use whatever information that is available to it about you to stop such behaviour.

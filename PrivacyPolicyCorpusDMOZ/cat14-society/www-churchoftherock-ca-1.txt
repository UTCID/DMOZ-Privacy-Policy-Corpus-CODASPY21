Statement of Privacy and Security
Church of the Rock is committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to Church of the Rock web site and governs data collection and usage. Church of the Rock is a general audience website, intended for users of all ages. Personal information of all users is collected, used and disclosed as described in this Statement of Privacy.
Collection of your Personal Information
As an E-commerce Website, Church of the Rock collects personal information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address and telephone number.
Information collected by Church of the Rock is used solely for the purpose of billing and shipping your online purchases or billing your event registration fees.
Church of the Rock does not collect any information about your computer hardware and software.
We are not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Church of the Rock website, including websites that link to the Church of the Rock website.
As a faith community Website with interactive forms, Church of the Rock collects personal information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address and telephone number.
Information is collected to facilitate inquiries, responses to inquiries and fulfillment of ministry services.
We adhere to a very strict privacy policy—we will not give your name or e-mail address out to outside parties under any circumstances.
In support of Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation, we do not send Commercial Electronic Messages without having received consent. Individuals may always freely opt out of our electronic communications (which are non-commercial in nature) by replying with “unsubscribe” or contacting our office by telephone (204-261-0070).
Use of your Personal Information
Church of the Rock collects and uses your personal information to process product orders and deliver the products requested. It is possible that Church of the Rock may also use personal information to inform you of other products or services available but will not sell or disclose your personal information to any other entity.Church of the Rock does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists, or any information therein, to third parties.Church of the Rock may access and/or disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Church of the Rock or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Church of the Rock, including its website; or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Church of the Rock or the public.
Transmission and Security
Church of the Rock ensures that all sensitive personal information is transmitted over SSL encrypted connections. Personal information may be stored on secure Internet servers that host the Church of the Rock website. Credit card numbers may be stored on secure servers but are never stored in clear text, always stored with public/private key encryption, accessible only by Church of the Rock when provided with the proper credentials to do so.

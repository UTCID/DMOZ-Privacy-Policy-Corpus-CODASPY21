Meals on Wheels
Meals on Wheels empowers homebound adults in Missoula County to continue living independently by providing home-delivered meals and regular safety checks. Click here for more details.
Community Lunches
Community Lunches are offered regularly at locations throughout Missoula County. Click here for more information about how you can register for a lunch and enjoy good food with neighbors!
Farmer's Market Coupons
Missoula has great local Farmer’s Markets – read more here about how you may qualify to receive coupons to purchase fresh fruits and vegetables during June through October.
Supplements
Missoula Aging Services offers liquid nutritional supplements at reduced cost for older adults in Missoula County. Read here for more details.

Updated: Dec. 3, 2020
Temporary Solution to access Casemaker and/or Fastcase as of Nov. 13, 2020We were alerted to a potential data security incident involving our website myWSBA.org. As a precaution, the myWSBA.org portal is currently offline, causing disruption for members who want to login into their Fastcase and/or Casemaker accounts, purchase a CLE product, pay their license fee, and report MCLEs. We are working quickly to reestablish access. Please note: Casemaker has established a temporary login for members. Go to https://casemakerlegal.com/public/login and use email address  wsbaorg@casemakerlegal.comand password Winter2020 until we reestablish connection. Fastcase has also established a temporary login as of November 19, 2020 for members. Go to https://gateway.fastcase.com/login?to=fc7 and enter username WSBA and password Winter2020 in the meantime.COVID-19 UpdatesApril 23: The Washington State Law Library has resources for accessing extended free trials to a variety of tools, including Westlaw and LexisNexis. To learn more, check out this blog post from the Washington State Law Library.April 9: Casemaker Support is back to operating on its normal business hours. You can reach them 5 a.m. to 5 p.m. PDT Monday-Friday.

Get Help
If you are a WSBA member who is having trouble using the WSBA legal research benefits, check out the following resources:
If myWSBA is Preventing Access to the Tool
Make sure you are logging into myWSBA with your WSBA license number (instead of your email address).
Try using a different web browser.
If you are still encountering a log-in error, contact the WSBA at LegalResearch@wsba.org.
All Other Issues
For any other questions about using the legal research tools, including help using the legal research platform, you may contact the vendor directly.

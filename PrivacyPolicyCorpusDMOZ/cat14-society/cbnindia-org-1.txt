Privacy Policy
CBN India is committed to protecting the privacy of people and organisations that it works with. It is also committed to protecting the privacy of its donors, supporters, employees, registered members and other stakeholders. We value the trust of those we deal with, and of the public, and recognize that maintaining this trust requires that we be transparent and accountable in how we treat the personal information that you choose to share with us.
During the course of our various activities, we frequently gather and use personal information. Anyone from whom we collect such personal information should expect that it will be carefully protected and that any use of or dealings with this personal information is subject to express or implied consent.

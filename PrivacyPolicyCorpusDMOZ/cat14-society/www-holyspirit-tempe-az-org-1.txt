The privacy of our visitors is very important to us. Here is information on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you use and browse our website and how we safeguard all of your personal information.
Firstly, we never sell your personal information to third parties. As with most other websites, we collect and use the data contained in log files. The information in the log files include your ip address, your internet service provider, the browser you used to visit our website, the time you visited our website and which pages you visited throughout our website.
We may also collect data that enables us to process your donation.  However, at no time do we store this information for any other purposes.
We may use cookies to store information on certain pages, such as your personal preferences when you visit our website. This could include your ability to login to some of our features, such as the website search, daily blog or discussion forums.
If you have any questions in regards to our privacy policy or need more information, please visit the Contact Us section; submit your inquiry and we will reply to you as soon as possible.
We do not offer refunds at this time.

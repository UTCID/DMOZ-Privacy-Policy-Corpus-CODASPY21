Legal Project Management
Our specialist financial services team provides clients with expert advice and practical commercial solutions to a range of transactional and regulatory issues as well as disputes.
Collaborating with other experts throughout the firm where required, we assist our clients with issues in relation to regulation and compliance, commercial lending, residential lending, as well as all aspects of recoveries, restructuring and disputes.
We use our deep experience to deliver a commercially driven approach to all matters. We are committed to mitigating our clients’ risk and understand the importance of sensitively working with all stakeholders.
Our team provides end-to-end legal financial services advice including:
ACL and AFSL matters
Compliance, policy and procedure matters
Facility and security documents
PEXA transactions
PPSR registration and issues
Consent to leases, transfers, assignments, surrenders, subleases, registration of subdivisions and consolidations.
Recoveries, restructuring, litigation and disputes

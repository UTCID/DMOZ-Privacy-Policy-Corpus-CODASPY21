Donate Stock
By donating stock, you gain the satisfaction of supporting local people in need and receive specific tax advantages at the same time. Donors enjoy a two-fold advantage in donating long-term appreciated assets: they avoid capital gains tax and receive a charitable tax deduction for the full fair market value of the asset. Call (757) 321-2222 for more information.

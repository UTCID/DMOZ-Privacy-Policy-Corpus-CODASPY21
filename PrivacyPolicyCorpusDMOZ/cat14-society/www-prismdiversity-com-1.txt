PrismDiversity.com Privacy Policy
PRISM International, Inc., is committed to providing privacy for our web site visitors. PRISM promotes the tenets of permission-based marketing and privacy among our partners and employees. We are continuously evaluating our internal procedures and technology to ensure privacy at all levels in our organization.
Web Site Privacy
Information Tracked
At times we may track browser types to help us understand our visitors' needs related to our web site or broadcast email design.
There is an optional section of our web site where input forms require your name, company, and e-mail address. This form provides the ability to opt-out of receiving future emails of any sort from PRISM. If you choose not to opt-out, we may send information to you on our products or services.
Cookies
Cookies are used to verify the login status of customers using products or services linked directly with our website. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site.
Security and PrivacyPRISM’s web site uses security measures to protect against the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. We store the information in a database in a secure environment at our secure data center. We will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone.
Period.
Links to other Web Sites
PRISM is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any other web sites in which we may link.
If you have any questions about this privacy statement or this web site, please contact us at: info@prismdiversity.com

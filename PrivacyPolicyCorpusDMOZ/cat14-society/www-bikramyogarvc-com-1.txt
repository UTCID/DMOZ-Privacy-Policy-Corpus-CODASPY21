Bikram Yoga RVC Privacy Policy
Bikram Yoga Rockville Centre cares about your privacy
Any information submitted to us through our website does not get shared with any third party.
If you use the contact form, we will simply use the provided information to contact you.
If you sign up for our email list, your email simply gets added to our private email list, after you opt-in through the link that automatically gets sent to the email provided to us. This opt-in email, insures that your email only gets added to our list if the link within the email is clicked by someone who has access to the email address. If at any time you would like to be removed from our email list, please click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email you receive from us.

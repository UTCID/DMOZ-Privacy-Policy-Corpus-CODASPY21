a. recognise you whenever you visit this website (this speeds up your access to the site as you do not have to log on each time)b. obtain information about your preferences, online movements and use of the internetc. carry out research and statistical analysis to help improve our content, and to help us better understand our visitor requirements and interestsd. target our communications campaigns more effectivelye. make your experience of our website and broadcasting services more efficient and enjoyable.12.

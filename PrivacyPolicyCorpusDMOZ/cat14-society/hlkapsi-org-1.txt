Privacy Policy
HomePrivacy Policy
This statement discloses the policies and privacy practices for the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter - Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. website. In an effort to build users' trust and confidence, the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter promotes the principles of disclosure and informed consent. The Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter has a commitment to users' privacy and thus discloses its policies on data collecting, uses and sharing of information. The Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter may capture implicit information from users who visit the website for aggregate statistical analysis and site customization. Implicit information is data that can be gathered about users without having to explicitly ask a user for such information. Importantly, aggregate statistical analysis means that the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter reviews trends based on information as a whole, but does not do so in an effort to identify the individual identity of a user to its site.
The Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter may solicit e-mail for site feedback, information requests, and contribution information. Users may choose to submit such information at their own discretion. In all cases, the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter does not disclose any personal identifying information to third parties.
IP addresses are logged to track visitors to the website. An IP address is the unique numerical identity of your host computer. When the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter "logs" an IP address, it means that the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter website associates a visit or series of visits to that unique numerical identity.
The Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter website may contain links to other websites that are not under the control of the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter. These links are provided as a convenience, and such links do not imply that the Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter endorses such sites. The Hyattsville/Landover (MD) Alumni Chapter is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites.

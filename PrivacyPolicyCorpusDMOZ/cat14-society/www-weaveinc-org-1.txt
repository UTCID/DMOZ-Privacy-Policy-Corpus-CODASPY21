Privacy Policy
Post March 13, 2016
WEAVE respects the privacy of those that subscribe to our e-mail
alerts. WEAVE does not sell, trade or distribute information
about our subscribers. WEAVE e-mail alerts are meant to provide
information to our friends at the lowest cost possible. If at any
time you wish to be removed from the list, you may unsubscribe.
Donor Privacy Policy
Confidentiality is very important to WEAVE, both for the
individuals we serve and for our donors. WEAVE will never share
your information.

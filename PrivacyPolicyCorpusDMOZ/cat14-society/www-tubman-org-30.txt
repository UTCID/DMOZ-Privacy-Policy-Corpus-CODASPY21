Kids in TransitionKids In Transition (KIT) is a free, six-week closed support group for youth who have experienced violence, relationship transitions, and/or loss. Groups are offered to youth in two age groups: 5-12 years and 13-17 years.
Groups meet weekly, each Thursday, 6:30 p.m. to 8 p.m., during the available sessions.
KIT is now being offered through Zoom, and participants will receive KIT supplies by mail for group participation.

Notice of Availability of Documents under the
Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act
Torkin Manes LLP is committed to maintaining an accessible environment for persons with disabilities by serving our clients in a manner which respects their dignity and independence, and permits equal access to our legal services.
We have established policies, practices and procedures for the delivery of our legal services to persons with disabilities, as required by the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (“AODA”) and its regulations. Our Multi-Year Accessibility Plan & Policies describes our policies, practices and procedures relating to:
Providing services to persons with disabilities
The use of assistive devices, service animals and support persons by persons with disabilities
Information and communications supports
Temporary disruption of our facilities and services
Accessible emergency information
Staff training
Employment
Our Feedback Process

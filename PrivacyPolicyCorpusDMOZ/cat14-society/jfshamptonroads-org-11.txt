Kroger
Community Rewards
Donating to JFS has never been so easy! If you grocery shop at Kroger, you can easily help JFS. Simply link your Kroger Rewards Card to JFS. To do so, visit the Kroger website and log in to your account. Then click on Community/Community Rewards and follow instructions from there.
Annually, JFS receives an average of over $500 for its Food and Financial Assistance program from Kroger shoppers.

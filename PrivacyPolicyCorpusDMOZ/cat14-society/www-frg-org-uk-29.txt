Who is the group  for?
The Big Cup project is a free parent and baby group, for vulnerable families within the communities of Stoke on Trent and Newcastle under Lyme. Big Cup is aimed to re-introduce parents/carers and their babies and toddlers back in to the community, improve confidence and self esteem and promote positive parenting
Where does the group meet?
Kidsgrove and Clayton
Who runs the group?
Big Cup is ran by Alice Charity staff and volunteers
How often does the group meet?
Kidsgrove – MondayClayton - WednesdayEvery week, term-time only10am to 12pm snack and refreshments included

Who is the group  for?
The More than Grandparents group is a support group ran for and by kinship carers offering support, guidance and friendship to all kinship carers and their children.
Where does the group meet?
Sunderland
How often does the group meet?
Every Thursday from 12.30 til 2.30 for a cuppa and a chat. Children are welcome and we have access to a free soft play area and a sensory play room.

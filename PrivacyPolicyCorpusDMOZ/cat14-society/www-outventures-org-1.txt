Outventures knows that you care how information about you is collected, used, or shared. We appreciate that you trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. This notice describes our privacy policy. By visiting outventures.org, you are accepting the practices described herein.
Outventures fully supports the right to privacy of our members.  Strictest confidentiality will be maintained at all times pertaining to any information related to our members. Further Outventures will not disclose any confidential information including membership, address, email, payment, or club participation to any other member or third party.
Outventures uses a third party for any and all payment information and does not collect or store any bank, bank account, credit card, or other payment information. We collect and store information related to membership and use cookies to assist in the navigation of the Outventures website.
A cookie is a small file that can be placed on your device that allows us to recognize and remember you and make your next visit easier. It is sent to your browser and stored on your computer’s hard drive or tablet or mobile device. What you visit our site; we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.
There are two types of cookies: persistent and session. The cookies Outventures uses are PHP session cookies, which mean that they are used to maintain the state of the site when moving from page to page. They are used when logged into the member's area, signing up for events, or using database driven parts of the site. These are necessary to navigate the Outventures website.
Additionally, we use cookies to create our visitor logs. Visitor logs are used on this site for statistical purposes only. No attempt will be made to identify or collect personal information about users. Outventures may automatically receive and record information from your browser, which may include your IP address, session time, click path analysis, and the pages you request. Outventures will only use this type data in an aggregate form which does not identify you personally.
If you'd like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser.

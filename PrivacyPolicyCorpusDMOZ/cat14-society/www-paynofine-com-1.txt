Our
Privacy Policy
PayNoFine.com respects the privacy of users who
visit. No effort is made to identify individuals
without their knowledge.
When a visitor requests pages at our site, our Web
server automatically recognizes only the browser's
domain name, and IP address but not the e-mail
address. We collect only the domain name and IP
address in order to aggregate information on what
pages users access or visit. This information is
collected solely for statistical purposes and is
not used to identify individuals.
Should you purchase the PayNoFine Guide, all
information submitted shall remain confidential and
will never be revealed to anyone.

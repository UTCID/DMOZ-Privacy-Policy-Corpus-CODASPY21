Yoga Tree Privacy PolicyThere are places at our sites where you can share your personal information with us and where you can communicate with others.Yoga Tree (ABN 82 633 965 787) (we, our, us or Yoga Tree) recognises that your privacy is very important to you. Providing your personal information is an act of trust that we take very seriously. We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) (Privacy Act) and the applicable Privacy Principles in the Privacy Act when handling personal information. We will not sell, trade, give or rent any information obtained from you to a third party, unless you have specifically agreed to this occurring. Further Information can be found at the Government Privacy web site: www.privacy.gov.au.Registration and Collection of InformationSome sites let you register with us and sometimes registration is required before certain site features can be used.We collect personal information for the purposes of delivering our products and services and communicating with Customers regarding our services through the Yoga Tree website located at www.yogatree.com.au (Website). We collect personal information:From users – when they register an enquiry, book a class through the online booking form or purchase a Phoenix Yoga Studios membership;From suppliers or third parties – when they are engaged to supply services to us, provide information to us or invoice us for the provision of services;When we receive enquiries through the Website and when the Website is otherwise accessed or used.The kind of personal information we may collect will depend on who you are (e.g. a customer or supplier) and the nature of your interaction with us, and may include your full name, email address, company name, billing information and other personal information. If you do not provide us with all the information we request, we may not be able to deliver our services.You may be assigned a site username and/or password. We use this to communicate with you and provide a personalised experience.ConsentBy engaging us or registering your details on our Website, agreeing to the Customer Terms and Conditions or Supplier Terms and Conditions and/or using the Website or supplying documents to us, you consent to us collecting, using and disclosing your personal information for the purposes specified in this Privacy Policy.Automatic Data CollectionOur websites have features that automatically collect information and show personalised user preferences.Some of our sites use cookies. These are small data files containing information about the user that are stored on the user’s hard drive.Cookies are used to make login to our sites easier (like remembering usernames and passwords) and to track movements to enhance your experience online.If users reject cookies, they may be limited in using some areas of our sites.For internal purposes, we may gather information (date, time, browser type, navigation history, and IP address) for our internal security audit log, trend analysis, system administration, and to gather demographic information.In the course of serving advertisements to the Website (if any), third-party advertisers or ad servers may place or recognise a unique cookie on your browser. The use of cookies by such third party advertisers or ad servers is not subject to this Privacy Policy, but is subject to their own respective privacy policies.SecurityYour security is extremely important to us. We store your personal information securely to protect it from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.Upon request, we will delete any information that you no longer want us to hold.Third Party Websites (Links)We may provide links to non-Yoga Tree sites.Please note that by following a link on our site that directs you to our partners, advertisers and other third party websites, your activity on the third party website will be governed by that website’s privacy policy. Please ensure you review these policies before submitting personal information to these websites as we do not accept responsibility or liability for policies pertaining to third party websites.Communication With UsWhen you contact us, we retain your email and contact information to serve you best.Newsletter and Other Communications From UsRegistered users and customers may receive newsletters or other email communications from us.When you sign up for newsletters, we ask for contact information (name, email address, demographic and profile information). We use this information to learn about you so we can better serve you and to send e-newsletter announcements about our administration details.We offer guests the choice to receive mailings from other organisations that we think you will like. Recipients of our mailings can unsubscribe by following instructions that appear at the end of the e-newsletter.Interactive Community FeaturesOur sites may offer interactive and community features (message boards, chat, email accounts, instant messaging, profiles, personal homepages, etc.). Recipients can unsubscribe by following instructions that may appear at the end of the email newsletter.With Whom Your Information Is SharedWe may share your information with others if you choose to let us.We do not share your personal information with other companies.

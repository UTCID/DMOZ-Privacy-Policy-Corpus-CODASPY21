Privacy Policy
This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for Jenkins Law Library.  Jenkins Law Library is the sole owner of the information we collect. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement.What We Do with Your InformationFacilitating communications with you.Reviewing activities that may result in a fee, such as overdue materials or on-site printing.Aggregate statistical purposes to help us improve and expand the services we offer and personalized statistical reports for you or your organization, upon request.Normal business operations, such as invoicing, technical support, and research/document delivery services.If you are a member or business partner, your information is used to periodically contact you about events, new services, and scheduled service disruptions.When We Share Your InformationIn order to communicate with potential members and improve our services to help us better serve our customers, members, and the public, Jenkins Law Library occasionally shares aggregated demographic information regarding website usage, traffic patterns, and members’ activities with our partners.In the course of doing business or providing services for members, customers, or the public we may record information that personally identifies you and details your relationship with Jenkins Law Library and your specific service requests using websites and software services (SaaS) provided by third-parties.Occasionally publishers require members to be identified to provide personalized services, such as saved search history and a local profile. Jenkins Law Library may communicate with those same publishers or with other service providers using that information to troubleshoot a technical problem you or another member has reported.With the exception of the cases described in this policy, Jenkins Law Library does not share personal information that can identify you -- except with you, your firm’s principals, our vendors and technical partners or when it is reasonably necessary to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.A Note about Forms & EmailAll members, customers, and public users should consider the sensitivity of their data before emailing or submitting information using our website forms.  Neither method of communication carries absolute security guarantees.What this Policy Doesn’t CoverOur website and onsite computers contain links to other sites and databases. Please be aware that Jenkins Law Library does not claim any responsibility for the privacy practices of such other sites, including external databases accessible via your membership.How We’ll Tell You About Changes to this PolicyIf we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our website so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. Who to Contact for More InformationUse the information on our contact page to contact us during normal business hours.

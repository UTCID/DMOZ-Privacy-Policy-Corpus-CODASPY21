Privacy Policy
The American Iranian Council (AIC) is committed to protecting the privacy of its visitors, members and donors online.AIC makes every effort to ensure that all the communications and transactions that occur on its website are secure. AIC does not share any financial information provided during donation on the site to third parties. AIC secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol. Credit card information is not stored permanently on the Web server. AIC does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.AIC Email PolicyAIC sends its members and subscribers regular newsletters on latest events, updates and news. If you do not wish to receive AIC newsletters, you may opt out when you receive the next issue by clicking the “unsubscribe” button.

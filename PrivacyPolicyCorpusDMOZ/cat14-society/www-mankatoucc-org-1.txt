COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
When you engage in certain activities on this site, you may be asked to provide information about yourself by filling out and submitting an online form. It is completely voluntary for you to engage in these activities. If you elect to engage in these activities, however, you may be required to provide personal information, such as your name, e-mail address, and other personal identifying information.
When you submit personal information, you understand and agree that we may transfer, store, and process your information without limitation. We will never sell or distribute your information without your express consent save when required by law to do so. We collect this information solely to record and support your participation in the activities you select.

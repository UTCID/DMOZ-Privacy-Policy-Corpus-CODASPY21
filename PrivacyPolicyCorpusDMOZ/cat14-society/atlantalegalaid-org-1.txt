AtlantaLegalAid.org is committed to your privacy. It is important to us.
This website is owned and operated by Atlanta Legal Aid Society, Inc. Our privacy policy describes how we collect and use information about you. This policy doesn’t apply to other sites you reach through AtlantaLegalAid.org. You should read the privacy policies of other Web sites to learn how they collect and use information about you.
Google Analytics
AtlantaLegalAid.org uses use Google Analytics to gather usage statistics. AtlantaLegalAid.org will use the information gathered to improve web services for its users.  Google Analytics employs cookies to define user sessions, which allows for the collection of important data about how our users are using AtlantaLegalAid.org.  Google Analytics uses only first-party cookies for data analysis.  This means that the cookies are linked to the AtlantaLegalAid.org website domain(s), and Google Analytics will only use that cookie data for statistical analysis related to your browsing behavior on the AtlantaLegalAid.org websites.  According to Google, the data collected cannot be altered or retrieved by services from other domains.  If you choose, you can opt out by turning off cookies in the preferences settings in your browser. For more information on Google Analytics, please visit Google’s web site.

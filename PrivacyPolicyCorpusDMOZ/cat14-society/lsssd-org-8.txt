Together We Transformed People's Lives 45,781 TimesWhether you are in Sioux Falls, Aberdeen, Huron, Rapid City or Watertown, you can help respond to the changing needs in your community with a corporate sponsorship to Lutheran Social Services. Together with corporate sponsors, LSS cares for, supports and strengthens thousands of people—individuals, families and communities right here in South Dakota. Join LSS in providing hope, guidance and healing to those who need.
Corporate Sponsorship Form

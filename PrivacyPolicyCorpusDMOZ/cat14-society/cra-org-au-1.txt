Privacy Policy
Collection of your personal information
There are many aspects of the site which can be viewed without providing personal information, however,  to purchase from our shop you are required to submit personally identifiable information.We will collect the email address you nominate and any other identifying information you provide, such as a name or phone number.Please do not give us other personal or sensitive information.Other than circumstances such as unlawful activity or serious threats to health and safety, we do not share Personal Information.
Use of your personal information
We will use your personal information to:
Provide the goods or services ordered by you,
to let you know of other goods and services that we provide. This is done by quarterly e-mail that can be opted out of at any stage.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
The Christian Research Association reserves the right to make amendments to this Privacy Policy at any time. If you have objections to the Privacy Policy, you should not access or use the Site.

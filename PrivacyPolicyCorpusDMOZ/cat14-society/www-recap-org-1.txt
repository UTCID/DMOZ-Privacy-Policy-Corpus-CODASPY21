Your privacy is important to us. Through our website we collect information from users only in connection with online donations, sales of tickets to events, and other similar purposes that are clearly and explicitly identified. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to others under any circumstances. When you make a donation to RECAP, your personal information will not be shared with anyone.
RECAP does not utilize any means of information collection or third parties to collect information and create profiles of visitors to our website. Like most standard web servers,we use log files that include internet protocol addresses, type, internet service provider,referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp and clicks to analyze trends,administer the site, track user’s movement in aggregate. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

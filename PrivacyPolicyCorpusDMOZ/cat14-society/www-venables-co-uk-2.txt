Privacy policy
What information do we collect about you?
We don’t collect personal information on this website. Website usage information is collected using cookies.
Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behaviour information. This information is used to track visitor use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity. For further information visit AboutCookies.org from Pinsent Mason. You can set your browser not to accept cookies and remove cookies from your browser.
Other websites
Our website contains links to other websites. This privacy policy only applies to this website so when you link to other websites you should read their own privacy policies.
Changes to our privacy policy

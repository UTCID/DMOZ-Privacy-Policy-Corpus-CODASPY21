This Privacy Policy was published on October 5, 2018, and last updated on October 5, 2018. We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time.We Take Our Privacy Seriously.This privacy policy contains information about Offit Kurman, P.A. and how and why we collect, store, use and share your personal data. It also explains your rights concerning your personal data and how to contact us or supervisory authorities in the event you have a complaint.This website is not intended for children and we do not knowingly collect data relating to children.Controller, Processor, and Data Privacy OfficerThis privacy notice is issued on behalf of Offit Kurman, P.A.  The words "we", "us", "our", or “Offit Kurman” in this privacy notice refer to Offit Kurman, P.A. which is responsible for processing your data and is the controller and responsible party for this website.Our Data Privacy Officer is Robert Skinner, who can be reached at 301-575-0300 or via email at _rskinner@offitkurman.com, or regular mail at 8171 Maple Lawn Boulevard, Suite 200, Maple Lawn, MD 20759, USA.We hope that we can resolve any concern you may raise about this website or our use of your information.   If you live or work in the European Union or European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation gives you right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority where you work, normally live, or where any alleged violation of data protection laws occurred.  We would, however, appreciate the chance to deal with your concerns ourselves first, and will endeavor to address all issues to your reasonable satisfaction if you contact us in the first instance.Please inform us of changes to your Personal DataTo assure that any personal data we hold about you is accurate and current, please keep us informed if your personal data changes during your relationship with us.Third-party links will take you to third party sites for which we are not responsibleThis website may include links to third-party websites and third-party applications. We do not control these third-party sites.  If you click on a link and leave our website, we encourage you to read the privacy notice of any linked site for that site’s privacy policy.

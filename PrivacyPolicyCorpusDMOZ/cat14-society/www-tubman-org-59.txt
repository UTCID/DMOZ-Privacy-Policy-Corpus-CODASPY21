Host an EventTubman is grateful to have many community partners who host events that support Tubman and raise awareness about our services. We collaborate as a charitable partner with a wide variety of groups, businesses, and organizations. Past examples of events have included donation drives, 5K runs, raffles, bake sales, concerts, and special collections.
If you are interested in hosting an event, we would be happy to assist you in making the event a success. Please fill out the form below so that we may provide you with the information or materials you may need.
If you have additional questions, please contact Jocelyn Beard, Community Relations Manager, at jbeard@tubman.org or 612.767.6144.

Return Policy
If not fully satisfied, most merchandise may be returned within 30 days of the date of purchase. First, call our office at 1-855-492-6657 to request a return. Workbooks may be returned at any time for credit if in “new” condition and still in print from the publisher. When mailing your package, include a copy of the invoice or clearly written name and address for person or church receiving the credit. Credit will be applied to your next order; refunds are made upon request. Dated class curriculum, VBS curriculum, CDs, videos, DVDs or software products cannot be returned for credit.

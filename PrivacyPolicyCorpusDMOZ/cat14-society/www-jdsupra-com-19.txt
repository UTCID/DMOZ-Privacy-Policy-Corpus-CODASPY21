Speakers: Kathryn Hickey and Sharon Heaton of sbLiftOff
Healthcare businesses represent essential transaction opportunities in the middle market. Despite the current pandemic, healthcare remains a principal area of investment; however, M&A activity has its own unique challenges that require strategic advance planning to avoid merger complications.
Join sbLiftOff’s Sharon Heaton and PilieroMazza’s Kathryn Hickey for a one-hour webinar to discuss what the current healthcare M&A marketplace looks like and how you can help maximize your efforts for a successful transaction.
Areas to be discussed include:
recognizing motivations of both sellers and buyers in the healthcare space;
getting your house in order… the importance of due diligence prior to going to market;
structuring the sale;
data privacy;
HIPAA compliance;
licensing requirements;
non-competes;
and more…

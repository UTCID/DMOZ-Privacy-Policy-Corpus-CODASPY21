Privacy Policy
Home / Privacy Policy
Although Green Seal operates in an open and transparent manner, there may be times when a business or government provides information to Green Seal upon the condition that it remain confidential. All Green Seal staff, contractors, and consultants sign confidentiality agreements and take all reasonable steps to assure the confidentiality of such information.  In general, it is the policy of Green Seal to liberally grant requests for public release of information where such release would not harm the organization or its clients, or infringe on privacy rights.

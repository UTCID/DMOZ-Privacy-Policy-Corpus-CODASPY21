Privacy Policy
Northern Land Council recognises the importance of protecting your privacy.
We use information provided directly from you (through the contact form Northern Land Council add any other relevant ways) so we can communicate with you through emails and newsletters (Northern Land Council add any other relevant ways).
When visiting our website, information is recorded (such as your browser, date, time and pages accessed) for statistical purposes and remains anonymous. It will only be used to improve the website, in order to give you a better experience!
The information you provide through this website is used only by Northern Land Council and not sold or given to third-parties unless required by law.
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
This website contains secure areas located at https://nlc.org.au/ indicated by a padlock symbol in the browser.
Northern Land Council uses SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) for transmitting private information by encrypting the data transferred over the Internet. This protocol is a standard used by many websites when you submit confidential information, such as credit card numbers and other personal data (eg permit form applications).
If you have any concerns or enquiries about your security while using our website, please contact us for more information.

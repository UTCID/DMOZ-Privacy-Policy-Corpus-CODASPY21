Links policy
This document sets out our policy with regard to linking both to and from our site.
Linking to our site
You do not need to ask permission to link directly to the home page of our site at www.phf.org.uk. However, if you wish to link to pages deeper in our site than the home page, we would like you to contact us first by emailing information@phf.org.uk.
Linking to our site using our logo
If you are in receipt of a grant or other support you may use our logo image to link to our home page. Logo files are available to download from this site here. Please email communications@phf.org.uk with a link to the logo in situ.

Although we try to keep these up to date we cannot guarantee that these links will work all the time, and we have no control over the availability of linked pages.
Social media policy
The Foundation maintains a range of social media accounts including Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn.
We are grateful for messages received via Twitter, and for responses to our messages. We cannot always respond to queries via Twitter as they may require complex answers, which are difficult to give through a medium with limited characters. Where a response is needed, we may contact you directly or provide information on our website (with a twitter message to alert followers to it).

Privacy Notice
We use the information you provide to process your gifts and to communicate with you. We have taken extensive security measures to protect your personal information. We handle your credit/debit card information with the utmost respect and confidentiality. Credit card numbers are used only for gift processing. Your credit/debit card information is never stored on our web server or retained for any other purpose. From time to time, we may use your address information to provide you with ministry materials we think would be of particular interest. Our site uses first- and third-party cookies including Facebook to help us learn how to better serve people like you. By continuing on this site, you agree to their use. Otherwise, you can disable cookies in your browser. However, we will never sell, trade, share, or rent your personal information with any other organizations, companies or individuals.

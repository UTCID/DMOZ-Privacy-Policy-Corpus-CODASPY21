Our
Commitment to Privacy
ArtScroll.com
values its customers and respects their privacy. We collect customer information
in an effort to improve your shopping experience and to communicate with you
about our products, services and promotions. We do not sell, rent or share your
personal information or email address to third parties. Occasionally, we send
relevant offers to selected groups of ArtScroll.com customers on behalf of other
businesses. When we do this, we do not give that business your name and address
or email.
We respect your preference
not to receive our promotional emails and give you the ability to remove
yourself from our email lists at any time. Unsubscribing from our email list is
effective immediately.  If you would like to unsubscribe from receiving our
promotional emails in the future, make sure you are
logged in to your account and then click
here.

Security
Security is the most critical segment of the Kansas.gov network. Kansas.gov considers the security of our partners’ and citizens’ information critical. Kansas.gov is committed to protecting our partners’ and citizens’ confidentiality through the use of state-of-the-art information security and the adoption of appropriate privacy policies and procedures.

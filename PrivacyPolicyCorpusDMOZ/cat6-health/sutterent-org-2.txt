Sutter Medical Foundation Snapshot
As a not-for-profit organization, Sutter Medical Foundation is dedicated to enhancing
the health of people in our communities. This includes more than 867,000 patients
and countless community members across Amador, Butte, El Dorado, Placer, Sacramento,
Solano, Sutter, Yolo and Yuba counties.
Together, Sutter Medical Foundation medical groups provide comprehensive care to
patients of all ages through more than 1,300 primary care doctors and medical specialists.

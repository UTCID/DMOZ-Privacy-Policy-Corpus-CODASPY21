Privacy
TeenHealthFX.com Web site is maintained by Atlantic Health System, 475 South Street, Morristown, New Jersey. Atlantic can be reached by our feedback portal here.
OUR COMMITMENT TO YOUR PRIVACY:
We are dedicated to maintaining the privacy of our users.
HOW TeenHealthFX.com USES INFORMATION PROVIDED BY YOU: TeenHealthFX.com or any other Atlantic Health System site does not collect personally identifiable information (for example, your name, home address, email address, etc.) about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals.
TeenHealthFX does not provide, sell or lease your personally identifiable information to any parties. TeenHealthFX collects anonymous data for statistical purposes.
REGISTRATION: Users do not have to register to use TeenHealthFX.
PARENTS: Atlantic Health System encourages you to get involved with your children's online usage and to be aware of the activities in which they are participating. You should also discourage your children from giving any personally identifiable information on the Internet. If you have any concerns about your child's or your use of www.teenhealthfx.com or any other Atlantic Health System Internet site, please contact us at info@atlantichealth.org.
When you follow a link from TeenHealthFX.com Internet site to any third-party site, you are no longer protected by TeenHealthFX.com Privacy Policy.
TeenHealthFX may make changes to this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this site

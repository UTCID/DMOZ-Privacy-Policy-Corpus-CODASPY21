Alta Bates Summit Medical Center Snapshot
Alta Bates Summit Medical Center offers comprehensive services designed to meet
the healthcare needs of the diverse communities of the greater East Bay Area.
The medical center is the East Bay's largest private, not-for-profit medical center
and an affiliate of Sutter Health.
Alta Bates Summit's 100 year tradition of commitment and service continues today,
with recognition as one of the nation's top hospitals for clinical excellence and
patient safety.

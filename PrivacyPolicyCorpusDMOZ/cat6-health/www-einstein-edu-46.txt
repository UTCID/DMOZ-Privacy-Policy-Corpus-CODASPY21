Notice of Privacy Practices
Columns Layout = 2
Einstein's Notice of Privacy Practices informs you of how your health information is used within the Einstein network. It also tells you what rights you have to access and protect your health information.
The Einstein Notice of Privacy Practices is posted in our registration areas and is given to patients as they are admitted to the hospital or visit outpatient areas for services and treatment.

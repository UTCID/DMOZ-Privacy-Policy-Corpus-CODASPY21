Conditions we treat
Whether it’s for you or a loved one, an adult or a child, you’ll find the help you need at at AHN. We care for a wide-variety of issues, from sleep problems to anxiety, to post-traumatic stress disorder, and beyond.
Psychiatric and behavioral health conditions we treat
We use advanced, evidence-based therapies to address treatment-resistant psychiatric illnesses. These illnesses don’t respond to traditional mental health care. Our therapies include electro-convulsive therapy (ECT), transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS), and deep brain stimulation (DBS).
Our wide range of services covers adults and children experiencing serious and persistent mental illness, as well as common mood disorders, including:

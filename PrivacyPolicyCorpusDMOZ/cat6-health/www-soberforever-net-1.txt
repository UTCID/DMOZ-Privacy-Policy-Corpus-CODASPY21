The Freedom Model for the FamilyImproving Family Dynamics by Providing the Truth about AddictionCLICK HERE FOR FAMILY WORKSHOPSFor those close to a substance user, life can be frustrating, frightening, and challenging. Dealing with the constant barrage of problems that often surround substance users can become all consuming. Many family members and close friends begin to struggle with depression, anxiety, sleep problems, and physical ailments that can result from their own high stress levels.It seems everywhere you turn is so much misinformation about addiction. Addiction treatment often labels the family as sick, and, in some cases, actually blames them for the substance user’s ongoing struggles. This is absolutely false and extremely harmful to families. The guilt and shame can be debilitating.If you feel as if you’ve lost control of aspects of your life while dealing with a loved one who struggles with addiction, there is help available. The Freedom Model for the Family was designed especially for you.The Freedom Model for the Family is a book written to provide a comprehensive overview of The Freedom Model for Addictions while also addressing many of the most common problems families face when trying to provide help and support to a heavy substance user.The Freedom Model Family Workshop will:Dubunk commonly held addiction mythsExplain what addiction is and is not based on some of the most rigorous research ever done on the topicDispel addiction myths surrounding familiesProvide effective strategies to communicate with the substance userOvercome your anxiety, guilt, shame, and feelings of helplessnessHelp you to plan for the future – whether your loved one comes back home or notShow you how you can take back control of your life without guilt or fearYou deserve to be happy and fulfilled in life. You deserve to have the life you truly want.

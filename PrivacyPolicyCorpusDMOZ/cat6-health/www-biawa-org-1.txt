'Cookies' are used to store and sometimes track your preferences in order to provide you with customized and personalized services. A cookie is a data sent to your Internet browser from a web server and stored on your computer hard drive. Cookies make your web experience easier by storing passwords and preferences. Cookies are used by most Internet websites. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse all cookies or to inform you when a cookie is being placed on your hard drive.

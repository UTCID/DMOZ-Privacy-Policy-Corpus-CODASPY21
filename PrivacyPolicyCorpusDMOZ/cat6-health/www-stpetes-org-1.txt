Privacy Notice
St. Peter’s Health collects information about patients that is important to providing the best possible care. We are committed to protecting such information to the highest standards. The St. Peter’s Health privacy policy explains our legal duties and privacy practices with respect to Protected Health Information (PHI). The policy discusses how we may use and disclose PHI about you. The policy also explains your rights with respect to your PHI and how and where you may file a privacy-related complaint.

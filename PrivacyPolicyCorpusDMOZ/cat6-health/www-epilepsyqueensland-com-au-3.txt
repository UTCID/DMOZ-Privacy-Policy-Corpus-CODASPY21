Legal Status
Epilepsy Queensland Inc is a registered charity operating in Queensland. The Australian Taxation Office has granted us Deductible Gift Recipient legal status, meaning your donations of $2 and over are tax-deductible.
Incorporated Association: Epilepsy Queensland Inc is a not-for-profit incorporated association, registered under the Associations Incorporation Act (1981) in Queensland. It was incorporated in 1985.
Charitable and Australian Taxation Office status: Epilepsy Queensland Inc is a registered charity in Queensland. It has been classified as a Public Benevolent Institution. The Australian Taxation Office has granted us Deductible Gift Recipient and Income Tax Exempt Charity status.
Our Australian Business Number Registration is 42 025 269 961
Epilepsy Queensland is also registered with the Australian Charities and Not-for-profits Commission (ACNC), Australia’s national regulator of charities. You can find our information here.

TJC Public Notice Statement
Columns Layout = 2
At Einstein Healthcare Network, we strive to deliver compassionate, high-quality healthcare, excellent patient safety and high patient satisfaction. If you have a comment, question, or concern, we want to hear it--and resolve it. Please contact Patient Services by calling 215-456-6334.
If you have a privacy concern, please contact our Privacy Officer at 215-456-3517 or click on the link below for more information or to send an e-mail.
If you have a complaint about the quality of care delivered here and you're not satisfied with how we resolved your complaint, you may contact the Pennsylvania Department of Health at 1-800-254-5164 or The Joint Commission, One Renaissance Boulevard, Oakbrook Terrace, IL 60181 (800-994-6610 or complaint@jointcommission.org ). From The Joint Commission's home page, www.jointcommission.org, click on "General Public," then "Public Input," then "Report a Complaint."  Or you can fax your concern to The Joint Commission at 1-630-792-5636.

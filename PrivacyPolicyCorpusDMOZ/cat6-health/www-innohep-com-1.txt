Data Privacy Policy
This privacy statement describes our current policies and practices with regard to Personal Data collected by us from you directly and/or through the Website. For the purpose of the Data Privacy Policy, “we”, “us”, “our” means LEO Pharma A/S and LEO Laboratories Limited. When you register for the CancerClot Patient Support Service through this Website you give your explicit consent to us collecting and using the information as set out in APPENDIX 1 to this Privacy Policy.Before you submit any information through this Website you must read this Privacy Policy and the Consent Form below and give your explicit consent to the use of your personal data in accordance with the Privacy Policy and Consent Form.The term "Personal Data" refers to personally identifiable information about you, such as your name, birthday, health information, e-mail address or mailing address and telephone number.Data Privacy – Use of collected personal information about youPersonal Data submitted on our Website will be kept confidential and will only be used for the purposes specified in this Privacy Policy and in the consent form.We may use your Personal Data: To administer the Website and our business including statistical analysis  To enable your use of the services available on the Website  To personalise your visit to the Website, assist you while you use the Website and deal with your comments and respond to your queries  To contact you via email, SMS, telephone or by ordinary mail if you have requested this  To carry out surveys, studies and evaluations  To understand who uses the Website and how and to better understand needs and interests of visitors and users in order to improve the Website  To comply with applicable laws, regulations or codes of practice  For all other purposes to which you have consentedData Privacy – How is your personal information secured?To prevent unauthorised access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.The employees who have access to Personal Data have been trained to handle such data properly and in accordance with our security protocols and strict standards of confidentiality. Although we cannot guarantee against any loss, misuse, unauthorised disclosure, alteration or destruction of data, we take appropriate measures to try to prevent such unfortunate occurrences.

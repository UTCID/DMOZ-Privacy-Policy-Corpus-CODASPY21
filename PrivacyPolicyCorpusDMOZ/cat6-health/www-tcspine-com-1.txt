Privacy policy and nondiscrimination notice
Protecting your privacy
Twin Cities Spine Center understands that patient health information is personal and confidential. We are required by law to ensure that health information identifying patients is kept private and to notify patients of our legal duties and privacy policies.
Download a PDF of our privacy policy.
If you have questions or concerns regarding TCSC privacy practices, please contact our Health Information Services Manager via email at info@tcspine.com.
Nondiscrimination notice
Twin Cities Spine Center complies with applicable Federal civil rights laws and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, age, disability, or sex. TCSC does not exclude people or treat them differently because of race, color, national origin, age, disability, gender identity or sex.
Download a PDF of our nondiscrimination notice.
If you have questions or concerns regarding TCSC nondiscrimination practices, please contact our Grievance Coordinator via email at GrievanceCoordinator@tcspine.com.

Glimmers of Hope Monthly Giving ProgramDarkness cannot drive out darkness: only light can do that. ~ MLK
Thank you for your monthly pledge! Time and again, our clients tell us that their Support Volunteer was a beacon of hope when they needed it most.
Your recurring gift helps Cancer Hope Network reach patients and their loved ones with the light of hope as they face life’s darkest days. Your donation helps underwrite the recruitment, training and ongoing professional support our cancer survivor Support Volunteers receive. Questions? Contact Beth Blakey, Associate Director, Development - bblakey@cancerhopenetwork.org.
Join the club. Spark hope.
Your monthly gift makes a difference. Together, we will ensure that patients and caregivers fighting cancer receive the support and encouragement they need. Please consider giving at one of the levels listed below.
$5/month ($60/year) ~ Glimmer
$10/month ($120/year) ~ Sparkle
$20.83/month ($250/year) ~ Beacon
$41.66/month ($500)~ Shine
All monthly donors will receive a digital Glimmers of Hope badge to share on social media, as well as a special gift from Cancer Hope Network.
Cancer Hope Network is entirely funded by generous support from individuals, corporations and foundations. Your gift to CHN is tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law – and means no one need face cancer alone.
Thank you!

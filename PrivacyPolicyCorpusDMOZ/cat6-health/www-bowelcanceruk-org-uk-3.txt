Professionals network
Join our community that brings together healthcare professionals to improve the treatment, care and support for people with bowel cancer in the UK.
By signing up, you will be joining a growing network of multidisciplinary professionals who are driving forward improvements in bowel cancer treatment.
You will receive:
alerts about our updated health information booklets and online resources
educational opportunities to support your learning and development
access to professional groups to discuss best practice ideas
bespoke news and updates relevant to your profession and location
opportunities to work in collaboration with us and others
If you are a colorectal cancer nurse specialist and looking to join the National Colorectal Cancer Nurses Network please go here.
For more information about the professionals network, read our FAQs

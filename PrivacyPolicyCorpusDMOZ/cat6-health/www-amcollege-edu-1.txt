Privacy Policy
AMC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our Institution’s commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: www.amcollege.edu.
AMC’s inquiry forms request that users provide contact information (such as their name, mailing address, telephone number, and e-mail address). We use this contact information to send the user additional information about our school. A user’s contact information may also be used by us to contact the user directly. Users may choose not to receive future mailings or other communications from AMC at any time by sending an email to marketing@amcollege.edu with the subject line “Opt out request”

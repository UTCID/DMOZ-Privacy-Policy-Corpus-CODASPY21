Un-Resolved Chronic Pain
Ongoing, daily pain lasting more than three months can severely impact patients physically and emotionally.
If you are suffering from any of the symptoms below, we will work with you to return you to a more functional and manageable status. We will do a full examination to determine the cause of the pain.
Our patients report successful outcomes from our interventional pain treatments including decreased duration and severity of pain, better sleep, an increase in daily activity, increased comfort, and a restoration to their quality of life.

U.S. News & World Report Best Hospitals
Best in Ophthalmology in the USA
Bascom Palmer Eye Institute is ranked Best in Ophthalmology by both U.S. News & World Report and Ophthalmology Times. This is the 19th time that we have received the #1 ranking from US News since the publication began surveying U.S. physicians for its annual rankings 30 years ago. (1994, 1998, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2020).
We are honored to receive these prestigious awards. This recognition is a tribute to our exceptional team of 1,300 staff and faculty members who deliver excellent, personalized and compassionate care for each of our patients. While we are honored to be #1, it’s really our patients who come out on top.
PIONEERING VISION RESEARCH
Advancing the Frontiers of Ophthalmology
Using the tools of 21st century medicine – including genetics, cellular biology, molecular diagnostics and advanced imaging – Bascom Palmer researchers are poised to understand why the eye may become susceptible to disease and how biotechnologies may help to prevent these conditions.

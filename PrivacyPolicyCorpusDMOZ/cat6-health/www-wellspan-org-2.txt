Disclaimer & Policies
WellSpan Health provides this website as a community service. The information it contains is offered to you with the understanding that it should not be considered medical or professional advice. If you have or suspect a health problem, you should seek the services of an actual health care professional.

Editorial Policy
Our Editorial Policy outlines the evaluation process all health information content goes through. We want you to rely on us as a trustworthy, quality resource of health information. Medical experts have reviewed the content on this site for clinical accuracy. You'll have information on who wrote an article and when it was written and updated.
Refund Policy
Refund requests for online purchases are discretionary and will be reviewed by WellSpan management for appropriateness.
Site Ownership and Use
WellSpan Health directly funds the operation and hosting of wellspan.org. We have disclosed the ownership and contributors to this site for your evaluation. We feel that you have the right to know who owns this site and what entities, if any, provide us with funding or significant contributions.
Accessibility Statement
WellSpan Health strives to provide a useful online experience for all visitors to our website.  If you have questions or suggestions regarding the accessibility of this site or cannot access any of the material on our website, please contact us at webmaster@wellspan.org.

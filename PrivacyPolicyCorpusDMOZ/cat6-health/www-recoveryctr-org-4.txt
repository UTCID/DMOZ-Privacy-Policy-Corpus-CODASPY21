Non-Discrimination PolicyGCB serves the community without discrimination in services and employment.  GCB makes a firm commitment to provide services and to employ, promote and contract without regard to race, color, sex, age, religion, ancestry, ethnicity, national origin, handicap, sexual orientation, status of a Vietnam Era Veteran, special disabled veterans, recently separated veterans, and veterans who served on active duty during a war or in a campaign or expedition for which a campaign badge has been authorized or any other characteristic protected by applicable federal or state law.  The rights and privileges provided by this policy are established by the Civil Rights Act of 1964, Titles VI and VII, Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, Age Discrimination Act of 1967, American Disabilities Act of 1992, and ADA Amendment Act of 2009.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS
What are your rights as a student and our policies regarding courses?
Polestar Pilates has strict policies regarding course registration, cancellation, and transfers. Please read these over carefully to understand our expectations and regulations for our students.
STUDENT OBLIGATIONS
You are responsible for completing the reading and writing assignments, and ensuring your logbook is checked off by a Polestar Educator or Mentor prior to sitting for the graduation exam.
You are responsible for making arrangements with your Educator and/or mentor to complete your self-mastery and observation hours.
Polestar course manuals will be issued to you at the beginning of the course. You must purchase any additional text books required for assignments.
REGISTRATION POLICIES
All registrations require a valid email address.
Your registration will be pending until payment is processed. Allow 2-5 business days for your registration to be processed (if paying by check, that is after check is received). Once registered, you will be able to view your registration details on-line.
A $25 fee will be charged for any returned check or declined credit card debit due to insufficient funds.
Polestar is not responsible for any hotel or airline expenses, nor transportation to or from a course.
STUDENT CANCELLATION POLICY
Cancellations are accepted for full refund minus non-refundable portion, with written notice post-marked 30 days prior to series start date. (Non-refundable portion: Rehab, Studio, Reformer, Mat: $500; Gateway $100, Individual courses: $100). No refunds issued for Transition Course.
No refunds issued for notice less than 30 days prior to the start of the series or individual course.
No refunds issued after completion of S/R1, M1, and/or Ref 1.
In the event that a student has missed one or more courses and failed to make payments under the payment plan, the student is still responsible for course payment in full.
Any remaining balance may be applied to a future course taken within 12 months. The credit must be used for a future course within 12 months of the cancellation date. Polestar Pilates will assist the student in finding a future course in which to participate.
TRANSFERS/CHANGE REQUESTS
Change requests are accepted with written notice post-marked 30 days prior to start date. There is a $250 fee per course change, and it must accompany notice. No refunds issued after any change. We reserve the right to postpone or cancel any course.

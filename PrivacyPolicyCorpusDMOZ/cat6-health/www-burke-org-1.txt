Privacy Statement
As a patient you have certain rights pertaining to protected health information. Burke Rehabilitation Hospital takes these issues very seriously. We want all patients to know that Burke is in accordance with federal privacy laws and takes steps to protect your health information. For this reason, Burke has created a Notice of Privacy Practice. The statement covers important areas including:
How Burke may use and disclose your Personal Health Information
Your privacy rights in your Personal Health Information
Our obligations concerning the use and disclosure of your Personal Health Information
Please review this policy regarding the use of personal health information so you understand your rights and how information is used in healthcare.
View our Notice of Privacy Practices here.

Health Alert:
**VISITATION CHANGE ALERT**
Due to the increase in COVID cases in the community, Burke is changing our Visitation Policy to limit exposure and further protect our patients, staff & visitors.
*** This takes effect on 12/8/2020 ***
Effective December 8th
Visitation will happen from 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. on
Tuesdays
Thursdays
Sundays
ONE Visitor per patient PER DAY
Are there any patients who cannot receive visitors?
Yes.
As of 12/3/20, visitation is restricted for patients who are under special pathogen precautions and are in isolation.
Please check the website regularly to stay current on any future changes in visitation.
Your safety is our top priority.

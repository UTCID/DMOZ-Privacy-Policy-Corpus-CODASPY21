Privacy Policy
The European Hair Research Society (EHRS), www.ehrs.org (the Web Site) or www.ehrs.net (the Web Site), their directors, officers, members, employees, or administrators do not collect personally identifiable information on the Web Site or otherwise unless you choose to give it to us via an electronic mail message, a phone call, by fax, or by postal mail.
European Hair Research Society (EHRS), www.ehrs.org (the Web Site) or www.ehrs.net (the Web Site), their directors, officers, members, employees, or administrators do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose mailing lists or other personally identifiable information to third parties not directly associated with the EHRS. We do maintain records of individuals who contact us in case the EHRS needs to contact you later or provide further information to you in the future.
The Server of our Internet Service Provider collects information about the date and time of which our web site was accessed and the Internet address of the web site from which you linked to our Web Site. We have the ability to access this information, This information is used to analyze web traffic, such as to measure the number of visitors to our site and the addresses of the web sites from which our visitors come. E-mail addresses and other personally identifiable information of Web Site visitors are not recorded.

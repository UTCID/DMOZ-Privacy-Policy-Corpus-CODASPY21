The Circle
The Circle is a dynamic group of passionate volunteers and community leaders who serve as ambassadors for Center for Hospice Care.  We are dedicated to making sure that everyone in Southeastern Connecticut knows about and receives quality end-of-life care and bereavement support.
LEADERSHIP
William Turner, Chair
BENEFITS OF MEMBERSHIP
As a member of The Circle, you will enjoy the following benefits:
Invitations to member social gatherings, twice yearly.
Invitations to educational opportunities and events.
Knowledge that your membership helps to provide our neighbors, friends and families in Southeastern Connecticut with deep end-of-life care and exceptional bereavement services.
Listing as a member in our annual report.
We offer different levels of Circle membership at Center for Hospice Care, so which one is right for you?
$100  Annual Member (one individual)
$175  Annual Patron Member (two individuals)
$500  Corporate Member: Membership is per business (up to 4 individuals per event)

California Pacific Medical Center is one of the largest not-for-profit medical
centers in California. At our four campuses located throughout San Francisco, we provide
exemplary inpatient emergency and outpatient services, including specialized care
for women and children, organ transplant programs and innovative orthopedic treatments.
CPMC is here to provide comprehensive care for you and your loved ones, at every stage
of life.

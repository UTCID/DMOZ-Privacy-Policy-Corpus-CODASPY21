Privacy Statement
Wellstart International respects and protects the privacy of our visitors. At the present time, we do not collect personally
identifying information from our users. In the future, we may
collect such information through online surveys, product purchases, and other methods. Generally this information will include name, e-mail address, postal address, and answers to survey questions. This information will be used for internal marketing purposes
and overall review to help us determine how to continually improve our site for the user. Postal and e-mail addresses may also be used by Wellstart International to share breastfeeding-related information and events with our visitors. Wellstart International may also use phone numbers when questions arise regarding orders. Wellstart International will not disclose any information about any individual user except to comply with applicable law or valid legal process.
There is information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Wellstart International. This information can include your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. Wellstart International uses this information for the operation of the website, to maintain quality of the website, and to provide general statistics regarding use of our website.
Wellstart International does not sell, rent, or lease customer information to third parties.
Any modifications to this policy will be posted on this page. We welcome any questions or comments you have; please direct them to info@wellstart.org. Your experience while on our website is vitally important to us and we respect your rights.

Module 3: Patient RightsA patient has the right to:Consent or refuse the collection, use & disclosure of their personal health information (PHI). Refusal is subject to legal exceptions, such as a warrant or a subpoena.Access their PHI at any time.Request changes to information they feel is inaccurate.Know who accesses and uses their PHI. The patient may request an audit.Know to whom their PHI has been disclosed.Challenge the hospital's compliance with Privacy laws.Click here to go to Module 4

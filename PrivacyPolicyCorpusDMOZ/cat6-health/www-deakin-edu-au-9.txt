International students
Specialist support is available for international students through advice and assistance with study and enrolment-related matters, transition to university and life in Australia.
We can help you with:
Your enrolment conditionsUnderstanding your student visa and what is expected of youApplying for a new visaApplying for intermission/leave of absenceSettling into life at Deakin and in AustraliaGeneral and personal issues that may affect your ability to studyComplaints and grievancesFinancial difficultiesHomesicknessSocial or cultural adjustment difficultiesCrisis managementSocial activities, events and programsSpecific support for school age children, Under-18 and Australia Award studentsReferrals to other key services at Deakin and in the local community
Have a question?Please contact Student Central for assistance.

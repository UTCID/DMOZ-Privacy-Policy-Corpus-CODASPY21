Donor Privacy Policy
Our Commitment to Our Donors
Your privacy is important to us. We will not sell, share or trade our donors’ names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.
This policy applies to all information received by the Bladder Cancer Advocacy Network, both online and offline, on any Platform (“Platform”, includes the Bladder Cancer Advocacy Network website and mobile applications), as well as any electronic, written, or oral communications.
To the extent any donations are processed through a third-party service provider, our donors’ information will only be used for purposes necessary to process the donation.
Should you have a question relating to our donor privacy policy and practices — or wish to review or correct any personal information we may have on file for you — please contact us by:
Email:  info@bcan.org
Phone:  301-215-9099
Toll Free:  888-901-BCAN

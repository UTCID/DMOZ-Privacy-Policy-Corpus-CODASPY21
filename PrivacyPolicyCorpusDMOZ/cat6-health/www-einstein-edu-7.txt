Conditions & Treatments
When it comes to your digestive health, you want the most experienced specialists and the most advanced care. As regionally recognized leaders in digestive disease diagnosis and treatment, the gastroenterology team at Einstein uses a collaborative approach to offer comprehensive, personalized care. We treat a full range of digestive health conditions, including:
Direct Access Colonoscopy
Einstein’s Direct Access Colonoscopy (DAC) allows patients to skip the pre-specialist doctor’s visit and schedule a colonoscopy directly. At Einstein, our dedicated team of fellowship-trained staff want to make preventative screenings as easy as possible for you.
Learn More
Colon & Small Bowel Diseases
Conditions affecting your colon and lower gastrointestinal tract cause a variety of symptoms that disrupt your quality of life. At Einstein, we offer discrete, compassionate, personalized care, including treatment for a full range of colon and small bowel conditions.
Learn More
Gastrointestinal Cancer
A diagnosis of gastrointestinal cancer is life-changing and deciding on next steps can be overwhelming. Our team supports patients throughout diagnosis and treatment and helps them manage a variety of gastrointestinal cancers and their treatments.
Learn More
Esophageal & Motility Disorders
Esophageal conditions can range from mild heartburn to disorders that can become serious and life-threatening. At Einstein, we are experts in the latest tests and treatment options for a full range of these conditions, including radio-frequency ablation and other advanced endoscopic procedures.
Learn More
Stomach Disorders
Stomach conditions can range from mild discomfort to disorders that can become serious and life-threatening.
At Einstein, we are experts in the latest tests and treatment options for a full range of these conditions including treatment of stomach ulcers, treatment of infections, as well as advanced endoscopic procedures including removal of lesions in the stomach endoscopically.
Learn More
Inflammatory Bowel Disease
Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) is an umbrella term for Crohn’s disease and ulcerative colitis, which is a chronic inflammation of your digestive tract that can often be debilitating. Einstein offers the most advanced and team-based treatment approach to help you manage your inflammatory bowel disease.
Learn More
Pancreatic & Bile Duct Diseases
Your pancreas and bile ducts help your body break down the food you eat. Conditions that affect them can be sudden or chronic, painful or symptomless. Our gastroenterology team offers compassionate, personalized care for a full range of pancreas and bile duct, or biliary conditions.
Learn More
Advanced Therapeutic Endoscopy
Endoscopic procedures are nonsurgical procedures that use a small, flexible tube with a camera attached to diagnose conditions in the upper and lower digestive tract. During the procedure, a variety of treatments can also be performed using specialized endoscopic attachments.
Learn More

Share
Join us online for one of our sessions and hear about an interesting and informative topic. You’ll hear about others’ experiences and gather plenty of information about living with this complex and highly individual cancer. Listen to experts talk about the latest treatment strategies and new developments, as well as how to manage some of the side effects of treatment.
Our digital Infoday sessions are designed to provide you with up to date information presented by experts and available for you to watch from the comfort of your own home. We always make sure information is delivered in a way that is easy for everyone to understand and there’s something for all levels of knowledge and stage of myeloma. See below for our events and register to watch these live!
Learn from experts
Discover the latest research into myeloma and hear updates on current treatments direct from doctors and nurses.
Ask questions
Get answers to your questions from expert speakers and find out more about the support we offer.
Share experiences
Listen to short presentations from people who know what you're going through.
Watch at home
Join live or watch on demand later from the comfort of your home.

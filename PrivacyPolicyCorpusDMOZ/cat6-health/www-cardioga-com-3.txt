Your California Privacy Rights
The Savvier Family collects various types of personal information about you online during the course of our
customer relationship.  Under California Civil Code sections 1798.83, California residents who have an
established business relationship with the Savvier Family may make a written request to Savvier about how we have
shared your information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. In response to your written request,
Savvier is allowed to provide you with a notice describing the cost-free means to opt-out of our sharing your
information with third parties with whom we do not share the same brand name, if the third party will use it for
their direct marketing purposes.
If you would like to exercise your rights under California law, please
click here. We will implement your request to opt
out no later than thirty (30) to forty-five (45) days following our receipt of your request.

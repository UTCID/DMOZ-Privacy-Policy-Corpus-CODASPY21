Frequently Asked Questions
How do I view video content on Health.com?
Health.com videos are delivered in Adobe Flash Player format. If you can't see videos on Health.com, please install the Adobe Flash Player.
How do I get help with a Health magazine subscription?
For information about your subscription, to update your address information, or to renew or buy a gift subscription visit our Online Customer Service, or call 800-274-2522.
How do I unsubscribe to Health.com emails?
The easiest way to unsubscribe from an email is to use the "unsubscribe" link, found at the bottom of every email that you receive from us.
How do I change the email address where I receive a Health.com newsletter?
Simply unsubscribe from the link contained at the bottom of each of our emails, and then re-subscribe to the newsletter.
How do I find past articles from Health magazine?
Many articles that have appeared in Health magazine are archived right here on Health.com. Find them by keyword or by title using the Health.com search form, located at the top of the site. Or, go to our magazine page to find stories.
What happens to the personal information I provide?
Health.com takes your privacy seriously. Please read our Privacy Policy for details. Health.com contains links to other sites whose information practices may be different from ours. Visitors should consult the other sites' privacy statements, as we have no control over information that is submitted to or collected by these third parties.

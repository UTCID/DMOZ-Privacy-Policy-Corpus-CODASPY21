What Is My ND?
My ND is your personalized area of Nutrition Data.
The suite of tools that make up My ND help you to analyze your diet quickly and easily.
Click on the links below to find out more about each section of My ND:
My Foods: Save, edit, and manage the foods you eat most often
My Recipes: Create, edit, and analyze your recipes
My Tracking: Log the foods you eat to track the nutritional value of your diet
My Preferences: Personalize many of Nutrition Data's default settings
My Profile: Update your account information
To access any of the My ND features, click on "My ND" in the navigation bar at the top of each page, then select a tool.
How My ND works
My ND stores all your information in a secure database. Registration is required and provides many benefits, including:
Access to My ND from any computer in any location
Separate My ND log-ins for individual family members
The ability to store an unlimited quantity of foods, recipes, and tracking data

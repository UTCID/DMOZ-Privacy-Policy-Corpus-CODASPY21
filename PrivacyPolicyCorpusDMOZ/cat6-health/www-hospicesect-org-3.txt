Opportunities for Support at Any Level
Why it’s a good idea
When you support Center for Hospice Care, you’re supporting the community’s ability to take care of its own. Donations made to us go to work right here, in programs that enhance the quality of life for terminally ill patients and their families.
Levels of support: The Circle of Promise
We include our benefactors in what we call The Circle of Promise. Most Circle of Promise donations fall into one of the following categories.
Sustainer
$5,000.00
Guardian
$2,500.00
Angel
$1,000.00
Benefactor
$500.00
Patron
$250.00
Friend
$100.00
Donors of $250 or more will be listed in Center for Hospice Care’s Annual Report.

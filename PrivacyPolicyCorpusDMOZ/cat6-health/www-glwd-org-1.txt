Our Commitment to Our Community
We will not sell, share or trade our donors’ names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations.
This policy applies to all information received by God’s Love We Deliver, both online and offline, on any Platform (“Platform”, includes the God’s Love We Deliver website and mobile applications), as well as any electronic, written, or oral communications.
To the extent any donations are processed through a third-party service provider, our donors’ information will only be used for purposes necessary to process the donation.
All donor information (address, phone number, email address, and giving history) is stored in a secure database.
If you would like to review your personal information and request changes you may contact our Development Department at development@glwd.org.
For more information, please see our State Nonprofit Disclosures page.

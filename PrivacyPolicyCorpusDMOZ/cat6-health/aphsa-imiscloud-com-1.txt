Privacy Statement
American Public Human Services Association (APHSA) is a non-profit organization that is building users’ trust and confidence on the Internet by promoting the use of fair information practices. During a visit to APHSA's Website the following information will be collected and stored automatically about the visit:
Domain names and IP address:
Our Web server will automatically recognize a visitor's domain name and IP address (an IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to a computer when surfing the Web), but not the visitor's e-mail address.
E-mail addresses:
We collect the e-mail addresses of those who subscribe to our list services and those who register for our online products and services. APHSA will not share email addresses with any group.
Other collected information:
We collect aggregate information on what pages our visitors access or visit. We also receive information volunteered by visitors, such as information provided in onsite conference registrations, job postings, and book purchases. Information we collect may be used to notify visitors about updates to our Website and may be used to contact visitors for customer service purposes.
Cookies:
We use cookies to record session information as a method to improve the user experience for those visiting the site. APHSA will not share any personal information about Website visitors unless required for law enforcement or otherwise required by law.
Security:
We transfer certain types of data, such as credit card information, only to our online ordering fulfillment company.

Get Involved
As leaders in our nonprofit community, we pride ourselves on helping to prepare new behavioral health and business professionals.
We welcome persons from all backgrounds or cultures, and our staff is motivated by a strong desire to make a positive impact in the lives of the people we serve.
Please email us at jobs@centerpointe.org to notify us of your interest in internship opportunities, including your days/hours of availability. Please use "Internship" in the subject line. Please do not send attachments.
CenterPointe does not currently have any Counseling internships.

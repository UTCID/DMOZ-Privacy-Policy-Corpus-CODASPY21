Privacy Policy
The following states EPDSC’s policy regarding the privacy rights of visitors to this website. We respect your right to privacy and your desire for a secure online experience.
Aggregate Information
We collect aggregate information on visits made to our pages. This information helps us improve the content of our site.
Other Information We Collect
When you purchase a product or service from EPDSC, we ask you for the following information: name, address, phone number, e-mail address, and sometimes credit card. This information is used to process your order and if necessary, to contact you for help when we are processing an order. All information we collect is stored in a secure database.
About Children (Under 18)
For their protection, we ask that children do not submit information to us without the consent of a parent or guardian. Disclosure of Information Policy We never make your information, including e-mail address, available to any other company or organization.
Need to make a change?
If you would like to change any of your transactional information in our database please feel free to Contact Us.

Novant Health Forsyth Medical Center is a Winston-Salem hospital providing emergency services, medical services, surgical services, rehabilitation and behavioral health services. Our centers of excellence include Novant Health Rehabilitation, Maya Angelou Women's Health & Wellness Center, Heart & Vascular Institute, Derrick L.

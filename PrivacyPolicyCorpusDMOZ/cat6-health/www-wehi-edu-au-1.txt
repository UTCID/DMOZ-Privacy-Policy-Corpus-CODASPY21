Policies
The Institute is committed to the highest standards of governance in our research and workplace activities.
A number of processes and policies are in place to ensure we foster a positive and safe workplace for staff, students and visitors.
Appropriate Workplace Behaviour policy
A framework defining the Institute’s expectations for appropriate workplace behavior, and processes for managing inappropriate conduct such as harassment, bullying or discrimination.
Child Safe StandardsA policy and code of conduct are in place to ensure a safe and positive experience is provided to children visiting the Institute.
Workplace response to family violence
The Institute aims to assist employees who are experiencing family violence to progress toward a life free from violence and its effect.
Whistleblower policy
This policy provides information on the process and protections in place to manage whistleblowing.
Supporting trans and gender diverse people in the workplace
This policy outlines the support available to employees who are trans or gender diverse.
Parental leave policy
Our parental leave policy assists employees in balancing their career and parenting roles.
Scientific integrityOur statement on research practice has been developed to foster integrity in scientific research at the Institute.
Diversity and Inclusion Strategy
Our strategy focuses on five key principles around which we focus our activities and initiatives to foster a diverse and inclusive organisation.
Privacy policyThe Institute protects the privacy of personal information we collect and hold about individuals.
Terms of useThese terms apply to use of content, features and services available from our website.
For further information about Institute policies and procedures email communityrelations@wehi.edu.au.

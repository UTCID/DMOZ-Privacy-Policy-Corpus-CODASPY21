Website Privacy Policy
Website Privacy Policy
Cabell Huntington Hospital recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of information provided by users of its website. We have created the following data collection policies to demonstrate our commitment to the issue of privacy.User information collected by Cabell Huntington HospitalWe collect information about our users through the various web forms present on our website. These forms include Contact and Speaker Request forms, and our double opt-in e-mail newsletter subscription form. The latter also allows users to unsubscribe at any time.Additionally, our server automatically collects the domain name and IP address of users, as well as the browsing habits of users within our website.How we use the information we collectInformation provided by our users through web forms is used solely for the purpose of fulfilling the user's request. For example, when a user contacts us, we require the user's name, e-mail address and/or telephone number in order to answer the inquiry.Our double opt-in e-mail newsletter service requires users to provide an e-mail address in order for us to deliver the newsletter. Our website also allows subscribers to remove this e-mail address from our mailing list at any time.Information collected by our server is used to improve the content of our web pages, and to monitor the operation of our website.If you have a question with regard to our privacy policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

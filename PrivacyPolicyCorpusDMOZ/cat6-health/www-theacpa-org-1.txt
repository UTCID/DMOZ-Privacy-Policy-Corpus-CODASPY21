PolicyLinking
PolicyACPA Link PolicyThe ACPA is happy to link to web sites that provide information that:Relates to the ACPA’s missionComplements and enhances the information on the ACPA siteProvides credible, accurate, and current informationPrimarily offers information or education, rather than promoting of a product or serviceApplies adequate procedures for the review and updating of materialAdheres to adequate policies regarding external links and the privacy of user data.The American Chronic Pain Association does not participate in any sort of advertising or make unsolicited phone calls.
If you receive an unsolicited phone call from someone claiming to be the American Chronic Pain Association, please report it to www.FTC.gov.

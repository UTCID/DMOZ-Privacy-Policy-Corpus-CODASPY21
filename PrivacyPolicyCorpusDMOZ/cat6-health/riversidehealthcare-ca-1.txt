Freedom of Information, Privacy & Confidentiality
Our Commitment to Your Privacy & Confidentiality
At Riverside Health Care we recognize that the health information of our patients deserves to be treated with respect and sensitivity and our patient’s privacy must be protected. Access to your personal health information is available to those who need to know to provide care, including: physicians, nurses, technicians, therapists and other health professionals. Furthermore, when it comes to collecting and using your personal health information, we take measures to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of the information is protected.

The Privacy Office
Our Privacy Office is committed to protecting the privacy of personal information under the custody and control of RHC in accordance with Ontario’s Health Information Protection Act, public expectations for privacy and internationally accepted fair information principals. The Health Information Protection Act, comprised of both the Personal Health Information Act and the Quality of Care Information Protection Act came into force November 1, 2004.

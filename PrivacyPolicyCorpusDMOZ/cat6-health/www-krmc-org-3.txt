Privacy Policy
Kalispell Regional Healthcare values your business and respects your right to privacy. Please review our Notice of Privacy Practices to learn more about our privacy policies.
If you have any questions or concerns, contact the KRH Privacy Office at (406) 752-1742.
Refund Policy
In the event of an overpayment on an individual invoice for you or your family, the overpayment will be transferred to another unpaid invoice. In the event of an overpayment on your balance as a whole, Kalispell Regional Healthcare will refund the overpayment to you in its normal monthly refund process.
If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us.
Credit Card Transactions
For online payments, credit card information provided to us is transmitted using SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption. SSL is a proven coding system that lets your browser automatically encrypt, or scramble, data before you send it to us. Absolutely no use whatsoever is made of credit card information aside from payments made on this site, and there is no disclosure to third parties except as required for the online credit card processing or as otherwise required under law.
If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us.

You can access your exam timetable and results through StudentConnect, or find out more at the assessments and results website.

The Student Advisers at Student Central provide course and enrolment advice.

You can also nominate a third party (e.g a parent) to act on your behalf. To do this email Student Central who will provide you with further details.

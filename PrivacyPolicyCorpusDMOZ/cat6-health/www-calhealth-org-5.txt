CHA-Developed Privacy Tools
June 27, 2013
The model appendixes prepared by CHA should not be used without careful review of the information about the form provided in the manual it comes from. Some appendixes do not contain information that is required for some, but not all, hospitals. Hospitals must modify each form to meet their unique circumstances, and have their own legal counsel review the final draft before adopting it.
Members, please log in to access the CHA-Developed Privacy Tools. Need help logging in? Contact us at (916) 552-7510 or info@calhospital.org for assistance.

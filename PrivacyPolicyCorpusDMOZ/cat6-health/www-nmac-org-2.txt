Legislation with Provisions Related to HIV
H.R.2312
Elder Pride Act
Includes provisions that direct the Assistant Director of the Office of Inclusivity and Sexual Health should collect and disseminate information about the challenges experienced by LGBT older individuals.
H.R.1351
SURVIVE Act
Includes provisions granting funding for Indian tribes to fund prophylactic treatment to prevent an individual from contracting HIV/AIDS or any other sexually transmitted disease or infection.
H.R.2701
Youth Access to Sexual Health Services Act of 2019
Includes provisions ordering the Secretary of Health and Human Services may (1) train individuals that work with marginalized youth on the prevention of sexually transmitted infections including HIV (2) award grants that plan to serve marginalized youth that are not served by Federal adolescent programs for the prevention of pregnancy, HIV, and other sexually transmitted infections.
H.R.1226
ACE Kids Act of 2019
Includes provisions defining the term ‘chronic condition’ to mean a serious, long-term physical, mental, or developmental disability or disease, including HIV/AIDS.
H.R.2452
Medicare for America Act of 2019
Includes provisions for a 100% reimbursement for medical conditons including complications from HIV, defines “screening, testing, treatment, and pre- and post-test counseling for sexually transmitted diseases and HIV” as medically necessary, and extends the waiver for the ““Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program grants under title XXVI of the Public Health Service Act.”
H.R.826
End Neglected Tropical Diseases Act
Includes provisions ordering the Office Of The Global AIDS Coordinato should take into account evolving research on the impact of neglected tropical diseases on efforts to control HIV/AIDS when making future programming decisions.
H.R.1226
ACE Kids Act of 2019
Includes provisions defining the term ‘chronic condition’ to mean a serious, long-term physical, mental, or developmental disability or disease, including HIV/AIDS.
H.R.1328
ACCESS BROADBAND Act
The term “Federal broadband support program” includes Housing Opportunities for Persons with AIDS of the Department of Housing and Urban Development” since the program offers broadband internet service or programs for promoting broadband access.
H.R.1839
Medicaid Services Investment and Accountability Act of 2019 (PASSED)
Includes provisions defining the term ‘chronic condition’ to mean a serious, long-term physical, mental, or developmental disability or disease, including HIV/AIDS.
H.R.2720
Real Education for Healthy Youth Act of 2019
One of the purposes of this bill is to address health inequalities, and to withold federal funding to sexual education programs that do not address certain topics including HIV.
H.R.5267
International Violence Against Women Act of 2019
Includes provisions that address gender norms and inequities to reduce HIV risk and increase access to HIV prevention, care, and treatment services for women and men following a study that showed women who have experienced violence are up to three times more at risk to contract HIV.
H.R.1581
Reproductive Rights are Human Rights Act of 2019
Includes provisions to promote the prevention, detection, and treatment of sexually transmitted infections, including HIV.
H.R.267
Transportation, Housing and Urban Development, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2019
Includes a provision for $375,000,000 to remain available until September 30, 2020 to be used for housing opportunities for individuals with AIDS.

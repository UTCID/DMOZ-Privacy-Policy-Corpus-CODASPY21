Patient Privacy Rights and Quality Care at Forbes Hospital
Patient privacy and rights
The physicians and staff of Forbes Hospital are dedicated to making our patients and visitors as comfortable as possible. We are committed to treating our guests with competence, compassion, and courtesy. Our physicians, nurses, and other members of our healthcare team are dedicated to responding to patients' needs and answering their questions. Additionally, our Patient Relations staff are dedicated to assisting our patients and visitors, answering their non-medical questions, and providing further service to enhance their stay at our hospital.
As a patient you or your legally responsible party, have the right to:
receive care without discrimination due to age, sex, race, color, religion, sexual orientation, income, education, national origin, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, genetic information, marital status, culture, language, physical or mental disability, gender identity or expression, or who will pay your bill
safe, respectful, and dignified care at all times. You will receive services and care that are medically suggested and within the hospital’s services, its stated mission, and required law and regulation.
Patient safety & quality of care
The physicians and staff of Forbes Hospital are dedicated to making our patients and visitors as comfortable as possible. We are committed to treating our guests with competence, compassion, and courtesy.
Representatives from Patient and Guest Relations identify and act upon improvement opportunities presented by hospital patients and guests. Physicians, patients, families, and visitors rely on Patient and Guest Relations to address special needs, identify and seek solutions to problems, and represent their interests.

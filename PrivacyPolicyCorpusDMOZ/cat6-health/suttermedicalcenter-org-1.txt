Sutter Medical Center, Sacramento Snapshot
Throughout the Sutter Health organization, our mission is to enhance the health
and well-being of people in the communities we serve through compassion and excellence.
In all of our greater Sacramento area communities, our customers are the reason behind
this mission.
Open since 1923, Sutter Medical Center, Sacramento, has served the Sacramento area
for generations. Located downtown, our medical center comprises several facilities,
including Ose Adams Medical Pavilion, Anderson Luchetti Women & Children's Center
and the Sutter Center for Psychiatry. We’re proud to be a
Baby-Friendly Hospital. We offer a full array of programs and services to serve
the needs of the region.

Your privacy is very important to APTEI.  This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that APTEI collects.
Personal information collection and protection
We collect information when you register on our site, as appropriate, you be asked to enter your name, email, mailing address, phone number, credit card information and other details to help you with your experience.
We do NOT sell trade or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personal information.
We do NOT include or offer third party products or services on our website.
Securing your data
APTEI will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.
APTEI will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers. Information relating to electronic transactions entered into via this website will be protected by encryption technology.
Updating this statement
APTEI may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.
You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.

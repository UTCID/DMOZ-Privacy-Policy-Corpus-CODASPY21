Thank you for choosing to subscribe to our newsletter. We value your privacy. This page explains how we collect information from you and how we use it. Please read on for more details about our privacy practices.

We collect your private information in order to conduct an online transaction or communicate with you by email through her newsletter or via social media. The kind of private information that is collected in these instances includes your name, e-mail address, and information about your interest in our products or services.
How Do We Use The Information?
We use the information you provide about yourself to respond to your inquiries about our products or services. We will never sell, trade, or otherwise transfer private information that visitors voluntarily provide to us.

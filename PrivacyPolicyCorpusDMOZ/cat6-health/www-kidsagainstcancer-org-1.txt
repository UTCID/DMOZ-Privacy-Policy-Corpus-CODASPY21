Privacy Statement Kids Against Cancer has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: KidsAgainstCancer.org.Our site uses an order form for donors to make monetary contributions to Kids Against Cancer, Inc. using credit card information. We collect visitor's contact information (name, address, email address, etc.). The donor's contact information is used to get in touch with the donor when necessary.All information collected on this site is kept strictly confidential. Kids Against Cancer, Inc. guarantees all contributors that this information will not be sold, leased, or transfered to any other third party under any circumstances.SecurityThis site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We use and have a secure server.

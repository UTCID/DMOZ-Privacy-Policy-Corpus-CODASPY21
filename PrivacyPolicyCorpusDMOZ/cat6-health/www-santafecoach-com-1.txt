Privacy Policy:
Christopher J. Coulson ("Christopher J. Coulson")
operates santafecoach.com and may operate other websites. It
is Christopher J. Coulson's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect
while operating our websites.
Website Visitors
Like most website operators, Christopher J. Coulson collects non-personally-identifying information of the
sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference,
referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Christopher J. Coulson's purpose in
collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand
how Christopher J. Coulson's visitors use its website. From time to
time, Christopher J. Coulson may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by
publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.
Christopher J. Coulson also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol
(IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on santafecoach.com
blogs/sites. Christopher J. Coulson only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same
circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that
commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog/site
where the comment was left.
Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information
Certain visitors to Christopher J. Coulson's websites choose to interact
with Christopher J. Coulson in ways that require Christopher J. Coulson to gather
personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Christopher J. Coulson gathers
depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up at santafecoach.com to provide a
username and email address. Those who engage in transactions with Christopher J. Coulson are asked to
provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to
process those transactions. In each case, Christopher J. Coulson collects such information only insofar as
is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with
Christopher J. Coulson. Christopher J. Coulson does not disclose personally-identifying information
other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with
the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.
Aggregated Statistics
Christopher J. Coulson may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its
websites. Christopher J. Coulson may display this information publicly or provide it to others.
However, Christopher J. Coulson does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as
described below.
Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information
Christopher J. Coulson discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information
only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information

Cookies
A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser
provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Christopher J. Coulson uses cookies to
help Christopher J. Coulson identify and track visitors, their usage of Christopher J. Coulson
website, and their website access preferences. Christopher J. Coulson visitors who do not wish to have
cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before
using Christopher J. Coulson's websites, with the drawback that certain features
of Christopher J. Coulson's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Ads
Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies.

compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among
other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy
Policy covers the use of cookies by Christopher J. Coulson and does not cover the use of cookies by any
advertisers.

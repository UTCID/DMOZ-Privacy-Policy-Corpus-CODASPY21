Pursuing the goals of Refresco requires the processing of personal data. When processing such data, we are required to comply with the applicable laws and regulations. That goes for personal data pertaining to ‘you’ as our (potential) customer, consumer, prospect, job applicant, visitor of our plant(s) and business partner (supplier, agent or advisor). In the context of this Privacy Policy you and other natural persons of which we process personal data will be referred to as ‘external contacts’.This Privacy Policy describes the personal data that Refresco collects from or about you, and how we use and to whom we disclose that personal data.Application and scope of this Privacy PolicyThis Privacy Policy applies to the personal data of all of our external contacts.Organizations covered by this Privacy PolicyThis Privacy Policy applies to Refresco, its parent company and its subsidiaries and other affiliated entities. All references in this Privacy Policy to Refresco", "we", "us", "our" and like terms should be interpreted accordingly.It is Refresco’s policy to comply with the privacy legislation within each jurisdiction in which we operate. Sometimes the privacy legislation and / or an individual's right to privacy are different from one jurisdiction to another. If you are unsure if or how this Privacy Policy applies to you, please contact the office of our Legal Department.What is personal data?Personal data is any information about an identifiable individual, other than the person's business title or business contact information when used or disclosed for the purpose of business communications. Personal information does not include anonymous or non-personal information (i.e., information that cannot be associated with or tracked back to a specific individual).What personal data do we process?In our business operation we process different types of personal data of our external contacts, including, but not limited to, the personal data contained in:completed contact forms;submitted complaints;communications, via post, e-mail, phone, websites or social media platforms;job application letters;the books in which names and signatures of visitors of our factories are recorded;public sources (such as the Commercial Trade Registers);databases from third parties.In addition to the examples listed above, personal data also includes information such as gender, date of birth, education and/or training details, bank details and any other information necessary to achieve Refresco’s business purposes, which is disclosed in the context of having contact or doing business with, or applying for a job at, Refresco.As a general rule, Refresco processes personal data directly provided by external contacts. From time to time, we may utilize the services of third parties in our business and may also receive personal data collected by those third parties in the course of the performance of their services for us or otherwise. Where this is applicable, we will take reasonable steps to ensure that such third parties have represented to us that they have the right to disclose the personal data of external contacts to us.Where permitted or required by applicable law or regulatory requirements, we may process information about external contacts without their knowledge or consent.Why do we collect personal data?Refresco processes personal data of external contacts for its business purposes only.

The Boomer Esiason Foundation Privacy PolicyThe Boomer Esiason Foundation (BEF) is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust, and maintain the following privacy policy to protect personal information you provide and will not trade, share or sell a donor’s personal information with anyone else, nor send donor mailings on behalf of other organization and will only share or sell personal information once the donor has given the charity specific permission to do so.This policy is comprehensive and applies to all information received by BEF both online and offline, on any Platform (“Platform”, includes the BEF website and mobile applications), as well as any electronic, written, or oral communications. To the extent any donations are processed through a third-party service provider, our donors’ information will only be used for purposes necessary to process the donation.If your personal information changes or you would like to be removed please e-mail said requests to info@esiason.org.

Advertisement
Squirrel Wildlife Rehabilitation has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this website: Squirrel Wildlife Rehabilitation.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. This helps us to know which pages are popular and allows us to change the content as necessary to better suit our visitors. Any information given at this web site is not collected and shared with any outside third parties.
We use your e-mail address to get back in touch with you when you contact us to help you locate a wildlife rehabiliator. If you signed up for the Weekly Squirrel Updates, your e-mail address is used to send you the updates.
When you send a Squirrel Greeting Card, the names and e-mail addresses are used for the sole purpose of notifying the person you are sending the card to and letting them know that you sent them the card.
This site contains links to other sites.
Squirrel Wildlife Rehabilitation is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. While we do screen the pages that we link to, we do not check the pages every day.

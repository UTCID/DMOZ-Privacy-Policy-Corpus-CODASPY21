Privacy policy
The privacy and safety of our subscribers and customers is of great importance to us. Fun For Kidz does not sell, share, or release names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, credit card information, or any information gathered on our websites to any third parties. Information we collect will be used only by Fun For Kidz as a website improvement and/or marketing analysis tool.
You must be over 18 and have a valid credit card to order subscriptions and back issue themes online. Subscribers will only be contacted by email or phone if there is a question about their online order. We will not telephone you or contact you by email in an effort to sell you products.
If our policies change in the future, we will post the changes on our website.

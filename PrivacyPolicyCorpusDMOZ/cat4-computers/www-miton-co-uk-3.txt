Environmental Policy
Sue Quelch
2018-03-17T18:44:36+00:00
Miton Systems Ltd. is committed to environmental stewardship and environmentally sound practices in the manufacture of quality Software Products and Services.
To fulfill this commitment, Miton Systems Ltd. shall implement an Environmental Management System (EMS) that endeavours to:
Comply with all relevant environmental legislation, regulations and other requirements to which Miton subscribes and go beyond established standards when and where feasible.
Recognize community concerns and respond to any inquiries involving environmental aspects that may impact our employees or neighbours.
Maintain our commitment to minimize the impact on the environment by establishing methods to continually evaluate and improve the EMS.
Maintain a commitment to prevention of pollution.
Train personnel on relevant ISO 14001 information.
Miton Systems Ltd. believes that our environmental goals and objectives can be achieved through teamwork and communication with our employees, vendors, customers and community.
All Miton Systems Ltd. employees are expected to adhere to this policy and report environmental concerns.

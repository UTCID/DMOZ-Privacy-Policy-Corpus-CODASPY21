eMarketing Logic is Committed to Protecting your Information
eMarketing Logic Privacy Policy demonstrates our firm commitment to your privacy and the protection of your information.
Who is eMarketing Logic?
We are a marketing agency. Our web-based software enables businesses to send permission-based emails to their subscribers.
Why did you receive an email from our system?
If you received an email from us, it is because one of our clients used the eMarketing Logic software system to send you an email.
How did we get your email address?
Your email address was loaded into our system by one of our clients. We request that our clients only send emails to individuals who have expressly given their permission (“opt-in”) to receive this type of information. We promise to keep your email address private and agree not to use it for other purposes.
How do we protect your privacy?
We use strict security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system.
Will your information ever be shared, sold or rented?
We will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information without your advance permission, unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to employees responsible for managing this data.
How can you stop receiving email from our system?
Each email sent contains an easy and automated way for you to unsubscribe. Also, you can change your expressed interests at any time. If you wish to unsubscribe or change your interests, simply follow the instructions at the bottom of any email. If you have received an unwanted email sent by the eMarketing Logic system, please forward a copy of the email with your comments to abuse@e-maillogic.com.

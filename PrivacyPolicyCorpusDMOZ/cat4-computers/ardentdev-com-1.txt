Privacy, online and offline, is important to us and we know it’s important to you. We have created this policy in order to be open and transparent about how we deal with information you may share with us.
We will gather and use individual data only for the reasons indicated by us and for other good purposes, unless we get the assent of the individual concerned or as required by law.
We will only hold individual data the length of time essential for the satisfaction of those reasons.
We will gather individual data by legal and reasonable means and, where fitting, with the information or assent of the individual concerned.
We will protect individual data against misfortune or burglary, and also unapproved access, divulgence, duplicating, use or alteration.
We are focused on conducting ourselves as per these standards with the goal to guarantee that the privacy of individual data is secure and maintained.

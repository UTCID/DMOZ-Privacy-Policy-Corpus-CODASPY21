Privacy Policy
For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes only the person's domain name and/or IP address, but not the email address.
We collect the names, email addresses, and other information of those who communicate with us via email, forms, or other means. We also collect aggregate information on what pages visitors access or visit and information volunteered by the visitor, such as survey information and/or site registrations.
The information we collect is used for internal review, sales, and marketing. We use it to improve the content of our website and our service, and to grow our business. If you supply us with your email address and/or your postal address, you may receive periodic emailings and/or postal mailings from us with information on our company and/or products and services. If you do not want to receive mail or email from us in the future, please let us know via email at info@vilocity.com. Please provide us with your exact name and address. We will remove your name from our lists.

Oracle and AmberPoint
Oracle Buys AmberPoint
Oracle has acquired AmberPoint, a best-in-class SOA Management provider. AmberPoint has been recognized for its innovation in SOA Management, Business Transaction Management and run-time SOA Governance.
The combination of Oracle and AmberPoint is expected to extend Oracle's leadership in Fusion Middleware SOA Management Solutions. Adding AmberPoint's capabilities to Oracle's solutions is expected to provide customers with more agility and visibility into their business processes, enabling increased control and performance of critical applications.

To help make our site more responsive to the needs of our visitors, cable-modem.net may passively collect certain information without your prior consent. Such passively collected information is used in standard Web traffic analysis, in conjunction with demographic/aggregate analysis of collected user records.We may also collect information through the use of “cookies” which are small computer files that are transferred to a computer’s hard drive and which permit us to know about site visits. We may use cookies to improve your experience on the Web Site by making it easier for you to navigate through the Web Site or to make a Web Site feature work better. Information collected from a “cookie” might include information regarding your browser, type of computer, operating system, Internet service provider and other similar information. Cookie technology may also gather information about the areas you visit on our Web Site and about the links you may select from within our Web Site to other areas of the Internet. We can build a better Web Site if we know which pages our users are visiting and how often.We do not extract Personal Information during this process. Currently, only session cookies are used to maintain tracking of a user through an application. Session cookies expire at the end of a Web page visit. Session cookies contain only a numeric session identifier. We use such information in the aggregate to understand which services and resources our visitors prefer and how those services and resources are used.We may share aggregate demographic/statistical information with our media partners, service providers and other third parties. In addition, cable-modem.net may allow some of our advertisers to display ads which solicit or contain technology to collect information about users.Most browsers are set to accept cookies, however, you can set your browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent.

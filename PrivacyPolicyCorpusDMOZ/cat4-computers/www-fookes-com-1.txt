Introduction
Fookes Software Ltd (“Fookes Software” or "Company" or "We") respect user (“you” or “your” or “user”) privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. We recognize that the European Union (“EU”) has an “omnibus” data protection regime established pursuant to the European Data Protection Directive (95/46/EC) (the “Directive”) and that Switzerland has a comprehensive data protection law (together, the “Data Protection Laws”). Consistent with its pledge to protect personal privacy, Fookes Software adheres to the Data Protection Laws.
This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) sets forth the privacy principles that Fookes Software follows with respect to transfers of personal information from users to the EU and Switzerland, including the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the websites aid4mail.com, fookes.com, notetab.com ("Website" or "Websites") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting and disclosing that information.
This policy applies to information we collect: On this Website; In e-mail, text and other electronic messages between you and this Website; When you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites and services, if those applications or advertising include links to this policy.
Information We Collect About You and How We Collect It
When we collect personal information directly from individuals anywhere in the world, we will inform you about the purposes for which it collects and uses your personal information, the types of non-agent third parties, if any, to which we disclose that information, and the choices and means, if any, that we offer individuals for limiting the use and disclosure of your personal information. Notice will be provided in clear and conspicuous language when you are first asked to provide personal information to Fookes Software, or as soon as practicable thereafter, and in any event before we use the information for a purpose other than that for which it was originally collected.

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act). Our Website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Disclosure of Your Information
We have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act.

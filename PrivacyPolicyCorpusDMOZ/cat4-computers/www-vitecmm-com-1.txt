Privacy Policy IntroductionVITEC takes the privacy and protection of your personal information very seriously. We will only use your personal information in a lawful and fair manner. We will only collect information where it is necessary for us to do so and we will only collect information if it is relevant when dealing with you. This Privacy Policy explains how and for what purposes we use the information collected about you through this website, the VITEC Support Portal, and during the recruiting process. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to take account of changing requirements, and we request that you refer back to this Policy frequently to review any revision. VITEC, 99 rue Pierre Sémard, 92320 Chatillon is the data controller under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (‘GDPR’) and has designated a data protection officer who can be contacted at privacy(at)vitec.com.If you have any query regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy(at)vitec.com. Collection of Your Personal InformationAs part of our activities, we collect and maintain your personal information. For example: When you register on our website to request information or a login to the Support Portal, you may provide us with certain personal information including your name and email address.To login to the VITEC Support Portal, you will also be asked to choose a unique password, which will be used solely for the purpose of providing access to your user account.During the recruiting process, by applying per email to a job opening you may provide us with personal information.You may provide us with information in the course of customer support interactions, email exchanges and surveys.We use the Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, referring URL, files accessed, errors generated, time zone, operating system and other visitor details collected in our log files to analyze visit trends, administer the website, track visitor's movements and to improve our website. We link this automatically collected data to other information we collect about you.We also collect data via cookies (see section below).We use your personal information for specified, explicit and legitimate purposes. Temporary cookies will be removed from your computer each time you close your browser. By selecting ‘keep me signed-in’ option in VITEC services, a permanent cookie will be stored in your computer and you will not be required to sign-in by providing complete login information each time you return to our website. If you have turned cookies off, you may not be able to use registered areas of the website.

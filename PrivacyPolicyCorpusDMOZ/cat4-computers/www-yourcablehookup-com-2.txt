What are cookies? How doesYour Cable Hookup use cookies on its site?A cookie is a small data file that Web sites often store on your computers hard drive when you visit their sites. A cookie may contain information (such as a unique user ID), that is used to track the pages of the sites you've visited. We use cookies but do not store personally identifiable information in your cookie.
We use cookies in order to improve your shopping experience. When you visit Your Cable Hookup, your cookie helps us keep track of your order as you shop at our site.

Privacy
Our privacy statement is very simple…
We do not collect any personal information about you when you visit our website, nor when you install and use any of our software products on your computer. We do not “spy” on you or your personal data in any form or fashion. Any information that you may happen to provide us with will be used strictly by us for its intended purpose only (such as providing you with technical support, creating your personalized registration information for one of our software products, etc.). We will not give this information to any external organizations. Nor do we ever collect any information for commercial marketing purposes.
Basically, we do not monitor, collect, or divulge anything to anyone. Period!
It doesn’t get much simpler than that folks.

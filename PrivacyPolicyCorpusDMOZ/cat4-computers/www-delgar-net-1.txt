privacy policy
We know you're concerned about protecting your privacy on-line. So are we. DELGAR Designs Inc. created this privacy and
security statement in order to inform you and demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
We believe your personal information should remain just that - personal. DELGAR does not sell, rent or give names
or any other information about our customers to third parties.  We do collect information on this site for internal
use. This data helps us to improve this site.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our Web site.
Our site uses forms for customers to request information about our web design services. The customer's contact information
is also used to get in touch with the visitor when necessary.
Spam
Delgar Designs Inc. does not send out any unsolicited emails to any one, no exceptions. We have found Spammers using
our email addresses as a "Reply-To" email. Please contact your ISP for a solution to this problem.
Contacting the Web Site
If you have any questions or comments about this privacy statement or your experiences with this site, please contact us.

Last Updated: August 12, 2020

Website Collection and Use Practices
HelpSystems collects personally identifiable information provided by users when they submit a web form on one of our websites. This information includes non-sensitive data such as your email address, name, company name, home and/or work address and/or telephone number.
In the event that you contact us through the website, we may also request that you provide additional user information relevant to the support you request. Users are under no obligation to provide such information. However, we cannot guarantee that we can provide customer support in the event that relevant information related to the use of our products or services is withheld. These features may collect your IP address, which page you are visiting on our site, and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly. Social media features and widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our site. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

Our site includes links to other website(s) whose privacy practices may differ from those of HelpSystems. Use of Personal Information: Opt Out and Unsubscribe
HelpSystems has a legitimate interest in collecting and using your personal information for the reasons set forth in this section.  HelpSystems collects and uses your personal information to operate the HelpSystems website and deliver the services you have requested.  In addition, HelpSystems collects and uses your personal information in order to process requests submitted by website users and to support communication with customers or those with contractual obligations. HelpSystems may also use your personally identifiable information for sales and marketing purposes related to other HelpSystems products or services. You can withdraw your consent at any time but withdrawing your consent does not affect the lawfulness of any communications HelpSystems has sent to you before your consent was withdrawn. HelpSystems may also collect your personal information via voluntary surveys or contests in order to conduct market research or to collect feedback in order to improve our products and services. You will always have the option to unsubscribe (see “To Unsubscribe From HelpSystems” below). HelpSystems does not sell, rent, or lease its customer lists to third parties in which we do not have a formal agreement to provide services to HelpSystems and our customers or prospects. HelpSystems does not share personally identifiable information except as described in this policy.
HelpSystems may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (email, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, HelpSystems may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries or contact you based on your interest to receive such communications. Such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to HelpSystems, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information. HelpSystems does not collect sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.
HelpSystems also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites using log files.  We may use and share aggregate or non-personal information without restriction. If you access this website from outside the U.S., you acknowledge, agree, and consent that any information you provide, including any personal information, will be transferred to and processed by a computer server located within the U.S.
There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by HelpSystems. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, and referring website addresses. This information is used by HelpSystems for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the HelpSystems website. For these purposes, we do link this automatically collected data to personally identifiable information such as name, email address, address, and phone number.

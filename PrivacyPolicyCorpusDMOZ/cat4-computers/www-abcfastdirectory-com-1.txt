Privacy PolicyABCFastDirectory.com
understands the importance of protecting personal privacy and is committed to protecting
customers privacy. When orders are placed, ABCFastDirectory.com
uses the information from customers to correctly process orders and to
improve our ability to understand our customer's needs. ABCFastDirectory.com
policy applies only to ABCFastDirectory.com . Orders are
processed securely by TakeTwoSolutions.com, and their complete privacy policy can be viewed at www.TakeTwoSolutions.com. Description of
Information Collected and UsageABCFastDirectory.com
stores only an e-mail address when a customer wishes to be added to our
newsletter. When a customer makes a purchase, except for credit card details, information supplied on the purchase order is passed to
ABCFastDirectory.com . This information may be used to notify the customer automatically of product updates, upgrades, new products,
support and/or special pricing offers on future products. Orders are handled through TakeTwoSolutions.com and their complete privacy
policy can be seen from their website at www.TakeTwoSolutions.com. When an order is processed, except for credit card details, information
supplied on the purchase order form is passed through to ABCFastDirectory.com
from TakeTwoSolutions.com. No credit card details (credit card
number, expiry date) is ever seen, received or stored by ABCFastDirectory.com . ABCFastDirectory.com
also reserves the right to
provide aggregate information about site usage and traffic patterns, stripped of any individual identification, to third parties for
marketing research. Information Collected for Internal Feedback ABCFastDirectory.com
reserves the right to aggregate
the data collected and use it for market research purposes in order to improve ABCFastDirectory.com
customer service and products. This
aggregated data will not include information that would personally identify a customer. Policies Regarding Third Parties
Orders are processed securely by TakeTwoSolutions.com, and their privacy policy can be viewed on their website at
www.TakeTwoSolutions.com.ABCFastDirectory.com
current policy is to not sell, rent or trade personal information to others; however,
ABCFastDirectory.com
reserves the right to do so in the future. If ABCFastDirectory.com
does decide to do this, ABCFastDirectory.com
will email each customer and give each customer 30 days to inform us if he or she wishes for your information to remain private.
Customers with concerns about disclosing personal information may send an e-mail to privacy at ABCFastDirectory.com.com, and
your information will remain private. In this email, customers should include a current email address and the words "remain private" in
the subject field. ABCFastDirectory.com
Privacy Policy ChangesIf ABCFastDirectory.com
decides to change its privacy
policy, ABCFastDirectory.com
will post these changes here. Please feel free to contact ABCFastDirectory.com
to our webmaster, at
ABCFastDirectory.com.com if you have further questions regarding our privacy policy.

M² Solutions, Inc. has created this email privacy policy to demonstrate our
firm commitment to your privacy and to the protection of your information.
Why did you receive an email from us?
If you received a mailing from M² Solutions, (a) your email address is either
listed with us as someone who has expressly shared the email address for the
purpose of receiving information in the future ("opt-in"), or (b) you have
registered or purchased one of our products or otherwise have an existing
relationship with us.
Sharing and Usage
You can be assured that M² Solutions will never share, sell, or rent
individual personal information with anyone without your advance permission.
Information submitted to us is only available to employees managing this
information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your
request for information. Notwithstanding the foregoing, if you reside outside
the US or Canada, we may forward your email address and contact information to
one of M² Solutions' authorized distributors in the country where you reside
provided that you have downloaded one of our evaluation versions.
How can you stop receiving email from us?
Each email sent from M² Solutions contains an easy, automated way for you to
cease receiving email from us, or to change your expressed interests. If you
wish to do this, simply follow the instructions at the end of any email.
If you have received unwanted, unsolicited email from M² Solutions, or
purporting to be sent from M² Solutions, please forward a copy of that email
with your comments to support@m2solutionsinc.com.

Main sustainability policiesGlobal personal data protection policy
On March 31, 2015 the Board of Directors of Pirelli & C. S.p.A. approved the Policy ” Global Personal Data protection”. The Policy provides information regarding the basic principles of conduct to be followed in the processing of personal data; the policy is directed, in particular, towards directors, employees and all those who represent the company to third parties and that, in the course of its business activities, perform the processing of personal data.

Privacy Policy
Gate Comm Software has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy.
Your privacy is important to us. Your information will be kept confidential and will not be shared or distributed to any third party.
You will never receive any unwanted postal or electronic mail as a result of information you have given to Gate Comm Software.
Contact information is used to send information about our company to our customers. Users may opt-out of receiving future mailings.
You may receive occasional follow up e-mails from Gate Comm Software to inform you of product news. This is to inform you of new features and our new products and services.
You may unsubscribe at any time. Simply reply with ‘remove’ in subject of your e-mail message.
Gate Comm Software does not collect any personal information about individuals except when specifically and knowingly provided by such individuals.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site.
Our web site uses cookies to make sure you don’t see the same ad repeatedly or to personalize your browsing experience.
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this Web site, please contact us at noric@gatecomm.com

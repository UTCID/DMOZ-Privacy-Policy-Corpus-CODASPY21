Information We Collect from You
Personal information is information that can be used to
identify or contact a specific individual, such as a name,

personal information in several ways from different parts of
our Web site.
How We Use Collected Information
We use your personal information to provide you with products
and services, to better understand your needs and interests,
and to facilitate your use of and purchases from our store.
If you request something from our web site (a product, a
product evaluation, or support services as examples), we will
use the information you provide to fulfill your request. For product and service purchases, contact
information is used to ship products to you, and financial
information is used to bill you for those products and
services.

We will not sell or rent your personal information to others.

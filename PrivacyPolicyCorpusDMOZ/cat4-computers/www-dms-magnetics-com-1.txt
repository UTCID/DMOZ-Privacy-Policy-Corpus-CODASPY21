Privacy PolicyThis Privacy Policy covers how MicroSense, LLC, (known as “MicroSense”) treats Personal Information (as defined below) that MicroSense collects and receives through your use of MicroSense websites. By using the MicroSense Site, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy Only Applies to MicroSenseThis Privacy Policy does not apply to the practices of companies that MicroSense does not own or control, or to the people that MicroSense does not employ or manage.Children’s Privacy PolicyThe MicroSense Site is not intended for use by any children, including children under the age of thirteen. MicroSense does not knowingly collect Personal Information online from children under thirteen. If you think that MicroSense has collected Personal Information from your child, please contact us at webmaster@microsense.net (or the address provided below). Because MicroSense is committed to protecting children’s privacy online, use of the MicroSense Site by children under the age of thirteen is prohibited.Collection of Personal Information“Personal Information” is information about you that is personally identifiable, like your name, address, email address or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. MicroSense collects Personal Information in a number of circumstances, including under the following circumstances: Registrations: To use certain features of the MicroSense Site, you must first complete a form which requires you to provide certain Personal Information, such as your name and email address (providing demographic information, such as your job title, is optional). We keep this registration information on file with any information you've already given us on previous visits to the MicroSense Site. MicroSense collects this information and uses it internally for purposes such as marketing, customer support and administration, technical administration of the MicroSense Site, or to contact you.Purchases and Information: You may order e-mail newsletters or purchase MicroSense products or services. Personal Information collected by MicroSense is often limited to e-mail address, language, country or location, but may include other information. For example, if you choose a service or transaction that requires payment, such as making a purchase in the shopping area of the MicroSense Site, we or our third party service providers will request Personal Information necessary for billing and/or shipping, such as: name, address, telephone number, and credit card number.Other: MicroSense may, from time to time, collect other information from you when you voluntarily provide it (for instance, when we conduct surveys). Information collected by these means is used internally for purposes such as marketing, customer administration, technical administration of the MicroSense Site, or to contact you.Use and Security of Personal InformationIn addition to the uses described above, we may use your Personal Information for the following purposes:To make the MicroSense Site easier for you to use by not making you enter your Personal Information more than once.To respond to your inquiries and to deliver services that you request or purchase and to provide you with related customer service.To help us create and publish content and offers most relevant to you.To send administrative information to you, such as information regarding the MicroSense Site and changes to our terms, conditions and policies.To send you marketing communications that we believe may be of interest to you.As we believe to be necessary or appropriate: (a) under applicable law, including laws outside your country of residence; (b) to comply with legal process; (c) to respond to requests from public and government authorities, including public and government authorities outside your country of residence; (d) to enforce our terms and conditions; (e) to protect our operations or those of any of our affiliates; (f) to protect our rights, privacy, safety or property, and/or that of our affiliates, you or others; and (g) to allow us to pursue available remedies or limit the damages that we may sustain.For our business purposes, such as data analysis, audits, fraud monitoring and prevention, developing new products, enhancing, improving or modifying our products and services, identifying usage trends, determining the effectiveness of our promotional campaigns and operating and expanding our business activities. MicroSense strictly protects the security of your Personal Information and honors your choices for its intended use. We carefully protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction.MicroSense takes precautions to protect our users’ information both online and offline. Inside MicroSense, data is stored in password-controlled servers with limited access.We seek to use reasonable organizational, technical and administrative measures to protect Personal Information within our organization. Unfortunately, no data transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure (for example, if you feel that the security of your account has been compromised), please immediately notify us in accordance with the “Contact Us” section below.Your Personal Information may be transferred to countries other than your own to process and store data in accordance with our Privacy Policy and to provide you with products and services. Some of these countries may not have the same data protection safeguards as the country where you reside. By using the MicroSense Sites, you consent to us transferring your data to these countries. What information MicroSense collects and how we use it, depends upon your activity on the MicroSense Site.MicroSense and its service providers collects non-personally identifiable information from all visitors to the MicroSense Site, such as IP address, browser type, and the date and time a user accessed the MicroSense Site. We use this information for system administration purposes in order to enhance the operation of the MicroSense Site and make it more useful to visitors. MicroSense uses cookies. A cookie is a small amount of data placed on your computer or device. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie terminates automatically after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains on your computer or device, but can be removed by following the directions on your browser and/or mobile operating system. You can set your browser to disable cookies, but this may prevent you from enjoying all of the features of the MicroSense Site.We use cookies to deliver content that is specific to your interests, to streamline the login process, and to collect anonymous user traffic data. The cookies make your use of the MicroSense Site easier, may allow you to retrieve an earlier shopping cart, make the MicroSense Site run more smoothly, and help us to maintain the security of the MicroSense Site. We also use cookies to understand how the Site is used so that MicroSense can improve the Site and resolve questions about it.If you choose to not have your browser or device accept cookies from the MicroSense Site, you will be able to view the text on the screens, however you will not experience a personalized visit nor will you be able to subscribe to the service offerings on the MicroSense Site. Your Shopping Cart will also not remember items added but not checked out. It could mean that we advertise to previous visitors who haven't completed a task on our Sites, for example using the contact form to make an enquiry. This could be in the form of an advertisement on the Google search results page, or a site in the Google Display Network. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone's past visits to the website. Of course, any data collected will be used in accordance with our own privacy policy and Google's privacy policy.

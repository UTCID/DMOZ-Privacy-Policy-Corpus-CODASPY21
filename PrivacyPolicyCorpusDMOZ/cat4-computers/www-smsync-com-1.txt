SECURE SYSTEMS

USE OF INFORMATION
None of the information you provide will be sold to other parties or used for marketing
purposes aside from notifying you by e-mail of new titles and upgrades. Such messages are
usually sent out between one and two times per month. When you receive such a notice, you
have the option of removing your e-mail address from our mailing list.
DATA WE COLLECT AND HOW WE USE IT:
Customer is identified on Order Form, which is filled out
by Customer when purchasing SmartSync Software products.
Personal data of Customers that purchase SmartSync Software licenses
is stored on SmartSync Software servers in the data center located in USA.
SmartSync Software collects Email, Phone Number, Full Name for
for the purpose of account management and user support.
SmartSync Software collects Product Name, Full Name (as recorded in payment / license
management system), Order ID (as recorded in payment / license management system),
Email (as recorded in payment / license management system)
for license activation and periodic license check.
SmartSync Software collects Product Version, OS Version,
Order ID (as recorded in payment / license management system)
during product auto-update. User needs to opt-in to use that option.
SmartSync Software only uses minimum of user data necessary to manage user account
or to process payment. No other information is collected.
HOW WE SHARE YOUR DATA:
SmartSync Software will not send personal data stored on SmartSync Software servers to other companies or individuals,
except for cases when sending of such data is required by SmartSync Software business processes.
SmartSync Software discloses Email, Full Name, Phone Number and Address to
BlueSnap, Inc. (USA)which is
credit card processor.
MARKETING:
SmartSync Software will send infrequent product update newsletters and promotional offers
to the email stored by SmartSync Software, but
Customer shall have an option to unsubscribe from such email newsletters and offers.
SmartSync Software will also present infrequent offers of SmartSync Software and 3rd party software
on post-update web page that is shown when user installs new version of SmartSync Software.

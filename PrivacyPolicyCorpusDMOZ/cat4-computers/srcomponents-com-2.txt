SR Components - Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
At SR Components, Inc., we respect the privacy of all visitors who access and use our Web site. The information we collect on this site is used to understand how we can enhance our site and improve your experience. This policy describes the limited circumstances in which we collect personal information, why we collect it and how we use it. Our policy also describes the choices you can make about how we collect and use your information.
If our information practices change in the future, we will post an updated policy on this Web site. You can tell if the policy has changed by checking the revision date that appears above. If we change the policy in a material way, we will provide appropriate notice to you.
If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@srcomponents.com.
What kind of information does SR Components, Inc. collect?
We may collect personal information from you, such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, etc., when you visit our website or contact us with a question or concern.
How does SR Components, Inc. use my information?
We may use your personal information in the following ways:
•
To
communicate with you and to send you information by email, postal mail,
telephone, text message, or other means about our company and other topics we
think may be of interest to you
•
To
help us address problems with and improve our site design, products and
services
•
To
analyze trends and statistics
•
To
protect the security or integrity of our websites and our business
•
To
contact you if necessary
You always have the choice not to receive information or communications from us. For instructions on opting out, please see the section entitled, "How do I make choices about receiving promotional communications?", below.
Does SR Components, Inc. share my information?
We do not sell or rent the information you provide to us to third parties. However, we do contract with service providers to assist us in maintaining and managing our databases and to communicate with those who are interested in our company. We do not authorize any of those third parties to make any other use of your information. We also may use aggregate, non-personally identifiable information about you to enhance our site. Additionally, we may disclose and maintain user information in special cases, including:
•
In
the unlikely event of a sale of some or all of our business, we will disclose
your personal information to a purchaser that agrees to abide by the terms
and conditions of this privacy policy
•
If
necessary, in our good faith judgment, to comply with laws or regulations or
in response to a valid subpoena, order, or government request, or to protect
the rights of SR Components, Inc. or others.
How do I access or change my information?
You can ask us to update your personal information by contacting us as described in the section entitled, "How do I contact you?", below. Please note that any request to update your personal information may take up to 10 days for your email request.
How do I make choices about receiving promotional communications?
SR Components, Inc. wants to communicate with you only if you want to hear from us.  If you prefer not to receive promotional information or other communications from us, you can let us know in you profile by taking yourself of the news letter.
You can remove your name and address from promotional lists in your profile area.
How does SR Components, Inc. use cookies?
We make limited use of cookies on this Web site. A cookie is a small data file that Web sites often store on your computer's hard drive when you visit their sites. A cookie may contain information (such as a unique user ID) that is used to track the pages of the sites you've visited. We use cookies for the limited purpose of determining whether you have visited a particular page before, so that you will not have to view introductory information that is played for first-time visitors. We do not store personally identifiable information in your cookie.
How do I know my personal information is secure?
We have in place certain physical, electronic, contractual, and managerial safeguards to protect the security and privacy of your personal information.
What is "phishing"?
"Phishing" is a scam designed to steal your personal information. If you receive an email that looks like it is from us asking you for your personal information, do not respond. We will never request your password, user name, credit card information or other personal information through email.
What about links to other websites?
Please remember that the privacy policy stated here applies only to the srcomponents.com website.  In addition, this site may contain links to other third-party sites that are provided solely for the convenience and information of our visitors. We do not control the privacy policies or practices of these third-party sites and are not responsible for the content or practices of any linked websites. You should review those policies before providing any personal information.
How do I contact you?
For questions about our privacy policy, to make choices about receiving promotional or other communications, or to update your personal information, you can contact us by email at support@srcomponents.com.

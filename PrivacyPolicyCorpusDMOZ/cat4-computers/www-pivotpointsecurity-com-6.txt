External Linking Policy
We have created this External Linking Policy to outline our requirements for accepting a link from an external site. Pivot Point Security welcomes links from websites that strictly adhere to the guidelines set forth in this policy:
If you would like to backlink to the resources on the Pivot Point Security website, please be sure that the following guidelines are strictly followed:
The webpage where the link is being placed must be related to information security.
The linking website should contain original content relevant to Pivot Point Security and to the content on our site.
No content on the Pivot Point Security website may be copied or re-hosted on another website without consent of Pivot Point Security.
Referral traffic coming from a backlinked site that is in violation of this policy will be blocked.

Security
When you place an order at ineedhits, your personal and credit card details are
transmitted to our secured server over a secure link, using industry standard 128
bit encryption technology. At no time do we transmit unencrypted personal information
over the Internet.
Your details are retained in a secure area of our server so that you can place orders
more easily in the future. For more information about how we treat your personal
information, please refer to our Privacy Information.

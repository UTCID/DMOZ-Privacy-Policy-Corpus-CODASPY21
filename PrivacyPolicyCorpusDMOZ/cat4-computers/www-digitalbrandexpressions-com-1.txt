Digital Brand Expressions Privacy Policy
For each visitor to our website, our Web server automatically recognizes only the visitor’s domain name, but not the e-mail address. We collect only the domain name, but not the e-mail address of visitors to our website.
For visitors who communicate with us via email or voluntarily provide information to us via requests, the contact form, newsletter sign-ups, etc., we collect contact information, including e-mail addresses.
We may, from time to time, contact visitors who have volunteered contact information via the above methods. At all times, visitors we contact will have the opportunity to permanently opt-out of receiving further contact from DBE.
With respect to cookies: we set cookies to analyze your interaction with the website. We do not collect any personal data. Persons who supply us with their phone numbers online will only receive phone calls from us for the purpose intended when submitting contact information (e.g., contact request, webinar sign-up, etc.).
With respect to ad servers, we do not serve ads via the DBE website.
From time to time, we may use customer information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change we will post the policy changes to our website and we will use for these new purposes only data collected from the time of the policy change forward. If you are concerned about how your information is used, you should check back at our website periodically.
Upon authorized request we provide a site visitor with access to the information [including proprietary information] that we maintain about him/her. Visitors can request this information by e-mail us at info@digitalbrandexpressions.com .

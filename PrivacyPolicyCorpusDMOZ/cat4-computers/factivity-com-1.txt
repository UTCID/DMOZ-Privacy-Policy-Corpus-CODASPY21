Privacy PolicyFACTIVITY respects the privacy of our customers and those who visit our site. No effort is made to identify individuals without their knowledge. When a visitor requests pages on the FACTIVITY site, our Web servers and statistics package automatically recognize the browser’s domain name, and IP address. Our servers do not record email addresses. We collect the domain names and IP addresses in order to aggregate information on what pages users access or visit. This information is collected solely for statistical purposes and is not used to identify individuals.
FACTIVITY maintains a strict “no spam” policy. If you choose to provide your email address for purposes of requesting a quote, contacting FACTIVITY, requesting free research, etc., your email address will kept private and never be sold to a third party. If you opt-in, you will receive only the emails or notifications you have requested. At any point, you can choose to remove your email address from our list and will not receive any future mailings.

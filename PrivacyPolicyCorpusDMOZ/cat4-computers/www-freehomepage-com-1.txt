Privacy Policy
FreeHomePage.com collects only aggregate information pertaining to visitors to its network
of sites, including domain name, visited pages, referring
URLs, and other publicly available information. FreeHomePage.com
uses this information to help improve its web site and services,
and to customize the content of its pages for each individual
consumer.
FreeHomePage.com
does not sell or commercially make available specific information
about its users or members other than in aggregate form, except
in cooperation with law enforcement bodies in regards to content
violations or violations of applicable laws.
FreeHomePage.com maintains a database of member information which is used only for internal
purposes such as advertising, collections, technical support and notifying
members of changes or enhancements to the service. FreeHomePage.com reserves
the right to regularly communicate with members pertaining to their web
site and other services, available products, news, and special discounts
which may be of interest members of the community.
Any
FreeHomePage.com member may cancel their membership at any time by going
to http://members.freehomepage.com/cgi-bin/util/deleteaccount.
When an account is canceled all user information is removed from the FreeHomePage.com
system except that in aggregate form.

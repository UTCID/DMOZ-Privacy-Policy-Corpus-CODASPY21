2. Message boards/forums policy / comment posting
Users of the site's Message Boards and Forums must register separately for these services (both are free of charge) in order to post messages, although they needn't register to visit the site. During registration the user is required to supply a username, password, and email address. While posting comments user has to submit name and email address. Name will be displayed but email address will not be exposed at the public view pages.

Privacy Statement for www.reliablegroup.com
Reliable Group Ventures LLC has created this privacy statement in order to demonstrate our firm’s commitment to privacy. The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for www.reliablegroup.com.
We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and/or to administer our Web site. This gives us an idea of which parts of our site users are visiting. We do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means that a users session will be tracked, but the user will be anonymous.
Our site’s registration form requires you to give us contact information like your name and email address. We use this contact information from the registration form to send you information about our company. This site contains links to other sites. Reliable Group is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and/or alteration of information under our control. The data resides behind a firewall, with access restricted to authorized Infosys personnel.

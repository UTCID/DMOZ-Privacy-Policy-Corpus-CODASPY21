Privacy policy
When you order on our website, a certain amount of data will be
required to process your orders.
The new European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
requires that you be informed of your rights in the processing
of the data collected.
The data collector is Active+ Software, represented by
Daniel Doguet.
Identification data are collected in order to process your
orders and invoices, and to inform you about our products new
releases.
Your email address is not used for any purpose other than to
send you order confirmations, product upgrades and news. It will
not be sold to any third parties. If at anytime you decide that
you do not want to receive further messages from us, each email
will include a link to unsubscribe.
You can also request the portability of your data from
Active+ Software. It means being able to retrieve the data
collected in a simple electronic format that you can transmit if
needed. The format in which you will recover your data will be
specified when you request this from Active+ Software.

information about your rights, you can contact the French data

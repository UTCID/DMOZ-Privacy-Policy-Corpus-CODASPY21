The IAB workshop on Internet Privacy, jointly organized with the W3C, ISOC, and MIT CSAIL, was hosted by MIT on 8-9 December 2010.
Internet Privacy Workshop: Slides Presentations
At this workshop we tried to minimize the number of slide talks. Still, we had a few presentations to stimulate discussion and to frame the debate.

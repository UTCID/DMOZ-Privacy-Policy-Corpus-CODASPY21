Policy Staff
Last Updated: 24 July 2019
The GNSO Policy Development Support Team supports the GNSO community's development of bottom-up, consensus policies and guidelines that help advance the stable and secure operation of the Internet's unique identifier systems. Biographies of staff members can be found on the ICANN Staff Page. To reach the ICANN policy staff, please write to policy-staff [at] icann.org. We welcome your questions and comments.

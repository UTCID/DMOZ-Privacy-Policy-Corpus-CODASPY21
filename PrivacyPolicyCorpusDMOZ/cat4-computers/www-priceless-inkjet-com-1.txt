Privacy Policy
At Priceless Inkjet, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect about you to process orders
and to provide a more personalized shopping experience. Your personal information will never be, under any circumstances, released,
sold or rented to any third party. Guaranteed! The following discloses the information gathering and dissemination policies for
Priceless-Inkjet.com Web site.
We collect only specific information required to process orders, and maintain personal contact with our customers.
For example, we will retain in our files the name, address, telephone numbers, email address, shipping information and type of printer owned.
It is not our intent to send our customers unsolicited mailings. Customers may request at their option to receive occasional special offers and
may unsubscribe at any time. Financial data provided by the customers is used only to complete the specified transaction and only stored with
the payment processor we use. Our payment processor allows us to reuse this data, given that the associated shipping and billing address
informaion has not changed.
Security
All communication with our web-server is always 100% encrypted. Only authorized personnel have access to order data or personal details.
We accept responsibility for your information while in our direct possession.
Surveys
From time to time we may initiate an on-line survey to gather specific information to better serve our customers. Be assured such information
will always be used for the purpose intended and is never shared with any third party.
External Links
Priceless-Inkjet.com may feature certain links to other company sites, which we believe maybe of interest to our clients. However, we cannot
assume responsibility for the security or privacy practices of these third party sites.
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the security being used on the web site to protect your information and data,
please feel free to contact us.

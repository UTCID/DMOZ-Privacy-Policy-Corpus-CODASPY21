Personal Information
You can visit Recovery Specialties on the Internet without telling us who you are and
without providing any personal information about you or your company.
Recovery Specialties, LLC collects
no information whatsoever regarding your visits to this site. As we do not collect or maintain any of this
type of information, we can not sell, share, or otherwise disclose any information about your visits.
There are times, however, when we or our partners may need information from you.
You may choose to give us personal information in a variety of situations. For example, you may want to
provide us information, such as your name and address or e-mail id, to correspond with you, to register
for a conference or special event, or to provide you with a newsletter or other materials.
If such information is
provided, it will be provided solely at your discretion and via email, regular mail or telephone.
This information is never solicited by Recovery Specialties via a web portal nor is this
information maintained on this site.
If you indicate that you do not want us to use this information to make further contact with you beyond fulfilling your requests, we will respect your wishes. If you provide personal information about someone else, such as a spouse or work colleague, we will assume that you have their permission to do so.

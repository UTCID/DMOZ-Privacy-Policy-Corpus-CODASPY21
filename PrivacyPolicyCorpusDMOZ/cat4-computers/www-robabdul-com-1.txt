RobAbdul.com is committed to privacy
RobAbdul.com is committed to privacy. I am committed to protecting the privacy of those using my site and the confidentiality of the personal information with which my subscribers provide.
Your personal information is safe with RobAbdul.com. RobAbdul.com will never sell personal information or share personal information with third parties.
At RobAbdul.com I use the information I collect to serve my customers in the following ways:
If you contact me via my website, I will reply to you with the email address and the address my email reply to the name you provided whilst completing the online form on my contact page.
If you become a subscriber to the RobAbdul.com website or use other services your name, e-mail address, address and other information on the subscription form are kept by me and used alert you to new products and or services of which you may be interested in.
I may also use your contact information to let you know about enhancements to the site and your subscription entitlements.
If you would rather not receive this information please inform my by e-mail at privacy at robabdul.com.

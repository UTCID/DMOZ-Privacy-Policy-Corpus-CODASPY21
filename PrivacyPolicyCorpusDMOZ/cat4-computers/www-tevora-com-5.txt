Risk Program Development
Tevora’s Risk Program Development services guarantee alignment with your organization’s unique needs.
Developing a comprehensive and tailored risk program that addresses the unique needs of your organization requires a deep understanding of security frameworks and experience in managing and eliminating potential risks. Today risk management has shifted from merely meeting compliance requirements to tactical and ‘by design’ methods. At Tevora, we can provide you with the necessary high-level guidance to develop your Risk Management Program. This is particularly important to organizations working with emerging technologies.
Our risk program development services will:
Align with specific frameworks such as ISO 27001/18, NIST and COSO
Meet your unique operating needs

Around the World in 80 Clicks: Privacy
As you may have noticed, there are several ways in which you can leave your personal data at Around the World in 80 Clicks. The most obvious is the mailinglist, where you can be notified of any changes to or news about the site. Another one is the facility to send any picture of this site as a postcard, or suggesting this site to a friend you know might be interested.
We can assure you that all and any information of private character, like email addresses and names, will be treated in a discreet way. Your personal data will always be protected and not revealed or sold to third parties. They will only be used for the purpose you entrusted them with us.

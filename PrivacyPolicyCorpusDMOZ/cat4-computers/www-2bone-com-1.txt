- Your privacy and Third party advertising & cookies

Your privacy is important to us.
We value you as a user of this site, and we want you to always enjoy your
experience with 2bone.com.
On a personal level, we are as sick and tired of spam and unsolicited commercial email
(UCE) as everybody else.
That's why any and all of the personal and/or business information that you
provide to us will remain with us, and will be used only for those reasons that we have
expressly informed you about.

Privacy re: the 'Send to a Friend' feature on 2bone.com
Your personal informaton, such as your name, email address, and any personal
comments that you have added to the "Send to a Friend" form will only be sent/given
to the email address(es) that you have specifically entered on that same form,
or for uses for
which you have specifically requested and/or given us permission.
We maintain a secure log of the computer addresses that are used to submit the "Send to
a friend" form, this log may also contain the email addresses that you enter into
the form page. This information is retained only for our personal reference, and
is used only in the event that we suspect that the program is being abused.
Abusers of the "Send to a friend" program will find that their email address and
various other information is added to a "banned" list, obtained from our logged
information.

Hosting and access logging
The LucasFonts website is hosted on a web server provided by Amazon Web Services (AWS). The server is located in Frankfurt/Main, Germany.
Every visit of the LucasFonts website generates an entry in the access log on the web server. This log file contains the following information:
the IP address from which you issued the request
the date and time
the URL you requested
the URL of any page that referred you to the page
the type of browser and operating system you are using
and whether your request was successful.
Your IP adress will be anonymized, so that your visit of the LucasFonts website can not be assigned to a specific person. These log files are deleted by us from time to time.
AWS is a subsidiary of Amazon.com, Inc. or one of its affiliates. Please refer to Amazon’s privacy information: aws.amazon.com/compliance/data-privacy-faq.
Use of cookies
In order for the navigation of this website and its shopping system to work for you, information commonly known as ‘cookies’ get placed on your computer. A cookie is a piece of information that is placed on your computer by a web server and can only be read by the server that gave it to you. It functions as your identification card, helping to correlate your passwords, purchases, and preferences to your personal browser. It cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses.
If you do not wish to receive cookies, you may disable the cookie function in your browser. In this case, you may not be able to use the full functionality of the LucasFonts website.
LucasFonts will never use cookies to retrieve information that is unrelated to the LucasFonts site or services.

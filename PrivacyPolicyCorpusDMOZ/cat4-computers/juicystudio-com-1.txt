Cookies
The Internet is a stateless medium. It doesn't retain information, and so cookies are sometimes required to help retain information between the client and the server. Cookies are small, harmless text files. Cookies are only used on this site if you choose to be remembered on the comment section of articles, or to remember your choice if you select the High Contrast Layout from the Navigation Menu.

Privacy policy
Personal Information
Personal information you provide us, either through our online store or on our sample request form, will never be sold to or shared with any third parties. We may occasionally send you product information or newsletters, but you may unsubscribe at any time with the link found at the bottom of each email. If you have not purchased anything from us, you can request that your information be removed from our system by emailing us at privacy@beezix.com. If you have created an account with our online store, you can modify the type of communications you receive in your Account Settings. All financial data collected by our online store, such as credit card information, is encrypted and submitted securely.
While you browse our site, certain types of information, such as the content you view, the date and time that you view this content or the location information associated with your IP address, are automatically transmitted. This information is used to monitor the usage of our website, and for advertising purposes – it is statistical and does not include personal information.
Security
We implement a secure processing server that uses banking-level encryption on our site when collecting financial information to ensure a high level of security for any information entered.

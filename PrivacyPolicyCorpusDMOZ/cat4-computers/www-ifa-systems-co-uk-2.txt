Privacy Policy
IFA Systems Limited respects your privacy. The information that you provide us with, or that is gathered automatically, helps us to monitor our service and provide you with the services that you have subscribed to. Although we may need to share your contact details with our partner companies as part of the service we offer, we undertake not to pass on the details which you supply to us to any other parties without your express prior permission.
While we strive to keep the information that you supply directly or indirectly secure, please note that the Internet is not a fully secure medium. Please therefore be aware that any personal information that you submit through forums, letters or emails for publication and similar may be collected by others.
By using our site you agree to accept this privacy policy and are aware that our policy may change with time. Any changes to this policy will appear on this web page.

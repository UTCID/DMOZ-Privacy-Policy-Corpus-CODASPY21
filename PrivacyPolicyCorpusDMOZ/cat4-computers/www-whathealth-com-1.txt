What Health - Privacy Policy
Whenever a web page is requested by a users browser,
information is sent by the browser to the web server. This information
includes the IP address and the name of the browser the visitor is using.
For each visitor to this web site, our server logs the IP address, operating system and browser type. We use this information for statistical purposes which helps us improve the content of our web site. This information is not shared with other organizations.
Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information generated by a Web server and stored in the user's computer ready for future access. If cookies have been enabled in a users browser, we track the pages a user visits and the length of time they have been browsing this website. This information is used to improve the content of this website. Please note that this website has been designed to remain 'functional' if cookies have been disabled by a users browser.
This website service is provided free of charge to our visitors. To help cover operating costs we run Google Adsense advertisements on many of our pages.
Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to
our users based on their visit to our sites and other sites on the
Internet.
Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

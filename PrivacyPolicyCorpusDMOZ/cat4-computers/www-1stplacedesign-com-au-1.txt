Log Files
We use visitors’ IP addresses to analyse trends, administer the site, track user’s movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. This information is used by us for marketing purposes and will not be sold or shared with anyone else.

We do not sell or allow other persons or organisations access to your personal or business information. This information is solely used for contact purposes and for reasons directly related to maximising our effort to supply an effective service.
The information you provide may be included in customised documentation for your interest only.
The information indicated above remains strictly the property of 1st Place Design only and will not be sold to or shared with any other parties.

General Advisement
At Tevora, our tailored General Advisement services
ensure you have a deep understanding of the regulations
your organization is required to meet.
Running a business or organization today requires both an awareness of threats posed by cybersecurity breaches as well as adhering to best practices in security protocols.
At Tevora, we take a flexible approach to assisting your unique organization in understanding the current legal, regulatory, and fiduciary requirements and expectations of data security and privacy.
We support our clients via general advisement services including:
Teaching GDPR and Data Privacy workshops
Providing guidance surrounding evolving regulations
Designing a technical and procedural infrastructure to conform within defined security controls

Privacy
This privacy policy sets out how ITCourseware uses and protects information that you provide when you use this website. ITCourseware is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement. ITCourseware may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are aware of any changes.
Use of Collected Information
In order to purchase our books online, we ask that you provide us with your name, e-mail address, address, phone number, and payment information that enables us to bill and ship your purchase accurately. All of this information is stored, except for your credit card number. You will be required to re-enter that number every time you visit.
ITCourseware uses the information that we collect solely for internal statistics and to update our products, customer service, and website. We will not share your personal information with any business or third party.
Security
ITCourseware, LLC uses a variety of security procedures to protect your personal information. We store your information on ITCourseware's computer servers, kept in limited-access facilities. When we transmit data (such as your credit card number) over the Internet, we encrypt that information, as well as using secure protocols, such as Secure Socket Layer (SSL).
Cookies
Our site requires cookies in order to use the shopping cart and order functions. Information stored in these cookies is also considered private. If you do not wish to use cookies when placing an order, you will need to contact ITCourseware Customer Support who can place the order for you.
Third-Party Sites
There are links to third-party sites on ITCourseware's website. We are not responsible for the content or privacy of those sites and any interactions that you may have with those companies.
Communication
We periodically communicate with clients about the availability of new products. We usually use email and/or regular mail for such communication. You can remove your email or physical address from our lists by contacting us ITCourseware Customer Support.

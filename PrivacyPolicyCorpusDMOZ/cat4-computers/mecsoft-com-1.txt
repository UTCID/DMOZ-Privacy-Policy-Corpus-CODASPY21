This Privacy Policy lets You (as defined below) know how MecSoft collects and uses Your Personal Data and how You can control its use. MecSoft Corporation, a California Corporation headquartered at 18019 Sky Park Circle Ste: KL, Irvine, CA-92614, U.S.A., (hereinafter “MecSoft”) processes Personal Data (defined below) in the course of its business, including the Personal Data of people using and browsing the MecSoft Website (the “Website”) and providing information via demo downloads and product registration.
SCOPE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY
This Privacy Policy sets forth the principles and guidelines governing the protection of Your Personal Data (as defined below) including Your Personal Data collected during demo product download, product registration and on or through the Website and concerning users of our products and services and the Website’s visitors.
Personal data (“Personal Data”) means any information that may result in the identification of an individual. Non-Personal Data means any information that does not result in the identification of an Individual. MecSoft collects Personal Data online and offline and this Privacy Policy is applicable regardless of the means of collection. Your use of the Website and any Personal Data You provide to us are subject to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
PERSONAL DATA COLLECTED
When you download information from our Website, we collect certain personal information from you that can be used to identify you, including any information that we deem necessary to provide you with our Service or which you may provide to us voluntarily. The information we collect from you to the extent that it is private is disclosed only in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Whenever you visit our Website, we may collect non-identifying information from you, such as your IP address, referring URL, browser, operating system, cookie information, and Internet Service Provider. Without a subpoena, voluntary compliance on the part of your Internet Service Provider, or additional records from a third party, this information alone cannot usually be used to identify you.
PURPOSES FOR COLLECTING PERSONAL DATA
MecSoft collects and uses Your Personal Data for the purpose of carrying out its business based on the following purposes:
Processing activities for Personal Data collected from those who visit a Website without entering information, and which are based on Your prior consent if required by law
To improve Your user experience and to recognize that You may have given us information and do not need to provide such information again
Processing activities for Personal Data collected from those who provide information to us for purposes listed above, and which are based on Your prior consent if required by law
To enable You to request and receive information related to MecSoft products and services from MecSoft and its resellers
To provide You with a personalized interactive use of the Website
To ascertain Your requirements and interests and provide You with the most suitable products and/or services
To enable You to participate in marketing activities and surveys
To enable You to register for a seminar, webinar, or event.
To allow You to open and maintain an account in order to obtain specialized documentation and technical assistance
To enable You to make online purchases of some of our products and/or services
To enable You to exchange information with other users of the Website;
To enable MecSoft to manage its business relationships, including sales opportunities, commercial offers, purchasing, contracts, orders, and invoices, and which may include conversational data (e.g., chat, email, and voice messaging data)
To provide You with commercial or support services
To enable MecSoft to manage its Research & Development activities, including enhancement requests and bug incidents
To manage Your training and/or certification on MecSoft products and services.
To process an employment application from You and manage the recruitment sources
To manage the Anti-Piracy & Compliance program
To manage the MecSoft’ obligations related to applicable export laws, trade sanctions, and embargoes issued, without limitation, by the European Union and its member states, and of the United States of America. This may include (i) automated checks of any user registration data as set out herein and other information You provide about Your identity against applicable sanctioned party lists; (ii) regular repetition of such checks whenever a sanctioned-party list is updated or when You update Your information; (iii) blocking of access to MecSoft services and systems in case of a potential match; and (iv) contacting You to confirm Your identity in case of a potential match.
Subject to applicable local law, by providing Personal Data such as but not limited to an email address, You hereby expressly authorize MecSoft to use it together with other relevant Personal Data to send You commercial or marketing communications. MecSoft might also use Your email address for administrative and other non-marketing purposes.
RETENTION OF PERSONAL DATA
MecSoft does not store Your Personal Data for more time than necessary. Retention periods may vary depending on the data categories and the processing activities.
Where MecSoft is processing and using Your Personal Data as permitted by law or under your consent, MecSoft will store Your Personal Data (i) only as long as required to fulfill the purposes set out above, (ii) where MecSoft has a legitimate interest in using Your Personal Data, until You object to MecSoft’ use of Your Personal Data, or (iii) where You gave Your consent, until You withdraw it. However, where MecSoft is required by applicable law to retain Your Personal Data longer or where Your Personal Data is required to assert or defend against legal claims, MecSoft will retain Your Personal Data until the end of the relevant retention period or until the claims in question have been settled.
TRANSFER OF PERSONAL DATA
If You are accessing the Website from a location outside of the United States, please note that through Your continued use of the Website, which is governed by California law, the corresponding Terms of Use and this Privacy Policy, You may be transferring Your Personal Data to the United States and You consent to that transfer.
MecSoft will disclose Personal Data to third parties if MecSoft determines that such disclosure is necessary for technical reasons or to protect MecSoft’ legal interests (such as in the event of acquisition or merger by a third company or total or partial liquidation of MecSoft).
Additionally, MecSoft may share Your Personal Data with the reseller assigned to You or in the territory where You are located. MecSoft may also share Your Personal Data with business partners when MecSoft and the business partner sponsor an event together or participate in a marketing promotion together and in which You engage.
MecSoft may also disclose such personal information if required by law or if MecSoft believes in good faith that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with legal process (for example, a warrant, a subpoena, or other court order) or protect the rights, property, or personal safety of MecSoft, our customers, or the public.
RIGHTS OF ACCESS TO AND RECTIFICATION OF PERSONAL DATA
MecSoft will take reasonable steps to ensure that the Personal Data collected is used for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy and that such Personal Data is correct and up to date.
You have a right to access Your Personal Data. In addition, You have a right to request the rectification, completion, update, and erasure of Your Personal Data. You also have a right to obtain a copy of the Personal Data stored by MecSoft.
If You have an account, You may be able to exercise Your rights of access to and rectification of the Personal Data by logging on to Your account. Otherwise, You may exercise Your rights of access to and rectification of the data by sending an email to privacyadmin@mecsoft.com.
Depending on the scope of the request, MecSoft reserves the right to charge a reasonable fee to cover any out-of-pocket costs incurred in connection with such access, modification, and deletion of Personal Data. MecSoft may deny access to Personal Data in limited circumstances, defined by applicable laws and regulations.
DATA SECURITY
MecSoft undertakes to ensure the protection and security of Personal Data that You choose to communicate, in order to ensure the confidentiality of Your Personal Data and prevent Your Personal Data from being distorted, damaged, destroyed or disclosed to unauthorized parties.
MecSoft maintains reasonable physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect Personal Data from loss, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, and destruction. However, while MecSoft strives to protect Your Personal Data, in light of the inevitable risks of data transmission over the Internet, MecSoft cannot guarantee full protection against any error occurring during the course of Personal Data transmission that is beyond MecSoft’ reasonable control.
Since Personal Data may be confidential, access is limited to employees, contractors & resellers who have a need to know such data in carrying out their tasks. All the people who have access to Your Personal Data are bound by a duty of confidentiality and subject to disciplinary actions and/or other sanctions if they fail to meet these obligations.
COOKIES
The Website may use cookies or other technologies (together referred to as “cookies”) that may collect or store Personal Data. Cookies are text files stored and used to record non-personal and personal information concerning Your browsing and use of the Website. Cookies can be either persistent (remain after You close Your browser) or transient (removed when You close Your browser).
MecSoft may use cookies to:
Improve Your user experience through, for example:
enabling a service to recognize Your device so You don’t have to give the same information several times, and
recognizing that You may already have given a username and password so You don’t need to do so repeatedly.
Analyze traffic and data on a Website to:
measure how many people are using the Website, so they can be made easier to use and navigate and have enough capacity to ensure have adequate response times, and
help MecSoft understand how people interact with a Website so MecSoft can improve the functionality and performance of the Website.
MecSoft may also use third-party vendor services to measure and analyze the effectiveness of the Website and to:
analyze Your browsing habits and measure a Website’s audience,
analyze Your interests and offer You targeted marketing or advertising, and
allow You to share content from the Website with other people on social networks and to let other people know what You are looking at and how You feel about content
In such cases, cookies provided by such third party vendors may be used and stored. By transmitting the information generated by these cookies, the cookies’ settings ensure that IP addresses are anonymous before geo-localization and before storage. Our third party vendors will use this information for the evaluation of Your use of the Website, compiling reports on the Website activity for MecSoft to provide better services to You suited to Your needs and provide advertisements to You that are suited to Your needs. Cookies will not associate Your IP address with any other data they store.
When You browse the Website, cookies are enabled by default and data may be read or stored locally on Your device if Your browser settings allow. You can configure Your browser software to refuse all cookies (including cookies used for web analytics services); if You do so, however, some areas and features of the Website may not function properly and/or You may not be able to access some parts or services of the Website.

Although MecSoft has put in place reasonable safeguards to protect Personal Data, we recognize that there is no method of transmitting or storing Personal Data that is completely secure. MecSoft is committed to ensuring the privacy of Personal Data. If You have a reason to believe that the security of Your Personal Data has been compromised or misused, You should contact MecSoft by sending an email to privacyadmin@mecsoft.com.
MecSoft will investigate and attempt to resolve complaints regarding use and disclosure of Personal Data in accordance with the principles contained in this Privacy Policy.

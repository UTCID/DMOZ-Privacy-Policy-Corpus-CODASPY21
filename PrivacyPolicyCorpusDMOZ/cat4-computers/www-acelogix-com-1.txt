Privacy Policy
Gathering of informationAcelogix Software respects your privacy.  In a few areas on our web site and online customer support tools, we
ask you to provide information that will enable us to enhance your site
visit, to assist you with technical support issues or to follow up with
you after your visit. It is completely optional for you to participate.We request information from you when you:
Place an order
Fill out a support request
Join our forum
In each of the instances
above, we may ask for your name, e-mail address, phone number, address,
as well as other similar personal information that is needed to register
or subscribe you to services or offers. In the case of newsletters or
mailing lists, you will be able to "unsubscribe" to these mailings at
any time.Use of informationInformation gathered on our website is used for internal purposes related
to accounting and site demographics. Additionally, Acelogix
Software may send announcements to our customers
via email. Customers include people who have
purchased our products, downloaded them or
registered their copy of our product. Sharing of informationAcelogix Software does not share your personal information in any way
with any third parties without your permission.

ISO 27018
ISO 27018
Tevora guides you through the ISO certification process to ensure you are complying with global privacy regulations.
Meeting ISO standards defined by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO) can be advantageous to your organization if you are required to comply with global regulations, such as GDPR. We assist clients in achieving ISO certifications such as ISO 27001 and ISO 27018.
Program Development
At Tevora, we can help you:
Define the scope of your program
Identify gaps and risks to receiving certification
Guide you through the process of certification
Organizations that implement such programs that meet ISO standards benefit from the recognition and acceptance that comes with meeting such certifications, enabling them to continue international operations and comply with new standards.

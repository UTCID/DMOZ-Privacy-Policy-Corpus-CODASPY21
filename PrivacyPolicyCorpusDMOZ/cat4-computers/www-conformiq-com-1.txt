In general, you can visit Conformiq's Web pages without actively telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. By ensuring that you are aware of and understand the Conformiq Privacy Policy, we can provide you with better service. We collect information about you in several ways. You are not required under law to provide any of the aforesaid information, however, as aforesaid, your abstention from providing such information could restrict your ability to use and/or receive services from the Web Site.

Protecting your personal information
Conformiq safeguards the security of the data you send us with physical, electronic, and managerial procedures. We urge you to take every precaution to protect your personal data when you are on the Internet. Change your passwords often, use a combination of letters and numbers, and make sure you use a secure browser.
The Conformiq website uses industry-standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption on all Web pages where personal information is required.

To the extent that you do provide us with personal information, Conformiq wishes to maintain it accurately. Cookies  Collection of Information by Use of Technological Means
Conformiq uses technological means known as Cookies for the purpose operating the Web Site and maintaining the Web Site in normal working order, within the framework of which Conformiq also collects statistical information in connection with the use of the Web Site, verification of data, and to suit the Web Site to your personal preferences and data security requirements.
Cookies are text files that are created by your Internet browser in accordance with instructions provided to it by the system computers. Some of the Cookies will be deleted from your computer once you close the Internet browser, however others will be saved on your computer.
Cookies contain information such as the Internet WebPages you have visited, the time spent on each site, from where you arrived at the Web Site etc. They are also used for a quick and easy entrance into your account on the Web Site, by memorizing your details in parts of the Web Site that require registration. The information in a Cookie is encrypted, and Conformiq uses security means that are commonly acceptable in the industry in order to protect the security of the information and its computers

PRIVACY POLICY
RedStatCounter.com - Privacy Policy1. RedStatCounter.com collects personal
information when you register. RedStatCounter.com may combine information about
you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other
companies.
2. When you login to your account and request information from
RedStatCounter.com we do not log your IP address or domain name etc.
3. RedStatCounter.com will not sell or distribute your e-mail address and/or
account details to a third party without prior approval from yourself.
4. RedStatCounter.com has a no spam policy. The e-mail address you supply to
RedStatCounter.com will only be used to contact you for any specific reason
associated with your account.
5. RedStatCounter.com respects the privacy and rights of its visitors. No
attempt is made to identify any individual without their prior approval or
knowledge.

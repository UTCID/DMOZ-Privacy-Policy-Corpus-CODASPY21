Privacy Policy
Knowledge Transfer Consulting Services, Inc. of Minnesota is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our
students, clients and customers. We will only use your personal information for the following purposes:
To deliver services, such as newsletters, events and training updates that you request or purchase.
To alert you about special offers, updated information and other new services from Knowledge Transfer
Consulting Services, Inc., if you so request.
We hope you see the value in receiving our informational emails to better understand the educational
solutions and resources we offer. However, if you would prefer not to receive email from us, please select
"remove" in our emails to unsubscribe.
We will never share your email address or personal information with others

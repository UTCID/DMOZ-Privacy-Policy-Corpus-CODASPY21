Privacy Policy
We will actively fight any attempt to force Progressive Technology Project to disclose user information or logs.
We will not read, search, or process any of your data other than to protect you from viruses and spam, debug problems affecting our shared infrastructure, or when directed to do so by you when troubleshooting.
Your data is encrypted. All data saved on our servers and all backups are stored in an encrypted format.
Our policy is to immediately notify you if we learn that you are under investigation.
To protect the privacy of our members, we regularly purge log files.

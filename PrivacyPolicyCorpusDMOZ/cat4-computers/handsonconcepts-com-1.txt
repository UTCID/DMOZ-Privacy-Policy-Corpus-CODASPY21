Privacy Statement
We hope the following policy will help you understand how Hands On Concepts collects, uses and safeguards the personal information you provide on our website.
Information Collection
If you browse the Hands On Concepts website, you do so anonymously. We don`t collect personal information – not even your email address.
At times, we may request that you voluntarily supply us with personal information, such as your email address and postal address, for purposes such as:
corresponding with us
placing on-line orders and training registrations
subscribing to our on-line emailed updates service
We use this information for the purposes of responding to your requests for services only. We forward our emailed updates only to web visitors who have voluntarily subscribed to the service.
Unsubscribe
Any subscriber can automatically remove their name from the emailed updates recipients list, at any time, by sending us an email with ‘unsubscribe’ in the subject line of the message.
Information Disclosure
We do not sell, rent, give away or otherwise disclose your email address or personal information gathered through forms on this website.
Contacting Hands On Concepts
If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this website, please contact us.

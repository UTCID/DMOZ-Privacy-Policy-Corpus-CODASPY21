Privacy
This Web site does not record any details about users.
A cookie is sent to your computer to store a session Id.
This session Id will be used to store your language choice and will be destroyed at the end of your visit.
An internal network IP address check is performed on the welcome page (http://ip-lookup.net/).
The result of this test (your LAN address) is not sent back
to our site or anywhere else and thus, is not broadcast over the Internet.
The LAN address found is visible only from your browser.
We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website.
These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about
goods and services of interest to you.
If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here.

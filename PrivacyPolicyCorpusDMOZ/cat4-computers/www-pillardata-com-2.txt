Oracle Buys Pillar Data Systems
Oracle has acquired Pillar Data Systems, a leading provider of innovative and highly scalable SAN Block I/O storage systems that provide exceptional performance scaling characteristics with patented Quality of Service. The combination is expected to help Oracle deliver a complete line of storage products that runs Oracle software faster and more efficiently.

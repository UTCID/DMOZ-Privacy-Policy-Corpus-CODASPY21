Cookie Policy
General information about cookies
Like most websites, WinCatalog.com uses cookies and web log files to track site usage.
Cookies are small files that are sent to and stored on your computer by the websites you visit. Cookies are stored in your browser’s file directory. Next time you visit the site, your browser will read the cookie and relay the information back to the website or element that originally set the cookie.
Cookies cannot damage your computer or the files stored on your computer.
You can adjust your browser so that you don’t receive any cookies during your next visit of the web site. In order to know more about these functions you can consult the Help function of your browser.
How WinCatalog.com uses cookies
This website stores cookies on your computer. These cookies are used to collect information about how you interact with our website and allow us to remember you.
We use this information in order to improve and customize your browsing experience and for analytics and metrics about our visitors on this website.
We use this information anonymously and do not know anything about you except the pages you visited on our site, where have you come from to our site, and some technical parameters that do not contain personal information. Please read more about the information we collect below in the Privacy Policy section.

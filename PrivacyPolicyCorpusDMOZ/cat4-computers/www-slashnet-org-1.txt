Privacy Policy
SlashNET uses certain provided personal information (email addresses and passwords) in order to maintain structured registration of nicknames and channels on the IRC network. SlashNET Services will send automated messages for purposes of email address verification. This is used to ensure that in the event of a lost password, you are able to use your valid email address in order to recover your registration.
SlashNET will not share personal information, including email addresses, passwords, usernames, or hostnames, with any third party organization, nor send email that is not initiated by the end user.

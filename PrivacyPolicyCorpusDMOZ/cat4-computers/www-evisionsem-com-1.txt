We value your privacy! We will never give, lease, sell or otherwise disclose your personal information. Period! Resale or disclosure of information to third parties
eVision does not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease any personal information collected at our site, including contact forms, download requests or email lists.
Browser information collected on the web site
eVision analyzes the web site logs to improve the value of the materials available on the web site. These web site logs are not personally identifiable, and eVision makes no attempt to link them with the individuals that actually browse the site.
Privacy of our email lists / newsletter
Individuals must affirmatively ask to join our mailing lists using the forms provided on this web site or other means expressly sanctioned by eVision. We do not sell, rent, loan, trade, or lease the addresses on our list to anyone. In addition, we configure our software to refuse to divulge the email addresses of our list subscribers to anyone but authorized eVision staff, including other list subscribers.
Unsubscribe Policy
We strive to only send e-mail to those who want to receive it. If you would not like to receive future e-mails from us, reply to any email with UNSUBSCRIBE as the subject.

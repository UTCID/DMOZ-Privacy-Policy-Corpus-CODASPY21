Privacy policy
Protecting your privacy is important to us. We hope the following statement will help you understand how MW6 collects,
uses and safeguards the personal information you provide to us on our web pages. Unless you directly give us consent
to do otherwise, MW6 will only collect and use your personal information as set out below.
1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy
MW6 recognizes that you have a right to control your personal information. This statement sets out the Privacy Policy
which governs the use of personal information by MW6. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the MW6 web
pages. Usage of the MW6 web pages and services is conditional upon you agreeing to be bound by this Privacy Policy.
2. Information We Collect
All of our web pages are cookie free, MW6 does not collect information about your visit to
our site nor do we track the pages you visit on. We only collect your information when you place an online or offline order,
we request the information needed to complete the processing of your order and billing.
3. How We Use Your Personal Information
If you place an order using a credit card, we only share your credit card number and expiry date with outside credit card
processing companies in order to bill your order, but we do not provide any more information than necessary for this
billing purpose. We may also use the information to contact you regarding your order.

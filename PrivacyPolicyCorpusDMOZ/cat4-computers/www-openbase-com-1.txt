Privacy Policy
OpenBase International, LLC. ("OpenBase") will not trade, sell, or disclose to any third party any form of customer identifiable information without the consent of the customer (except as required by subpoena, search warrant or other legal process or in the case where failure to disclose information will lead to imminent harm to the customer or others). This includes information derived from registration, subscription, and use of the OpenBase software and services.
Collection / Use of Information
OpenBase shall collect and use customer identifiable information for billing, provisioning of service, to solve problems associated with service, and to inform customers of new products or services that will better meet their needs. OpenBase will use customer identifiable information to market products and services to the customer, but will not disclose or make available any customer identifiable information to any third parties seeking to market products. If a customer chooses not to participate in direct marketing of new products and services from OpenBase, that customer's information will not be used for the purpose of marketing new products.
Security
OpenBase has invested and deployed a wide variety of technology and security features to ensure the privacy of customer information on its network. In addition, OpenBase has implemented strict operations guidelines to ensure customer privacy is safeguarded at every level of its organization. OpenBase will continue to revise policies and implement additional security features as new technologies becomes available.
Communications
OpenBase will not read, listen to or disclose to any third parties private e-mail, conversations, or other communications that are transmitted using OpenBase services except as required to ensure proper operation of services or as otherwise authorized by law.
Unlawful Activity
OpenBase may use customer identifiable information to investigate and help prevent potentially unlawful activity or activities that threaten the integrity of service or network integrity or otherwise violate OpenBase's Service Level Agreement.
Account Information
OpenBase will do its best to honor requests from customers for account information, for example, name, address, company, or billing information. The customer is responsible for ensuring that the information on file with OpenBase is current and accurate.
Children's Privacy Policy
OpenBase does not sell products or services for purchase by children. OpenBase does not knowingly solicit or collect customer identifiable information from children or teenagers under the age of eighteen. In addition, OpenBase will not knowingly link to any third party web site that solicits or collects customer identifiable information from minors.

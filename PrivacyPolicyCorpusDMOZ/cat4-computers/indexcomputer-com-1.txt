INTRODUCTION:
Indexcomputer.com is committed to protecting your privacy. The information that you enter while placing an order or registering for our newsletters or promotions is the only information that we collect. We use the information to enhance your shopping experience. We do not distribute your personal information to third parties.
Information We Collect:
IndexComputer.com does not collect personal information from visitors. If you are just browsing, we do not seek personal information about you. If you wish to receive special promotions, our registration form will ask you to provide:
Your name
Your e-mail address
Periodically, we send an IndexComputer.com newsletter to our e-mail list. The newsletter features news about watches, new product offerings and seasonal specials. We do not make our mailing list available to other companies. If you no longer wish to receive any promotional materials, you may opt-out of receiving these communications by using the unsubscribe feature in our email.
When placing an order, you will need to provide the following information:
Your name
Your e-mail address
Your billing and shipping address
Your telephone number
The type of credit card you are using if applicable
Your credit card number and expiration date, or other form of payment
The information is used to process and send your order. We also use the information to contact you if there is a problem with your order. We do not share this information with third parties.
Protection of Your Information:
IndexComputer.com takes every precaution to protect our your information. When you submit sensitive information via our website, your information is protected both online and off-line. IndexComputer.com uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software to protect your data and secure your transactions. SSL encrypts all your personal information, including your credit card number, name and address during transactions, to provide a safe, secure shopping environment.
Your Consent to This Policy
By using the IndexComputer.com web site, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Changes to our privacy policy will be posted on this page so you will always know about the information we collect and how we use it.

Privacy Policy
We respect and honor your privacy.
This Privacy Policy applies to all of the products, services and websites offered by TopLang Software.
Information we collect and how we use it
When you request pages from this site,
our server automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website,
such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request.
Our server does not record e-mail addresses.
When you submit feedback or support request via our online web forms,
we may ask you for your name and email address, but we do not record the information.
When you place an order online with us,
we ask you for personal information for completing the order.
This information also is used to record your ownership of product license so that we can provide you with customer service.
We do not collect or store any credit card information.
We collect the domain names and IP addresses in order to aggregate information on the pages
users access or visit. This information is collected solely for statistical purposes and is not used to identify individuals.
Purchasing on our site is 100% secure and customers' private information is secure.
Orders are processed by our ordering services providers. Your personal information provided
through our online store is fully secured and protected through Secure Socket Layers (SSL).
All of your personal information such as your name, email address, and credit card number
are encrypted and made unintelligible to hackers.
Information sharing
We will not disclose your personal information outside of TopLang Software without your consent.
We will not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party.
We will take special care to protect the privacy and security of your information.
We only shares personal information with third parties outside of TopLang Software
if we believe such action is reasonably necessary to:
(a) satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request, or
(b) protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of TopLang Software, its users or
the public as required or permitted by law.
Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may decide to change this Privacy Policy from time to time.
If there are changes or additions to this Privacy Policy, we will post those changes here.
We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent.
Please contact us if you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy.

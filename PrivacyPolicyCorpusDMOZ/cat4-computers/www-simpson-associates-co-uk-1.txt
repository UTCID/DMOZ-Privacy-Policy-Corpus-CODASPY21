Your Consent & Cookies
By accessing our website you are giving us consent to collect your personal information. The site uses cookies or similar technology to collect information about your access to the site. We do this to find out things such as the number of visitors to the various parts of the site, to ensure the content of our website is useful and appropriate.
Cookies are small files that we transfer to your device, via our web browser, that enable us to remember certain information. We use cookies both to track visitor behaviour and to store site preferences. By using our website, you agree that we can place different types of cookies on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser. We use cookies to improve our website’s user experience and to measure and report on marketing performance.
Third-party cookies: In the course of serving advertisements to this website, our third-party ad servers (including Google) may place or recognise a unique cookie on your browser. User interaction with ads and ad-views across the Google Display Network and/or other display advertising networks are reported in conjunction with activity on this site. Information about your visit to this website, such as number of times you have viewed and/or clicked an ad (but not your name, address, or other personal information), is used for internal reporting and advertising optimisation only.
Users may opt out of Google’s use of cookies by visiting the Google Advertising Opt-out Page. Users may opt out of Google Analytics by visiting the Google Analytics Opt-out Page. Users may opt out of third party vendor use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative Opt-out Page.
We use Hotjar in order to better understand our users’ needs and to optimise this service and experience. Hotjar is a technology service that helps us better understand our users’ experience (e.g. how much time they spend on which pages, which links they choose to click, what users do and don’t like, etc.) and this enables us to build and maintain our service with user feedback. Hotjar uses cookies and other technologies to collect data on our users’ behavior and their devices. This includes a device’s IP address (processed during your session and stored in a de-identified form), device screen size, device type (unique device identifiers), browser information, geographic location (country only), and the preferred language used to display our website. Hotjar stores this information on our behalf in a pseudonymized user profile. Hotjar is contractually forbidden to sell any of the data collected on our behalf. For further details, please see the ‘about Hotjar’ section of Hotjar’s support site.
